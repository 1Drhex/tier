Guest Question.

EXPECTED TIMES AND VARIANCES FOR MILWAUKEE PAPER. Julie Ann Williams and the project management team at Milwaukee Paper want an expected time and variance for Activity F (Installing the Pollution Control System) where:
a = 1 week, m = 2 weeks, b = 9 weeks
APPROACH \(\blacktriangleright\) Use Equations (3–6) and (3–7) to compute the expected time and variance for F.

t = ( a + 4 m + b ) ∕ 6 (3-6)

Variance = [( b − a ) ∕ 6]\(^2\) (3-7)

Admin
Solution

SOLUTION \(\blacktriangleright\) The expected time for Activity F is:
\(t = \frac{a + 4m + b}{6} = \frac{1 + 4(2) + 9}{6} = \frac{18}{6}\) = 3 weeks
The variance for Activity F is:

\(Variance = \left[\frac{(b – a)}{6}\right]^2 = \left[\frac{(9 – 1)}{6}\right]^2 = \left(\frac{8}{6}\right)^2 = \frac{64}{36} = 1.78\)

INSIGHT \(\blacktriangleright\) Williams now has information that allows her to understand and manage Activity F. The expected time is, in fact, the activity time used in our earlier computation and identification of the critical path.
