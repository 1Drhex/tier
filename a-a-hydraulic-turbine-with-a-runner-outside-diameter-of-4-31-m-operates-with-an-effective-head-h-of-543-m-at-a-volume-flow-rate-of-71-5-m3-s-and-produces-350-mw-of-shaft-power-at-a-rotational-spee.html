Guest Question.

a. A hydraulic turbine with a runner outside diameter of 4.31 m operates with an effective head, H, of 543 m at a volume flow rate of 71.5 \(m^{3}/s\) and produces 350 MW of shaft power at a rotational speed of 333 rpm. Determine the specific speed, the specific diameter, and efficiency of this turbine.

b. Another geometrically and dynamically similar turbine with a runner 6.0 m diameter is to be built to operate with an effective head of 500 m. Determine the required flow rate, the expected power output, and the rotational speed of the turbine.

Admin
Solution

a. Note: All speeds are first converted to rad/s; therefore, \(\Omega =333\times \pi /30=34.87 rad/s.\)

Using Eq. (2.14),

\(\Omega _{s} =\frac{\Phi ^{1/2}_{1} }{\psi ^{3/4}_{1} } =\frac{\Omega Q^{1/2} }{\left(gH\right) ^{3/4} } \)

the specific speed is

\(\Omega _{s} =\Omega Q^{1/2}/ \left(gH\right) ^{3/4} =\frac{34.87\times 71.5^{0.5} }{\left(9.81\times 543\right) ^{0.75} } =0.473 rad \)

Using Eq. (2.17),

\(D_{s} =\frac{\psi ^{1/4}_{1} }{\Phi ^{1/2}_{1} } =\frac{D\left(gH\right) ^{1/4} }{Q^{1/2} } \)

the specific diameter is

\(D_{s} =\frac{D\left(gH\right) ^{1/4} }{Q^{1/2} } =\frac{4.31\times \left(9.81\times 543\right) ^{1/4} }{71.4^{1/2} } =4.354\)

For the turbine, the net hydraulic power is

\(P_{n}=\rho gQH =9810\times 71.5\times 543=380.9\times 10^{6} =380.9 MW \)

The turbine efficiency is

Î· = 350/380.9 = 0.919

b. Transposing Eq. (2.17), we can find the volume flow rate:

\(Q=\left(D/D_{s} \right) ^{2} \left(gH\right) ^{1/2} =\left(6/4.354\right) ^{2} \left(9.81\times 500\right) ^{1/2} =133 m^{3}/s \)

and the power output is

\(P=\eta \rho gQH =0.919\times 9810\times 133\times 500=599.5 MW \)

We can determine the rotational speed in rpm from Eq. (2.14) as

\(\Omega=\Omega _{s} \left(gH\right) ^{3/4} / Q^{1/2}=0.473\times \frac{30}{\pi } \times \left(9.81\times 500\right) ^{3/4} /133^{1/2} =229.6 rpm \)
