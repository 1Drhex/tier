Guest Question.

The angle shown in Figure P9.63 is subjected to a vertical shear force of V = 3.5 kips. Sketch the distribution of shear flow along the leg AB. Indicate the numerical value at all peaks.

Admin
Solution

Moment of inertia about the neutral axis: Recognizing that the wall thickness is thin, the moment of inertia for the channel shape can be calculated as:

\(\begin{aligned}&b=\frac{0.25 in .}{\sin 45^{\circ}}=0.3535534 in . \\&h=(5 \text { in. }) \cos 45^{\circ}=3.5355339 in . \\&I_{N A}=2\left[\frac{(0.3535534 in .)(3.5355339 in .)^{3}}{12}\right]=2.6041667 in .{ }^{4}\end{aligned}\)

 

First moment of area Q:

 

\(\begin{aligned}Q &=\bar{y}^{\prime} A^{\prime}=\left[\left(2.5 in.-\frac{y}{\sin 45^{\circ}}\right) \frac{\sin 45^{\circ}}{2}+y\right]\left[(0.25 in .)\left(2.5 \text { in. }-\frac{y}{\sin 45^{\circ}}\right)\right] \\&=\left[\frac{(2.5 in .) \sin 45^{\circ}}{2}-\frac{y}{2}+y\right]\left[(0.25 in .)\left(2.5 in .-\frac{y}{\sin 45^{\circ}}\right)\right] \\&=\left[0.8838835 in .+\frac{y}{2}\right]\left[0.625 in .^{2}-(0.3535534 in .) y\right] \\&=0.5524272 in .{ }^{3}+\left(0.3125 in .^{2}\right) y-\left(0.3125 in. ^{2}\right) y-(0.1767767 \text { in. }) y^{2} \\&=0.5524272 in .^{3}-(0.1767767 \text { in. }) y^{2}\end{aligned}\)

 

Shear flow:

\(\begin{aligned}q &=\frac{V Q}{I} \\&=\frac{(3,500 lb )\left(0.5524272 in .{ }^{3}-(0.1767767 in .) y^{2}\right)}{2.6041667 in .{ }^{4}} \\&=724.4621473 lb / in .-\left(237.5878818 lb / in .^{3}\right) y^{2}\end{aligned}\)

 

At y = 0,

\(q=q_{\max }=724 lb / in .\)
