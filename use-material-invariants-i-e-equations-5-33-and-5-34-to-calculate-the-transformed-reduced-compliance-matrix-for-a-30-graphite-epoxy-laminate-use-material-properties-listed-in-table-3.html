Solution

From , the reduced compliance matrix for this material system is

\(\left [ \begin{matrix} S_{11} & S_{12} & 0 \\ S_{12} & S_{22} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & S_{66} \end{matrix} \right ]=\left [ \begin{matrix} 5.88\times 10^{-12} & -1.76\times 10^{-12} & 0 \\ -1.76\times 10^{-12} & 100.0\times 10^{-12} &0 \\ 0 & 0& 76.9\times 10^{-12} \end{matrix} \right ]\left\lgroup\frac{1}{Pa} \right\rgroup\)

The compliance invariants may be calculated using these values and Equations 5.34:

\(\mathcal{U} ^S_1 =\frac{1}{8}(3S_{11}+3S_{22}+2S_{12}+S_{66})\)

 

\(=\frac{1}{8}[3(5.88\times 10^{-12}+3(100\times 10^{-12})+2(-1.76^{-12})+76.9\times 10^{-12}]=48\times 10^{-12}\)

 

\(\mathcal{U} ^S_2=\frac{1}{2}(S_{11}-S_{22})= \frac{1}{2}[5.88\times 10^{-12}-100.0\times 10^{-12}]= -47.1\times 10^{-12}\)

 

\(\mathcal{U} ^S_3 =\frac{1}{8}(S_{11}+S_{22}-2S_{12}-S_{66})\)

 

\(= \frac{1}{8}[5.88\times 10^{-12}+100.0\times 10^{-12}-2(-1.76\times 10^{-12})-76.9\times 10^{-12}]=4.06\times 10^{-12}\)

 

\(\mathcal{U} ^S_4 =\frac{1}{8}(S_{11}+S_{22}+6S_{12}-S_{66})\)

 

\(= \frac{1}{8}[5.88\times 10^{-12}+100.0\times 10^{-12}+6(-1.76\times 10^{-12})-76.9\times 10^{-12}]=2.296\times 10^{-12}\)

 

\(\mathcal{U} ^S_5 =\frac{1}{8}(S_{11}+S_{22}-2S_{12}+S_{66})\)

 

\(= \frac{1}{8}[5.88\times 10^{-12}+100.0\times 10^{-12}-2(-1.76\times 10^{-12})-76.9\times 10^{-12}]=93,17\times 10^{-12}\)

Using the first of Equations 5.33 and setting θ = 30°:

\(\overline{S}_{11}=\mathcal{U} ^S_1+\mathcal{U} ^S_2\cos20+\mathcal{U} ^S_3 \cos40\)

 

\(=(4.8\times10^{-12})+(-47.1\times10^{-12})\cos(60°)+(4.06\times10^{-12})\cos(120°)\)

 

\(= 23.3\times10^{-12}Pa^{-1}\)

Similarly, using the second of Equations 5.33 and setting θ = 30°:

\(\overline{S}_{12}=\overline{S}_{21}=\mathcal{U} ^S_4-\mathcal{U} ^S_3\cos40\)

 

\(= (2.296\times10^{-12})-(4.06\times10^{-12})\cos(120°)\)

 

\(=4.33\times10^{-12}Pa^{-1}\)

The additional terms within the\( [\overline{S}] \)matrix are calculated using the rest of Equations 5.33. A summary of our results is

\([\overline{S}]=\left [ \begin{matrix} 23.3\times 10^{-12} & 4.33\times 10^{-12} & -33.7\times 10^{-12} \\ 4.33\times 10^{-12} & 70.4\times 10^{-12} & -47.8\times 10^{-12} \\ -33.7\times 10^{-12} & -47.8\times 10^{-12} & 101.3\times 10^{-12} \end{matrix} \right ]\left\lgroup\frac{1}{Pa} \right\rgroup \)

Note that the [S] matrix is identical to that calculated in .
