Guest Question.

The mean diameter of a screw jack having pitch of 10 mm is 50 mm. A load of 25 kN is lifted through a distance of 180 mm. Find the work done in lifting the load and efficiency of the screw when

(a) The load rotates with the screw.

(b) The load rests on the loose head that does not rotate with the screw. The outer and inner diameters of the bearing surface of the loose head are 60 mm and 10 mm, respectively. The coefficient of friction for the screw as well the bearing surface may be taken as 0.10.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } p=10 mm , d_{ m }=50 mm , W=25 kN , h=180 mm , \mu=\mu_{ c }=0.10, R_{2}=30 mm , R_{1}=5 mm \).

\( \text { Helix angle of screw, } a=\tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{p}{\pi d_{m}}\right)=\tan ^{-1}\left(\frac{10}{\pi \times 50}\right)=3.6426^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Angel of friction, } \phi=\tan ^{-1} \mu=\tan ^{-1} 0.10=5.7106^{\circ} \).

Force required at the circumference of screw to lift the load,

\( P=W \tan (\alpha+\phi)=25 \tan \left(3.6426^{\circ}+5.7106^{\circ}\right)=4.118 kN \).

\( \text { Frictional torque of screw, } T_{f}=P \times \frac{d_{m}}{2}=4.118 \times \frac{50}{2}=102.94 N \cdot m \).

(a) Number of rotations made by screw to lift the load through 180 mm,

\( N=\frac{h}{p}=\frac{180}{10}=18 \).

\( \text { Work done in lifting the load }=2 \pi N T_{f}=2 \pi \times 18 \times 102.94=11642.6 N \cdot m \).

\( \text { Efficiency, } \eta=\frac{\tan \alpha}{\tan (\alpha+\phi)}=\frac{\tan 3.6426^{\circ}}{\tan \left(3.6426+5.7106^{\circ}\right)}=0.3865 \text { or } 38.65 \% \).

\( \text { (b) Mean radius of bearing surface, } R_{m}=\frac{1}{2}\left(R_{1}+R_{2}\right)=\frac{1}{2}(5+30)=17.5 mm \).

Total frictional torque required at the screw and collar,

\( T_{f}=P \times \frac{d_{ m }}{2}+\mu_{C} W R_{m} \).

\( =4.118 \times \frac{50}{2}+0.10 \times 25 \times 17.5=146.7 N \cdot m\).

\( \text { Work done in lifting the load }=2 \pi N T_{ f }=2 \pi \times 18 \times 146.7=16591.4 N \cdot m \).

Torque required to lift the load without friction,

\( T_{o}=W \tan a \times \frac{d_{ m }}{2} \).

\( =25 \tan 3.6426^{\circ} \times \frac{50}{2}=39.79 N \cdot m \).

\( \text { Efficiency of screw jack, } \eta=\frac{T_{o}}{T_{f}}=\frac{39.79}{146.7}=0.2712 \text { or } 27.12 \% \).
