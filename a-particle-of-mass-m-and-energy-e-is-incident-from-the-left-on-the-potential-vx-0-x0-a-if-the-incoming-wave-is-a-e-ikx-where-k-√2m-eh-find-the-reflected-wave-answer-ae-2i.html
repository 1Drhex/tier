Guest Question.

A particle of mass m and energy E is incident from the left on the potential

\( V(x)= \begin{cases}0, & (x<-a) \\ -V_{0}, & (-a \leq x \leq 0) \\ \infty, & (x>0)\end{cases} \).

(a) If the incoming wave is \( \left.A e^{i k x} \text { (where } k=\sqrt{2 m E} / \hbar\right) \) , find the reflected wave. Answer:

\( A e^{-2 i k a}\left[\frac{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right] e^{-i k x} \text {, where } k^{\prime}=\sqrt{2 m\left(E+V_{0}\right)} / \hbar \).

(b) Confirm that the reflected wave has the same amplitude as the incident wave.

(c) Find the phase shift δ (Equation 10.40) for a very deep well \( \left(E \ll V_{0}\right) \) .

\( \text { Answer: } \delta=-k a \).

\( \psi(x)=A\left(e^{i k x}-e^{i(2 \delta-k x)}\right) \quad(V(x) \neq 0) \) (10.40).

Admin
Solution

(a) In the region to the left

\( \psi(x)=A e^{i k x}+B e^{-i k x} \quad(x \leq-a) \).

In the region -a < x < 0, the Schrödinger equation gives

\( -\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m} \frac{d^{2} \psi}{d x^{2}}-V_{0} \psi=E \psi \quad \Rightarrow \quad \frac{d^{2} \psi}{d x^{2}}=-\left(k^{\prime}\right)^{2} \psi \).

where \( k^{\prime}=\sqrt{2 m\left(E+V_{0}\right)} / \hbar \). The general solution is

\( \psi=C \sin \left(k^{\prime} x\right)+D \cos \left(k^{\prime} x\right) \).

But ψ(0) = 0 implies D = 0, so

\( \psi(x)=C \sin \left(k^{\prime} x\right) \quad(-a \leq x \leq 0) \).

The continuity of \( \psi(x) \text { and } \psi^{\prime}(x) \text { at } x=-a \) says

\( A e^{-i k a}+B e^{i k a}=-C \sin \left(k^{\prime} a\right), \quad i k A e^{-i k a}-i k B e^{i k a}=k^{\prime} C \cos \left(k^{\prime} a\right) \).

Divide and solve for B:

\( \frac{i k A e^{-i k a}-i k B e^{i k a}}{A e^{-i k a}+B e^{i k a}}=-k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right) \).

\( i k A e^{-i k a}-i k B e^{i k a}=-A e^{-i k a} k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)-B e^{i k a} k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right) \).

\( B e^{i k a}\left[-i k+k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)\right]=A e^{-i k a}\left[-i k-k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)\right] \).

\( B=A e^{-2 i k a}\left[\frac{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right] \).

(b)

\( |B|^{2}=|A|^{2}\left[\frac{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right] \cdot\left[\frac{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right]=|A|^{2} \).

(c) From part (a) the wave function for x < -a is

\( \psi(x)=A e^{i k x}+A e^{-2 i k a}\left[\frac{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right] e^{-i k x} \).

But by definition of the phase shift (Eq. 10.40)

\( \psi(x)=A\left(e^{i k x}-e^{i(2 \delta-k x)}\right) \quad(V(x) \neq 0) \) (10.40).

\( \psi(x)=A\left[e^{i k x}-e^{i(2 \delta-k x)}\right] \).

so

\( e^{-2 i k a}\left[\frac{k-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{k+i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right]=-e^{2 i \delta} \).

This is exact. For a very deep well, \( E \ll V_{0}, \quad k=\sqrt{2 m E} / \hbar \ll \sqrt{2 m\left(E+V_{0}\right)} / \hbar=k^{\prime} \), so

\( e^{-2 i k a}\left[\frac{-i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}{i k^{\prime} \cot \left(k^{\prime} a\right)}\right]=-e^{2 i \delta} ; \quad e^{-2 i k a}=e^{2 i \delta} ; \quad \delta=-k a . \)
