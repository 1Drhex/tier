Guest Question.

Consider a 10-\(ft ^{3}\) rigid tank containing an air-water vapor mixture at 14.7 \(lbf / in .^{2}\), 90 F, with a 70% relative humidity. The system is cooled until the water just begins to condense. Determine the final temperature in the tank and the heat transfer for the process.

Admin
Solution
\(P _{ v 1}=\phi P _{ G 1}=0.7 \times 0.6988=0.489 lbf / in ^{2}\)

Since \(m _{ v }=\text { const } \& V =\text { const } \& \text { also } P _{ v }= P _{ G 2} \text { : }\)

\(P _{ G 2}= P _{ v 1} \times T _{2} / T _{1}=0.489 \times T _{2} / 549.7\)

For \(T _{2}\) = 80 F: 0.489×539.7/549.7 = 0.4801 =/ 0.5073 ( = \(P _{ G }\) at 80 F )

For \(T _{2}\) = 70 F: 0.489×529.7/549.7 = 0.4712 =/ 0.3632 ( = \(P _{ G }\) at 70 F )

interpolating → \(T _{2}\) = 78.0 F

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{2}= w _{1}=0.622 \frac{0.489}{(14.7-0.489)}=0.0214 \\& m _{ a }=\frac{ P _{ a 1} V }{ R _{ a } T _{1}}=\frac{14.211 psi \times 144 in ^{2} / ft ^{2} \times 10 ft ^{3}}{53.34 lbf – ft / lbm – R \times 549.7 R }=0.698 lbm\end{aligned}\)

Energy Eq.:

\(\begin{aligned}{ }_{1} Q_{2} &=U_{2}-U_{1}=m_{a}\left(u_{a 2}-u_{a 1}\right)+m_{v}\left(u_{v 2}-u_{v 1}\right) \\&=m_{a}\left[C_{v}\left(T_{a 2}-T_{a 1}\right)+w_{1}\left(u_{v 2}-u_{v 1}\right)\right] \\&=0.698 lbm [0.171(78-90)+0.0214(1036.3-1040.2)] Btu / lbm \\&=0.698 lbm (-2.135 Btu / lbm )=-1.49 Btu\end{aligned}\)
