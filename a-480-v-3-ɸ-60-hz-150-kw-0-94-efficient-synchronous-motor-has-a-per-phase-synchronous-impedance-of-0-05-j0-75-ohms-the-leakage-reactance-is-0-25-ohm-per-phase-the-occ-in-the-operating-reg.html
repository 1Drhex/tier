Guest Question.

A 480 V, 3-ɸ, 60 Hz, 150 kW, 0.94 efficient synchronous motor has a per-phase synchronous impedance of 0.05 + j0.75 ohms. The leakage reactance is 0.25 ohm per phase. The OCC in the operating region is given by

\(V_{f} =10 +20I_{f}\)

When the motor draws rated current at unity power factor, determine:

a.The approximate and exact values of the torque angle.

b.The magnetizing component of the field current.

c. The field current.

d. The armature reaction in equivalent field amperes.

e. The effective turns ratio.

Admin
Solution

a.The rated current is

\( I_{a}=\frac{P_{in}}{\sqrt{3}V_{L-L}\cos \theta } =\frac{150,000}{0.94}\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}(480)(1) } \right)=191.94\angle0^{°} A \)

The magnetizing voltage is found by applying KVL to the equivalent circuit of Fig. 5-12.

\( V_{m}=V_{t}-I_{a}(R_{a}+jX_{a}) \)

=\( \frac{480}{\sqrt{3} }\angle 0^{°} – 191.94 \angle0^{°}(0.05+j0.25) \)

=\( 271.8\angle-10.2^{°} V/phase \)

Similarly, the excitation voltage is

\( V_{f}=\frac{480}{\sqrt{3} }\angle0^{°}-191.94\angle0^{°}(0.05+j0.75) \)

=\( 303.8\angle-28.3^{°} V/phase \)

Thus, the approximate and exact values of the torque angle in electrical degrees are

\( \delta _{appr}=\underline{\underline {-28.3^{°}}} \)

 

\( \delta _{ex}=-28.3^{°} -(-10.2^{°})=\underline{\underline{-18.1^{°}} } \)

b.The magnitude of the magnetizing component of the field current can be found from the given open-circuit characteristic:

\( I_{m}=\frac{V_{m}-10}{20} =\frac{271.8\sqrt{3}-10 }{20}=23.04 A \)

This current lags its corresponding voltages by 90°. Thus,

\( I_{m}=\underline{\underline{23.04} } \angle -90^{°}-10.2^{°} \)

=\( \underline{\underline{23.04 \angle-100.2^{°}A} } \)

c.Similarly, the magnitude of the field current is

\( I_{f}=\frac{303.8\sqrt{3}-10 }{20}=25.81 A \)

In phasor form:

\( I_{f}=25.81\angle-90°-28.3° \)

=\( \underline{\underline{25.81\angle -118.3^{°}A} } \)

d.The armature reaction is found by using Eq. (5.11) \( I_{f}=I_{m} – I^{′}_{a} \).

\( I^{′}_{a}=I_{m}- I_{f}=23.04\angle -100.2^{°}-25.81\angle-118.3^{°} \)

=\( \underline{\underline{8.16 A} }\)

e.The effective turns ratio is obtained from Eq. (5.9) \( N_{e}=\frac{I_{a}}{I^{′}_{a}} \).

\( N_{e}=\frac{I_{m}}{I^{′}_{a}}=\frac{191.94}{8.16}= \underline{\underline{23.51} } \)
