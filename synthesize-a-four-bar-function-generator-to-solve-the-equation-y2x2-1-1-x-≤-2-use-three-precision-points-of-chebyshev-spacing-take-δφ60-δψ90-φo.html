Guest Question.

Synthesize a four-bar function generator to solve the equation:\( y=2 x^{2}-1,1<x \leq 2 \). Use three precision points of Chebyshev spacing. Take \( \Delta \phi=60^{\circ}, \Delta \psi=90^{\circ}, \phi_{\circ}=30^{\circ} \text { and } \psi_{\circ}=60^{\circ} \text {, where } \Delta \phi, \Delta \psi \) are ranges of input and output link rotations and \( \phi_{o}, \psi_{o} \) are initial angular positions of input and output links respectively.

Admin
Solution

Chebyshev’s precision points are given by,

\( x_{m}=a+b \cos \left[\frac{(2 m-1) \pi}{2 n}\right], m=1,2,3 \).

\( n=3, x_{i}=1, x_{f}=2 \).

\( a=\frac{x_{i}+x_{f}}{2}=\frac{1+2}{2}=1.5, b=\frac{x_{f}-x_{i}}{2}=\frac{2-1}{2}=0.5 \).

∴ \( x_{i}=1.5+0.5 \cos \left[\frac{(2 m-1) \pi}{6}\right] \).

\( x_{1}=1.5+0.5 \cos \frac{\pi}{6}=1.933 \).

\( x_{2}=1.5+0.5 \cos \frac{\pi}{2}=1.5 \).

\( x_{3}=1.5+0.5 \cos \frac{5 \pi}{2}=1.067 \).

Now \( y=2 x^{2}-1 \).

\( y_{1}=2 \times(1.933)^{2}-1=6.473 \).

\( y_{2}=2 \times(1.5)^{2}-1=3.5 \).

\( y_{3}=2 \times(1.067)^{2}-1=1.277 \).

Scale factors are:

\( r_{x}=\frac{\Delta \phi}{x_{f}-x_{i}}=\frac{60}{2-1}=60 \).

\( r_{x}=\frac{\Delta \psi}{y_{f}-y_{i}} \).

Now \( y_{i}=2 \times 1^{2}-1=1 \).

\( y_{f}=2 \times 2^{2}-1=7 \).

\( r_{y}=\frac{90}{7-1}=\frac{90}{6}=15 \).

\( r_{x}=\frac{\phi_{m}-\phi_{i}}{x_{m}-x_{i}}, \phi_{i}=\phi_{0}=30^{\circ}, x_{i}=1 \).

\( \frac{\phi_{1}-30}{1.933-1}=60, \phi_{1}=85.98^{\circ} \).

∴ \( \frac{\phi_{2}-30}{1.5-1}=60, \phi_{2}=60^{\circ} \).

\( \frac{\phi_{3}-30}{1.067-1}=60, \phi_{3}=34.02^{\circ} \).

\( r_{y}=\frac{\psi_{m}-\psi_{i}}{y_{m}-y_{i}}, \phi_{i}=\phi_{0}=60^{\circ}, y_{i}=34.02^{\circ} \).

\( \frac{\psi_{1}-60}{6.473-1}=15, \psi_{1}=142.1^{\circ} \).

\( \frac{\psi_{2}-60}{3.5-1}=15, \psi_{2}=97.5^{\circ} \).

\( \frac{\psi_{3}-60}{1.277-1}=15, \psi_{3}=64.155^{\circ} \).

The Freudenstein’s equations become:

\( \left[\begin{array}{rll} -0.789 & 0.070 & 1 \\ -0.130 & 0.5 & 1 \\ 0.436 & 0.829 & 1 \end{array}\right]\left\{\begin{array}{l} k_{1} \\ k_{2} \\ k_{3} \end{array}\right\}=\left\{\begin{array}{l} 0.557 \\ 0.793 \\ 0.865 \end{array}\right\} \).

\( [A]\{k\}=\{B\} \).

\( |A|=-0.02657 \).

\( \left|A_{1}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{lll} 0.557 & 0.070 & 1 \\ 0.793 & 0.5 & 1 \\ 0.865 & 0.829 & 1 \end{array}\right|=0.04668, k_{1}=\frac{\left|A_{1}\right|}{|A|}=1.757 \).

\( \left|A_{2}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{rrr} -0.789 & 0.557 & 1 \\ -0.130 & 0.793 & 1 \\ 0.436 & 0.865 & 1 \end{array}\right|=-0.57183, k_{2}=\frac{\left|A_{2}\right|}{|A|}=21.522 \).

\( \left|A_{3}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{lll} -0.789 & 0.070 & 0.557 \\ -0.130 & 0.5 & 0.793 \\ 0.436 & 0.865 & 1 \end{array}\right|=-0.54377, k_{3}=\frac{\left|A_{3}\right|}{|A|}=20.465 \).

\( k_{1}=\frac{d}{a}, a=\frac{d}{k_{1}}=-\frac{1}{1.757}=-0.569 \).

\( k_{2}=-\frac{d}{c}, c=-\frac{d}{k_{2}}=-\frac{1}{21.522}=-0.046 \).

\( k_{3}=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}+c^{2}+d^{2}}{2 a c}, 20.465 \times 2 \times(-0.569) \times(-0.046)^{2}=(-0.569)^{2}-b^{2}+(0.046)^{2}+1 \).

\( b^{2}=0.25457, b=0.504 \).

\( a=-0.569, b=0.504, c=-0.046, d=1 \).

The sign indicates the reversed direction of length.
