Guest Question.

Determine the volume of air supplied for complete combustion of one \(\textbf{m}^{3}\) of fuel gas. If 40% excess air is supplied then find the percentage contraction in volume after the products of combustion have been cooled. The composition of fuel gas by volume is, \(H_{2} = 20\%, CH_{4} = 3\%, CO = 22\%, CO_{2} = 8\%, N_{2} = 47\%\).Consider air to have \(21\% O_{2}\) and \(79\% N_{2}\) by volume.

Admin
