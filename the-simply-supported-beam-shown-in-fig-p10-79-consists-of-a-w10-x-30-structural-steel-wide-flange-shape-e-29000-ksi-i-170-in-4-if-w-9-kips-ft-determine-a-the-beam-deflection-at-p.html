Guest Question.

The simply supported beam shown in Fig. P10.79 consists of a W10 × 30 structural steel wide-flange shape [E = 29,000 ksi; I = 170 \(\text { in. }^{4}\)]. If w = 9 kips/ft, determine:
(a) the beam deflection at point A.
(b) the beam deflection at point D.

Admin
Solution

(a) Beam deflection at point A
Consider cantilever beam deflection of 85 kip-ft concentrated moment.
[Appendix C, Cantilever beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{M L^{2}}{2 E I}\)

Values:

\(M=85 \text { kip-ft, } L=3 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }{ }^{2}\)

Computation:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{M L^{2}}{2 E I}=-\frac{(85 \text { kip-ft })(3 ft )^{2}(12 in. / ft )^{3}}{2\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}=-0.134069 in .\)

 

Consider rotation at B caused by 85 kip-ft concentrated moment.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I} \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(M=85 kip – ft , L=15 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip \text {-in. }^{2}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I}=\frac{(85 kip – ft )(15 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{3\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}=0.0124138 rad \\&v_{A}=-(3 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0124138 rad )=-0.446897 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider cantilever beam deflection of 9 kips/ft uniformly distributed load.
[Appendix C, Cantilever beam with uniformly distributed load.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{w L^{4}}{8 E I}\)

Values:

\(w=9 kips / ft , L=3 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }{ }^{2}\)

Computation:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{w L^{4}}{8 E I}=-\frac{(9 kips / ft )(3 ft )^{4}(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{8\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}=-0.031939 in .\)

 

Consider rotation at B caused by 9 kips/ft uniformly distributed load.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I} \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=(9 kips / ft )(3 ft )(1.5 ft )=40.5 kip – ft ,\\&L=15 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I}=\frac{(40.5 kip – ft )(15 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{3\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}=0.0059148 rad \\&v_{A}=-(3 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0059148 rad )=-0.212933 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 9 kips/ft uniformly distributed load on segment BC.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over a portion of the span.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}(2 L-a)^{2} \quad \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=9 kips / ft , L=15 ft , a=5 ft, \\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{B}=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}(2 L-a)^{2}=\frac{(9 kips / ft )(5 ft )^{2}(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{24(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in. ^{2}\right)}[2(15 ft )-(5 ft )]^{2}=0.0114097 rad \\&v_{A}=(3 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0114097 rad )=0.410748 in.\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 25-kip concentrated load. [Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated load not at midspan.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{P b\left(L^{2}-b^{2}\right)}{6 L E I} \quad \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&P=25 \text { kips, } L=15 ft , b=5 ft ,\\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{B}=\frac{P b\left(L^{2}-b^{2}\right)}{6 L E I}=\frac{(25 kips )(5 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{6(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}\left[(15 ft )^{2}-(5 ft )^{2}\right]=0.0081136 rad \\&v_{A}=(3 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0081136 rad )=0.292089 in .\end{aligned}\)

Beam deflection at A

\(\begin{aligned}v_{A} &=-0.134069 in .-0.446897 in .-0.031939 in .-0.212933 in .+0.410748 in .+0.292089 in . \\&=-0.123001 in .=0.1230 in. \downarrow\end{aligned}\)

 

(b) Beam deflection at point D
Consider 85 kip-ft concentrated moment.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{D}=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right)\) (elastic curve)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=-85 kip – ft , L=15 ft , x=10 ft ,\\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{D} &=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(-85 kip – ft )(10 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{6(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in. ^{2}\right)}\left[2(15 ft )^{2}-3(15 ft )(10 ft )+(10 ft )^{2}\right]=0.331034 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider moment at B caused by 9 kips/ft uniformly distributed load on overhang AB.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{D}=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right)\) (elastic curve)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=-(9 kips / ft )(3 ft )(1.5 ft )=-40.5 kip – ft , \\&L=15 ft , x=10 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{D} &=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(-40.5 kip – ft )(10 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{6(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in. ^{2}\right)}\left[2(15 ft )^{2}-3(15 ft )(10 ft )+(10 ft )^{2}\right]=0.157729 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 9 kips/ft uniformly distributed load on segment BC.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over a portion of the span.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{D}=-\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}\left(2 x^{3}-6 L x^{2}+a^{2} x+4 L^{2} x-a^{2} L\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=9 kips / ft , L=15 ft , a=5 ft , x=10 ft ,\\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{D} &=-\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}\left(2 x^{3}-6 L x^{2}+a^{2} x+4 L^{2} x-a^{2} L\right) \\&=-\frac{(9 kips / ft )(5 ft )^{2}(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{24(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}\left[2(10 ft )^{3}-6(15 ft )(10 ft )^{2}+(5 ft )^{2}(10 ft )+4(15 ft )^{2}(10 ft )-(5 ft )^{2}(15 ft )\right] \\&=-0.410751 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 25-kip concentrated load. [Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated load not at midspan.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{D}=-\frac{P a b}{6 L E I}\left(L^{2}-a^{2}-b^{2}\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&P=25 \text { kips, } L=15 ft , a=10 ft , b=5 ft, \\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }{ }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{D} &=-\frac{P a b}{6 L E I}\left(L^{2}-a^{2}-b^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(25 kips )(10 ft )(5 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{6(15 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}\left[(15 ft )^{2}-(10 ft )^{2}-(5 ft )^{2}\right]=-0.486815 in .\end{aligned}\)

Beam deflection at D

\(\begin{aligned}v_{D} &=0.331034 in .+0.157729 \text { in. }-0.410751 \text { in. }-0.486815 in . \\&=-0.408803 in .=0.409 in. \downarrow\end{aligned}\)
