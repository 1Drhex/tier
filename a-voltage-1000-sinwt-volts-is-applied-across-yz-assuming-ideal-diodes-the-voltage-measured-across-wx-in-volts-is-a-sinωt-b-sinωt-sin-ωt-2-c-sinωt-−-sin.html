Guest Question.

A voltage 1000 sinwt volts is applied across YZ. Assuming ideal diodes, the voltage measured across WX in volts, is

(a) sinωt

(b) (sinωt + |sin ωt|)/2

(c) (sinωt − |sin ωt|)/2

(d) 0 for all t

Admin
Solution

During the half cycle,
all diodes are OFF & hence

\(\begin{aligned}&\left|V_{1}\right|=\left|V_{2}\right| \\&V=0 V\end{aligned}\)

During –ve half cycle

V = 0 V

Hence, the correct option is (d).
