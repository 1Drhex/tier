Guest Question.

Air at 327째C, 400 kPa with a volume flow 1 \(m ^{3} / s\) runs through an adiabatic turbine with exhaust pressure of 100 kPa. Neglect kinetic energies and use constant specific heats. Find the lowest and highest possible exit temperature. For each case find also the rate of work and the rate of entropy generation.

Admin
Solution

C.V Turbine. Steady single inlet and exit flows, q = 0.

Inlet state: (T, P) \(v _{ i }= RT _{ i } / P _{ i }=0.287 \times 600 / 400=0.4305 m ^{3} / kg\)

\(\dot{ m }=\dot{ V } / v _{ i }=1 / 0.4305=2.323 kg / s\)

The lowest exit T is for maximum work out i.e. reversible case

Process: Reversible and adiabatic => constant s from Eq.7.9

Eq.6.23: \(T _{ e }= T _{ i }\left( P _{ e } / P _{ i }\right)^{\frac{ k -1}{ k }}=600 \times(100 / 400)^{0.2857}= 4 0 3 . 8 K\)

\(\Rightarrow \quad w=h_{i}-h_{e}=C_{P_{o}}\left(T_{i}-T_{e}\right)=1.004 \times(600-403.8)=197 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\dot{ W }_{ T }=\dot{ m } w =2.323 \times 197= 4 5 7 . 6 kW \quad \text { and } \quad \dot{ S }_{ gen }= 0\)

 

Highest exit T occurs when there is no work out, throttling

\(q =\varnothing ; \quad w =\varnothing \quad \Rightarrow h _{ i }- h _{ e }=0 \Rightarrow T _{ e }= T _{ i }= 6 0 0 K\)

 

\(\dot{ S }_{ gen }=\dot{ m }\left( s _{ e }- s _{ i }\right)=-\dot{ m } R \ln \frac{ P _{ e }}{ P _{ i }}=-2.323 \times 0.287 \ln \frac{100}{400}= 0 . 9 2 4 k W / K\)
