Guest Question.

Determine the reactions and draw the shear and bending moment diagrams for the beam shown in Fig. \( 13.14 (\mathrm{a})\) by the method of consistent deformations.

Admin
Solution

Degree of Indeterminancy. \(i=2\).

Primary Beam. The vertical reactions \(C_{y}\) and \(E_{y}\) at the roller supports \(C\) and \(E\), respectively, are selected as the redundants. These supports are then removed to obtain the cantilever primary beam shown in Fig. 13.14(b). Next, the primary beam is subjected separately to the external loading and the unit values of the redundants \(C_{y}\) and \(E_{y}\), as shown in Fig. \(13.14(\mathrm{~b}),(\mathrm{c})\), and (d), respectively.

Compatibility Equations. See Fig. 13.14(a) through (d).

\( \begin{array}{l} \Delta_{C O}+f_{C C} C_{y}+f_{C E} E_{y}=0 \\\end{array} \) (1)

\( \begin{array}{l} \Delta_{E O}+f_{E C} C_{y}+f_{E E} E_{y}=0 \end{array} \) (2)

Deflections of Primary Beam. By using the deflection formulas, we obtain

\( \begin{array}{ll} \Delta_{C O}=-\frac{82,500 \mathrm{kN} \cdot \mathrm{m}^{3}}{E I} & \Delta_{E O}=-\frac{230,000 \mathrm{kN} \cdot \mathrm{m}^{3}}{E I} \\ f_{C C}=\frac{333.333 \mathrm{~m}^{3}}{E I} & f_{E C}=\frac{833.333 \mathrm{~m}^{3}}{E I} \\ f_{E E}= \frac{2,666.667 \mathrm{~m}^{3}}{E I} \end{array} \)

By applying Maxwell’s law,

\( f_{C E}=\frac{833.333 \mathrm{~m}^{3}}{E I} \)

Magnitudes of the Redundants. By substituting the deflections of the primary beam into the compatibility equations, we obtain

\( \begin{array}{r} -82,500+333.333 C_{y}+833.333 E_{y}=0 \\ -230,000+833.333 C_{y}+2,666.667 E_{y}=0 \end{array} \)

or

\( \begin{array}{c} 333.333 C_{y}+833.333 E_{y}=82,500 \\\end{array} \) (1a)

\( \begin{array}{c} 833.333 C_{y}+2,666.667 E_{y}=230,000 \end{array} \) (2a)

Solving Eqs. (1a) and (2a) simultaneously for \(C_{y}\) and \(E_{y}\), we obtain

\( C_{y}=145.714 \mathrm{kN} \uparrow \quad E_{y}=40.714 \mathrm{kN} \uparrow \)

Reactions. The remaining reactions can now be determined by applying the three equations of equilibrium to the free body of the indeterminate beam (Fig. 13.14(e)):

\( \begin{array}{c} +\rightarrow \sum F_{x}=0 \quad A_{x}=0 \\ +\uparrow \sum F_{y}=0 \quad \begin{array}{c} A_{y}-120+145.714-120+40.714=0 \\ A_{y}=53.572 \mathrm{kN} \uparrow \end{array} \\ +\curvearrowleft \sum M_{A}=0 \quad M_{A}-120(5)+145.714(10)-120(15)+40.714(20)=0 \\ M_{A}=128.58 \mathrm{kN} \cdot \mathrm{m} \curvearrowleft \end{array} \)

Shear and Bending Moment Diagrams. See Fig. 13.14(f).
