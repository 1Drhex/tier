Guest Question.

Factory A1 in Fig. 33a absorbs an apparent power of 4600 kVA from a 25 kV, 60 Hz network. Nonlinear loads in the plant produce a \(5^{\text {th }} \text { and } 29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic current. Compared to the fundamental current, the \(5^{\text {th }}\) harmonic has a value of 0.12 pu (12%) and the \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic has a value of 0.024 pu (2.4%). The feeder at the point of common coupling has a short-circuit capacity of 97 MVA.

Calculate:

a. The reactance \(X_{ S }\) of the feeder

b. The value of the \(5^{\text {th }}\) and \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonics

c. The value of the harmonic voltages at the PCC

d. The relative values, with respect to the 25 kV feeder voltage, of the harmonic voltages at the PCC

e. The THD at the PCC

Admin
Solution

a. Referring to Fig. 33, the 60 Hz reactance of the feeder is:

 

\(X_{ S }=\frac{E_{ S }^{2}}{S_{ SC }}=\frac{25^{2}}{97}=6.44 \Omega\)

 

b. Fundamental current drawn by factory A1:

 

\(I_{1}=\frac{S}{E_{ S } \sqrt{3}}=\frac{4600 \times 10^{3}}{25000 \sqrt{3}}=106 A\)

 

The \(5^{\text {th }}\) harmonic current:

 

\(I_{5}=0.12 \times 106=12.7 A\)

 

The \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic current:

 

\(I_{29}=0.024 \times 106=2.5 A\)

 

c. The \(5^{\text {th }}\) harmonic voltage, line-to-line:

 

\(\begin{aligned}E_{5} &=I_{5} h X_{ S } \sqrt{3} \\&=12.7 \times 5 \times 6.44 \times \sqrt{3}=708 V\end{aligned}\)

 

The \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic voltage, line to line

 

\(\begin{aligned}E_{29} &=I_{29} h X_{ S } \sqrt{3} \\&=2.5 \times 29 \times 6.44 \times \sqrt{3}=809 V\end{aligned}\)

 

d. Relative value of \(E_{5}\)

 

\(E_{5}( pu )=708 / 25000=0.028=2.8 \%\)

 

Relative value of \(E_{29}\)

 

\(E_{29}( pu )=809 / 25000=0.032=3.2 \%\)

 

Note that the voltage distortion due to the \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic is greater than that due to the \(5^{\text {th }}\) harmonic even though the \(29^{\text {th }}\) harmonic current is 5 times smaller than the \(5^{\text {th }}\)

 

e. Total voltage distortion at the PCC

 

\(\left. THD =\sqrt{(} 0.028^{2}+0.032^{2}\right)=0.043=4.3 \%\)

 

This THD is considered to be high.
