\(^{b}\) The plasticity index of A-7-5 soils is ≤ liquid limit – 30. For A-7-6 soils, it is > than the liquid limit – 30

Compute group index:

Group Index = \(\left(F-35\right) \left[0.2+0.005 \left(w_{L}-40\right) \right] +0.001\left(F-15\right) \left(I_{P}-10\right) =\left(54-35\right) \left[0.2+0.005\left(44-40\right) \right] +0.01\left(54-15\right) \left(23-10\right) =9\)
