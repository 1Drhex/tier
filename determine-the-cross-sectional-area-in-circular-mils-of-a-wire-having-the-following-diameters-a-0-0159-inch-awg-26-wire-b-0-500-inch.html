Guest Question.

Determine the cross-sectional area in circular mils of a wire having the following diameters:
a. 0.0159 inch (AWG 26 wire)
b. 0.500 inch

Admin
Solution

a. \(d=0.0159\) inch

\(=(0.0159\) inch \()(1000\) mils/inch \()\)

 

\(=15.9 \) mils

Now, using Equation 3–5, we obtain

\(A_{CM}=d_{mil}\) \(^{2}\) [circular mils, CM] (3–5)

\(A_{CM}=(15.9)^{2}=253\) CM

From Table 3–2, we see that the previous result is precisely the area given for AWG 26 wire.
