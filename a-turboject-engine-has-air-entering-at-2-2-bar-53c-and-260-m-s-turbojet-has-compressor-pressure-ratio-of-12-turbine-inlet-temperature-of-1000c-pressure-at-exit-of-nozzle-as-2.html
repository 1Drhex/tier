Guest Question.

A turboject engine has air entering at 2.2 bar, – 53°C and 260 m/s. Turbojet has compressor pressure ratio of 12, turbine inlet temperature of 1000°C, pressure at exit of nozzle as 2.2 bar, isentropic processes in diffuser & nozzle and isentropic efficiecies of compressor and turbine as 85% and 88% respectively. Neglecting pressure drop in combustion chamber determine the pressure and temperature at all salient points and the velocity at exit of nozzle. Also consider air as working fluid throughout.
