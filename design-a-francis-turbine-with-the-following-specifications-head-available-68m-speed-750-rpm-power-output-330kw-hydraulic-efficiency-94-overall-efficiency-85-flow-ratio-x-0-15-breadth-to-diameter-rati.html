Guest Question.

Design a Francis turbine with the following specifications:
Head available 68 m
Speed 750 rpm
Power output 330 kw
hydraulic efficiency \(94\%\)
overall efficiency \(85\%\)
Flow ratio X 0.15
Breadth to diameter ratio at inlet 0.1
outer to inner diameter ratio \(\frac{1}{2}\)

Assume \(6\%\) of circumferential area of runner occupied by thickness of vanes and flow velocity remains constant and flow is radial at exit.

Admin
Solution

Given \( H = 68 m; \chi = 0.15; N = 750 rpm, n_{1} = 0.1\)

\( P = 330 kw; {D_{1}}/{D_{2}} = \frac{1}{2}; \eta_{h} = 94\%; \eta_{0} = 85\%; area reduction = 6\%; k_{1} = 0.94;\)

overall efficiency \(\eta_{0} = \frac{P}{\gamma QH}; 0.85 = \frac{330 \times 1000}{9800 \times Q \times 68}\)

\(\therefore Q = 0.582 {m^{3}}/{s}\)

 

\( \chi = \frac{V_{f}}{\sqrt{2gH}};\)

 

\( V_{f} = 0.15 \sqrt{2 \times 9.8 \times 68}\)

 

\( V_{f} = 5.47 {m}/{s}\)

 

\( V_{f} = V_{1f} = V_{2f} =5.47 {m}/{s} \)

From eq. 6.9 \( w_{T}=\frac{w\left\lgroup p/\rho\right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{2}} }{\left(gH\right)^{5/4} }\)

\( Q = \left(k \pi n_{1} D^{2}_{1}\right)V_{1f}\) and substituting proper values

 

\( 0.582 = 0.94 \times \pi \times 0.1 \times D^{2}_{1} \times 5.47\)

 

\( D_{1} = 0.6 m = 600 mm\)

 

\( \frac{B_{1}}{D{1}} = 0.1 \)

 

\( \therefore B_{1} = 600 \times 0.1 = 60 mm\)

 

\( u_{1} = \frac{\pi D_{1}N}{60}\)

 

\(\therefore u_{1} = \frac {\pi \times 0.6 \times 750}{60} = 23.5 {m}/{s}\)

 

\( \eta_{h} = \frac {u_{1} V_{1w} – u_{2} V_{2w}}{gH}\)

as \(V_{2w} = 0\) because radial discharge

\( \eta_{h} = \frac {u_{1} V_{1w}}{gH}\)

 

\(\therefore V_{1w} =\frac{0.94 \times 9.8 \times 68}{23.5}\)

 

\( = 26.6 {m}/{s}\)

From Fig. 6. 16 we have

\(\therefore \tan \alpha_{1} = \frac{V_{1f}}{V_{1w}} = \frac {5.47}{26.6} = 0.2\)

 

\( \alpha_{1} = 11.6^{\circ}\)

outer diameter \( D_{2} = \frac {1}{2} D_{1} = \frac{1}{2} \times 600 = 300 mm\)

\( u_{2} = \frac{\pi D_{2} N}{60}\)

 

\(\therefore u_{2} = \frac{\pi \times 0.3 \times 750}{60} = 11.7 {m}/{s}\)

 

\( \tan \beta_{2} = \frac{V_{2f}}{u_{2}} = \frac{5.47}{11.7} = 0.46\)

 

\( \beta_{2} = 25^{\circ}\)

 

\( Q = K_{1} \pi D^{2}_{1} n_{1} V_{1f} = k_{2} \pi D^{2}_{2} n_{1} V_{2f} \)

assume \( k_{1} = k_{2}; V_{1f} = V_{2f}\)

\(\therefore 0.6^{2} \times 0.1 = n_{2} \times 0.3^{2} \)

 

\( n_{2} =0.4\)

 

\( n_{2} = \frac{B_{2}}{D_{2}}\)

 

\(\therefore B_{2} = 300 \times 0.4 = 120 mm\)
