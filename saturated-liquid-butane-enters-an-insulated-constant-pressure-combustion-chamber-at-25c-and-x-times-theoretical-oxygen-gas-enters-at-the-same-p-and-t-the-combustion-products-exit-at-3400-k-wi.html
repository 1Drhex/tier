Solution

Butane: \(T _{1}= T _{ o }=25^{\circ} C , \text { sat liq., } x _{1}=0, \quad T _{ c }=425.2 K , \quad P _{ c }=3.8 MPa\)

Do the properties from the generalized charts

Fig. D.1: \(T _{ r1 }=0.7, P _{ r1}=0.1, \quad P _{1}= P _{ r1 } P _{ c }=380 kPa\)

Figs. D.2 and D.3: \(\left(\overline{ h }_{1}^{*}-\overline{ h }_{1}\right)_{ f }=4.85 \overline{ R } T _{ c }, \quad\left(\overline{ s }_{1}^{*}-\overline{ s }_{1}\right)_{ f }=6.8 \overline{ R }\)

Oxygen: \(T _{2}= T _{ o }=25^{ \circ } C\), X – Theoretical \(O _{2}\)

Products: \(T _{3}=3400 K\), Assumes complete combustion

\(C _{4} H _{10}+6.5 X O _{2} \rightarrow 4 CO _{2}+5 H _{2} O +6.5 ( X -1) O _{2}\)

Energy Eq.: \(Q _{ cv }+ H _{ R }= H _{ P }+ W _{ cv } ; Q _{ cv }=0, \quad W _{ cv }=0\)

\(H _{ R }= n \left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ C 4 H 10}=1(-126200+-17146)=-143346 kJ\)

Products:

\(\begin{array}{ll}CO _{2} & n \left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ CO 2}=4(-393522+177836)=-862744 kJ \\H _{2} O & n \left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ H 2 O }=5(-241826+149073)=-463765 kJ \\O _{2} & n \left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ O 2}=6.5( X -1)(0+114101)=( X -1) 741657 kJ \\H _{ P }= & \sum n _{ i }\left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ i }=741657 X -2068166\end{array}\)

Energy Eq.: \(H _{ P }= H _{ R }\) solve for X; X = 2.594

Assume that the exit pressure equals the inlet pressure: \(P _{ e }= P _{ i }=380 kPa\)

\(\overline{ s }_{ C _{4} H _{10}}=\left[\overline{ s }_{ f }^{ o }-\overline{ R } \ln \frac{ P _{1}}{ P _{ o }}-\left(\overline{ s }_{1}^{*}-\overline{ s }_{1}\right)_{ f }\right] ; \quad \overline{ s }_{ O _{2}}=\left[\overline{ s }^{ o }-\overline{ R } \ln \frac{ P _{1}}{ P _{ o }}\right]\)
