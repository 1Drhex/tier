Guest Question.

A particle in the harmonic oscillator potential starts out in the state

\( Ψ(x,0) = A[3ψ_0(x) + 4ψ_1(x)]\).

(a) Find A.

(b) Construct Ψ(x,t) and \( |\Psi(x, t)|^{2} \). Don’t get too excited if \( |\Psi(x, t)|^{2} \) oscillates at exactly the classical frequency; what would it have been had I specified \( ψ_2(x) \), instead of \( ψ_1(x) \)?.

(c) Find \( \left\langle x\right\rangle \) and \( \left\langle p\right\rangle \) Check that Ehrenfest’s theorem (Equation 1.38) holds, for this wave function.

(d) If you measured the energy of this particle, what values might you get, and with what probabilities?

\( \frac{d\langle p\rangle}{d t}=\left\langle-\frac{\partial V}{\partial x}\right\rangle \). (1.38).

Admin
Solution

(a)

\( 1=\int|\Psi(x, 0)|^{2} d x=|A|^{2} \int\left(9\left|\psi_{0}\right|^{2}+12 \psi_{0}^{*} \psi_{1}+12 \psi_{1}^{*} \psi_{0}+16\left|\psi_{1}\right|^{2}\right) d x \).

\( =|A|^{2}(9+0+0+16)=25|A|^{2} \Rightarrow A = 1/5\).

(b)

\( \Psi(x, t)=\frac{1}{5}\left[3 \psi_{0}(x) e^{-i E_{0} t / \hbar}+4 \psi_{1}(x) e^{-i E_{1} t / \hbar}\right] = \frac{1}{5}\left[3 \psi_{0}(x) e^{-i \omega t / 2}+4 \psi_{1}(x) e^{-3 i \omega t / 2}\right] \).

(Here \( ψ_0 \) and \( ψ_1 \) are given by Eqs. 2.60 and 2.63; \( E_0 \) and \( E_1 \) by Eq. 2.62

\( \psi_{0}(x)=\left(\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}\right)^{1 / 4} e^{-\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar} x^{2}} \). (2.60).

\( \psi_{1}(x)=A_{1} \hat{a}_{+} \psi_{0}=\frac{A_{1}}{\sqrt{2 \hbar m \omega}}\left(-\hbar \frac{d}{d x}+m \omega x\right)\left(\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}\right)^{1 / 4} e^{-\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar} x^{2}} =A_{1}\left(\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}\right)^{1 / 4} \sqrt{\frac{2 m \omega}{\hbar}} x e^{-\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar} x^{2}} \) (2.63)

\( \psi_{n}(x)=A_{n}\left(\hat{a}_{+}\right)^{n} \psi_{0}(x) \), with \( E_{n}=\left(n+\frac{1}{2}\right) \hbar \omega \) (2.62).

\( |\Psi(x, t)|^{2}=\frac{1}{25}\left[9 \psi_{0}^{2}+12 \psi_{0} \psi_{1} e^{i \omega t / 2} e^{-3 i \omega t / 2}+12 \psi_{0} \psi_{1} e^{-i \omega t / 2} e^{3 i \omega t / 2}+16 \psi_{1}^{2}\right] \).

= \( \frac{1}{25}\left[9 \psi_{0}^{2}+16 \psi_{1}^{2}+24 \psi_{0} \psi_{1} \cos (\omega t)\right] \)

(With \( ψ_2 \) in place of \( ψ_2 \) the frequency would be \( \left.\left(E_{2}-E_{0}\right) / \hbar=[(5 / 2) \hbar \omega-(1 / 2) \hbar \omega] / \hbar=2 \omega .\right) \).

(c)

\( \langle x\rangle=\frac{1}{25}\left[9 \int x \psi_{0}^{2} d x+16 \int x \psi_{1}^{2} d x+24 \cos (\omega t) \int x \psi_{0} \psi_{1} d x\right] \).

But \( \int x \psi_{0}^{2} d x=\int x \psi_{1}^{2} d x=0 \), (see or), while

\( \int x \psi_{0} \psi_{1} d x=\sqrt{\frac{m \omega}{\pi \hbar}} \sqrt{\frac{2 m \omega}{\hbar}} \int x e^{-\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar} x^{2}} x e^{-\frac{m \omega}{2 \hbar} x^{2}} d x=\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}}\left(\frac{m \omega}{\hbar}\right) \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x^{2} e^{-\frac{m \omega}{\hbar} x^{2}} d x \).

\( =\sqrt{\frac{2}{\pi}}\left(\frac{m \omega}{\hbar}\right) 2 \sqrt{\pi} 2\left(\frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{m \omega}}\right)^{3}=\sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}} \).

So

\(\left\langle x\right\rangle = \frac{24}{25} \sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}} \cos (\omega t) \);

\( \langle p\rangle=m \frac{d}{d t}\langle x\rangle =-\frac{24}{25} \sqrt{\frac{m \omega \hbar}{2}} \sin (\omega t) \).

Ehrenfest’s theorem says \( d\left\langle p\right\rangle/dt = – \left\langle ∂V/∂x\right\rangle \). Here

\( \frac{d\langle p\rangle}{d t}=-\frac{24}{25} \sqrt{\frac{m \omega \hbar}{2}} \omega \cos (\omega t) \);

\( V=\frac{1}{2} m \omega^{2} x^{2} \Rightarrow \frac{\partial V}{\partial x}=m \omega^{2} x \).

so

\( -\left\langle\frac{\partial V}{\partial x}\right\rangle=-m \omega^{2}\langle x\rangle=-m \omega^{2} \frac{24}{25} \sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2 m \omega}} \cos (\omega t)=-\frac{24}{25} \sqrt{\frac{\hbar m \omega}{2}} \omega \cos (\omega t) \)

so Ehrenfest’s theorem holds.

(d) You could get \( E_{0}=\frac{1}{2} \hbar \omega \), with probability \( \left|c_{0}\right|^{2}= 9/25 \), or \( E_{1}=\frac{3}{2} \hbar \omega \), with probability \( \left|c_{1}\right|^{2}= 16/25 \).
