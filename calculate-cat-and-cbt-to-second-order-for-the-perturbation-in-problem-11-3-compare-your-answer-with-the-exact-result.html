Guest Question.

Calculate \( c_{a}(t) \text { and } c_{b}(t) \) , to second order, for the perturbation in . Compare your answer with the exact result.

Admin
Solution

For H′ independent of t, Eq. 11.21 ⇒ \( c_{b}^{(2)}(t)=c_{b}^{(1)}(t)=-\frac{i}{\hbar} H_{b a}^{\prime} \int_{0}^{t} e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}} d t^{\prime} \Longrightarrow \)

\( c_{b}^{(2)}(t)=-\left.\frac{i}{\hbar} H_{b a}^{\prime} \frac{e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}}}{i \omega_{0}}\right|_{0} ^{t}= -\frac{H_{b a}^{\prime}}{\hbar \omega_{0}}\left(e^{i \omega_{0} t}-1\right) \).

\( \frac{d c_{a}^{(1)}}{d t}=0 \Rightarrow c_{a}^{(1)}(t)=1 \);

\( \frac{d c_{b}^{(1)}}{d t}=-\frac{i}{\hbar} H_{b a}^{\prime} e^{i \omega_{0} t} \Rightarrow c_{b}^{(1)}=-\frac{i}{\hbar} \int_{0}^{t} H_{b a}^{\prime}\left(t^{\prime}\right) e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}} d t^{\prime} \) (11.21).

Meanwhile Eq. 11.22 ⇒ \( c_{a}^{(2)}(t)=1-\frac{1}{\hbar^{2}}\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} \int_{0}^{t} e^{-i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}}\left[\int_{0}^{t^{\prime}} e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime \prime}} d t^{\prime \prime}\right] d t^{\prime}=1-\frac{1}{\hbar^{2}}\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} \frac{1}{i \omega_{0}} \int_{0}^{t}\left(1-e^{-i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}}\right) d t^{\prime} \).

\( =1+\left.\frac{i}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}\left(t^{\prime}+\frac{e^{-i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}}}{i \omega_{0}}\right)\right|_{0} ^{t}=1+\frac{i}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}\left[t+\frac{1}{i \omega_{0}}\left(e^{-i \omega_{0} t}-1\right)\right] \).

\( \frac{d c_{a}^{(2)}}{d t}=-\frac{i}{\hbar} H_{a b}^{\prime} e^{-i \omega_{0} t}\left(-\frac{i}{\hbar}\right) \int_{0}^{t} H_{b a}^{\prime}\left(t^{\prime}\right) e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}} d t^{\prime} \Rightarrow \)

\( c_{a}^{(2)}(t)=1-\frac{1}{\hbar^{2}} \int_{0}^{t} H_{a b}^{\prime}\left(t^{\prime}\right) e^{-i \omega_{0} t^{\prime}}\left[\int_{0}^{t^{\prime}} H_{b a}^{\prime}\left(t^{\prime \prime}\right) e^{i \omega_{0} t^{\prime \prime}} d t^{\prime \prime}\right] d t^{\prime} \) (11.22).

For comparison with the exact answers (), note first that \( c_{b}(t) \) is already first order (because of the \( H_{b a}^{\prime} \) in front), whereas ! differs from \( \omega_{0} \) only in second order, so it suffices to replace \( \omega \rightarrow \omega_{0} \) in the exact formula to get the second-order result:

\( c_{b}(t) \approx \frac{2 H_{b a}^{\prime}}{i \hbar \omega_{0}} e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2} \sin \left(\omega_{0} t / 2\right)=\frac{2 H_{b a}^{\prime}}{i \hbar \omega_{0}} e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2} \frac{1}{2 i}\left(e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2}-e^{-i \omega_{0} t / 2}\right)=-\frac{H_{b a}^{\prime}}{\hbar \omega_{0}}\left(e^{i \omega_{0} t}-1\right) \).

in agreement with the result above. Checking \( c_a \) is more difficult. Note that

\( \omega=\omega_{0} \sqrt{1+\frac{4\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0}^{2} \hbar^{2}}} \approx \omega_{0}\left(1+2 \frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0}^{2} \hbar^{2}}\right)=\omega_{0}+2 \frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}} ; \quad \frac{\omega_{0}}{\omega} \approx 1-2 \frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0}^{2} \hbar^{2}} \).

Taylor expansion:

\( \left\{\begin{array}{l} \cos (x+\epsilon)=\cos x-\epsilon \sin x \Longrightarrow \cos (\omega t / 2)=\cos \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}+\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\right) \approx \cos \left(\omega_{0} t / 2\right)-\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}} \sin \left(\omega_{0} t / 2\right) \\ \sin (x+\epsilon)=\sin x+\epsilon \cos x \Longrightarrow \sin (\omega t / 2)=\sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}+\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\right) \approx \sin \left(\omega_{0} t / 2\right)+\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}} \cos \left(\omega_{0} t / 2\right) \end{array}\right. \).

\( c_{a}(t) \approx e^{-i \omega_{0} t / 2}\left\{\cos \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)-\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}} \sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)+i\left(1-2 \frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0}^{2} \hbar^{2}}\right)\left[\sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)+\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2} t}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}} \cos \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)\right]\right\} \).

\( =e^{-i \omega_{0} t / 2}\left\{\left[\cos \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)+i \sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)\right]-\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left[t\left(\sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)-i \cos \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)\right)+\frac{2 i}{\omega_{0}} \sin \left(\frac{\omega_{0} t}{2}\right)\right]\right\} \).

\( =e^{-i \omega_{0} t / 2}\left\{e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2}-\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left[-i t e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2}+\frac{2 i}{\omega_{0}} \frac{1}{2 i}\left(e^{i \omega_{0} t / 2}-e^{-i \omega_{0} t / 2}\right)\right]\right\} \).

\( =1-\frac{\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left[-i t+\frac{1}{\omega_{0}}\left(1-e^{-i \omega_{0} t}\right)\right]=1+\frac{i}{\omega_{0} \hbar^{2}}\left|H_{a b}^{\prime}\right|^{2}\left[t+\frac{1}{i \omega_{0}}\left(e^{-i \omega_{0} t}-1\right)\right], \text { as above } \).
