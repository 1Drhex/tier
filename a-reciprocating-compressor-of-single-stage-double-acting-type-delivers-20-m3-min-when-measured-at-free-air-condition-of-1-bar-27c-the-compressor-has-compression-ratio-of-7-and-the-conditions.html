Guest Question.

A reciprocating compressor of single stage, double acting type delivers 20 m\(^{3}\)/min when measured at free air condition of 1 bar, 27°C. The compressor has compression ratio of 7 and the conditions at the end of suction are 0.97 bar, 35°C. Compressor runs at 240 rpm with clearance volume of 5% of swept volume. The L/D ratio is 1.2. Determine the volumetric efficiency and dimensions of cylinder and isothermal efficiency taking the index of compression and expansion as 1.25. Also show the cycle on P-V diagram.

Admin
Solution
\(P_{1} = 0.97 bar, T_{1} = 273 + 35 = 308 K, \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} = 7, N = 240 rpm\\\begin{aligned}V_{3}&= 0.05 V_{s}, V_{s} = V_{1} – V_{3}, V_{1} = 1.05 V_{s}\\\text{For free air condition of 1}&\text{ bar, 27°C the mass of air delivered,}\\m&= \frac{PV}{RT} = \frac{1 × 10^{2} × 20}{0.287 × 300}\\m&= 23.23 kg/min\\\text{For compression process } 1& – 2, PV^{1.25} = Constant\\T_{2}&= T_{1} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.25 – 1}{1.25}}\\&= 308 × (7)^{0.2}\\T_{2}&= 454.54 K\\\text{For expansion process, } 3& – 4, PV^{1.25} = Constant\\V_{4}&= V_{3} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{3}}{P_{4}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{ 1}{1.25}}\\V_{4}&= 0.05 V_{s} (7)^{1/1.25}\\V_{4}&= 0.273 V_{s}\\V_{1} – V_{4}& = 1.05 V_{s} – 0.237 V_{s} = 0.813 V_{s}\end{aligned}\\\text{Volume of air corresponding to the suction conditions can be obtained using the volume of free air delivered}\\\begin{aligned}&= \frac{0.97 × 300}{1 × 308} × (V_{1} – V_{4}) = \frac{0.97 × 300}{1 × 308} × 0.813 V_{s}\\&= 0.768 V_{s}\\\quad \text{Volumetric efficiency}&= \frac{ 0.768 V_{s}}{V_{s}} = 0.768 or 76.8\%\\\text{Volumetric effici}&\text{ency }= 76.8\%\\\end{aligned}\\\text{In order to find out the dimension of cylinder the volume of air sucked in one cycle. Volume of air}\\\text{sucked in a cycle }= \frac{20}{2 × 240} = 0.0208 m^{3}/cycle.\\\qquad\qquad \text{Volume per cycle = Swept volume}\\\begin{aligned}0.0208&= \frac{\pi}{4} D^{2} . L = \frac{\pi}{4} D^{2} . (1.2 D)\\⇒ \qquad\qquad \qquad\qquad \qquad D&= \text{0.2805 m or 28.05 cm}\\L&= 33.66 cm\\Bore&= 28.05 cm\\Stroke&= 33.66 cm\\\text{Work required in reciprocating}&\text{ compressor}\\W&= \frac{n}{n – 1} mR (T_{2} – T_{1})\\W&= \left\lgroup\frac{1.25}{1.25 – 1} \right\rgroup × 23.23 × 0.287 (454.54 – 308)\\&= 4884.92 kJ/min\\&= \frac{4884.92}{60 × 0.7457}, \left\{\text{as 1 hp = 0.7457 kW}\right\} \\W&= 109.18 hp\\\text{Work required when compressi}&\text{on is isothermal, }W_{iso} = m RT_{1} \ln \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup\\&= 23.23 × 0.287 × 308 \ln (7)\\&= 3995.81 kJ/min\\&= \frac{3995.81}{60 × 0.7457}\\W_{iso}&= 89.31 hp\\\text{Isothermal efficiency = }\frac{W_{iso}}{W}&= \frac{89.31}{109.81} = 0.8133 or 81.33\%\\\text{Isothermal efficiency} = 81.33\%\end{aligned}\)
