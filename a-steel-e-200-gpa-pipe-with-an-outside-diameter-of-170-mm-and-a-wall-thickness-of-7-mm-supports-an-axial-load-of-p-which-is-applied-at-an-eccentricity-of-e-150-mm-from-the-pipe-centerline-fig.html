Guest Question.

A steel [E = 200 GPa] pipe with an outside diameter of 170 mm and a wall thickness of 7 mm supports an axial load of P, which is applied at an eccentricity of e = 150 mm from the pipe centerline (Fig P16-44). The column is fixed at its base, free at its upper end, and its length is L = 4.0 m. The maximum compression stress in the column must be limited to \(\sigma_{\max }\) = 80 MPa.
(a) Use a trial-and-error approach or an iterative numerical solution to determine the allowable eccentric load P that can be applied.
(b) Determine the lateral deflection at the upper end of the column for the allowable load P.

Admin
Solution

Section properties:

\(\begin{array}{ll}A=\frac{\pi}{4}\left[(170 mm )^{2}-(156 mm )^{2}\right]=3,584.6 mm ^{2} & d=170 mm -2(7 mm )=156 mm \\I=\frac{\pi}{64}\left[(170 mm )^{4}-(156 mm )^{4}\right]=11,926,718 mm ^{4} & r=\sqrt{\frac{11,926,718 mm ^{4}}{3,584.6 mm ^{2}}}=57.682 mm\end{array}\)

(a) Allowable eccentric load P:
The secant formula is:

\(\sigma_{\max }=\frac{P}{A}\left[1+\frac{e c}{r^{2}} \sec \left(\frac{K L}{2 r} \sqrt{\frac{P}{E A}}\right)\right]\)

It is convenient to calculate the eccentricity ratio for use in the secant formula.

\(\frac{e c}{r^{2}}=\frac{(150 mm )(170 mm / 2)}{(57.682 mm )^{2}}=3.8320\)

For this column, the secant formula can be written as:

\(\sigma_{\max }=\frac{P}{3,584.6 mm ^{2}}\left[1+(3.8320) \sec \left(\frac{(2)(4,000 mm )}{2(57.682 mm )} \sqrt{\frac{P}{\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(3,584.6 mm ^{2}\right)}}\right)\right]\)

which can be further simplified to:

\(\sigma_{\max }=\frac{P}{3,584.6 mm ^{2}}\left[1+(3.8320) \sec \left((69.3457) \sqrt{\frac{P}{716,911,444 N }}\right)\right]\)

By trial-and-error, determine the value of P that gives \(\sigma_{\max }\) = 80 MPa. We will begin with a trial value of P that corresponds to 25% of \(\sigma_{\max }\), i.e., P = 0.25(80 MPa)(3,584.557 \(mm ^{2}\) ) = 71,691 N.

\(\begin{array}{c|c}\hline \text { Trial value } & \text { Corresponding } \\\text { of } P & \sigma _{\max } \\( N ) & ( MPa ) \\\hline 71,691 & 119.656 \\\hline 60,000 & 96.375 \\\hline 50,000 & 77.814 \\\hline 55,000 & 86.947 \\\hline 52,500 & 82.344 \\\hline 51,250 & 80.070 \\\hline 51,200 & 79.980 \\\hline 51,230 & 80.034 \\\hline 5 1 , 2 1 1 . 2 6 5 & 8 0 . 0 0 0 \\\hline\end{array}\)

Thus, the allowable eccentric load is:

\(P=51,211.265 N =51.2 kN\)

 

(b) Lateral deflection at the upper end of the column:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{\max } &=e\left[\sec \left(\frac{K L}{2} \sqrt{\frac{P}{E I}}\right)-1\right] \\&=(150 mm )\left[\sec \left(\frac{(2)(4,000 mm )}{2} \sqrt{\frac{51,211.265 N }{\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(11,926,718 mm ^{4}\right)}}\right)-1\right] \\&=30.049 mm =30.0 mm\end{aligned}\)
