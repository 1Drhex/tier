Guest Question.

Calculate numerically the centroid of the shaded area shown in Fig. 5.5(a).

Admin
Solution

(i) The given figure is symmetric about x-axis

\( \therefore \overline{y} = 0 \).

(ii) Consider semicircle : part 1

\( A_1 = \frac{π × 15^2 }{2} = 353.43 cm^2 \).

\( x_1 = \frac{-4× 15}{3 π} = -6.37 cm \).

(iii) Consider two equal triangles : part 2

\( 2(A_2) = 2 \Bigl(\frac{1}{2} ×15 ×100\Bigr) = 2(750) cm^2 \).

\( x_2 =\frac{100}{3} = 33.33 cm \).

(iv) Consider a sector of circle : part 3

\( A_3 = 103.08^2 × 14.04 × \frac{π}{180} = 2603.71 cm^2 \).

\( x_3 =\frac{2 × 103.08 \sin 14.04}{3× 14.04×\frac{π}{180}} = 68.03 cm \).

(v) Consider triangle : part 4

\( -A_4 = – \Bigl(\frac{1}{2}×15 ×100\Bigr) = -2500 cm^2 \).

\( x_4 = \frac{2}{3}×100= 66.67 cm \).

(vi) Coordinates of the centroid

\( \overline{x}= \frac{353.43 × (-6.37) + 2(750 × 33.33) + 2603.71 × 68.03 + (-2500) × (66.67)}{353.43 + 2(750) + 2603.71 – 2500} = 29.74 cm \).

\( \therefore \) Coordinates of centroid w.r.t. origin O are G (29.74, 0) cm. Ans.
