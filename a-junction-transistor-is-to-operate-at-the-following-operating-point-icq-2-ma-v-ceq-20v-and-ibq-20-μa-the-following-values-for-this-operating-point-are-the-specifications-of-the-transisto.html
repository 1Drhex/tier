Guest Question.

A junction transistor is to operate at the following operating point \(I_{CQ} = 2 mA, V_{CEQ} = 20v and I_{BQ} = 20 \mu A\). The following values for this operating point are the specifications of the transistor. \(\beta_O = 100; f_T = 50 MHz; C_{0b} = 3 pf; h_{ie} = 1.4 K\Omega ; h_{re} = 2.5 \times 10^{-4}; h_{0e} = 25 \mu \) mhos.

Determine the hybrid-π model parameters of the transistor. Assume that the operating temperature is 300 K.

Admin
Solution

1 \(g_m=\frac{\left|I_{CQ}\right| }{V_T}\) mA/volt where \(V_T=\frac{\overline{K}T }{e}=\frac{T}{11600}\) At T=300K \(V_{T}=\frac{300}{11600}\cong 26\) mv.

∴ \(g_m=\frac{2 mA}{26 mv}=\frac{2\times 10^{-3}}{26\times 10^{-3}} =77.3\times 10^{-3} \) mhos when \(I_{CQ}=2\) mA

2 \(r_{b^\prime e}=\frac{\beta _0}{g_m} =\frac{100}{77.3\times 10^{-3}}=1293\Omega \cong 1300 \) ohms

3 \(r_{bb^\prime }= h_{ie} – r_{b^\prime e} = 1400 – 1300 = 100 \) ohms

4 \(r_{b^\prime c}=\frac{r_{b^\prime e}}{h_{re}} =\frac{1300}{2.5\times 10^{-4}}=5.2\times 10^6 \Omega \)

5 \(g_{ce}=h_{oe}-g_m.h_{re}=25\times 10^{-6}-\left[\frac{\left|I_{CQ}\right| }{V_T} .h_{re}\right] \)

∴ \(g_{ce}=25\times 10^{-6}-\left[\frac{2\times 10^{-3}\times 2.5\times 10^{-4} }{26\times 10^{-3}} \right]=5.8\times 10^{-6} \) mhos

6 \( C_{b^\prime c} = C_{ob} = 3 \) pf

7 \( C_{b^\prime c} or C_e = \frac{g_m}{2\pi f_T}\cong 250 \) pf
