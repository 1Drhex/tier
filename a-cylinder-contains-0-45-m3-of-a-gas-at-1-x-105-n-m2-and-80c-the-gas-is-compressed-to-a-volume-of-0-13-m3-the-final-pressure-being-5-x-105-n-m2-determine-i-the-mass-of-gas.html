Guest Question.

A cylinder contains \(0.45 m^{3}\) of a gas at \(1 × 105 \frac{N}{m^{2}}\) and 80°C. The gas is compressed to a volume of\( 0.13 m^{3}\), the final pressure being \(5 × 10^{5} \frac{N}{m^{2}}\). Determine :
(i) The mass of gas ;
(ii) The value of index ‘n’ for compression ;
(iii) The increase in internal energy of the gas ;
(iv) The heat received or rejected by the gas during compression.
Take γ = 1.4, R = 294.2 J/kg°C.

Admin
Solution

Initial volume of gas, \( V_{1} = 0.45 m^{3}\)

 

Initial pressure of gas, \( P_{1} = 1 × 10^{5} \frac{N}{m^{2}}\)

 

Initial temperature, \( T_{1} = 80 + 273 = 353 \) K

 

Final volume after compression, \( V_{2} = 0.13 m^{3}\)

 

The final pressure, \( P_{2} = 5 × 10^{5} \frac{N}{m^{2}}\) .

 

(i) To find mass ‘m’ using the relation

 

\( m=\frac{P_{1}V_{1}}{RT_{1}}=\frac{1\times 10^{5}\times 0.45}{294.2\times 353}=0.433\) kg.

 

(ii) To find index ‘n’ using the relation

 

\( p_{1}V_{1}^{n}=p_{2}V_{2}^{n}\)

 

or \( \left ( \frac{V_{1}}{V_{2}} \right )^{n}=\frac{p_{2}}{p_{1}}\)

 

\( \left ( \frac{0.45}{0.13} \right )^{n}=\left ( \frac{5\times 10^{5}}{1\times 10^{5}} \right )=5\)

 

or \( \left ( 0.365 \right )^{n}=5\)

 

Taking log on both sides, we get

 

\( n\log_{e}3.46 =\log_{e} 5\)

 

\( n=\log_{e}5/\log_{e}3.46=1.296\) .

 

(iii) In a polytropic process,

 

\( \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}=\left ( \frac{V_{1}}{V_{2}} \right )^{n-1}=\left ( \frac{0.45}{0.13} \right )^{1.296-1}=1.444\)

 

\(\therefore\) \( T_{2}=353 \times 1.444=509.7 \) K

 

Now, increase in internal energy,

 

\( ΔU = mc_{v}(T_{2} – T_{1})\)

 

\(=0.433\times \frac{R}{\left ( \gamma -1 \right )}\left ( T_{2}-T_{1} \right )\) \( \left [ \because c_{v}=\frac{R}{\left ( \gamma -1 \right )} \right ]\)

 

\( =0.433\times \frac{294.2}{\left ( 1.4-1 \right )1000}\left ( 509.7-353 \right )\)

 

= 49.9 kJ.

 

(iv) Q = ΔU + W

 

Now, \( W=\frac{p_{1}V_{1}-p_{2}V_{2}}{n-1}=\frac{mR\left ( T_{1}-T_{2} \right )}{n-1}\)

 

\( =\frac{0.433 294.2(353 – 509.7)}{1.296 – 1}\)

 

\( = – 67438 \) N-m or – 67438 J = \(– 67.44 \) kJ

 

\( \therefore\) \( Q = 49.9 + (– 67.44) = – 17.54\) kJ

 

\( \therefore \) Heat rejected = 17.54 kJ.
