Guest Question.

Consider the observables \( A=x^{2} \text { and } B=L_{z} \).

(a) Construct the uncertainty principle for \( \sigma_{A} \sigma_{B} \).

(b) Evaluate \( σ_B \) in the hydrogen state \( \psi_{n \ell m} \).

(c) What can you conclude about 〈xy〉 in this state?

Admin
Solution

(a) Using Eqs. 3.64 and 4.122:

\( [\hat{A}+\hat{B}, \hat{C}]=[\hat{A}, \hat{C}]+[\hat{B}, \hat{C}] \) (3.64).

\( \left[L_{z}, x\right]=i \hbar y, \quad\left[L_{z}, y\right]=-i \hbar x, \quad\left[L_{z}, z\right]=0 \),

\( \left[L_{z}, p_{x}\right]=i \hbar p_{y}, \quad\left[L_{z}, p_{y}\right]=-i \hbar p_{x}, \quad\left[L_{z}, p_{z}\right]=0 \). (4.122).

\( [A, B]=\left[x^{2}, L_{z}\right]=x\left[x, L_{z}\right]+\left[x, L_{z}\right] x=x(-i \hbar y)+(-i \hbar y) x=-2 i \hbar x y \).

Equation 3.62 \( \Rightarrow \sigma_{A}^{2} \sigma_{B}^{2} \geq\left[\frac{1}{2 i}(-2 i \hbar)\langle x y\rangle\right]^{2}=\hbar^{2}\langle x y\rangle^{2} \Rightarrow \sigma_{A} \sigma_{B} \geq \hbar|\langle x y\rangle| \).

\( \sigma_{A}^{2} \sigma_{B}^{2} \geq\left(\frac{1}{2 i}\langle[\hat{A}, \hat{B}])\right)^{2} \) (3.62).

(b) Equation 4.118 \( \Rightarrow\langle B\rangle=\left\langle L_{z}\right\rangle=m \hbar ; \quad\left\langle B^{2}\right\rangle=\left\langle L_{z}^{2}\right\rangle=m^{2} \hbar^{2} ; \quad \text { so } \quad \sigma_{B}=m^{2} \hbar^{2}-m^{2} \hbar^{2}=0 \).

\( L^{2} f_{\ell}^{m}=\hbar^{2} \ell(\ell+1) f_{\ell}^{m} ; \quad L_{z} f_{\ell}^{m}=\hbar m f_{\ell}^{m} \) (4.118).

(c) Since the left side of the uncertainty principle is zero, the right side must also be:\( \langle x y\rangle=0 \) or eigenstates of \( L_z \).
