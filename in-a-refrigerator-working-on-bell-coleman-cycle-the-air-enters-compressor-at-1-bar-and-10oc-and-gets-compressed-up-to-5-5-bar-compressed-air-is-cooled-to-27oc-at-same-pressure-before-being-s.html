Guest Question.

In a refrigerator working on Bell Coleman cycle the air enters compressor at 1 bar and –10ºC and gets compressed up to 5.5 bar. Compressed air is cooled to 27ºC at same pressure before being sent to expander for expansion up to 1 bar and then passes through refrigerated space. Determine refrigeration capacity, hp required to run compressor and COP of system if air flow rate is 0.8 kg/s.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Given:}\\T_{1} = –10ºC\text{ or 263 K, }P_{1} = 1 bar, T_{3} = 27ºC\text{ or 300 K, }P_{2} = 5.5 bar, m = 0.8 kg/s.\\\text{For process 1–2,}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}}&= \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}\\T_{2}&= 263 × (5.5)^{\frac{(1.4 – 1)}{1.4}}\\T_{2}&= 428.04 K\end{aligned}\\\text{For process 3–4,}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\qquad\left\lgroup\frac{P_{3}}{P_{4}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}}&= \frac{T_{3}}{T_{4}}\\T_{4}&= T_{3} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{4}}{P_{3}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}} = T_{3} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{1}}{P_{2}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}} \\T_{4}&= 184.33 K\\\text{Refrigeration capacity}&= mC_{p} (T_{1} – T_{4})\\&= 0.8 × 1.005 (263 – 184.33)\\&=\text{63.25 kJ/s or 18.07 ton}\\\text{Refrigeration capacity}&=\textbf{ 18.07 ton}\\\text{Work required to run the com}&\text{pressor }= \frac{m × n}{n – 1}(p_{2}v_{2} – p_{1}v_{1})\\&= \frac{m × n}{n – 1} R(T_{2} – T_{1})\\&= \frac{0.8 × 1.4}{(0.4)} × 0.287 (428.04 – 263)\\&= 132.63 kJ/s\\\text{or}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad&\\\text{HP required to run compressor}&= \frac{132.63}{0.7457} = 177.86 hp\\\text{HP required to run compressor}&= \textbf{177.86 hp}\\\text{Net work input}&= mC_{p} \left\{(T_{2} – T_{3}) – (T_{1} – T_{4})\right\} \\&= 0.8 × 1.005 \left\{(428.04 – 300) – (263 – 184.33)\right\}\\W&= 39.69 kJ/s\\COP& = \frac{\text{Refrigeration capacity}}{\text{Work}} = \frac{63.25}{39.69} = 1.59\\COP& = \textbf{1.59}\end{aligned}\)
