Guest Question.

A needle 35 mm long rests on a water surface at 20°C. What force over and above the needle’s weight is required to lift the needle from contact with the water surface?

Admin
Solution

From Table 1C,

σ=0.0728 N/m

σ=F/L

0.0728=F/(2×0.035)

F=0.00510 N
