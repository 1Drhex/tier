Guest Question.

A Proell governor has all four arms of length 300 mm. The upper arms are pivoted on the axis of rotation and the lower arms are attached to a sleeve at a distance of 40 mm from the axis. The mass of each ball is 5 kg and are attached to the extension of the lower arms, which are 100 mm long. The mass on the sleeve is 50 kg. The minimum and maximum radii of governor are 160 mm and 210 mm. Assuming that the extensions of lower arms are parallel to the governor axis at the minimum radius, find the corresponding equilibrium speeds.

Admin
Solution

Refer to Fig.9.38 for minimum radius of governor.

\( \text { Given: } l=300 mm , c=40 mm , m=5 kg , M=50 kg , B C=100 mm , r_{1}=160 mm \).

\( \sin \alpha=\frac{B G}{B D}=\frac{r_{1}}{l}=\frac{160}{300}=0.533 \).

\( \alpha=32.23^{\circ} \).

\( A B=B G-A G=r_{1}-c=160-40=120 mm \).

\( \sin \beta=\frac{A B}{B E}=\frac{120}{300}=0.4 \).

\( \beta=23.58^{\circ} \).

\( \tan \alpha=\tan 32.23^{\circ}=0.6305 \).

\( \tan \beta=\tan 23.58^{\circ}=0.4365 \).

\( k=\frac{\tan \beta}{\tan \alpha}=\frac{0.4365}{0.6305}=0.6923 \).

\( h=D G=l \cos \alpha=300 \cos 32.23^{\circ}=253.8 mm \).

\( F E=r_{1}-c=A B=120 mm \).

\( B F=l \cos \beta=300 \cos 23.58^{\circ}=275 mm \).

\( C F=B C+B F=100+275=375 mm \).

\( \omega_{1}^{2}=\left(\frac{B F}{C F}\right)\left[1+\left(\frac{M}{2 m}\right)(1+k)\right]\left(\frac{g}{h}\right) \).

\( =\left(\frac{275}{375}\right)\left[1+\frac{50}{2 \times 5}(1+0.6923)\right]\left(\frac{9.81}{0.2538}\right) \).

\( =268.188 \).

\( \omega_{1}=16.376 rad / s \).

\( N_{1}=\frac{60 \times 16.376}{2 \pi}=156.4 rpm \).

The configuration of governor at maximum radius is shown in Fig.9.39. CBE is a one rigid link.

\( \text { Therefore } C_{1} E_{1} \text { and } \angle C_{1} E_{1} B_{1} \text { does not change. } \)

\( r_{2}=210 mm \).

From Fig.9.38,

\( C E=\sqrt{C F^{2}+F E^{2}}=\sqrt{375^{2}+120^{2}}=393.7 mm \).

\( \tan \gamma=\frac{F E}{C F}=\frac{120}{375}=0.32 \).

\( \gamma=17.7^{\circ} \).

From Fig.9.39,

\( \sin \alpha_{1}=\frac{B_{1} G_{1}}{B_{1} D_{1}}=\frac{r_{2}}{l}=\frac{210}{300}=0.7 \).

\( \alpha_{1}=44.43^{\circ} \).

\( A_{1} B_{1}=r_{2}-c=210-40=170 mm \).

\( \sin \beta_{1}=\frac{A_{1} B_{1}}{B_{1} E_{1}}=\frac{170}{300}=0.567 \).

\( \beta_{1}=34.52^{\circ} \).

\( C_{1} E_{1}=C E=393.7 mm \).

\( \beta_{1}-\gamma_{1}=\beta-\gamma \).

\( \gamma_{1}=\beta_{1}-\beta+\gamma=34.52-23.58+17.7=28.64^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Radius of rotation, } r_{2}^{\prime}=L_{1} E_{1}+E_{1} H_{1}=C_{1} E_{1} \sin \gamma_{1}+c \).

\( =393.7 \sin 28.64^{\circ}+40=228.7 mm \).

\( C_{1} L_{1}=C_{1} E_{1} \cos \gamma_{1}=393.7 \cos 28.64^{\circ}=345.53 mm \).

\( B_{1} F_{1}=l \cos \beta_{1}=300 \cos 34.52^{\circ}=247.18 mm \).

\( I_{1} F_{1}=B_{1} F_{1} \tan \alpha_{1}=247.18 \tan 44.43^{\circ}=242.9 mm \).

\( F_{1} E_{1}=l \sin \beta_{1}=300 \sin 34.52^{\circ}=170 mm \).

\( I_{1} E_{1}=I_{1} F_{1}+F_{1} E_{1}=242.9+170=412.9 mm \).

\( L_{1} E_{1}=C_{1} E_{1} \sin \gamma_{1}=393.7 \sin 28.64^{\circ}=188.7 mm \).

\( I_{1} L_{1}=I_{1} E_{1}-L_{1} E_{1}=412.9-188.7=224.2 mm \).

\( F_{c}=m \omega_{2}^{2} r_{2}^{\prime}=5 \times\left(\frac{2 \pi N_{2}}{60}\right)^{2} \times 0.2287=0.01254 N^{2} N \).

\( \text { Taking moments about } I_{1} \text {, } \)

\( F_{c} \times C_{1} L_{1}=m g \times I_{1} L_{1}+\frac{M g}{2} \times I_{1} E_{1} \).

\( 0.01254 N ^{2} \times 345.53=5 \times 9.81 \times 224.2+\frac{50 \times 9.81}{2} \times 412.9 \).

\( N ^{2}=25908.6 \).

\( N =161 rpm \).
