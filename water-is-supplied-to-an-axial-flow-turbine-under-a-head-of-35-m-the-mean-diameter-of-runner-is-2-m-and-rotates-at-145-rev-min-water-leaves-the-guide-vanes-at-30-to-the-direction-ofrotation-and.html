Guest Question.

Water is supplied to an axial flow turbine under a head of 35 m. The mean diameter of runner is 2 m and rotates at 145 rev/min. Water leaves the guide vanes at 30° to the direction ofrotation and blade angle at exit is 28°, losses is guide vanes is 7% of the head and the flow of exit is axial. Determine blade inlet angle and Euler’s head.

Admin
Solution

Given

 

\( H = 35 m, D = 2 m, N = 145 rev/min; \alpha _{l} = 30°, \beta_{ 2} = 28'\ h_{g} = 7\)% of H,

 

– Head available \(H = 0.93 \times 35 = 32.6\) m

– inlet velocity \(V_{I} = \sqrt{2gH} = \sqrt{2 \times 9.8 \times 32.6} = 25.3\) m/s

\(u=\frac{\pi DN}{60}=\frac{ \pi \times 2 \times 145}{ 60} = 15.2\) m/s
– From inlettriangle of velocities

 

\(V^{2}_{r1}=V^{2}_{t}+u^{2}-2uv_{1}\cos\alpha_{1}\) in fig 7.13

 

\(=25.3^{2}+15.2^{2}-(2\times 15.2 \times 25.3 \cos 30)\)

 

\(V_{r1}=14.3\) m/s

 

Again \(u^{2}=V^{2}_{r1}+V^{2}_{1}-(2 \times V_{r1} \times V_{1} \cos(\pi -\beta_{1}))\)

 

\(15.2^{2}=14.3^{2}+25.3^{2}-(2 \times 14.3 \times 25.3 \cos(\pi -\beta _{1} ) )\)

 

\(231=204.5+640-723.5\cos(\pi -\beta_{1})\)

 

\(613.5=723.5\cos(\pi -\beta_{1})\)

 

\(0.8479=\cos(\pi -\beta_{1})\)

 

\(\therefore \beta_{1}=148º \)

 

\(V_{1w}=V_{1} \cos\alpha _{1}=25.3 \times 0.866=21.9\) m/s

 

\(E=\frac{u V_{1w}}{g}=\frac{15.2 \times 21.9}{9.8}=33.9\) m
