Guest Question.

A water-filled reactor of 1 \(m ^{3}\) is at 20 MPa, 360°C and located inside an insulated containment room of 100 \(m ^{3}\) that contains air at 100 kPa and 25°C. Due to a failure the reactor ruptures and the water fills the containment room. Find the final pressure.

Admin
Solution

CV Total container.

\(m_{ v }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ m _{ a }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)={ }_{1} Q _{2}-{ }_{1} W _{2}=0\)

Initial water: \(v _{1}=0.0018226, u _{1}=1702.8 kJ / kg , m _{ v }= V / v =548.67 kg\)

Initial air: \(m _{ a }=\frac{ PV }{ RT }=\frac{100 \times 99}{0.287 \times 298.2}=115.7 kg\)

Substitute into energy equation

\(\begin{aligned}&548.67\left( u _{2}-1702.8\right)+115.7 \times 0.717\left( T _{2}-25\right)=0 \\& u _{2}+0.1511 T _{2}=1706.6 kJ / kg \quad \& \quad v _{2}= V _{2} / m _{ v }=0.18226 m ^{3} / kg\end{aligned}\)

Trial and error 2-phase \(\left( T _{\text {guess }}, v _{2} \Rightarrow x _{2} \Rightarrow u _{2} \Rightarrow LHS \right)\)

\(\begin{array}{lr}T =150^{\circ} C LHS =1546 & T =160^{\circ} C \quad LHS =1820.2 \\T =155^{\circ} C LHS =1678.1 \Rightarrow T & =156^{\circ} C LHS =1705.7 OK\end{array}\)

 

\(x _{2}=0.5372, P _{ sat }=557.5 kPa\)

 

\(\begin{gathered}P _{ a 2}= P _{ a 1} V _{1} T _{2} / V _{2} T _{1}=100 \times 99 \times 429.15 /(100 \times 298.15)=142.5 kPa \\\Rightarrow \quad P _{2}= P _{ a 2}+ P _{ sat }= 7 0 0 kPa.\end{gathered}\)
