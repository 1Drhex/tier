Guest Question.

Let

\( T =\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1-i \\ 1+i & 0 \end{array}\right) \) .

(a) Verify that T is hermitian.

(b) Find its eigenvalues (note that they are real).

(c) Find and normalize the eigenvectors (note that they are orthogonal).

(d) Construct the unitary diagonalizing matrix S, and check explicitly that it diagonalizes T.

(e) Check that det (T) and Tr(T) are the same for T as they are for its diagonalized form.

Admin
Solution

(a)

\( T ^{\dagger}=\tilde{ T }^{*}=\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1-i \\ 1+i & 0 \end{array}\right)= T \).

(b)

\( \left|\begin{array}{cc} (1-\lambda) & (1-i) \\ (1+i) & (0-\lambda) \end{array}\right|=-(1-\lambda) \lambda-1-1=0 ; \lambda^{2}-\lambda-2=0 ; \lambda=\frac{1 \pm \sqrt{1+8}}{2}=\frac{1 \pm 3}{2} . \quad \lambda_{1}=2, \lambda_{2}=-1 \).

(c)

\( \left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & (1-i) \\ (1+i) & 0 \end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{l}\alpha \\ \beta \end{array}\right)=2\left(\begin{array}{l} \alpha \\ \beta \end{array}\right) \Longrightarrow \alpha+(1-i) \beta=2 \alpha \Longrightarrow \alpha=(1-i) \beta \).

\( |\alpha|^{2}+|\beta|^{2}=1 \Longrightarrow 2|\beta|^{2}+|\beta|^{2}=1 \Longrightarrow \beta=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot \quad a ^{(1)}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\left(\begin{array}{c} 1-i \\ 1 \end{array}\right) \).

\( \left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & (1-i) \\ (1+i) & 0 \end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{l}\alpha \\ \beta \end{array}\right)=-\left(\begin{array}{c} \alpha \\ \beta \end{array}\right) \Longrightarrow \alpha+(1-i) \beta=-\alpha ; \quad \alpha=-\frac{1}{2}(1-i) \beta \).

\( \frac{1}{4} 2|\beta|^{2}+|\beta|^{2}=1 \Longrightarrow \frac{3}{2}|\beta|^{2}=1 ; \beta=\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} . \quad a ^{(2)}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{6}}\left(\begin{array}{c} i-1 \\ 2 \end{array}\right) \).

\( a ^{(1) \dagger} a ^{(2)}=\frac{1}{3 \sqrt{2}}((1+i) 1)\left(\begin{array}{c} (i-1) \\ 2 \end{array}\right)=\frac{1}{3 \sqrt{2}}(i-1-1-i+2)=0 \).

(d)

\( \text { Eq. } A .81 \Longrightarrow\left( S ^{-1}\right)_{11}=a_{1}^{(1)}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}(1-i) ; \quad\left( S ^{-1}\right)_{12}=a_{1}^{(2)}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{6}}(i-1) \).

\( \left( S ^{-1}\right)_{i j}=\left( a ^{(j)}\right)_{i} \) (A.81).

\( \left( S ^{-1}\right)_{21}=a_{2}^{(1)}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} ; \quad\left( S ^{-1}\right)_{22}=a_{2}^{(2)}=\frac{2}{\sqrt{6}} . \)

\( S ^{-1}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\left(\begin{array}{cc} (1-i) & (i-1) / \sqrt{2} \\ 1 & \sqrt{2} \end{array}\right) ; \quad S =\left( S ^{-1}\right)^{\dagger}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\left(\begin{array}{cc} (1+i) & 1 \\ (-i-1) / \sqrt{2} & \sqrt{2} \end{array}\right) \).

\( STS ^{-1}=\frac{1}{3}\left(\begin{array}{cc} (1+i) & 1 \\ -(1+i) / \sqrt{2} & \sqrt{2}\end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & (1-i) \\ (1+i) & 0 \end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{cc} (1-i) & (i-1) / \sqrt{2} \\ 1 & \sqrt{2} \end{array}\right) \).

\( =\frac{1}{3}\left(\begin{array}{cc} (1+i) & 1 \\ -(1+i) / \sqrt{2} & \sqrt{2} \end{array}\right)\left(\begin{array}{cc} 2(1-i) & (1-i) / \sqrt{2} \\ 2 & -\sqrt{2} \end{array}\right)=\frac{1}{3}\left(\begin{array}{cc} 6 & 0 \\ 0 & -3 \end{array}\right)=\left(\begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array}\right) \).

(e)

\( \operatorname{Tr}( T )=1 ; \quad \operatorname{det}( T )=0-(1+i)(1-i)=-2 . \quad \operatorname{Tr}\left( STS ^{-1}\right)=2-1=1 . \quad d \quad \operatorname{det}\left( STS ^{-1}\right)=-2 \).
