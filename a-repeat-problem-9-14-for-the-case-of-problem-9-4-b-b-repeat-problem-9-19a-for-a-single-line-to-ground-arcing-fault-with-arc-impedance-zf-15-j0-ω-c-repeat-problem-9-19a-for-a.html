Solution

(a) Bolted single-line-to-ground fault at bus 1.

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{I}_{0} &=\bar{I}_{1}=\bar{I}_{2}=\frac{\bar{V}_{F}}{\bar{Z}_{0}+\bar{Z}_{1}+\bar{Z}_{2}} \\&=\frac{1.0 \angle 0^{\circ}}{j(.08158+.1223+.1247)}=\frac{1.0 \angle 0^{\circ}}{j 0.3286} \\&=-j 3.043 \text { per unit }\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{I}_{A}^{\prime \prime} \\\bar{I}_{B}^{\prime \prime} \\\bar{I}_{C}^{\prime \prime}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a^{2} & a \\1 & a & a^{2}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}-j 3.043 \\-j 3.043 \\-j 3.043\end{array}\right]=&\left[\begin{array}{c}-j 9.130 \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] \text { per unit } \\&=\left[\begin{array}{c}-j 10.54 \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] kA\end{aligned}\)

 

Using Eq. (9.1):

\(\begin{aligned}\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{V}_{0} \\\bar{V}_{1} \\\bar{V}_{2}\end{array}\right] &=\left[\begin{array}{c}0 \\1.0 \angle 0^{\circ} \\0\end{array}\right]-\left[\begin{array}{ccc}j .08158 & 0 & 0 \\0 & j .1223 & 0 \\0 & 0 & j .1247\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}-j 3.043 \\-j 3.043 \\-j 3.043\end{array}\right] \\&=\left[\begin{array}{c}-0.2483 \\0.6278 \\-0.3795\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{V}_{A g} \\\bar{V}_{B g} \\\bar{V}_{C g}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a^{2} & a \\1 & a & a^{2}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}-.2483 \\.6278 \\-.3795\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{c}0 \\0.9485 \angle 246.9^{\circ} \\0.9485 \angle 113.1^{\circ}\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

\(\begin{aligned}Z_{\text {base H }} &=\frac{(500)^{2}}{1000}=250 \Omega \\Z_{F} &=\frac{15}{250}=0.06 \angle 0^{\circ} \text { per unit }\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{I}_{0} &=\bar{I}_{1}=\bar{I}_{2}=\frac{\bar{V}_{F}}{\bar{Z}_{0}+\bar{Z}_{1}+\bar{Z}_{2}+3 Z_{F}} \\&=\frac{1.0 \angle 0^{\circ}}{0.18+j 0.3286}=\frac{1.0 \angle 0^{\circ}}{0.3747 \angle 61.29^{\circ}} \\&=2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \text { per unit }\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{I}_{A}^{\prime \prime} \\\bar{I}_{B}^{\prime \prime} \\\bar{I}_{C}^{\prime \prime}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a^{2} & a \\1 & a & a^{2}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{c}8.007 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] \text { per unit } \times 1.155\)

 

\(=\left[\begin{array}{c}9.246 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] kA\)

 

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{V}_{0} \\\bar{V}_{1} \\\bar{V}_{2}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{c}0 \\1.0 \angle 0^{\circ} \\0\end{array}\right]-\left[\begin{array}{ccc}j .08158 & 0 & 0 \\0 & j .1223 & 0 \\0 & 0 & j .1247\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\2.669 \angle-61.29^{\circ}\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(=\left[\begin{array}{c}0.2177 \angle 208.71^{\circ} \\0.7307 \angle-12.9^{\circ} \\0.3328 \angle 208.71^{\circ}\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

 

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}\bar{V}_{A g} \\\bar{V}_{B g} \\\bar{V}_{C g}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a^{2} & a \\1 & a & a^{2}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}.2177 \angle 208.71^{\circ} \\.7307 \angle-12.4^{\circ} \\.3328 \angle 208.71^{\circ}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{l}0.4804 \angle-61.29^{\circ} \\0.9094 \angle 244.0^{\circ} \\1.009 \angle 113.6^{\circ}\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)
