Guest Question.

A Rankine cycle feeds 5 kg/s ammonia at 2 MPa, 140°C to the turbine, which has an extraction point at 800 kPa. The condenser is at -20°C and a closed feed water heater has an exit state (3) at the temperature of the condensing extraction flow and it has a drip pump. The source for the boiler is at constant 180°C. Find the extraction flow rate and state 4 into the boiler .

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}& P _{1}=190.2 kPa \\& h _{1}=89.05 kJ / kg \\& v _{1}=0.001504 m ^{3} / kg \\& s _{5}=5.5022 kJ / kg K \\& h _{5}=1738.2 kJ / kg \\& T _{6 a }= T _{\text {sat } 800 kPa }=17.85^{\circ} C \\&\Rightarrow h _{6 a }=264.18 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Turbine: Reversible, adiabatic so constant s from inlet to extraction point

\(s _{6}= s _{ IN }=5.5022 kJ / kg K \Rightarrow T _{6}=63.4^{\circ} C , h _{6}=1580.89 kJ / kg\)

C.V. P1:

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P 1} &= v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=0.001504(2000-190.2)=2.722 kJ / kg \\& \Rightarrow h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ P 1}=91.772 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

C.V. P2:

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P 2} &= v _{6 a }\left( P _{4}- P _{6}\right)=0.0016108(2000-800)=1.933 kJ / kg \\& \Rightarrow h _{6 b }= h _{6 a }+ w _{ P 2}=266.11 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Total FWH and pump (notice \(h _{3}= h _{6 a }\) as we do not have table for this state)

The extraction fraction is: \(y =\dot{ m }_{6} / \dot{ m }_{4}\)

Energy: \((1-y) h_{2}+y h_{6}=(1-y) h_{3}+y h_{6 a}\)

\(\begin{gathered}y =\frac{ h _{3}- h _{2}}{ h _{3}- h _{2}+ h _{6}- h _{6 a }}=\frac{264.18-91.772}{264.18-91.772+1580.89-264.18}=0.1158 \\\dot{ m }_{6}= y \dot{ m }_{4}=0.1158 \times 5= 0 . 5 7 8 9 kg / s\end{gathered}\)

C.V. The junction after FWH and pump 2.

\(h _{4}=(1- y ) h _{3}+ y h _{6 b }=(1-0.1158) \times 264.18+0.1158 \times 266.11=264.4 kJ / kg\)

Interpolate in Table B.2.1 : \(T _{4}=17.92^{\circ} C\)
