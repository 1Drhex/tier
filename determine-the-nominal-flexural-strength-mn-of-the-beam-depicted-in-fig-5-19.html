Guest Question.

Determine the nominal flexural strength \(M_{n}\) of the beam depicted in Fig. 5.19. Assume\(f^{'}_{c} \) = 4,000 psi and Grade 60 reinforcement (\(f_{y}\) = 60,000 psi).

Admin
Solution

The flowchart shown in Fig. 5.18 is utilized to determine \(M_{n}\).

Step 1: Determine \(M_{s,min}\) . The minimum amount of reinforcement is determined by ACI 10.5.1. Because the compressive strength of the concrete is less than 4,400 psi, the minimum amount is determined by the lower limit given in ACI 10.5.1:

\(M_{s,min}=\frac{200b_{w}d }{f_{y} }=\frac{200\times 12\times 20.6}{60,000}=0.82in^{2}\) \(M_{s,min}\lt A_{s}=8\times 0.60= 4.80in^{2}\)

Step 2: Determine\(\beta _{1} \). According to ACI 10.2.73, \(\beta _{1} \) = 0.85 for \(f^{'}_{c} \)= 4,000 psi (see Section 5.2).
Step 3: Determine the depth of the equivalent stress block a. Assuming that the compression steel yields, the depth of the equivalent stress block a is determined by Eq. (5.19c):

\(\frac{ε^{'}_{c}}{7.7-2.3}=\frac{0.0030}{7.7}or ε^{'}_{c}=0.0021\gt ε_{y} =0.00207\)

Step 3: Determine \(ε_{t}\). The strain εt is determined by similar triangles (see Fig. 5.19):

\(a=\frac{(A_{s}-A^{'}_{s} )f_{y} }{0.85f^{'}_{c}b } =\frac{(4.80-0.40)\times 60}{0.85\times 4\times 12} =6.5in \)\

Step 4: Determine the neutral axis depth c.

\(c=\frac{a}{\beta _{1} }=\frac{6.5}{0.85}= 7.7 in\)

Step 5: Determine \(d^{'}_{y} \). Because Grade 60 reinforcement is specified in this example, use Eq. (5.18) to determine \(d^{'}_{y} \) :

\(d^{'}_{y} \)=0.31c = 0.31 × 7.7 = 2.4 in

Thus, the compression reinforcement layer located 2.3 in from the extreme compression fiber yields because \(d^{'} \)< \(d^{'}_{y} \). Verify this by determining the strain in the compression reinforcement:

\(\frac{\varepsilon ^{'}_{s} }{7.7-2.3}=\frac{0.0030}{7.7}or\varepsilon ^{'}_{s}= 0.0021 > εy = 0.00207\)

Step 6: Determine \(\varepsilon _{t} \). The strain \(\varepsilon _{t} \) is determined by similar triangles (see Fig. 5.19):

\(\frac{c}{0.0030}=\frac{d_{t}-c }{ε_{t} } or {ε_{t}}=0.0030(\frac{d_{t} }{c}-1 )\)

Therefore,

\( {ε_{t}}=0.0030(\frac{21.6 }{7.7}-1 )=0.0054\gt 0.0040\)

Because \(ε_{t}\) > 0.0040, the maximum reinforcement requirement of ACI 10.3.5 is satisfied. Also, the section is tension-controlled because \(ε_{t}\) > 0.0050 (ACI 10.3.4).
Verify that the tension reinforcement located 19.7 in from the extreme compression fiber yields:

\(\frac{c_{s} }{19.7-7.7}=\frac{0.00302}{7.7} or {c_{s} }=0.0047\gt {ε_{y} }=0.00207\)

Step 7: Determine the nominal flexural strength \(M _{n} \). Equation (5.22) is used to determine \(M _{n} \):

\(M _{n}=(A_{s}-A^{'}_{c} ) A_{s}F_{y}(d-\frac{a}{2} )+A^{'}_{c}F_{y}(d-d^{'} )=[(4.80-0.40)1.5\times 60,000(20.6-\frac{6.5}{2})+0.40\times 60,000\times (20.6-2.3)]/12,000=418.3\)ft kips
