Guest Question.

A pair of parallel helical gears consists of a 20 teeth pinion meshing with a 40 teeth gear. The helix angle is 25° and the normal pressure angle is 20°. The normal module is 3 mm.
Calculate
(i) the transverse module;
(ii) the transverse pressure angle;
(iii) the axial pitch;
(iv) the pitch circle diameters of the pinion and the gear;
(v) the centre distance; and
(vi) the addendum and dedendum circle diameters of the pinion.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } \quad z_{p}=20 \quad z_{g}=40 \quad m_{n}=3 mm \).

\( \psi=25^{\circ} \quad \alpha_{n}=20^{\circ} \).

Step I Transverse module

\( m=\frac{m_{n}}{\cos \psi}=\frac{3}{\cos (25)}=3.31 mm \) (i)

Step II Transverse pressure angle

\( \tan \alpha=\frac{\tan \alpha_{n}}{\cos \psi}=\frac{\tan (20)}{\cos (25)} \text { or } \alpha=21.88^{\circ} \) (ii).

Step III Axial pitch

\( p_{a}=\frac{p}{\tan \psi}=\frac{\pi m}{\tan \psi}=\frac{\pi(3.31)}{\tan (25)}=22.3 mm \) (iii).

Step IV Pitch circle diameters of pinion and gear

\( d_{p}=\frac{z_{p} m_{n}}{\cos \psi}=\frac{20(3)}{\cos (25)}=66.2 mm \).

\( d_{g}=\frac{z_{g} m_{n}}{\cos \psi}=\frac{40(3)}{\cos (25)}=132.4 mm \) (iv).

Step V Centre distance

\( a=\frac{d_{p}+d_{g}}{2}=\frac{66.2+132.4}{2}=99.3 mm \) (v).

Step VI Addendum and dedendum circle diameters of pinion

\( d_{a}=m_{n}\left[\frac{z}{\cos \psi}+2\right] \).

\(=3\left[\frac{20}{\cos (25)}+2\right]=72.2 mm \).

\( d_{f}=m_{n}\left[\frac{z}{\cos \psi}-2.5\right] \).

\( =3\left[\frac{20}{\cos (25)}-2.5\right]=58.7 mm \) (vi).
