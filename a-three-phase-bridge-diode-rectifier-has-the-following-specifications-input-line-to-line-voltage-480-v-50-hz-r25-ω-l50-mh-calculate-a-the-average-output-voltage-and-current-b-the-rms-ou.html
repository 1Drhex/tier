Guest Question.

A three-phase bridge diode rectifier has the following specifications: Input line-to-line voltage 480 V 50 Hz, R = 25 立, L = 50 mH. Calculate:

a) The average output voltage and current.
b) The rms output current fundamental component.
c) The rms input current.
d) The average and rms diode current.
e) The output apparent power.

Admin
Solution

Solution

a) From Eq. (4.94) the average output voltage and current are given by:

 

\(\overline{ V }_{ o }=\frac{3 \sqrt{2} V _{\text {line-to-line }}}{\pi}=\frac{3 \sqrt{2} \times 480}{\pi}=648 V\)

 

\(\overline{ I }_{ o }=\frac{\overline{ V }_{ o }}{ R }=\frac{648}{25}=25.9 A\)

 

b) As can be seen from Fig. 4.12, the first output current high harmonic component is the sixth and its amplitude is:

 

\(\widehat{ I }_{o, 6}=\frac{\widehat{ V }_{ o , 6}}{\left| Z _{o,6}\right|}\)

 

Also, using Eq. (4.55) the amplitude of the output voltage sixth harmonic component is:

 

\(P _{ i }=\frac{1}{ T } \int_{0}^{ T }\left(\sum_{ n =1}^{\infty} \widehat{ V }_{ i , n } \sin \left( n \omega t -\theta_{ n }\right)\right)\left(\sum_{ n =1}^{\infty} \widehat{ I }_{ i , n } \sin \left( n \omega t -\varphi_{ n }\right)\right) dt\) (4.55)

 

\(\widehat{ V }_{ o , 6}=\frac{6 \sqrt{2} \times 480}{\pi(36-1)}=37 V\)

 

\(\left| Z _{ o , 6}\right|=\sqrt{ R ^{2}+(6 \omega L )^{2}}=\sqrt{25^{2}+[6(314)(0.05)]^{2}}=97.5 \Omega\)

 

\(\widehat{ I }_{ o , 6}=\frac{37}{97.5}=0.379 A \quad \widetilde{ I }_{ o , 6}=\frac{0.379}{\sqrt{2}}=0.268 A\)

 

\(\widetilde{ I }_{0}=\sqrt{\overline{ I }_{ o }^{2}+\widetilde{ I }_{ o , 6}^{2}+\widetilde{ I }_{ o , 12}^{2}+\cdots} \approx \sqrt{(25.9)^{2}+(0.268)^{2}} \approx 25.9 A\)

 

c) \(\widetilde{ I }_{ i }=\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} \overline{ I }_{ o }=\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} \times 25.9=21.2 A\)

 

d) \(\overline{ I }_{ D }=\frac{\overline{ I }_{ o }}{3}=\frac{25.9}{3}=8.63 A , \widetilde{ I }_{ D }=\frac{ I _{ o }}{\sqrt{3}}=\frac{25.9}{\sqrt{3}}=15 A\)

 

e) \(S =\sqrt{3} \widetilde{ V }_{ in } \widetilde{ I }_{ in }=\sqrt{3}(480)(21.2)=17.6 kVA\)
