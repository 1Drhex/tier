Guest Question.

Three plates ABC and BCDE and DEF are welded together as shown in Fig. 5.2(a). Circle of diameter 1.5 m is cut from the composite plate. Determine the centroid of the remaining area.

Admin
Solution

(i) The composite area can be viewed as a triangle \(\oplus \) rectangle \( \oplus \) semicircle \( \ominus \) circle. The areas and centroidal coordinates for each of these shapes can be determined by referring to Fig. 5.2(b).

(ii) Consider triangle ABC

\( A_1 = \frac{1}{2} ×3×4 = 6 m^2 \).

\( x_1 = \frac{2}{3} ×3 = 2 m \).

\( y_1 = \frac{1}{3} ×4 = 1.33 m \).

(iii) Consider rectangle BCDE

\( A_2 = 3×4 = 12 m^2 \).

\( x_2 = 3+1.5 = 4.5 m \) and \( y_2 = 3+1.5 = 4.5 m \).

(iv) Consider semicircle EFD

r = 2 m .

\( A_3 = \frac{π × 2^2}{2} = 6.283 m^2 \)

\( x_3 = 3 + 3 + \frac{4 ×2}{3π} = 6.848 m \) and \( y_3 = 2 m \) .

(v) Consider circle with centre O and radius r

r = 0.75 m .

\( – A_4 = -(π × 0.75^2) = -1.767 m^2 \).

\( x_4 = 6 m \) and \( y_4 = 2 m \).

(vi) Centroid of the given shaded area is given as

\( \overline{x} = \frac{6 × 2 + 12 × 4.5 + 6.283 × 6.848 – 1.767 × 6}{6 + 12 + 6.283 – 1.767} \) .

\( \overline{x} = 4.37 m \).

\( \overline{y} = \frac{6 × 1.33 + 12 × 2 + 6.283× 2 – 1.767 × 2}{6 + 12 + 6.283 – 1.767} \) .

\( \overline{y} = 1.82 m \) .

\( \therefore \) Coordinates of centroid w.r.t. origin A are G (4.37, 1.82) m. Ans.
