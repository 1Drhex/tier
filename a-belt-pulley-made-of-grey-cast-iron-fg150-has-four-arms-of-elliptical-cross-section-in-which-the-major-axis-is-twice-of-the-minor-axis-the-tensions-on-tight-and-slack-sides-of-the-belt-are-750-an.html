Guest Question.

A belt pulley, made of grey cast iron FG150, has four arms of elliptical cross-section, in which the major axis is twice of the minor axis. The tensions on tight and slack sides of the belt are 750 and 250 N respectively. The mean diameter of the pulley is 300 mm, while the hub diameter 60 is mm. Assume that half the number of arms transmit torque at any time. The factor of safety is 5. Determine the dimensions of the cross-section of the pulley arm near the hub.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } P_{1}=750 N \quad P_{2}=250 N \quad D=300 mm \).

\( \text { hub diameter }=60 mm \quad S_{u t}=150 N / mm ^{2}(f s)=5 \).

N = 4 b = 2a
Step I Permissible stress

\( \sigma_{t}=\frac{S_{u t}}{(f s)}=\frac{150}{5}=30 N / mm ^{2} \).

Step II Bending moment on arm
As shown in Fig. 13.22

\( M_{t}=\left(P_{1}-P_{2}\right)(D / 2)=(750-250)(300 / 2) \).

= 75000 N-mm
Assuming that one-half of the arms carry the load at any time, the tangential force P at the end of the arm is given by,

\( M_{t}=P R\left(\frac{N}{2}\right) \quad \text { or } \),

\( P=\frac{2 M_{t}}{R N}=\frac{2(75000)}{(150)(4)}=250 N \).

The arm l for the bending moment at the section XX near the hub is given by,

\( l=\frac{D}{2}-\frac{d}{2}=\frac{300}{2}-\frac{60}{2}=120 mm \).

At the section XX,

\( M_{b}=P \times l=250 \times 120=30000 N – mm \).

Step III Cross-section of arm

b = 2a

\( I=\frac{\pi a b^{3}}{64}=\frac{\pi a(2 a)^{3}}{64}=\frac{\pi a^{4}}{8} mm ^{4} \).

\( y=\frac{b}{2}=\frac{(2 a)}{2}=a mm \).

\( \sigma_{b}=\frac{M_{b} y}{I} \quad \text { or } \quad 30=\frac{(30000)(a)}{\left(\frac{\pi a^{4}}{8}\right)} \).

∴ a = 13.66 or 15 mm
b = 2a = 30 mm.
