MACRS with a Trade-in

In May 2017, your company traded in a computer and peripheral equipment, used in its business, that had a BV at that time of $25,000. A new, faster computer system having a fair MV of $400,000 was acquired. Because the vendor accepted the older computer as a trade-in, a deal was agreed to whereby your company would pay $325,000 cash for the new computer system.

(a) What is the GDS property class of the new computer system?

(b) How much depreciation can be deducted each year based on this class life? (Refer to Figure 7-1.)

Admin
