R-134a is filled into an insulated 0.2 \(m ^{3}\) initially empty container from a line at 500 kPa, saturated vapor until the flow stops by itself. Find the final mass and temperature in the container and the total irreversibility in the process.

Solution

Continuity Eq.4.20: \(m _{2}-\emptyset= m _{ in }\);

Energy Eq.4.21: \(m _{2} u _{2}-\emptyset= m _{ in } h _{\text {line }}+\emptyset+\emptyset \Rightarrow u _{2}= h _{\text {line }}\)

State 2: \(P _{2}= P _{\text {line }} \text { and } u _{2}= h _{\text {line }}=407.45 kJ / kg > u _{ g } \Rightarrow T _{2}= 4 0 ^{\circ} C\)
