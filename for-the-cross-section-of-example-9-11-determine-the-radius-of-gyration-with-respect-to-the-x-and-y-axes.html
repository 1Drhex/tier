Guest Question.

For the cross section of , determine the radius of gyration with respect to the x and y axes.

Admin
Solution

From Table A.3 of the Appendix, the W16 × 67 has a cross-sectional area of \(A = 19.7 in.^{2}\) From Eq. (9.14),

\(r_{x}=\sqrt{\frac{I_{x}}{A} }=\sqrt{\frac{954}{19.7} }=6.96\ in.\) \(r_{y}=\sqrt{\frac{I_{y}}{A} }=\sqrt{\frac{119}{19.7} }=2.46\ in.\)

These values for the radius of gyration are also given in Table A.3 of the Appendix.
