Guest Question.

Check that the bound state of the delta-function well (Equation 2.132) is orthogonal to the scattering states (Equations 2.134 and 2.135).

\( \psi(x)=\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar} e^{-m \alpha|x| / \hbar^{2}} ; \quad E=-\frac{m \alpha^{2}}{2 \hbar^{2}} \) (2.132).

\( \psi(x)=A e^{i k x}+B e^{-i k x} \) (2.134).

\( \psi(x)=F e^{i k x}+G e^{-i k x} \) (2.135).

Admin
Solution
\( \left\langle\psi_{\text {bound }} \mid \psi_{\text {scattering }}\right\rangle \)

\( =\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\int_{-\infty}^{0} e^{m \alpha x / h^{2}}\left(A e^{i k x}+B e^{-i k x}\right) d x+\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-m \alpha x / h^{2}}\left(F e^{i k x}+G e^{-i k x}\right) d x\right] \).

\( =\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[A \int_{-\infty}^{0} e^{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k\right) x} d x+B \int_{-\infty}^{0} e^{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k\right) x} d x+F \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{\left(-\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k\right) x} d x+G \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{\left(-\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k\right) x} d x\right] \).

\( =\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\left.A \frac{e^{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k\right) x}}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k}\right|_{-\infty} ^{0}+\left.B \frac{e^{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k\right) x}}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k}\right|_{-\infty} ^{0}+\left.F \frac{e^{\left(-\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k\right) x}}{-\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}+i k}\right|_{0} ^{\infty}+\left.G \frac{e^{\left(-\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}-i k\right) x}}{-\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}-i k}\right|_{0} ^{\infty}\right] \).

\( =\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\frac{A}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k}+\frac{B}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k}-\frac{F}{-\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}+i k}-\frac{G}{-\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k}\right]=\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\frac{A+G}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}+i k}+\frac{B+F}{\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}-i k}\right] \).

\( =\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\frac{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}-i k\right)(A+G)+\left(\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}+i k\right)(B+F)}{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}\right)^{2}+k^{2}}\right]=\frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\frac{\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}(A+G+B+F)+i k(B+F-A-G)}{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}}\right)^{2}+k^{2}}\right] \).

But Equation 2.136 says (A + G + B + F) = 2(A + B), and Equation 2.137 says \( ik(B + F – A – G)=-(2mÎ±/\hbar^2)(A + B)\),so

F + G = A + B (2.136).

\( i k(F-G-A+B)=-\frac{2 m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}(A+B) \) (2.137).

\( \left\langle\psi_{\text {bound }} \mid \psi_{\text {scattering }}=\right\rangle \frac{\sqrt{m \alpha}}{\hbar}\left[\frac{\frac{m \alpha}{h^{2}} 2(A+B)-\frac{2 m \alpha}{h^{2}}(A+B)}{\left(\frac{m \alpha}{\hbar^{2}}\right)^{2}+k^{2}}\right]=0\).
