Guest Question.

The high-temperature heat source for a cyclic heat engine is a steady flow heat exchanger where R-134a enters at 80°C, saturated vapor, and exits at 80°C, saturated liquid at a flow rate of 5 kg/s. Heat is rejected from the heat engine to a steady flow heat exchanger where air enters at 150 kPa and ambient temperature 20°C, and exits at 125 kPa, 70°C. The rate of irreversibility for the overall process is 175 kW. Calculate the mass flow rate of the air and the thermal efficiency of the heat engine.

Admin
Solution

C.V. R-134a Heat Exchanger,

\(\dot{ m }_{ R 134 a }=5 kg / s\) , Table B.5.1

Inlet: \(T _{1}=80^{\circ} C , \text { sat. vapor } x _{1}=1.0\),

\(\begin{aligned}& h _{1}= h _{ g }=429.189 kJ / kg \\& s _{1}= s _{ g }=1.6862 kJ / kg – K\end{aligned}\)

Exit: \(T _{2}=80^{\circ} C , \text { sat. liquid } x _{2}=0.0\)

\(\begin{aligned}& h _{2}= h _{ f }=322.794 kJ / kg ,\\& s _{2}= s _{ f }=1.3849 kJ / kg – K\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Air Heat Exchanger, \(C _{ p }=1.004 kJ / kg – K , \quad R =0.287 kJ / kg – K\)

Inlet: \(T _{3}=20^{\circ} C , \quad P _{3}=150 kPa\) Exit: \(T _{4}=70^{\circ} C , \quad P _{4}=125 kPa\)

\(s _{4}- s _{3}= C _{ p } \ln \left( T _{4} / T _{3}\right)- R \ln \left( P _{4} / P _{3}\right)=0.2103 kJ / kg – K\)

Entropy Eq. for the total system as control volume (since we know \(\text { İ }\) ) :

\(\dot{ I }/ T _{ o }=\dot{ S }_{ net }=\dot{ m }_{ R 134 a }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)+\dot{ m }_{ air }\left( s _{4}- s _{3}\right)\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} &=\frac{\dot{ I } / T _{o}-\dot{ m }_{ R 134 a }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)}{ s _{4}- s _{3}}=\frac{(175 / 293.15)-5(1.3849-1.6862)}{0.2103} \frac{ kW / K }{ kJ / kg – K } \\&= 1 0 . 0 kg / s\end{aligned}\)

 

Energy Eq. for each line: \(\dot{ Q }+\dot{ m } h _{ in }=\dot{ m } h _{ ex }+\dot{ W } ; \quad \dot{ W }=0\)

R-134a: \({ }_{1} \dot{ Q }_{2}=-\dot{ Q }_{ H }=\dot{ m }_{ R 134 a }\left( h _{2}- h _{1}\right)=-532 kW\)

Air: \(\dot{ Q }_{ L }={ }_{3} \dot{ Q }_{4}=\dot{ m }_{ air }\left( h _{4}- h _{3}\right)=\dot{ m }_{ air } C _{ p }\left( T _{4}- T _{3}\right)=501.8 kW\)

Control volume heat engine

\(\dot{ W }_{ net }=\dot{ Q }_{ H }-\dot{ Q }_{ L }=532-501.8=30.2 kW\) ;

 

\(\eta_{ th }=\dot{ W }_{ net } / \dot{ Q }_{ H }= 0 . 0 5 7 , \quad \text { or } 5 . 7 \%\)
