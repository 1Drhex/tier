Guest Question.

In a typical floor support, a short timber post is capped with a steel channel to provide a larger bearing area for the joists. The joists are \(4^{\prime \prime }\times 12^{\prime \prime }\)rough cut. The steel base plate is provided to increase the bearing area on the concrete footing. The load transmitted from each floor joist is 5.0 k.

Find the following:
a. The minimum length of channel required to support the joists if the maximum allowable bearing stress perpendicular to the grain is 400 psi.

b. The minimum size of post required to support the load if the maximum stress allowed in compression parallel to the grain is 1,200 psi.
c. The size of base plate required if the allowable bearing on concrete is 450 psi.
d. The footing size if allowable soil \(f_{p}\)= 2,000 psf.

Admin
Solution

a. \(f_{p} =\frac{P}{A} \) Examine one joist; \( P = 5,000\# \)

 

\(f_{allowable}=400\# / in.^{2} \)

 

\(A=\frac{P}{f} =\frac{5,000\# }{400\# / in.^{2} } =12.5 in.^{2} \)

 

\(A=4^{\prime \prime }\times L/2\)

 

\(4^{\prime \prime }\times L/2=12.5 in.^{2}\)

 

\(\therefore L=6.25 \space in.\)

b. \(f_{c} =\frac{P}{A} ; \space \space P=2\times 5.0 \space k=10 \space k \)

 

\(f_{allowable}=1,200\# / in.^{2} \)

 

\(A_{required}=\frac{P}{f} =\frac{10,000\# }{1,200\# / in.^{2} } =8.33 in.^{2} \space , \space L=\sqrt{A}\)

 

Minimum size requirement of square post is \(2.89^{\prime \prime }\times 2.89^{\prime \prime }\).

 

Practical to use at least a \(4^{\prime \prime }\times 4^{\prime \prime }\) post.

c. \(A_{required}=\frac{P}{f} =\frac{10,000\# }{450\# / in.^{2} } =22.2 \space in.^{2} \)

 

Minimum base plate \(=4.72^{\prime \prime }\) square

 

Use at least a \(5^{\prime \prime }\times 5^{\prime \prime } \) square plate.

d. \(A_{required}=\frac{P}{f} =\frac{10,000\# }{2,000\# / ft.^{2} } =5 \space ft.^{2} \space ; \space x=2.24^\prime \)

 

Use a \(2^{\prime }-3^{\prime \prime }\times 2^{\prime }-3^{\prime \prime }\) footing size.
