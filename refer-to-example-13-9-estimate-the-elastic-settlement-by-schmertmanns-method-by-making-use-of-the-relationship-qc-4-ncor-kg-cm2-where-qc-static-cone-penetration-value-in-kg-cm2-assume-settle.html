Guest Question.

Refer to . Estimate the elastic settlement by Schmertmann’s method by making use of the relationship \(q_{c}=4 N_{\text {cor }} kg / cm ^{2} \text { where } q_{c}=\) static cone penetration value in \(kg / cm ^{2}\). Assume settlement is required at the end of a period of 3 years.

Admin
Solution

The average value of for \(N_{c o r}\) each layer given in is given below

 

Layer No Average Average \(q_{c}\)
\(N_{\text {cor }}\) \(kg / cm ^{2}\) Mpa
1 9 36 3.6
2 12 48 4.8
3 17 68 6.8

 

The vertical strain influence factor \(I_{z}\) with respect to depth is calculated by making use of Fig. 13.10.

At the base of the foundation \(I_{z}=0.1\)

 

At depth B/2, \(I_{z}=0.5+0.1 \sqrt{\frac{q_{n}}{p_{o}^{\prime}}}\)

 

where \(q_{n}=120\) kpa

\(p_{o}^{\prime}=\) effective average overburden pressure at depth = (2 + B/2) = 6 m below ground level.

\(=2 \times 16.5+4 \times 8.5=67 kN / m ^{2}\).

 

\(I_{z}(\max )=0.5+0.1 \sqrt{\frac{120}{67}}=0.63\)

 

\(I_{z}=0 \text { at } z=H=16\) 16m below base level of the foundation. The distribution of \(I_{z}\) is given in Fig. Ex. 13.11. The equation for settlement is

 

\(S=C_{1} C_{2} q_{n}{ }_{0}^{2 B} \frac{I_{z}}{E_{s}} \Delta z\)

 

where \(C_{1}=1-0.5 \frac{q_{0}^{\prime}}{q_{n}}=1-0.5 \frac{2 \times 16.5}{120}=0.86\)

 

\(C_{2}=1+0.2 \log \frac{t}{0.1}=1+0.2 \log \frac{3}{0.1}=1.3\)

 

where t = 3 years.

The elastic modulus \(E_{s}\) for normally consolidated sands may be calculated by Eq. (13.29).

 

\(E_{s}=4 q_{c} \text { for } q_{c}<10\) (13.29)

 

\(E_{s}=4 \bar{q}_{c} \text { for } q_{c}<10\) Mpa

 

where \(\bar{q}_{c}\) is the average for each layer.

Layer 2 is divided into sublayers 2a and 2b for computing \(I_{z}\) . The average of the influence factors for each of the layers given in Fig. Ex. 13.11 are tabulated along with the other calculations

 

Layer No. \(\Delta z( cm )\) \(\bar{q}_{c}( MPa )\) \(E_{s}( MPa )\) \(I_{z}(a v)\) \(\frac{I_{z} \Delta z}{E_{s}}\)
1 300 3.6 14.4 0.3 6.25
2a 100 4.8 19.2 0.56 2.92
2b 500 4.8 19.2 0.5 13.02
3 700 6.8 27.2 0.18 4.63
Total 26.82

 

Substituting in the equation for settlement S, we have

 

\(S=0.86 \times 1.3 \times 0.12 \times 26.82=3.6 cm =36 mm\)
