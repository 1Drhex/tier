Guest Question.

Check the adequacy of the column in , assuming that the factored shear force \(V_{u}\) is equal to 39 kips for load combination 3.

Admin
Solution

Equation (8.44) will be used to determine \(V_{c}\) .
It was shown in that the effective depth d of the section is equal to 13.9 in for all four load combinations.
The moment Mm is calculated from the following equation for the third load combination (see Table 8.12):

\(M_{m}=M_{u}-N_{u}\frac{4h-d}{8}\)
\(=(197\times 12) -213\left[\frac{(4 \times 20)-13.9}{8}\right]=604.1\) in kips

Also, the reinforcement ratio ρw is determined using the area of tensile reinforcement in the section:

\(ρ_{w} =\frac{A_{s}}{b_{0}d}=\frac{8\times 0.44}{20 \times 13.9}=0.0127\)

The nominal shear strength of the concrete \(V_{c}\) is

\(V_{c}=(1.9\lambda \sqrt{f '_{c}}+2,500ρ_{w} \frac{V_{u}d}{M_{m}})b_{w}d\)
\(=\left[1.9 \times 1.0 \sqrt{4,000}+\left(2,500 \times 0.0127 \times \frac{39 \times 13.9 }{604.1} \right) \right] \times 20 \times 13.9/1,000=41.3 \) kips

Check the upper limit on \(V_{c }\):

\(V_{c} = 3.5λ\sqrt{ f '_{c} }b_{w}d\sqrt{1+ \frac{ N_{u}}{500A_{g}}}\)
\(=3.5 ×1.0\sqrt{4,000}×20×13.9\sqrt{1+ \frac {213,000} {500×20^{2}}}/1,000\)
=88.4 kips > 41.3 kips

Since \(V_{u} = 39 kips > φV_{c} = 31\) kips, the maximum spacing of the ties is equal to d/2 = 13.9/2 = 6.95 in (see ACI 11.4.5.1). Assume the ties are spaced 6 in on center.
Therefore, the design shear strength \(φV_{n}\) is

\(φV_{n} = φ(V_{c} + V_{s}) = 0.75 (41.3 + 61.2) = 76.9\) kips > \(V_{u} = 39\) kips

Comments
The nominal shear strength of the concrete \(V_{c}\) determined by Eq. (8.43) is

\(V_{c} = 2\left(1+ \frac{ N_{u}}{2,000A_{g}} \right) λ\sqrt{ f '_{c} }b_{w}d\)
\(=2\left(1+ \frac{ 213,000}{2,000×20^{2} } \right )× 1.0\sqrt{4,000}×20×13.9/1,000=44.5\) kips
