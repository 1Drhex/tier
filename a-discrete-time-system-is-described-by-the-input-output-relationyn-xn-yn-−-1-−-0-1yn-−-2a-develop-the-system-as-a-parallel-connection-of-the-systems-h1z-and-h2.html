Guest Question.

A discrete-time system is described by the input-output relation:

y[n] = x[n] + y[n − 1] − 0.1y[n − 2]

a. Develop the system as a parallel connection of the systems H1(z) and H2(z). Then, give its schematic representation.

b. Find and plot the impulse response of the system. Which pole determines the response of the system?

Admin
Solution

a. y[n] = x[n] + y[n − 1] − 0.1y[n − 2] (1)

From the difference equation (1) of the discrete system, the transfer function is calculated in the form of equation (2), and the implementation diagram in a parallel form is designed.

\((1)\Rightarrow H(z)=\frac{Y(z)}{X(Z)}=\frac{1}{1-z^{-1}+0.1z^{-2}}=\frac{z^{2}}{z^{2}-z+0.1}=\frac{z^{2}}{(z-0.887)(z-0.113)}\Rightarrow \)
\(H(z)=\frac{1.146z}{\underbrace{z-0.887}_{H_{1}(z)} }-\frac{0.146z}{\underbrace{z-0.113}_{H_{2}(z)} }\) (2)

b. The impulse response of the system is calculated:

\(h(n)=IZT\left\{H(z)\right\}=[1.146(0.887)^{n}-0.146(0.113)^{n}]u[n]\) (3)
\((3)\Rightarrow h[n]=h_{1}[n]-h_{2}[n]\) (4)

The impulse response is plotted next:

We notice that the term \(h_{2}[n]\) has a much smaller contribution (in terms of values) to h[n] and is practically zero for n ≥ 4. Thus, the impulse response is approximated by:

\(h[n]\cong h_{1}[n]=1.146(0.887)^{n}u[n], n\geq 4\)
