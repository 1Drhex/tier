Guest Question.

The simply supported non-uniform welded beam of S275 steel shown in Figure 6.33 has a central concentrated design load Q acting at the bottom flange. The beam has a 960 × 16 web, and its equal flanges are 300 × 32 in the central 2.0 m region and 300 × 20 in the outer 3.0 m regions. Determine the maximum value of Q.

Admin
Solution

Section properties.

\(W_{pl,yi} = 300 × 32 × (960 + 32) + 960^2 × 16/4 = 13.21 × 10^6 mm^3.\)

 

\(W_{pl,yo} = 300 × 20 × (960 + 20) + 960^2 × 16/4 = 9.566 × 10^6 mm^3. \)

 

\(I_{zi} = 2 × 300^3 × 32/12 = 144 × 10^6 mm^4. \)

 

\(I_{ti} = 2 × 300 × 32^3/3 + 960 × 16^3/3 = 7.864 × 10^6 mm^4. \)

 

\(I_{wi} = 144 × 10^6 × (960 + 32)^2/4 = 35.43 × 10^{12} mm^6. \)

 

Section resistances.

\(t_{fi} = 32 mm, f_{y} = 265 N/mm^2. \)

 

\(t_{fo} = 20 mm, f_{y} = 265 N/mm^2. \)

 

\(ε = √(235/265) = 0.942\)

 

\(c_{fo}/(t_{fo}ε) = (300 − 16)/2/(20 × 0.942) = 7.54 < 9\) and the flange is Class 1.

 

\(c_{w}/(t_{w}ε) = 960/(16 × 0.942) = 63.7 < 72\) and the web is Class 1.

 

\(M_{c,Rd,o} = 265 × 9.566 × 10^6 Nmm = 2535 kNm. \)

 

\(M_{c,Rd,i} = 265 × 13.21 × 10^6 Nmm = 3501 kNm. \)

 

Critical section.

At mid-span, \(M_{Ed,4} = Q × 8/4 = 2.0 Q kNm. \)

 

At the change of section, \(M_{Ed,3} = (Q/2) × 3 = 1.5 Q kNm\)

 

\(M_{Ed,4}/M_{c,Rd,i} = 2.0 × Q/3501 = 0.000571 Q\)

 

\(M_{Ed,3}/M_{c,Rd,o} = 1.5 × Q/2535 = 0.000592 Q > 0.000571 Q\)

and so the critical section is at the change of section.

 

Elastic buckling.

For a uniform beam having the properties of the central cross-section, using equation 6.3,

\(M_{zx} =\sqrt{}\left\{\left(\frac{π^2EI_{z}}{L^2} \right)\left(GI_{t} +\frac{π^2EI_{w}}{L^2} \right) \right\} \)

 

\(M_{zxi} =\sqrt{\left\{\frac{π^2× 210 000 × 144 × 10^6}{8000^2}\times \left(81 000 × 7.864 × 10^6 +\frac{π^2× 210 000 × 35.43 × 10^{12}}{8000^2} \right) \right\}}Nmm= 2885 kNm. \)

 

Using equation 6.12, \(N_{cr,z} = π^2EI_{z}/L^2\)

 

\(N_{cr,z} = π^2 × 210000 × 144 × 10^6/8000^2 N = 4663 kN. \)

 

Using Figure 6.7, \( α_{m} = 1.35\)

 

\(\frac{0.4α_{m}z_{Q}N_{cr,z}}{M_{zxi}} =\frac{0.4 × 1.35 × (960/2) × 4663 × 10^3}{2885 × 10^6} = 0.491\)

 

Using equation 6.11,

\(\frac{M_{cr}}{M_{zx}}=\alpha _{m}\left\{\sqrt{}\left[1+\frac{0.4α_{m}z_{Q}N_{cr,z}}{M_{zx}} \right]^2+\frac{0.4α_{m}z_{Q}N_{cr,z}}{M_{zx}} \right\}\)

 

\(M_{cri}= 1.35 × 2885\left\{√[1 + 0.491^2] + 0.491\right\} Nmm = 5854 kNm. \)

 

(Using the computer program PRFELB [18] leads to \(M_{cri} = 5972 kNm.) \) For the non-uniform beam,

 

\(L_{r}/L = 6.0/8.0 = 0.75\)

 

\(\left(0.6 + 0.4\frac{d_{min}}{d_{max}} \right)\frac{A_{f min}}{A_{f max}} = \left(0.6 + 0.4 ×\frac{960 + 2 × 20}{960 + 2 × 32} \right)\frac{300 × 20}{300 × 32} = 0.619\)

 

Using equation 6.80,

\(α_{st} = 1.0 − 1.2\left(\frac{L_{r}}{L}\right) \left\{1 −\left(0.6 + 0.4\frac{d_{min}}{d_{max}}\right)\frac{A_{f min}}{A_{f max}} \right\}\)

 

\(α_{st} = 1.0 − 1.2 ×\left(\frac{6000}{8000} \right) × (1 − 0.619) = 0.657\)

 

\(M_{cr} = 0.657 × 5854 = 3847 kNm. \)

 

(Using the computer program PRFELB [18] leads to \(M_{cr} = 4420 kNm].)\)

 

Moment resistance.

At the critical section, \(M_{cro} = 3847 × 3000/4000 = 2885 kNm. \)

 

Using the values for the critical section in equation 6.25, \(\bar{\lambda }_{LT}=\sqrt{(W_{y}f_{y}/M_{cr})}\)

\(\bar{\lambda }_{LT}=\sqrt{\left(2535/2885\right) }=0.937\)

\(h/b = (960 + 2 × 20)/300 = 3.33 > 2. \)

 

Using the EC3 simple general method with \(β = 1.0 and \bar{\lambda }_{LT,0} = 0.2\) (Clause 6.3.2.2) and \(α_{LT} = 0.34\) (Tables 6.4 and 6.3) and equation 6.27,

\(Φ_{LT} = 0.5\left\{1 + α_{LT} (\bar{\lambda }_{LT} − \bar{\lambda }_{LT,0}) + β\bar{\lambda }^2_{LT}\right\},\)

 

\(Φ_{LT} = 0.5{\left\{1 + 0.34(0.937 − 0.2) + 0.937^2\right\} } = 1.065\)

 

Using equation 6.26,

\(\frac{M_{b,Rd}γ_{M1}}{W_{y}f_{y}}=\frac{1}{Φ_{LT} +\sqrt{Φ^2_{LT} − β\bar{\lambda }^2_{LT}} }\)

 

\(M_{b,Rd} = 2535/{\left\{1.065 + √(1.065^2 − 0.937^2)\right\} } kNm\)

 

\(= 1615 kNm = (Q_{b,Rd} /2)× 3.0\)

 

\(Q_{b,Rd} = 1615 × 2/3.0 = 1077 kN.\)
