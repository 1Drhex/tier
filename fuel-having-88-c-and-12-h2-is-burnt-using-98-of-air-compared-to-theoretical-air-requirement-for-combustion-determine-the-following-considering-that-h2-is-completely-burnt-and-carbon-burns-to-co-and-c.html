Fuel having 88% C and 12% H\(_{2}\) is burnt using 98% of air compared to theoretical air requirement for combustion. Determine the following considering that H\(_{2}\) is completely burnt and carbon burns to CO and CO\(_{2}\) leaving no free carbon.
