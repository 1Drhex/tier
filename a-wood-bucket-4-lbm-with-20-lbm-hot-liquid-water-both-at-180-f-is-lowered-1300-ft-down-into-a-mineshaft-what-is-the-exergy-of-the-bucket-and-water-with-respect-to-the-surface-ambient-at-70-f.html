Guest Question.

A wood bucket (4 lbm) with 20 lbm hot liquid water, both at 180 F, is lowered 1300 ft down into a mineshaft. What is the exergy of the bucket and water with respect to the surface ambient at 70 F?

Admin
Solution

C.V. Bucket and water. Both thermal exergy and potential energy terms.
\(v _{1} \approx v _{0}\)A for both wood and water so work to atm. is zero.

Use constant heat capacity table F.2 for wood and table F.7.1 (sat. liquid) for water.
From Eq.8.28
