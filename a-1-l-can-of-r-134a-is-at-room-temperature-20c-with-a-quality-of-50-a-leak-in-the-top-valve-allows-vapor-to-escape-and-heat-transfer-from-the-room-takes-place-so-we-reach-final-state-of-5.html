Guest Question.

A 1 L can of R-134a is at room temperature 20°C with a quality of 50%. A leak in the top valve allows vapor to escape and heat transfer from the room takes place so we reach final state of 5°C with a quality of 100%. Find the mass that escaped, the heat transfer and the entropy generation not including that made in the valve.
