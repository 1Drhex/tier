Guest Question.

A T-beam is made up of two plates and two angles as shown in Fig. 8.21. Determine the moment of inertia of T-section about an axis passing through the centroid of the section and parallel to the top plate.

Admin
Solution

Refer to Fig. 8.22.
The given section is divided into four rectangles as shown. Since the section is symmetrical about y-axis we shall find out \(\bar{y}\) for which we have the follows table :

 

at(\(cm^{3}\)) Centroidal distance ‘y’ from LL (cm) Area ‘a’ (\(cm^{2}\)) Components
8 \(\frac{1}{2}=0.5\) 16 × 1 = 16 Rectangle (1)
19.5 \(1+\frac{1}{2}=1.5\) 13 × 1 = 13 Rectangle (2)
67.5 \(\frac{5}{2}+1+1=4.5\) 3 × 5 = 15 Rectangle (3)
120 \(\frac{10}{2}+5+1+1=12\) 1 × 10 = 10 Rectangle (4)
\(\sum ay=215\) _ \(\sum a=45\) Total

 

\(\bar{y}=\frac{\sum ay}{\sum a}=\frac{215}{54}=3.98\) cm from LL

 

\(I_{xx}\)=?

 

\(I_{xx}=\left [ \frac{16\times 1^{3}}{12}+12\times 1\times \left ( 3.98-0.5 \right )^{2} \right ]\)

 

\(+\left [ \frac{13\times 1^{3}}{12}+13\times 1\times \left ( 3.98-1.5 \right )^{2} \right ]\)

 

\(+\left [ \frac{3\times 5^{3}}{12}+3\times 5\times \left ( 4.5-3.98 \right )^{2} \right ]\)

 

\(+\left [ \frac{1\times 10^{3}}{12}+10\times 1\times \left ( 12-3.98 \right )^{2} \right ]\)

 

= (1.33 + 193.76) + (1.08 + 79.95) + (31.25 + 4.056) + (83.33 + 643.2)

 

= 195.09 + 81.03 + 35.31 + 726.53

 

= 1037.96 \(cm^{4}\).
