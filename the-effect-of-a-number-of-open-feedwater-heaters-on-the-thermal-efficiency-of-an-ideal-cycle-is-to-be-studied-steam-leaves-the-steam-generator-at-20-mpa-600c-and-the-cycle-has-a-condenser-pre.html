Guest Question.

The effect of a number of open feedwater heaters on the thermal efficiency of an ideal cycle is to be studied. Steam leaves the steam generator at 20 MPa, 600째C, and the cycle has a condenser pressure of 10 kPa. Determine the thermal efficiency for each of the following cases. A: No feedwater heater. B: One feedwater heater operating at 1 MPa. C: Two feedwater heaters, one operating at 3 MPa and the other at 0.2 MPa.

Admin
Solution

a) no feed water heater

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P } &=\int_{1}^{2} v dP \\& \approx 0.00101(20000-10) \\&=20.2 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ P }=191.8+20.2=212.0\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}s _{4} &= s _{3}=6.5048 \\&=0.6493+ x _{4} \times 7.5009\end{aligned}\)

 

\(x _{4}=0.78064\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}h _{4} &=191.83+0.78064 \times 2392.8 \\&=2059.7\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ T }= h _{3}- h _{4}=3537.6-2059.7 \\&=1477.9 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ N }= w _{ T }- w _{ P }=1477.9-20.2=1457.7 \\& q _{ H }= h _{3}- h _{2}=3537.6-212.0=3325.6\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\eta_{ TH }=\frac{ w _{ N }}{ q _{ H }}=\frac{1457.7}{3325.6}= 0 . 4 3 8\)

 

b) one feedwater heater

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P 12} &=0.00101(1000-10) \\&=1.0 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ P 12}=191.8+1.0=192.8 \\& w _{ P 34}=0.001127(20000-1000) \\&\quad=21.4 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& h _{4}= h _{3}+ w _{ P 34}=762.8+21.4=784.2 \\& s _{6}= s _{5}=6.5048 \\&\quad=2.1387+ x _{6} \times 4.4478\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&x_{6}=0.9816 \\&h_{6}=762.8+0.9816 \times 2015.3=2741.1\end{aligned}\)

 

CV: heater
const: \(m _{3}= m _{6}+ m _{2}=1.0 kg\)

1st law: \(m _{6} h _{6}+ m _{2} h _{2}= m _{3} h _{3}\)

\(m _{6}=\frac{762.8-192.8}{2741.1-192.8}=0.2237\)

 

\(\left. m _{2}=0.7763, h _{7}=2059.7\left(= h _{4} \text { of part a }\right)\right)\)

 

CV: turbine

\(\begin{gathered}w _{ T }=\left( h _{5}- h _{6}\right)+ m _{2}\left( h _{6}- h _{7}\right) \\=(3537.6-2741.1)+0.7763(2741.1-2059.7)=1325.5 kJ / kg\end{gathered}\)

CV: pumps

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ P }= m _{1} w _{ P 12}+ m _{3} w _{ P 34}=0.7763(1.0)+1(21.4)=22.2 kJ / kg \\& w _{ N }=1325.5-22.2=1303.3 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

CV: steam generator

\(\begin{aligned}& q _{ H }= h _{5}- h _{4}=3537.6-784.2=2753.4 kJ / kg \\&\eta_{ TH }= w _{ N } / q _{ H }=1303.3 / 2753.4= 0 . 4 7 3\end{aligned}\)

 

c) two feedwater heaters

\(\begin{aligned}w _{P12}=& 0.00101 \times \\&(200-10) \\=& 0.2 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}h _{2} &= w _{ P12}+ h _{1} \\&=191.8+0.2 \\&=192.0\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P 34}=& 0.001061 \times \\&(3000-200) \\=& 3.0 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}h _{4} &= h _{3}+ w _{ P 34} \\&=504.7+3.0 \\&=507.7\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}w _{ P 56} &=0.001217(20000-3000) \\&=20.7 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(h _{6}= h _{5}+ w _{ P 56}=1008.4+20.7=1029.1\)

 

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}s _{8}= s _{7}=6.5048 \\\text { at } P _{8}=3 MPa\end{array}\right\} \begin{aligned}& T _{8}=293.2{ }^{\circ} C \\& h _{8}=2974.8\end{aligned}\)

 

\(s _{9}= s _{8}=6.5048=1.5301+ x _{9} \times 5.5970\)

 

\(x _{9}=0.8888 \Rightarrow h _{9}=504.7+0.888 \times 2201.9=2461.8 kJ / kg\)

 

CV: high pressure heater

cont: \(m _{5}= m _{4}+ m _{8}=1.0 kg ; \quad \text { 1st law: } m _{5} h _{5}= m _{4} h _{4}+ m _{8} h _{8}\)

\(m _{8}=\frac{1008.4-507.7}{2974.8-507.7}=0.2030 \quad m _{4}=0.7970\)

CV: low pressure heater

cont: \(m _{9}+ m _{2}= m _{3}= m _{4} ; \quad \text { 1st law: } m _{9} h _{9}+ m _{2} h _{2}= m _{3} h _{3}\)

\(\begin{aligned}& m _{9}=\frac{0.7970(504.7-192.0)}{2461.8-192.0}=0.1098 \\& m _{2}=0.7970-0.1098=0.6872\end{aligned}\)

 

CV: turbine

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ T }=\left( h _{7}- h _{8}\right)+\left(1- m _{8}\right)\left( h _{8}- h _{9}\right)+\left(1- m _{8}- m _{9}\right)\left( h _{9}- h _{10}\right) \\&=(3537.6-2974.8)+0.797(2974.8-2461.8) \\&\quad+0.6872(2461.8-2059.7)=1248.0 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

CV: pumps

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ P }= m _{1} w _{ P 12}+ m _{3} w _{ P 34}+ m _{5} w _{ P 56} \\&=0.6872(0.2)+0.797(3.0)+1(20.7)=23.2 kJ / kg \\& w _{ N }=1248.0-23.2=1224.8 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

CV: steam generator

\(\begin{aligned}& q _{ H }= h _{7}- h _{6}=3537.6-1029.1=2508.5 kJ / kg \\&\eta_{ TH }= w _{ N } / q _{ H }=1224.8 / 2508.5= 0 . 4 8 8\end{aligned}\)
