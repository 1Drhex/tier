Guest Question.

VidMark, a manufacturer of cell phones, is currently developing a new model (VidPhone X70) that will be released on the market when development is complete. This phone will will allow the user to place ultra high definition video phone calls. VidMark is concerned about the development cost and time. It is also worried about market estimates of the sales of the new VidPhone X70. The cost estimates and forecast are given in the following table.
