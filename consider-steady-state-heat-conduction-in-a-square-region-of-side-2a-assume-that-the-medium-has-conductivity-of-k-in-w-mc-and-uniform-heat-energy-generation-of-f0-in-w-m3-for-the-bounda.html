Guest Question.

Consider steady-state heat conduction in a square region of side 2a. Assume that the medium has conductivity of k in W/(\(m^{\circ}\)C) and uniform heat (energy) generation of f0 (in W/\(m^{3}\)). For the boundary conditions and mesh shown in Fig. P8.30, write the finite element algebraic equations for nodes 1, 3, and 7.

Admin
Solution

The algebraic equations associated with nodes 1, 3, and 7 are:

\(K_{11}T_{1}+K_{12}T_{2}+K_{14}T_{4}+K_{15}T_{5}=F_{1}\)

 

\(K_{32}T_{2}+K_{33}T_{3}+K_{35}T_{5}+K_{36}T_{6}=F_{3}\)

 

\(K_{74}T_{4}+K_{75}T_{5}+K_{77}T_{7}+K_{78}T_{8}=F_{8}\)

 

where \(T_{3}=T_{6}=T_{0}\) and

\(K_{11}=K_{11}^{1}\) , \(K_{12}=K_{12}^{1}\) , \(K_{14}=K_{14}^{1}\) , \(K_{15}=K_{13}^{1}\) , \(F_{1}=\frac{g_{0}a^{2}}{4}+\frac{q_{0}a}{2}\)

\(K_{32}=K_{21}^{2}\) , \(K_{33}=K_{22}^{2}\) , \(K_{35}=K_{24}^{2}\) , \(K_{36}=K_{23}^{2}\) , \(F_{3}=\frac{g_{0}a^{2}}{4}+Q_{2}^{2}\)

\(K_{74}=K_{41}^{3}\) , \(K_{75}=K_{42}^{3}\) , \(K_{77}=K_{44}^{3}+H_{44}^{3}\) , \(K_{78}=K_{43}^{3}+H_{43}^{3}\)

\(F_{7}=\frac{g_{0}a^{2}}{4}+\frac{q_{0}a}{2}+P_{4}^{3}\)

Note that the equation associated with node 3 will be used to compute \(Q_{2}^{2}\). The coefficients \(H_{ij}^{e}\) and \(P_{i}^{e}\) are defined in Eqs. (8.5.10a, b):

\(H_{44}^{e}=\frac{a\beta }{3}\) , \(H_{43}^{e}=\frac{a\beta }{6}\) , \(P_{4}^{e}=\frac{aT_{\infty }\beta }{2}\)

We have

\(K_{11}=\frac{4}{6}k\) , \(K_{12}=-\frac{k}{6}\) , \(K_{13}=-\frac{2}{6}k\) , \(K_{14}=-\frac{k}{6}\) , \(K_{22}=\frac{4}{6}k\)

\(K_{23}=-\frac{k}{6}\) , \(K_{24}=-\frac{2}{6}k\) , \(K_{33}=\frac{4}{6}k\) , \(K_{34}=-\frac{k}{6}\) , \(K_{44}=\frac{4}{6}k\)

For a = 0.01m, k = 30 W/(\(m\cdot ^{\circ}C\)), \(\beta \) = 60 W/(\(m^{2}\cdot ^{\circ}C\)), \(T_{\infty }\) = 0.0, \(T_{0}\) = \(100^{\circ}\)C , \(q_{0}\) = 2 × \(10^{5}\) W/\(m^{2}\), and \(g_{0}\) = \(10^{7}\) W/\(m^{2}\), the nodal values are:

\(T_{1}\) = 297.06, \(T_{2}\) = 214.58, \(T_{4}\) = 295.98, \(T_{5}\) = 213.83

\(T_{7}\) = 292.15, \(T_{8}\) = 210.88
