Guest Question.

Do with R-22 as the working fluid.
A flow with 2 kg/s of water is available at 95°C for the boiler. The restrictive factor is the boiling temperature of 85°C. Therefore, break the process up from 2-3 into two parts as shown in the diagram.

Admin
Solution

Properties from CATT3.

\(\begin{array}{r}-\dot{ Q }_{ AB }=\dot{ m }_{ H 2 O }\left( h _{ A }- h _{ B }\right)=2(397.94-355.88)=84.12 kW \\\quad=\dot{ m }_{ R -22}(253.69-165.09) \Rightarrow \dot{ m }_{ R -22}=0.949 kg / s\end{array}\)

To verify that \(T _{ D }= T _{3}\) is the restrictive factor, find \(T _{ C }\) .

\(\begin{aligned}&-\dot{ Q }_{ AC }=0.949(165.09-96.48)=65.11=2.0\left(355.88- h _{ C }\right) \\& h _{ C }=323.32 kJ / kg \Rightarrow T _{ C }=77.2^{\circ} C \quad OK\end{aligned}\)

State 1: \(40^{\circ} C , \quad 1533.5 kPa , v _{1}=0.000884 m ^{3} / kg\)

CV Pump: \(w _{ P }= v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=0.000884(4036.8-1533.5)=2.21 kJ / kg\)

CV: Turbine

\(\begin{aligned}& s _{4}= s _{3}=0.7918=0.3417+ x _{4} \times 0.5329 \Rightarrow x _{4}=0.8446 \\& h _{4}=94.27+0.8446 \times 166.88=235.22 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

Energy Eq.: \(w _{ T }= h _{3}- h _{4}=253.69-235.22=18.47 kJ / kg\)

Cycle:

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ NET }= w _{ T }- w _{ P }=18.47-2.21=16.26 kJ / kg \\&\dot{ W }_{ NET }=\dot{ m }_{ R 22} w _{ NET }=0.949 \times 16.26= 1 5 . 4 3 k W\end{aligned}\)
