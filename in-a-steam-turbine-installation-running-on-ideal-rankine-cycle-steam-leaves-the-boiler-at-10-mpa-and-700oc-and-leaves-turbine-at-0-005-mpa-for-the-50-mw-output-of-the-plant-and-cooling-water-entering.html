Guest Question.

In a steam turbine installation running on ideal Rankine cycle steam leaves the boiler at 10 MPa and 700ºC and leaves turbine at 0.005 MPa. For the 50 MW output of the plant and cooling water entering and leaving condenser at 15ºC and 30ºC respectively determine
(a) the mass flow rate of steam in kg/s
(b) the mass flow rate of condenser cooling water in kg/s
(c) the thermal efficiency of cycle
(d) the ratio of heat supplied and rejected (in boiler and condenser respectively).
Neglet K.E. and P.E. changes.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{From steam table}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad \qquad \qquad\text{At inlet to turbine, }h_{2}&= h_{\text{at 10 MPa, 700ºC}}\\h_{2}&= 3870.5 kJ/kg\\s_{2}&= 7.1687 kJ/kg · K\end{aligned}\\\text{For process 2-3, }s_{2} = s_{3} \text{ and }s_{3} < s_{\text{ƒ at 0.005 MPa}}\text{ so state 3 lies in wet region. Let dryness fraction at state 3 be }x_{3}.\\\begin{aligned}\qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad s_{3}&= 7.1687 = s_{\text{ƒ at 0.005 MPa}} + x_{3} · s_{\text{ƒg at 0.005 MPa}}\\7.1687&= 0.4764 + (x_{3} × 7.9187)\\x_{3}&= 0.845\\h_{3}&= h_{\text{ƒ at 0.005 MPa}} + x_{3} · h_{\text{fg at 0.005 MPa}}\\&= 137.82 + (0.845 × 2423.7)\\h_{3}&= 2185.85 kJ/kg\\h_{4}&= h_{\text{ƒ at 0.005 MPa}} = 137.82 kJ/kg\\\text{For pumping process, }(h_{1} – h_{4})&= v_{4} × (p_{1} – p_{4})\\v_{4}&= v_{\text{ƒ at 0.005 MPa}} = 0.001005 m^{3}/kg\\h_{1}&= 137.82 + (0.001005 × (10 – 0.005)) × 10^{2}\\h_{1}&= 138.82 kJ/kg\\\text{Net output per kg of steam, }w_{net}&= (h_{2} – h_{3}) – (h_{1} – h_{4})\\&= (3870.5 – 2185.85) – (138.82 – 137.82)\\w_{net}&= 1683.65 kJ/kg\\\text{Mass flow rate of steam,} m_{s}&= \frac{50 × 10^{3}}{1683.65} = 29.69 kg/s\end{aligned}\\\text{By heat balance on condenser, for mass flow rate of water being }m_{w}\text{ kg/s.}\\\begin{aligned}(h_{3} – h_{4}) × m_{s}&= m_{w} · C_{\text{p, w}} (T_{\text{w, out}} – T_{\text{w, in}})\\29.69 × (2185.85 – 137.82)&= m_{w} × 4.18 (15)\\m_{w} &= 969.79 kg/s\\\text{The heat added per kg of steam }q_{add}&= (h_{2} – h_{1}) = 3731.68 kJ/kg\\\text{Thermal efficiency}&= \frac{w_{net}}{q_{add}} = \frac{1683.65}{3731.68} = \text{0.4512 or 45.12 }\%\\\text{Ratio of heat supplied and rejected}&= \frac{(h_{2} – h_{1})}{(h_{3} – h_{4})} = 1.822\\\text{Mass of flow rate of steam}&= \textbf{29.69 kg/s}\\\text{Mass flow rate of condenser cooling water}&= \textbf{969.79 kg/s}\\\text{Thermal efficiency}&= \textbf{45.12 }\pmb{\%}\\\text{Ratio of heat supplied and rejected}&= \textbf{1.822}\end{aligned}\)
