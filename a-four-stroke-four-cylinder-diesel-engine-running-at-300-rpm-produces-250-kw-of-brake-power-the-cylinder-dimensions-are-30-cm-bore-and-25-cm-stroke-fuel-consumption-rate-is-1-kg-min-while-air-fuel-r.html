Guest Question.

A four stroke four cylinder diesel engine running at 300 rpm produces 250 kW of brake power. The cylinder dimensions are 30 cm bore and 25 cm stroke. Fuel consumption rate is 1 kg/min while air fuel ratio is 10. The average indicated mean effective pressure is 0.8 MPa. Determine indicated power, mechanical efficiency, brake thermal efficiency and volumetric efficiency of engine. The calorific value of fuel is 43 MJ/kg. The ambient conditions are 1.013 bar, 27ºC.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Given, }D = 0.3 m, L = 0.25 m, N = 300 rpm, m_{f} = 1 kg/min, F/A = 20, P_{imep} = 0.8 MPa, \text{Brake power} = 250 kW\\\begin{aligned}\text{Indicated power kW}&= P_{imep} × L × A × \frac{N × 4}{60} = 0.8 × 0.25 × \frac{π}{4} × (0.3)^{2} × \frac{300}{60} × 4 × 10^{3}\\&= 282.74 kW\\\text{Mechanical efficienc}&\text{y} = \frac{\text{Brake power}}{\text{Indicated power}} = \frac{250}{282.74} = \text{0.8842 or 88.42}\%\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Brake specific fuel consumption}&= \frac{1×60}{250} = \text{0.24 kg/kW hr}\\\text{Brake thermal efficiency}&= \frac{3600}{bsfc × CV} = \frac{3600}{0.24× 43000} = \text{0.3488 or 34.88}\%\\\text{Swept volume, }V_{s} = \frac{π}{4} D^{2} L&= \frac{π}{4} × (0.30)^{2} × 0.25 = 0.01767 m^{3}\\\text{Mass of air corresponding to ab}&\text{ove swept volume, using Perfect gas equation}\\PV_{s}&= m_{a} RT\\m_{a}&= \frac{1.013 × 10^{2} × 0.017}{0.287 × 300}\\&= 0.02 kg\\\text{Volumetric efficiency , }η_{vol}&= \frac{\text{Mass of air taken/minute}}{\text{Mass corresponding to swept volume per minute}}\\\text{Mass of air taken per minute}&= 1 × 10 = kg/min\\\text{Mass corresponding to swept v}&\text{olume per minute = }0.02 × 4 × \frac{300}{2} = 12 kg/min\\\text{Volumetric efficiency}&= \frac{10}{12} = \text{0.8333 or 83.33}\%\\\text{Indicated power}&= \text{282.74 kW, Mechanical efficiency = 88.42}\%\\\text{Brake thermal efficiency}&= \textbf{34.88}\pmb{\%}\text{, Volumetric efficiency = }\textbf{83.33}\pmb{\%}\end{aligned}\)
