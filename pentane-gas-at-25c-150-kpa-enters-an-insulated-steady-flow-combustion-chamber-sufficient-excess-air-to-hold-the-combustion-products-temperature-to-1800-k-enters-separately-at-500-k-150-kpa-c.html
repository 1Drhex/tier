Solution
\(C _{5} H _{12}+8 X \left( O _{2}+3.76 N _{2}\right) \rightarrow 5 CO _{2}+6 H _{2} O +8( X -1) O _{2}+30.08 X N _{2}\)

Energy Eq.: \(Q _{ cv }+ H _{ R }= H _{ P }+ W _{ CV } ; W _{ CV }=0, Q _{ cv }=0\)

Reactants: \(C _{5} H _{12}: \overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }\) from A.9 and \(\Delta \overline{ h }_{500}\) for \(O _{2}\) and \(N _{2}\) from A.9

\(\begin{aligned}H _{ R } &=\left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }\right)_{ C 5 H 12}+8 X \Delta \overline{ h }_{ O 2}+30.08 X \Delta \overline{ h }_{ N 2} \\&=-146500+8 X 6086+30.08 \times 5911=226491 X -146500\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}H _{ P }=& 5\left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ CO 2}+6\left(\overline{ h }_{ f }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }\right)_{ H 2 O }+8( X -1) \Delta \overline{ h }_{ O 2}+30.08 X \Delta \overline{ h }_{ N 2} \\=& 5(-393522+79432)+6(-241826+62693)+8( X -1) 51674 \\&+30.08 X 48979=1886680 X -3058640\end{aligned}\)

 

Energy Eq. solve for X;

\(\begin{gathered}H _{ R }= H _{ P }=226491 X -146500=1886680 X -3058640 \\\Rightarrow X =1.754\end{gathered}\)

b) Reactants: \(P _{ in }=150 kPa , P _{ o }=100 kPa , \quad \overline{ s }_{ f }^{ o }\)
