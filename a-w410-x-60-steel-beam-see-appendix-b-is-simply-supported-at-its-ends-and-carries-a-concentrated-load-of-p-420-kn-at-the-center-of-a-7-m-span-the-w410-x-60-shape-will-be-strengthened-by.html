Guest Question.

A W410 × 60 steel beam (see Appendix B) is simply supported at its ends and carries a concentrated load of P = 420 kN at the center of a 7-m span. The W410 × 60 shape will be strengthened by adding two 250 mm wide by 16 mm thick cover plate to its flanges, as shown in Figure P9.55. Each cover plate is attached to its flange by pairs of bolts spaced at intervals of s = 250 mm along the beam span. The allowable average shear stress in the bolts is 96 MPa, and bending occurs about the z centroidal axis. Determine the minimum required diameter for the bolts.

Admin
Solution

Moment of inertia about the z axis (with cover plates):

\(\begin{aligned}&\begin{array}{lcccc}{\text { Shape }} & \begin{array}{c}I_{C} \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}d=y_{i}-\bar{y} \\( mm )\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}d^{2} A \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}I_{C}+d^{2} A \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{array} \\\hline \text { top cover plate } & 85,333.33 & 211 & 178,084,000 & 178,169,333 \\W 410 \times 60 & 216,000,000 & 0 & 0 & 216,000,000 \\\text { bottom cover plate } & 85,333.33 & -211 & 178,084,000 & 178,169,333 \\\hline\end{array}\\&\text { Moment of inertia about the } z \text { axis }\left( mm ^{4}\right)=572,338,666\end{aligned}\)

 

Maximum shear force:
V = P/2 = 420 kN/2 = 210 kN

Minimum bolt diameter
Consider top cover plate, which is held in place by two bolts:

\(\begin{aligned}&Q=(250 mm )(16 mm )(406 mm / 2+16 mm / 2)=844,000 mm ^{3} \\&q=\frac{V Q}{I}=\frac{(210,000 N )\left(844,000 mm ^{3}\right)}{572,338,666 mm ^{4}}=309.677 N / mm\end{aligned}\)

Relate the shear flow and the required bolt area with Eq. (9.14). Note that the shear flow will be transmitted by means of two fasteners.

\(\begin{aligned}&q s \leq n_{f} \tau_{f} A_{f} \\&\therefore A_{f} \geq \frac{q s}{n_{f} \tau_{f}}=\frac{(309.677 N / mm )(250 mm )}{2\left(96 N / mm ^{2}\right)}=403.225 mm ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Use the minimum required cross-sectional area of the bolt to calculate the minimum bolt diameter:

\(\begin{aligned}A_{\text {bolt }} &=\frac{\pi}{4} d^{2} \geq 403.225 mm ^{2} \\& \therefore d_{\min }=22.658 mm =22.7 mm\end{aligned}\)
