Determining Cost of Exergy Destruction

If exergy is valued at $0.08 per kW ⋅ h, determine the daily cost of electricity to operate the compressor and the daily cost of exergy destruction in each component. Let \(T_{0}=273 K \left(0^{\circ} C \right)\), which corresponds to the temperature of the outside air.
