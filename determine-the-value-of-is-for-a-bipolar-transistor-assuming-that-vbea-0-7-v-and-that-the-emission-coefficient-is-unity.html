Solution

Under forward active conditions,

\( I_{C}\approx IS\exp \left\lgroup\frac{qV_{BE} }{NFkT} \right\rgroup \)

If the collector current is 1 mA with \( V_{BEA} =0.7V \), then

\( IS=\frac{I_{C} }{\exp \left\lgroup\frac{qV_{BE} }{NFkT} \right\rgroup}=\frac{10^{-3}A }{\exp \left\lgroup\frac{0.7V }{(1.0)(0.026V)} \right\rgroup}=2.0\times 10^{-15}A \)
