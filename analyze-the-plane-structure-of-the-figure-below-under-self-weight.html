Guest Question.

Analyze the plane structure of the figure below under self-weight.

Admin
Solution

The assembly process is similar to that for matrix analysis of bar structures (Figure 4.10). The global system of equations has the following form

 

\(\begin{bmatrix} & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & 5 &6 \\ 1 & \left( K _{11}^{(1)}+ K _{11}^{(2)}\right) & K _{13}^{(2)} & 0 & K _{12}^{(1)} &\left( K _{13}^{(1)}+ K _{12}^{(2)}\right) &0 \\ 2 & & \begin{aligned}&\left( K _{33}^{(2)}+ K _{11}^{(3)}\right. \\&\left.\quad+ K _{11}^{(4)}\right)\end{aligned} & K _{13}^{(4)} & 0 & \left( K _{32}^{(2)}+ K _{12}^{(3)}\right) &\left( K _{13}^{(3)}+ K _{12}^{(4)}\right) \\ 3 & & & K _{33}^{(4)} & 0 & 0 &K _{32}^{(4)} \\ 4 & & & & K _{22}^{(1)} & K _{23}^{(1)} &0 \\ &Symm.\\5 & & & & & \begin{aligned}&\left( K _{33}^{(1)}+ K _{22}^{(2)}\right. \\&\left.\quad+ K _{22}^{(3)}\right)\end{aligned} &K _{23}^{(3)} \\ 6 & & & & & &\left( K _{22}^{(4)}+ K _{33}^{(3)}\right) \end{bmatrix} \left \{ \begin{aligned}&a _{1}\\&a _{2}\\&a _{3}\\&a _{4}\\&a _{5}\\&a _{6}\end{aligned} \right \}=\left\{\begin{array}{c}\left( r _{1}+ f _{1}^{(1)}+\right. \\\left. f _{1}^{(2)}\right) \\\left( f _{3}^{(2)}+ f _{1}^{(3)}+\right. \\\left.+ f _{1}^{(4)}\right) \\f _{3}^{(4)} \\r _{4}+ f _{2}^{(1)} \\\left( f _{3}^{(1)}+ f _{2}^{(2)}+\right. \\\left.+ f _{2}^{(3)}\right) \\f _{3}^{(3)}+ f _{2}^{(4)}\end{array}\right\}\)

 

where \(K _{i j}^{(e)}\) is obtained from Eq.(4.63) and \(f _{i}^{(e)}\) from Eq.(4.66) with \(b_{x}=0\) and \(b_{y}=-\rho g\). In both cases t = 1 should be taken.

 

\(K _{i j}^{(e)}=\iint_{A^{(e)}} \frac{1}{2 A^{(e)}}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}b_{i} & 0 & c_{i} \\0 & c_{i} & b_{i}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}d_{11} & d_{12} & 0 \\d_{21} & d_{22} & 0 \\0 & 0 & d_{33}\end{array}\right] \frac{1}{2 A^{(e)}}\left[\begin{array}{cc}b_{j} & 0 \\0 & c_{j} \\c_{j} & b_{j}\end{array}\right] t d A\) (4.63a)

 

\(K _{i j}^{(e)}=\left(\frac{t}{4 A}\right)^{(e)}\left[\begin{array}{l}b_{i} b_{j} d_{11}+c_{i} c_{j} d_{33} b_{i} c_{j} d_{12}+b_{j} c_{i} d_{33} \\c_{i} b_{j} d_{21}+b_{i} c_{j} d_{33} b_{i} b_{j} d_{33}+c_{i} c_{j} d_{22}\end{array}\right]\) (4.63b)

 

\(f _{b_{i}}^{(e)}=\frac{(A t)^{(e)}}{3}\left\{\begin{array}{l}b_{x} \\b_{y}\end{array}\right\}\) (4.66)

 

The above system can be solved in the usual way by eliminating the rows and columns corresponding to the prescribed displacements \(a _{1} \text { and } a _{4}\). Once the nodal displacements have been obtained the corresponding reaction vectors \(r _{1} \text { and } r _{4}\) can be computed.

The constant strains and stresses within each element can be found “a posteriori” from the known nodal displacements by Eqs.(4.36) and (4.41). The reader is encouraged to repeat this exercise by him/herself.

 

\(\varepsilon= B a ^{(e)}\) (4.36)

 

\(\sigma= D \varepsilon= DBa ^{(e)}\) (4.41)
