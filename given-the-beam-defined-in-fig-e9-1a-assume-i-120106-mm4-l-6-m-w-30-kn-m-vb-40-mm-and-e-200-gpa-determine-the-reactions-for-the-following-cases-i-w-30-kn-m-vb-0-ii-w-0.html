Guest Question.

Given: The beam defined in Fig. E9.1a. Assume \(I=120(10)^{6}mm^{4}\), L = 6 m, w = 30 kN/m, \(v_{B}=40\) mm, and E = 200 GPa

Determine: The reactions for the following cases:

(i) w = 30 kN/m, \(v_{B}=0\)

(ii) w = 0, \(v_{B}=40\) mm

(iii) w = 30 kN/m, \(v_{B}=40\) mm

Admin
Solution

The beam is indeterminate to the first degree. We work with the primary structure shown below (Fig. E9.1b).

 

Applying the external loading and the unit load results in the following deflected shapes (Figs. E9.1c and E9.1d):

 

The deflection terms are given in Table 3.1.

 

\(\Delta _{B,0}=\frac{wL^{4}}{8EI}\downarrow\)

 

\(\delta _{BB}=\frac{L^{3}}{3EI}\uparrow\)

 

Then

 

\(+\uparrow \; \; \; \left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}=\Delta _{B,0}+\delta _{BB}R_{B}\)

 

\(\Downarrow\)

 

\(\left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}=-\frac{wL^{4}}{8EI}+\frac{L^{3}}{3EI}R_{B}\; \; \therefore R_{B}=\frac{\left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}+(wL^{4}/8EI)}{(L^{3}/3EI)}\)

 

Case (i): For \(\left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}=0\)

 

\(R_{B}=((wL^{4}/8EI)/(L^{3}/3EI))=\frac{3}{8}wL=\frac{3}{8}(30)(6)=67.5KN\uparrow\)

 

Knowing the value of \(R_{B}\), we determine the remaining reactions by using the static equilibrium equations.

 

\(\sum F_{y}=0\; \; R_{A}=\frac{5}{8}wL=\frac{5}{8}(30)(6)=112.5KN\uparrow\)

 

\(\sum M_{@A}=0\; \; M_{A}=\frac{wL^{2}}{8}=135\) KNm counterclockwise

 

Case (ii): For \(w=0, \left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}=-v_{B}\)

 

\(R_{B}=\frac{(-v_{B})}{(L^{3}/3EI)}=-\frac{3EI}{L^{3}}v_{B}=\frac{3(200)(10)^{6}120(10)^{-6}}{(6)^{3}}(0.040)=-13.33KN\; \therefore \; R_{B}=13.33KN\downarrow\)

 

The reactions are

 

\(\sum F_{y}=0\; \; R_{A}=\frac{3EI}{L^{3}}v_{B}=13.3KN\uparrow\)

 

\(\sum M_{@A}=0\; \; M_{A}=\frac{3EI}{L^{2}}v_{B}=80\) KNm counterclockwise

 

Case (iii): For \(w\neq 0\) and \(\left.\begin{matrix}\Delta _{B}\end{matrix}\right|_{actual}=0\)

 

\(R_{B}=\frac{-v_{B}+(wL^{4}/8EI)}{(L^{3}/3EI)}=+\frac{3}{8}wL-\frac{3EI}{L^{3}}v_{B}=67.5-13.33=54.2KN\uparrow\)
