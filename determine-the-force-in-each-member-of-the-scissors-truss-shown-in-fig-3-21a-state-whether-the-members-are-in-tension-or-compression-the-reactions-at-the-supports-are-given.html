Guest Question.

Determine the force in each member of the scissors truss shown in Fig. \( 3-21 (\mathrm{a})\). State whether the members are in tension or compression. The reactions at the supports are given.

Admin
Solution

The truss will be analyzed in the following sequence:

Joint E, Fig. 3-21 b. Note that simultaneous solution of equations is avoided by the \(x, y\) axes orientation.

\( \begin{array}{c} +\nearrow \Sigma F_{y}=0 ; \quad 191.0 \cos 30^{\circ}-F_{E D} \sin 15^{\circ}=0 \\ F_{E D}=639.1 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{C}) \\ +\searrow \Sigma F_{x}=0 ; \quad 639.1 \cos 15^{\circ}-F_{E F}-191.0 \sin 30^{\circ}=0 \\ F_{E F}=521.8 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{T}) \end{array} \)

Joint D, Fig. 3-21c

\( \begin{array}{lll} +\swarrow \Sigma F_{x}=0 ; & -F_{D F} \sin 75^{\circ}=0 & F_{D F}=0 \\ +\nwarrow \Sigma F_{y}=0 ; & -F_{D C}+639.1=0 & F_{D C}=639.1 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{C}) \end{array} \)

Joint C, Fig. 3-21d

\( \begin{array}{cc} \stackrel{+}{\rightarrow} \Sigma F_{x}=0 ; & F_{C B} \sin 45^{\circ}-639.1 \sin 45^{\circ}=0 \\ & F_{C B}=639.1 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{C}) \\ +\uparrow \Sigma F_{y}=0 ; & -F_{C F}-175+2(639.1) \cos 45^{\circ}=0 \\ & F_{C F}=728.8 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{T}) \end{array} \)

Joint B, Fig. 3-21e

\( \begin{array}{c} +\nwarrow \Sigma F_{y}=0 ; \quad F_{B F} \sin 75^{\circ}-200=0 \\ F_{B F}=207.1 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{C}) \\ +\swarrow \Sigma F_{x}=0 ; \quad 639.1+207.1 \cos 75^{\circ}-F_{B A}=0 \\ F_{B A}=692.7 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{C}) \end{array} \)

Joint A, Fig. 3-21f

\( \begin{array}{c} \stackrel{+}{\rightarrow} \Sigma F_{x}=0 ; \quad F_{A F} \cos 30^{\circ}-692.7 \cos 45^{\circ}-141.4=0 \\ F_{A F}=728.9 \mathrm{lb}(\mathrm{T}) \\ +\uparrow \Sigma F_{y}=0 ; \quad 125.4-692.7 \sin 45^{\circ}+728.9 \sin 30^{\circ}=0 \end{array} \)

Notice that since the reactions have been calculated, a further check of the calculations can be made by analyzing the last joint \(F\). Try it and find out.
