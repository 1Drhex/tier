Guest Question.

Let the transfer function of a low-pass Chebyshev filter of order n = 3

\(H_{\alpha }(s)=\frac{0.4431}{(s+0.4606)(s+0.2303-j0.9534)(s+0.2303+j0.9534)} \)

Find:
a. The frequency response of \(H_{α}(s)\) (magnitude-phase).
b. The equivalent to the impulse invariant method digital filter (magnitude– phase of H(z))
c. The impulse response \(h_{α}(t)\) of \(H_{α}(s)\).
d. The impulse response h[nT] of the digital filter.
e. The filter difference equation
f. The realization of the digital filter, using the Parallel form, for T = 1sec.

Admin
Solution

a. To find the frequency response of \(H_{α}(s),\) we substitute s = jω, which yields:

\(H_{\alpha }(j\omega)=\frac{0.4431}{(j\omega +0.4606)(j\omega+0.2303-j0.9534)(j\omega+0.2303+j0.9534)} \)(1)

The magnitude \(|H_{α}(jω)|\) is:

\(|H_{\alpha }(j\omega)|=\frac{0.4431}{|jw+0.4606||0.2303+j(w-0.9534)||0.2303+j(w+0.9534)|}\Rightarrow\)
\(|H_{\alpha }(j\omega)|=\frac{0.4431}{\sqrt{(0.4606)^{2}+\omega ^{2}}\sqrt{(0.2303)^{2}+(\omega -0.9534)^{2}}\sqrt{(0.2303)^{2}+(\omega +0.9534)^{2}} } \Rightarrow\) (2)
\(|H_{\alpha }(j\omega)|=\frac{0.4431}{\sqrt{0.2122+\omega ^{2}}\sqrt{0.05303+(\omega -0.9534)^{2}}\sqrt{0.05303+(\omega +0.9534)^{2}} }\)

The magnitude \(|H_{α}(jω)|\) in dB is:

\(20 \log |H_{\alpha }(j\omega)|=-7.07-20 \log \sqrt{0.2122+\omega ^{2}}-20 \log \sqrt{0.05303+(\omega -0.9534)^{2}}-20 \log \sqrt{0.05303+(\omega +0.9534)^{2}} \)(3)

and \(Arg(H_{α}(jω))\) is:

\(Arg (H_{\alpha }(j\omega))=-\tan ^{-1}\frac{\omega }{0.4606}- \tan ^{-1}\frac{\omega -0.9534}{0.2303}- \tan ^{-1}\frac{\omega +0.9534}{0.2303}\) (4)

NOTE: Another way of the derivation of magnitude and phase of \(H_{α}(jω)\) would be:

\(H_{\alpha }(j\omega)=\frac{0.4431}{(0.4431-0.9212\omega ^{2})+j(1.1742\omega -\omega ^{3})}\Rightarrow\)
\(\left. \begin{matrix} |H_{\alpha }(j\omega)|=\frac{0.4431}{\sqrt{(0.4431-0.9212\omega ^{2})^{2}+(1.1742\omega -\omega ^{3})^{2}} } \\ Arg (H_{\alpha }(j\omega))=-\tan ^{-1}\frac{1.1742\omega -\omega ^{3}}{0.4431-0.9212\omega ^{2}} \end{matrix} \right\}\) (5)

The magnitude and phase diagram of \(H_{α}(s) \)are illustrated in the following figures:

\(H_{\alpha }(s)=\frac{0.4431}{(s+0.4606)(s+0.2303-j0.9534)(s+0.2303+j0.9534)}\)
\(=\frac{0.4431}{(s+0.4606)[(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}]}\Rightarrow\)
\(H_{\alpha }(s)=\frac{k_{1}}{s+0.4606}+\frac{k_{2}s+k_{3}}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}\)

where \(k_{1}=\underset{s\rightarrow -0.4606 }{\lim} H_{\alpha }(s)(s+0.4606)=0.4606\) and
\(k_{1}[(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}+(k_{2}s+k_{3})(s+0.4606)]\equiv 0.4431\Rightarrow\)
\(k_{1}+k_{2}=0\Rightarrow k_{2}=-k_{1}=-0.4606\)
\(0.4606(k_{1}+k_{2})+k_{3}=0\Rightarrow k_{3}=0\)

Thus:

\(H_{\alpha }(s)=\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}-\frac{0.4606s}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}\) (6)

The transfer function of the digital filter of the impulse invariant method is:

\(H(z)=Z[H_{\alpha }(s)]\overset{(6)}{=} Z[\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}-\frac{0.4606s}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}]\Rightarrow\)

\(H(z)=Z[\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}]-Z[\frac{0.4606s}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}]\) (7)
But \(Z[\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}]=\frac{0.4606}{1-z^{-1}e^{-0.4606T}}=\frac{z.0.4606}{z-e^{-0.4606T}}\) and
\(Z[\frac{0.4606s}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}]=Z[\frac{0.4606[s+0.2303-0.2303]}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}]\)
\(=0.4606Z[\frac{s+0.2303}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}} ]-0.11128Z[\frac{0.9534}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}} ]\)
\(=0.4606\frac{z^{2}-ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)}{z^{2}-2ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}}-0.11128\frac{ze^{-0.2303T}\sin (0.9534T)}{z^{2}-2ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}}\)

Thus,

\(\overset{(7)}{\Rightarrow }H(z)=0.4606[\frac{z}{z-e^{-0.4606T}}-\frac{z^{2}-ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)}{z^{2}-2ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}}+0.2416\frac{ze^{-0.2303T}\sin (0.9534T)}{z^{2}-2ze^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}} ]\) (8)

The requested frequency response of the digital filter is obtained by substituting \(Z = e^{jωT}\)

\(\overset{(8)}{\Rightarrow }H(e^{j\omega })=0.4606[\frac{e^{j\omega T}}{e^{j\omega T}-e^{-0.4606T}}-\frac{e^{j2\omega T}-e^{j\omega T}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)}{e^{j2\omega T}-2e^{j\omega T}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}}+0.2416 \frac{e^{j\omega T}e^{-0.2303T}\sin (0.9534T) }{e^{j2\omega T}-2e^{j\omega T}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534T)+e^{-0.4606T}} ]\) (9)

c. The impulse response \(h_{α}(t) \)of \(H_{α}(s)\) is:

\(h_{\alpha }(t)=L^{-1}\left\{H_{\alpha }(s)\right\}\overset{(6)}{=}L^{-1}\left\{\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}-\frac{0.4606s}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}} \right\}\)
\(=L^{-1}\left\{\frac{0.4606}{s+0.4606}-0.4606\frac{s+0.2303}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}}+0.11128\frac{0.9534}{(s+0.2303)^{2}+(0.9534)^{2}} \right\} \Rightarrow\)
\(h_{\alpha }(t)=0.4606[e^{-0.4606t}-e^{-0.2303t}\cos (0.9534t)+0.2416e^{-0.2303t}\sin (0.9534t)]\) (10)

d. The impulse response of the digital filter is:

\(h[nT]=Z[h_{\alpha }(t)]\overset{(10)}{=}0.4606[e^{-0.4606nT}-e^{-0.2303nT}\cos (0.9534nT)+e^{-0.2303nT}\sin (0.9534nT)]\) (11)

h[nT] can also be derived from the inverse z-transform of H(z) (Eq. 8), that is \(h[nT] = Z^{−1}[H(z)].\)
e. The difference equation of the filter is derived as follows:

\(H(z)=\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}\overset{(8)}{=}0.4606[\frac{1}{1-e^{-0.4606T}z^{-1}}-\frac{1-z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)+z^{-2}e^{-0.4606T}}+0.2416\frac{z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)+z^{-2}e^{-0.4606T}} ]\)
\(\Rightarrow H(z)=0.4606[H_{1}(z)+H_{2}(z)+H_{3}(z)]\)
\(=0.4606[\frac{Y_{1}(z)}{X(z)}+\frac{Y_{2}(z)}{X(z)}+\frac{Y_{3}(z)}{X(z)}]=\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}\) (12)

where

\(Y(z)=(Y_{1}(z)+Y_{2}(z)+Y_{3}(z)).0.4606\) (13)

We can see that the digital filter consists of three subsystems connected in parallel. Each of them has a difference equation:

\(\frac{Y_{1}(z)}{X(z)}=\frac{1}{1-e^{-0.4606T}z^{-1}}\Rightarrow (1-e^{-0.4606T}z^{-1})Y_{1}(z)=X(z)\Rightarrow\)
\(Y_{1}(z)=X(z)+e^{-0.4606T}z^{-1}Y_{1}(z)\overset{I.Z.T}{\Rightarrow }\)

\(y_{1}[nT]=x[nT]+e^{-0.4606T}y_{1}[(n-1)T]\) (14)
\(\frac{Y_{2}(z)}{X(z)}=\frac{1-z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)+z^{-2}e^{-0.4606T}}\)
\(Y_{2}(z)=-X(z)+z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)z^{-1}X(z)+2e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)z^{-1}Y_{2}(z)-e^{-0.4606}z^{-2}Y_{2}(z)\overset{I.Z.T}{\Rightarrow }\)(15)

\(y_{2}[nT]=-x[nT]+e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)x[(n-1)T]+2e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)y_{2}[(n-1)T]-e^{-0.4606}y_{2}[(n-2)T]\)
\(\frac{Y_{3}(z)}{X(z)}=0.2416\frac{z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\sin (0.9534T)}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)+z^{-2}e^{-0.4606T}} \Rightarrow\)
\(Y_{3}(z)=0.416e^{-0.2303T}z^{-1}X(z).\sin (0.9534T)+2e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)z^{-1}Y_{3}(z)-e^{-0.4606T}z^{-2}Y_{3}(z)\overset{I.Z.T}{\Rightarrow } \)(16)
\(y_{3}[nT]=0.2416e^{-0.2303T}x[(n-1)T]\sin (0.9534T)+2e^{-0.2303T}\cos (0.9534t)y_{3}[(n-1)T]-e^{-0.4606T}y_{2}[(n-2)T]\)

f. Relations (14), (15), (16) for T = 1sec yield:

(14)\(\Rightarrow y_{1}[n]=x[n]+0.6309y_{1}[n-1]\Rightarrow\)
\(\frac{Y_{1}(z)}{X(z)}=\frac{1}{1-0.6309z^{-1}}\) (17)
(15)\(\Rightarrow y_{2}(n)=-x(n)+0.4598x(n-1)+-.9196y_{2}(n-1)-0.6309y_{2}(n-2)\)
\(\frac{Y_{2}(z)}{X(z)}=\frac{-(1-0.4598z^{-1})}{1-0.9196z^{-1}+0.6309z^{-2}}\) (18)
(16)\(\Rightarrow y_{3}(n)=0.1565x(n-1)+0.9196y_{3}(n-1)-0.6309y_{3}(n-2)\)
\(\frac{Y_{3}(z)}{X(z)}=\frac{0.1565z^{-1}}{1-0.9196z^{-1}+0.6309z^{-2}}\) (19)
