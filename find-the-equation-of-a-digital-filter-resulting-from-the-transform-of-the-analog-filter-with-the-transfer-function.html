Guest Question.

Find the equation of a digital filter resulting from the transform of the analog filter with the transfer function

\(H(s)=\frac{s+1}{(s+1)^{2}+4} \)

applying the pole-zero match method for T=1sec.

Admin
Solution

H(s) can be written as:

\(H(s)=\frac{s+1}{(s+1+2j)(s+1-2j)}\) (1)

Because the denominator degree is n = 2 and the numerator degree is m = 1, it is assumed to have n-m = 2-1 = 1 zero at infinity. Note that the H(s) zeros that tend to infinity are substituted by z = –1 in the z-plane. So, H(z) will be:

\(H(z)=\frac{(1-z^{-1}e^{-j\pi })^{*}(1-z^{-1}e^{-T})}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-T}\cos (2T)+e^{-2T}z^{-2}} \) (2)

Explanation of the term explanation of the term \(1–z^{-1}e^{-jp}\): The frequency of the s-plane, \(ω_{s} → ∞\) (when zero gets to infinity) is equivalent to the frequency of the z-field, so that the match of the zero at infinity with the zero in the z-plane is:

\(1-z^{-1}e^{j\omega _{s}T}=1-z^{-1}e^{j\frac{\omega }{T} T}=1-z^{-1}e^{j\pi }=1-z^{-1}(-1)=1+z^{-1}\) (3)
\(\overset{ (2),(3)}{\Rightarrow }H(z)=\frac{(1+z^{-1})(1-z^{-1}e^{-T})}{1-2z^{-1}e^{-T}\cos (2T)+e^{-2T}z^{-2}}\Rightarrow H(z)=\frac{(z+1)(z-e^{-T})}{z^{2}-2ze^{-T}\cos (2T)+e^{-2T}}\) (4)

For T = 1sec, it is:

\((4)\Rightarrow H(z)=\frac{(z+1)(z-0.3678)}{z^{2}+0.306z+0.135} \) (5)

but,

\(H(z)=\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}\overset{(5)}{=}\frac{z^{2}+0.6322z-0.3678}{z^{2}+0.306z+0.135}\) (6)

Equation (6) must be multiplied by the dc gain correction factor, i.e.,

\(H(z)=\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}=k_{dc}.\frac{z^{2}+0.6322z-0.3678}{z^{2}+0.306z+0.135} \) (7)

where \(k_{dc}\) is calculated as follows:

\(k=\frac{\underset{t\rightarrow \infty }{\lim }y(t) }{\underset{k\rightarrow \infty }{\lim }y(k)}\) (8)
\(\underset{t\rightarrow \infty }{\lim }y(t)\overset{L.T}{=}\underset{s\rightarrow 0 }{\lim }s(\frac{1}{s} )H(s)=\underset{s\rightarrow 0 }{\lim }H(s)=0.2\)
\(\underset{k\rightarrow \infty }{\lim }y(k)\overset{Z.T}{=}\underset{z\rightarrow 1 }{\lim }(z-1)\frac{z}{z-1}H(z)=\underset{z\rightarrow 1 }{\lim }zH(z)=\frac{1.2644}{1.441}=0.877 \)
\((8)\Rightarrow k=\frac{0.2}{0.877}=0.23\)
\((7)\Rightarrow H(z)=\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}=0.23\frac{1+0.6322z^{-1}-0.3678z^{-2}}{1+0.306z^{-1}+0.135z^{-2}}\Rightarrow \)
\((1+0.306z^{-1}+0.135z^{-2})Y(z)=0.23(1+0.6322z^{-1}-0.3678z^{-2})X(z)\Rightarrow \)
\(Y(z)=-0.135z^{-2}Y(z)-0.306z^{-1}Y(z)+0.23X(z)+0.1454z^{-1}X(z)-0.084z^{-2}X(z)\)

Therefore, the digital filter of the difference equation will be:

\(y[k]=-0.135y[k-2]-0.306y[k-1]+0.23x[k]+0.1454x[k-1]-0.0846x[k-2]\) (9)
