Guest Question.

Minimum Wage and Inflation
The federal minimum wage was $1 per hour in 1960, $6.55 in 2008, and $7.25 in 2009. Did the pay of a minimum-wage worker really increase?

Admin
Solution

This is the same as asking if the costs per hour increased faster than inflation. From Exhibit 15.1:

\(\text { Hourly Wage }_{2008}=(1 / 29.8) 210.2=\$ 7.05\)
