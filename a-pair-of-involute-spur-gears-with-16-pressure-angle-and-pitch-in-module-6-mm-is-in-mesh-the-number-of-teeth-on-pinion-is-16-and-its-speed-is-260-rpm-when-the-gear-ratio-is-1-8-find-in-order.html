Guest Question.

A pair of involute spur gears with 16째 pressure angle and pitch in module 6 mm is in mesh. The number of teeth on pinion is 16 and its speed is 260 rpm. When the gear ratio is 1.8, find in order that the interference is just avoided, (i) the addenda on pinion and the gear wheel, (ii) the length of path of contact (iii) the maximum velocity sliding of teeth on either side of the pitch point. [PTU, Dec, 2007

Admin
Solution

Given: \( \alpha=16^{\circ}, m=6 mm , z=16, N =260 rpm \),

\( i=1.8 \).

\( z_{2}=16 \times 1.8=28.8 \simeq 29 \).

\( d_{1}=m z_{1}=6 \times 16=96 mm , r_{1}=48 mm \).

\( d_{2}=m z_{2}=6 \times 29=174 mm , r_{2}=87 mm \).

(i) \( r_{a 1}=r_{1}+h_{a 1}=48+h_{a 1} \).

\( r_{b 1}=r_{1} \cos \alpha=48 \cos 16^{\circ}=46.14 mm \).

\( r_{a 2}=r_{2}+h_{a 2}=87+h_{a 2} \).

\( r_{b 2}=r_{2} \cos \alpha=87 \cos 16^{\circ}=83.63 mm \).

Length of path of approach, \( L_{a}=\sqrt{r_{a 2}^{2}-r_{b 2}^{2}}-r_{2} \sin \alpha \).

Length of path of recess, \( L_{r}=\sqrt{r_{a 1}^{2}-r_{b 1}^{2}}-r_{1} \sin \alpha \).

Maximum length of approach to avoid interference, \( \left(L_{a}\right)_{\max }=r_{1} \sin \alpha \).

Maximum length of recess to avoid interference, \( \left(L_{r}\right)_{\max }=r_{2} \sin \alpha \).

\( \therefore \quad \sqrt{\left(48+h_{a 1}\right)^{2}-(46.14)^{2}}-48 \sin 16^{\circ}=87 \sin 16^{\circ} \).

\( \left(48+h_{a 1}\right)^{2}-(46.14)^{2}=\left[(87+48) \sin 16^{\circ}\right]^{2} \).

\( =1384.66 \).

\( \left(48+h_{a 1}\right)^{2}=2513.56 \).

\( 48+h_{a 1}=59.27 \).

\( h_{a l}=11.27 mm \).

\( \sqrt{\left(87+h_{a 2}\right)^{2}-(83.63)^{2}}-87 \sin 16^{\circ}=48 \sin 16^{\circ} \).

\( \left(87+h_{a 2}\right)^{2}-(83.63)^{2}=1384.66 \).

\( h_{a 2}=4.53 mm \).

(ii) Length of path of contact, \( L_{p}=\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) \sin \alpha \).

\( =(48+87) \sin 16^{\circ}=37.21 mm \).

(iii) \( \omega_{1}=\frac{2 \pi N_{1}}{60}=\frac{2 \pi \times 260}{60}=27.227 rad / s \).

\( \omega_{2}=\frac{\omega_{1}}{1.8}=15.126 rad / s \).

\( \text { Path of recess, } L_{r}=r_{2} \sin \alpha=87 \sin 16^{\circ}=23.98 mm \).

\( \text { Path of approach, } L_{a}=r_{1} \sin \alpha=48 \sin 16^{\circ}=13.23 mm \).

Maximum velocity of sliding during approach \( =\left(\omega_{1}+\omega_{2}\right) L_{a} \).

\( =(27.227+15.126) \times 13.23 \).

\( =560.33 mm / s \).

Maximum velocity of sliding during recess \( =\left(\omega_{1}+\omega_{2}\right) L_{r} \).

\( =(27.227+15.126) \times 23.98 \).

\( =1015.62 mm / s \).
