Solution

\(V_e= \sqrt{1.4 ×287 ×252.5} = 318.5 m/s. \therefore \dot{m}= \frac{211.3}{0.287 ×252.5} \pi × 0.05^2 ×318.5= \underline{7.29 kg/s} \).
