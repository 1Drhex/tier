Guest Question.

Find the moments of inertia of the composite area shown in Fig. 9.21(a) for the x and y centroidal axes.

Admin
Solution

The composite area is made up of three rectangles. The areas, centroids, and centroidal moments of inertia are calculated in Fig. 9.21(b)–(d) from formulas given in Tables A.1 and A.2 of the Appendix. They are listed in Table (A) together with the required calculations. From the sums in the table and the parallel-axis theorem, we have

\(I_{x} = I_{x_{c}} + Ad^{2}\)

= \(1.85 × 10^{9} + 3.75 × 10^{9} = 5.60 × 10^{9}mm^{4}\)

\(I_{y} = I_{y_{c}} + Ad^{2}\)

= \(0.5 × 10^{9} + 2.4 × 10^{9} = 2.90 × 10^{9}mm^{4}\)
