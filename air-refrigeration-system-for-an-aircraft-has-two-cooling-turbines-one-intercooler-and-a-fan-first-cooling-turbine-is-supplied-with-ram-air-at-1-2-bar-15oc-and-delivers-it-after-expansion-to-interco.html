Guest Question.

Air refrigeration system for an aircraft has two cooling turbines, one intercooler and a fan. First cooling turbine is supplied with ram air at 1.2 bar, 15ºC and delivers it after expansion to intercooler at 0.9 bar for cooling the air coming from compressor at 4 bar. The cooling air from intercooler is sucked by fan and discharged to atmosphere and the cooled air from intercooler is expanded upto 1 bar in second turbine and discharged into passenger cabin. The air is exhausted from cabin at 25ºC and 10 ton of refrigeration is required in cabin. Index of compression and expansion are 1.45 and 1.30 respectively.
Determine the air mass flow rate in cabin, ram air mass flow rate, and COP of system if compressed air is cooled to 50ºC in intercooler and temperature rise in intercooler for ram air is limited to 30ºC.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Given:}\\P_{1} = P_{6} = 1.2 bar, T_{1} = T_{6} = 15ºC = 288 K, P_{7} = 0.9 bar \\ P_{3} = P_{2} = 4 bar, P_{4} = 1 bar, T_{5} = 25ºC, T_{3} = 50ºC \text{ or 323 K, }T_{8} = 30ºC\\\text{For process 1–2, n = 1.45;}\\\begin{aligned}\frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } \\T_{2}&= 288 \left\lgroup\frac{4}{1.2} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.45 – 1}{1.45} } \\T_{2}&= 418.47 K\\\text{For process 3–4, n}^{\prime} = 1.3, &\\\frac{T_{4}}{T_{3}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{4}}{P_{3}} \right\rgroup^{\left\lgroup\frac{n^{\prime} – 1}{n^{\prime}} \right\rgroup}\\T_{4}&= 323 \left\lgroup\frac{1}{4} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.3 – 1}{1.3}}\\T_{4}&= 234.57 K\\\text{Refrigeration effect in pass}&\text{enger cabin with m kg/s mass flow rate of air.}\\10 × 3.5&= m × C_{P} (T_{5} – T_{4})\\10 × 3.5&= m × 1.005 (298 – 234.57)\\m&= 0.55 kg/s\\\text{Air mass flow rate in cabin}&= \textbf{0.55 kg/s}\\\text{Let the mass flow rate of a}&\text{ir intercooler be m}_{1}\text{ kg/s then the energy balance upon intercooler yields,}\\m_{1} × C_{P} × (T_{8} – T_{7})&= m × C_{P} (T_{2} – T_{3})\\\text{During process 6 – 7, }\frac{T_{7}}{T_{6}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{7}}{P_{6}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.3 – 1}{1.3}}\\T_{7}&= 288 \left\lgroup\frac{0.9}{1.2} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.3 – 1}{1.3}} = 269.5 K\\\text{Substituting }T_{2}, T_{3}, T_{7}, T_{8}&\text{ and m in energy balance on intercooler,}\\m_{1}&= 1.005 × (303 – 269.5) = 0.55 × 1.005 × (418.47 – 323)\\m_{1}&= 1.57 kg/s\\\text{Total ram air mass flow ra}&\text{te }= m + m_{1} = 0.55 + 1.57\\&= 2.12 kg/s\\\text{Ram air mass flow rate}&= \textbf{2.12 kg/s}\\\text{Work input to compressor}&= m × C_{P} (T_{2} – T_{1})\\&= 0.55 × 1.005 (418.47 – 288)\\&= 72.12 kJ/s\\COP&= \frac{\text{Refrigeration effect}}{\text{Work input}}\\&= \frac{10×3.5}{72.12}\\COP&= 0.485\\COP&= \textbf{0.485}\end{aligned}\)
