Guest Question.

Finding the limiting voltages of an op-amp limiting circuit The adjustable limiter in Fig. 16.11(a) has \( R_{1}=15 k \Omega, R_{ F }=60 k \Omega, R_{2}=4 k \Omega, R_{3}=1 k \Omega, R_{4}=5 k \Omega, R_{5}=1 k \Omega, V_{ A }=15 V ,-V_{ B }=-15 V \), and \( V_{ D }=0.7 V \). Determine (a) the positive clamping voltage \( V_{ O (\max )} \) and the corresponding input voltage \(V_{S(\min)}\), (b) the negative clamping voltage \( V_{ O (\min )} \) and the corresponding input voltage \( V_{ S (\max )} \), and (c) the output voltage when the input voltage is \( v_{ S }=5 V \).

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}&R_{1}=15 k \Omega, R_{ F }=60 k \Omega, R_{2}=4 k \Omega, R_{3}=1 k \Omega, R_{4}=5 k \Omega, R_{5}=1 k \Omega, V_{ A }=15 V ,-V_{ B }=-15 V , V_{ D }= \\&0.7 V , \text { and } v_{ S }=5 V .\end{aligned}\)

(a) From Eq. (16.10),

\(V_{ O (\max )}=v_{ O }=V_{ D }+\left(V_{ B }+V_{ D }\right) \frac{R_{5}}{R_{4}}=V_{ D }\left(1+\frac{R_{5}}{R_{4}}\right)+V_{ B } \frac{R_{5}}{R_{4}}\) (16.10)

 

\( V_{ O (\max )}=0.7+(15+0.7) \times \frac{1 k }{5 k }=3.84 V \)

From Eq. (16.11),

\( V_{ S (\min )}=-\frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }} V_{ O (\max )}=-\frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }}\left[\frac{R_{5}}{R_{4}} V_{ B }+\left(1+\frac{R_{5}}{R_{4}}\right) V_{ D }\right] \) (16.11)

 

\( V_{ S (\min )}=-V_{ O (\max )} \frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }}=-3.84 \times \frac{15 k }{60 k }=-0.96 V \)

(b) From Eq. (16.5),

\( V_{ O ( min )}=v_{ O }=-V_{ D }-\left(V_{ A }+V_{ D }\right) \frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}}=-V_{ D }\left(1+\frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}}\right)-V_{ A } \frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}} \) (16.5)

\( V_{ O ( min )}=-0.7-(15+0.7) \times \frac{1 k }{4 k }=-4.625 V \)

From Eq. (16.6),

\(V_{ S (\max )}=-\frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }} V_{ O (\min )}=\frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }}\left[\frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}} V_{ A }+\left(1+\frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}}\right) V_{ D }\right]\) (16.6)

 

\( V_{ S (\max )}=-V_{ O (\min )} \frac{R_{1}}{R_{ F }}=4.625 \times \frac{15 k }{60 k }=1.15625 V \)

(c) From Eq. (16.8) ,

\( v_{ O }=-\frac{R_{3}}{R_{1}} v_{ S }-\frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}} V_{ A }-\left(1+\frac{R_{3}}{R_{2}}\right) V_{ D } \quad\left(\text { for } v_{ S }>V_{ S (\max )}\right) \) (16.8)

 

\( v_{ O }=-5 \times \frac{1 k }{15 k }-15 \times \frac{1 k }{4 k }-\left(1+\frac{1 k }{4 k }\right) \times 0.7=-4.958 V \)
