Guest Question.

Determine the response of the two-story shear frame of Fig. E14.11a with\( c = 2 \sqrt{km}\) due to unit impulse ground acceleration, \( \ddot{u_{g}}(t) = \delta (t).\)

Admin
Solution

\( B^{g}_{n}\) are determined by substituting m, c, and \( \phi _{n}\) :

\( B^{g}_{1}= \frac{-\psi ^{T}_{1} \mathbf{m}\iota }{2\lambda _{1} \psi ^{T}_{1} \mathbf{m} \psi _{1}+ \psi ^{T}_{1} \mathbf{c} \psi _{1} } = \sqrt {\frac{m}{k}}(0.2232 +0.7311i)\) (a.1)

\( B^{g}_{2}= \frac{-\psi ^{T}_{2} \mathbf{m}\iota }{2\lambda _{2} \psi ^{T}_{2} \mathbf{m} \psi _{2}+ \psi ^{T}_{2} \mathbf{c} \psi _{2} } = (-0.5408) \sqrt {\frac{m}{k}}\) (a.2)

\( B^{g}_{3}= \frac{-\psi ^{T}_{3} \mathbf{m}\iota }{2\lambda _{3} \psi ^{T}_{3} \mathbf{m} \psi _{3}+ \psi ^{T}_{3} \mathbf{c} \psi _{3} } = 0.0945 \sqrt {\frac{m}{k}}\) (a.3)

Using the \( B^{g}_{n}\) in Eq. (a) and \( \phi _{n}\) in Eq. (i) , \( \beta ^{g}_{1}\) and \( \gamma ^{g}_{1}\) are determined, and \( B^{g}_{2}\) and \( \gamma ^{g}_{2}\) from Eqs. as follows:
\( \beta ^{g}_{1} = Re(2 B^{g}_{1} \psi _{1} = \begin{Bmatrix} -0.0539 \\0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k}\)

\( \gamma ^{g}_{1}=Im ( 2 B^{g}_{1} \psi _{1}) = \begin{Bmatrix} 1.2828 \\ 1.4621 \end{Bmatrix}\) (b.1)

\( \beta ^{g}_{2} = Re(2 B^{g}_{2} \psi _{2} = \begin{Bmatrix} 0.0539 \\- 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k}\)

\( \gamma ^{g}_{2}= B^{g}_{2} \psi _{2} – B^{g}_{3} \psi _{3} = \begin{Bmatrix} -0.1319 \\ 0.6353 \end{Bmatrix}\) (b.2)

The response of the system is given by

\( \mathbf {u}(t) = \mathbf {u_{1}}(t) + \mathbf {u_{2}}(t)\) (c)

where u1(t) is determined by substituting \( B^{g}_{1}\) and \( \gamma ^{g}_{1}\) from Eq. (b.1) into the n = 1 term :

\( \mathbf{u_{1}}(t) = e^{-\zeta _{1} \omega _{1} t} [\begin{Bmatrix} -0.0539 \\0.4468 \end{Bmatrix} cos \omega _{1D} t – \begin{Bmatrix} 0.9828 \\-1.9671 \end{Bmatrix} sin \omega _{1D} t] \frac{m}{k}\) (d)

and \( u_{2}(t)\) is determined by substituting \( B^{g}_{2}\) and \( \gamma ^{g}_{2}\) from Eq. (b.2) :

\( \mathbf{u_{2}}(t) = – e^{-\zeta _{2} \omega _{2} t} [ \begin{Bmatrix} 0.0539 \\ – 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} cosh \omega _{2D} t – \begin{Bmatrix} – 0.1319 \\ 0.6353 \end{Bmatrix} sinh \omega _{2D} t] \frac{m}{k}\) (e)

Substituting Eqs. (d) and (e) into Eq. (c) gives the total response u(t). The individual terms [Eqs. (d) and (e)] and the total response are plotted in Fig. ; note that \( u_{2}(t)\) is plotted at a scale different from that for \( u_{1}(t).\)
The response can also be expressed in terms of the unit impulse response functions \( h_{n}(t)\) . For this purpose, give

\( \alpha ^{g}_{1}= \zeta _{1} \beta ^{g}_{1} -\sqrt{1- \zeta _{1}^{2}} \gamma ^{g}_{1} =\begin{Bmatrix} -1.2836 \\ -1.4312 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k}\) (f.1)

\( \alpha ^{g}_{2}= \zeta _{2} \beta ^{g}_{2} -\sqrt{1- \zeta _{2}^{2}} \gamma ^{g}_{2} = \begin{Bmatrix} 0.1386 \\ -0.8838 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k}\) (f.2)

The response of the system can be determined from

\( u(t) = u_{1}(t) + u_{2}(t)\) (g)

The first term u1(t) is determined by substituting αg 1 from Eq. (f.1) and \( \beta ^{g}_{1}\) from Eq. (b.1) into the n = 1 term :

\( \mathbf{u_{1}}(t)= \begin{Bmatrix} 1.0231 \\ 1.4107 \end{Bmatrix}h_{1}(t) + \begin{Bmatrix} 0.0539 \\ -0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k} \dot{h_{1}}(t)\) (h)

where \( h_{1}(t)\) and \( \dot {h_{1}}(t)\) are given by Eqs respectively. The second term \( u_{2}(t)\) in Eq. (g) is determined by substituting αg 2 from Eq. (f.2) and \( \beta ^{g}_{2}\) from Eq. (b.2) :

\( \mathbf{u_{2}}(t)= \begin{Bmatrix} -0.1739 \\ 1.1088 \end{Bmatrix}h_{2}(t) + \begin{Bmatrix} -0.0539 \\ 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} \frac{m}{k} \dot {h_{2}}(t)\) (i)

where \( h_{2}(t)\) and \( \dot {h_{1}}(t)\) are given by Eqs. respectively.
The vectors \( \beta ^{g}_{n}\) and \( \gamma ^{g}_{n}\) satisfy :

\( \beta ^{g}_{1}+ \beta ^{g}_{2}= \frac{m}{k} [ \begin{Bmatrix} -0.0539 \\ 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix}+ \begin{Bmatrix} 0.0539 \\ – 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} ]= \begin{Bmatrix} 0 \\0 \end{Bmatrix}\) (j)

\( \sum ^{N}_{n=1} \omega _{n} [ \alpha ^{g}_{n} – 2\zeta _{n} \beta ^{g}_{n}] = \omega _{1} \frac{m}{k}[ \begin{Bmatrix} -1.2836 \\-1.4312 \end{Bmatrix}- 2 \zeta _{1} \begin{Bmatrix} -0.0539 \\ 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix}]\)
\( + \omega _{2} \frac{m}{k}[ \begin{Bmatrix} 0.1386 \\ -0.8838 \end{Bmatrix} – 2 \zeta _{2} \begin{Bmatrix} 0.0539 \\ – 0.4463 \end{Bmatrix} ]= \begin{Bmatrix} -1 \\ -1 \end{Bmatrix}\) (k)

Equation (k) can be verified by substituting for ω1 and ω2 .
