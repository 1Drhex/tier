Guest Question.

The following systems are connected in series:

\(H_{1}(z)=\frac{5}{1+3z^{-1}},H_{2}(z)=1+z^{-1},H_{3}(z)=\frac{z^{-2}}{(1+2z^{-1})^{3}}\)

Determine the systems that need to be connected in parallel to lead to the same result as the connection in series.

Admin
Solution

\(H_{1}(z), H_{2}(z), H_{3}(z)\) can be given in the following form:

\(H_{1}(z)=\frac{5z}{z+3},H_{2}(z)=\frac{1+z}{z},H_{3}(z)=\frac{z}{(z+2)^{3}}\)

Connection of the above systems in series yields:

\(H(z)=H_{1}(z).H_{2}(z).H_{3}(z)=\frac{5z.(z+1)}{(z+3)(z+2)^{3}}\) (1)

Obviously, the systems to be connected in parallel will be computed by the technique of partial fraction decomposition of H(z).

\(\frac{H(z)}{z}=\frac{5.(z+1)}{(z+3)(z+2)^{3}}=\frac{A}{(z+3)}+\frac{B_{1}}{z+2}+\frac{B_{2}}{(z+2)^{2}}+\frac{B_{3}}{(z+2)^{3}}\) (2)

The coefficients of the fractions are computed next:

\(A=\frac{H(z)}{z}(z+3)|_{z=-3}=\frac{5(z+1)}{(z+2)^{3}}|_{z=-3}=\frac{5(-3+1)}{(-3+2)^{3}}=\frac{5(-2)}{(-1)^{3}}=10\)

\(B_{1}=\frac{1}{2}\frac{d^{2}}{dz^{2}}\left\{\frac{H(z)}{z}(z+2)^{3}\right\}|_{z=-2}=\frac{1}{2}\frac{d^{2}}{dz^{2}}\left\{\frac{5(z+1)}{(z+3)} \right\}|_{z=-2}\)
\(=\frac{1}{2}\frac{d}{dz}\left\{\frac{5(z+3)-5(z+1)}{(z+3)^{2}} \right\}|_{z=-2}=\frac{1}{2}\frac{d}{dz}\left\{\frac{10}{(z+3)^{2}} \right\}|_{z=-2}=\frac{1}{2}\left\{\frac{-10.2.(z+3)}{(z+3)^{4}} \right\}|_{z=-2}=-\frac{10}{(z+3)^{3}}|_{z=-2}=-\frac{10}{(-2+3)^{3}}=-10\)
\(B_{2}=\frac{d}{dz}\left\{\frac{H(z)}{z}(z+2)^{3}\right\}|_{z=-2}=\frac{d}{dz}\left\{\frac{5(z+1)}{(z+3)} \right\}|_{z=-2}=-\frac{10}{(-2+3)^{2}}=10\)
\(B_{3}=\frac{H(z)}{z}(z+2)^{3}|_{z=-2}=\frac{5(z+1)}{z+3}|_{z=-2}=\frac{5(-2+1)}{(-2+3)}=-5\)

Thus,

\(\frac{H(z)}{z}=\frac{10z}{\underbrace{z+3}_{H_{1}^{'}(z)} }+\frac{(-10)z}{\underbrace{z+2}_{H_{2}^{'}(z)} }+\frac{10z}{\underbrace{(z+2)^{2}}_{H_{3}^{'}(z)} }+\frac{(-5)z}{\underbrace{(z+2)^{3}}_{H_{4}^{'}(z)} }\) (3)

From relation (3), it is concluded that the systems that need to be connected in parallel to lead to the same result as the connection in series are:

\(H_{1}^{'}(z)=10.\frac{1}{1+3z^{-1}},H_{2}^{'}(z)=(-10).\frac{1}{1+2z^{-1}}\)
\(H_{3}^{'}(z)=10.\frac{z^{-1}}{(1+2z^{-1})^{2}},H_{4}^{'}(z)=(-5).\frac{z^{-2}}{(1+2z^{-1})^{3}}\)
