PUMPS WORKING IN PARALLEL
In , the duty point – discharge 1,350 L/min and head 67 m – was calculated for a centrifugal pump and a 150 mm diameter pipeline 950 m long supplying an open storage tank with a water level 45 m above the river. Determine the revised duty point when a second similar pump is connected in parallel into the system.

Admin
Solution

Characteristic of single pump:

94 87 80 65 50 30 Total head (m)
0 500 800 1,410 1,750 2,000 Discharge (L/min)

Determine the characteristic of two similar pumps working in parallel by doubling the discharge values for the same head. The new characteristic is:

94 87 80 65 50 30 Total head (m)
0 1,000 1,600 2,820 3,500 4,000 Discharge (L/min)
