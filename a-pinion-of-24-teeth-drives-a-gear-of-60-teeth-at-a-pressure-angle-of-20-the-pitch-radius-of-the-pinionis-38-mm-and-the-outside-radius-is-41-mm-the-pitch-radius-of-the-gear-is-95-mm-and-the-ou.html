Guest Question.

A pinion of 24 teeth drives a gear of 60 teeth at a pressure angle of 20째. The pitch radius of the pinionis 38 mm and the outside radius is 41 mm. The pitch radius of the gear is 95 mm and the outside radius is 98.5 mm. Calculate the length of action and contact ratio.

Admin
Solution

Length of path of contact,

\( L_{p}=\left(r_{a 1}^{2}-r_{b 1}^{2}\right)^{0.5}+\left(r_{a 2}^{2}-r_{b 2}^{2}\right)^{0.5}-\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) \sin \alpha \).

Here, \( r_{a 1}=41 mm , r_{a 2}=98.5 mm , r_{1}=38 mm , r_{2}=95 mm , \alpha=20^{\circ} \).

\( r_{b 1}=r_{1} \cos \alpha=38 \cos 20^{\circ}=35.7 mm \).

\( r_{b 2}=r_{2} \cos \alpha=95 \cos 20^{\circ}=811.27 mm \).

\( L_{p}=\left(41^{2}-35.7^{2}\right)^{0.5}+\left(98.5^{2}-811.27^{2}\right)^{0.5}-(38+95) \sin 20^{\circ} \).

\( =20.16+41.63-45.49 \).

\( =16.30 mm \).

Contact ratio, \( m_{c}=\frac{L_{p}}{P_{b}} \).

\( P_{b}=2 \pi \frac{r_{b 1}}{z_{1}} \).

\( =2 \pi \times \frac{35.7}{24}=11.37 mm \).

\( m_{c}=\frac{16.30}{11.34}=1.744 \).
