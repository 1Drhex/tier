Guest Question.

The gas mixture from is compressed in a reversible adiabatic process from the initial state in the sample cylinder to a volume of 0.2 L. Determine the final temperature of the mixture and the work done during the process.

Admin
Solution

From Eq.11.15

\(\begin{aligned}R _{\operatorname{mix}}=& \Sigma c _{ i } R _{ i }=0.02 \times 4.1243+0.45 \times 0.2968+0.28 \times 0.1889 \\&+0.25 \times 0.2968=0.34314 kJ / kg K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(m = PV / R _{\operatorname{mix}} T =100 \times 10^{-3} /(0.34314 \times 293.15)= 9 . 9 4 1 \times 10^{-4} kg\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}C _{ V 0 MIX }=& \Sigma c _{ i } C _{ V 0 i }=0.02 \times 10.085+0.45 \times 0.744 \\&+0.28 \times 0.653+0.25 \times 0.745=0.9056 kJ / kg K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& C _{ P 0 MIX }= C _{ V 0 MIX }+ R _{ mix }=0.9056+0.34314=1.2487 kJ / kg K \\&\rightarrow k = C _{ P 0} / C _{ V 0}=1.2487 / 0.9056=1.379\end{aligned}\)

 

The process (adiabatic and reversible) is isentropic expressed in Eq.6.32

\(\rightarrow T _{2}= T _{1}\left(\frac{ V _{1}}{ V _{2}}\right)^{ k -1}=293.15\left(\frac{1}{0.2}\right)^{0.379}= 5 3 9 . 5 K\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}{ }_{1} W _{2} &=-\Delta U _{12}=- mC _{ V 0}\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right) \\&=-9.941 \times 10^{-4} kg \times 0.9056 kJ / kg – K \times(539.5-293.15) K =- 0 . 2 2 kJ\end{aligned}\)
