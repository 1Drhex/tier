Guest Question.

A speed reducer has a 22 tooth spur pinion made of steel, driving a 60 tooth gear made of cast iron. The transmitted power is 10kW. The pinion speed is 1200rpm, module 4 and face width 50mm. Determine the contact stress.

Admin
Solution

\( N_{p}=22 \) ; steel pinion,\( N_{G}=60 \); cast iron gear, n = 1200rpm, m = 4, F = 50 mm, H = 10kW.

\( d_{p}=mN_{p} =4×22=88 \) mm

\( d_{G}=4×60=240 \)mm

\( V=\frac{0.088}{2} \times 1200\times \frac{2\pi }{60}=5.529 \) m/s

\( K^{′}_{V}=\frac{6.1+5.529}{6.1}=1.906 \)

 

\( W_{t}=\frac{10000}{5.529}=1809 \) N

From Table 7.1: Values of the elastic coefficient \( C_{P} (\sqrt{MPa} ) \) (AGMA 218.01)

for a steel pinion and cast iron gear, \( C_{p}=174(\sqrt{MPa} \)

From Eqs 7.2:

\( r_{1}=\frac{d_{P}\sin \phi }{2} \)

 

\( r_{1}=\frac{88×10^{-3}\sin 20 }{2}=0.01505 \) m

and 7.3:

\( r_{2}=\frac{d_{G}\sin \phi }{2} \)

 

\( r_{2}=\frac{240×10^{-3}\sin 20 }{2}=0.04104 \) m

From Eq. 7.1:

\( \sigma _{c}=-C_{P}\frac{K^{′}_{V}W_{t}}{F\cos \Phi }^{\downarrow }_{\rightarrow }\frac{1}{r_{1} }+\frac{1}{r_{2} } \)
