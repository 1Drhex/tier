Guest Question.

Find the irreversibility in the cooling process of the glass plate in .
In a glass factory a 2 m wide sheet of glass at 1500 K comes out of the final rollers that fix the thickness at 5 mm with a speed of 0.5 m/s. Cooling air in the amount of 20 kg/s comes in at 17째C from a slot 2 m wide and flows parallel with the glass. Suppose this setup is very long so the glass and air comes to nearly the same temperature (a co-flowing heat exchanger) what is the exit temperature?

Admin
Solution

Energy Eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} h _{\text {glass } 1}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} h _{\text {air } 2}=\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} h _{\text {glass } 3}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} h _{\text {air } 4}\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }}=\rho \dot{ V }=\rho AV =2500 \times 2 \times 0.005 \times 0.5=12.5 kg / s\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} C_{\text {glass }}\left(T_{3}- T _{1}\right)+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} C_{ Pa }\left( T _{4}- T _{2}\right)=\varnothing\)

 

\(T _{4}= T _{3}, C _{ glass }=0.80 kJ / kg K , \quad C _{ Pa }=1.004 kJ / kg K\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}T _{3} &=\frac{\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} C _{\text {glass }} T _{1}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} C _{ Pa } T _{2}}{\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} C _{\text {glass }}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} C _{ Pa }}=\frac{12.5 \times 0.80 \times 1500+20 \times 1.004 \times 290}{12.5 \times 0.80+20 \times 1.004} \\&=692.3 K\end{aligned}\)

 

To find the irreversibility we need the entropy generation term

 

Entropy Eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} s _{\text {glass } 1}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} s _{\text {air } 2}+\dot{ S }_{\text {gen }}=\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} s _{\text {glass } 3}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} s _{\text {air } 4}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ S }_{\text {gen }} &=\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }}\left( s _{3}- s _{1}\right)_{\text {glass }}+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }}\left( s _{4}- s _{2}\right)_{\text {air }} \\&=\dot{ m }_{\text {glass }} C _{\text {glass }} \ln \left( T _{3} / T _{1}\right)+\dot{ m }_{\text {air }} C _{ Pa } \ln \left( T _{4} / T _{2}\right) \\&=12.5 \times 0.8 \times \ln (692.3 / 1500)+20 \times 1.004 \times \ln (692.3 / 290) \\&=-7.732 kW / K +17.472 kW / K =9.74 kW / K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\dot{ I }=\dot{\Phi}_{\text {destruction }}= T _{o} \dot{ S }_{ gen }=298.15 K \times 9.74 kW / K = 2 9 0 4 k W\)
