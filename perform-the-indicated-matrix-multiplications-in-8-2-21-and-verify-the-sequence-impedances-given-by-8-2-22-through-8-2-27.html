Guest Question.

Perform the indicated matrix multiplications in (8.2.21) and verify the sequence impedances given by (8.2.22) through (8.2.27).

________________________

(8.2.21) : \(\left[\begin{array}{ccc}Z_{0} & Z_{01} & Z_{02} \\Z_{10} & Z_{1} & Z_{12} \\Z_{20} & Z_{21} & Z_{2}\end{array}\right]=\frac{1}{3}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a & a^{2} \\1 & a^{2} & a\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}Z_{a a} & Z_{a b} & Z_{a c} \\Z_{a b} & Z_{b b} & Z_{b c} \\Z_{a c} & Z_{c b} & Z_{c c}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a^{2} & a \\1 & a & a^{2}\end{array}\right]\)

(8.2.22) : \(Z_{0}=\frac{1}{3}\left(Z_{a a}+Z_{b b}+Z_{c c}+2 Z_{a b}+2 Z_{a c}+2 Z_{b c}\right)\)

(8.2.27) : \(Z_{21}=\frac{1}{3}\left(Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}+2 a^{2} Z_{a b}+2 a Z_{a c}+2 Z_{b c}\right)\)

 

Admin
Solution
\(\left[\begin{array}{lll}Z_{0} & Z_{01} & Z_{02} \\Z_{10} & Z_{1} & Z_{12} \\Z_{20} & Z_{21} & Z_{2}\end{array}\right]=\frac{1}{3}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}1 & 1 & 1 \\1 & a & a^{2} \\1 & a^{2} & a\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\left[\begin{array}{l}\left(Z_{a a}+Z_{a b}+Z_{a c}\right)\left(Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{a b}+a Z_{a c}\right)\left(Z_{a a}+a Z_{a b}+a^{2} Z_{a c}\right) \\\left(Z_{a b}+Z_{b b}+Z_{b c}\right)\left(Z_{a b}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{b c}\right)\left(Z_{a b}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{b c}\right) \\\left(Z_{a c}+Z_{b c}+Z_{c c}\right)\left(Z_{a c}+a^{2} Z_{b c}+a Z_{c c}\right)\left(Z_{a c}+a Z_{b c}+a^{2} Z_{c c}\right)\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(=\frac{1}{3}\left[\begin{array}{l}\left(Z_{a a}+Z_{b b}+Z_{c c}\right)+2\left(z_{a b}+Z_{a c}+Z_{b c}\right) \\\left(Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}(1+a)+Z_{a c}\left(1+a^{2}\right)+Z_{b c}\left(a+a^{2}\right) \\\left(z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}\left(1+a^{2}\right)+Z_{a c}(1+a)+Z_{b c}\left(a^{2}+a\right)\end{array}\right.\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\left(Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}\left(a^{2}+1\right)+Z_{a c}(a+1)+Z_{b c}\left(a+a^{2}\right) \\&\left(Z_{a a}+a^{3} Z_{b b}+a^{3} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}\left(a^{2}+a\right)+Z_{a c}\left(a+a^{2}\right)+Z_{b c}\left(a^{2}+a^{4}\right) \\&\left(Z_{a a}+a^{4} Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}\left(a^{2}+a^{2}\right)+Z_{a c}(2 a)+Z_{b c}\left(a^{2}+a^{4}\right)\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}\left(Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}(1+a)+Z_{a c}\left(1+a^{2}\right)+Z_{b c}\left(a+a^{2}\right) \\\left(Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a^{4} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}(2 a)+Z_{a c}\left(2 a^{2}\right)+Z_{b c}(2) \\\left(Z_{a a}+a^{3} Z_{b b}+a^{3} Z_{c c}\right)+Z_{a b}\left(a+a^{2}\right)+Z_{a c}\left(a+a^{2}\right)+Z_{b c}\left(a+a^{2}\right)\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(=\frac{1}{3}\left[\begin{array}{c}Z_{a a}+Z_{b b}+Z_{c c}+2 Z_{a b}+2 Z_{a c}+2 Z_{b c} \\Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}-a^{2} Z_{a b}-a Z_{a c}-Z_{b c} \\Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{c c}-a Z_{a b}-a^{2} Z_{a c}-Z_{b c}\end{array}\right.\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{c c}-a Z_{a b}-a^{2} Z_{a c}-Z_{b c} \\&Z_{a a}+Z_{b b}+Z_{c c}-Z_{a b}-Z_{a c}-Z_{b c} \\&Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}+2 a^{2} Z_{a b}+2 a Z_{a c}+2 Z_{b c}\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}Z_{a a}+a Z_{b b}+a^{2} Z_{c c}-a^{2} Z_{a b}-a Z_{a c}-Z_{b c} \\Z_{a a}+a^{2} Z_{b b}+a Z_{c c}+2 a Z_{a b}+2 a^{2} Z_{a c}+2 Z_{b c} \\Z_{a a}+Z_{b b}+Z_{c c}-Z_{a b}-Z_{a c}-Z_{b c}\end{array}\right]\)
