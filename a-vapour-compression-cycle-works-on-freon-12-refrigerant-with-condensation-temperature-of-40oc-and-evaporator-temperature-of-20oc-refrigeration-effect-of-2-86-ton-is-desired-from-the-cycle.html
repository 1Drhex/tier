Guest Question.

A vapour compression cycle works on Freon-12 refrigerant with condensation temperature of 40ºC and evaporator temperature of – 20ºC. Refrigeration effect of 2.86 ton is desired from the cycle. The compressor runs with 1200 rpm and has clearance volume of 2%. Considering compression index of 1.13 and following data determine.
(a) the COP
(b) the piston displacement in the reciprocating compressor used for compression.

Solution
\(\text{Given }T_{1} = – 20ºC = 253 K.\text{ During expansion (throttling) between 3 and 4,}\\h_{3} = h_{4}, h_{3} = h_{\text{f at 40ºC}} = 74.53 kJ/kg = h_{4}\\\text{Process 1–2 is adiabatic compression so}\\\begin{aligned}s_{1}&= s_{2}\\\text{and }\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad s_{1}&= s_{\text{g at – 20ºC}} = 0.7082 kJ/kg · K\\0.7082&= s_{\text{g at 40ºC}} + C_{\text{pg at 40ºC}} · \ln \left\lgroup\frac{T_{2}}{313} \right\rgroup \\0.7082&= 0.682 + 0.747 \ln \left\lgroup\frac{T_{2}}{313} \right\rgroup\\\text{Temperature after compression }T_{2}&= 324.17 K\\\text{Enthalpy after compression, }h_{2}&= h_{\text{g at 40ºC}} + C_{\text{pg at 40ºC}} · (324.17 – 313)\\&= 203.05 + (0.747 × (324.17 – 313))\\h_{2}&= 211.39 kJ/kg\\\text{Compression work required, per kg}&= h_{2} – h_{1}\\&= 211.39 – h_{\text{g at – 20ºC}}\\&= 211.39 – 178.61\\W_{c}&= 32.78 kJ/kg\\\text{Refrigeration effect during cycle, per kg}&= h_{1} – h_{4}\\&= 178.61 – 74.53\\&= 104.08 kJ/kg\\\text{Mass flow rate of refrigerant, } m&= \frac{2.86 × 3.5}{104.08}\\m&= 0.096 kg/s\\COP&= \frac{104.08}{32.78} = 3.175\\\text{Volumetric efficiency of reciprocating c}&\text{ompressor, given C = 0.02}\\η_{vol}&= 1 + C – C \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n}} \\&= 1 + 0.02 – 0.02 \left\lgroup\frac{9.61}{1.51} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{1.13}}\\η_{vol}&= 0.9171\\\text{Let piston displacement by V, }m^{3}.\qquad&\\\text{Mass flow rate, 0.096}&= \frac{V × 0.9171 × 1200}{60 × v_{\text{g at – 20ºC}}}\\0.096&= \frac{V × 0.9171 × 1200}{60 × 0.1088}\\V&= 569.45 cm^{3}\\COP&= \textbf{3.175}\\\text{Piston displacement}&= \textbf{569.45 cm}^{3}\end{aligned}\)
