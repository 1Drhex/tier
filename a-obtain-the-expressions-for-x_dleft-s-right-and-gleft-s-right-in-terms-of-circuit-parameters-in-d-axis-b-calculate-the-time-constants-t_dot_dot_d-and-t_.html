Guest Question.

(a) Obtain the expressions for \(X_{d}\left ( s \right )\) and \(G\left ( s \right )\) in terms of circuit parameters in d-axis

(b) Calculate the time constants \(T_{do}^{'},T_{do}^{''},T_{d}^{'}\) and \(T_{d}^{''}\) if \(x_{d}=1.803,x_{df}=x_{dh}=x_{ad}=1.571,x_{fh}=1.6624,x_{f}=1.8334,x_{h}=1.674,R_{f}=0.000856,R_{h}=0.00871,f_{B}=60\) Hz. Also compute \(x_{d}^{'}\) and \(x_{d}^{''}\)

Admin
Solution

(a) The expression for \(X_{d}\left ( s \right )\) can be obtained as

\(\left.\begin{matrix}\frac{\psi _{d}\left ( s \right )}{I_{d}\left ( s \right )}\end{matrix}\right|E_{fd}=0\)

 

\(=\frac{x_{d}\left ( 1+a_{1}s+a_{2}s^{2} \right )}{\left ( 1+b_{1}s+b_{2}s^{2} \right )}=x_{d}\frac{\left (1+sT_{d}^{'} \right )\left ( 1+sT_{d}^{''} \right )}{\left ( 1+T_{do}^{'} \right )\left ( 1+T_{do}^{''} \right )}\)

 

From the equivalent circuit in Fig. 3.13, we have

 

\(X_{d}=x_{a\sigma }+\frac{1}{\frac{1}{x_{ad}}+\frac{1}{\left ( x_{h\sigma }+\frac{R_{h}w_{B}}{s} \right)}+\frac{1}{\left ( x_{f\sigma }+\left ( \frac{R_{f}w_{B}}{s} \right ) \right )}}\)

 

\(=\frac{x_{d}\left ( 1+a_{1}s+a_{2}s^{2} \right )}{\left ( 1+b_{1}s+b_{2}s^{2} \right )}\)

 

where

 

\(b_{1}=\frac{\left ( x_{ad}+x_{f\sigma } \right )}{w_{B}R_{f}}+\frac{\left ( x_{ad}+x_{h\sigma } \right )}{w_{B}R_{h}}=\frac{x_{f}}{w_{B}R_{f}}+\frac{x_{h}}{w_{B}R_{h}}\)

 

\(b_{2}=\frac{1}{R_{f}R_{h}w_{B}^{2}}\left [ x_{ad}\left ( x_{f\sigma }+x_{h\sigma } \right )+x_{f\sigma }x_{h\sigma } \right ]\)

 

\(a_{1}=x_{a\sigma }b_{1}+x_{ad}\left ( \frac{x_{f\sigma }}{R_{f}w_{B}}+\frac{x_{h\sigma }}{R_{h}w_{B}} \right )\)

 

\(a_{2}=x_{a\sigma }b_{2}+\frac{x_{ad}x_{f\sigma }x_{h\sigma }}{R_{f}R_{h}w_{B}^{2}}\)

 

\(T_{do}^{'}\) and \(T_{do}^{''}\) are obtained as the negative reciprocals of the roots of the equation

 

\(1+b_{1}s+b_{2}s^{2}=0\)

 

Similarly \(T_{d}^{'}\) and \(T_{d}^{''}\) are obtained as the negative reciprocals of the roots of the equation

 

\(1+a_{1}s+a_{2}s^{2}=0\)

 

Note: The calculations are identical if in the equivalent circuit of Fig.1, \(x_{a\sigma }\) is replaced by \(x_{c}\) and \(x_{h\sigma }\) and \(x_{f\sigma }\) are replaced by \(x_{hc}^{'} and x_{fc}^{'}\) respectively.

 

\(G\left ( s \right )=\frac{\psi _{d}\left ( s \right )}{E_{fd}\left ( s \right )}\mid I_{d}=0\)

 

From the equivalent circuit of Fig. 1, we can obtain

 

\(G\left ( s \right )=\frac{R_{f}w_{B}}{x_{ad^{s}}\left ( x_{f\sigma }+\frac{R_{f}w_{B}}{s} \right )}\times\left [ \frac{1}{\frac{1}{x_{ad}}+\left ( x_{f\sigma }+\frac{R_{f}w_{B}}{s} \right )+\left ( x_{h\sigma }+\frac{R_{h}w_{B}}{s} \right )} \right ]\)

 

\(=\frac{\left ( 1+s\frac{x_{h\sigma }}{R_{h}w_{B}} \right )}{\left ( 1+b_{1}s+b_{2}s^{2} \right )}=\frac{\left ( 1+sT_{1} \right )}{\left ( 1+sT_{do}^{'} \right )\left ( 1+sT_{do}^{''} \right )}\)

 

where

\(T_{1}=\frac{x_{h\sigma }}{R_{h}w_{B}}\)

 

(b) Substituting the values, the time constants are obtained as

\(T_{d}^{'}=1.497 sec, T_{d}^{''}=0.035, sec T_{1}=0.00353 sec\)

 

\(T_{do}^{'}=6.14 sec, T_{do}^{''}=0.047 sec\)

 

The reactances, \(x_{d}^{'}\) and \(x_{d}^{''}\) are obtained by applying Eqs. (3.93) and (3.94).

 

These are \(x_{d}^{''}=0.327, x_{d}^{'}=0.442\)
