Guest Question.

A cylinder contains ethylene, \(C _{2} H _{4}\) , at 1.536 MPa, −13°C. It is now compressed in a reversible isobaric (constant P) process to saturated liquid. Find the specific work and heat transfer.

Admin
Solution

Ethylene \(C _{2} H _{4} ; \quad P _{1}=1.536 MPa = P _{2}, \quad T _{1}=-13^{\circ} C =260.2 K\)

State 2: saturated liquid, \(x _{2}=0.0\)

\(T _{ r1 }=\frac{260.2}{282.4}=0.921 \quad P _{ r1 }= P _{ r 2}=\frac{1.536}{5.04}=0.305\)

From Figs. D.1, D.2: \(Z_{1}=0.85,\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{1}\right) / RT _{ c }=0.40\)

\(\begin{aligned}& v _{1}=\frac{ Z _{1} RT _{1}}{ P _{1}}=\frac{0.85 \times 0.29637 \times 260.2}{1536}=0.042675 \\&\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{1}\right)=0.29637 \times 282.4 \times 0.40=33.5\end{aligned}\)

From Figs. D.1, D.2: \(T _{2}=0.824 \times 282.4=232.7 K\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& Z _{2}=0.05,\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{2}\right) / RT _{ c }=4.42 \\& v _{2}=\frac{ Z _{2} RT _{2}}{ P _{2}}=\frac{0.05 \times 0.29637 \times 232.7}{1536}=0.002245 m ^{3} / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{2}\right)=0.29637 \times 282.4 \times 4.42=369.9 \\&\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{1}^{*}\right)= C _{ P 0}\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)=1.5482(232.7-260.2)=-42.6\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}w _{12}=& \int Pdv = P \left( v _{2}- v _{1}\right)=1536 kPa (0.002245-0.042675) m ^{3} / kg \\&=- 6 2 . 1 k J / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}q _{12}=&\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ w _{12}=\left( h _{2}- h _{1}\right)=-369.9-42.6+33.5 \\&=- 3 7 9 k J / kg\end{aligned}\)
