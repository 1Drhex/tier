Guest Question.

A W10 × 45 simply supported beam with a span of 12 ft has a concentrated load of 10 kip and a distributed load of 1.5 kip/ft, as shown in Fig. 15.14(a). The reactions have been calculated and the moment diagram drawn by parts [Fig. 15.14(b)]. Find the deflection at the middle of the beam.

Admin
Solution

The modulus \(E = 29 × 10^{3}\) ksi for A36 steel (Table A.10 of the Appendix), and the moment of inertia \(I = 248 in.^{4}\) for the W10 × 45 (Table A.3 of the Appendix). To find the tangent deviation of B from A, we construct the M/EI diagram [Fig. 15.15(a)] and tabulate the calculations in Table (A). The calculations for the tangent deviation of M from A are shown in Fig. 15.15(b) and tabulated in Table (B). The deflection at the middle of the beam is given by Eq. (15.7):

\(\delta _{M}=-\left(\frac{t_{B/A}}{2}-t_{M/A} \right)\)
\(A^{*}\) (from A to B) \((kip-ft^{2})\)/EI \(\bar{x}_{B}\)(ft) \(\bar{x}_{B}A^{*}\) (A to B) \((kip-ft^{3})\)/EI
(1) \(\frac{12(117)}{2EI}=\frac{702}{EI}\) 4 \(\frac{2808}{EI}\)
(2) \(\frac{9(-90)}{2EI}=-\frac{405}{EI}\) 3 \(-\frac{1215}{EI}\)
(3) \(\frac{6(-27)}{3EI}=-\frac{54}{EI}\) 1.5 \(-\frac{81}{EI}\)
\(\Sigma \frac{1512}{EI}\)
\(t_{B/A}\)

 

\(A^{*}\) (from A to M) \((kip-ft^{2})\)/EI \(\bar{x}_{M}\)(ft) \(\bar{x}_{M}A^{*}\) (A to M) \((kip-ft^{3})\)/EI
(4) \(\frac{6(58.5)}{2EI}=\frac{175.5}{EI}\) 2 \(\frac{351}{EI}\)
(5) \(\frac{3(-30)}{2EI}=-\frac{45}{EI}\) 1 \(-\frac{45}{EI}\)
\(\Sigma \frac{306}{EI}\)
\(t_{B/A}\)

Substituting for \(t_{B/A}\) and \(t_{M/A}\), the sums shown in the tables, we have

\(\delta _{M}=-\left(\frac{1512}{2EI}-\frac{306}{EI} \right)=-\frac{450\ kip-ft^{3}}{EI}\)

Changing ft\(^{3}\) to in.\(^{3}\) by multiplying by \((12 in./ft)^{3} = 1728 in.^{3}/ft^{3}\), we get

\(\delta_{\max }=-\frac{450(1728) \text { kip-in. }^{3}}{E I}\)

The flexural stiffness \(EI=29\times 10^{3}kip/in.^{2}(248in.^{4})=29\times 10^{3}(248)kip-in.^{2}\);therefore,

\(\delta _{max}=-\frac{450(1728)\ kip-in.^{3}}{29\times 10^{3}(248)\ kip-in.^{2}}=-0.1081\ in.\)
