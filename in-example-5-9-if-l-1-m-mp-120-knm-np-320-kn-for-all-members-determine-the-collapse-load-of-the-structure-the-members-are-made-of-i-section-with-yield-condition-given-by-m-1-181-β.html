Guest Question.

In , if \( L = 1 m; M_{p} = 120 kNm; N_{p} = 320 kN \) for all members, determine the collapse load of the structure. The members are made of I section with yield condition given by \( m = 1.18(1-\beta) \, \text{where}\, m =\frac{ M} {M_{P}}\, \text{and}\, \beta = \frac{N} { N_{P}} \text{for}\beta > 0.15, \text{otherwise}\, m ≤ 1.\)

Admin
Solution

The first iteration gives \( P_{w} = 72\) and the axial forces in the members are AB = 64 kN (β = 0.2), BD = 60 kN (β = 0.1875), ED = 80 kN (β = 0.25) .The reduced plastic moments for the members are \( (M_{p1})_{AB}=113.28\, kNm,\,(M_{p1})_{BD}=115.05\,kNm,\,(M_{p1})_{ED}=106.2\,kNm.\)

The subscript “1” indicates the plastic moments at the end of the first iteration. The collapse load factors for the collapse mechanisms are

(i) Beam mechanism:

\(P=\frac{\left(M_{p}\right)_{A B}+2\left(M_{p}\right)_{B D}+\left(M_{p}\right)_{E D}}{6 L} .\)

(ii) Sway mechanism:

\(P=\frac{\left(M_{p}\right)_{A B}+\left(M_{p}\right)_{E D}}{2 L} .\)

(iii) Combined mechanism:

\(P=\frac{\left(M_{p}\right)_{A B}+2\left(M_{p}\right)_{B D}+3\left(M_{p}\right)_{E D}}{10 L} .\)

For the combined mechanism, the axial forces in the members are

\(N_{B D}=\frac{\left(M_{p}\right)_{E D}}{2 L}, \,\,N_{E D}=\frac{\left(M_{p}\right)_{E D}+\left(M_{p}\right)_{B D}}{3 L}, \,\,N_{A B}=2 P_{w}-N_{E D}.\)
