Guest Question.

Figure P3.18 shows a schematic description of the global carbon cycle (Li, 2009). In the figure, \(m_{A}(t)\) represents the amount of carbon in gigatons (GtC) present in the atmosphere of earth; \(m_{V}(t) \) the amount in vegetation; \(m_{S}(t)\) the amount in soil; \(m_{SO}(t)\) the amount in surface ocean; and \(m_{I D o}(t)\) the amount in intermediate and deep-ocean reservoirs. Let \(u_{E}(t)\) stand for the human generated CO2 emissions (GtC/yr). From the figure, the atmospheric mass balance in the atmosphere can be expressed as:

\( \begin{aligned} \frac{d m_{A}}{d t}(t)=& u_{E}(t)-\left(k_{O 1}+k_{L 1}\right) m_{A}(t)+k_{L 2} m_{V}(t) \\ &+k_{O 2} m_{S O}(t)+k_{L A} m_{S}(t) \end{aligned} \)

where the kâ€™s are exchange coefficients (\(yr^-1\)).
a. Write the remaining reservoir mass balances. Namely, write equations for \( \begin{aligned} \frac{d m s o(t)}{d t}, \frac{d m_{I D o}(t)}{d t}, &\frac{d m_{V}(t)}{d t}, \text { and } \frac{d m_{S}(t)}{d t} \end{aligned} \)

b. Express the system in state space form.

Admin
Solution

a.

\(\begin{array}{l}\frac{d m_{\text {SO }}}{dt}=k_{O1}m_{A}(t)-\left(k_{O 2}+k_{O 3}\right) m_{S O}(t)+k_{O4}m_{IDO}(t) \\\frac{d m_{\text {IDO }}}{d t}=k_{O 3} m_{S O}(t)-k_{O 4} m_{IDO}(t) \\\frac{d m_{V}}{d t}=k_{L 1} m_{A}(t)-\left(k_{L 2}+k_{L 3}\right) m_{V}(t) \\\frac{d m_{S}}{d t}=k_{L 3} m_{V}(t)-k_{L 4} m_{S}(t)\end{array}\)

 

b.

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}\dot{m}_{A} \\\dot{m}_{S O} \\\dot{m}_{IDO}\\\dot{m}_{V}\\\dot{m}_{S}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{ccccc}-\left(k_{01}+k_{L 1}\right) & k_{02} & 0 & k_{L 2} & k_{L 4} \\k_{02} & -\left(k_{02}+k_{03}\right) & k_{04} & 0 & 0 \\0 & k_{02} & -k_{04} & 0 & 0 \\k_{L 1} & 0 & 0 & -\left(k_{L2}+k_{L 3}\right) & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & k_{L 2} & -k_{L 4}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}m_{A} \\m_{S O} \\m_{IDO} \\m_{V}\\m_{S}\end{array}\right]+\left[\begin{array}{l}1 \\0 \\0 \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] u_{E}(t)\)

 

\(\mathbf{y}=\left[\begin{array}{lllll}1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}m_{A} \\m_{S O} \\m_{I D O} \\m_{V} \\m_{S}\end{array}\right]\)
