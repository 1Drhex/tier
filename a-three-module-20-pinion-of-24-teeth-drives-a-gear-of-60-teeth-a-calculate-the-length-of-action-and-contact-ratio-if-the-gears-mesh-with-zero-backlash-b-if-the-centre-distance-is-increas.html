Guest Question.

A three-module, 20째 pinion of 24 teeth drives a gear of 60 teeth. (a) Calculate the length of action and contact ratio, if the gears mesh with zero backlash. (b) If the centre distance is increased 0.5 mm, calculate the radii of the operating pitch circles, the operating pressure angle and the backlash produced.

Admin
Solution

Given: \( m=3 mm , \alpha=20^{\circ}, z _{1}=24, z _{2}=60 \).

(a) \( r_{1}=\frac{z_{1} m}{2}=24 \times \frac{3}{2}=36 mm \).

\( r_{2}=\frac{z_{2} m}{2}=60 \times \frac{3}{2}=90 mm \).

\( r_{b 1}=r_{1} \cos a=36 \times \cos 20^{\circ}=33.83 mm \).

\( r_{b 2}=r_{2} \cos \alpha=90 \times \cos 20^{\circ}=84.57 mm \).

\( h_{a 1}=h_{a 2}=m=3 mm \).

\( r_{a 1}=r_{1}+h_{a 1}=36=3=39 mm \).

\( r_{a 2}=r_{2}+h_{a 2}=90+3=93 mm \).

\( C =r_{1}+r_{2}=36+90=126 mm \).

Length of path of contact, \( A B=\left[r_{a 1}^{2}-r_{b 1}^{2}\right]^{0.5}+\left[r_{a 2}^{2}-r_{b 2}^{2}\right]^{0.5}-\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) \sin \alpha \).

\( =\left(39^{2}-33.83^{2}\right)^{0.5}+\left(93^{2}-84.57^{2}\right)^{0.5}-126 \sin 20^{\circ} \).

\( =14.997 mm \).

Contact ratio, \( m_{c}=\frac{A B}{p_{b}} \).

Base Pitch, \( p_{b}=2 \pi \times \frac{33.83}{24}=8.856 mm \).

\( m_{c}=\frac{14.997}{8.856}=1.693 \simeq 2 \).

(b) \( C^{\prime}=C+\Delta C=126+0.5=126.5 mm \).

\( r_{1}^{\prime}=\left[\frac{z_{1}}{z_{1}+z_{2}}\right] C^{\prime}=\left[\frac{24}{84}\right] 126.5=36.143 mm \).

\( r_{2}^{\prime}=C^{\prime}-r_{1}^{\prime}=126.5-36.143=90.357 mm \).

\( \cos \alpha^{\prime}=\frac{C \cos \alpha^{\prime}}{C^{\prime}}=\frac{126 \cos 20^{\circ}}{126.5}=0.93598 \).

\( \alpha^{\prime}=20.61^{\circ} \).

Backlash, \( B=2 C^{\prime}\left[\operatorname{inv}\left(\alpha^{\prime}\right)-\operatorname{inv}(\alpha)\right] \).

\( =2 \times 126.5\left[\operatorname{inv}\left(20.61^{\circ}\right)-\operatorname{inv}\left(20^{\circ}\right)\right] \).

\( =253[0.016362-0.014904] \).

\( =0.3689 mm \).
