Guest Question.

An engine crankshaft drives a reciprocating pump through a mechanism, as shown in Fig.2.22(a). The crank OA rotates in the counter-clockwise direction at 150 rpm. The diameter of the pump piston at F is 180 mm and OA = 175 mm, AB = 650 mm, CD = 160 mm, and DE = 600 mm.

Determine (a) the velocity of cross head E, (b) the rubbing velocities at pins A, B, C, and D having diameters of 40 mm each, and (c) the torque required at the crank to overcome a pressure of 0.35 MPa at the pump piston at F.

Admin
Solution

\( \omega=2 \pi \times \frac{150}{60}=15.708 rad / s \).

\( v_{a}=15.708 \times 0.175=2.75 m / s \).

Draw \( o a \perp O A \text { to represent } v_{a} \text { to a scale of } 1 cm =0.5 m / s \), as shown in Fig.2.22(b). From ‘a’ draw a line perpendicular to AB and from c draw another line perpendicular to BC to intersect at b. Then \( a b=v_{b a} \text { and } c b=v_{b c} \).

\( c d=c b \cdot\left(\frac{C D}{B C}\right)=5 \times \frac{160}{310}=2.58 cm \).

Draw a line perpendicular to CD from c and cut it equal to cd. From d draw a line perpendicular to DE and from o draw another line parallel to the path of motion of the slider E, to meet at e. Then \( d e=v_{e d} \text { and } o e=v_{e} \). By measurements, we have

(a) Velocity of cross-head E,

\( v_{e}=o e=2.5 cm =1.25 m / s \).

(b) Rubbing velocities,

\( \omega_{2}=15.708 rad / s ccw \text { about } O \).

\( \omega_{3}=\frac{a b}{A B}=\frac{v_{b a}}{A B}=2.9 \times \frac{0.5}{0.650}=3 rad / s ccw \text { about } A \).

\( \omega_{32}=\omega_{3}-\omega_{2}=3-15.708=-12.708 rad / s \).

Pin A:

\( \omega_{32} \times 0.020=-0.254 m / s\).

\( \omega_{3}=\frac{b a}{A B} \text { ccw about } B=2.9 \times \frac{0.5}{0.65}=3 rad / s \),

\( \omega_{4}=\frac{b c}{B C} \text { cw about } B=4 \times \frac{0.5}{0.31}=6.45 rad / s \),

\( \omega_{43}=\omega_{4}-\omega_{3}=6.45+3=9.45 rad / s \).

Pin B:

\( \omega_{43} \times 0.020=9.45 \times 0.020=0.189 m / s \).

Pin C:

\( \left(\frac{c b}{B C}\right) \times 0.020=(4 \times 0.5 / 0.31) 0.020=0.129 m / s \).

\( \omega_{5}=\frac{d e}{D E} cw \text { about } D \),

\( =0.6 \times \frac{0.5}{0.6}=0.5 rad / s\).

\( \omega_{4}=\frac{d c}{D C} cw \text { about } D \),

\( =2.6 \times \frac{0.5}{0.160}=8.125 m / s \).

\( \omega_{54}=\omega_{5}-\omega_{4}=0.5-8.125=-7.625 rad / s \).

Pin D:

\( \omega_{54} \times 0.020=7.625 \times 0.020=0.1525 m / s \).

(c) Velocity of the piston, \( v_{f}=v_{e}=2.5 \times 0.5=1.25 m / s \).

Let T be the torque required at the crank OA. Then,

\( T \omega=F v_{f} \),

\( 15.708 T=\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)(180)^{2} \times 0.35 \times 10^{6} \times 1.25 \).

T = 708.75 Nm.
