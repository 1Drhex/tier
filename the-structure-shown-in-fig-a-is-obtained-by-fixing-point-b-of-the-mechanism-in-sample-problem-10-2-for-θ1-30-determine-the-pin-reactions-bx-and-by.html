Guest Question.

The structure shown in Fig. (a) is obtained by fixing point B of the mechanism in For \(θ_{1} =30^{\circ}\), determine the pin reactions \(B_{x}\) and \(B_{y}\).

Admin
Solution

Using \(θ_{1} =30^{\circ}\), we found from geometry in that \(θ_{2} =50.66^{\circ}\).

Because the system in Fig. (a) is a structure rather than a mechanism, it has no DOF. To determine the reactions at B by the method of virtual work, we must turn \(B_{x}\) and \(B_{y}\) into active forces by removing the support at B.

Figure (b) shows the system with both \(B_{x}\) and \(B_{y}\) as active forces. In this case, angles \(θ_{1}\) and \(θ_{2}\) are independent position coordinates; consequently, the system possesses two DOF. The principle of virtual work states that at equilibrium

\(δU =W δy_{1} + W δy_{2} + B_{x} δx_{B} + By δy_{B} =0\) (a)

The virtual displacements in Eq. (a) can be related to \(δθ_{1}\) and \(δθ_{2}\) as follows:

\(y_{1}=\frac{L}{2}\cos \theta _{1}\)

 

\(\delta y_{1}=\frac{dy_{1}}{d\theta _{1}}\delta \theta _{1}=-\frac{L}{2}\sin \theta _{1}\delta \theta _{1}\) (b)

 

\(y_{2}=L\cos \theta _{1}+\frac{L}{2}\cos \theta _{2}\)

 

\(\delta y_{2}=\frac{∂y_{2}}{∂\theta _{1}}\delta \theta _{1}+\frac{∂y_{2}}{∂\theta _{2}}\delta \theta _{2}\)

 

\(\delta y_{2}=-L\sin \theta _{1} \delta \theta _{1}-\frac{L}{2}\sin \theta _{2} \delta \theta _{2}\) (c)

 

\(x_{B} = L \sin θ_{1} + L \sin θ_{2}\)

 

\(\delta x_{B}=\frac{∂x_{B}}{∂\theta _{1}}\delta \theta _{1}+\frac{∂x_{B}}{∂\theta _{2}}\delta \theta _{2}\)

 

\(δx_{B} = L \cos θ_{1} δθ_{1} + L \cos θ_{2} δθ_{2}\) (d)

 

\(y_{B} = L cos θ_{1} + L cos θ_{2}\)

 

\(\delta y_{B}=\frac{∂y_{B}}{∂\theta _{1}}\delta \theta _{1}+\frac{∂y_{B}}{∂\theta _{2}}\delta \theta _{2}\)

 

\(δy_{B} = −L \sin θ_{1} δθ_{1} − L \sin θ_{2} δθ_{2}\) (e)

Substituting Eqs. (b)–(e) into Eq. (a) and regrouping terms, we obtain

\(\delta U=\left(-\frac{W}{2}\sin \theta _{1}-W\sin \theta _{1}+B_{x}\cos \theta _{1}-B_{y}\sin \theta _{1} \right)L\delta \theta _{1}+\left(-\frac{W}{2}\sin \theta _{2}+B_{x}\cos \theta _{2}-B_{y}\sin \theta _{2} \right) L \delta \theta _{2}=0\) (f)

Because \(δθ_{1}\) and \(δθ_{2}\) are independent, Eq. (f) will be satisfied only if each of the terms in parentheses is zero; that is,

\(-\frac{3W}{2}\sin \theta _{1}+B_{x}\cos \theta _{1}-B_{y}\sin \theta _{1}=0\) (g)

and

\(-\frac{W}{2}\sin \theta _{2}+B_{x}\cos \theta _{2}-B_{y}\sin \theta _{2}=0\) (h)

Substituting \(θ_{1} =30^{\circ}\) and \(θ_{2} =50.66^{\circ}\), and solving Eqs. (g) and (h) simultaneously, we obtain

\(B_{x} =1.096W\) and \(B_{y} =0.398W\)
