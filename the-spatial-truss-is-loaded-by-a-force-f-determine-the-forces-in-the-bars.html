Guest Question.

The spatial truss is loaded by a force F. Determine the forces in the bars.

Admin
Solution

We consider the force acting in bar 9 (hinged column) as a reaction force. Now, the truss consists of j = 5 joints, m = 8 bars and r = 1 + 2 × 3 = 7 re-action forces. Thus, the
necessary condition for statical determination is fulfilled:
\(f = 3j − (m+ r) = 15 − (8 + 7) = 0 .\)
We introduce the unit vectors \(e_{1}\) to \(e_{9}\) in order to express the direc-tions of the bars and their respective components:

\(e_{1}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\left(\begin{matrix} 1 \\-4 \\ -1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{2}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\left(\begin{matrix} -1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{3}=\left(\begin{matrix} 1 \\0 \\0 \end{matrix} \right),\)

 

\(e_{4}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{26} }\left(\begin{matrix} -3 \\4 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{5}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\left(\begin{matrix} 1 \\ -4 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{6}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} } \left(\begin{matrix} -1 \\-4 \\-1 \end{matrix} \right),\)

 

\(e_{7}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\left(\begin{matrix} -1 \\0 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{8}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\left(\begin{matrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right), e_{9}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } \left(\begin{matrix} 0 \\1 \\ 1 \end{matrix} \right).\)

With the definition of all tensile forces to be positive, the equilibrium conditions at nodes I, II and III in vectorial (component) form read:
node I:

\( S_{1}e_{1}+S_{2}e_{2}-S_{3}e_{3}-Fe_{z}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{1}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{2}-S_{3}=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }S_{1}-\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }S_{2}=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{1}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{2}-F=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{S_{1}=-\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F } }, \underline{\underline{S_{2}=\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F } }, \underline{\underline{S_{3}=-F} }.\)

node II:

\( – S_{4}e_{4}+S_{5}e_{5}+S_{6}e_{6}+S_{3}e_{3}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \frac{3}{\sqrt{26} }S_{4}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{5}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{6}-F=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{4}{\sqrt{26} }S_{4}-\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }S_{5}-\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }S_{6}=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{1}{\sqrt{26} }S_{4}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{5}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }S_{6}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{S_{4}=\frac{1}{4}\sqrt{26}F } }, \underline{\underline{S_{5}=0} }, \underline{\underline{S_{6}=-\frac{3}{4}\sqrt{2}F } }.\)

node III:

\( -S_{7}e_{7}-S_{8}e_{8}-S_{9}e_{9}-S_{2}e_{2}-S_{5}e_{5}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{7}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{8}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2} \sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{9}+\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{7}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{8}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }S_{9}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{S_{7}=-\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F } }, \underline{\underline{S_{8}=-\sqrt{2}F } }, \underline{\underline{S_{9}=2\sqrt{2}F } }.\)

The equilibrium condition at nodes IV and V as well as at bearing B can be used to determine the Cartesian components of the reaction forces:
node IV :

\( C_{x}e_{x}+C_{y}e_{y}+C_{z}e_{z}+S_{8}e_{8}-S_{6}e_{6}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow C_{x}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\sqrt{2}F-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{4}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( C_{y}-\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{4}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( C_{z}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\sqrt{2}F-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{4}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{C_{x}=\frac{5}{4}F } }, \underline{\underline{C_{y}=F} }, \underline{\underline{C_{z}=\frac{5}{4}F }}.\)

node V :

\( A_{x}e_{x}+A_{y}e_{y}+A_{z}e_{z}-S_{1}e_{1}+S_{4}e_{4}+S_{7}e_{7}=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow A_{x}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2} \sqrt{2}F-\frac{3}{\sqrt{26} }\frac{1}{4}\sqrt{26}F+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( A_{y}-\frac{4}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F+\frac{4}{\sqrt{26} }\frac{1}{4}\sqrt{26}F=0,\)

 

\( A_{z}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{18} }\frac{3}{2} \sqrt{2}F+\frac{1}{\sqrt{26} }\frac{1}{4}\sqrt{26}F-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} }\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{2}F=0,\)

 

\( \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{A_{x}=-\frac{5}{4}F } }, \underline{\underline{A_{y}=F} }, \underline{\underline{A_{z}=\frac{7}{4}F }}.\)

bearing B:

\( \underline{\underline{B_{x}=0} }, \underline{\underline{B_{y}=B_{z}=-\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}S_{9}=-2F } }.\)

Remark:
• The largest force acts in bar 9.
• The magnitude of the reaction forces is \( A=\sqrt{90}F/4=2.37F , B=S_{9}=2\sqrt{2}F=2.83F\) and \(C=\sqrt{66}F/4=2.03F.\)

• C lies in the plane which also contains \(S_{6}\) and \(S_{8}.\)
