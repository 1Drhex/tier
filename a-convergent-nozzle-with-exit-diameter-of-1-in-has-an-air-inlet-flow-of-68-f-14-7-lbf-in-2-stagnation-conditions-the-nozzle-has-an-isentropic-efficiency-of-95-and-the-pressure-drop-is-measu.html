Guest Question.

A convergent nozzle with exit diameter of 1 in. has an air inlet flow of 68 F, 14.7 \(lbf / in .^{2}\) (stagnation conditions). The nozzle has an isentropic efficiency of 95% and the pressure drop is measured to 20 in. water column. Find the mass flow rate assuming compressible adiabatic flow. Repeat calculation for incompressible flow.

Admin
Solution

Convert ∆P to \(lbf / in ^{2}\)

\(\begin{aligned}&\Delta P =20 in H _{2} O =20 \times 0.03613=0.7226 lbf / in ^{2} \\& T _{0}=68 F =527.7 R \quad P _{0}=14.7 lbf / in ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Assume inlet \(V _{ i }=0 \quad P _{ e }= P _{0}-\Delta P =14.7-0.7226=13.977 lbf / in ^{2}\)

\(T _{ e }= T _{0}\left(\frac{ P _{ e }}{ P _{0}}\right)^{\frac{ k -1}{ k }}=527.7 \times\left(\frac{13.977}{14.7}\right)^{0.2857}=520.15 R\)

 

\(\begin{gathered}V _{ e }^{2} / 2= h _{ i }- h _{ e }= C _{ p }\left( T _{ i }- T _{ e }\right)=0.24 \times(527.7-520.15)=1.812 Btu / lbm \\V _{ e ac }^{2} / 2=\eta V _{ e }^{2} / 2=0.95 \times 1.812=1.7214 Btu / lbm\end{gathered}\)

 

\(\Rightarrow V _{ e \text { ac }}=\sqrt{2 \times 25037 \times 1.7214}=293.6 ft / s\)

 

Recall conversion \(1 Btu / lbm =25037 ft ^{2} / s ^{2}(=32.174 \times 778.1693)\)

\(T _{ e ac }= T _{ i }-\frac{ V _{ e ac }^{2} / 2}{ C _{ p }}=527.7-\frac{1.7214}{0.24}=520.53 R\)

 

\(\rho_{ e a c}=\frac{ P _{ e }}{ RT _{ e ac} }=\frac{13.977 \times 144}{53.34 \times 520.53}=0.07249 lbm / ft ^{3}\)

 

\(\dot{ m }=\rho A V =0.07249 \times \frac{\pi}{4} \times\left(\frac{1}{12}\right)^{2} \times 293.6= 0 . 1 1 6 lbm / s\)

 

Incompressible: \(\rho_{ i }=\frac{ P _{0}}{ RT _{0}}=\frac{14.7 \times 144}{53.34 \times 527.7}=0.0752 lbm / ft ^{3}\)

\(\begin{aligned}& V _{ e }^{2} / 2= v _{ i }\left( P _{ i }- P _{ e }\right)=\frac{\Delta P }{\rho_{ i }}=\frac{0.7226 \times 144}{0.0752 \times 778}=1.7785 Btu / lbm \\& V _{ e ac }^{2} / 2=\eta V _{ e }^{2} / 2=0.95 \times 1.7785=1.6896 Btu / lbm\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\Rightarrow V _{\text {e ac }}=\sqrt{2 \times 25037 \times 1.6896}=290.84 ft / s \\&\dot{ m }=\rho A V =0.0752 \times \frac{\pi}{4} \times\left(\frac{1}{12}\right)^{2} \times 290.84= 0 . 1 1 9 lbm /s\end{aligned}\)
