Any heat transfer if it comes in is from a 325째C reservoir and if it goes out it is to the ambient at 300 K. Sketch the process in a P-v and a T-s diagram. Solution

Process : \({P _{ V }}^{ n }= C\)

\(P _{2} / P _{1}=\left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)^{ n /( n -1)}=(500 / 300)^{1.3 / 0.3}=9.148\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}_{1} w _{2}=\int P d v &=\left(\frac{ P _{2} v _{2}- P _{1} v _{1}}{1- n }\right)=\frac{ R }{1- n }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right) \\=& \frac{0.287}{1-1.3}(500-300)=-191.3 k J / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

Energy equation

\(\begin{aligned}_{1}q_{2} &= u _{2}- u _{1}+{ }_{1} w _{2}= C _{ v }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)+{ }_{1} w _{2} \\&=0.717(500-300)-191.3=- 4 7 . 9 3 k J / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

The \(_{1} q _{2}\) is negative and thus goes out. Entropy is generated between the air and ambient.

\(s _{2}- s _{1}={ }_{1} q _{2} / T _{ amb }+{ }_{1} s _{2 gen} \)

 

\({ }_{1} s _{2 \text { gen }} = s _{2}- s _{1}-{ }_{1} q _{2} / T _{ amb }= C _{ p } \ln \left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)-{ }_{1} q _{2} / T _{ amb }\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}{ }_{1} s _{2 \text { gen }} &=1.004 \ln \left(\frac{500}{300}\right)-0.287 \ln 9.148-\left(\frac{-47.93}{300}\right) \\&=0.51287-0.635285+0.15977 \\&= 0 . 0 3 7 3 6 k J / k g K\end{aligned}\)
