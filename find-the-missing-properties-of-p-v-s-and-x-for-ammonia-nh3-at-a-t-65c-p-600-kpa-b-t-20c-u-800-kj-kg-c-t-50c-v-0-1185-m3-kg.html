Guest Question.

Find the missing properties of P, v, s and x for ammonia ( \(NH _{3}\)) at
a. T = 65°C, P = 600 kPa
b. T = 20°C, u = 800 kJ/kg
c. T = 50°C, v = 0.1185 \(m ^{3} / kg\)

Admin
Solution

a) B.2.2 average between 60°C and 70°C
v = (0.25981 + 0.26999)/2 = 0.26435 \(m ^{3}\)/kg
s = (5.6383 + 5.7094)/2 = 5.6739 kJ/kgK

 

b) B.2.1: \(u < u _{ g }=1332.2 kJ / kg \Rightarrow P = P _{ sat }=857.5 kPa\)

\(x =\left( u – u _{ f }\right) / u _{ fg }=\frac{800-272.89}{1059.3}=0.49666\)

v = 0.001638 + x × 0.14758 = 0.07494 \(m ^{3}\)/kg,
s = 1.0408 + x × 4.0452 = 3.04989 kJ/kg-K

 

c) B.2.1: \(v > v _{ g }=0.06337 m ^{3} / kg \Rightarrow\)

B.2.2 superheated vapor so x is undefined
very close to 1200 kPa, s = 5.1497 kJ/kgK
