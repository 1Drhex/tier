Guest Question.

Consider the stirred-tank blending process for and Equations
\( ρv\frac{dx}{dt}=w_{1}x_{1} + w_{2}x_{2} - wx \)
and
\( 0 = w_{1}\bar{x_{1}}+w_{2}x_{2}-w\bar{x} \)
with \( w_{1} = 600 kg/min,\space w_{2}=2kg/min, \)
\( x_{1} = 0.050, \space and \space x_{2} = 1 \) (for pure solute).
The liquid volume and density are constant: \( V = 2m^{3} \space and \space ρ = 900kg/m^{3} \) respectively.

(a) Calculate the nominal exit concentration, \( \bar{x}.\)
(b) Derive an expression for the response, x(t), to a sudden change in \( x_{1} \) from 0.050 to 0.075 that occurs at time, t=0.
Assume that the process is initially at the nominal steady state.

Admin
Solution

(a) Exit flow rate w can be calculated from a steady-state mass balance
\( w = w_{1} + w_{2} = 600 +2 = 602 kg/min \).
and weight fraction \( \bar{x} \) can be determined from Equation
\( 0 = w_{1}\bar{x_{1}} + w_{2}\bar{x_{2}}-w\bar{x} \)
\( \bar{x} = \frac{w_{1}\bar{x_{1}}+w_{2}x_{2}}{w} = \frac{(600)(0.05)+(2)(1)}{602} = 0.053 \)

(b) To determine x(t) for a sudden change in \( x_{1} \), we first derive an expression for \( x^{\prime }_{1}(t) \) and then obtain \( X^{\prime }_{1}(s) \).
Thus, the appropriate starting point for the derivation is the transfer function in Equation
\( \frac{X^{\prime }(s)}{X^{\prime }_{1}(s)} = G(s) \triangleq \frac{K_{1}}{\tau s+1} \) (1)
where \( K_{1} = 600/602 =0.997 \) and τ=ρV/w=(900)(2)/(602)=2.99 min.
The sudden change in \( x_{1} \) can be expressed in deviation variable form as
\( x^{\prime }_{1}(t) = x_{1}-\bar{x}_{1} = 0.075-0.050 = 0.025 \)
\( \Rightarrow X^{\prime }_{1}(s) = \frac{0.025}{s} \)
Rearranging Equation (1) and substituting numerical values gives
\(X^{\prime }(s) = (\frac{K_{1}}{\tau s+1} ) X^{\prime }_{1}(s) = (\frac{0.997}{2.99s+1} )(\frac{0.025}{s} )=\frac{0.0249}{s(2.99s+1)} \)
Using Item 13 inTable 3.1, the inverse Laplace transform is
\( x^{\prime }(t) = 0.0249(1-e^{-t/2.99}) \)
From Equation
\( x^{′}_{ 1} ≜ x_{1}-\bar{x}_{1} \)
\( x(t)= \bar{x}+x^{\prime }(t)=0.053+0.0249(1-e^{-t/2.99}) \).
