Guest Question.

Your small consulting company is evaluating a circuit board testing machine. The device costs $35,000, and the maker estimates that it will have a salvage value of $6,000 after 5 years of use. Determine the annual depreciation using both the straight-line and the MACRS methods.

Admin
Solution

a. Straight-line

\(D_{n}=\frac{I-S}{n}=\frac{35,000-6,000}{5}=\$5,800Per Year \)

b. MACRS

Note that a 5-year recovery period is depreciated over 6 years. This is based on the assumption that the equipment will be sold during the sixth year. Also note that if a piece of capital equipment is sold before it can be depreciated fully, one-half the normal amount can be depreciated during that year.
