Guest Question.

Let

\( E=[v_1,v_2,v_3]=[(1,1,1)^T,(2,3,2)^T,(1,5,4)^T] \)

\( F=[u_1,u_2,u_3]=[(1,1,0)^T,(1,2,0)^T,(1,2,1)^T] \)

Find the transition matrix from E to F. If

\( x = 3v_1 + 2v_2 − v_3 and y = v_1 − 3v_2 + 2v_3 \)

find the coordinates of x and y with respect to the ordered basis F.

Admin
Solution

As in the transition matrix is given by

\(U^{-1} V=\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 2 & -1 & 0 \\ -1 & 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 1 & 2 & 4 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup =\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 1 & 1 & -3 \\ -1 & -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 4 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \)

The coordinate vectors of x and y with respect to the ordered basis F are given by

\(\left[X\right] _{F} =\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 1 & 1 & -3 \\ -1 & -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 4 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 3 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup =\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 8 \\ -5 \\ 3 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \)

and

\(\left[X\right] _{F} =\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 1 & 1 & -3 \\ -1 & -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 4 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \left\lgroup\begin{matrix} 1 \\ -3 \\ 2 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup =\left\lgroup\begin{matrix} -8 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{matrix} \right\rgroup \)

The reader may verify that

\(8u_{1} -5u_{2} +3u_{3} =3v_{1} +2v_{2} -v_{3} \)

\(-8u_{1} +2u_{2} +3u_{3} =v_{1} -3v_{2} +2v_{3} \)
