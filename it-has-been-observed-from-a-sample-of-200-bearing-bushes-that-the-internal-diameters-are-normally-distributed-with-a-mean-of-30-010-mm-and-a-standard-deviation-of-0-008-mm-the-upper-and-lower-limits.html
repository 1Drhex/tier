Guest Question.

It has been observed from a sample of 200 bearing bushes that the internal diameters are normally distributed with a mean of 30.010 mm and a standard deviation of 0.008 mm. The upper and lower limits for the internal diameter, as specified by the designer, are 30.02 and 30.00 mm respectively. Calculate the percentage of rejected bushes.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } \quad \mu=30.010 mm \quad \hat{\sigma}=0.008 mm \).

\( X_{1}=30.02 mm \quad X_{2}=30.00 mm \).

\( \text { Step I Standard variables } Z_{1} \text { and } Z_{2} \).

From Eq. 24.7,

\( Z_{1}=\frac{X_{1}-\mu}{\hat{\sigma}} \). (24.7).

\( Z_{1}=\frac{X_{1}-\mu}{\hat{\sigma}}=\frac{30.02-30.01}{0.008}=+1.25 \).

\( Z_{2}=\frac{X_{2}-\mu}{\hat{\sigma}}=\frac{30.00-30.01}{0.008}=-1.25 \).
