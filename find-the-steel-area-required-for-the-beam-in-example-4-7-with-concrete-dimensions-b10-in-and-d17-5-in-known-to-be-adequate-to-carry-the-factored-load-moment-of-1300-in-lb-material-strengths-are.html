Guest Question.

Find the steel area required for the beam in , with concrete dimensions b=10 in. and d=17.5 in. known to be adequate to carry the factored load moment of 1300 in-lb. Material strengths are \(\acute{f} _{c}\)=4000 psi and \(f_{y}\)=60,000 psi.

Admin
Solution

Note that in cases in which the concrete dimensions are known to be adequate and only the reinforcement must be found, the iterative method used earlier is not required. The necessary flexural resistance factor is

\(R=\frac{M_{u}}{\phi bd^{2} } =\frac{1300\times 1000}{0.90\times 10\times 17.5^{2} }=472\) psi
