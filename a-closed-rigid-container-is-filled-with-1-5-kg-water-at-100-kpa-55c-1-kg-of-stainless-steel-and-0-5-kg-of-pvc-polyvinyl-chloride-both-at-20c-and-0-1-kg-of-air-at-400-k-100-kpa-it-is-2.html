Guest Question.

A closed rigid container is filled with 1.5 kg water at 100 kPa, 55°C, 1 kg of stainless steel and 0.5 kg of PVC (polyvinyl chloride) both at 20°C and 0.1 kg of air at 400 K, 100 kPa. It is now left alone with no external heat transfer and no water vaporizes. Find the final temperature and the entropy generation for the process.

Admin
Solution

CV. Container.
Process: V = constant => \({ }_{1} W _{2}=0\) and also given \({ }_{1} Q _{2}=0\)

Energy Eq.: \(U _{2}- U _{1}=\sum m _{ i }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)_{ i }={ }_{1} Q _{2}-{ }_{1} W _{2}=0\)

Entropy Eq.6.37: \(S _{2}- S _{1}=\sum m _{ i }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)_{ i }=0+{ }_{1} S _{2, gen }\)

For the liquid and the metal mass we will use the specific heat (Tbl A.3, A.4) so

\(\begin{array}{l}\sum m _{ i }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)_{ i }=\sum m _{ i } C _{ v i }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)_{ i }= T _{2} \sum m _{ i } C _{ v i }-\sum m _{ i } C _{ v i } T _{1 i } \\\sum m _{ i }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)_{ i }=\sum m _{ i } C _{ v i } \ln \left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)_{ i }\end{array}\)

noticing that all masses have the same \(T _{2}\) but not same initial T.

\(\sum m _{ i } C _{ v i }=1.5 \times 4.18+1 \times 0.46+0.5 \times 0.96+0.1 \times 0.717=7.282 kJ / K\)

Energy Eq.:

\(\begin{aligned}7.282 T _{2}=1.5 \times & 4.18 \times 55+(1 \times 0.46+0.5 \times 0.96) \times 20 \\+& 0.1 \times 0.717 \times(400-273.15)=372.745 kJ\end{aligned}\)

\(T _{2}\) = 51.2°C

The volume of the air is constant so entropy change from Eq.6.17 is the same expression as for the solids and liquids given above.

\(\begin{aligned}_{1} S _{2, gen } &= S _{2}- S _{1}=\sum m _{ i }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)_{ i }=1.5 \times 4.18 \times \ln (324.35 / 328.15) \\&+(1 \times 0.46+0.5 \times 0.96) \times \ln \left(\frac{324.35}{293.15}\right)+0.1 \times 0.717 \times \ln \left(\frac{324.35}{400}\right) \\&=-0.07304 kJ / K +0.09507 kJ / K -0.01503 kJ / K = 0 . 0 0 7 kJ / K\end{aligned}\)
