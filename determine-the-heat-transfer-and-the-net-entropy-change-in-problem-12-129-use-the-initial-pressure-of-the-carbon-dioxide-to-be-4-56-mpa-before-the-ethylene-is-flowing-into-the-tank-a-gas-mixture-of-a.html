Guest Question.

Determine the heat transfer and the net entropy change in . Use the initial pressure of the carbon dioxide to be 4.56 MPa before the ethylene is flowing into the tank.

A gas mixture of a known composition is frequently required for different purposes, e.g., in the calibration of gas analyzers. It is desired to prepare a gas mixture of 80% ethylene and 20% carbon dioxide (mole basis) at 10 MPa, 25°C in an uninsulated, rigid 50-L tank. The tank is initially to contain CO2 at 25°C and some pressure P1. The valve to a line flowing C 2 H 4 at 25°C, 10 MPa, is now opened slightly, and remains open until the tank reaches 10 MPa, at which point the temperature can be assumed to be 25°C. Assume that the gas mixture so prepared can be represented by Kay’s rule and the generalized charts. Given the desired final state, what is the initial pressure of the carbon dioxide, P1?

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}& A = C _{2} H _{4}, B = CO _{2} \\& T _{1}=25^{\circ} C \\& P _{2}=10 MPa , T _{2}=25^{\circ} C \\& y _{ A 2}=0.8, y _{ B 2}=0.2\end{aligned}\)

Mixture at 2 :

\(\begin{aligned}& P _{ C 2}=0.8 \times 5.04+0.2 \times 7.38=5.508 MPa \\& T _{ C 2}=0.8 \times 282.4+0.2 \times 304.1=286.7 K \\& T _{ r 2}=298.15 / 286.7=1.040 ; P _{ r 2}=10 / 5.508=1.816\end{aligned}\)

D.1 : \(Z_{2}=0.32\)

\(\begin{aligned}& n _{2}=\frac{ P _{2} V }{ Z _{2} \overline{ R } T _{2}}=\frac{10000 \times 0.05}{0.32 \times 8.3145 \times 298.2}=0.6302 kmol \\& n _{ A 2}= n _{ i }=0.8 n _{2}=0.5042 kmol C _{2} H _{4} \\& n _{ B 2}= n _{1}=0.2 n _{2}=0.1260 kmol CO _{2} \\& T _{ r1 }=\frac{298.2}{304.1}=0.981 \text { and } P _{ r 1}=\frac{4560}{7380}=0.618\end{aligned}\)

Energy Eq.: \(Q _{ CV }+ n _{1} \overline{ h }_{ i }= n _{2} \overline{ u }_{2}- n _{1} \overline{ u }_{1}= n _{2} \overline{ h }_{2}- n _{1} \overline{ h }_{1}-\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right) V\)

or \(Q_{ CV }= n _{2}\left(\overline{ h }_{2}-\overline{ h }_{2}^{*}\right)- n _{1}\left(\overline{ h }_{1}-\overline{ h }_{1}^{*}\right)- n _{ i }\left(\overline{ h }_{ i }-\overline{ h }_{ i }^{*}\right)-\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right) V\)

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { (since } \left. T _{ i }= T _{1}= T _{2}, \overline{ h }_{ i }^{*}=\overline{ h }_{1}^{*}=\overline{ h }_{2}^{*}\right) \\&\left(\overline{ h }_{1}^{*}-\overline{ h }_{1}\right)=0.83 \times 8.3145 \times 304.1=2099 kJ / kmol\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\left(\overline{ h }_{2}^{*}-\overline{ h }_{2}\right)=3.40 \times 8.3145 \times 286.7=8105 kJ / kmol \\& T _{ ri }=\frac{298.2}{282.4}=1.056, P _{ ri }=\frac{10}{5.04}=1.984 \\&\left(\overline{ h }_{ i }^{*}-\overline{ h }_{ i }\right)=3.35 \times 8.3145 \times 282.4=7866 kJ / kmol\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}Q_{ CV } &=0.6302(-8105)-0.126(-2099)-0.5042(-7866)-(10000-4560) \times 0.05 \\&=-1149 kJ\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\Delta S _{ CV }= n _{2} \overline{ s }_{2}- n _{1} \overline{ s }_{1}, \Delta S _{ SURR }=- Q _{ CV } / T _{0}- n _{ i } \overline{ s}_{ i } \\&\Delta S _{ gen }= n _{2} \overline{ s }_{2}- n _{1} \overline{ s }_{1}- Q _{ CV } / T _{0}- n _{ i } \overline{ s }_{ i }\end{aligned}\)

Let \(\overline{ s }_{ A 0}^{*}=\overline{ s }_{ B 0}^{*}=0 \text { at } T _{0}=25^{\circ} C , P _{0}=0.1 MPa\)

Then \(\overline{ s }_{ MIX 0}^{*}=-8.3145(0.8 \ln 0.8+0.2 \ln 0.2)=4.161 kJ / kmol K\)

\(\begin{aligned}\overline{ s }_{1} &=\overline{ s }_{ B 0}^{*}+\left(\overline{ s }_{ P 1 T1}^{*} – s _{ P 0 T 0}^{*} \right)_{ B }+\left(\overline{ s }_{1}-\overline{ s }_{ P 1 T1}^{*} \right)_{ B } \\&=0+\left(0-8.3145 \ln \frac{4.56}{0.1}\right)-0.60 \times 8.3145=-36.75 kJ / kmol K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\overline{ s }_{ i } &=\overline{ s }_{ A 0}^{*}+\left(\overline{ s }_{ Pi Ti}^{*} – s _{ P 0 T 0}^{*} \right)_{ A }+\left(\overline{ s }_{ i }-\overline{ s }_{ Pi Ti}^{*} \right)_{ A } \\&=0+\left(0-8.3145 \ln \frac{10}{0.1}\right)-2.44 \times 8.3145=-58.58 kJ / kmol K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\overline{ s }_{2} &=\overline{ s }_{ MIX 0}^{*}+\left(\overline{ s }_{ P 2 T 2}^{*} – \overline{ s }_{ P 0 T 0}^{*} \right)_{ MIX }+\left(\overline{ s }_{2}-\overline{ s }_{ P 2 T 2}^{*} \right)_{ MIX } \\&=4.161+\left(0-8.3145 \ln \frac{10}{0.1}\right)-2.551 \times 8.3145=-55.34 kJ / kmol K\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}S _{ gen }&=0.6302(-55.33)-0.126(-36.75)-0.5042(-58.58)+1149 / 298.2 \\&=+ 3 . 1 5 kJ / K\end{aligned}\)
