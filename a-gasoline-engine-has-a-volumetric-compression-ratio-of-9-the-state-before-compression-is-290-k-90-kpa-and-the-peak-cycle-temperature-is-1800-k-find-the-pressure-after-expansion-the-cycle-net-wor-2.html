Guest Question.

A gasoline engine has a volumetric compression ratio of 9. The state before compression is 290 K, 90 kPa, and the peak cycle temperature is 1800 K. Find the pressure after expansion, the cycle net work and the cycle efficiency using properties from Table A.7.

Admin
Solution

Use table A.7.1 and interpolation.

Compression 1 to 2: \(s _{2}= s _{1} \Rightarrow\) From Eq.6.19

\(\begin{aligned}&0= s _{ T 2}^{ o }- s _{ T 1}^{o}- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)= s _{ T 2}^{ o }- s _{ T 1}^{ o }- R \ln \left( T _{2} v _{1} / T _{1} v _{2}\right) \\& s _{ T 2}^{ o }- R \ln \left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)= s _{ T 1}^{ o }+ R \ln \left( v _{1} / v _{2}\right)=6.83521+0.287 \ln 9=7.4658\end{aligned}\)

This becomes trial and error so estimate first at 680 K and use A.7.1.

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { LHS }_{680}=7.7090-0.287 \ln (680 / 290)=7.4644 \text { (too low) } \\&\text { LHS }_{700}=7.7401-0.287 \ln (700 / 290)=7.4872 \text { (too high) }\end{aligned}\)

Interpolate to get: \(T _{2}=681.23 K , \quad u _{2}=497.9 kJ / kg\)

\(\begin{aligned}& P _{2}= P _{1}\left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)\left( v _{1} / v _{2}\right)=90(681.23 / 290) \times 9=1902.7 kPa \\&{}_{1 }w _{2}= u _{1}- u _{2}=207.2-497.9=-290.7 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

Combustion 2 to 3: constant volume \(v _{3}= v _{2}\)

\(\begin{aligned}& q _{ H }= u _{3}- u _{2}=1486.3-497.9=988.4 kJ / kg \\& P _{3}= P _{2}\left( T _{3} / T _{2}\right)=1902.7(1800 / 681.2)=5028 kPa\end{aligned}\)

Expansion 3 to 4: \(s _{4}= s _{3} \Rightarrow\) From Eq.6.19 as before

\(s _{ T 4}^{ o }- R \ln \left( T _{4} / T _{3}\right)= s _{ T 3}^{ o }+ R \ln \left( v _{3} / v _{4}\right)=8.8352+0.287 \ln (1 / 9)=8.2046\)

This becomes trial and error so estimate first at 850 K and use A.7.1.

\(\begin{aligned}& LHS _{850}=7.7090-0.287 \ln (850 / 1800)=8.1674 \text { (too low) } \\& LHS _{900}=7.7401-0.287 \ln (900 / 1800)=8.2147 \text { (too high) }\end{aligned}\)

Interpolation \(\Rightarrow T _{4}=889.3 K , \quad u _{4}=666 kJ / kg\)

\(\begin{aligned}& P _{4}= P _{3}\left( T _{4} / T _{3}\right)\left( v _{3} / v _{4}\right)=5028(889.3 / 1800)(1 / 9)=276 kPa \\&{}_{3} w _{4}= u _{3}- u _{4}=1486.3-666.0=820.3 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

Net work and overall efficiency

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ NET }={ }_{3} w _{4}+{ }_{1} w _{2}=820.3-290.7= 5 2 9 . 6 \mathrm { kJ } / \mathrm { kg } \\&\eta= w _{ NET } / q _{ H }=529.6 / 988.4= 0 . 5 3 6\end{aligned}\)
