Guest Question.

In a quenching process, a copper plate of 3 mm thick is heated up to 350°C and then suddenly, it is dropped into a water bath at 25°C. Calculate the time required for the plate to reach the temperature of 50°C. The heat transfer coefficient on the surface of the plate is 28 W/m^2.K. The plate dimensions may be taken as length 40 cm and width 30 cm. Also calculate the time required for infinite long plate to cool to 50°C. Other parameters remain same. Take the properties of copper as C = 380 J/kg.K, ρ = 8800\( kg/m^3\), k = 385 W/m.K.

Admin
Solution

Given : The quenching of a copper plate in water bath.
Size = 40 cm × 30 cm, L = 3 mm,
\(T_i = 350°C, T_∞ = 25°C,\)
\(T = 50°C, h = 28 W/m^2.K,\)
\(C = 380 J/kg.K, ρ = 8800 kg/m^3,\)
k = 385 W/m.K.

To find : Time required to cool the plate to 50°C, if
(i) Finite long plate size 40 cm × 30 cm,
(ii) Infinite long plate.
Assumptions :
1. The effect of edges of plate for cooling.
2. Internal temperature gradients are negligible.
3. No radiation heat exchange.
4. Constant properties.
Analysis : (i) The characteristic length of finite
long plate (as shown in Fig. 6.8)
\(δ = \frac{Volume of plate}{Exposed area of plate}\)
\(= \frac{0.4 × 0.3 × 0.003}{(2 × 0.4 + 2 × 0.3) × 0.003 +2 × 0.3 × 0.4}\)

\(= 1.474 × 10^{–3} m\)
The Biot number
\(Bi = \frac{hδ}{k} =\frac{28 × 1.474 × 10^{-3}}{385} = 1.072 × 10^{–4}\)
which is much smaller than 0.1, thus the lumped system analysis can be applied with reasonable accuracy. Using eqn. (6.10) ;
\(\frac{T – T_∞}{T_i – T_∞}=exp \left \{- \frac{ht}{ρCδ} \right \}\)

Using numerical values.
\(\frac{50 – 25}{350 – 25}=exp \left \{- \frac{28t}{8800 × 380 × 1.474 × 10^{-3} } \right \}\)

or \(t = –\frac{8800 × 380 × × 1.474 × 10^{-3} }{28} × ln \left (\frac{25}{325} \right)\)
= 451.5 s = 7.52 min.
(ii) Characteristic length of infinite long plate eqn. (6.11)
\(δ = \frac{L}{2}= 0.0015 m\)
\(Bi = \frac{hδ}{k} =\frac{28 × 0.0015 }{385} = 1.09× 10^{–4}\)

which is much less than 0.1, therefore, using lumped system analysis.
\(\frac{50 – 25}{350 – 25}=exp \left [- \frac{28t}{8800 × 380 × 0.0015 } \right ]\)

or t = 459.5 s = 7.65 min.
