Guest Question.

A moist soil has these values: \(V = 7.08 \times 10^{-3}\,m^{3},\, m = 13.95 \,kg,\, w = 9.8\)% , and \(G_{s} = 2.66.\)

Determine the following:

a. \(\rho\)

b. \(\rho_{d}\)

c. e

d. n

e. S (%)

f. Volume occupied by water

Admin
Solution

\(\textbf{Part a.}\)
From Eq. (3.13).

\(\large{\rho = \frac{m}{V} = \frac{13.95}{7.08 \times 10^{-3}}}=\textbf{1970.3 kg/}\textbf{m}^{3}\\\)

\(\textbf{Part b.}\)
From Eq. (3.12).

\(\large{\gamma_{d} = \frac{\gamma}{1 + w}}\\ \rho_{d} = \frac{\rho}{1 + w} = \frac{1970.3}{1 + (\frac{9.8}{100})} = \textbf{1794.4 kg/}\textbf{m}^{3}\\\)

\(\textbf{Part c.}\)
From Eq. (3.22).

\(\text { Dry density }=\Large{\rho_{d}=\frac{G_{s} \rho_{w}}{1 + e}}\\\) \(\large{e = \frac{G_{s}\rho_{w}}{\rho_{d}} – 1}\\\) \(\large{e = \frac{(2.66)(1000)}{1794.4} – 1 = \textbf{0.48}}\\\)

\(\textbf{Part d.}\)
From Eq. (3.7).

\(\large{n = \frac{e}{1 + e} = \frac{0.48}{1 + 0.48} } = \textbf{0.324}\\ \)

\(\textbf{Part e.}\)
From Eq. (3.19).

\(\large{S_{e} = wG_{s} \\}\) \(\\S(\%)=\left(\frac{w G_{s}}{e}\right)(100)=\frac{(0.098)(2.66)}{0.48}(100)=\textbf{ 54 .3} \% \\\) \(\textbf{Part f.}\)

Mass of soil solids is

\(\large{m_{s} = \frac{m}{1 + w} = \frac{13.95}{1 + 0.098} = 12.7\, kg} \)

Thus, mass of water is

\(\large{m_{w} = m – m_{s} = 13.95 – 12.7 = 1.25\,kg }\)

Volume of water is

\(\large{V_{w} = \frac{m_{w} }{\rho _{w} } = \frac{1.25}{1000} = \textbf{0.00125}\, m^{3}}\)
