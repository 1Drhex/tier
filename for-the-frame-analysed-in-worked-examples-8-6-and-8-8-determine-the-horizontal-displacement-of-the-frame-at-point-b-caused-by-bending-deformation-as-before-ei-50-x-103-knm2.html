Guest Question.

For the frame analysed in and , determine the horizontal displacement of the frame at point B caused by bending deformation. As before, EI = 50 × 10³ kNm².

Admin
Solution

For the virtual force method, the frame may be reduced to a statically determinate primary frame by removing the pin support at D. We will call this primary frame 1. Another possibility is to create the primary frame by setting \( M_A \) and \( H_A \) to zero (i.e. by replacing the fixed support at A with a roller support) — primary frame 2. Figure 8.17 shows the reactions and bending moment diagrams caused by a unit horizontal virtual force applied at B for each of these two primary frames.

Flexural deformations: The bending moment diagram and reactions caused by the external load on the frame are shown in Figure 8.16. In Member AB,\( (M_0)_{AB} = −203.3 + 78.64 x \), where x is measured upward from A. In BC, \( (M_0)_{BC} = +111.3 − 24.59 x \), where x is measured from B. In CD, \( (M_0)_{CD} = −21.36 x \), where x is measured upward from D.

Considering the virtual force field for primary frame 1, internal work only occurs in member AB, the virtual moment caused by the unit virtual force at B is \( (\overline{m} )_{AB} = −4 +x \) . Therefore, \( \overline{W}_{ ext} = \overline{1} u_B \) and:

\( \overline{W}_{ int} = \int\limits_{0}^{4}{\frac{M_0}{EI} (\overline{m} )_{AB}} dx = \int\limits_{0}^{4}{\frac{- 203.3 + 78.64x}{50 \times 10^3} \times (-4 + x)} dx \) .

\( = \frac{1}{50 \times 10^3} [ 813.2 x – 258.9 x^2 + 26.21x^3]^4_0 = 0.0157 \) .

and the horizontal displacement at B caused by bending is \( u_B = 0.0157 \) m.

The same results are obtained by considering the virtual force field for primary frame 2. In this case, internal work only occurs in members BC and CD, with \( (\overline{m} )_{BC} = −0 5 x \) and \( (\overline{m} )_{CD} = − x \) . With \( \overline{W}_{ext} = \overline{1} u_B \) and:

\( \overline{W}_{ int} = \int\limits_{0}^{8}{\frac{(M_0)_{BC}}{EI} (\overline{m} )_{BC}} dx + \int\limits_{0}^{4}{\frac{(M_0)_{CD}}{EI} (\overline{m} )_{CD}} dx \).

\( = \int\limits_{0}^{8}{\frac{ 111.3 – 24.59 x}{50 \times 10^3} \times (-0.5 x)} dx + \int\limits_{0}^{4}{\frac{-21.36 x}{50 \times 10^3} \times (- x)} dx \) .

\( = \frac{1}{50 \times 10^3} [ -27.82 x^2 + 4.10x^3]^8_0 + \frac{1}{50 \times 10^3} [7.12 x^3]^4_0 = 0.0157 \) .

The horizontal displacement at B caused by bending is \( u_B = 0.0157 \) m (as previously calculated using primary frame 1).
