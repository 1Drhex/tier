Guest Question.

A column with the cross section shown in Fig. 8-15 is constructed from a \(W24 \times 84 \) wide-flange section and a \(C12 \times 30\) channel. Determine the second moments and radii of gyration of the cross-sectional area with respect to horizontal and vertical axes through the centroid of the cross section.

Admin
Solution

Properties and dimensions for the structural shapes can be obtained from Tables A-1 and A-5 of Appendix A. In Fig. 8-15, the x-axis passes through the centroid of the wide-flange section and the x′-axis passes through the centroid of the channel. The centroidal \(x_C\)-axis for the composite section can be located by using the principle of moments as applied to areas.
The total area \(A_T\) for the composite section is

\(A_T=A_{WF}+A_{CH}=24.7+8.82=33.52in^2\)

The moment of the composite area about the x-axis is

\(M_x=A_{WF}(yc)_{WF}+A_{CH}(yc)_{CH}=24.7(0)+8.82(11.886)=104.835in^3\)

The distance \(y_C\) from the x-axis to the centroid of the composite section is

\(yc=\frac{M_x}{A_T}=\frac{104.835}{33.52}=3.128in.\)

The second moment \(I_{xCWF}\) for the wide-flange section about the centroidal \(x_C\)-axis of the composite section is

\(I_{xCWF}=I_{xWF}+(yc)_{WF}^2A_{WF}=2370+(3.128)^2(24.7)=2611.7in^4\)

The second moment \(I_{xCCH}\) for the channel about the centroidal \(x_C\)-axis of the composite section is

\(I_{xCCH}=I_{x′CH}+[(yc)_{CH}-(yc)]^2A_{CH}=5.14+(11.886-3.128)^2(8.82)=681.7in^4\)

For the composite area:

\(I_{xC}=I_{xCWF}+I_{xCCH}=2611.7+681.7=3293.4≅3290in^4\)

The y-axis passes through the centroid of each of the areas; therefore, the second moment \(I_{yC}\) for the composite section is

\(I_{yC}=I_{yCWF}+I_{yCCH}=94.4+162=256.4≅256in^4\)

The radius of gyration about the \(x_C\)-axis for the composite section is

\(k_{xC}=[\frac{I_{xC}}{A_T} ]^{1/2}=[\frac{3293.4}{33.52} ]^{1/2}=9.912\cong 9.91in.\)

The radius of gyration about the \(y_C\)-axis for the composite section is

\(k_{yC}=[\frac{I_{yC}}{A_T} ]^{1/2}=[\frac{256.4}{33.52} ]^{1/2}=2.766\cong 2.77in.\)
