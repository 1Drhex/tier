Solution

For a line-to-line fault the magnitude of the per unit fault currents are 125,121 A for a bus 1 fault, 2,672 A for a bus 2 fault, 8,341 A for a bus 3 fault, 6,443 A for a bus 4 fault, and 5,163 A for a bus 5 fault. All values are lower than for the single line-to-ground case, except at bus 2 it is slightly higher.
