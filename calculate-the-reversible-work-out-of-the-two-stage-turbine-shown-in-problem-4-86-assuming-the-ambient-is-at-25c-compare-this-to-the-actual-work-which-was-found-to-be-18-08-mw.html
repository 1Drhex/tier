Calculate the reversible work out of the two-stage turbine shown in , assuming the ambient is at 25°C. Compare this to the actual work which was found to be 18.08 MW.

Solution

C.V. Turbine. Steady flow, 1 inlet and 2 exits.
Use Eq. 8.11 for steady flow with q = 0 for adiabatic turbine.

Supply state 1: 20 kg/s at 10 MPa, 500°C
Process steam 2: 5 kg/s, 0.5 MPa, 155°C,

Exit state 3: 20 kPa, x = 0.9

Table B.1.3:

\(\begin{array}{ll}h _{1}=3373.7, & h _{2}=2755.9 kJ / kg , \\s _{1}=6.5966, & s _{2}=6.8382 kJ / kg K\end{array}\)

Table B.1.2:

\(\begin{array}{l}h _{3}=251.4+0.9 \times 2358.3=2373.9 kJ / kg ,\\s _{3}=0.8319+0.9 \times 7.0766=7.2009 kJ / kg K\end{array}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ W }^{ rev }=&\left(\dot{ m }_{1} h _{1}-\dot{ m }_{2} h _{2}-\dot{ m }_{3} h _{3}\right)- T _{0}\left(\dot{ m }_{1} s _{1}-\dot{ m }_{2} s _{2}-\dot{ m }_{3} s _{3}\right) \\=& 20 \times 3373.7-5 \times 2755.9-15 \times 2373.9 \\& \quad-298.15(20 \times 6.5966-5 \times 6.8382-15 \times 7.2009) \\=& 21.14 MW =\dot{ W }^{ ac }+\dot{ Q }^{ rev }=18084 kW +3062.7 kW\end{aligned}\)

Eq.8.11 :
\(\begin{aligned}\dot{W}^{ rev }=& \sum\left(1-\frac{T_{0}}{T_{j}}\right) \dot{Q}_{j} \\&+\sum \dot{m}_{i}\left(h_{ tot i}-T_{0} s_{i}\right)-\sum \dot{m}_{e}\left(h_{ tot e}-T_{0} s_{e}\right) \\&-\left[\frac{d E_{ c.v. }}{d t}-T_{0} \frac{d S_{ c.v. }}{d t}\right]\end{aligned}\)
