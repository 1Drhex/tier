From Table 12.31, we have

\( \sum M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta i=380 r+480 r \cos \theta_{4} \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r i \sin \theta i=580 r+480 r \sin \theta_{4} \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} \text { rili } \cos \theta i=-494 r \)

\( \Sigma M_{i} \text { rili } \sin \theta i=1624 r\).

\( \tan \theta_{4}=\frac{-\sum M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}}{-\sum M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}}=\frac{-(1624 r)}{-(-494)}=-3.28745 \theta_{4}\).

\( =-73.98 \text { i.e. } 286.92^{\circ} \).

Since the numerator is negative and denominator is positive, therefore \( \theta_{4} \) lies in the fourth quadrant.

\( M_{2} r_{2}=\left[\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \cos \theta_{M}\right)^{2}+\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \sin \theta_{M}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( M_{2} r=\left[\left(380 r+480 r \cos 286.92^{\circ}\right)^{2}+\left(580 r+480 r \sin 286.92^{\circ}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( = r \left[(519.7)^{2}+(120.78)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=535.55 r \).

\( M_{2}=535.55 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{2}=\frac{-\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \sin \theta_{M}\right)}{-\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \cos \theta_{M}\right)}=\frac{-120.78}{-519.7}=0.2324 \).

\( \theta 2=13.08^{\circ} \text {, i.e. } 193.08^{\circ} \).

Since the number and denominator are both negative, therefore \( \theta_{2} \) lies in the third quadrant.
