It is good practice to check the reasonableness of the final answer by verifying that X lies between the values of the known factors in approximately the correct proportions. In this case, since 0.14437 is less than 0.5 of the distance between 0.14238 and 0.14903, the answer seems reasonable. If Equation [2.7] \( A=P\left[\frac{i(1+i)^n}{(1+i)^n-1} \right] \) [2.7] is applied, the exact factor value is 0.144358.
