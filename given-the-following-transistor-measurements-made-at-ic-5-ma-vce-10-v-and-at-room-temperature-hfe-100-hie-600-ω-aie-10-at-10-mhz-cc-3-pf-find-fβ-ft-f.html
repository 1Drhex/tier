Guest Question.

Given the following transistor measurements made at Ic= 5 mA, VCE = 10 V, and at room temperature:

hfe = 100 hie = 600 Ω

|Aie| = 10 at 10 MHz Cc = 3 pF

Find fβ, fT, fα, Ce, r b′e, and rbb′.

Admin
Solution

From Eq. (11.43) , \(A_{i}=\frac{-h_{fe}}{1+j(f/f_{\beta })} \) , we get

\(\left|A_{ie}\right| =\left|\frac{-h_{fe}}{1+j\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} } \right| =\frac{h_{fe}}{\left[ 1+\left\lgroup\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} \right\rgroup ^{2} \right] ^{\frac{1}{2} }} =\frac{100}{\left[ 1+\left\lgroup\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} \right\rgroup ^{2} \right] ^{\frac{1}{2} }} =10 \)

or, \(1+\left\lgroup\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} \right\rgroup ^{2} =100 \)

or, \(\left\lgroup\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} \right\rgroup ^{2} = 99 \)

or, \(\frac{f}{f_{\beta }} = 9.95 \)

Since, f = 10 MHz, we obtain

\(f_{\beta }=\frac{f}{9.95}=\frac{10MHz}{9.95}=1.005 \) MHz

From Eq. (11.45), \(f_{T}\approx h_{fe}f_{\beta }=\frac{g_{m}}{2\pi (C_{e}+C_{c})} \approx \frac{g_{m}}{2\pi C_{e}} \) ,we obtain

\(f_{T}\approx h_{fe}f_{\beta }=100\times 1.005 MHz=100.5 \) MHz

Using \(g_{m}=\frac{|I_{C}|}{V_{T}} \) from Eq. (11.26) , \(g_{m}\approx \frac{|I_{C}|}{V_{T}} \) , in Eq. (11.47) , \(C_{e}=\frac{g_{m}}{2\pi f_{T}} \) , and (11.39), \(r_{bb^{′}}=h_{ie}-r_{b^{′}e}\) , we obtain

\(C_{e}=\frac{|I_{C}|}{2\pi f_{T}V_{T}}=\frac{5mA}{2\pi \times 100.5\times 10^6Hz\times 26mV}=304.5 \) pF

\(r_{b^{′}e}=\frac{h_{fe}V_{T}}{I_{C}}=\frac{100\times 26mV}{5mA} =520 \) Ω
