Guest Question.

In the swivelling point mechanism shown in Fig.3.31(a), OA=25 mm, AB=150 mm, AD=DE, DE=150 mm, EF=100 mm, BC=60 mm, DS=40 mm, and OC=150 mm. Crank OA rotates at 200 rpm. Determine the acceleration of sliding link DE in the trunnion.

Admin
Solution

\( \omega=2 \pi \times 200 / 60=20.944 rad / s \).

\( v_{a}=20.944 \times 0.025=0.523 m / s \).

The velocity diagram is shown in Fig.2.31(b) to a scale of 1 cm=0.1 m/s, in which

\( o a=v_{a} \perp O A \).

\( a b \perp A B \).

\( c b \perp B C \).

Locate b.

\( c b=v_{b}=4.6 cm =0.46 m / s \).

\( a b=v_{b a}=2.3 cm =0.23 m / s \).

\( \frac{a d}{a b}=\frac{A D}{A B}=\frac{1}{2} \).

or \( a d=0.5 \times 2.3=1.15 cm \).

\( \text { Draw } o s \| D E, d e \perp D E: \)

\( v_{s d}=d s=1.8 cm =0.18 m / s \).

\( v_{s}=g s=4.4 cm =0.44 m / s \).

\( \frac{d e}{d s}=\frac{D E}{D S}=150 / 45 \).

or \( d e=1.8 \times \frac{150}{45}=6 cm \).

\( v_{e d}=6 cm =0.6 m / s \).

\( \omega_{D E}=\frac{v_{d e}}{D E}=\frac{0.6}{0.1}=6 rad / s ( ccw ) \)

\( \text { Locate } e \text {. Draw } e f \perp E F \text { and if } \| X X \).

\( \text { of }=v_{f} \).

\( e f=v_{f e}=6.6 cm =0.66 m / s \).
