Guest Question.

Carbon dioxide gas enters a turbine at 5 MPa, 100째C, and exits at 1 MPa. If the isentropic efficiency of the turbine is 75%, determine the exit temperature and the second-law efficiency using the generalized charts.

Admin
Solution

\(CO _{2}\) turbine: \(\eta_{ S }= w / w _{ S }=0.75\)

inlet: \(T _{1}=100^{\circ} C , P _{1}=5 MPa\) , exhaust: \(P _{2}=1 MPa\)

a) \(P _{ r1}=\frac{5}{7.38}=0.678, T _{ r1 }=\frac{373.2}{304.1}=1.227, P _{ r 2}=\frac{1}{7.38}=0.136\)

From D.2 and D.3,

\(\begin{aligned}&\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{1}\right)=0.18892 \times 304.1 \times 0.52=29.9 \\&\left( s _{1}^{*}- s _{1}\right)=0.18892 \times 0.30=0.0567\end{aligned}\)

Assume \(T _{2 S }=253 K , T _{ r 2 S }=0.832\)

From D.2 and D.3: \(\left( h _{2 S }^{*}- h _{2 S }\right)= RT _{ C } \times 0.20=11.5\)

\(\left( s _{2 S }^{*}- s _{2 S }\right)= R \times 0.17=0.0321\)

 

\(\begin{gathered}\left( s _{2 S }^{*}- s _{1}^{*}\right)=0.8418 \ln \frac{253}{373.2}-0.18892 \ln \frac{1}{5}=-0.0232 \\\left( s _{2 S }- s _{1}\right)=-0.0321-0.0232+0.0567 \approx 0 \\\Rightarrow T _{2 S }=253 K\end{gathered}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\left( h _{2 S }^{*}- h _{1}^{*}\right)=0.8418(253-373.2)=-101.2 \\& w _{ S }=\left( h _{1}- h _{2 S }\right)=-29.9+101.2+11.5=82.8 kJ / kg \\& w =\eta_{ S } \times w _{ S }=0.75 \times 82.8=62.1 kJ / kg =\left( h _{1}- h _{1}^{*}\right)+\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{2}^{*}\right)+\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{2}\right)\end{aligned}\)

 

Assume \(T _{2}=275 K , T _{ r 2}=0.904\)

\(\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{2}^{*}\right)=0.8418(373.2-275)=82.7\)

From D.2 and D.3,

\(\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{2}\right)= RT _{ C } \times 0.17=9.8 ; \quad\left( s _{2}^{*}- s _{2}\right)= R \times 0.13=0.0245\)

Substituting,

\(w =-29.9+82.7+9.8=62.7 \approx 62.1 \quad \Rightarrow T _{2}= 2 7 5 K\)

For the exergies we need the entropy changes

\(\begin{aligned}&\left( s _{2}^{*}- s _{1}^{*}\right)=0.8418 \ln \frac{275}{373.2}-0.18892 \ln \frac{1}{5}=+0.0470 \\&\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)=-0.0245+0.0470+0.0567=+0.0792\end{aligned}\)

Assuming \(T _{0}=25^{\circ} C\) ,

\(\left(\varphi_{1}-\varphi_{2}\right)=\left( h _{1}- h _{2}\right)- T _{0}\left( s _{1}- s _{2}\right)=62.1+298.2(0.0792)=85.7 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\eta_{2 \text { nd Law }}=\frac{w}{\varphi_{1}-\varphi_{2}}=\frac{62.1}{85.7}=0.725\)
