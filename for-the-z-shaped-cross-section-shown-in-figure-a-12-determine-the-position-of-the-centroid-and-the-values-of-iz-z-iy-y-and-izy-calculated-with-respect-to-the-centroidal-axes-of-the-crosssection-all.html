Guest Question.

For the Z-shaped cross-section shown in Figure A.12, determine the position of the centroid and the values of \( I_{\overline{zz} }, I_{\overline{yy} } \) and \(I_{\overline{zy} } \) calculated with respect to the centroidal axes of the crosssection. All dimensions are in millimetres.

Admin
Solution

For convenience, we sub-divide the cross-section into three rectangular elements as shown.
The top flange plate (element 1) is 30 × 120, the vertical web plate (element 2) is 300 × 40, and the bottom flange plate (element 3) is 40 × 240.
