Guest Question.

An air refrigeration cycle has pressure ratio of 8, temperature of air at beginning of compression is –30ºC and air is cooled up to 27ºC after compression. Compression and expansion processes have isentropic efficiency of 85% and 90% respectively. Determine the refrigeration capacity and coefficient of performance. Consider C\(_{p}\) = 1.005 kJ/kg ·K, \(\pmb{γ}\) = 1.4 and air flow rate of 1 kg/s.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Given:}\\T_{1} = –30ºC, T_{3} = 27ºC \text{ or 300 K}\\\text{During process 1–2}^{\prime},\\\begin{aligned}\frac{p_{2}}{p_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{T_{2^{\prime}}}{T_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ}{γ – 1}} \\T_{1}&= –30 + 273 = 240 K\\\frac{T_{2^{\prime}}}{T_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}}\\T_{2^{\prime}}&= 240 × (8)^{\frac{(1.4 – 1)}{1.4}}\\\text{Theoretical temperature after compre}&\text{ssion }T_{2^{\prime}} = 434.75 K\\\text{For compression process, } \quad\qquad\qquad &\\η_{\text{isen,compr.}}&= 0.85 = \frac{T_{2^{\prime}} – T_{1}}{T_{2} – T_{1}}\\(T_{2} – 240)&= \frac{(434.75 – 240)}{0.85}\\T_{2}&= 469.12 K\\\text{For expansion process, }3–4^{\prime}, \qquad\frac{T_{4^{\prime}}}{T_{3}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{1}}{P_{2}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ}}\\T_{4^{\prime}}&= 300 × \left\lgroup\frac{1}{8} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{1.4 – 1}{1.4}}\\T_{4^{\prime}}&= 165.61 K\\η_{\text{isen, expansion}}&= 0.90 = \frac{T_{3} – T_{4}}{T_{3} – T_{4^{\prime}}} = \frac{300 – T_{4}}{300 – 165.61}\\\text{or,}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad T_{4}&= 179.05 K\\\text{Work during compression, }\quad\qquad W_{c}&= 1 × C_{p} × (T_{2} – T_{1})\\&= 1 × 1.005 (469.12 – 240)\\&= 230.27 kJ/s\\\text{Work during expansion }\qquad\qquad W_{T}&= 1 × C_{p} × (T_{3} – T_{4})\\W_{T}&= 1 × 1.005 × (300 – 179.05)\\&= 121.55 kJ/s\\\text{Refrigeration effect is realized during}&\text{ process, 4–1. So refrigeration effect shall be,}\\Q_{ref}&= 1 × C_{p} × (T_{1} – T_{4})\\&= 1 × 1.005 (240 – 179.05)\\Q_{ref}&= \text{61.25 kJ/s or 17.5 ton}\\\text{Net work required} = W_{C} – W_{T} = 230&.2 – 121.55 = 108.72 kJ/s\\COP&= \frac{Q_{ref}}{W_{C} – W_{T}} = \frac{61.25}{108.72} = 0.56\\\text{Refrigeration capacity}&= \textbf{17.5 }\text{ton or }\textbf{61.25 kJ/s}\\COP&= \textbf{0.56}\end{aligned}\)
