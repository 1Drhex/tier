Two rigid tanks, shown in Fig. P6.97, each contain 10 kg \(N _{2}\) gas at 1000 K, 500 kPa. They are now thermally connected to a reversible heat pump, which heats one and cools the other with no heat transfer to the surroundings. When one tank is heated to 1500 K the process stops. Find the final (P, T ) in both tanks and the work input to the heat pump, assuming constant heat capacities.
