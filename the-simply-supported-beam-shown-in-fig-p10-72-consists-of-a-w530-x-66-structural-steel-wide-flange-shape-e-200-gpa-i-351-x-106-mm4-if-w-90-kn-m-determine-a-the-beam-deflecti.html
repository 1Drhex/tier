Guest Question.

The simply supported beam shown in Fig. P10.72 consists of a W530 × 66 structural steel wide-flange shape [E = 200 GPa; I = 351 × \(10^{6} mm ^{4}\)]. If w = 90 kN/m, determine:
(a) the beam deflection at point C.
(b) the beam deflection at point E.

Admin
Solution

(a) Beam deflection at point C
Consider concentrated moment from overhang AB.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \text { (elastic curve) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=(35 kN )(4 m )=-140 kN – m , L=8 m , \\&x=4 m , E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(-140 kN – m )(4 m )}{6(8 m )\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}\left[2(8 m )^{2}-3(8 m )(4 m )+(4 m )^{2}\right]=0.0079772 m\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider uniformly distributed loads between C and D.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over portion of span.]
Relevant equations from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{w a^{3}}{24 L E I}\left(4 L^{2}-7 a L+3 a^{2}\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=90 kN / m , L=8 m , a=4 m, \\&E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{w a^{3}}{24 L E I}\left(4 L^{2}-7 a L+3 a^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(90 kN / m )(4 m )^{3}}{24(8 m )\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}\left[4(8 m )^{2}-7(4 m )(8 m )+3(4 m )^{2}\right]=-0.0341881 m\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider concentrated moment from overhang DE.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \text { (elastic curve) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=(90 kN / m )(2 m )(1 m )=180 kN – m, \\&L=8 m , x=4 m , E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(-180 kN – m )(4 m )}{6(8 m )\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}\left[2(8 m )^{2}-3(8 m )(4 m )+(4 m )^{2}\right]=0.0102564 m\end{aligned}\)

Beam deflection at C

\(v_{C}=0.0079772 m -0.0341881 m +0.0102564 m =-0.0159545 m =15.95 mm \downarrow\)

 

(b) Beam deflection at point E
Consider deflection at E resulting from rotation at D caused by concentrated load on overhang
AB. [Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{D}=\frac{M L}{6 E I} \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=(35 kN )(4 m )=140 kN – m , L=8 m, \\&E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}\theta_{D} &=\frac{M L}{6 E I}=\frac{(140 kN – m )(8 m )}{6\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}=0.0026591 rad \\v_{E} &=-(2 m )(0.0026591 rad )=-0.0053181 m\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider uniformly distributed loads between C and D.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over portion of span.]
Relevant equations from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{D}=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}(2 L-a)^{2} \quad \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=90 kN / m , L=8 m , a=4 m , \\&E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{D}=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}(2 L-a)^{2}=\frac{(90 kN / m )(4 m )^{2}}{24(8 m )\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}[2(8 m )-(4 m )]^{2}=0.0153846 rad \\&v_{E}=(2 m )(0.0153846 rad )=0.0307692 m\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider deflection at E resulting from rotation at D caused by uniform load on overhang DE.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{D}=\frac{M L}{3 E I} \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=(90 kN / m )(2 m )(1 m )=180 kN – m ,\\&L=8 m , E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{D}=\frac{M L}{3 E I}=\frac{(180 kN – m )(8 m )}{3\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}=0.0068376 rad \\&v_{E}=-(2 m )(0.0068376 rad )=-0.0136753 m\end{aligned}\)

 

Determine cantilever deflection due to uniformly distributed load on overhang DE.
[Appendix C, Cantilever beam with distributed load.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{E}=-\frac{w L^{4}}{8 E I} \quad(\text { assuming fixed support at } D \text { ) }\)

Values:

\(w=90 kN / m , L=2 m , E I=7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\)

Computation: \(v_{E}=-\frac{w L^{4}}{8 E I}=-\frac{(90 kN / m )(2 m )^{4}}{8\left(7.02 \times 10^{4} kN – m ^{2}\right)}=-0.0025641 m\)

Beam deflection at E

\(\begin{aligned}v_{E} &=-0.0053181 m +0.0307692 m -0.0136753 m -0.0025641 m \\&=0.0092117 m =9.21 mm \uparrow\end{aligned}\)
