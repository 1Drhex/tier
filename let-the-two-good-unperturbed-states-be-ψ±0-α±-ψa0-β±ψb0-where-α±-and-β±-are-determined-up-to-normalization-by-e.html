Guest Question.

Let the two “good” unperturbed states be

\( \psi_{\pm}^{0}=\alpha_{\pm} \psi_{a}^{0}+\beta_{\pm} \psi_{b}^{0} \) ,

where \( \alpha_{\pm} \text {and } \beta_{\pm} \) are determined (up to normalization) by Equation 7.27 (or Equation 7.29). Show explicitly that

\( \alpha W_{a a}+\beta W_{a b}=\alpha E^{1} \) (7.27).

\( \alpha W_{b a}+\beta W_{b b}=\beta E^{1} \) (7.29).

(a) \( \psi_{\pm}^{0} \text { are orthogonal }\left(\left\langle\psi_{+}^{0} \mid \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=0\right) \);

(b) \( \left\langle\psi_{+}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=0 \);

(c) \( \left\langle\psi_{\pm}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{\pm}^{0}\right\rangle=E_{\pm}^{1}, \text { with } E_{\pm}^{1} \) given by Equation 7.33.

\( E_{\pm}^{1}=\frac{1}{2}\left[W_{a a}+W_{b b} \pm \sqrt{\left(W_{a a}-W_{b b}\right)^{2}+4\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}}\right] \) (7.33).

Admin
Solution

(a)

\( \left\langle\psi_{+}^{0} \mid \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=\left\langle\left(\alpha_{+} \psi_{a}^{0}+\beta_{+} \psi_{b}^{0}\right) \mid\left(\alpha_{-} \psi_{a}^{0}+\beta_{-} \psi_{b}^{0}\right)\right\rangle \).

\( =\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0} \mid \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\alpha_{+}^{*} \beta_{-}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0} \mid \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0} \mid \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{+}^{*} \beta_{-}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0} \mid \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle \).

\( =\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}+\beta_{+}^{*} \beta_{-} . \quad \text { But Eq. } 7.27 \Rightarrow \beta_{\pm}=\alpha_{\pm}\left(E_{\pm}^{1}-W_{a a}\right) / W_{a b} \), so

\( \alpha W_{a a}+\beta W_{a b}=\alpha E^{1} \) (7.27).

\( \left\langle\psi_{+}^{0} \mid \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left[1+\frac{\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}{ }^{*} W_{a b}}\right]=\frac{\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}}{\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}}\left[\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}+\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)\right] \).

The term in square brackets is:

\( []=E_{+}^{1} E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\left(E_{+}^{1}+E_{-}^{1}\right)+\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}+W_{a a}^{2} . \quad \text { But Eq. } 7.33 \Rightarrow E_{\pm}^{1}=\frac{1}{2}\left[\left(W_{a a}+W_{b b}\right) \pm \sqrt{]}\right. \) where √ is shorthand for the square root term. So \( E_{+}^{1}+E_{-}^{1}=W_{a a}+W_{b b} \).

\( E_{\pm}^{1}=\frac{1}{2}\left[W_{a a}+W_{b b} \pm \sqrt{\left(W_{a a}-W_{b b}\right)^{2}+4\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}}\right] \) (7.33).

and \( E_{+}^{1} E_{-}^{1}=\frac{1}{4}\left[\left(W_{a a}+W_{b b}\right)^{2}-\left(\sqrt{)}^{2}\right]=\frac{1}{4}\left[\left(W_{a a}+W_{b b}\right)^{2}-\left(W_{a a}-W_{b b}\right)^{2}-4\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}\right]=W_{a a} W_{b b}-\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}\right. \).

Thus \( []=W_{a a} W_{b b}-\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}-W_{a a}\left(W_{a a}+W_{b b}\right)+\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}+W_{a a}^{2}=0, \quad \text { so } \quad\left\langle\psi_{+}^{0} \mid \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=0 \). QED

(b)

\( \left\langle\psi_{+}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\alpha_{+}^{*} \beta_{-}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{+}^{*} \beta_{-}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle \).

\( =\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-} W_{a a}+\alpha_{+}^{*} \beta_{-} W_{a b}+\beta_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-} W_{b a}+\beta_{+}^{*} \beta_{-} W_{b b} \).

\( =\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left[W_{a a}+W_{a b} \frac{\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}}+W_{b a} \frac{\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}^{*}}+W_{b b} \frac{\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}^{*}} \frac{\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}}\right] \).

\( =\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left[W_{a a}+E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}+E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}+W_{b b} \frac{\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}}\right] \).

But we know from (a) that \( \frac{\left(E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)\left(E_{-}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{\left|W_{a b}\right|^{2}}=-1 \), so

\( \left\langle\psi_{+}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{-}^{0}\right\rangle=\alpha_{+}^{*} \alpha_{-}\left[E_{-}^{1}+E_{+}^{1}-W_{a a}-W_{b b}\right]=0 \) . QED

(c)

\( \left\langle\psi_{\pm}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{\pm}^{0}\right\rangle=\alpha_{\pm}^{*} \alpha_{\pm}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\alpha_{\pm}^{*} \beta_{\pm}\left\langle\psi_{a}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{\pm}^{*} \alpha_{\pm}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{a}^{0}\right\rangle+\beta_{\pm}^{*} \beta_{\pm}\left\langle\psi_{b}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{b}^{0}\right\rangle \).

\( =\left|\alpha_{\pm}\right|^{2}\left[W_{a a}+W_{a b} \frac{\left(E_{\pm}^{1}-W_{a a}\right)}{W_{a b}}\right]+\left|\beta_{\pm}\right|^{2}\left[W_{b a} \frac{\left(E_{\pm}^{1}-W_{b b}\right)}{W_{b a}}+W_{b b}\right] \).

(this time I used Eq. 7.29 to express α in terms of β, in the third term).

\( \alpha W_{b a}+\beta W_{b b}=\beta E^{1} \) (7.29).

\( \therefore\left\langle\psi_{\pm}^{0}\left|H^{\prime}\right| \psi_{\pm}^{0}\right\rangle=\left|\alpha_{\pm}\right|^{2}\left(E_{\pm}^{1}\right)+\left|\beta_{\pm}\right|^{2}\left(E_{\pm}^{1}\right)=\left(\left|\alpha_{\pm}\right|^{2}+\left|\beta_{\pm}\right|^{2}\right) E_{\pm}^{1}=E_{\pm}^{1} \) . QED
