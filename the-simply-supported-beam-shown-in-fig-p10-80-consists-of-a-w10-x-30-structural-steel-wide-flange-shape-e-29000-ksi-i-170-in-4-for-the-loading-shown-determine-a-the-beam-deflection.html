Guest Question.

The simply supported beam shown in Fig. P10.80 consists of a W10 × 30 structural steel wide-flange shape [E = 29,000 ksi; I = 170 \(\text { in. }^{4}\)]. For the loading shown, determine:
(a) the beam deflection at point A.
(b) the beam deflection at point C.

Admin
Solution

(a) Beam deflection at point A
Consider cantilever beam deflection of linearly distributed load on overhang AB.
[Appendix C, Cantilever beam with linearly distributed load.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{w_{0} L^{4}}{30 E I}\)

Values:

\(w_{0}=8 kips / ft , L=9 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\)

Computation:

\(v_{A}=-\frac{w_{0} L^{4}}{30 E I}=-\frac{(8 kips / ft )(9 ft )^{4}(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{30\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\right)}=-0.613247 in.\)

 

Consider rotation at B caused by linearly distributed load on overhang AB.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I} \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=1 / 2(8 kips / ft )(9 ft )(3 ft )=108 kip – ft ,\\&L=18 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}&\theta_{B}=\frac{M L}{3 E I}=\frac{(108 kip – ft )(18 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{3\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}=0.0189274 rad \\&v_{A}=-(9 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0189274 rad )=-2.044157 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider linearly distributed load from 8 kips/ft to 0 kips/ft over span BD.
[Appendix C, SS beam with linearly distributed load.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{w_{0} L^{3}}{45 E I} \quad \text { (slope magnitude) }\)

Values:

\(w_{0}=8 kips / ft , L=18 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }{ }^{2}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}\theta_{B} &=\frac{w_{0} L^{3}}{45 E I}=\frac{(8 kips / ft )(18 ft )^{3}(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{45\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\right)}=0.0302838 rad \\v_{A} &=(9 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0302838 rad )=3.270652 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 4 kips/ft uniformly distributed load on segment CD.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over a portion of the span.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(\theta_{B}=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-a^{2}\right)\) (slope magnitude)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=4 kips / ft , L=18 ft , a=9 ft, \\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}\theta_{B} &=\frac{w a^{2}}{24 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-a^{2}\right) \\&=\frac{(4 kips / ft )(9 ft )^{2}(12 in . / ft )^{2}}{24(18 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}\left[2(18 ft )^{2}-(9 ft )^{2}\right]=0.0124211 rad \\v_{A} &=(9 ft )(12 in . / ft )(0.0124211 rad )=1.341478 in .\end{aligned}\)

Beam deflection at A

\(v_{A}=-0.613247 \text { in. }-2.044157 in .+3.270652 in .+1.341478 in .=1.954726 in .=1.955 in . \uparrow\)

 

(b) Beam deflection at point C
Consider moment at B caused by linearly distributed load on overhang AB.
[Appendix C, SS beam with concentrated moment at one end.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&M=-1 / 2(8 kips / ft )(9 ft )(3 ft )=-108 kip – ft, \\&L=18 ft , x=9 ft , E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{M x}{6 L E I}\left(2 L^{2}-3 L x+x^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(-108 \text { kip-ft })(9 ft )(12 \text { in } . / ft )^{3}}{6(18 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} \text { kip-in. }^{2}\right)}\left[2(18 ft )^{2}-3(18 ft )(9 ft )+(9 ft )^{2}\right]=0.766559 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider linearly distributed load from 8 kips/ft to 0 kips/ft over span BD.
[Appendix C, SS beam with linearly distributed load.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{w_{0} x}{360 L E I}\left(7 L^{4}-10 L^{2} x^{2}+3 x^{4}\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w_{0}=8 kips / ft , L=18 ft , x=9 ft ,\\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in. ^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{w_{0} x}{360 L E I}\left(7 L^{4}-10 L^{2} x^{2}+3 x^{4}\right) \\&=-\frac{(8 kips / ft )(9 ft )(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{360(18 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .{ }^{2}\right)}\left[7(18 ft )^{4}-10(18 ft )^{2}(9 ft )^{2}+3(9 ft )^{4}\right]=-1.916398 in .\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider 4 kips/ft uniformly distributed load on segment CD.
[Appendix C, SS beam with uniformly distributed load over a portion of the span.]
Relevant equation from Appendix C:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{w a^{3}}{24 L E I}\left(4 L^{2}-7 a L+3 a^{2}\right)\)

Values:

\(\begin{aligned}&w=4 kips / ft , L=18 ft , a=9 ft, \\&E I=4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\end{aligned}\)

Computation:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{C} &=-\frac{w a^{3}}{24 L E I}\left(4 L^{2}-7 a L+3 a^{2}\right) \\&=-\frac{(4 kips / ft )(9 ft )^{3}(12 in . / ft )^{3}}{24(18 ft )\left(4.93 \times 10^{6} kip – in .^{2}\right)}\left[4(18 ft )^{2}-7(9 ft )(18 ft )+3(9 ft )^{2}\right]=-0.958199 in .\end{aligned}\)

Beam deflection at C

\(v_{C}=0.766559 \text { in. }-1.916398 \text { in. }-0.958199 in .=-2.108037 in .=2.11 in . \downarrow\)
