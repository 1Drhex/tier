Guest Question.

A mixture of 0.5 kg nitrogen and 0.5 kg oxygen is at 100 kPa, 300 K in a piston cylinder keeping constant pressure. Now 800 kJ is added by heating. Find the final temperature and the increase in entropy of the mixture using Table A.5 values.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Mixture in the piston cylinder.

Energy Eq.: \(m\left(u_{2}-u_{1}\right)={ }_{1} Q_{2}-{ }_{1} W_{2}\)

Process: P = constant \(\Rightarrow \quad \quad_{1} W _{2}=\int P d V = P \left( V _{2}- V _{1}\right)\)

\(\begin{aligned}&{ }_{1} Q _{2}= m \left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+{ }_{1} W _{2}= m \left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ mP \left( v _{2}- v _{1}\right)= m \left( h _{2}- h _{1}\right) \\& h _{2}- h _{1}={ }_{1} Q _{2} / m \cong C _{ P \operatorname{mix}}\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)\end{aligned}\)

From Eq.11.23 and Table A.5:

\(\begin{aligned}& C _{ P \operatorname{mix}}=(1 / 2) \times 0.922+(1 / 2) \times 1.042=0.982 kJ / kg K \\& T _{2}= T _{1}+{ }_{1} Q _{2} / mC _{ P \text { mix }} \\&\quad=300 K +800 kJ /(1 \times 0.982) kJ / K = 1 1 1 4 . 7 K\end{aligned}\)

From Eq.11.24:

\(\begin{aligned}& m \left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)= m \left[ C _{ P \operatorname{mix}} \ln \left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)\right] \\&\quad=1 kg \times 0.982 kJ / kg – K \times \ln (1114.7 / 300)= 1 . 2 9 kJ / K\end{aligned}\)

……………………………………..

Eq.11.23 : \(C_{p 0 mix }=c_{A} C_{p 0 A}+c_{B} C_{p 0 B}\)

Eq.11.24 :

\(\begin{aligned}s_{2}-s_{1} &=c_{A}\left(s_{2}-s_{1}\right)_{A}+c_{B}\left(s_{2}-s_{1}\right)_{B} \\&=c_{A} C_{p 0 A} \ln \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}-c_{A} R_{A} \ln \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}}+c_{B} C_{p 0} \ln \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}-c_{B} R_{B} \ln \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \\&=C_{p 0 mix } \ln \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}-R_{\operatorname{mix}} \ln \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}}\end{aligned}\)
