Guest Question.

Refer to . Assume that the clay is overconsolidated. Given OCR = 3, swell index (\(\textbf{C}_{\textbf{s}}) \textbf{≈}\frac{ \textbf{1}}{\textbf{4}}\textbf{C}_{\textbf{c}}\)

a. Calculate the primary consolidation settlement, \(\textbf{S}_{\textbf{p}}\).

b. Assuming the three dimensional effect, modify the settlement calculated in Part a.

Admin
Solution
Part a.

From , \(\sigma^{\prime}_{o} = 95.52\,kN/m^{2}.\) Since OCR = 3, the preconsolidation pressure \(\sigma^{\prime}_{c} = (OCR)(\sigma^{\prime}_{o}) = (3)(35.52) = 286.56\,kN/m^{2}\) . For this case

\(\sigma^{\prime}_{o} + \Delta\sigma^{\prime}_{av} = 95.52 + 16.5 \lt \sigma^{\prime}_{c}\)

So, Eq. (7.16) may be used

\( S_{p}=\frac{C_{c} H}{1+e_{0}} \log \left(\frac{\sigma_{o}^{\prime}+\Delta \sigma^{\prime}_{av}}{\sigma_{o}^{\prime}}\right) =\frac{ \left(\frac{0.72}{4}\right)(3000)}{1 + 1}\log\left(\frac{95.52 + 16.5}{95.52}\right) = \textbf{7.0 mm} \)
Part b.

Assuming that the 2 : 1 method of stress increase holds good, the area of distribution of stress at the top of the clay layer will have dimensions of

\(B^{\prime} = width = B + z = 1.5 + 4.5 = 6\,m\\L^{\prime} = width = L + z = 1.5 + 4.5 = 6\,m\)

The diameter of an equivalent circular area, \(B_{eq}\), can be given as

\(\frac{\pi}{4} B_{ cq }^{2}=B^{\prime} L^{\prime} \\B_{ eq } =\sqrt{\frac{4 B^{\prime} L^{\prime}}{\pi}}=\sqrt{\frac{(4)(6)(6)}{\pi}}=6.77 m \\\frac{B_{e q}}{H}=\frac{6.77}{3}=2.26\)

From Table 7.4, for OCR = 3 and \(B_{eq}/H = 2.26,\,K_{cir(OC)} \approx 0.95.\) Hence

\(S_{p} = K_{cir(OC)}S_{p(oed)} = (0.95)(7.0) = \textbf{6.65 mm}\)
