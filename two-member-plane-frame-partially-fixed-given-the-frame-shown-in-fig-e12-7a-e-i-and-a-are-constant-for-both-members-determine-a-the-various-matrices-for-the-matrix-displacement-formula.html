Two-Member Plane Frame—Partially Fixed

Given: The frame shown in Fig. E12.7a. E, I, and A are constant for both members.

Determine: (a) The various matrices for the matrix displacement formulation. (b) Solve for the joint displacements, member forces, and reactions due to the loading defined in Fig. E12.7b. Use the following values for the parameters: L = 15 ft, I = 170 \(\text { in. }^{4}\), A = 10 \(\text { in. }^{2}\) , and E = 29,000 ksi.

Solution

Generate stiffness matrices:

Substituting for these matrices, the system stiffness matrix takes the following form:

\(K=\begin{bmatrix} \frac{AE}{L} & 0 & 0 & – \frac{AE}{L} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{12EI}{L^{3}} & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & 0 & -\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & \frac{4EI}{L} & 0 & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & \frac{2EI}{L} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -\frac{AE}{L} & 0 & 0 & (\frac{AE}{L}1.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.64) & -0.48(\frac{AE}{L} -\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}} (-0.8) & -(\frac{AE}{L}0.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.64 ) & -0.48(-\frac{AE}{L}+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) \\ 0 & -\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & -0.48(\frac{AE}{L}-\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & (\frac{AE}{L}0.64+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}1.36 ) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.4) & -0.48(-\frac{AE}{L} +\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & -(\frac{AE}{L}0.64+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.36 ) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}0.6 \\ 0 & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & \frac{2EI}{L} & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.4) & \frac{8EI}{L} & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & \frac{2EI}{L} \\ 0& 0 & 0 & -(\frac{AE}{L}0.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.64 ) & -0.48(-\frac{AE}{L} +\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & (\frac{AE}{L} 0.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} 0.64) & -0.48(\frac{AE}{L}-\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) &\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0.48(\frac{AE}{L}+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} )& -(\frac{AE}{L}0.64 +\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.36 ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & -0.48(\frac{AE}{L}-\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & (\frac{AE}{L}0.64+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.36 ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}} (0.6) \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & \frac{2EI}{L} & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} (-0.8) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & \frac{4EL}{L} \end{bmatrix} \)

\(K^{\prime}_{11}=\begin{bmatrix} (\frac{AE}{L}1.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.64 ) & (-0.48) (\frac{AE}{L}-\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) \\ (-0.48) (\frac{AE}{L}-\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & (\frac{AE}{L}0.64+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}1.36 ) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.4) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & \\ -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.4) & \frac{8EI}{L} & \frac{2EI}{L} \\ -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(0.6) & \frac{2EI}{L} & \frac{4EI}{L}\end{bmatrix} \)

\(K^{\prime}_{21}=\begin{bmatrix} -\frac{AE}{L} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & -\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & 0 \\ 0 & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}} & \frac{2EI}{L} & 0 \\ -(\frac{AE}{L}0.36+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.64) & (-0.48)(-\frac{AE}{L}+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}} ) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) & \frac{6EI}{L^{2}}(-0.8) \\ -(\frac{AE}{L}+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}})(-0.48) & -(\frac{AE}{L}0.64+\frac{12EI}{L^{3}}0.36 ) & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}0.6 & -\frac{6EI}{L^{2}}0.6 \end{bmatrix} \)
