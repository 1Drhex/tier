Compressed, reversible T = const, to \(V _{2}= V _{1} / 5\)

\(T _{ r1 }=\frac{353.2}{425.2}=0.831, P _{ r1 }=\frac{500}{3800}=0.132\)

From D.1 and D.3: \(Z_{1}=0.92, \quad\left( s _{1}^{*}- s _{1}\right)=0.143 \times 0.16=0.0230\)

\(v _{1}=\frac{ Z _{1} RT _{1}}{ P _{1}}=\frac{0.92 \times 0.143 \times 353.2}{500}=0.09296 m ^{3} / kg\)

State 2: \(v _{2}= v _{1} / 5=0.01859 m ^{3} / kg ; \quad T _{ r 2}= T _{ r1 }=0.831\)

From D.1: \(P _{ G }=0.325 \times 3800=1235 kPa\)

sat. liq.: \(Z_{ F }=0.05, \quad\left( s ^{*}- s _{ F }\right)= R \times 5.08=0.7266\)

sat. vap.: \(Z_{G}=0.775, \quad\left(s^{*}-s_{G}\right)=R \times 0.475=0.0680\)

\(\begin{aligned}& v _{ F }=\frac{0.05 \times 0.143 \times 353.2}{1235}=0.00205 m ^{3} / kg \\& v _{ G }=\frac{0.775 \times 0.143 \times 353.2}{1235}=0.0317 m ^{3} / kg\end{aligned}\)
