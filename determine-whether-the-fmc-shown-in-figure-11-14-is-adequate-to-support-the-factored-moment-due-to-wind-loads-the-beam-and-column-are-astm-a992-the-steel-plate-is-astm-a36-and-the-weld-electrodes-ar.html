Guest Question.

Determine whether the FMC shown in Figure 11-14 is adequate to support the factored moment due to wind loads. The beam and column are ASTM A992, the steel plate is ASTM A36, and the weld electrodes are E70XX.

Admin
Solution

From AISCM, Table 1-1,

Table 1-1 Structural steel material properties

d = 12.2 in., and

\(t_{f}= 0.38\) in.

Flange force:

\(P_{uf}= \frac{M}{d}=\frac{50}{12.2/12}= 49.2\) kips

Tension on gross plate area:

\(\phi P_{n}=\phi A_{g}F_{y}= (0.9)(0.375)(5)(36) = 60.7 kips \gt 49.2\) kips OK

Compression:

K = 0.65 (Figure 5-3)

L = 0.5 in. + 7.5 in. = 8.0 in.

\(r_{y}=\frac{t}{\sqrt{12} }= \frac{0.375}{\sqrt{12} } = 0.108\) in.

\(\frac{KL}{r} = \frac{(0.65)(8.0)}{0.108}= 48.0\)

From AISCM, Table 4-22,\(\phi F_{rc}= 28.7\) ksi.

\(\phi P_{n}=\phi F_{cr}A_{g}= (28.7)(0.375)(5) = 53.8 kips \gt 49.2\) kips OK

From equation (10-7),

\(\phi R_{n}= \phi R_{wl}+ \phi R_{wt}\) (10-7)

=\(1.392DL_{l} + 1.392DL_{t}\)
