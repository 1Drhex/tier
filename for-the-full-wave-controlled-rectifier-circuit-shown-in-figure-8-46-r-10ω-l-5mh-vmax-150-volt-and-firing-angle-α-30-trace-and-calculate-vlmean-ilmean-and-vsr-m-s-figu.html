Guest Question.

For the full-wave controlled rectifier circuit shown in Figure 8.46, \(R=10 \Omega, L=5 \mathrm{mH}\), \(V_{\max }=150\) volt, and firing angle \((\alpha)=30^{\circ}\). Trace and calculate \(V_{\text {Lmean }} I_{\text {Lmean, }}\) and \(v_{s_{r. m .s}}\) (Figure 8.47).

Admin
Solution

\( \begin{array}{c} \phi=\tan ^{-1} \frac{\omega L}{R}=8.9^{\circ} \\ V_{L_{\text {mean }}=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \int_{\alpha}^{\pi+f} V_{\max } \sin \theta d \theta \times 2=\frac{V_{\max }}{\pi}[\cos \phi+\cos \alpha]=88.5 \mathrm{volts}} \\ I_{L_{\text {mean }}}=\frac{V_{L_{\text {mam }}}}{R}=\frac{88.5}{10}=8.85 \text { Amper } \\ V_{s_{\text {RMS }}=}=\frac{V_{\max }}{\sqrt{2}}=\frac{150}{\sqrt{2}}=106.01 \mathrm{volts.} \end{array} \)
