Guest Question.

Sinfelt has studied ethane hydrogenolysis, \(C_{2}H_{6} + H_{2} \longrightarrow 2 CH_{4}\) over the Group VIII metals, and he has proposed the following sequence of steps on cobalt [55]:

\(C_{2}H_{6 \left(g\right) } \xrightleftharpoons[K_{-1}]{ K_{1}} C_{2}H_{5 \left(ad\right) } + H_{\left(ad\right)}\)

 

\(\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{5} \text { (ad) }+\mathrm{H}_{(\mathrm{ad})} \stackrel{\mathrm{K}_{2}}{\rightleftharpoons} \mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{4}(\mathrm{ad})+\mathrm{H}_{2(\mathrm{~g})}\)

 

\(C_{2}H_{4 \left(ad\right)} + H_{ 2 \left(ad\right)} \xrightarrow[]{K_{3}} 2 CH_{3 \left(ad\right) } \)

 

\(2 \mathrm{CH}_{3(\mathrm{ad})}+\mathrm{H}_{2} \stackrel{\mathrm{K}_{4}}{\rightleftharpoons} 2 \mathrm{CH}_{4}(\mathrm{~g})\)

Defining the rate as:

\(r= -d \left[C_{2}H_{6}\right]/dt = -d \left[H_{2}\right]/dt= L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2}H_{4}}P_{H_{2}} , \)

(a) derive the rate expression for this reaction, as written, in terms of C2H6 and H2 pressures. This sequence was taken from the paper as written.
(b) Rewrite the sequence to include active sites – note the subtle differences regarding the inclusion of active sites – and derive the rate equation again. What assumption must be made to bring agreement between the two rate expressions?

Admin
Solution

(a) \(r= \frac{-d \left\lfloor C_{2} H_{6} \right\rfloor }{dt} = L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} P_{H_{2}}\)

 

\(K_{2} = \frac{\theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} P_{H_{2}}}{\theta _{C_{2} H_{5}} \theta _{H}}\)

Use steady-state approximation for total surface carbon atoms to get another equation for\(\theta _{C_{2} H_{5}} \) ,

i.e, \(\frac{d \theta _{C \left(total\right) } }{dt} = 0\) . Then, because steps 2 and 4 are very rapid:

\(\frac{ d \theta _{C \ Total }}{dt} = K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} – L K_{-1} \theta _{C_{2} H_{5}} \theta _{H} – L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} P_{H_{2}} = 0 \Rightarrow \)

 

\(K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} – L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} P_{H_{2}}= L K_{-1} \theta _{C_{2} H_{5}} \theta _{H} \Rightarrow \theta _{C_{2} H_{5}} = \frac{K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}}- L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} P_{H_{2}} }{ L K_{-1} \theta _{H} } \)

 

\(\theta _{C_{2}H_{4}}= \left\lgroup\frac{K_{2} \theta _{H}}{P_{H_{2}}} \right\rgroup \left\lgroup\frac{K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} – L K_{3} \theta _{C_{2}H_{4}} P_{H_{2}} }{ L K_{-1} \theta _{H}} \right\rgroup = \frac{K_{2} K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} }{L K_{-1} P_{H_{2}}} – \frac{L K_{2} K_{3} \theta _{C_{2}H_{4}}}{L K_{-1}} \Rightarrow \)

 

\(\theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} = \left(1+ K_{2}K_{3}/K_{-1}\right)= \frac{K_{1}K_{2} P_{C_{2} H_{6}}}{ L K_{-1} P_{H_{2}} } \Rightarrow \theta _{C_{2} H_{4}}= \frac{\left( K_{1}K_{2} /LK_{-1}\right) P_{C_{2} H_{6}}/ P_{H_{2}} }{ \left(1+ K_{2} K_{3} /K_{-1}\right) } \) and

\(r=\frac{L K_{3} P_{H_{2}} K_{1} K_{2} P_{C_{2} H_{6}}/ L K_{-1} P_{H_{2}} }{ \left(1+ K_{2} K_{3} /K_{-1}\right) }= \frac{K_{1} K_{2} K_{3} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} }{\left(1+ K_{2} K_{3} /K_{-1}\right)} = K^{\prime } P_{C_{2} H_{6}} \)

but \(\theta _{C_{2} H_{4}}\) can be rewritten to give:

\(\theta _{C_{2} H_{4}} = \frac{\left(K_{1}/ K_{3}\right)P_{C_{2} H_{6}}/ P_{H_{2}} }{ L \left(1+ K_{-1}/K_{2}K_{3}\right) } \) so that \(r=\frac{K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}} }{1+ K_{-1}/K_{2}K_{3}} \)

which is notation consistent with reference 55.

(b) \(C_{2}H_{6}+ 2 S \xrightleftharpoons[K_{-1}]{K_{1}} C_{2}H_{5} \cdot S + H\cdot S\)

 

\(C_{2}H_{5} \cdot S + H\cdot S \xrightleftharpoons[]{K_{2}} C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S +S+ H_{2}\)

 

\(C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S + H_{2}+S \xrightarrow[]{K_{3}} 2 CH_{3} \cdot S\)

 

\(2 CH_{3} \cdot S + H_{2} \xrightleftharpoons[]{K_{4}} 2 CH_{4}+ 2 S \)

—————————————————————–

\(C_{2}H_{6}+ H_{2} \Longrightarrow 2 CH_{4}\)

 

\(r= – \frac{d \left[C_{2}H_{6} \right] }{dt} = K_{3} \left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] \left[S\right] P_{H_{2}} \)

 

\(K_{2} = \frac{\left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] \left[S\right] P_{H_{2}} }{\left[C_{2}H_{5} \cdot S \right] \left[H \cdot S \right]} \)

Steady-state approximation on all surface C atoms gives:

\(\mathrm{k}_{1} \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{6}}[\mathrm{~S}]^{2}-\mathrm{k}_{-1}\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{5} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right][\mathrm{H} \cdot \mathrm{S}]-\mathrm{k}_{3}\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{4} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right][\mathrm{S}] \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{H}_{2}}=0\)

 

\(\mathrm{k}_{1} \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{6}}[\mathrm{~S}]^{2}-\mathrm{k}_{3}\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{4} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right][\mathrm{S}] \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{H}_{2}}=\mathrm{k}_{-1}\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{5} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right][\mathrm{H} \cdot \mathrm{S}] \) and

\(\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{5} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right]=\frac{\mathrm{k}_{1} \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{6}}[\mathrm{~S}]^{2}}{\mathrm{k}_{-1}[\mathrm{H} \cdot \mathrm{S}]}-\frac{\mathrm{k}_{3}\left[\mathrm{C}_{2} \mathrm{H}_{4} \cdot \mathrm{S}\right][\mathrm{S}] \mathrm{P}_{\mathrm{H}_{2}}}{\mathrm{k}_{-1}[\mathrm{H} \cdot \mathrm{S}]}\)

 

\(\left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right]= \left\lgroup\frac{K_{2} \left[H \cdot S \right] }{ \left[S\right] P_{H_{2}}} \right\rgroup \left\lgroup\frac{K_{1} P_{C_{2} H_{6}}\left[S\right]^{2} – K_{3} \left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] \left[S\right] P_{H_{2}} }{K_{-1} \left[H \cdot S \right]} \right\rgroup \)

 

\(\left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] \left(1+ K_{2} K_{3}/K_{-1}\right)= \frac{K_{1} K_{2} P_{C_{2} H_{6}}}{K_{-1} P_{H_{2}}} \Rightarrow \left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] = \frac{\left(K_{2} K_{3}/K_{-1}\right) P_{C_{2} H_{6}}/ P_{H_{2}} }{1+ K_{2} K_{3}/K_{-1}}\)

Now, site balance to get [S] is:

\(L = \left[S \right] \left[H \cdot S \right] + \left[C_{2}H_{5} \cdot S \right] + \left[C_{2}H_{4} \cdot S \right] + \left[CH_{3} \cdot S \right]\)

Agreement with (a) is achieved only if [S]≅L, i.e., the surface is essentially free of all adsorbed species, i.e.,

\(\theta _{H} , \theta _{C_{2}H_{5}}, \theta _{C_{2}H_{4}} \theta _{CH_{3}}<<1 \). This a questionable assumption.
