Solution

The reaction forces are determined through solving Equations 8.40 and 8.41.

\(R_{xx}^b|_A+N_B^b+N_C^b+R_{xx}^b|_D=0\) (8.40)

 

\(u_0|_{AD}=\frac{\left(N_B^b+N_C^b+R_{xx}^b|_D\right)L_{AB}}{(\overline{AE}_{xx} )_{AB}}+\frac{\left(N_C^b+R_{xx}^b|_D\right)L_{BC} }{(\overline{AE}_{xx} )_{BC}}+\frac{\left(R_{xx}^b|_D\right)L_{CD} }{(\overline{AE}_{xx} )_{CD}} =0\) (8.41)

The effective axial rigidities provided by the beam cross-sections involved were determined as a part of and found to be

\((\overline{AE}_{xx})|_{AB}=(\overline{AE}_{xx})|_{BC}=20.6\times 10^6 N\)

 

\((\overline{AE}_{xx})|_{CD}=14.1\times 10^6 N\)

 

Using the values provided, Equation 8.41 becomes:

\(\frac{\left(25KN+R_{xx}^b|_D\right)(0.25m) }{(20.6\times 10^6N)}+\frac{\left(10KN+R_{xx}^b|_D\right)(0.5m) }{(20.6\times 10^6N)}+\frac{\left(R_{xx}^b|_D\right)(0.25m) }{(14.1\times 10^6N)}=0\)

 

Solving for the redundant force:

\(R_{xx}^b|_D= -10.1KN\)

 

The fact that \(R_{xx}^b|_D\) is negative implies that it acts in the direction opposite to that shown in Figure 8.16b.

The unknown reaction force \(R_{xx}^b|_A\) can now be determined through application of the Equation 8.40:

\(R_{xx}^b|_A+N_B^b+N_C^b+R_{xx}^b|_D=R_{xx}^b|_A+15KN+10KN – 10.1KN=0\)

 

\(R_{xx}^b|_A=14.9KN\)

 

Reaction \(R_{xx}^b|_A\) is also negative, implying that it acts the direction opposite to that shown in Figure 8.16b.
