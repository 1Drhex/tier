Guest Question.

A piston/cylinder system contains 50 L of air at 300°C, 100 kPa, with the piston initially on a set of stops as shown. A total external constant force acts on the piston so a balancing pressure inside should be 200 kPa. The cylinder is made of 2 kg of steel initially at 1300°C. The system is insulated so that heat transfer only occurs between the steel cylinder and the air. The system comes to equilibrium. Find the final temperature and the work done by the air in the process and plot the process P-V diagram.

Admin
Solution

C.V.: Steel + water out to ambient \(T _{0}\). This is a control mass.

Energy Eq.: \(U _{2}- U _{1}= m _{ air }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ m _{ st }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)={ }_{1} Q _{2}-{ }_{1} W _{2}\)

Entropy Eq.: \(S _{2}- S _{1}= m _{ air }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)+ m _{ st }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)=\int d Q / T +{ }_{1} S _{2 gen} ={ }_{1} S _{2 gen} \)

Process: \({ }_{1} Q _{2}=0\) and must be on P-V diagram shown

\(m _{\text {air }}- P _{1} V _{1} / RT _{1}-100 \times 0.05 /(0.287 \times 573.15)=0.0304 kg\)

 

Since \(V _{1 a }= V _{1} \text { then } T _{1 a }= T _{1}\left( P _{\text {float }} / P _{1}\right)=573.15 \times 200 / 100=1146.3 K\)

 

Use constant \(C _{ v}\) for air at 900 K: \(C _{ v }=\Delta u / \Delta T =0.833 kJ / kgK\) (from A.7)

 

To reach state 1a: \(\Delta U _{ air }= m C _{ v } \Delta T =0.0304 \times 0.833 \times(1146-573)=14.5 kJ\)

 

\(\Delta U _{ st }= m C _{ v } \Delta T =2 \times 0.46 \times(1146-1573)=-392.8 kJ\)

 

Conclusion from this is: \(T _{2}\) is higher than \(T _{ 1a }\) = 1146 K, piston lifts, \(P _{2}= P _{\text {float }}\)

 

Write the work as \({ }_{1} W _{2}= P _{2}\left( V _{2}- V _{1}\right)\) and use constant \(C _{ v }\) in the energy Eq. as

 

\(m _{ air } C _{ v }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)+ m _{ st } C _{ st }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)=- P _{2} m _{ air } v _{2}+ P _{2} V _{1}\)

 

now \(P _{2} v _{2}= RT _{2}\) for the air, so isolate \(T _{2}\) terms as

 

\(\left[ m _{\text {air }}\left( C _{ v }+ R \right)+ m C _{ st }\right] T _{2}= m _{\text {air }} C _{ v } T _{1} \text { air }+ mC _{ st } T _{1 st }+ P _{2} V _{1}\)

 

\([0.0304 \times 1.12+2 \times 0.46] T _{2}=0.0304 \times 0.833 \times 573.15 +2 \times 0.46 \times 1573.15+200 \times 0.05<br /> \)

 

Solution gives: \(T _{2}= 1 5 4 2 . 7 K , V _{2}=0.0304 \times 0.287 \times 1542.7 / 200=0.0673 m ^{3}\)

 

\({ }_{1} W _{2}= P _{2}\left( V _{2}- V _{1}\right)=200(0.0673-0.05)= 3 . 4 6 k J\)
