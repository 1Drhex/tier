Guest Question.

Consider the truss analysed in Worked Example 8.2 and determine the vertical displacement at joint C caused by a temperature rise of 30°C in members BD and DF.

The analysis of the stable primary truss obtained by releasing the diagonal members AD and DE is again used, with the virtual internal forces \( \overline{N} _6 \) associated with the unit vertical virtual force at C as shown in Figure 8.7 and repeated here in column 5 of Table 8.8.
Equating the external virtual work to the internal virtual work gives the vertical displacement at C (rounded to 3 significant figures):

\( v_C = +0.470 \) mm (i.e. upwards).
