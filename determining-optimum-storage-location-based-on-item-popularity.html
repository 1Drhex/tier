A receiving/shipping ratio of less than 1.0 indicates that fewer trips are required to receive the product than to ship. Therefore, products having ratios of less than 1.0 should be located closer to shipping. In order of importance of being close to shipping, the products with ratios less than 1.0 are E, B, C, D.
