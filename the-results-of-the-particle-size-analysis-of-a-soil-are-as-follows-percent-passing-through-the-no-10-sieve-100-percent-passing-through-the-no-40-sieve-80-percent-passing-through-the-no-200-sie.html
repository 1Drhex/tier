Guest Question.

The results of the particle-size analysis of a soil are as follows:
Percent passing through the No. 10 sieve = 100
Percent passing through the No. 40 sieve = 80
Percent passing through the No. 200 sieve = 58
The liquid limit and plasticity index of the minus No. 40 fraction of the soil are 30 and 10, respectively.
Classify the soil by the Unified classification system. Give the group symbol and group name.

Admin
Solution

Since 58% of the soil passes through the No. 200 sieve, it is a fine-grained soil. Referring to the plasticity chart in Figure 3.20, for LL = 30 and PI = 10, it can be classified (group symbol) as CL.
In order to determine the group name, we refer to Figure 3.22. The percent passing No. 200 sieve is more than 30%. Percent of gravel = 0; percent of sand = (100 – 58) – (0) = 42. Hence, percent sand > percent gravel. Also percent gravel is less than 15%. Hence, the group name is sandy lean clay.
