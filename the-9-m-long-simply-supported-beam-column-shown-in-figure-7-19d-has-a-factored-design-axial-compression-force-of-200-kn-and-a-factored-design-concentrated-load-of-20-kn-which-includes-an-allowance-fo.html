Guest Question.

The 9 m long simply supported beam-column shown in Figure 7.19d has a factored design axial compression force of 200 kN and a factored design concentrated load of 20 kN (which includes an allowance for self-weight) acting in the major principal plane at mid-span. Lateral deflections v and twist rotations φ are prevented at the ends and at mid-span. The beam-column is the 254 × 146 UB 37 of S275 steel shown in Figure 7.19a. Check the adequacy of the beam-column.

Admin
Solution

Design bending moment.

\(M_{y,Ed} = 45.0 kNm\) as in Section 7.7.2.

Simplified approach for cross-section resistance.

The cross-section resistance was checked in Section 7.7.2.

Beam-column member buckling resistance – simplified approach (Annex B).

While the member major axis in-plane resistance without lateral buckling was checked in Section 7.7.2, the lateral buckling resistance between supports should be used in place of the in-plane bending resistance to check against equation 6.61 of EC3.

 

From Section 6.15.2, \(M_{b,Rd} = 121.4 kNm.\)

From Section 7.7.2, \(N_{b,y,Rd} = 900 kN, kyy = 1.052, and M_{y,Ed} = 45.0 kNm.\)

 

\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{b,y,Rd}} + k_{yy}\frac{ M_{y,Ed}}{ M_{b,Rd}}=\frac{200}{ 900} + 1.052 × \frac{45.0}{ 121.4} = 0.222 + 0.390 = 0.612 ≤ 1\)

and the member in-plane resistance is adequate.

For the member out-of-plane resistance (equation 6.62 of EC3),

From Section 7.7.3, \(N_{b,z,Rd} = 449 kN, χ_{z} = 0.346, \bar{\lambda }_{z} = 1.490, \bar{\lambda }_{max} = 1.490, N_{cr,z} = 584.4 kN, and w_{z} = 1.5.\)

 

ψ = 0, \(C_{mLT} = 0.6 + 0.4 ψ = 0.6 > 0.4\)

 

\((1-\frac{0.1\bar{\lambda }_{z}}{(C_{mLT} − 0.25)}\frac{N_{Ed}}{χ_{z}N_{Rk}/γ_{M1}} )\)

 

\(=(1-\frac{0.1 × 1.490}{(0.6 − 0.25)} ×\frac{200 × 10^3}{0.346 × 47.2 × 10^2 × 275/1.0} )= 0.810\)

 

\((1 −\frac{0.1}{ (C_{mLT} − 0.25)}\frac{N_{Ed}}{ χ_{z}N_{Rk}/γ_{M1}})\)

 

\(=(1-\frac{0.1}{ (0.6 − 0.25)} × \frac{200 × 10^3}{ 0.346 × 47.2 × 10^2 × 275/1.0})= 0.873\)

 

\(k_{zy} = 0.873\)

 

\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{b,z,Rd}} + k_{zy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}}{ M_{b,Rd}} = \frac{200}{ 449}+ 0.873 ×\frac{45.0}{ 121.4} = 0.445 + 0.324 = 0.769 < 1\)

 

and the member out-of-plane resistance is adequate.

Beam-column member buckling resistance – more exact approach (Annex A).

The member in-plane resistance was checked in Section 7.7.2, but as for the simplified approach, equation 6.61 should be checked by taking the lateral buckling resistance in place of the in-plane major axis bending resistance. The interaction factor \(k_{yy}\) also requires re-calculating due to the possibility of lateral buckling.

 

\(\bar{\lambda }_{y} = 0.960, \bar{\lambda }_{max} = \bar{\lambda }_{z} = 1.490\)

 

Using \(M_{zx} = 111.2 kNm\) from Section 6.15.2,

 

\(\bar{\lambda }_{0} =\sqrt{132.8/111.2}= 1.093\)

 

Since cross-section is doubly-symmetrical, using equation 7.37 with

7.37, \(N_{cr,T} =\frac{GI_{t}}{i^2} \left(1 +\frac{π^2EI_{w}}{GI_{t}L^2} \right)\)

 

\(i_{p} =\sqrt{(I_{y} + I_{z})/A}=\sqrt{(5537 × 10^4 + 571 × 10^4)/47.2 × 10^2}= 113.8 mm,\)

 

\(N_{cr,T} =\frac{1}{ i^2_{p}}(G I_{t} +\frac{π^2EI_{w}}{ L^2_{cr,T}} )\)

 

\(=\frac{ 1}{ 113.8^2} (81000 × 15.3 × 10^4 + \frac{π^2 × 210 000 × 0.0857 × 10^{12}}{4500^2} )= 1636 kN.\)

 

Using \(α_{m}\) of equation 6.5 \(α_{m} = 1.75 + 1.05β_{m} + 0.3β^2_{m} ≤ 2.56.\)

for \(C_{1} with β_{m} = 0 leads to C_{1} = 1.75\)

 

\(0.2\sqrt{C_{1}}\sqrt[4]{(1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{cr,z}})(1 −\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{cr,T}})}\)

 

\(= 0.2 ×\sqrt{1.75}×\sqrt[4]{( 1 − \frac{200}{ 584.4})(1 − \frac{200}{ 1636})}\)

 

\(= 0.231 < 1.093 = \bar{\lambda }_{0}\)

 

\(a_{LT} = 1 – I_{t}/I_{y} = 1 − 15.4/5537 = 0.997 > 0\)

 

\(ε_{y} =\frac{M_{y,Ed}}{ N_{Ed}}\frac{A}{ W_{el,y}} =\frac{45 × 10^6}{ 200 × 10^3}× \frac{47.2 × 10^2} {433 × 10^3}= 2.453\)

 

Using \(N_{cr, y} = 1417\) kN from Section 7.7.2,

 

\(C_{my,0} = 1 − 0.18N_{Ed} /N_{cr, y} = 1 − 0.18 × 200/1417 = 0.975\)

 

\(C_{my} = C_{my,0} + (1 – C_{my,0})\frac{\sqrt{ε_{y}}a_{LT}}{1 +\sqrt{ε_{y}}a_{LT} }\)

 

\(= 0.975 + (1 − 0.975) × \frac{\sqrt{2.453} × 0.997}{1+ \sqrt {2.453} × 0.997}= 0.990\)

 

\(C_{mLT} = C^2_{my}\frac{aLT}{\sqrt{ (1 −\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{cr,z}} )( 1 −\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{cr,T}} } )} = 0.990^2 × \frac{0.997}{\sqrt{\left(1-\frac{200}{584.4} \right)\left(1-\frac{ 200}{1636} \right) } } = 1.287 > 1\)

 

\(W_{y} =\frac{W_{pl,y}}{ W_{el,y}} =\frac{483}{ 433} = 1.115,\)

 

\(n_{pl} = \frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{Rk}/γ_{M1}} = \frac{200 × 10^3}{ 47.2 × 10^2 × 275/1.0} = 0.154\)

 

\(C_{yy} = 1 + (w_{y} − 1)[( 2 − \frac{1.6}{ w_{y}} C^2_{my}\bar{\lambda }_{max}−\frac{1.6}{ w_{y}} C^2_{my}\bar{\lambda }^2_{max} ) n_{pl} – b_{LT} ]\)

 

\(≥ \frac{W_{el,y}} {W_{pl,y}}\)

 

\(= 1 + (1.115 − 1) [(2 − \frac{1.6}{ 1.115} × 0.990^2 × 1.490 − \frac{1.6}{ 1.115} × 0.990^2 × 1.490^2 ) × 0.154 – 0] = 0.942 > 0.896\)

 

Using \(χ_{y} = 0.693\) from Section 7.7.2,

 

\(µ_{y} = \frac{1 – N_{Ed} /N_{cr, y}}{ 1 – χ_{y}N_{Ed} /N_{cr, y}} =\frac{1 − 200/1417}{ 1 − 0.693 × 200/1417} = 0.952\)

 

\(k_{yy} = C_{my}C_{mLT}\frac{µ_{y}}{ 1 – N_{Ed} /N_{cr, y}}\frac{1}{ C_{yy}} = 0.9900 × 1.287\) \(× \frac{0.952}{ 1 − 200/1417}\frac{1}{ 0.942} = 1.499\)

 

\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{b,y,Rd}} + k_{yy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}}{ M_{b,Rd}} = \frac{200}{ 900} + 1.499 × \frac{45.0}{ 121.4} = 0.222 + 0.556 = 0.778 < 1\)

 

and the member in-plane resistance (with lateral buckling between the lateral supports) is adequate.

For the member out-of-plane resistance (equation 6.62 of EC3),

 

\(C_{zy} = 1 + (w_{y} − 1)[ (2 – 14 \frac{C^2_{my} \bar{\lambda }^2_{max} }{w^5_{y}} ) n_{pl} – d_{LT}] ≥ 0.6 \sqrt{\frac{W_{y}}{W_{z}} }\frac{ W_{el,y}}{ W_{pl,y}}\)

 

\(= 1 + (1.115 − 1)[( 2 − 14 × \frac{0.990^2 × 1.490^2}{ 1.115^5} ) × 0.154 – 0] = 0.722 > 0.46\)

 

Using \(χ_{z} = 0.346\) from Section 7.7.3,

 

\(µ_{z} =\frac{ 1 – N_{Ed} /N_{cr,z}}{ 1 – χ_{z}N_{Ed} /N_{cr,z}} = \frac{1 − 200/584.4}{ 1 − 0.346 × 200/584.4} = 0.746\)

 

\(k_{zy} = C_{my}C_{mLT}\frac{ µ_{z}}{ 1 − \frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{cr, y}} } \frac{1}{ C_{zy}} 0.6 \sqrt{\frac{W_{y}}{W_{z}} } =0.990 × 1.287 ×\frac{ 0.746}{ 1 − \frac{200}{ 1417} }× \frac{1}{ 0.722} × 0.6 ×\sqrt{\frac{1.115}{ 1.5}} = 0.793 \)

 

\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{ N_{b,z,Rd}} + k_{zy}\frac{ M_{y,Ed}}{ M_{b,Rd}} = \frac{200}{ 449} + 0.793 × \frac{45.0}{ 121.4} = 0.445 + 0.294 = 0.739 < 1\)

 

and the member out-of-plane resistance is adequate.
