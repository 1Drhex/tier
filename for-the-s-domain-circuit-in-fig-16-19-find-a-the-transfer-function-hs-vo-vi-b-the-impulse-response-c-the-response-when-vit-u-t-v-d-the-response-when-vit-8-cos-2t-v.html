Guest Question.

For the s-domain circuit in Fig. 16.19, find: (a) the transfer function \( H(s) = V_o/V_i \), (b) the impulse response, (c) the response when \( υ_i(t) =u (t) V \), (d) the response when \( υ_i(t) = 8 \cos 2t V \).

Admin
Solution

(a) Using voltage division,

\( V_o = \frac{1}{s+1} V_{\alpha b} \) (16.9.1)

But

\( V_{\alpha b}=\frac{1\parallel (s+1)}{1+1\parallel (s+1)}V_i =\frac{(s+1)/(s+2)}{1+(s+1)/(s+2)} V_i \)

or

\( V_{\alpha b}=\frac{s+1}{2s+3} V_i \) (16.9.2)

Substituting Eq. (16.9.2) into Eq. (16.9.1) results in

\( V_o = \frac{V_i}{2s+3} \)

 

Thus, the transfer function is

\( H(s) = \frac{V_o}{V_i} =\frac{1}{2s+3} \)

 

(b) We may write H(s) as

\( H(s) = \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{s+\frac{3}{2} } \)

 

Its inverse Laplace transform is the required impulse response:

\( h(t) = \frac{1}{2} e^{-3t/2}u(t) \)

 

(c) When \( \upsilon _i(t) = u(t),V_i(s)=1/s \) and

\( V_o(s) = H(s)V_i(s) = \frac{1}{2s(s+\frac{3}{2} )} = \frac{A}{s} +\frac{B}{s+\frac{3}{2} } \)

 

where

\( A=sV_o(s)\mid _{s=0} = \frac{1}{2(s+\frac{3}{2} )} \mid _{s=0} = \frac{1}{3} \)

 

\( B = \biggl(s+\frac{3}{2}\biggr) V_o(s)\mid _{s=-3/2} = \frac{1}{2s}\mid _{s=-3/2} =-\frac{1}{3} \)

 

Hence, for \( υ_i(t) = u(t) \),

\( V_o(s) = \frac{1}{3}\biggl(\frac{1}{s}-\frac{1}{s+\frac{3}{2} } \biggr) \)

 

and its inverse Laplace transform is

\( \upsilon _o(t) = \frac{1}{3} (1-e^{-3t/2}) u(t) V \)

 

(d) When \( \upsilon _i(t) = 8 \cos 2t \), then \( V_i(s) = \frac{8s}{s^2+4} \) and

\( V_o(s) = H(s)V_i(s) = \frac{4s}{(s+\frac{3}{2} )(s^2+4)} =\frac{A}{s+\frac{3}{2} } +\frac{Bs+C}{s^2+4} \) (16.9.3)

where

\( A = \biggl(s+\frac{3}{2}\biggr) V_o(s) \mid _{s=-3/2} = \frac{4s}{s^2+4} \mid _{s=-3/2} = -\frac{24}{25} \)

 

To get B and C, we multiply Eq. (16.9.3) by \( (s + 3/2)(s^2 + 4) \). We get

\( 4s = A(s^2+4)+ B\biggl(s^2+\frac{3}{2}s\biggr) +C\biggl(s+\frac{3}{2}\biggr) \)

 

Equating coefficients,

Constant: \( 0= 4A +\frac{3}{2}C \) \( \Rightarrow \) \( C= -\frac{8}{3}A \)

s: \( 4= \frac{3}{2}B+C \)

\( s^2 \) : 0 = A + B \( \Rightarrow \) B = -A

Solving these gives A = -24/25, B = 24/25, C = 64/25. Hence, for \( υ_i(t) = 8 \cos 2t V \),

\( V_o(s) = \frac{-\frac{24}{25} }{s+\frac{3}{2} } +\frac{24}{25} \frac{s}{s^2+4} +\frac{32}{25} \frac{2}{s^2+4} \)

and its inverse is

\( \upsilon _o(t) = \frac{24}{25}\biggl(-e^{-3t/2} +\cos 2t+\frac{4}{3}\sin 2t\biggr) u(t) V \).
