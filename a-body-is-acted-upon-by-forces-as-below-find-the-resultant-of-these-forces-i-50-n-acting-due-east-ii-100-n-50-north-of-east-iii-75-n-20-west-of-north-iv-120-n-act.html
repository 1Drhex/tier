Guest Question.

A body is acted upon by forces as below. Find the resultant of these forces.

(i) 50 N acting due East

(ii) 100 N \(50^o\) North of East

(iii) 75 N \(20^o\) West of North

(iv) 120 N acting \(30^o\) South of West

(v) 90 N acting \(25^o\) West of South

(vi) 80 N acting \(40^o\) South of East

All forces are acting from the point O.

Admin
Solution

Draw the sketch of all forces acting at point O. Show geographical axis North (N), South (S), East (E) and West (W). [Refer to Fig. 2.17(a)]

Note : \(50^o\) North of East means measure an angle of \(50^o\) from East toward North. Similar guideline is to be followed for all forces.

(i) \(\sum{}F_{x}=50 + 100 cos 50^o – 75 sin 20^o – 120 cos 30^o – 90 sin 25^o + 80 cos 40^o\)

 

\(\therefore \sum{}F_{x}= 7.95 N \left(\rightarrow \right)\) Ans.

 

(ii) \(\sum{}F_{y}=100 sin 50^o + 75 cos 20^o – 120 sin 30^o – 90 cos 25^o – 80 sin 40^o\)

 

\(\sum{}F_{y}= -45.91 N\)

 

\(\therefore \sum{}F_{y}= 45.91 N \left(\downarrow \right)\)

 

(iii) \(R=\sqrt{(7.95)^2 + (45.91)^2)}\)

R = 46.59 N

 

(iv) \(θ= tan^{-1}( \frac{45.91}{7.95})\)

 

\(\therefore θ= 80.18^o\)

 

(v) Position of the resultant w.r.t point O is as shown in Fig. 2.17(b).
