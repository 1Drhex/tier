Guest Question.

A reciprocating compressor of single stage and double acting type is running at 200 rpm with mechanical efficiency of 85%. Air flows into compressor at the rate of 5 m\(^{3}\)/min measured at atmospheric condition of 1.02 bar, 27°C. Compressor has compressed air leaving at 8 bar with compression following polytropic process with index of 1.3. Compressor has clearance volume of 5% of stroke volume. During suction of air from atmosphere into compressor its temperature rises by 10°C. There occurs pressure loss of 0.03 bar during suction and pressure loss of 0.05 bar during discharge passage through valves. Determine the dimensions of cylinder, volumetric efficiency and power input required to drive the compressor if stroke to bore ratio is 1.5.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Considering the losses at suction and discharge, the actual pressure at suction and delivery shall be as under.}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Atmospheric pressure, }P_{a}&= 1.02 bar, T_{a} = 273 + 27 = 300 K, V_{a} = 5 m^{3}/min\\\text{Pressure at suction, }\quad P_{1}&= 1.02 – 0.03 = 0.99 bar\\T_{1}&= 300 + 10 = 310 K\\\text{Pressure at delivery, }P_{2}&= 8 + 0.05 = 8.05 bar\\\text{Volume corresponding to}&\text{ suction condition of }P_{1}, T_{1},\\V_{1}&= \frac{P_{a} . T_{1} . V_{a}}{P_{1} . T_{a}} = \frac{1.02 × 310 × 5}{0.99 × 300} = 5.32 m^{3}/min\\\text{Therefore, work required}&\text{ for compression,}\\W&= \frac{n}{n – 1}P_{1} V_{1} \left[\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } – 1 \right] \\&= \left\lgroup\frac{1.3}{1.3 – 1} \right\rgroup × \frac{0.99 × 10^{2} × 5.32}{60} \left[\left\lgroup\frac{8.05}{0.99} \right\rgroup^{\frac{0.3}{1.3} } – 1\right] \\&= \text{23.66 kW or 31.73 hp}\\\text{Power input required}&= \frac{31.73}{0.85} = 37.33 hp\\&\text{Power input = 37.33 hp}\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Volumetric efficiency, }\eta_{vol}&= \frac{P_{1} T_{a}}{P_{a}T_{1}}\left[1 + C – C\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} } \right] \\\text{Here,}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad C&= 0.05, so \eta_{vol} = \frac{0.99 × 300}{1.02 × 310}\left[1 + 0.05 – 0.05 \left\lgroup\frac{8.05}{0.99} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{1.3} } \right] \\&= \text{0.7508 or 75.08}\%\\\text{Stroke volume per cycle}&= \frac{5}{2 × 200} = 0.0125 m^{3}/cycle\\\text{Actual stroke volume tak}&\text{ing care of volumetric efficiency = }\frac{0.0125}{0.7508}\\&= 0.0167 m^{3}/cycle\\\text{Stroke volume}&=\frac{\pi}{4} D^{2} L = 0.0167 \\\frac{\pi}{4} D^{2} × 1.5 D&= 0.0167\\D&= \text{0.2420 m or 24.20 cm}\\\text{Stroke L}&= 1.5 D = 36.3 cm\\\text{Cylinder dimensions}&=\text{bore of 24.20 cm, stroke of 36.3 cm.}\\\text{Volumetric efficiency}&=75.08\%\end{aligned}\)
