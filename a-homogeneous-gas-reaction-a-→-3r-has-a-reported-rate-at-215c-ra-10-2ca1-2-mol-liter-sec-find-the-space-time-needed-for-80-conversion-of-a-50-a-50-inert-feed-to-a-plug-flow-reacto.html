Guest Question.

A homogeneous gas reaction \(\mathrm{A} \rightarrow 3 \mathrm{R}\) has a reported rate at \(215^{\circ} \mathrm{C}\)

\(-r_{\mathrm{A}}=10^{-2} C_{\mathrm{A}}^{1 / 2}, \quad[\mathrm{~mol} / \text { liter } \cdot \mathrm{sec}]\)

Find the space-time needed for \(80 \%\) conversion of a \(50 \%\) A-50\% inert feed to a plug flow reactor operating at \(215^{\circ} \mathrm{C}\) and \(5 \mathrm{~atm}\left(C_{\mathrm{A} 0}=0.0625 \mathrm{~mol} / \mathrm{liter}\right)\)

Admin
Solution

For this stoichiometry and with \(50 \%\) inerts, two volumes of feed gas would give four volumes of completely converted product gas; thus \(\varepsilon_{\mathrm{A}}=\frac{4-2}{2}=1\) in which case the plug flow performance equation, Eq. \(17,\) becomes

\(\tau=C_{\mathrm{A} 0} \int_{0}^{X_{\mathrm{Af}}} \frac{d X_{\mathrm{A}}}{-r_{\mathrm{A}}}=C_{\mathrm{A} 0} \int_{0}^{X_{\mathrm{Af}}} \frac{d X_{\mathrm{A}}}{k C_{\mathrm{A} 0}^{1 / 2}\left(\frac{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1+\varepsilon_{\mathrm{A}} X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2}}=\frac{C_{\mathrm{A} 0}^{1 / 2}}{k} \int_{0}^{0.8}\left(\frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2} d X_{\mathrm{A}}\)

The integral can be evaluated in any one of three ways: graphically, numerically, or analytically. Let us illustrate these methods.

\(\begin{array}{lcc} {\text { Table E5.4 }} \\\hline X_{\mathrm{A}} & \frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}} & \left(\frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2} \\\hline 0 & 1 & 1 \\0.2 & \frac{1.2}{0.8}=1.5 & 1.227 \\0.4 & 2.3 & 1.528 \\0.6 & 4 & 2 \\0.8 & 9 & 3 \\\hline\end{array}\)

Counting squares or estimating by eye we find

\(\text { Area }=\int_{0}^{0.8}\left(\frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2} d X_{\mathrm{A}}=(1.70)(0.8)=\underline{\underline{1.36}}\)

Numerical Integration. Using Simpson’s rule, applicable to an even number of uniformly spaced intervals on the \(X_{\mathrm{A}}\) axis, we find for the data of Table \(\mathrm{E} 5.4\)

\(\begin{aligned}\int_{0}^{0.8}\left(\frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2} d X_{\mathrm{A}} &=(\text { average height })(\text { total width }) \\&=\left[\frac{1(1)+4(1.227)+2(1.528)+4(2)+1(3)}{12}\right](0.8) \\&=\underline{\underline{1.331}}\end{aligned}\)

Analytical Integration. From a table of integrals

\(\begin{aligned}\int_{0}^{0.8}\left(\frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}}\right)^{1 / 2} d X_{\mathrm{A}} &=\int_{0}^{0.8} \frac{1+X_{\mathrm{A}}}{\sqrt{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}^{2}}} d X_{\mathrm{A}} \\&=\left.\left(\arcsin X_{\mathrm{A}}-\sqrt{1-X_{\mathrm{A}}^{2}}\right)\right|_{0} ^{0.8}=\underline{\underline{1.328}}\end{aligned}\)

The method of integration recommended depends on the situation. In this problem probably the numerical method is the quickest and simplest and gives a good enough answer for most purposes. So with the integral evaluated, Eq. (i) becomes

\(\underline{\tau}=\frac{(0.0625 \mathrm{~mol} / \mathrm{liter})^{1 / 2}}{\left(10^{-2} \mathrm{~mol}^{1 / 2} / \mathrm{liter}^{1 / 2} \cdot \mathrm{sec}\right)}(1.33)=\underline{\underline{33.2 \mathrm{sec}}}\)
