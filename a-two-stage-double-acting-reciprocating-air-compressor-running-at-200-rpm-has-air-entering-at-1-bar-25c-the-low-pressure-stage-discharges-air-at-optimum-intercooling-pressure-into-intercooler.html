Guest Question.

A two stage double acting reciprocating air compressor running at 200 rpm has air entering at 1 bar, 25°C. The low pressure stage discharges air at optimum intercooling pressure into intercooler after which it enters at 2.9 bar, 25°C into high pressure stage. Compressed air leaves HP stage at 9 bar. The LP cylinder and HP cylinder have same stroke lengths and equal clearance volumes of 5% of respective cylinder swept volumes. Bore of LP cylinder is 30 cm and stroke is 40 cm. Index of compression for both stages may be taken as 1.2. Determine,
(i) the heat rejected in intercooler,
(ii) the bore of HP cylinder,
(iii) the hp required to drive the HP cylinder.

Admin
Solution

\(\text{Optimum intercooling pressure }= \sqrt{9} = 3 bar\\\text{LP stage pressure ratio = HP stage pressure ratio = 3}\\\text{From the given dimensions of LP cylinder, the volume of LP cylinder, in }m^{3}/min\\\begin{aligned}V_{LP}&= \frac{\pi}{4} × (0.30)^{2} × (0.40) × 200 × 2\\V_{LP}&= 11.31 m^{3}/min\\\text{Volumetric efficiency }&\text{of LP compressor, here ambient and suction conditions are same,}\\\eta_{vol, LP}&= 1 + C – C \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} } = 1 + 0.05 – \left\lgroup0.05\left\lgroup\frac{3}{1} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{1.2} } \right\rgroup \\\eta_{vol, LP}&= \text{0.9251 or 92.51}\%\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Volume of air inhaled in LP stage }&= V_{LP} × \eta_{vol, LP}\\&= 11.31 × 0.9251\\&= 10.46 m^{3}/min\\\text{Mass of air per minute, }\quad\qquad m&= \frac{P_{1}V_{1}}{RT_{1}}\\&= \frac{1 × 10^{2} × 10.46}{0.287 × 298} = 12.23 kg/min\\\text{Temperature after compression in}&\text{ LP stage,}\\T_{2}&= T_{1} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} }\\&= 298 × \left\lgroup\frac{3}{1} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.2 – 1}{1.2} }\\T_{2}&= 357.88 K\\\text{Volume of air going into HP cylind}&\text{er}\\V_{5}&= \frac{mRT_{5}}{P_{5}}\\\text{After intercooling, }T_{5} = 298 K, P_{5}&= 2.9 bar,\\V_{5}&= \frac{12.23 × 0.287 × 298}{2.9 × 10^{2}}\\V_{5}&= 3.61 m^{3}/min\end{aligned}\\\)Since the clearance volume fraction and pressure ratio for both HP and LP stages are same so the volumetric efficiency of HP stage referred to LP stage suction condition shall be same
\(\\\begin{aligned}\eta_{vol, HP}&= \eta_{vol, LP} = 0.9251\\\text{Hence, the volume of HP cylinder/}&\text{min} = \frac{V_{5}}{\eta_{vol, HP}} = \frac{3.61}{0.9251} = 3.902 m^{3}/min\\\text{Let bore of HP cylinder be }D_{HP}, & \\3.902&= \frac{\pi}{4} × (D_{HP})^{2} × 0.40 × 2 × 200\\D_{HP}&= \text{0.1762 m or 17.62 cm}\\\text{Heat rejected in intercooler, Q = m}& C_{p} (T_{2} – T_{5})\\&= 12.23 × 1.0032 × (357.88 × 298)\\&= 734.68 kJ/min\\\text{In HP stage, }\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad \frac{T_{6}}{T_{5}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{6}}{P_{5}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } ⇒ T_{6} = 298 × \left\lgroup\frac{9}{2.9} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.2 – 1}{1.2} }\\T_{6}&= 359.91 K\\\text{Work input required for HP stage, }&W_{HP} = \left\lgroup\frac{n}{n – 1}\right\rgroup mR (T_{6} – T_{5})\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}&= \left\lgroup\frac{1.2}{1.2 – 1}\right\rgroup × 12.23 × 0.287 . (359.9 – 298)\\W_{HP}&= 1303.62 kJ/min\\\text{or}\qquad\quad W_{HP}&= 29.14 hp\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Heat rejected in intercooler}&= 734.68 kJ/min\\\text{Bore of HP cylinder}&= 17.62 cm\\\text{Horse power required to drive HP stage}&= 29.14 hp\end{aligned}\)
