Guest Question.

Steam enters the turbine of a power plant at 5 MPa and 400째C, and exhausts to the condenser at 10 kPa. The turbine produces a power output of 20 000 kW with an isentropic efficiency of 85%. What is the mass flow rate of steam around the cycle and the rate of heat rejection in the condenser? Find the thermal efficiency of the power plant and how does this compare with a Carnot cycle.

Solution
\(\dot{ W }_{ T }=20000 kW \quad \text { and } \quad \eta_{ Ts }=85 \%\)

State 3: \(h _{3}=3195.6 kJ / kg , \quad s _{3}=6.6458 kJ / kgK\)

State 1: \(P _{1}= P _{4}=10 kPa , \text { sat liq }, x _{1}=0\)

\(T _{1}=45.8^{\circ} C , h _{1}= h _{ f }=191.8 kJ / kg , v _{1}= v _{ f }=0.00101 m ^{3} / kg\)

 

C.V Turbine energy Eq.: \(q _{ T }+ h _{3}= h _{4}+ w _{ T } ; \quad q _{ T }=0\)

\(w _{ T }= h _{3}- h _{4}\), Assume Turbine is isentropic

 

\(s _{4 s }= s _{3}=6.6458 kJ / kgK , s _{4 s }= s _{ f }+ x _{4 s } s _{ fg }, \text { solve for } x _{4 s }=0.7994\)

 

\(h _{4 s }= h _{ f }+ x _{4 s } h _{ fg }=191.81+ x _{4 s } 2392.82=2104.6 kJ / kg\)

 

\(w _{ Ts }= h _{3}- h _{4 s }=1091 kJ / kg , \quad w _{ T }=\eta_{ Ts } w _{ Ts }=927.3 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\dot{ m }=\frac{\dot{ W }_{ T }}{ w _{ T }}= 2 1 . 5 6 8 kg / s , \quad h _{4}= h _{3}- w _{ T }=2268.3 kJ / kg\)

 

C.V. Condenser: energy Eq.: \(h _{4}= h _{1}+ q _{ c }+ w _{ c } ; \quad w _{ c }=0\)

\(q _{ c }= h _{4}- h _{1}=2076.5 kJ / kg , \quad \dot{ Q }_{ c }=\dot{ m } q _{ c }= 4 4 7 8 6 k W\)

C.V. Pump: Assume adiabatic, reversible and incompressible flow

\(w _{ ps }=\int v dP = v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=5.04 kJ / kg\)

energy Eq.: \(h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ p }=196.8 kJ / kg\)

C.V Boiler : energy Eq.: \(q _{ B }+ h _{2}= h _{3}+ w _{ B } ; \quad w _{ B }=0\)

\(\begin{aligned}& q _{ B }= h _{3}- h _{2}=2998.8 kJ / kg \\& w _{ net }= w _{ T }- w _{ P }=922.3 kJ / kg \\&\eta_{ th }= w _{ net } / q _{ B }= 0 . 3 0 7\end{aligned}\)

Carnot cycle : \(T _{ H }= T _{3}=400^{\circ} C , T _{ L }= T _{1}=45.8^{\circ} C\)

\(\eta_{ th }=\frac{ T _{ H }- T _{ L }}{ T _{ H }}=0.526\)
