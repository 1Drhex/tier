Guest Question.

(Bra-ket algebra)

Consider the states \(| ψ〉=3i | \phi _1〉-7i | \phi _2〉 and | \chi 〉=-| \phi _1〉+2i | \phi _2〉\), where \(| \phi _1〉\) and \(| \phi _2〉\) are orthonormal.

(a) Calculate | ψ + χ | and 〈ψ + χ |.

(b) Calculate the scalar products 〈ψ | χ 〉 and 〈χ | ψ 〉. Are they equal?

(c) Show that the states | ψ 〉 and | χ 〉 satisfy the Schwarz inequality.

(d) Show that the states | ψ 〉 and | χ 〉 satisfy the triangle inequality.

Admin
Solution

(a) The calculation of | ψ + χ 〉 is straightforward:

\(|\psi +\chi 〉=|\psi 〉+|\chi 〉=(3i|\phi _1〉-7i|\phi _2〉)+(-|\phi _1〉+2i|\phi _2〉) \\ =(-1+3i)|\phi _1〉-5i|\phi _2〉. \) (2.41)

This leads at once to the expression of \(〈\psi +\chi |: \)

\(〈\psi +\chi |=(-1+3i)^\ast 〈\phi _1|+(-5i)^\ast 〈\phi _2|=(-1-3i)〈\phi _1|+5i〈\phi _2|. \) (2.42)

(b) Since \(〈\phi _1|\phi _1〉 =〈\phi _2|\phi _2〉=1,〈\phi _1|\phi _2〉=〈\phi _2|\phi _1 〉=0,\) and since the bras corresponding to the kets \(|\psi〉 =3i|\phi _1〉-7i|\phi _2〉\) and \(|\chi 〉 =-|\phi _1〉+2i|\phi _2〉\) are given by

\(〈\psi |=-3i〈\phi _1|+7i〈\phi _2|\) and \(〈\chi |=-〈\phi _1|-2i〈\phi _2|\), the scalar products are

\(〈\psi |\chi 〉=(-3i〈\phi _1|+7i〈\phi _2|)(-|\phi _1〉+2i|\phi _2〉) \\ =(-3i)(-1)〈\phi _1|\phi _1〉+(7i)(2i)〈\phi _2|\phi _2〉 \\ = – 14 + 3i, \) (2.43)

\(〈\chi |\psi 〉=(-〈\phi _1|-2i〈\phi _2|)(3i|\phi _1〉-7i|\phi _2〉) \\ =(-1)(3i)〈\phi _1|\phi _1〉+(-2i)(-7i)〈\phi _2|\phi _2〉= – 14 – 3i,\) (2.44)

We see that 〈ψ | χ〉 is equal to the complex conjugate of 〈χ | ψ〉.

(c) Let us first calculate 〈ψ | ψ〉 and 〈χ | χ〉:

\(〈ψ | ψ〉=(-3i〈\phi _1|+7i〈\phi _2|)(3i|\phi _1〉-7i|\phi _2〉)=(-3i)(3i)+(7i)(-7i)=58,\) (2.45)

\(〈χ | χ〉=(-〈\phi _1|-2i〈\phi _2|)(-|\phi _1〉+2i|\phi _2〉)=(-1)(-1)+(-2i)(2i)=5.\) (2.46)

Since 〈ψ | χ〉 = -14 + 3i we have \(|〈ψ | χ〉|^2=14^{2}+3^2=205.\) Combining the values of \(|〈ψ | χ〉|^2,\) 〈ψ | ψ〉, and 〈χ | χ〉, we see that the Schwarz inequality (2.34) \(|〈ψ | \phi 〉|^2\leq 〈ψ |ψ〉〈\phi |\phi〉. \) is satisfied:

\(205\lt (58)(5)\Longrightarrow |〈\psi |\chi 〉|^2\lt 〈\psi |\psi 〉〈\chi|\chi 〉. \) (2.47)

(d) First, let us use (2.41) and (2.42) to calculate 〈ψ + χ | ψ + χ〉:

\(〈ψ + χ | ψ + χ〉=[(-1-3i)〈\phi _1|+5i〈\phi _2|][(-1+3i)|\phi _1〉-5i|\phi _2〉] \\ = (-1 – 3i)(-1 + 3i)+(5i)(-5i) \\ = 35.\) (2.48)

Since 〈ψ | ψ〉 = 58 and 〈χ | χ〉 = 5, we infer that the triangle inequality (2.36) is satisfied:

\(\sqrt{〈ψ+\phi |\psi +\phi 〉}\leq \sqrt{〈ψ | ψ〉}+\sqrt{〈\phi | \phi 〉}. \) (2.36)

\(\sqrt{35}\lt \sqrt{58}+\sqrt{5}\Longrightarrow \sqrt{〈ψ + χ | ψ + χ〉} \lt \sqrt{〈ψ | ψ〉}+\sqrt{〈χ|χ〉}. \) (2.49)
