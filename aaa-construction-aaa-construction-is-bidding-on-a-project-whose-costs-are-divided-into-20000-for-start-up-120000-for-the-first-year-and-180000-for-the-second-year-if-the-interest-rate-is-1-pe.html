AAA Construction

AAA Construction is bidding on a project whose costs are divided into $20,000 for start-up, $120,000 for the first year, and $180,000 for the second year. If the interest rate is 1% per month, or 12.68% per year, what is the PW with annual compounding? With monthly compounding?
