Guest Question.

Saturated R-410A at -10째C is pumped/compressed to a pressure of 2.0 MPa at the rate of 0.5 kg/s in a reversible adiabatic process. Calculate the power required and the exit temperature for the two cases of inlet state of the R-410A:
a) quality of 100 %.
b) quality of 0 %.

Admin
Solution

C.V.: Pump/Compressor, \(\dot{ m }=0.5 kg / s , R -410 A\)

a) State 1: Table B.5.1, \(T _{1}=-10^{\circ} C , x _{1}=1.0\) Saturated vapor

\(P _{1}= P _{ g }=573.1 kPa , h _{1}= h _{ g }=275.78 kJ / kg , s _{1}= s _{ g }=1.0567 kJ / kg – K\)

Assume Compressor is isentropic, \(s _{2}= s _{1}=1.0567 kJ / kg – K\)

\(h _{2}=334.18 kJ / kg , T _{2}= 5 2 ^{\circ} C\)

Energy Eq.4.13: \(q _{ c }+ h _{1}= h _{2}+ w _{ c } ; \quad q _{ c }=0\)

\(w _{ cs }= h _{1}- h _{2}=-58.4 kJ / kg ; \quad \Rightarrow \quad \dot{ W }_{ C }=\dot{ m }w_{ C }=- 2 9 . 2 k W\)

 

b) State 1: \(T _{1}=-10^{\circ} C , x _{1}=0\) Saturated liquid. This is a pump.

\(P _{1}=573.1 kPa , h _{1}= h _{ f }=42.80 kJ / kg , v _{1}= v _{ f }=0.000827 m ^{3} / kg\)

Energy Eq.4.13: \(q _{ p }+ h _{1}= h _{2}+ w _{ p } ; q _{ p }=0\)

Assume Pump is isentropic and the liquid is incompressible, Eq.7.15:

\(\begin{array}{c}w _{ ps }=-\int v dP =- v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=-1.18 kJ / kg \\h _{2}= h _{1}- w _{ p }=42.80-(-1.18)=43.98 kJ / kg , \quad P _{2}=2 MPa\end{array}\)

Assume State 2 is approximately a saturated liquid \(\Rightarrow \quad T_{2} \cong-9.2^{\circ} C\)

\(\dot{ W }_{ P }=\dot{ m } w _{ P }=- 0 . 5 9 kW\)
