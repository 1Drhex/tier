Guest Question.

In a condenser the vacuum of 71 cm of Hg is maintained with barometer reading of 76 cm in Hg. Temperature in condenser is 35°C while hot well is at temperature of 30°C. The cooling water is circulated at the rate of 800 kg/min and condensate is available at 25 kg/min. The temperature of cooling water at inlet and outlet are 15°C and 25°C. Determine the mass of air in kg/m\(^{3}\) of condenser volume, dryness fraction of steam entering condenser and vacuum efficiency. Take mercury density as 0.0135951 kg/cm\(^{3}\), g = 9.81 m/s\(^{2}\)

Admin
Solution

\(\text{Absolute pressure in the condenser}\\\begin{aligned}p_{t}&= (76 – 71) × 10^{–2} × 0.0135951 × 10^{6} ×9.81 = 6668.396 N/m^{2}\\&= 6.67 kPa\\\text{Partial pressure of steam}&\text{ in condenser = Saturation pressure of steam corresponding to 35°C}\\\text{(from steam table)}\qquad\quad&\\p_{s}&= 5.62 kPa\\\text{Partial pressure of air, }p_{a}&= p_{t} – p_{s} = 6.67 – 5.63 = 1.04 kPa\\\text{Mass of air per m}^{3}\text{ of con}&\text{denser volume can be obtained from gas equation,}\\m_{a}&= \frac{p_{a} · V}{RT} = \frac{1.04 × 1}{(273 + 35) × 0.287} = 0.012 kg/m^{3}\end{aligned}\\\text{Let the enthalpy of steam entering condenser be h}_{s}\text{ so by heat balance,}\\\begin{aligned}m_{w} × C_{p_{w}} × (T_{w_{o}} – T_{w_{i}})&= m_{s} · (h_{s} – C_{p_{w}} · T_{c})\\m_{w}= 800 kg/min, m_{s} = 25 kg/min, T_{w_{o}}&= 25°C, T_{w_{i}} = 15°C, T_{c} = 30°C\\800 × 4.18 (25 – 15)&= 25 (h_{s} – 4.18 × 30)\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}h_{s}&= 1463 kJ/kg\\\text{Let dryness fraction of ste}&\text{am entering be x.}\\h_{s}&= 1463 = h_{\text{f at 35°C}} + x · h_{\text{fg at 35°C}}\\1463&= 146.68 + x · 2418.6\\⇒\qquad\qquad\quad\qquad \qquad x&= 0.5442\\\text{Vacuum efficiency}&= \frac{(76 – 5) × 0.0135951 × 10^{4} × 9.81}{((76 × 0.0135951 × 10^{4} × 9.81) – 5.63 × 10^{3} )}\\&= 0.9891 or 98.91\%\end{aligned}\\\text{Mass of air in kg/m}^{3}\text{ of condenser volume = 0.012 kg/m}^{3}\text{, dryness fraction of steam entering = 0.5442,}\\\text{Vacuum efficiency = 98.91}\%\)
