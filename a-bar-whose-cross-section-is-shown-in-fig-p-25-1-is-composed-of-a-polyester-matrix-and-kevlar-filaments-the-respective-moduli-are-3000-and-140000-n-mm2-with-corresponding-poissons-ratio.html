Guest Question.

A bar, whose cross-section is shown in Fig. P.25.1, is composed of a polyester matrix and Kevlar filaments; the respective moduli are 3,000 and \(140,000 N/mm^{2}\) with corresponding Poisson’s ratios of 0.16 and 0.28. If the bar is 1 m long and is subjected to a compressive axial load of 500 kN, determine the shortening of the bar, the increase in its thickness, and the stresses in the polyester and Kevlar

Admin
Solution

3.26 mm, 0.032 mm, \(9.78 N/mm^{2} , 456.4 N/mm^{2}\)
Use MATLAB for compressive axial loads (P) ranging from 300 to 700 kN in increments of 50 kN.
