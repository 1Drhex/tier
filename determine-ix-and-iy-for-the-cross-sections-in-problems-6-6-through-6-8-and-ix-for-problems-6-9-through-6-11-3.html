Determine Ix and Iy for the cross-sections in Problems 6.6 through 6.8 and Ix for Problems 6.9 through 6.11.

Admin
Solution
Component \(\Delta A\) \(I_{xc}\) \(d_{y}\) \(Ad^{2}_{y}\)

20 6.67 -6 720

\(I_{x} = \sum{I_{xc}} + \sum{Ad^{2}_{y}} = 346.7 +1440 = 1787 in.^{4}\)

\(I_{x} = \frac{bh^{3} – b_{1}h^{3}_{1}}{12} = \frac{(10)(14)^{3} – (6)(10)^{3}}{12} = 1787 in.^{4}\)
