A completely evacuated, insulated, rigid tank with a volume of 300 ft³ is filled from a steam line transporting steam at 800°F and 500 psia. Determine (a) the temperature of steam in the tank when its pressure is 500 psia and (b) the mass of steam that flowed into the tank.

Solution

(a) The energy equation used is (4.81). With Q = 0 and \( m_{i}=0, \text { we have } u_{f} m_{f}=m_{i} h_{1} \).
