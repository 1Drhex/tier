Guest Question.

The lines of actions of three forces concurrent at origin O pass respectively through point A, B, and C having coordinates

\( x_a=1 \) \( y_a=+2 \) \( z_a=+4 \)

\( x_b=+3 \) \( y_b=0 \) \( z_b=-3 \)

\( x_c=+2 \) \( y_c=-2 \) \( z_c=+4 \)

The magnitude of the forces are \( F_a = 40 \)N, \( F_b = 10 \)N and \( F_c = 30 \)N. Find the magnitude and direction of their resultant.

Admin
Solution

(i) Force vectors

\( \overline{F}_{a} = (F_a) \overline{e}_{OA} = (40) \biggl[\frac{i + 2 j + 4 k}{\sqrt{1^2+2^2+4^2} }\biggr] \) .

\( \overline{F}_{a} = 8.73( i + 2 j + 4 k)\) .

\( \overline{F}_{a} = 8.73 i + 17.46 j + 34.92 k\) .

\( \overline{F}_{b} = (F_b) \overline{e}_{OB} \).

\( \overline{F}_{b}= (10) \biggl[\frac{3i + 0 j – 3 k}{\sqrt{3^2+0^2+3^2} }\biggr] \) .

\( \overline{F}_{b} = 2.36(3 i + 0 j – 3 k) \) .

\( \overline{F}_{b} = 7.08 i + 0 j – 7.08 k \) .

\( \overline{F}_{c} = (F_c) \overline{e}_{OC} \).

\( \overline{F}_{c} = (30) \biggl[\frac{2i -2 j +4 k}{\sqrt{2^2+2^2+4^2} }\biggr] \) .

\( \overline{F}_{c} = 6.12(2 i – 2 j + 4 k) \) .

\( \overline{F}_{c} = 12.24 i – 12.24 j + 24.48 k \) .

(ii) Resultant vector

\( \overline{R} = \overline{F}_{a} + \overline{F}_{b} +\overline{F}_{c} \) .

\( \overline{R} = (8.73 + 7.08 + 12.24) i + (17.46 + 0 – 12.24) j + (34.92 – 7.08 + 24.48) k \) .

\( \overline{R} = 28.05 i + 5.22 j + 53.32 k \) .

(iii) Magnitude of resultant

\( R = \sqrt{(28.05)^2 + (5.22)^2 + (53.32)^2} \) .

R = 59.59 N.

(iv) Direction of \( R_x, R_y \) and \( R_z \) .

We know for \( \overline{R} = R_x i + R_y j + R_z k \) .

\( θ_x = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{R_x}{R}\Bigr) \) \( θ_y = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{R_y}{R}\Bigr) \) \( θ_z = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{R_z}{R}\Bigr) \).

\( θ_x = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{28.05}{59.59}\Bigr) \) \( θ_y = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{5.22}{59.59}\Bigr) \) \( θ_z = \cos^{-1} \Bigl(\frac{53.32}{59.59}\Bigr) \).

\( θ_x = 61.92° \) \( θ_y = 84.97° \) \( θ_z = 26.52° \).
