Guest Question.

The contents of the stirred-tank heating system shown in Fig. E4.10 are heated at a constant rate of \(Q( B t u / h )\) using a gas-fired heater. The flow rate \(w( lb / h )\) and volume \(V\left( ft ^{3}\right)\) are constant, but the heat loss to the surroundings \(Q_{L}( B t u / h )\) varies with the wind velocity \(v( ft / s )\) according to the expressions

\(\begin{aligned}Q_{L} &=U A\left(T-T_{a}\right) \\U(t) &=\bar{U}+b v^{2}(t)\end{aligned}\)

where \(\bar{U}, A, b\), and \(T_{a}\) are constants. Derive the transfer function between exit temperature \(T\) and wind velocity \(v\). List any additional assumptions that you make.

Admin
Solution

Additional assumptions

\(\begin{aligned}&\text{1. Perfect mixing in the tank}\\&\text{2. Constant density ρ and specific heat C.}\\&\text{3. } T_{i} \text{ is constant.}\end{aligned}\)

Energy balance for the tank,

\(\rho V C \frac{d T}{d t}=w C\left(T_{i}-T\right)+Q-\left(\bar{U}+b v^{2}\right) A\left(T-T_{a}\right)\)

Let the right-hand side be denoted by f(T,v),

\(\rho V C \frac{d T}{d t}=f(T, v)=\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial T}\right)_{s} T^{\prime}+\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial v}\right)_{s} v^{\prime}\) (1)

\(\begin{aligned}&\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial T}\right)_{s}=-w C-\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A \\&\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial v}\right)_{s}=-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right)\end{aligned}\)

Substituting for the partial derivatives in Eq. 1 and noting that \(\frac{d T}{d t}=\frac{d T^{\prime}}{d t}\)

\(\begin{aligned}&\rho V C \frac{d T^{\prime}}{d t}=-\left[w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A\right] T^{\prime}-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right) v^{\prime} \\&\rho V C \frac{d T^{\prime}}{d t}+\left[w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A\right] T^{\prime}=-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right) v^{\prime}\end{aligned}\)

Taking the Laplace transform and rearranging

\(\begin{aligned}&\rho V C s T^{\prime}+\left[w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A\right] T^{\prime}=-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right) V^{\prime} \\&{\left[\rho V C s+\left[w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A\right]\right] T^{\prime}=-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right) V^{\prime}}\end{aligned}\)

\(T^{\prime}=\frac{-2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right)}{\left[\rho V C s+\left[w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A\right]\right]} V^{\prime}\)

\(\frac{T^{\prime}(s)}{V^{\prime}(s)}=\frac{\frac{2 \bar{v} b A\left(\bar{T}-T_{a}\right)}{w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A}}{\left.\frac{\rho V C}{w C+\left(\bar{U}+b \bar{v}^{2}\right) A}\right] s+1}\)
