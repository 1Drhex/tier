Guest Question.

A smaller power plant produces steam at 3 MPa, 600°C in the boiler. It keeps the condenser at 45°C by transfer of 10 MW out as heat transfer. The first turbine section expands to 500 kPa and then flow is reheated followed by the expansion in the low pressure turbine. Find the reheat temperature so the turbine output is saturated vapor. For this reheat find the total turbine power output and the boiler heat transfer.

Admin
Solution

The states properties from Tables B.1.1 and B.1.3

1: \(45^{\circ} C , x =0: h _{1}=188.42 kJ / kg , v _{1}=0.00101 m ^{3} / kg , P _{ sat }=9.59 kPa\)

3: \(\begin{array}{cc}3.0 MPa , 600^{\circ} C : & h _{3}=3682.34 kJ / kg , & s _{3}=7.5084 kJ / kg K\end{array}\)

6: \(45^{\circ} C , x =1: h _{6}=2583.19 kJ / kg , s _{6}=8.1647 kJ / kg K\)

 

C.V. Pump Reversible and adiabatic.

Energy: \(w _{ p }= h _{2}- h _{1}\) ; Entropy: \(s _{2}= s _{1}\)

since incompressible it is easier to find work (positive in) as

\(\begin{array}{r}w _{ p }=\int v dP = v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=0.00101(3000-9.59)=3.02 kJ / kg \\h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ p }=188.42+3.02=191.44 kJ / kg\end{array}\)

C.V. HP Turbine section
Entropy Eq.: \(s _{4}= s _{3} \quad \Rightarrow h _{4}=3093.26 kJ / kg ; T _{4}=314^{\circ} C\)

C.V. LP Turbine section
Entropy Eq.: \(s _{6}= s _{5}=8.1647 kJ / kg K =>\text { state } 5\)

State 5: \(500 kPa , s _{5} \Rightarrow h _{5}=3547.55 kJ / kg , \quad T _{5}= 5 2 9 ^{\circ} C\)

C.V. Condenser.
Energy Eq.: \(q _{ L }= h _{6}- h _{1}= h _{ fg }=2394.77 kJ / kg\)

\(\dot{ m }=\dot{ Q }_{ L } / q _{ L }=10000 / 2394.77=4.176 kg / s\)

Both turbine sections

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ W }_{ T , tot } &=\dot{ m } w _{ T , tot }=\dot{ m }\left( h _{3}- h _{4}+ h _{5}- h _{6}\right) \\&=4.176(3682.34-3093.26+3547.55-2583.19)= 6 4 8 7 k W\end{aligned}\)

Both boiler sections

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ Q }_{ H } &=\dot{ m }\left( h _{3}- h _{2}+ h _{5}- h _{4}\right) \\&=4.176(3682.34-191.44+3547.55-3093.26)= 1 6 475 k W\end{aligned}\)
