Guest Question.

For the network of Fig. 8.29, determine:
(a) \(r_{e}\) .
(b) \(Z_{i}\) .
(c) \(Z_{o}\) .
(d) \(A_{v}\) .
(e) \(A_{i}\).
(f) Repeat parts (b) through (e) with \(r_{o} = 20 k \Omega\) and compare results.

Admin
Solution

(a) \(I_{B} = \frac{V_{CC} – V_{BE}}{R_{F} + \beta R_{C}} = \frac{9 V – 0.7 V}{180 k \Omega + (200) 2.7 k \Omega} = 11.53 \mu A\)

 

\(I_{E} = ( \beta + 1) I_{B} = (201)(11.53 \mu A) = 2.32 mA\)

 

\(r_{e} = \frac{26 m V}{I_{E}} = \frac{26 m V}{2.32 m A} = 11.21 \Omega\)

(b) \(Z_{i} = \frac{r_{e}} {\frac{1}{\beta} + \frac{R_{C}}{R_{F}}} = \frac{11.21 \Omega}{\frac{1}{200} + \frac{2.7 k \Omega}{180 k \Omega}} = \frac{11.21 \Omega}{0.005 + 0.015} = \frac{11.21 \Omega}{0.02} = 50(11.21 \Omega) = 560.5 \Omega\)

(c) \(Z_{o} = R_{C} \parallel R_{F} = 2.7 k \Omega \parallel 180 k \Omega = 2.66 k \Omega\)

(d) \(A_{v} = – \frac{R_{C}}{r_{e}} = – \frac{27 k \Omega}{11.21 \Omega} = – 240.86\)

(e) \(A_{i} = \frac{\beta R_{F}}{R_{F} + \beta R_{C}} = \frac{(200)(180 k \Omega )}{180 k \Omega + (200)(2.7 k \Omega)} = 50\)

(f) \(Z_{i}\): the condition \(r_{o} \geq 10 R_{C}\), is not satisfied. Therefore,
\(Z_{i} = \frac{1 + \frac{R_{C}\parallel r_{o}}{R_{F}}}{\frac{1}{\beta r_{e}} + \frac{1}{R_{F}} + \frac{R_{C}\parallel r_{o}}{R_{F} r_{e}}} = \frac{1 + \frac{2.7 k \Omega\parallel 20 k \Omega }{180 k \Omega}}{\frac{1}{(200)(11.21)} + \frac{1}{180 k \Omega} + \frac{2.7 k \Omega\parallel 20 k \Omega}{(180 k \Omega)(11.21 \Omega)}} = \frac{1 + \frac{2.38 k \Omega}{180 k \Omega}}{0.45 \times 10^{- 3} + 0.006 \times 10^{- 3} + 1.18 \times 10^{- 3}} = \frac{1 + 0.013}{1.64 \times 10^{- 3}} = 617.7 \Omega vs. 560.5 \Omega\) above
\(Z_{o}\):

\(Z_{o} = r_{o}\parallel R_{C}\parallel R_{F} = 20 k \Omega\parallel 2.7 k \Omega\parallel 180 k \Omega = 2.35 k \Omega vs. 2.66 k \Omega\) above

\(A_{v}\):

\(A_{v} = \frac{- \left[\frac{1}{R_{F}} + \frac{1}{r_{e}}\right](r_{o}\parallel R_{C}) }{1 + \frac{r_{o}\parallel R_{C}}{R_{F}}} = \frac{- \left[\frac{1}{180 k \Omega} + \frac{1}{11.21 \Omega}\right](2.38 k \Omega) }{1 + \frac{2.38 k \Omega}{180 k \Omega}} = \frac{- \left[5.56 \times 10^{- 6} – 8.92 \times 10^{- 2}\right](2.38 k \Omega) }{1 + 0.013} = – 209.56 vs. – 240.86\) above

\(A_{i}\):

\(A_{i} = – A_{v} \frac{Z_{i}}{R_{C}} = – (- 209.56) \frac{617.7 \Omega}{2.7 k \Omega} = 47.94 vs. 50\) above
