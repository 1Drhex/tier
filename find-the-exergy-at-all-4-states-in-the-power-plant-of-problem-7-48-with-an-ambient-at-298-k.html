Guest Question.

Find the exergy at all 4 states in the power plant of with an ambient at 298 K.

Admin
Solution

Flow exergy from Eq.8.22 neglecting kinetic and potential energy is:

\(\psi= h – h _{0}- T _{0}\left( s – s _{0}\right)\)

so we need (h,s) for all four states.

\(\begin{array}{c}P _{1}= P _{4}=20 MPa , T _{1}=700^{\circ} C \\h _{1}=3809.1 kJ / kg \\s _{1}=6.7993 kJ / kg K \\P _{2}= P _{3}=20 kPa , T _{3}=40^{\circ} C\end{array}\)

State 3: (P, T) Comp. liquid, take sat. liquid Table B.1.1

\(\begin{array}{l}h _{3}=167.5 kJ / kg, \\v _{3}=0.001008 m ^{3} / kg\end{array}\)

C.V. Turbine.
Entropy Eq.7.8: \(s _{2}= s _{1}=6.7993 kJ / kg K\)

Table B.1.2 \(s _{2}=0.8319+ x _{2} \times 7.0766 \Rightarrow x _{2}=0.8433\)

\(\begin{array}{l}h _{2}=251.4+0.8433 \times 2358.33=2240.1 kJ / kg \\w _{ T }= h _{1}- h _{2}=3809.1-2240.1=1569 kJ / kg\end{array}\)

CV. Pump, property relation v = constant gives work from Eq.7.15 as

\(\begin{array}{l}w_{P}=-v_{3}\left(P_{4}-P_{3}\right)=-0.001008(20000-20)=-20.1 kJ / kg \\h _{4}= h _{3}- w _{ P }=167.5+20.1=187.6 kJ / kg\end{array}\)

Flow exergy from Eq.8.23 and notice that since turbine work and pump work are reversible they represent also change in avalability.

\(\begin{aligned}\psi_{1} &= h _{1}- h _{0}- T _{0}\left( s _{1}- s _{0}\right)=3809.1-104.87-298(6.7993-0.3673) \\&= 1 7 8 7 . 5 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\psi_{2}= h _{2}- h _{0}- T _{0}\left( s _{2}- s _{0}\right)=\psi_{1}- w _{ T }=1787.5-1569= 2 1 8 . 5 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\psi_{3} &= h _{3}- h _{0}- T _{0}\left( s _{3}- s _{0}\right)=167.5-104.87-298(0.5724-0.3673) \\&= 1 . 5 1 k J / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\psi_{4}= h _{4}- h _{0}- T _{0}\left( s _{4}- s _{0}\right)=\psi_{3}- w _{ P }=1.51+20.1= 2 1 . 6 1 kJ / kg\)
