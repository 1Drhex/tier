Guest Question.

In a two stage reciprocating air compressor running at 200 rpm the air is admitted at 1 bar, 17°C and discharged at 25 bar. At low pressure stage suction conditions the rate of air flow is 4 kg/minute. The low pressure cylinder and high pressure cylinders have clearance volumes of 4% and 5% of respective cylinder stroke volumes. The index for compression and expansion processes in two stages are same as 1.25. Considering an optimum and perfect intercooling in between two stages determine the power required, isothermal efficiency, free air delivered, heat transferred in each cylinder and the cylinder volumes.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{For the optimum intercooling the pressure ratio in each stage }= \sqrt{\frac{25}{1}} = 5\\\begin{aligned}\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}}&= \frac{P_{6}}{P_{5}} = 5\\\text{Perfect intercooling indicates, }T_{1}&= T_{5} = 273 + 17 = 290 K\\T_{2}&= T_{1} \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } = 400.12 K\\T_{6}&= T_{5} \left\lgroup\frac{P_{6}}{P_{5}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } = 400.12 K\\\text{Actual compression work require}&\text{ment}\\W&= W_{HP} + W_{LP}\\&= 2 \left\lgroup\frac{n}{n – 1} \right\rgroup m RT_{1} \left[\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } – 1\right] \\&= 2 × \left\lgroup\frac{1.25}{1.25 – 1} \right\rgroup × 4 × 0.287 × 290 \left[(5)^{\frac{(1.25 – 1)}{1.25} } – 1\right] \\W&= \text{1264.19 kJ/min or 28.25 hp}\\\text{Work requirement if the process}&\text{ is isothermal compression,}\\W_{iso}&=m RT_{1} \ln \left\lgroup\frac{P_{6}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup = 4 × 0.287 × 290 \ln (25)\\W_{iso}&= 1071.63 kJ/min\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Isothermal efficiency}&= \frac{W_{iso}}{W} = \text{0.8477 or 84.77}\%\\\text{Free air delivered }&= \frac{m RT_{1}}{P_{1}} = \frac{4 × 0.287 × 290}{1 × 10^{2}} = 3.33 m^{3}/min\end{aligned}\\\text{Heat transferred in HP cylinder = Heat transferred in LP cylinder = Q}\\\text{(Due to optimum and perfect intercooling)}\\\begin{aligned}Q&= \left\lgroup\frac{W}{2} \right\rgroup – m C_{p} (T_{2} – T_{1})\\&= \left\lgroup\frac{1264.19}{2} \right\rgroup – 4 × 1.0032 × (400.12 – 290)\\Q&= 190.21 kJ/min\\\text{Volumetric efficiency }\eta_{vol}&= \frac{P_{1} T_{a}}{P_{a} . T_{1}}\left[1 + C – C\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} } \right] \\\text{Here the ambient conditions}&\text{ and suction conditions are same so expression gets modified as,}\\\eta_{vol}&= 1 + C – C \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} }\\\text{Volumetric efficiency of HP,}&\\\eta_{\text{vol, HP}}&= 1 + C_{HP} – C_{HP} \left\lgroup\frac{P_{6}}{P_{5}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} }\\C_{HP}&= 0.04\\&= 1 + 0.04 – 0.04 (5)^{1/1.25}\\\eta_{\text{vol, HP}}&= \text{0.895 or 89.5}\%\\\text{Volumetric efficiency of LP,}&\\\eta_{\text{vol, LP}}&= 1 + C_{LP} – C_{LP} \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{n} }\\C_{LP}&= 0.05\\&= 1 + 0.05 – 0.05 (5)^{1/1.25} =\text{ 0.8688 or 86.88}\%\\\text{Stroke volume of HP cylinder}&= \frac{\text{Free air deliver}}{\text{Pressure ratio × speed × } \eta_{\text{vol, HP}}}\\V_{s, HP}&= \frac{3.33}{5 × 200 × 0.895} = 3.721 × 10^{–3} m^{3}\end{aligned}\\\text{Clearance volume, }V_{c, HP}= 0.05 ×3.721 ×10^{-3} = 1.861 ×10^{-4} m^{3}\\\text{Total HP cylinder volume, }V_{HP} = V_{s, HP} + V_{c, HP }= 3.907 × 10^{–3} m^{3} ;\\V_{c, HP}=\text{Clearance volume of HP}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Stroke volume of LP cylinder }= \frac{\text{Free air delivery}}{\text{Speed × }\eta_{\text{vol , LP}}}&=\frac{3.33}{200 × 0.8688}\\V_{s , LP}&= 0.01916 m^{3}\end{aligned}\\\text{Clearance volume, }V_{c , LP} = 0.04 × V_{s, LP} = 7.664 × 10^{–4} m^{3}\\\text{Total LP cylinder volume, }V_{LP} = V_{s, LP} + V_{c, LP} = 0.019926 m^{3}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Power required}&= 28.25 hp,\\\text{Isothermal efficiency}&= 84.77\%\\\text{Free air delivered}&= 3.33 m^{3}/min,\\\text{Heat transfer in HP cylinder}&= 190.21 kJ/min\\\text{Heat transferred in LP cylin}&\text{der }= 190.21 kJ/min\\\text{HP cylinder volume}&= 3.907 × 10^{–3} m^{3}\\\text{LP cylinder volume}&= 0.019926 m^{3}\end{aligned}\)
