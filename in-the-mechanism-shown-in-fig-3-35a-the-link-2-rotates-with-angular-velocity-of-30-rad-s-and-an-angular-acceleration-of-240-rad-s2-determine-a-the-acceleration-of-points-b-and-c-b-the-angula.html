Guest Question.

In the mechanism shown in Fig.3.35(a), the link 2 rotates with angular velocity of 30 rad/s and an angular acceleration of 240 rad/s². Determine (a) the acceleration of points B and C, (b) the angular accelerations of link 3 and 4 and (c) the relative acceleration \( \alpha_{34} . O_{2} A=100 \) mm, AB=200 mm, AC=100 mm and BC=150 mm.

Admin
Solution

\( v_{a}=30 \times 0.1=3 m / s \).

The velocity diagram is shown in Fig.3.35(b) to a scale of 1 cm=1 m/s.

\( v_{b}=v_{a}+v_{b a} \).

\( v_{c}=v_{a}+v_{c a}=v_{b}+v_{c b} \).

\( v_{a}=o_{2} a \quad \text { and } \perp O_{2} A \).

\( v_{b}=o_{2} b \quad \text { and } \perp o_{4} b \perp=2.6 cm =2.6 m / s \).

\( v_{b a}=a b \quad \text { and } \quad \perp A B=2.3 cm =2.3 m / s \).

\( v_{c b}=b c \quad \text { and } \perp B C=1.7 cm =1.7 m / s \).

\( v_{c a}=o_{2} b \text { and } \perp o_{4} b=1.2 cm =1.2 m / s \).

\( v_{c}=o_{2} c \).

The acceleration diagram is shown in Fig.3.35(c) to a scale of 1 cm=20 m/s².

\( f_{b}^{n}=\frac{v_{b}^{2}}{O_{4} B}=\frac{2.6^{2}}{0.2}=64.8 m / s ^{2} \quad\left(\text { from } B \text { to } O_{4}\right) \).

\( f_{b}^{t} \perp f_{b}^{n} \).

\( f_{a}^{n}=\frac{v_{a}^{2}}{O_{2} A}=\frac{3^{2}}{0.1}=90 m / s ^{2}\left(\text { from } A \text { to } O_{2}\right) \).

\( f_{b a}^{n}=\frac{v_{b a}^{2}}{A B}=\frac{2.3^{2}}{0.2}=26.45 m / s ^{2}(\text { from } B \text { to } A) \).

\( f_{b a}^{t} \perp f_{b a}^{n} \).

\( f_{b}=f_{a}+f_{b a} \).

\( f_{b}^{n}+f_{b}^{t}=f_{a}^{n}+f_{a}^{t}+f_{b a}^{n}+f_{b a}^{t} \).

\( f_{b}=o_{2} b=2.5 cm =70 m / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{b}^{t}=b^{\prime} b=1.4 cm =28 m / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{b a}^{t}=b^{\prime \prime} b=6.2 cm =124 m / s ^{2} \).

\( \alpha_{3}=\frac{f_{b a}^{t}}{B A}=\frac{124}{0.2}=620 rad / s ^{2}( ccw ) \).

\( \alpha_{4}=\frac{f_{b}^{t}}{O_{4} B}=\frac{28}{0.2}=140 rad / s ^{2}( cw ) \).

\( \alpha_{34}=\alpha_{3}-\alpha_{4}=620+140=760 rad / s ^{2}( ccw ) \).

\( f_{c}=f_{a}+f_{c a}^{n}+f_{c a}^{t} \).

\( =f_{b}+f_{c b}^{n}+f_{c b}^{t} \).

\( f_{c a}^{n}=\frac{v_{c a}^{2}}{C A}=\frac{1.2^{2}}{0.1}=14.4 m / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{c a}^{t} \perp f_{c a}^{n} \).

\( f_{c b}^{n}=\frac{v_{c b}^{2}}{C B}=\frac{1.7^{2}}{0.150}=19.27 m / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{c b}^{t} \perp f_{c b}^{n} \).

\( f_{c}=o_{2} c=5 cm =100 m / s ^{2} \).
