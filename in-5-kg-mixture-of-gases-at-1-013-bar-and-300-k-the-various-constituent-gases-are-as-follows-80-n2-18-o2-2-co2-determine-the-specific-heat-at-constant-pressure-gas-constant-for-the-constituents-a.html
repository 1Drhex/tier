Guest Question.

In 5 kg mixture of gases at 1.013 bar and 300 K the various constituent gases are as follows, 80% \(N_{2}\), 18% \(O_{2}\), 2% \(CO_{2}\).

Determine the specific heat at constant pressure, gas constant for the constituents and mixture and also molar mass of mixture taking \(\gamma\) = 1.4 for \(N_{2}\) and \(O_{2}\) and \(\gamma\) = 1.3 for \(CO_{2}\).

Universal gas constant = 8314 J/kg · K

Admin
Solution
\( \text{Gas constants for constituent gases shall be,}\\\begin{aligned} R_{N_{2}}&= \frac{8314}{\text{mol.wt. of N}_{2}} = \frac{8314}{28} = \textbf{296.9 J/kg . K}\\R_{O_{2}}&= \frac{8314}{\text{mol.wt. of O}_{2}} = \frac{8314}{32} = \textbf{259.8 J/kg . K}\\R_{CO_{2}}&= \frac{8314}{\text{mol.wt. of CO}_{2}} = \frac{8314}{44} = \textbf{188.9 J/kg . K}\end{aligned}\\\text{Gas constant for mixture, }R_{mixture}\\\begin{aligned}&=\left\lgroup\frac{m_{N_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. R_{N_{2}} + \left\lgroup\frac{m_{O_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. R_{O_{2}} + \left\lgroup\frac{m_{CO_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. R_{CO_{2}} \\&= (0.80 × 296.9) + (0.18 × 259.8) + (0.02 × 188.9)\\R_{mixture}&= \textbf{288.06 J/kg . K}\end{aligned}\\\text{Specific heat at constant pressure for constituent gases.}\\\begin{aligned}C_{p, N_{2}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{\gamma }{\gamma – 1} \right\rgroup. R_{N_{2}} = \left\lgroup\frac{1.4}{0.4} \right\rgroup \times 296.9 = \textbf{1.039 kJ/kg . K}\\C_{p, O_{2}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{\gamma }{\gamma – 1} \right\rgroup. R_{O_{2}} = \left\lgroup\frac{1.4}{0.4} \right\rgroup \times 259.8 = \textbf{0.909 kJ/kg . K}\\C_{p, CO_{2}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{\gamma }{\gamma – 1} \right\rgroup. R_{CO_{2}} = \left\lgroup\frac{1.3}{0.3} \right\rgroup \times 188.9 = \textbf{0.819 kJ/kg . K}\\C_{p, mixture}&= \left\lgroup\frac{m_{N_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. C_{P ,N_{2}} + \left\lgroup\frac{m_{O_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. C_{P ,O_{2}} + \left\lgroup\frac{m_{CO_{2}}}{M} \right\rgroup. C_{P ,CO_{2}}\\C_{p, mixture}&= (0.80 × 1.039) + (0.18 × 0.909) + (0.02 × 0.819)\\&= \textbf{1.0276 kJ/kg . K}\\\text{Molar mass of mixture}&= Σ x_{i} . M_{i} = \frac{\text{Total mass of mixture}}{\text{Total no. of moles}}\\x_{i}&= \frac{n_{i}}{Σ n_{i}}, \text{here }M_{i} = \text{mol. wt. of ith constituent.}\\\text{No. of moles of constituent gases,}\\n_{N_{2}}&= \frac{m_{N_{2}}}{Mol.wt.N_{2}} = \frac{0.8 × 5}{28} = 0.143\\n_{O_{2}}&= \frac{m_{O_{2}}}{Mol.wt.O_{2}} = \frac{0.18 × 5}{32} = 0.028\\n_{CO_{2}}&= \frac{m_{CO_{2}}}{Mol.wt.CO_{2}} = \frac{0.02 × 5}{44} = 0.0023\\\text{Total mole numbers in mixture}&= n_{N_{2}} + n_{O_{2}} + n_{CO_{2}}\\&= (0.143 + 0.028 + 0.0023)\\Σ n_{i}&= 0.1733\\\text{Mole fractions of constituent gases,}\\x_{N_{2}}&= \frac{n_{N_{2}}}{Σ n_{i}} = \frac{0.143}{0.1733} = 0.825\\x_{O_{2}}&= \frac{n_{O_{2}}}{Σ n_{i}} = \frac{0.028}{0.1733} = 0.162\\x_{CO_{2}}&= \frac{n_{CO_{2}}}{Σ n_{i}} = \frac{0.0023}{0.1733} = 0.0133\\\text{Molecular wt. of mixture}&= \text{Molar mass of mixture =} Σx_{i} . M_{i}\\&= (0.825 × 28) + (0.162 × 32) + (0.0133 × 44)\\&=\textbf{28.87 kg/kmol}\end{aligned}\)
