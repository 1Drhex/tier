Guest Question.

Determine \( x_1, x_2 \) and \( x_3 \) for this set of simultaneous equations:

\( 25x_1 - 5x_2 - 20x_3 =50 \) .

\( -5x_1 + 10x_2 - 4x_3 = 0 \) .

\( -5x_1 - 4x_2 + 9x_3 = 0 \) .

Admin
Solution

In matrix form, the given set of equations becomes

\( \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 25 & -5 & -20 \\ -5 & 10 & -4 \\ -5 & -4 & 9 \end{matrix} \Biggr] \Biggl[\begin{matrix} x_1 \\x_2 \\x_3 \end{matrix} \Biggr] = \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 50 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \Biggr] \)

We apply Eq. (A.11) to find the determinants. This requires that we repeat the first two rows of the matrix. Thus,

\( Δ = α_{11}α_{22} α_{11 }α_{33} + α_{21}α_{32} α_{13} + α_{31}α_{12} α_{23} – α_{13}α_{22} α_{13} -α_{23}α_{32} α_{11} – α_{33}α_{12} α_{21} \) (A.11)

\( Δ = \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 25 & -5 & -20 \\ -5 & 10 & -4 \\ -5 & -4 & 9 \end{matrix} \Biggr] = 25(10)9 + (-5)(-4)(-20) + (-5)(-5)(-4) – (-20)(10)(-5) – (-4)(-4)25 – 9(-5)(-5) = 2250 – 400 – 100 – 1000 – 400 – 225 = 125 \)

Similarly,

\( Δ_1 = \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 50 & -5 & -20 \\ 0 & 10 & -4 \\ 0 & -4 & 9 \end{matrix} \Biggr] = 4500 + 0 + 0- 0 – 800 – 0 = 3700 \) .

\( Δ_2 = \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 25 & 50 & -20 \\ -5 & 0 & -4 \\ -5 & 0 & 9 \end{matrix} \Biggr] = 0 + 0 +1000 – 0 – 0 + 2250 = 3250 \) .

\( Δ_3 = \Biggl[\begin{matrix} 25 & -5 & 50 \\ -5 & 10 & 0 \\ -5 & -4 & 0 \end{matrix} \Biggr] = 0 + 1000 + 0 + 2500 – 0 – 0 = 3500 \)

Hence, we now find

\( x_1 = \frac{ Δ_1}{ Δ} = \frac{3700}{125} = 29.6 \).

\( x_2 = \frac{ Δ_2}{ Δ} = \frac{3250}{125} =26 \) .

\( x_1 = \frac{ Δ_3}{ Δ} = \frac{3500}{125} = 28 \).
