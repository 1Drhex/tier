Guest Question.

Consider the (eight) n = 2 states, \( \left|2 \ell m_{\ell} m_{s}\right\rangle \). Find the energy of each state, under strong-field Zeeman splitting. Express each answer as the sum of three terms: the Bohr energy, the fine-structure (proportional to α²), and the Zeeman contribution (proportional to \( \mu_{B} B_{ ext } \)). If you ignore fine structure altogether, how many distinct levels are there, and what are their degeneracies?

Admin
Solution

The Bohr energy is the same for all of them: \( E_{2}=-13.6 eV / 2^{2}=-3.4 eV \). The Zeeman contribution is the second term in Eq. 7.83: \( \mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }}\left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \). The fine structure is given by Eq. 7.86: \( E_{ fs }^{1}=(13.6 eV / 8) \alpha^{2}\{\cdots\}= (1.7 eV ) \alpha^{2}\{\cdots\} \). In the table below I record the 8 states, the value of \( \left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \), the value of \( \{\cdots\} \equiv \frac{3}{8}-\left[\frac{\ell(\ell+1)-m_{\ell} m_{s}}{\ell(\ell+1 / 2)(\ell+1)}\right] \), and (in the last column) the total energy, \( -3.4 eV \left[1-\left(\alpha^{2} / 2\right)\{\cdots\}\right]+\left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }} \).

\( E_{n m_{l} m_{s}}=-\frac{13.6 eV }{n^{2}}+\mu_{B} B_{ ext }\left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \) (7.83).

\( E_{ fs }^{1}=\frac{13.6 eV }{n^{3}} \alpha^{2}\left\{\frac{3}{4 n}-\left[\frac{\ell(\ell+1)-m_{\ell} m_{s}}{\ell(\ell+1 / 2)(\ell+1)}\right]\right\} \) (7.86).

Total Energy \( \{\cdots\} \) \( \left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \) \( \text { State }=\left|n \ell m_{\ell} m_{s}\right\rangle\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(5 / 16) \alpha^{2}\right]+\mu_{B} B_{ ext } \) -5/8 1 \( |1\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 0 & 0 & \left.\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(5 / 16) \alpha^{2}\right]-\mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }} \) -5/8 -1 \( |2\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 0 & 0 & \left.-\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(1 / 16) \alpha^{2}\right]+2 \mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }} \) -1/8 2 \( |3\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{ll} 21 & 1 & \left.\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(1 / 16) \alpha^{2}\right]-2 \mu_{B} B_{ ext } \) -1/8 -2 \( |4\rangle=\left|21-1-\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(7 / 48) \alpha^{2}\right]+\mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }} \) -7/24 1 \( |5\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 1 & 0 & \left.\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(7 / 48) \alpha^{2}\right]-\mu_{B} B_{\text {ext }} \) -7/24 -1 \( |6\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 1 & 0 & \left.-\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(11 / 48) \alpha^{2}\right] \) -11/24 0 \( |7\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 1 & \left.1-\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)
\( -3.4 eV \left[1+(11 / 48) \alpha^{2}\right] \) -11/24 0 \( |8\rangle=\mid \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 1 & \left.-1\frac{1}{2}\right\rangle\end{array}\)

Ignoring fine structure there are five distinct levels-corresponding to the possible values of \( \left(m_{\ell}+2 m_{s}\right) \).

\( \begin{array}{lllllll} 2 & (d=1) ; 1 & (d=2) ; 0 & (d=2) ;-1 & (d=2) ;-2 & (d=1)\end{array} \).
