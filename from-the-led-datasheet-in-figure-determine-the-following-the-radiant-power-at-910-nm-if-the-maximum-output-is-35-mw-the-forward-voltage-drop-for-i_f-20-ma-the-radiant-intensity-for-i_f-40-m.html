Solution

From the graph in Figure (c), the relative radiant power at 910 nm is approximately 0.25 and the peak radiant power is 35 mW. Therefore, the radiant power at 910 nm is

\( \phi_{e} = 0.25 \left ( 35 mW \right ) = 8.75 mW\)

From the graph in part (b), \( V_{F} \cong 1.25 V for I_{F} = 20 mA. \)

From the graph in part (e), \( I_{e} \cong 10 mW/sr for I_{F} = 40 mA. \)
