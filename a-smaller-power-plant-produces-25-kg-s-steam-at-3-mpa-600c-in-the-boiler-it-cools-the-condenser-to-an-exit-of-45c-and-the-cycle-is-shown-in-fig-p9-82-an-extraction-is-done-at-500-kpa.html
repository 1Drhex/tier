Guest Question.

A smaller power plant produces 25 kg/s steam at 3 MPa, 600°C, in the boiler. It cools the condenser to an exit of 45°C and the cycle is shown in Fig. P9.82. An extraction is done at 500 kPa to an open feedwater heater, in addition a steam supply of 5 kg/s is taken out and not returned. The missing 5 kg/s water is added to the feedwater heater from a 20°C, 500 kPa source. Find the needed extraction flow rate to cover both the feedwater heater and the steam supply. Find the total turbine power output.

Admin
Solution

The states properties from Tables B.1.1 and B.1.3

1: 45°C, x = 0: \(h _{1}=188.42 kJ / kg , v _{1}=0.00101 m ^{3} / kg , P _{ sat }=9.59 kPa\)

5: 3.0 MPa, 600°C: \(h _{5}=3682.34 kJ / kg , \quad s _{5}=7.5084 kJ / kg K\)

3: 500 kPa, x = 0: \(h _{3}=640.21 kJ / kg\) 8: \(h _{8}=84.41 kJ / kg\)

6: 500 kPa, \(s _{6}= s _{5}\) from HP turbine, \(h _{6}=3093.26 kJ / kg\)

C.V. Pump 1. Reversible and adiabatic. Incompressible so v = constant

Energy:

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ p1 }= h _{2}- h _{1}=\int v d P = v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right) \\&\quad=0.00101(500-9.6)=0.495 kJ / kg \\& h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ p 1}=188.42+0.495=188.915 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Turbine sections

Entropy Eq.: \(s _{7}= s _{5}=7.5084 kJ / kg K \Rightarrow \text { two-phase state }\)

\(\begin{aligned}s _{7} &=7.5084=0.6386+ x _{7} \times 7.5261 \Rightarrow x _{7}=0.9128 \\h _{7} &=188.42+0.9128 \times 2394.77=2374.4 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Feedwater heater, including the make-up water flow, \(y =\dot{ m }_{6} / \dot{ m }_{5}\).

Energy eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{8} h _{8}+\left(\dot{ m }_{5}-\dot{ m }_{6}\right) h _{2}+\left(\dot{ m }_{6}-\dot{ m }_{8}\right) h _{6}=\dot{ m }_{5} h _{3}\)

Divide by \(\dot{ m }_{5}\) and solve for y

\(\begin{aligned}y &=\frac{ h _{3}- h _{2}+\left( h _{6}- h _{8}\right) \dot{ m }_{8} / \dot{ m }_{5}}{ h _{6}- h _{2}}=\frac{640.21-188.915+(3093.26-84.41) 5 / 25}{3093.26-188.915} \\&=0.3626\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{6}= y \dot{ m }_{5}=0.3626 \times 25= 9 . 0 6 5 kg / s\)

 

C.V. Turbine energy equation

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ W }_{ T } &=\dot{ m }_{5} h _{5}-\dot{ m }_{6} h _{6}-\dot{ m }_{7} h _{7} \\&=25 \times 3682.34-9.065 \times 3093.26-16.935 \times 2374.4 \\&= 2 6 1 8 2 k W\end{aligned}\)
