Solution

The structure has 5 degrees of freedom with a degree of statical indeterminacy of 2. Freedom codes corresponding to the 5 degrees of freedom are shown in Figure 1.26b. The fixed-end force vector for member 1 is

\(\left\{P_{F}\right\} =\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 24 \\ 16 \\ 0 \\ 24 \\ -16 \end{matrix} \right \} \)

The equivalent force vector is

\(\left\{P_{E}\right\}=\left\{p^{g}_{E} \right\} =\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ -24 \\ -16 \\ 0 \\ -24 \\ 16 \end{matrix} \right \} \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{matrix} \)

which is added to the externally applied force to form

\(\left\{F\right\} =\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ -104 \\ 16 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right \} \begin{matrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \\ 4 \\ 5 \end{matrix}\)

The orientations of the members are member 1: \( \alpha = 0^{\circ}\), member 2: \( \alpha = -45^{\circ}\). The stiffness matrices of the members in the global coordinate system are

Hence, the structure stiffness matrix is assembled as

\([K]=\left [ \begin{matrix} 1.473\times 10^{7} & -4.698 \times 10^{6}& 4.714\times 10^{4} & -4.730 \times 10^{6}& 4.714\times 10^{4} \\ & 4.767\times 10^{6} & -2.786 \times 10^{4}& 4.698\times 10^{6} & 4.714\times 10^{4} \\ & & 3.886\times 10^{5} & -4.714 \times 10^{4}& 9.428\times 10^{4} \\ & Symmetric & & 4.730\times 10^{6} & -4.714 \times 10^{4}\\ & & & & 1.886\times 10^{5} \end{matrix} \right ] \)

By solving the structure equilibrium equation, the displacement vector is determined as

\(\left\{D\right\}=\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ -2.017 \times10^{-3}\\ -1.180 \times10^{-4}\\ 2.013 \times10^{-3} \\ 1.066 \times10^{-3} \end{matrix} \right \} \begin{matrix} m \\ m \\ radian \\ m \\ radian \end{matrix} \\ \left\{P\right\}_{1} =[K_{e}]\left\{d\right\}+\left\{P_{F}\right\} = \left\{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 66.8 \\ 139.5 \\ 0 \\ -66.8 \\ 127.7 \end{matrix} \right \}+\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 24 \\ 16 \\ 0 \\ 24 \\ -16 \end{matrix} \right \} =\left \{ \begin{matrix} 0 \\ 90.8 \\ 155.5 \\ 0 \\ -42.8 \\ 111.7 \end{matrix} \right \} \begin{matrix} kN \\ kN \\ kNm \\ kN \\ kN \\ kNm \end{matrix} \\ \left\{P\right\} _{2}=[K_{e}]\left\{d\right\}=\left\{ \begin{matrix} 26.3 \\ -26.3 \\ -111.7 \\ -26.3 \\ 26.3 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right\} \begin{matrix} kN \\ kN \\ kNm \\ kN \\ kN \\ kNm \end{matrix} \)

The shear force and bending moment diagrams of the structure are shown in Figure 1.27.
