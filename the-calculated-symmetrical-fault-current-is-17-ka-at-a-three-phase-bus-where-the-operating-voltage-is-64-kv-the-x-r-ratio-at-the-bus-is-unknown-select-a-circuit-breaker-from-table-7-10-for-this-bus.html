Guest Question.

Circuit breaker selection

The calculated symmetrical fault current is 17 kA at a three-phase bus where the operating voltage is 64 kV. The X/R ratio at the bus is unknown. Select a circuit breaker from Table 7.10 for this bus.

Admin
Solution

The 69-kV-class breaker has a symmetrical interrupting capability \(I\left(V_{max} /V\right) \) = 19(72.5/64) = 21.5 kA at the operating voltage V = 64 kV. The calculated symmetrical fault current, 17 kA, is less than 80% of this capability (less than 0.80 Ã— 21.5 = 17.2 kA), which is a requirement when X/R is unknown. Therefore, select the 69-kV-class breaker from Table 7.10.
