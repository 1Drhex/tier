Guest Question.

A rigid shallow foundation 1 m × 1 m in plan is shown in Figure 11.4. Calculate the elastic settlement at the center of the foundation

Admin
Solution

Given: B = 1 m and L = 1 m. Note that .\(\bar{z}=5m \) From Eq. (11.10),

\( E_{s} =\frac{\sum{E_{s(i)}\Delta z } }{\bar{z} } =\frac{(8000)(2)+(6000)(1)+(100000)(2)}{5} =8400 kN/m^{2}\)

For the center of the foundation,

α=4

\(m^{\prime } =\frac{L}{B} =\frac{1}{1} =1\)

 

 

\(n^{\prime }=\frac{H}{(\frac{B}{2} )} =\frac{5}{(\frac{1}{2} )} =10\)

From Tables 11.1 and 11.2,\(F_{1} =0.498 \)and \(F_{2}=0.016\)

From Eq. (11.2),

\(I_{s} =F_{1} +\frac{1-2\mu _{s} }{1-\mu _{s}} F_{2} =0.498+\frac{1-0.6}{1-0.3} (0.016)=0.507\)

Again, \(\frac{D_{f} }{B} =\frac{1}{1} =1,\frac{L}{B} =1,\mu _{s} =0.3 \).From Table 11.3, \(I_{f} =0.65\).Hence,

\(.S_{e(flexible)} =\Delta \sigma (\alpha B^{\prime } )\frac{1-\mu ^{2}_{S} }{E_{S} } I_{s} I_{f}\)

 

\(=(200)(4\times \frac{1}{2} )(\frac{1-0.3^{2} }{8400} )(0.507)(0.65)=0.0143 m = 14.3 mm\)

Since the foundation is rigid, from Eq. (11.9), \(S_{e(rigid)} \approx 0.93S_{e(flexible, center)} \)

\(S_{e(rigid)}=(0.93)(14.3)=13.3mm\)
