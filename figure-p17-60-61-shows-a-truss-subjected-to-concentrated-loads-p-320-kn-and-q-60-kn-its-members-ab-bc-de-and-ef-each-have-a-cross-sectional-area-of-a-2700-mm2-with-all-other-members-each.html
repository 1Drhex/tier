Guest Question.

Figure P17.60/61 shows a truss subjected to concentrated loads P = 320 kN and Q = 60 kN. Its members AB, BC, DE, and EF each have a cross-sectional area of A = 2,700 \(mm ^{2}\) , with all other members each having a crosssectional area of A = 1,060 \(mm ^{2}\) . All members are made of steel [E = 200 GPa]. For the given loads, utilize the virtualwork method to calculate the horizontal displacement of (a) joint F and (b) joint B.

Admin
Solution

Real Truss Reaction Forces

\(A_{y}=440.000 kN \uparrow \quad D_{x}=120.000 kN \rightarrow \quad D_{y}=200.000 kN \uparrow\)
