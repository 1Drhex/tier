Guest Question.

Consider the systems with the following input-output relations:

System 1 (S1): \(y[n]=\begin{cases} x[\frac{n}{2} ],n : even(2l) \\ 0,n:odd(2l+1) \end{cases}\)

System 2 (S2):\(y[n]=x[n]+1/2x[n-1]+1/4x[n-2]\)

System 3 (S3): \(y[n] = x[2n]\)

S1, S2, S3 are connected in the way illustrated in the figure below:

Find the input-output relation of this system, i.e., the difference equation.

Admin
Solution

The outputs of the three systems are given respectively by the relations:

S3: \(y[n] = x[2n]\)

S2:\(z[n]=w[n]+\frac{1}{2}w[n-1]+\frac{1}{4}w[n-2] \)

S1:\(w[n]=\begin{cases} x[\frac{n}{2} ], & n=2k \\ 0, & n=2k+1\end{cases} \)

From the above relations, we compute the output:

\(y[n]=z[2n]=w[2n]+\frac{1}{2}w[2n-1]+\frac{1}{4}w[2(n-1)]=\)
\(x[n]+0+\frac{1}{4} x[n-1]=x[n]+\frac{1}{4} x[n-1]\)

Therefore, the difference equation is:

\(y[n]=x[n]+\frac{1}{4} x[n-1]\)
