Guest Question.

(a) Consider complex power transmission via the three-phase short line for which the per-phase circuit is shown in Figure 5.19. Express \(S_{12}\), the complex power sent by bus 1 (or \(V_{1}\)), and (\(-S_{21}\)), the complex power received by bus 2 (or \(V_{2}\)), in terms of \(V _{1}, V _{2}, Z , \angle Z, \text { and } \theta_{12} = \theta_{1}-\theta_{2},\) which is the power angle.

(b) For a balanced three-phase transmission line in per-unit notation with \( Z=1 \angle 85^{\circ}, \theta_{12}=10^{\circ} \), determine \(S_{12}\) and (\(-S_{21}\)) for

(i) \(V _{1}= V _{2}=1.0\)
(ii) \(V _{1}=1.1 \text { and } V _{2}=0.9\)

Comment on the changes of real and reactive powers from parts (i) to (ii).

Admin
Solution

(a)

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{S}_{12}=\bar{V}_{1} \bar{I}_{1}^{*}=& \bar{V}_{1}\left(\frac{\bar{V}_{1}-\bar{V}_{2}}{\bar{Z}}\right)^{*}=\frac{V_{1}^{2}}{\bar{Z}^{*}}-\frac{\bar{V}_{1} \bar{V}_{2}^{*}}{\bar{Z}^{*}} \\&=\frac{V_{1}^{2}}{Z} e^{j \angle Z}-\frac{V_{1} V_{2}}{Z} e^{j \angle Z} e^{j \theta_{12}} \leftarrow(1)\end{aligned}\)

which is the power sent by \(\bar{V}_{1}\).

\(\bar{S}_{21}=\frac{V_{2}^{2}}{Z} e^{j \angle Z}-\frac{V_{2} V_{1}}{Z} e^{j \angle z} e^{-j \theta_{12}}\)

and \(-\bar{S}_{21}=-\frac{V_{2}^{2}}{Z} e^{j \angle z}+\frac{V_{2} V_{1}}{Z} e^{j \angle z} e^{-j \theta_{12}} \leftarrow 2\)

which is the power received by \(\bar{V}_{2}\).

(b)

(i) With \(V_{1}=V_{2}=1.0\)

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}\bar{S}_{12}=1 \angle 85^{\circ}-1 \angle 95^{\circ}=0.1743 \\-\bar{S}_{21}=-1 \angle 85^{\circ}+1 \angle 75^{\circ}=0.1717-j 0.0303\end{array}\right\} \leftarrow\)

 

(ii) With \(V_{1}=1.1 \text { and } V_{2}=0.9\)

\(\left.\begin{array}{rl}\bar{S}_{12} & =1.21 \angle 85^{\circ}-0.99 \angle 95^{\circ}=0.1917+j 0.2192 \\-\bar{S}_{21} & =-0.81 \angle 85^{\circ}+0.99 \angle 75^{\circ}=0.1856+j 0.1493\end{array}\right\} \leftarrow\)

 

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}\text{Comparing,} P_{12} \text{has not changed much,} \\\text{but} Q_{12} and -Q_{21} \text{have changed considerably.}\end{array}\right\} \leftarrow\)
