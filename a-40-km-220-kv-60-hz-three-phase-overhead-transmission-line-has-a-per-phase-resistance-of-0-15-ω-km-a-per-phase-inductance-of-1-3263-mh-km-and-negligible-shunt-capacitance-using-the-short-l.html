Guest Question.

A 40-km, 220-kV, 60-Hz, three-phase overhead transmission line has a per-phase resistance of 0.15 立/km, a per-phase inductance of 1.3263 mH/km, and negligible shunt capacitance. Using the short line model, find the sending-end voltage, voltage regulation, sending-end power, and transmission line efficiency when the line is supplying a three-phase load of (a) 381 MVA at 0.8 power factor lagging and at 220 kV and (b) 381 MVA at 0.8 power factor leading and at 220 kV

Admin
Solution

(a) The series impedance per phase \(\bar{Z}=(r+j \omega L) l\)

\(=\left(0.15+j 2 \pi(60) 1.3263 \times 10^{-3}\right) 40=6+j 20 \Omega\)

The receiving end voltage per phase \(\bar{V}_{R}=\frac{220}{\sqrt{3}} \angle 0^{\circ}=127 \angle 0^{\circ} kV\)

Complex power at the receiving end

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{S}_{R(3 \phi)} &=381 \angle \cos ^{-1} 0.8 MVA \\&=304.8+j 228.6 MVA\end{aligned}\)

The current per phase is given by \(\bar{S}_{R(3 \phi)}^{*} / 3 \bar{V}_{R}^{*}\)

\(\therefore \bar{I}_{R}=\frac{\left(381 \angle-36.87^{\circ}\right) 10^{3}}{3 \times 127 \angle 0^{\circ}}=1000 \angle-36.87^{\circ} A\)

The sending end voltage, as per KVL, is given by

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{S}=\bar{V}_{R}+\bar{Z} \bar{I}_{R} &=127 \angle 0^{\circ}+(6+j 20)\left(1000 \angle-36.87^{\circ}\right) 10^{-3} \\=& 144.33 \angle 4.93^{\circ} kV\end{aligned}\)

The sending end line-to-line voltage magnitude is then

\(V_{S(L-L)}=\sqrt{3}(144.33)=250 kV\)

The sending power is

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{S}_{S(3 \phi)} &=3 \bar{V}_{S} \bar{I}_{S}^{*}=3\left(144.33 \angle 4.93^{\circ}\right)\left(1000 \angle 36.87^{\circ}\right) 10^{-3} \\&=322.8 MW +j 288.6 MVAR =433 \angle 41.8^{\circ} MVA\end{aligned}\)

Voltage regulation is \(\frac{250-220}{220}=0.136\)

Transmission line efficiency is \(\eta=\frac{P_{R}(3 \phi)}{P_{S}(3 \phi)}=\frac{304.8}{322.8}=0.944\)

 

(b) With 0.8 leading power factor, \(\bar{I}_{R}=1000 \angle 36.87^{\circ} A\)

The sending end voltage is \(\bar{V}_{S}=\bar{V}_{R}+\bar{Z} \bar{I}_{R}=121.39 \angle 9.29^{\circ} kV\)

The sending end line-to-line voltage magnitude

\(\begin{aligned}V_{S(L-L)} &=\sqrt{3} \times 121.39 \\&=210.26 kV\end{aligned}\)

The sending end power \(S_{S(3 \phi)}=3 \bar{V}_{S} \bar{I}_{S}^{*}\)

\(\begin{gathered}=3\left(121.39 \angle 9.29^{\circ}\right)\left(1 \angle-36.87^{\circ}\right)=322.8 MW -j 168.6 M var \\=3618 \angle-27.58^{\circ} MVA\end{gathered}\)

Voltage regulation \(=\frac{210.26-220}{220}=-0.0443\)

Transmission line efficiency \(\eta=\frac{P_{R(3 \phi)}}{P_{S(3 \phi)}}=\frac{304.8}{322.8}=0.944\)
