Guest Question.

The hinged beam in Fig. 5.30 carries a concentrated force and a triangular line load. Determine the support reactions and the force in the hinge .

Admin
Solution

The hinged beam is statically determinate supported. We draw the free-body diagram for the complete system, for the left part ① and for the right part ②, respectively. The triangular line load is replaced by its resultant force \(R=3q_{0} a/2\) .

It is advantageous to use the following 6 equilibrium conditions to determine the 6 unknowns \(A_{H} , A_{V} , B , C , G_{H}\) and \(G_{V}\) . The force equilibrium in the horizontal direction for the complete system requires

\(\rightarrow :- A_{H}+F \cos \alpha =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{ A_{H} =F \cos \alpha} }\) .

The equilibrium conditions for the left part ① are

\(\overset{\curvearrowleft }{A} :-aF\sin \alpha +2aG_{V} =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{G_{V}=F/2 \sin \alpha} }\) ,

\(\overset{\curvearrowleft }{G} :- 2a A_{V}+aF \sin \alpha =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{A_{V}=F/2 \sin \alpha} }\) ,

\(\rightarrow : – A_{H}+F \cos \alpha – G_{H} =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{ G_{H} =0} }\)

and moment equilibrium for the right part ② requires

\(\overset{\curvearrowleft }{B}: a G_{V}-3a R + 4a C =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{C=(9q_{0} a -F \sin \alpha)/8 } }\) ,

\(\overset{\curvearrowleft }{C}: 5a G_{V} -4a B +a R =0 \rightarrow \underline{\underline{B=(3q_{0} a +5F \sin \alpha)/8 } }\) .
