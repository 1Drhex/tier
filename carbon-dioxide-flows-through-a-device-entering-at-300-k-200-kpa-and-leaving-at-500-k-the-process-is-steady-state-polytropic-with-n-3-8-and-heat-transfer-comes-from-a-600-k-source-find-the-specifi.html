Guest Question.

Carbon dioxide flows through a device entering at 300 K, 200 kPa and leaving at 500 K. The process is steady state polytropic with n = 3.8 and heat transfer comes from a 600 K source. Find the specific work, specific heat transfer and the specific entropy generation due to this process.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Steady state device, single inlet and single exit flow.

Energy Eq.4.13: \(h _{ i }+ q = h _{ e }+ w\) Neglect kinetic, potential energies

Entropy Eq.7.9: \(s _{ i }+\int dq / T + s _{ gen }= s _{ e }\)

Process Eq.6.28: \(P _{ e }= P _{ i }\left( T _{ e } / T _{ i }\right)^{\frac{ n }{ n -1}}=200(500 / 300)^{\frac{3.8}{2.8}}=400 kPa\)

and the process leads to Eq.7.18 for the work term

\(w =-\frac{ n }{ n -1} R \left( T _{ e }- T _{ i }\right)=-\frac{3.8}{2.8} \times 0.1889 \times(500-300)= – 5 1 . 3 kJ / kg\)

Energy equation gives

\(q = h _{ e }- h _{ i }+ w =401.52-214.38-51.3= 1 3 5 . 8 kJ / kg\)

Entropy equation gives (CV out to source)

\(\begin{aligned}s _{ gen } &= s _{ e }- s _{ i }- q / T _{\text {source }}= s _{ Te }^{ o }- s _{ Ti }^{ o }- R \ln \left( P _{ e } / P _{ i }\right)- q / T _{\text {source }} \\&=5.3375-4.8631-0.1889 \ln (400 / 200)-(135.8 / 600) \\&= 0 . 1 1 7 kJ / kg K\end{aligned}\)

Notice:

dP > 0 so dw <0

ds > 0 so dq > 0
