Guest Question.

Consider a rectangular cross-section with a width of 100 mm and a height of 360 mm bending about its strong axis. Assume the material to be elastic–perfectly plastic with E = 200 GPa and \( f_y = 300 \) MPa. Determine the deformations, expressed in terms of the strain at the level of the reference axis (taken at mid-height of the section) and the curvature, with a cross-sectional analysis implemented using the Newton–Raphson method for the following load steps:

(1) \( N_e = 0 \) kN, \( M_e = 648 \) kNm.
(2) \( N_e = 0 \) kN, \( M_e = 810 \) kNm.
(3) \( N_e = 0 \) kN, \( M_e = 950 \) kNm.
For the convergence criteria, use \( norm_{tol2} \) defined in Equation 15.59b equal to 0.001. Compare the results with those obtained from the moment–curvature expressions of Equations 15.25.

\( norm_{tol2} = \frac{\left|r ^{(i+1)}_R\right| }{\left|r_e\right| } \) (15.59b).

M = EIκ for \( \left|\kappa \right| \leq \kappa _y \) (15.25a).

\( M = M_p\Bigl(1-\frac{\kappa ^2_y}{3\kappa ^2}\Bigr) \) \( \left|\kappa \right| > \kappa _y \) (15.25b).

Admin
Solution

The first step in the solution is to discretise the cross-section. We will consider a very simple approach to select the number of layers. It involves the evaluation of the flexural rigidity based on different number of layers, assuming linear–elastic material properties. The preferred number of layers will be the number for which the error of the flexural rigidity is less than 0.1% of the value of EI calculated for the cross-section without layering. For the cross-section considered in this example:

\( EI = E\frac{bd^3}{12} = 200 × 10^3 \frac{100 × 360^3}{12} = 77.76 10^{12} \) Nmm².

A discretisation with 40 layers is preferred on the basis of the comparisons shown in the table below, where different numbers of layers have been considered.

\( \left\{\Bigl[\bigl(∑E_jy^2_jA_j\bigr) – (Ebd^3/12) \Bigr] /(Ebd^3/12)\right\} × 100 \) Error (%) \(∑E_jy^2_jA_j \) Nmm² Number of layers
-1 \(76.98 × 10^{12}\) 10
-0.25 \(77.56 × 10^{12}\) 20
-0.11 \(77.67 × 10^{12}\) 30
-0.06 \(77.71 × 10^{12}\) 40

The elastic–perfectly plastic material properties are calculated based on Equations 15.60 with E = 200 GPa and \( f_y = 300 \) MPa.

\( \frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon } = \frac{\partial (E\varepsilon )}{\partial \varepsilon } = E \) for \( \left|\varepsilon \right|\leq \varepsilon _y \) (15.60a).

\( \frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon } = \frac{\partial (f_y )}{\partial \varepsilon } = 0 \) for \( \left|\varepsilon \right|> \varepsilon _y \) (15.60b).

For each load step, Equations 15.45 through 15.59 are applied until the convergence criterion based on \( norm_{tol2}\) (Equation 15.59b) is satisfied, following the procedure adopted in .

\(\varepsilon = \begin{bmatrix} \varepsilon _r \\ \kappa \end{bmatrix}\) , \(r(\varepsilon) = \begin{bmatrix} N \\ M \end{bmatrix}\) , \(r_e = \begin{bmatrix} N_e \\ M_e \end{bmatrix}\) (15.45a–c).

\( r(ε) = r_e \) (15.46).

\( r(ε^{(i+1)}) ≈ r(ε^{(i)}) + r_t(ε^{(i)}) Δ ε^{(i)} = r_e \) (15.47).

\( r_t(ε^{(i)}) Δ ε^{(i)} = r^{(i)}_R \) (15.48).

\( r^{(i)}_R = r_e – r(ε^{(i)}) \) (15.49).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } Δ \varepsilon ^{(i)}_r + \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } Δ \kappa^{(i)} = N^{(i)}_R \) (15.50a).

\( \frac{∂M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } Δ \varepsilon ^{(i)}_r + \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } Δ \kappa^{(i)} = M^{(i)}_R \) (15.50b).

\( N^{(i)}_R = N_e – N\Bigl(\varepsilon^{(i)}_r , \kappa^{(i)}\Bigr) \) (15.51a).

\( M^{(i)}_R = M_e – M\Bigl(\varepsilon^{(i)}_r , \kappa^{(i)}\Bigr) \) (15.51b).

\( r_t(ε^{(i)}) = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } & \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } \\ \frac{∂M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } & \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } \end{bmatrix} \) (15.52a).

\( Δ ε^{(i)} = \begin{bmatrix}Δ ε^{(i)}_r \\ Δ \kappa^{(i)}\end{bmatrix} \) , \( r^{(i)}_R = \begin{bmatrix} N^{(i)}_R \\ M^{(i)}_R\end{bmatrix} \) (15.52b,c).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon _r} } dA \) , \( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \kappa} } dA \) (15.53a,b).

\( \frac{∂M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } =- \int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon _r} } dA \) , \( \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = – \int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \kappa} } dA \) (15.53c,d).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon _r} } dA = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon} \frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon _r}} dA = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon} \frac{\partial ( \varepsilon _r – y \kappa)}{\partial \varepsilon _r}} dA = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon}} dA \) (15.54a).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \kappa} } dA = \int\limits_{A}{\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon} \frac{\partial ( \varepsilon _r – y \kappa)}{\partial \kappa}} dA = -\int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon}} dA \) (15.54b).

\( \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } =- \int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon _r} } dA = – \int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon} \frac{\partial ( \varepsilon _r – y \kappa)}{\partial \varepsilon _r}} dA = -\int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon}} dA \) (15.54c).

\( \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = – \int\limits_{A}{y\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \kappa} } dA = -\int\limits_{A}{\frac{y\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon} \frac{\partial ( \varepsilon _r – y \kappa)}{\partial \kappa}} dA = +\int\limits_{A}{y^2\frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon}} dA \) (15.54d).

σ = Eε for \( \left|\varepsilon \right| \leq \varepsilon _y \) (15.55a).

\( σ = f_y \) for \( ε > ε_y \) and \( σ = −f_y \) for \( ε < −ε_y \) (15.55b,c).

\( \frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon } = \frac{\partial (E\varepsilon )}{\partial \varepsilon } = E \) for \( \left|\varepsilon \right|\leq \varepsilon _y \) (15.56a).

\( \frac{\partial \sigma }{\partial \varepsilon } = \frac{\partial (f_y )}{\partial \varepsilon } = 0 \) for \( \left|\varepsilon \right|> \varepsilon _y \) (15.56b).

\( N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr) = \int\limits_{A}{\sigma }dA = \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr) A_j} \) (15.57a).

\( M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr) =-\int\limits_{A}{y\sigma }dA = \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{y_j\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr) A_j} \) (15.57b).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } = \int\limits_{A} {\frac{∂ \sigma}{∂\varepsilon}}dA = \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{\frac{∂\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr)}{∂\varepsilon}} A_j \) (15.58a).

\( \frac{∂ N \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = – \int\limits_{A} {y\frac{∂ \sigma}{∂\varepsilon}}dA = – \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{y_j\frac{∂\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr)}{∂\varepsilon}} A_j \) (15.58b).

\( \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\varepsilon _r } =- \int\limits_{A} {y\frac{∂ \sigma}{∂\varepsilon}}dA = -\sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{y_j\frac{∂\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr)}{∂\varepsilon}} A_j \) (15.58c).

\( \frac{∂ M \Bigl(\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r , \kappa ^{(i)}\Bigr)}{∂\kappa } = + \int\limits_{A} {y^2\frac{∂ \sigma}{∂\varepsilon}}dA = \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_j}{y^2_j\frac{∂\sigma \bigl(y_j,\varepsilon ^{(i)}_r,\kappa ^{(i)}\bigr)}{∂\varepsilon}} A_j \) (15.58d).

\( norm_{tol1} = \frac{\left|Δ ε^{(i)}\right| }{\left|Δ ε^{(i+1)}\right| } \) , \( norm_{tol2} = \frac{\left|r ^{(i+1)}_R\right| }{\left|r_e\right| } \) (15.59a,b).
