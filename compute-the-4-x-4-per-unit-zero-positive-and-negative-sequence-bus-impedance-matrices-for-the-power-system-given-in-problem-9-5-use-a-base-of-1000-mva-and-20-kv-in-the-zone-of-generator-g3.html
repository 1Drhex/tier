Guest Question.

Compute the 4 × 4 per-unit zero-, positive-, and negative-sequence bus impedance matrices for the power system given in . Use a base of 1000 MVA and 20 kV in the zone of generator G3.

Admin
Solution

Zero sequence bus impedance matrix:

Step (1): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.24\) from the reference to bus 1 (type 1)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j[0.24] \text { per unit }\)

Step (2): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.6\) from bus 1 to bus 2 (type 2)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{ll}0.24 & 0.24 \\0.24 & 0.84\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

Step (3): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.6\) from bus 2 to bus 3 (type 2)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{lll}0.24 & 0.24 & 0.24 \\0.24 & 0.84 & 0.84 \\0.24 & 0.84 & 1.44\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

Step (4): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.10\) from the reference to bus 3 (type 3)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{ccc}.24 & .24 & .24 \\.24 & .84 & .84 \\.24 & .84 & 1.44\end{array}\right]-\frac{j}{1.54}\left[\begin{array}{c}.24 \\.84 \\1.44\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{lll}.24 & .84 & 1.44\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{ccc}0.2026 & 0.1091 & 0.01558 \\0.1091 & 0.3818 & 0.05455 \\0.01558 & 0.05455 & 0.09351\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

 

Step (5): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.6\) from bus 2 to bus 4 (type 2)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{cccc}0.2026 & 0.1091 & 0.01558 & 0.1091 \\0.1091 & 0.3818 & 0.05455 & 0.3818 \\0.01558 & 0.05455 & 0.09351 & 0.05455 \\0.1091 & 0.3818 & 0.05455 & 0.9818\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

Step (6): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.1333\) from the reference bus to bus 4 (type 3)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{llll}.2026 & .1091 & .01558 & .1091 \\.1091 & .3818 & .05455 & .3818 \\.01558 & .05455 & .09351 & .05455 \\.1091 & .3818 & .05455 & .9818\end{array}\right]\) \(-\frac{j}{1.1151}\left[\begin{array}{c}.1091 \\.3818 \\.05455 \\.9818\end{array}\right][.1091 .3818 .05455 .9818]\)

 

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-0}=j\left[\begin{array}{cccc}0.1919 & 0.07175 & 0.01024 & 0.01304 \\0.07175 & 0.2511 & 0.03587 & 0.04564 \\0.01024 & 0.03587 & 0.09084 & 0.006521 \\0.01304 & 0.04564 & 0.006521 & 0.1174\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

Positive sequence bus impedance matrix can be obtained as:

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-1}=j\left[\begin{array}{llll}0.2671 & 0.1505 & 0.0865 & 0.0980 \\0.1505 & 0.1975 & 0.1135 & 0.1286 \\0.0865 & 0.1135 & 0.1801 & 0.0739 \\0.0980 & 0.1286 & 0.0739 & 0.2140\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

 

Negative sequence bus impedance matrix:

Steps (1)–(4) are the same as for \(\overline{\underline{Z}}_{b u s-1}\)

Step (5): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.3\) from the reference bus to bus 3 (type 3)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-2}=j\left[\begin{array}{cccc}.64 & .64 & .64 & .64 \\.64 & .84 & .84 & .84 \\.64 & .84 & 1.04 & .84 \\.64 & .84 & .84 & 1.04\end{array}\right]-\frac{j}{1.34}\left[\begin{array}{c}.64 \\.84 \\1.04 \\.84\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{llll}.64 & .84 & 1.04 & .84\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{\text {bus }-2}=j\left[\begin{array}{llll}.3343 & .2388 & .1433 & .2388 \\.2388 & .3134 & .1881 & .3134 \\.1433 & .1881 & .2328 & .1881 \\.2388 & .3134 & .1881 & .5134\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

 

Step (6): Add \(\bar{Z}_{b}=j 0.3733\) from the reference to bus 4 (type 3)

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-2}=j\left[\begin{array}{llll}.3343 & .2388 & .1433 & .2388 \\.2388 & .3134 & .1881 & .3134 \\.1433 & .1881 & .2328 & .1881 \\.2388 & .3134 & .1881 & .5134\end{array}\right]\) \(-\frac{j}{.8867}\left[\begin{array}{l}.2388 \\.3134 \\.1881 \\.5134\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{llll}.2388 & .3134 & .1881 & .5134\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\underline{\bar{Z}}_{b u s-2}=j\left[\begin{array}{cccc}0.2700 & 0.1544 & 0.09264 & 0.1005 \\0.1544 & 0.2026 & 0.1216 & 0.1319 \\0.09264 & 0.1216 & 0.1929 & 0.07919 \\0.1005 & 0.1319 & 0.07919 & 0.2161\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)
