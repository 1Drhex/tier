Guest Question.

The cross-sectional dimensions of a beam are shown in Figure P8.9.
(a) If the bending stress at point K is 43 MPa (C), determine the internal bending moment \(M_{z}\) acting about the z centroidal axis of the beam.
(b) Determine the bending stress at point H. State whether the normal stress at H is tension or compression.

Admin
Solution

Centroid location in y direction: (reference axis at bottom of double-tee shape)

Shape \(\begin{gathered}\text{Area} A_{i} \\\left( mm ^{3}\right)\end{gathered}\) \(\begin{gathered}y_{i} \\\text { (from bottom) } \\( mm )\end{gathered}\) \(\begin{gathered}y_{i} A_{i} \\\left( mm ^{3}\right)\end{gathered}\)
top flange 375.0 47.5 17,812.5
left stem 225.0 22.5 5,062.5
right stem 225.0 22.5 5,062.5
825.0 \(mm ^{2}\) 27,937.5 \(mm ^{3}\)

 

\(\bar{y}=\frac{\Sigma y_{i} A_{i}}{\Sigma A_{i}}=\frac{27,937.5 mm ^{3}}{825.0 mm ^{2}}=33.864 mm =33.9 mm\) (measured upward from bottom of section)

Moment of inertia about the z axis:

Shape \(\begin{gathered}I_{C} \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{gathered}\) \(\begin{gathered}d=y_{i}-\bar{y} \\( mm )\end{gathered}\) \(\begin{gathered}d^{2} A \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{gathered}\) \(\begin{gathered}I_{C}+d^{2} A \\\left( mm ^{4}\right)\end{gathered}\)
top flange 781.250 13.636 69,731.405 70,512.655
left stem 37,968.750 −11.364 29,054.752 67,023.502
right stem 37,968.750 −11.364 29,054.752 67,023.502
Moment of inertia about the z axis \(\left( mm ^{4}\right)=\) 204,559.659

 

(a) Determine bending moment:

At point K, y = 50 mm − 5 mm − 33.864 mm = 11.136 mm. The bending stress at K is \(\sigma_{x}\) = −43 MPa; therefore, the bending moment magnitude can be determined from the flexure formula:

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{x} &=-\frac{M y}{I_{z}} \\& \therefore M=-\frac{\sigma_{x} I_{z}}{y}=-\frac{\left(-43 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(204,559.659 mm ^{4}\right)}{11.136 mm } \\=& 789,850.765 N – mm =790 N – m\end{aligned}\)

(b) Bending stress at point H:

At point H, y = −33.864 mm. The bending stress is computed with the flexure formula:

\(\sigma_{x}=-\frac{M y}{I_{z}}=-\frac{(789,850.765 N – mm )(-33.864 mm )}{204,559.659 mm ^{4}}=130.755 MPa =130.8 MPa ( T )\)
