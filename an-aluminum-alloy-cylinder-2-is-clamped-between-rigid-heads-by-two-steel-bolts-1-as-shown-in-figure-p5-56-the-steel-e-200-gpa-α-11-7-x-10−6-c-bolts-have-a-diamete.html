Guest Question.

An aluminum alloy cylinder (2) is clamped between rigid heads by two steel bolts (1), as shown in Figure P5.56. The steel [E = 200 GPa; α = 11.7 × \(10^{-6}\)/°C] bolts have a diameter of 16 mm. The aluminum alloy [E = 70 GPa; α = 23.6 × \(10^{-6}\)/°C] cylinder has an outside diameter of 150 mm and a wall thickness of 5 mm. Assume a = 600 mm and b = 700 mm. If the temperature of this assembly changes by ΔT = 50°C, determine:
(a) the normal stress in the aluminum cylinder.
(b) the normal strain in the aluminum cylinder.
(c) the normal strain in the steel bolts.

Admin
Solution

Equilibrium: By virtue of symmetry, the forces in the two steel bolts (1) are identical. Consider a FBD of the rigid head. Sum forces in the horizontal direction to obtain:

\(\Sigma F_{x}=2 F_{1}+F_{2}=0\) (a)

Geometry-of-deformation equation: For this configuration,

\(\delta_{1}=\delta_{2}\) (b)

Force-Temperature-Deformation Relationships: The relationship between internal force, temperature change, and deformation can be stated for members (1) and (2):

\(\delta_{1}=\frac{F_{1} L_{1}}{A_{1} E_{1}}+\alpha_{1} \Delta T L_{1} \quad \delta_{2}=\frac{F_{2} L_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}+\alpha_{2} \Delta T L_{2}\) (c)

Compatibility Equation: Substitute the force-deformation relationships (c) into the geometry-ofdeformation relationship (b) to derive the compatibility equation:

\(\frac{F_{1} L_{1}}{A_{1} E_{1}}+\alpha_{1} \Delta T_{1} L_{1}=\frac{F_{2} L_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}+\alpha_{2} \Delta T_{2} L_{2}\) (d)

Solve the Equations: For this situation, \(\Delta T_{1}=\Delta T_{2}=\Delta T\) . Solve Eq. (d) for \(F_{1}\) :

\(F_{1}=\left[\frac{F_{2} L_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}+\alpha_{2} \Delta T L_{2}-\alpha_{1} \Delta T L_{1}\right] \frac{A_{1} E_{1}}{L_{1}}=F_{2} \frac{L_{2}}{L_{1}} \frac{A_{1}}{A_{2}} \frac{E_{1}}{E_{2}}+\Delta T\left[\alpha_{2} L_{2}-\alpha_{1} L_{1}\right] \frac{A_{1} E_{1}}{L_{1}}\)

Substitute this expression into Eq. (a) and solve for \(F_{2}\) :

\(\begin{aligned}2\left[F_{2} \frac{L_{2}}{L_{1}} \frac{A_{1}}{A_{2}} \frac{E_{1}}{E_{2}}+\Delta T\left[\alpha_{2} L_{2}-\alpha_{1} L_{1}\right] \frac{A_{1} E_{1}}{L_{1}}\right]+F_{2}=0 \\F_{2}\left[2 \frac{L_{2}}{L_{1}} \frac{A_{1}}{A_{2}} \frac{E_{1}}{E_{2}}+1\right]=2 \Delta T\left[\alpha_{1} L_{1}-\alpha_{2} L_{2}\right] \frac{A_{1} E_{1}}{L_{1}} \\F_{2} & \frac{2 \Delta T\left[\alpha_{1} L_{1}-\alpha_{2} L_{2}\right] \frac{A_{1} E_{1}}{L_{1}}}{2 \frac{L_{2}}{L_{1}} \frac{A_{1}}{A_{2}} \frac{E_{1}}{E_{2}}+1} (e)\end{aligned}\)

For this structure, the areas, coefficients of thermal expansion, and elastic moduli are given below:

\(\begin{array}{ll}A_{1}=\frac{\pi}{4}(16 mm )^{2}=201.0619 mm ^{2} & A_{2}=\frac{\pi}{4}\left[(150 mm )^{2}-(140 mm )^{2}\right]=2,277.6547 mm ^{2} \\L_{1}=700 mm & L_{2}=600 mm \\E_{1}=200,000 MPa & E_{2}=70,000 MPa \\\alpha_{1}=11.7 \times 10^{-6} /{ }^{\circ} C & \alpha_{2}=23.6 \times 10^{-6} /{ }^{\circ} C\end{array}\)

Substitute these values along with ΔT = 50°C into Eq. (e) and calculate \(F_{2}\):

\(F_{2}=-23,943.1034 N\)

Backsubstitute this result into Eq. (a) to calculate \(F_{1}=11,971.5517 N\) .

 

(a) Normal Stress in Aluminum Cylinder: The normal stresses in each rod can now be calculated:

\(\sigma_{2}=\frac{F_{2}}{A_{2}}=\frac{-23,943.1034 N }{2,277.6547 mm ^{2}}=10.51 MPa ( C )\)

(b) Normal Strain in Aluminum Cylinder: The force-temperature-deformation relationships were expressed in Eq. (c). By definition, \(\varepsilon=\delta / L\). Therefore, the normal strain for each axial member can be determined by dividing the relationships in Eq. (c) by the respective member lengths:

\(\varepsilon_{1}=\frac{F_{1}}{A_{1} E_{1}}+\alpha_{1} \Delta T_{1} \quad \varepsilon_{2}=\frac{F_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}+\alpha_{2} \Delta T_{2}\) (f)

Substitute the appropriate values to calculate the normal strains in the aluminum cylinderr:

\(\begin{aligned}\varepsilon_{2} &=\frac{F_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}+\alpha_{2} \Delta T_{2} \\&=\frac{-23,943.1034 N }{\left(2,277.6547 mm ^{2}\right)\left(70,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)}+\left(23.6 \times 10^{-6} /{ }^{\circ} C \right)\left(50^{\circ} C \right) \\&=0.0010298 mm / mm =1,030 \mu \varepsilon\end{aligned}\)

(c) Normal Strain in Steel Bolts:

\(\begin{aligned}\varepsilon_{1} &=\frac{F_{1}}{A_{1} E_{1}}+\alpha_{1} \Delta T_{1} \\&=\frac{11,971.5517 N }{\left(201.0619 mm ^{2}\right)(200,000 MPa )}+\left(11.7 \times 10^{-6} /{ }^{\circ} C \right)\left(50^{\circ} C \right) \\&=0.0008827 mm / mm =883 \mu \varepsilon\end{aligned}\)
