Guest Question.

A 3.3 L minivan engine runs at 2000 RPM with a compression ratio of 10:1. The intake is at 50 kPa, 280 K and after expansion it is at 750 K. Find the highest T in the cycle, the specific heat transfer added by combustion and the mean effective pressure.

Admin
Solution

Compression: Reversible and adiabatic so constant s from Eq.6.24-25

\(T _{2}= T _{1}\left( v _{1} / v _{2}\right)^{ k -1}=280 \times(10)^{0.4}=703.3 K\)

Expansion: isentropic, \(T _{3}\) is the highest T and \(T _{4}\) was given

\(\begin{gathered}T _{3}= T _{4} CR ^{ k -1}=750 K \times 10^{0.4}= 1 8 8 3 . 9 K \\q _{ H }= u _{3}- u _{2}= C _{ V 0}\left( T _{3}- T _{2}\right)=0.717(1883.9-703.3)= 8 4 6 . 5 k J / kg\end{gathered}\)

Efficiency and net work

\(\begin{aligned}&\eta_{ TH }=1- T _{1} / T _{2}=1-280 / 703.3=0.602 \\& w _{\text {net }}=\eta_{ TH } \times q _{ H }=0.602 \times 846.5=509.6 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

Displacement and \(P _{ meff }\)

\(\begin{aligned}& v _{1}= RT _{1} / P _{1}=(0.287 kJ / kg – K \times 280 K ) / 50 kPa =1.6072 m ^{3} / kg \\& v _{2}=(1 / 10) v _{1}=0.16072 m ^{3} / kg \\& P _{ meff }=\frac{ w _{ NET }}{ v _{1}- v _{2}}=\frac{509.6}{1.6072-0.16072}= 3 5 2 . 3 kPa\end{aligned}\)
