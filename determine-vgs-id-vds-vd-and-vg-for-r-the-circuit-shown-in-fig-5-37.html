Guest Question.

Determine \(V_{GS} ,I_{D} ,V_{DS} ,V_{D}\) and \(V_{G} \) for the circuit shown in Fig. 5.37.

Admin
Solution

To find \(V_{GSQ}\) :

For a self bias circuit, \(V_{GSQ} =-I_{D} R_{S} =-I_{D} \times 10^{3} \)

 

To find \(I_{D}\) :

\(I_{D} =I_{DSS} \left[1-\frac{V_{GS} }{V_{P} }\right] ^{2}=8\times 10^{-3} \left[1+\frac{(1\times 10^{3} I_{D} ) }{-6 }\right] ^{2}\)

 

\(=8\times 10^{-3} \left[1-\frac{1000 I_{D} }{6 }\right] ^{2}\)

 

Therefore, \(36 I_{D} =8\times 10^{-3} \left[6-1000 I_{D} \right]^{2}\)

 

\(=8\times 10^{-3} \left[36-12\times 10^{3} I_{D}+ 10^{6} I_{D}^{2}\right] \)

 

\(=8\times 10^{3}I_{D}^{2}-132 I_{D}+0.288=0\)

 

\(I_{D}=\frac{132\pm \sqrt{(132)^{2}-4\times 8\times 10^{3}\times 0.288} }{2\times 8\times 10^{3} }\)

 

Therefore, \( I_{D}=13.9mA\) or \(2.5mA\)

But \( I_{D}\) cannot be higher than \( I_{DSS}\), Therefore, \( I_{D}=4.225mA\)

 

To find \( V_{DS}\) :

\(V_{DS} =V_{DD}- I_{D}(R_{D}+R_{S})=20-2.5\times 10^{-3} (3.3+1)\times 10^{3} =9.25V\)

 

To find \(V_{GS} ,V_{D}\) and \(V_{S} \) :

\(V_{GS} =1\times 10^{3} \times I_{D} =1\times 10^{3} \times 2.5\times 10^{-3} =2.5V\)

 

\(V_{S} =I_{D}R_{S} =2.5\times 10^{-3} \times 1\times 10^{3} =2.5V\)

 

\(V_{D} =V_{S}+V_{DS}=2.5+9.25=11.75V\)
