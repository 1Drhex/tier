λ=i⇒u=[-1-i1] , λ=-i⇒u=[-1+i1]

Thus,

Setting \(y_{1}(0)=-2\) and \(y_{2}(0)=1\), we obtain

-c1-c2=-2c1=1

Thus, \(c_{1}=1\), and \(c_{2}=1\). Therefore, \(y_{1}=-2cos t\) and \(y_{2} = cos t+ sin t\).
