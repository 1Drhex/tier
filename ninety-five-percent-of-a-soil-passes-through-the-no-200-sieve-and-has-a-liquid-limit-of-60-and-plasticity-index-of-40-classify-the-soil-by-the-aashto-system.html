Guest Question.

Ninety-five percent of a soil passes through the No. 200 sieve and has a liquid limit of 60 and plasticity index of 40. Classify the soil by the AASHTO system.

Admin
Solution

Ninety-five percent of the soil (which is \(\geq 36\%\)) is passing through No. 200 sieve. So it is a silty-clay material. Now refer to Table 5.1.

\(^a{For A-7-5, PI \leq LL – 30} \)
\(^b{For A-7-5, PI \gt LL – 30} \)

Starting from the left of the table, it falls under A-7-6 (see the table below).

Parameter Specifications in Table 5.1 Parameters of the given soil
Percent passing No. 200 sieve 36 min. 95
Liquid limit (LL) 41 min. 60
Plasticity index (PI) 11 min. 40
PI \(\gt LL- 30\) \(PI= 40 \gt LL-30=60-30=30\)

 

\(GI=(F_{200} -35)\left[0.2+0.005(LL-40)\right] + 0.001(F_{200} -15)(PI-10)\)

 

\(=(95 -35)\left[0.2+0.005(60-40)\right] + 0.001(95 -15)(40-10)\)

 

=42

So, the classification is A-7-6(42).
