Guest Question.

Determine the design bending strength, or moment capacity, \(\phi_{b}M_{n}\), for a W14 × 74 flexural member of ASTM A572, grade 50 steel, assuming

1. Continuous lateral support;
2. Unbraced length = 15 ft., \(C_{b} = 1.0\); and
3. Unbraced length = 15 ft., \(C_{b} = 1.30\).

Admin
Solution

Check the compact section criteria:
From part 1 of the AISCM,

\(\frac{b_{f}}{2t_{f}} =6.41\), and

\(\frac{h}{t_{w}} =25.4\)

Flange:

\(\lambda_{p f}=0.38 \sqrt{\frac{E}{F_{y}}}=0.38 \sqrt{\frac{29,000}{50}}=9.15>6.41\),

.’. the flange is compact.

Web:

\(\lambda_{p w}=3.76 \sqrt{\frac{E}{F_{y}}}=3.76 \sqrt{\frac{29,000}{50}}=90.5>25.4\),

.’. the web is compact.

1. Continuous lateral support, \(L_{b} = 0\) (upper linear zone in Figure 6-5)

\(\begin{aligned} M_{n} &=M_{p}=F_{y} Z_{x} \\ &=(50)(126)=6300 \text { in. kips or } 525 \mathrm{ft} \text { -kips } \\ \phi_{b} M_{n} &=(0.9)(525)=473 \mathrm{ft} . \text { kips } \end{aligned}\)

2. \(L_{b} = 15 ft.\) and \(C_{b} = 1.0\) ,

\(\begin{aligned} L_{p} &=1.76 r_{y} \sqrt{\frac{E}{F_{y}}}=1.76(2.48) \sqrt{\frac{29,000}{50}}=105.1 \mathrm{in.}=8.76 \mathrm{ft} . \\ L_{r} &=1.95 r_{t s} \frac{E}{0.7 F_{y}} \sqrt{\frac{J c}{S_{x} h_{o}}} \sqrt{ \left.1+\sqrt{1+6.76\left(\frac{0.7 F_{y} S_{x} h_{o}}{E} J_{c}\right.}\right)^{2}} \\ &=1.95(2.82) \frac{(29,000)}{0.7(50)} \sqrt{\frac{(3.87)(1.0)}{(112)(13.4)}} \sqrt{1+\sqrt{1+6.76\left(\frac{(0.7)(50)(112)(13.4)}{(29,000)(3.87)(1.0)}\right)^{2}}} \\ &=371.2 \mathrm{in.}=31.0 \mathrm{ft} . \end{aligned}\)

Since \(L_{p} < L_{b} < L_{r}\), the nominal moment strength is found from equation (6-10):

\(M_{n}=C_{b}\left[M_{p}-\left(M_{p}-0.7 F_{y} S_{x}\right)\left(\frac{L_{b}-L_{p}}{L_{r}-L_{p}}\right)\right] \leq M_{p}\) (6-10)

\(M_{n}=(1.0)\left[6300-(6300-(0.7)(50)(112))\left(\frac{15-8.76}{31.0-8.76}\right)\right] \leq 6300\) ,

\(M_{n}=5632\) in.-kips or 469 ft.-kips

\(\phi_{b} M_{n}=(0.9)(469)=422 \mathrm{ft}\)-kips

3. \(L_{b} = 15\) ft. and \(C_{b} = 1.3\) ,

\(M_{n}=C_{b}\left[M_{p}-\left(M_{p}-0.7 F_{y} S_{x}\right)\left(\frac{L_{b}-L_{p}}{L_{r}-L_{p}}\right)\right] \leq M_{p}\) ,

\(M_{n}=1.3\left[6300-(6300-(0.7)(50)(112))\left(\frac{15-8.76}{31.0-8.76}\right)\right] \leq 6300\) ,

\(M_{n}= 7321 in.-kips > 6300 in.-kips,\) .’. flexural yielding controls.

\(M_{n}=M_{p}=6300 in.-kips = 525 ft.-kips\) ,

\(\phi_{b}M_{n}=(0.9)(525)=473 ft.-kips\)
