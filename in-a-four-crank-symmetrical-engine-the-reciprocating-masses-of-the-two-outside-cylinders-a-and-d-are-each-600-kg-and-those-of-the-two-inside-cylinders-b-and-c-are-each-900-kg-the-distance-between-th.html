Guest Question.

In a four-crank symmetrical engine, the reciprocating masses of the two outside cylinders A and D are each 600 kg and those of the two inside cylinders B and C are each 900 kg. The distance between the cylinder axes of A and D is 5 m. Taking the reference line to bisect the angle between the cranks A and D, and the reference plane to bisect the distance between the cylinder axes of A and D, find the angles between the cranks and the distance between the cylinder axes of B and C for complete balance except for secondary couples.

Determine the maximum value of the unbalanced secondary couple if the length of the crank is 0.4 m, length of connecting rod 1.8 m and speed is 180 rpm.

Admin
Solution

Let the cranks 1 and 4 be inclined at an angle α with the reference line and cranks 3 and 4 at angle β.
Also let a = distance of B and C from the reference plane, as shown in Fig.12.42(a).

Primary Forces:
Horizontal component,

\( F_{H P}=\omega^{2} r\left[M_{1} \cos \alpha+M_{3} \cos \left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right)+M_{2} \cos \left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right)+M_{4} \cos \left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right)\right] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[600 \cos \alpha-900 \cos \beta-900 \cos \beta+600 \cos \alpha] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[1200 \cos \alpha-1800 \cos \beta]\).

Vertical component,

\( F_{V P}=\omega^{2} r\left[M_{1} \sin \alpha+M_{3} \sin \left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right)+M_{2} \sin \left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right)+M_{4} \sin \left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right)\right] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[600 \sin \alpha+900 \sin \beta-900 \sin \beta-600 \sin \alpha] \).

=0.

\( \text { For primary force balance, } F_{H P}=0 \).

\( 1200 \cos \alpha-1800 \cos \beta=0 \).

\( \frac{\cos \alpha}{\cos \beta}=1.5 \) (12.33).

Primary couple about RP:

\( C_{H P}=\omega^{2} r\left[M_{1} \cos \alpha \times(-2.5)+M_{3} \cos \left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right)-\alpha+M_{2} \cos \left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right) \times(-\alpha)+\right.\)

\( \left.M_{4} \cos \left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right) \times 2.5\right] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[-1500 \cos \alpha-900 \alpha \cos \beta+900 \alpha \cos \beta+1500 \cos \alpha] \).

=0.

\( C_{V P}=\omega^{2} r\left[M_{1} \sin \alpha \times(-2.5)+M_{3} \sin \left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right) \times \alpha+\right. \)

\( \left.M_{2} \sin \left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right) \times(-\alpha)+M_{4} \sin \left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right) \times 2.5\right] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[-1500 \sin \alpha+900 \alpha \sin \beta+900 \alpha \sin \beta-1500 \sin \alpha] \).

\( \omega^{2} r[-3000 \sin \alpha+1800 \alpha \sin \beta] \).

\( \text { For complete balance of primary couple, } C_{V P}=0-3000 \sin \alpha+1800 \alpha \sin \beta=0 \).

\( \frac{\sin \alpha}{\sin \beta}=0.6 a \) (12.34).

From (12.33) and (12.34), we get

\( \frac{\tan \alpha}{\tan \beta}=0.4 a \) (12.35).

Secondary Forces:

\( \text { Speed }=2 \omega, \text { Imaginary crank length }=\frac{r}{4 n}=\frac{r^{2}}{4 l} \).

\( F_{H S}=\frac{(2 \omega)^{2} r^{2}}{4 l}\left[M_{1} \cos 2 \alpha+M_{3} \cos 2\left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right)+M_{2} \cos 2\left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right)+M_{4} \cos 2\left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right)\right] \).

\( =\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)[600 \cos 2 \alpha+900 \cos 2 \beta+900 \cos 2 \beta+600 \cos 2 \alpha] \).

\( =\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)[1200 \cos 2 \alpha+1800 \cos 2 \beta] \).

For secondary force to be zero,

\( 1200 \cos 2 \alpha+1800 \cos 2 \beta=0 \).

\( \frac{\cos 2 \alpha}{\cos 2 \beta}=-1.5\).

Vertical component

\( F_{V S}=\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)\left[M_{1} \sin 2 \alpha+M_{3} \sin 2\left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right)+M_{2} \sin 2\left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right)+M_{4} \sin 2\left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right)\right] \).

\( =\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)[600 \sin 2 \alpha-900 \sin 2 \beta+900 \sin 2 \beta-600 \sin 2 \alpha] \).

=0.

From Eq. (4), we have

\(2 \cos ^{2} \alpha-1=-1.5\left(2 \cos ^{2} \beta-1\right) \).

\( 2 \cos ^{2} \alpha+3 \cos ^{2} \beta-2.5=0 \).

From Eq. (12.33), we have

\( \cos \beta=\frac{\cos \alpha}{1.5} \).

\( 2 \cos ^{2} \alpha+\frac{3 \cos ^{2} \alpha}{2.25}-2.5=0 \).

\( \cos ^{2} \alpha\left(2+\frac{4}{3}\right)-2.5=0 \).

\( \cos ^{2} \alpha=0.75, \cos a=0.866, a=30^{\circ}, \cos \beta=0.57735, \beta=54.73^{\circ} \).

\( 0.6 a=\frac{\sin 30^{\circ}}{\sin 54.73^{\circ}}=0.6124 \).

\( a=1.02 m \).

Secondary Couple

\( C_{H S}=\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)\left[M_{1} \cos 2 \alpha \times(-2.5)+M_{3} \cos 2\left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right) \times a\right. \)

\( \left.+M_{2} \cos 2\left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right) \times(-a)+M_{4} \sin 2\left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right) \times 2.5\right] \).

\( =\omega^{2} r[-1500 \cos 2 \alpha-900 a \cos 2 \beta+900 a \cos 2 \beta-1500 \cos 2 \alpha] \).

=0.

\( C_{V S}=\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)\left[M_{1} \sin 2 \alpha \times(-2.5)+M_{3} \sin 2\left(180^{\circ}-\beta\right) \times a\right. \)

\( \left.+M_{2} \sin 2\left(180^{\circ}+\beta\right) \times(-a)+M_{4} \sin 2\left(360^{\circ}-\alpha\right) \times 2.5\right] \).

\( =\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)[-1500 \sin 2 \alpha-900 a \sin 2 \beta-900 a \sin 2 \beta-1500 \sin 2 \alpha] \).

\( =\left(\frac{\omega^{2} r^{2}}{l}\right)[3000 \sin 2 \alpha+1800 a \sin 2 \beta] \).

\( =-\left[\left(2 \pi \times \frac{180}{60}\right)^{2} \times \frac{(0.4)^{2}}{1.8}\right]\left[3000 \sin 60^{\circ}+1800 \times 1.02 \sin 109.46^{\circ}\right] \).

\( =-136733 Nm \).
