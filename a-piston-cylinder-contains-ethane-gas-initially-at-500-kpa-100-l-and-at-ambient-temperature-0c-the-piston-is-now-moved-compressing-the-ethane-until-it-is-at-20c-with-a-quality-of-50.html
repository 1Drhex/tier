Guest Question.

A piston/cylinder contains ethane gas, initially at 500 kPa, 100 L, and at ambient temperature, 0°C. The piston is now moved, compressing the ethane until it is at 20°C, with a quality of 50%. The work required is 25% more than would have been required for a reversible polytropic process between the same initial and final states. Calculate the heat transfer and the entropy generation for the process.

Admin
Solution

Ethane:

\(\begin{aligned}& T _{ c }=305.4 K , P _{ c }=4.88 MPa , \\& R =0.2765 kJ / kg – K , C _{ p }=1.766 kJ / kg K\end{aligned}\)

State 1: \(T _{ r 1}=0.895, P _{ r 1}=0.102 \rightarrow Z _{1}=0.95\)

\(\begin{aligned}& v _{1}= Z _{1} RT _{1} / P _{1}=0.1435 m ^{3} / kg , \quad m _{1}= V _{1} / v _{1}=0.697 kg \\&\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{1}\right)=0.13 RT _{ c }=11.0 kJ / kg , \quad\left( s _{1}^{*}- s _{1}\right)=0.09 R =0.025 kJ / kg K\end{aligned}\)

State 2: \(T _{2}=20^{\circ} C , x _{2}=0.5, \quad{ }_{1} W _{2}=1.25 W _{ rev }\)

\(\begin{aligned}& T _{ r 2}=0.96, \quad P _{ r 2}=0.78, P _{2}= P _{ r 2} P _{ c }=3806 kPa \\& Z _{ f 2}=0.14, \quad Z _{ g 2}=0.54, \quad Z _{2}=\left(1- x _{2}\right) Z _{ f }+ x _{2} Z _{ g }=0.34 \\&\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{2}\right)=\left(1- x _{2}\right) 3.65 RT _{ c }+ x _{2}\left(1.39 RT _{ c }\right)=212.8 kJ / kg \\&\left( s _{2}^{*}- s _{2}\right)=\left(1- x _{2}\right) 3.45 R + x _{2} \times 1.10 R =0.629 kJ / kg K \\& v _{2}= Z _{2} RT _{2} / P _{2}=0.0072 m ^{3} / kg , \quad V _{2}= mv _{2}=0.005 m ^{3}\end{aligned}\)

Polytropic process

\(P _{1} V _{1}^{ n }= P _{2} V _{2}^{ n }, \quad \ln \frac{ P _{2}}{ P _{1}}= n \ln \frac{ V _{1}}{ V _{2}} \rightarrow \quad n =0.6783\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}W _{ rev } &=\int P d V =\frac{ P _{2} V _{2}- P _{1} V _{1}}{1- n }=-96.3 kJ , \\{ }_{1} W _{2}=& 1.25 W _{ rev }=-120.4 kJ\end{aligned}\)

 

a) Energy Eq.: \({ }_{1} Q _{2}= m \left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+{ }_{1} W _{2} ; \quad u = h – Pv\)

\(\begin{aligned}h _{2}- h _{1} &=\left( h _{2}- h _{2}^{*}\right)+\left( h _{2}^{*}- h _{1}^{*}\right)+\left( h _{1}^{*}- h _{1}\right) \\&=-212.8+1.766(20-0)+11.0=-166.5 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}& u _{2}- u _{1}=\left( h _{2}- h _{1}\right)-\left( P _{2} v _{2}- P _{1} v _{1}\right)=-122.2 kJ / kg \\&{ }_{1} Q _{2}=0.697 kg (-122.2) kJ / kg -120.4 kJ =- 2 0 5 . 6 k J\end{aligned}\)

 

b) Entropy Eq.: \(S _{ gen }= m \left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)-{ }_{1} Q _{2} / T _{ o } ; \quad T _{ o }=0^{\circ} C =273.15 K\)

\(\begin{aligned}& s _{2}- s _{1}=\left( s _{2}- s _{2}^{*}\right)+\left( s _{2}^{*}- s _{1}^{*}\right)+\left( s _{1}^{*}- s _{1}\right) \\&\left( s _{2}^{*}- s _{1}^{*}\right)= C _{ p } \ln \left( T _{2} / T _{1}\right)- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)=-0.436 kJ / kg K , \\& S _{ gen }=0.697(-0.629-0.436+0.025)+\frac{205.6}{273.15}= 0 . 0 2 8 kJ / K\end{aligned}\)
