Guest Question.

A given slope under steady-state seepage has the following: H = 21.62 m, \(\phi ^{\prime}\)= 25°, slope: 2H:1V,\(c^{\prime}= 20 kN/m^{2}\), γ= 18.5 kN/\(m^{3}\),\( r_{u}\) = 0.25. Determine the factor of safety, \(F_{s}\). Use Table 15.3.

Admin

\(\frac{c^{\prime}}{\gamma H} =\frac{20}{(18.5)(21.62)}=0.05\)
