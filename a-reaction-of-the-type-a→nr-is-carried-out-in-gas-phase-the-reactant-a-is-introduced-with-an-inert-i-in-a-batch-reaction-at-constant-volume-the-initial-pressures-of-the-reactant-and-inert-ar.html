Guest Question.

A reaction of the type A→nR is carried out in gas phase. The reactant A is introduced with an inert I in a batch reaction at constant volume. The initial pressures of the reactant and inert are 7.5 and 1.5 mmHg, respectively.
The reaction is irreversible and the total pressure reaches 31.5 mm Hg after a long time.
(a) Determine the stoichiometry n and calculate the conversion after 20 min of reaction, knowing that the pressure was 19 mmHg.
(b) What would be the final volume considering that we have plug-flow reactor at constant pressure (equal to the initial pressure) and the reactor reached the same conversion of the previous system? It is known that the initial volume is 0.5 L.

Admin
Solution

(a) For a system at constant volume, the partial pressure of the reactant can be calculated as a function of the total pressure P :
\(p_{A}=p_{A0}-\frac{a}{\Delta \nu }\left ( p-p_{0} \right )\)

The initial pressure is \(p_{0}=p_{A0}+p_{1}=9mmHg\).

When the time is long enough, all the reactant is converted. The initial pressure of the reactant A is zero when the final pressure of the system is equal to 31.5 mmHg. Therefore,
\(0=7.5-\frac{1}{\Delta \nu }\left ( 31.5-9 \right )\)
thus,
\(\Delta \nu =3\) and \(n=4\)
Therefore
\(A\rightarrow 4R\)
Considering that both the volume and temperature are constant
\(p_{A}=C_{A}RT\),
thus, we obtain:
\(X_{A}=\frac{C_{A0}-C_{A}}{C_{A0}}=\frac{p_{A0}-p_{A}}{p_{A0}}\)

Substituting \(p_{A}\) ,
\(X_{A}=\frac{1}{3}\frac{p-p_{0}}{p_{A0}}\)

Substituting the values of \(p_{0}\) and P after 20 min, we obtain:
\(X_{A} =0.44\)

(b) Considering P=9mmHg and the conversion \(X_{A} =0.44\), the volume change can be calculated,
\(V=V_{0}\left ( 1+\varepsilon _{A}X_{A} \right )\)

therefore,
\(\Delta V=V-V_{0}=V_{0}\varepsilon _{A}X_{A}\)

The molar fractions can be calculated:
\(p_{A}=y_{A}p\Rightarrow y_{A}=0.83\)
\(\Rightarrow y_{1}=0.16\)

Thus, the value of \(\varepsilon _{A}\) can be calculated since,
A→4R Inert

total I R A
1 0.16 0 0.83 Initial
3.5 0.16 3.3 0 Final
\(\varepsilon _{A}=2.5\)

Thus, the volume change is:
\(\Delta V=V_{0\varepsilon _{A}}X_{A}=0.55\)

Therefore, the final volume will be:
V =1.05 L
