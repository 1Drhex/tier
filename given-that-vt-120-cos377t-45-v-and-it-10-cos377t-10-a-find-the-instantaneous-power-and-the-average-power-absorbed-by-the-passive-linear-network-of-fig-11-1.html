Guest Question.

Given that
\(v(t)=120 \cos \left(377 t+45^{\circ}\right) V \quad \text { and } \quad i(t)=10 \cos \left(377 t-10^{\circ}\right) A\)
find the instantaneous power and the average power absorbed by the passive linear network of Fig. 11.1.

Admin
Solution

The instantaneous power is given by

\(p=v i=1200 \cos \left(377 t + 45^{\circ}\right) \cos \left(377 t – 10^{\circ}\right)\)

Applying the trigonometric identity

\(\cos A \cos B=\frac{1}{2}[\cos (A + B) + \cos (A – B)]\)

gives

\(p= 600 \left[\cos \left(754 t + 35^{\circ}\right) + \cos 55^{\circ}\right]\)

or

\(p(t)= 344.2 + 600 \cos \left(754 t + 35^{\circ}\right) W\)

The average power is

\(\begin{aligned}P=\frac{1}{2} V_{m} I_{m} \cos \left(\theta_{v}-\theta_{i}\right) & =\frac{1}{2} 120(10) \cos \left[45^{\circ} – \left(-10^{\circ}\right)\right] \\&=600 \cos 55^{\circ}=344.2 W\end{aligned}\)

which is the constant part of p(t) above.
