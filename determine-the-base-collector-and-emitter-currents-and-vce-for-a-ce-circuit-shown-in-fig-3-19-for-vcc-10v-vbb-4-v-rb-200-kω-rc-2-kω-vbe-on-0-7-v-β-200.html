Guest Question.

Determine the base, collector and emitter currents and \(V_{CE} \) for a CE circuit shown in Fig. 3.19. For \(V_{CC} =10V,V_{BB} =4V,R_{B} =200k\Omega ,R_{C} =2k\Omega ,V_{BE} (on)=0.7V,\beta =200\)

Admin
Solution

Referring to Fig. 3.19, the base current is

\(I_{B} =\frac{V_{BB}-V_{BE}(on) }{R_{B} } =\frac{4-0.7}{200\times 10^{3} } =16.5\mu A\)

 

The collector current is

\(I_{C} =\beta I_{B}=200\times 16.5\times 10^{-6}=3.3mA \)

 

The emitter current is

\(I_{E} =I_{C}+I_{B}=3.3\times 10^{-3} +16.5\times 10^{-6} =3.3165mA\)

 

Therefore,

\(V_{CE} =V_{CC} -I_{C} R_{C} =10-3.3\times 10^{-3} \times 2\times 10^{3} =3.4V\)
