Guest Question.

A symmetrical tangent cam operating a roller follower has the following particulars:
Radius of base circle of cam=40 mm
Roller radius=20 mm
Angle of ascent = 75째
Total lift=20 mm
Speed of cam shaft=300 rpm
Determine (a) the principal dimensions of the cam, (b) the equation of the displacement curve, when the follower is in contact with the straight flank, and (c) the acceleration of the follower, when it is in contact with the straight flank where it merges into the circular nose.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } r_{1}=40 mm , r_{3}=20 mm , h=20 mm , N=300 rpm , \alpha=75^{\circ} \).

(a) With reference to Fig.8.40, we have

\( O P+r_{2}=r_{1}+h=40+20 \).

\( O P=60-r_{2} \).

\( O Q+r_{2}=r_{1}=40 \).

\( O Q=40-r_{2} \).

\( \cos \alpha=\frac{O Q}{O P} \).

\( \cos 75^{\circ}=\frac{40-r_{2}}{60-r_{2}} \).

\( 0.25882=\frac{40-r_{2}}{60-r_{2}} \).

\( 15.52914-0.25882 r_{2}=40-r_{2} \).

Nose radius, \( r_{2}=\frac{24.47085}{0.74118}=33 mm \).

Distance between cam and nose centre,

\( r=O P=60-33=27 mm \).

\( \tan \phi=\frac{R A}{O A}=\frac{P Q}{O A} \).

\( =\frac{O P \sin \alpha}{O A} \).

\( =\frac{27 \sin 75^{\circ}}{60} \).

\( =0.43467 \).

\( \phi=23.49^{\circ} \).

(b) Equation of displacement curve
1. When contact is with straight flank

\( x=\left(r_{1}+r_{3}\right)\left(\frac{1}{\cos \theta}-1\right) \).

\( =(40+20)\left(\frac{1}{\cos \theta}-1\right) \).

\( =60\left(\frac{1}{\cos \theta}-1\right) mm \).

2. When contact is with circular nose

\( x=r\left(1-\cos \theta_{1}\right)+\left(r_{2}+r_{3}\right)-\left(l^{2}-r^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta_{1}\right)^{0.5} \).

\( =27\left(1-\cos \theta_{1}\right)+(33+20)-\left(53^{2}-27^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta_{1}\right)^{0.5} \).

\( =27\left(1-\cos \theta_{1}\right)+53-\left(2809-729 \sin ^{2} \theta_{1}\right)^{0.5} \).

where \( \theta_{1} \) is measured from apex position,

(c) Acceleration of follower
When in contact with straight flank,

\( f=\omega^{2}\left(r_{1}+r_{3}\right)\left[\frac{2-\cos ^{2} \theta}{\cos ^{3} \theta}\right] \).

\( \omega=\frac{2 \pi \times 300}{60}=31.416 rad / s \).

\( \text { When } \theta=0^{\circ} \), \( f=(31.416)^{2}(0.040+0.020)\left[\frac{2-1}{1}\right] \).

\( =59.22 m / s ^{2} \).

\( \text { When in contact with straight flank, } \theta=\phi \).

\( f=\omega^{2}\left(r_{1}+r_{3}\right) \times\left[\frac{2-\cos ^{2} \phi}{\cos ^{3} \phi}\right] \).

\( =\frac{(31.416)^{2}(0.04+0.02)\left(2-\cos ^{2} 23.493^{\circ}\right)}{\cos ^{3} 23.439^{\circ}} \).

\( =52.94 m / s ^{2} \).

When contact is on circular nose,

\( \theta_{1}=\alpha-\phi=75-23.493=51.507^{\circ} \).

\( f=\omega^{2} r \times\left[\cos \theta_{1} \frac{\left(l^{2} r \cos 2 \theta_{1}+r^{3} \sin ^{4} \theta_{1}\right)}{\left(l^{2}-r^{2} \sin ^{2} \theta_{1}\right)^{1.5}}\right] \).

\( =(31.416)^{2} \times 0.027\left[\cos 51.507^{\circ}+\left(0.060^{2} \times 0.027 \cos 103.014^{\circ}\right.\right. \)

\( \left.\left.+0.027^{3} \sin ^{4} 51.503^{\circ}\right) /\left(0.060^{2}-0.027^{2} \sin ^{2} 51.507^{\circ}\right)^{1.5}\right] \).

\( =13.33 m / s ^{2}\).

When at apex, \( \theta_{1}=0^{\circ} \).

\( f=\omega^{2} r \times\left[1+\frac{r}{l}\right] \).

\( =(31.416)^{2} \times 0.027\left[1+\frac{0.027}{0.060}\right]=38.64 m / s ^{2} \).
