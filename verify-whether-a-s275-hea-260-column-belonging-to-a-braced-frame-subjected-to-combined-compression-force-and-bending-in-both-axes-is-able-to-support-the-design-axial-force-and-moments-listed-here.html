Guest Question.

Verify whether a S275 HEA 260 column, belonging to a braced frame, subjected to combined compression force and bending in both axes, is able to support the design axial force and moments listed here (second order acting along both cross-sectional effects are included). The column is pinned at its end and subjected to a uniformly distributed load along both the axes. The unbraced length is 4m (13.1 ft) in both axes. The column is not braced along its height (lateral-torsional buckling is not prevented).
Geometrical properties:
H = 250 mm (9.84 in.) A = 86.8 \(cm^{2}\) (13.45 in\(.^{2}\))
\(b_{f}\)= 260 mm (9.2 in.) \(W_{pl,y}\)= 919.8 \(cm^{3} (56.13 in.^{3})\)
\(t_{f}\)= 12.5 mm (0.49 in.) \(W_{el,y}\)= 836.4 \(cm^{3} (51.04 in.^{3})\)
\(_{w}\)= 7.5 mm (0.259 in.) \(W_{pl,z}\)= 430.2 \(cm^{3} (26.25 in.^{3})\)
r= 24 mm (0.95 in.) \(W_{el,z}= 282.1 cm^{3} (17.22 in.^{3})\)
L=4 m(13.1 ft) \(J_{y}=10 450cm^{4}(251.1 in.^{4})\)
\(L_{cr,y}\)=L \(J_{z}=3667.6 cm^{4}(88.11 in.^{4})\)
\(L_{cr,z}\)=L \(I_{t}=52.4 cm^{4}(1.26 in.^{4})\)
\(L_{cr,LT}\)=L \(I_{w}=516 400 cm^{6}(88.11 in.^{6})\)
Material properties:
Steel S275 \(f_{y}\) = 275 MPa (34 ksi) \(f_{u}\) = 430 MPa( 62 ksi)
Axial load and maximum moments at the member midspan:
\(N_{Ed}\) = 400 kN (89.9) kips
\(M_{y,Ed}\) =71 kNm (52.4 kips-ft)
\(M_{z,Ed}\) = 30 kNm (22.1 kips-ft)

Solution

AM1 Check According to EC3 Alternative Method 1.
(a) Section Classification
• Compression:
Flange : (\(c/t_{f}\)) = [260 – 7.5 – (2×24)]/(2×12.5) =8.2 ≤ 8.3 Class 1
Web : (\(d/t_{w}\))=[250 –(2×12.5) – (2×24)]/7.5=23.6 ≤ 30.3 Class 1
Section : Class1
• Bending:
Flange : (\(c/t_{f}\)) = [260 – 7.5 – (2×24)]/(2×12.5) =8.2 ≤ 8.3 Class 1
Web : (\(d/t_{w}\)=[250 –(2×12.5) – (2×24)]/7.5=23.6 ≤ 66 Class 1
Section : Class1
For combined axial load and bending, the section is therefore classified as Class 1.
(b) Compression
Compression : unbraced length \(L_{cr,y} = β_{y}L\)=1.0×4.0=4.0=4 m (13.1 ft) ;
\(L_{cr,z} = β_{z}L\)=1.0×4.0=4.0=4 m (13.1 ft)
Evaluation of the elastic critical buckling load about both principal axes:
\(N_{cr,y}=\pi^{2}\frac{EJ_{y}}{L^{2}_{cr,y}}=\pi^{2}\times\frac{21000\times 10450}{400^{2}}\)=13536.8 kN(3043 kips)
\(N_{cr,z}=\pi^{2}\frac{EJ_{y}}{L^{2}_{cr,z}}=\pi^{2}\times\frac{21000\times 3667.6}{400^{2}}\)=4750.9 kN(1068 kips)
Evaluation of the relative slenderness and associated reduction factor χ about both axes:
\(\bar{\lambda}_{y}=\sqrt{\frac{A.f_{y}}{N_{cr,y}}}=\sqrt{\frac{86.8\times 27.50}{13536.8}}=0.420;\)
\(\bar{\lambda}_{z}=\sqrt{\frac{A.f_{y}}{N_{cr,z}}}=\sqrt{\frac{86.8\times 27.50}{4750.9}}=0.709\)
\(\Phi_{y}=0.5[1+\alpha_{y}(\bar{\lambda}_{y} -0.2)+\bar{\lambda}^{2}_{y}]=0.5\times [1+0.34\times (0.420-0.2)+0.420^{2}]=0.626\)
\(\chi_{y}=\frac{1}{\Phi_{y}+\sqrt{\Phi_{y}^{2}-\bar{\lambda ^{2}}_{y}}}=\frac{1}{0.626}+\sqrt{0.626^{2}}-0.420^{2}=0.918\)
\(\Phi_{z}=0.5[1+\alpha_{z}(\bar{\lambda}_{y} -0.2)+\bar{\lambda}^{2}_{z}]=0.5\times [1+0.49\times (0.709-0.2)+0.709^{2}]=0.876\)
\(\chi_{z}=\frac{1}{\Phi_{z}+\sqrt{\Phi_{z}^{2}-\bar{\lambda^{2}}_{z}}}=\frac{1}{0.876}+\sqrt{0.876^{2}}-0.709^{2}=0.719\)
Available column strength in axial compression about the y–y and z–z axes are:
\(N_{by,Rd}=\frac{\chi_{y}A_{i}f_{y}}{\gamma_{M1}}=\frac{0.918\times86.8\times 27.50}{1.00}\)=2191.9 kN (492.8 kips)
\(N_{bz,Rd}=\frac{\chi_{z}A_{i}f_{y}}{\gamma_{M1}}=\frac{0.719\times86.8\times 27.50}{1.00}\)=1717.0 kN (386 kips)
(c) Lateral-Torsional Buckling
Consider k = \(k_{w}\) = 1 (rotation around the vertical axis and warping not prevented at both ends), \(z_{g}\) = 0
(load applied at centroid); and \(C_{1}\) = 1.127; \(C_{2}\) = 0.454 (uniformly distributed load).
Critical moment:
\(M_{cr}=C_{1}\frac{\pi ^{2}EJ_{z}}{(kL_{cr,LT})^{2}}=\left \{ \sqrt{\left ( \frac{k}{k_{w}} \right )\frac{I_{w}}{J_{z}}+\frac{(kL_{cr,LT})^{2}GI_{t}}{\pi^{2}EJ_{z}}+(C_{2}z_{g})^{2}} -C_{2}z_{g}\right \}\)
\(=1.127\frac{\pi ^{2}\times 21000\times 3667.6}{(1\times 400)^{2}}= \sqrt{\left ( \frac{1}{1} \right )^{2}\times \frac{516400}{3667.6}+\frac{(1\times 400)^{2}\times 8077\times 52.4}{\pi^{2}\times 21000\times 3667.6} }.10^{-2}\)
= 811.7 kNm (598.7 kips−ft)
Terms containing \(z_{g}\) have been disregarded because \(z_{g}\) = 0.
\(\bar{\lambda }_{LT}=\sqrt{\frac{W_{y}f_{y}}{M_{cr}}}=\sqrt{\frac{919.4\times 27.50}{811.7.10^{2}}}=0.558; \bar{\lambda}_{LT,0}=0.2; \beta =1;\)
\(\alpha _{LT}=0,k_{c}=0.49;\)
\(f =1-0.5 (1-k_{c})[1-2.0(\bar{\lambda}_{LT}-0.8)^{2}] =1-0.5\times (1-0.94) \times [1-2.0\times (0.558-0.8^{2})] =0.974\)
\(\Phi _{LT}=0.5[1+\alpha_{LT}(\bar{\lambda}_{LT}-\bar{\lambda}_{LT,0})+\beta \bar{\lambda}^{2}_{LT}]\)=0.5 × [1+0.34 × (0.558−0.2) +1×\(0.558^{2}\)=0.717
\(\chi_{LT}=\frac{1}{\Phi_{LT}+\sqrt{\Phi_{LT^{2}}-\bar{\lambda}^{2}_{LT}}}=\frac{1}{0.717+\sqrt{0.717^{2}-0.558^{2}}}=0.857\leq 1\)
\(\chi_{LT,mod}=\chi_{LT}/f=0.857/0.974=0.880\)
Calculate flexural strength for bending along the y–y and z–z axes:
\(M_{b,Rd}=\chi_{LT,mod}\frac{W_{y}f_{y}}{\gamma _{M1}}=0.880\times \frac{919.8\times 27.50}{1.00}.10^{-2}=222.8 kNm(164.2kips-ft)\)
\(M_{z,Rd}=\frac{W_{y}f_{y}}{\gamma _{M1}}= \frac{430.2\times 27.50}{1.00}.10^{-2}=118.3 kNm(87.3kips-ft)\)
(d) Combined compression and bending
\(\mu _{y}=\frac{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,y}}}{1-\chi _{y}\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,y}}}=\frac{1-\frac{400}{13536.8}}{1-0.918\times \frac{400}{13536.8}}=0.998;\)
\(w_{y}=\frac{W_{pl,y}}{W_{el,y}}=\frac{919.8}{836.4}=1.100\)
\(\mu _{z}=\frac{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}}}{1-\chi _{y}\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}}}=\frac{1-\frac{400}{4750.9}}{1-0.719\times \frac{400}{4750.9}}=0.975;\)
\(w_{z}=\frac{W_{pl,z}}{W_{el,z}}=\frac{430.2}{282.1}=1.525> 1.500 hence: w_{z}=1.500\)
Calculate \(C_{my,0} and C_{mz,0}\) parameters accounting for a bending moment diagram shape.
\(C_{my,0}=1+0.03\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,y}}=1+0.03\times \frac{400}{13536.8}=1.001\)
\(C_{mz,0}=1+0.03\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}}=1+0.03\times \frac{400}{4750.9}=1.003\)
Check if the section is subjected to flexural-torsional buckling.
\(I_{t} =52.4 cm^{4} < I_{y} = 10450 cm^{4}\)
In accordance with the criteria proposed in Eq. (9.8)\(N_{Ed}\leq \frac{h_{w}.t_{w}.f_{y}}{\gamma _{M0}}\) the result is that the cross-section should be susceptible of torsional deformations. Then calculate:
\(M_{cr,0}=\frac{\pi ^{2}EI_{z}}{L^{2}_{cr,LT}}\sqrt{\frac{I_{w}}{I_{z}}+\frac{L^{2}_{cr,LT}GI_{t}}{\pi ^{2}EI_{z}}}\)
\(=\frac{\pi ^{2}\times 21000\times 3667.6}{400^{2}}\times \sqrt{\frac{516400}{3667.6}+\frac{400^{2}\times 8077\times 52.4}{\pi ^{2}\times 21000\times 3667.6}}.10^{-2}\)
= 720.3 kNm (531.3 kips−ft)
\(N_{cr,TF}=N_{cr,T}=\frac{A}{I_{y}+I_{z}}\left ( GI_{t}+\frac{\pi ^{2}EI_{w}}{L^{2}_{cr,T}} \right )\)
\(=\frac{86.8}{10450+3667.6}\times \left ( 8077\times 52.4+\frac{\pi ^{2}\times 21000\times 516400}{400^{2}} \right )=6715.1 kN(1510 kips)\)
\(\bar{\lambda_{0}}_{lim}=0.2\times \sqrt{C_{1}}\sqrt[4]{\left ( 1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}} \right )\left ( 1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,TF}} \right )}=0.2\times \sqrt{1.127}\times ,\sqrt[4]{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{4750.9} \right )\left ( 1-\frac{400}{6715.1} \right )}=0.205\)
\(\bar{\lambda_{0}}=\sqrt{\frac{W_{pl,y}f_{y}}{M_{cr,o}}}=\sqrt{\frac{919.8\times 27.50}{72-.3.10^{2}}}=0.593> \bar{\lambda_{0}}_{lim}=0.22.205;\)
Being \(\bar{\lambda_{0}} > \bar{\lambda_{0}}_{lim}\)the section can be subjected to flexural-torsional buckling. Compute the\( C_{my}, C_{mz} and C_{mLT}\) parameters.
\(a_{LT}=1-\frac{I_{T}}{I_{y}}=1-\frac{52.4}{10450}=0.995\geq 0;\varepsilon_{y}=\frac{M_{y,Ed}}{N_{Ed}}.\frac{A}{W_{el,y}}=\frac{71\times 100\times 86.8}{400\times 836.4}=1.842;\)
\(C_{my}=C_{my,0}+(1-C_{my,0})\frac{\sqrt{\varepsilon _{y}}\alpha _{LT}}{1+\sqrt{\varepsilon _{y}}\alpha _{LT}}=1.001+(1-1.001)\times \frac{\sqrt{1.842}\times 0.995 }{1+\sqrt{1.842}\times 0.995}=1.00;C_{mz}=C_{mz,0}=1.003\)
\(C_{mLT}=C^{2}_{my}\frac{\alpha _{LT}}{\sqrt{\left ( 1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}} \right )\left ( 1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,T}} \right )}}=1.00^{2}\times \frac{0.995}{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{4750.9\frac{400}{}} \right )\times \left (1- 6715.1 \right )}=1.073\geq 1\)
Compute the interaction parameters \(C_{yy}, C_{yz}, C_{zy}, C_{zz}\):
\(\bar{\lambda }_{max}=max\left \{ \bar{\lambda}_{y};\bar{\lambda}_{z}\right \}=max\left \{ 0.427;0.709\right \}=0.709;\)
\(b_{LT}=0.5a_{LT}\bar{\lambda }^{2}_{0}\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{y,Ed}}{\chi _{LT}f_{y}W_{pl,y}}.\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{z,Ed}}{f_{y}W_{pl,z}}=0.5\times 0.995\times 0.593^{2}\times \frac{1.00\times 71.10^{2}}{0.857\times 27.50\times 919.8}\times \frac{1.00\times 30.10^{2}}{27.50\times 430.2}=0.014\)
\(c_{LT}=10a_{LT}\frac{\bar{\lambda ^{2}_{0}}}{5+\bar{\lambda }^{4}_{z}}.\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{y,Ed}}{C_{my}\chi _{LT}f_{y}W_{pl,y}}\)
\(=10\times 0.995\times\frac{0.593^{2}}{5+0.709^{4}}\times\frac{1.00\times71.10^{2}}{1.00\times0.880\times27.50\times919.8}=0.212\)
\(d_{LT}=2a_{LT}\frac{\bar{\lambda_{0}}}{0.1+\bar{\lambda }^{4}_{z}}.\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{y,Ed}}{C_{my}\chi _{LT}f_{y}W_{pl,y}}.\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{z,Ed}}{C_{mz}f_{y}W_{pl,z}}\)
\(2\times0.995\times\frac{0.593}{0.1_0.709^{4}}\times\frac{1.00\times71.10^{2}}{1.00\times0.880\times27.50\times919.8}\times\frac{1.00\times30.10^{2}}{1.003\times27.50\times430.2}=0.269\)
\(e_{LT}=1.7a_{LT}\frac{\bar{\lambda_{0}}}{0.1+\bar{\lambda }^{4}_{z}}.\frac{\gamma _{M0}M_{y,Ed}}{C_{my}\chi _{LT}f_{y}W_{pl,y}}\)
\(=1.7\times 0.995\times \frac{0.593}{0.1+0.709^{4}}\times\frac{1.00\times71.10^{2}}{1.00\times0.880\times27.50\times919.8}=0.906\)
\(C_{yy}=1+(w_{y}-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{1.6}{w_{y}}C^{2}_{my} \left ( \bar{\lambda}_{max}+\bar{\lambda}_{max}^{2} \right ) \right ) .\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{f_{y}A_{i}}-b_{LT}\right ]\)
\(=1+(1.1-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{1.6}{1.1}1.0^{2} \times \left ( 0.709+0.709^{2} \right ) \right ) .\frac{1.00\times 400}{27.50\times 86.8}-0.014\right ]=1.003\)
\(\geq \frac{W_{el,y}}{W_{pl,y}}=\frac{836.4}{919.8}=0.909\)
\(C_{yz}=1+(w_{z}-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{14}{w_{z}^{5}}C^{2}_{mz} \bar{\lambda}_{max}^{2}\right ) .\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{f_{y}A_{i}}-c_{LT}\right ]\)
\(=1+(1.5-1)\times \left [ \left (2-\frac{14}{1.5^{5}}1.003^{2} \times 0.709^{2}\right )\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{27.50\times 86.8}-0.212\right ]=0.984 \)
\(\geq 0.6\sqrt{\frac{w_{z}}{w_{y}}}\frac{W_{el,z}}{W_{pl,z}}=0.6\times \sqrt{\frac{1.5}{1.1}}\times \frac{282.1}{430.2}=0.459\)
\(C_{zy}=1+(w_{y}-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{14}{w_{y}^{5}}C^{2}_{mz} \bar{\lambda}_{max}^{2}\right ) .\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{f_{y}A_{i}}-d_{LT}\right ]\)
\(=1+(1.1-1)\times \left [ \left (2-\frac{14}{1.1^{5}}1.00^{2} \times 0.709^{2}\right )\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{27.50\times 86.8}-0.269\right ]=0.933\)
\(\geq 0.6\sqrt{\frac{w_{y}}{w_{z}}}\frac{W_{el,y}}{W_{pl,y}}=0.6\times \sqrt{\frac{1.1}{1.5}}\times \frac{836.4}{919.8}=0.467\)
\(C_{zz}=1+(w_{z}-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{1.6}{w_{z}}C^{2}_{mz} \left ( \bar{\lambda}_{max}+\bar{\lambda}_{max}^{2} \right )\right )-e_{LT}\right ].\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{f_{y}A_{i}}\)
\(=1+(1.5-1)\left [ \left (2-\frac{1.6}{1.5}1.003^{2}\times \left ( 0.709+0.709^{2} \right )\right )-0.906\right ].\frac{1.00\times 400}{27.50\times 86.8}=0.983\)
\(\geq \frac{W_{el,z}}{W_{pl,z}}= \frac{282.1}{430.2}=0.656\)
Compute \(k_{ij}\):
\(k_{yy}=C_{my}C_{mLT}\frac{\mu_{y}}{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,y}}}\frac{1}{C_{yy}}=1.0\times 1.073\times \frac{0.998}{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{13536.8} \right )\times 1.003}=1.100\)
\(k_{yz}=C_{mz}\frac{\mu_{y}}{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}}}\frac{1}{C_{yz}}0.6\sqrt{\frac{w_{z}}{w_{y}}}=1.003\times \frac{0.998}{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{4750.9} \right )\times 0.984}\times 0.6\times \sqrt{\frac{1.5}{1.1}}=0.778\)
\(k_{zy}=C_{my}C_{mLT}\frac{\mu_{z}}{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,y}}}\frac{1}{C_{zy}}0.6\sqrt{\frac{w_{y}}{w_{z}}}=1.0\times 1.073\times \frac{0.975}{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{13536.8} \right )\times 0.933}\times 0.6\times \sqrt{\frac{1.1}{1.5}}=0.593\)
\(k_{zz}=C_{mz}\frac{\mu_{z}}{1-\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{cr,z}}}\frac{1}{C_{zz}}=1.003\times \frac{0.975}{\left ( 1-\frac{400}{4750.9} \right )\times 0.983}=1.086\)
And finally compute Eqs. (9.19a) and (9.19b):
\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{by,Rd}}+K_{yy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}+e_{N,y}N_{Ed}}{M_{b,Rd}}+K_{yz}\frac{M_{z,Ed}+e_{N,z}N_{Ed}}{M_{z,Rd}}=\frac{400}{2191.9}+1.10\times \frac{71}{222.8}+0.778\times \frac{30}{118.3}\)
=0.18 + 0.35 + 0.20 = 0.73 ≤ 1
\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{bz,Rd}}+K_{zy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}+e_{N,y}N_{Ed}}{M_{b,Rd}}+K_{zz}\frac{M_{z,Ed}+e_{N,z}N_{Ed}}{M_{z,Rd}}=\frac{400}{1717.0}+0.593\times \frac{71}{222.8}+1.086\times \frac{30}{118.3}\)
=0.23 + 0.19 + 0.28 = 0.70 ≤ 1
AM2 Check according to EC3 Alternative Method 2
Compute parameters \(C_{my}, C_{mz} and C_{mLT}\), using Table B.3 of EN 1993-1-1 Annex B.
\(α_{h} =M_{h}/M_{s}\) =0.0/71.0=0; ψ =0
(same value for the moment about the z–z axis)
\(C_{my} = C_{mz}= C_{mLT}\)=0.95 + 0.05\(\alpha_{h}\) =0.95 + 0.05 × 0 = 0.95
Compute \(k_{ij}\).
\(k_{yy}=C_{my}\left [ 1+(\bar{\lambda}_{y} -0.2)\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{y}f_{y}A_{i}}\right ] =0.95\times \left [ 1+(0.420-0.2)\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{0.918\times 27.50\times 86.8} \right ]=0.936\)
\(\leq C_{my}\left ( 1+0.8\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{y}f_{y}A_{i}} \right )=0.95\times \left ( 1+0.8\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{0.918\times 27.50\times 86.8} \right )=1.089\)
\(k_{zz}=C_{mz}\left [ 1+(2\bar{\lambda}_{z} -0.6)\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{z}f_{y}A_{i}}\right ] =0.95\times \left [ 1+(2 \times 0.709-0.6)\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{0.719\times 27.50\times 86.8} \right ]=1.071\)
\(\leq C_{mz}\left ( 1+1.4\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{z}f_{y}A_{i}} \right )=0.95\times \left ( 1+1.4\times \frac{1.00\times 400}{0.719\times 27.50\times 86.8} \right )=1.260\)
\(k_{yz} =0.6k_{zz}\) =0.6×1.071 = 0.643; \(\bar{\lambda}_{z}\) =0.709 > 0.4; hence:
\(K_{zy}=\left [ 1-\frac{0.1\bar{\lambda}_{z}}{(C_{mLT}-0.25)}\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{z}f_{y}A_{i}} \right ]=1-\frac{0.1\times0.709 }{(0.95-0.25)}\times\frac{1.00\times400}{0.719\times27.50\times86.8}=0.975\)
\(\geq \left [ 1-\frac{0.1}{(C_{mLT}-0.25)}\frac{\gamma _{M1}N_{Ed}}{\chi _{z}f_{y}A_{i}} \right ]=1-\frac{0.1 }{(0.95-0.25)}\times\frac{1.00\times400}{0.719\times27.50\times86.8}=0.967\)
And finally compute Eqs. (9.19a) and (9.19b):
\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{by,Rd}}+k_{yy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}+e_{N,y}N_{Ed}}{M_{b,Rd}}+k_{yz}\frac{M_{z,Ed}+e_{N,z}N_{Ed}}{M_{z,Rd}}=\frac{400}{2191.9}+0.936\times \frac{71}{222.8}+0.643\times \frac{30}{118.3}\)
=0.18 + 0.30 + 0.16 = 0.64 ≤ 1
\(\frac{N_{Ed}}{N_{bz,Rd}}+k_{zy}\frac{M_{y,Ed}+e_{N,y}N_{Ed}}{M_{b,Rd}}+k_{zz}\frac{M_{z,Ed}+e_{N,z}N_{Ed}}{M_{z,Rd}}=\frac{400}{1717.0}+0.967\times \frac{71}{222.8}+1.071\times \frac{30}{118.3}\)
=0.23 + 0.32 + 0.27 = 0.82 ≤ 1
