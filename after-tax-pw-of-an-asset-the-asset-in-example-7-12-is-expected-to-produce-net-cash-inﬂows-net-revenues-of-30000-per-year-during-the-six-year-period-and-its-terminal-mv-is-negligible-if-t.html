Guest Question.

After-Tax PW of an Asset
The asset in is expected to produce net cash inflows (net revenues) of $30,000 per year during the six-year period, and its terminal MV is negligible. If the effective income tax rate is 40%, how much can a firm afford to spend for this asset and still earn the MARR? What is the meaning of any excess in affordable amount over the $100,000 cost basis given in ? [Equals the after tax PW.]

Admin
Solution

After income taxes, the PW of net revenues is (1 − 0.4)($30,000)(P/A, 10%, 6) = $18,000(4.3553) = $78,395. After adding to this the PW of tax savings computed in , the affordable amount is $107,343.
