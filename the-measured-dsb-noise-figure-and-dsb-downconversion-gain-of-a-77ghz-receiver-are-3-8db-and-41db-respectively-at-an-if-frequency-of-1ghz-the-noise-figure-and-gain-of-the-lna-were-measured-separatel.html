Guest Question.

The measured DSB noise figure and DSB downconversion gain of a 77GHz receiver are 3.8dB and 41dB, respectively, at an IF frequency of 1GHz. The noise figure and gain of the LNA were measured separately as 3.7dB and 30dB. What is the SSB noise figure of the receiver at 1GHz IF if an image-reject filter is placed in front of the mixer, after the LNA? Assume that the gain and noise figure of the LNA and of the mixer remain constant from 76GHz to 78GHz.

Admin
Solution

According to (9.37), when an image-reject filter is placed at the LNA output \(G_2 = 0\) and the single-sideband noise figure and noise temperature of the receiver is going to be the same as the double-sideband one.

 

*equation 9.37 : \(F_{S S B}=\frac{S_{i S S B} N_{\mathrm{o}}}{S_{\mathrm{o}} N_{\mathrm{i}}}=\frac{V_{R F}^{2}\left[\left(G_{1}+G_{2}\right) k T \Delta f+N_{\mathrm{a}}\right]}{G_{1} V_{R F}^{2} k T \Delta f}=\left(1+\frac{G_{2}}{G_{1}}\right)\left[1+\frac{N_{a}}{k T \Delta f\left(G_{1}+G_{2}\right)}\right] \)
