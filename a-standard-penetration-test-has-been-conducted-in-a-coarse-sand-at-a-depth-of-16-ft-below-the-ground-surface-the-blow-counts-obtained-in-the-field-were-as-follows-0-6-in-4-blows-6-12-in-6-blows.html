Guest Question.

A standard penetration test has been conducted in a coarse sand at a depth of 16 ft below the ground surface. The blow counts obtained in the field were as follows: 0-6 in: 4 blows; 6-12 in: 6 blows; 12-18 in: 6 blows. The tests were conducted using a USA-style donut hammer in a 6 in diameter boring using a standard sampler with the liner installed. The vertical effective stress at the test depth was \(1500 lb/ft^{2}\). Determine \((N_{1})_{60}\).
