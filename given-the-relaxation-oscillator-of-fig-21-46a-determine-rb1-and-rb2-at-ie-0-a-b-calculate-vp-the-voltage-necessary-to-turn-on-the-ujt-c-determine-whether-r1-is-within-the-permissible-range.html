Guest Question.

Given the relaxation oscillator of Fig. 21.46:
(a) Determine \(R_{B_{1}} and R_{B_{2}} at I_{E} = 0 A\).
(b) Calculate \(V_{P}\) , the voltage necessary to turn on the UJT.
(c) Determine whether \(R_{1}\) is within the permissible range of values as determined by Eq. (21.8) \(\frac{V - V_{V}}{I_{V}} \lt R_{1} \lt \frac{V - V_{P}}{I_{P}}\) to ensure firing of the UJT.
(d) Determine the frequency of oscillation if \(R_{B_{1}} = 100 \Omega\) during the discharge phase.
(e) Sketch the waveform of \(v_{C}\) for a full cycle.
(f) Sketch the waveform of \(v_{R_{2}}\) for a full cycle.

Admin
Solution

(a) \(\eta = \frac{R_{B_{1}}}{R_{B_{1}} + R_{B_{2}}}\)

 

\(0.6 = \frac{R_{B_{1}}}{R_{BB}}\)

 

\(R_{B_{1}} = 0.6R_{BB} = 0.6(5 k \Omega) = 3 k \Omega\)

 

\(R_{B_{2}} = R_{BB} – R_{B_{1}} = 5 k \Omega – 3 k \Omega = 2 k \Omega\)

(b) At the point where \(v_{C} = V_{P}\) , if we continue with \(I_{E} = 0 A\) , the network of Fig. 21.47 will result, where

\(V_{P} = 0.7 V + \frac{(R_{B_{1}} + R_{2}) 12 V}{\underbrace{R_{B_{1}} + R_{B_{2}}}_{R_{BB}} + R_{2}} = 0.7 V + \frac{(3 k \Omega + 0.1 k \Omega) 12 V}{5 k \Omega + 0.1 k \Omega} = 0.7 V + 7.294 V \cong 8 V\)

(c) \(\frac{V – V_{V}}{I_{V}} \lt R_{1} \lt \frac{V – V_{P}}{I_{P}}\)

 

\(\frac{12 V – 1 V}{10 m A} \lt R_{1} \lt \frac{12 V – 8 V}{10 \mu A}\)

 

\(1.1 k \Omega \lt R_{1} \lt 400 k \Omega\)

The resistance \(R_{1} = 50 k \Omega\) falls within this range.

(d) \(t_{1} = R_{1} C \log_{e} \frac{V – V_{V}}{V – V_{P}} = (50 k \Omega)(0.1 pF) \log_{e} \frac{12 V – 1 V}{12 V – 8 V} = 5 \times 10^{- 3} \log_{e} \frac{11}{4} = 5 \times 10^{- 3}(1.01) = 5.05 ms\)

 

\(t_{2} = (R_{B_{1}} + R_{2})C \log_{e} \frac{V_{P}}{V_{V}} = (0.1 k \Omega + 0.1 k \Omega)(0.1 pF) \log_{e} \frac{8}{1} = (0.02 \times 10^{- 6})(2.08) = 41.6 \mu s\)

and \(T = t_{1} + t_{2} = 5.05 ms + 0.0416 ms = 5.092 ms\)

with \(f_{osc} = \frac{1}{T} = \frac{1}{5.092 m s} \cong 196 Hz\)

Using Eq. (21.17) gives us

\(f \cong \frac{1}{R_{1} C \log_{e}\left[1 /(1 – \eta)\right] }= \frac{1}{5 \times 10^{- 3} \log_{e} 2.5} = 218 Hz\)

(e) See Fig. 21.48.

(f) During the charging phase, (Eq. 21.9)

\(V_{R_{2}} = \frac{R_{2} V}{R_{2} + R_{BB}} = \frac{0.1 k \Omega (12 V)}{0.1 k \Omega + 5 k \Omega} = 0.235 V \)

When \(v_{C} = V_{P}\) (Eq. 21.12)

\(V_{R_{2}} \cong \frac{R_{2}(V_{P} – 0.7 V)}{R_{2} + R_{B_{1}}} = \frac{0.1 k \Omega(8 V – 0.7 V)}{0.1 k \Omega + 0.1 k \Omega} = 3.65 V\)

The plot of \(v_{R_{2}}\) appears in Fig. 21.49.
