Guest Question.

A governor of the Hartnell type has each ball of weight 15 N and the lengths of vertical and horizontal arms of the bell crank lever are 120 mm and 60 mm, respectively. The fulcrum of the bell crank lever is at a distance of 100 mm from the axis of rotation. The maximum and minimum radii of rotation of the balls are 120 mm and 80 mm and the corresponding equilibrium speeds are 325 and 300 rpm, respectively. Find the stiffness of the spring and the equilibrium speed when the radius of rotation is 100 mm.

Admin
Solution

Refer to Fig.9.23.

Given: \( w=15 N , a=120 mm , b=60 mm , r=100 mm , r_{1}=120 mm , r_{2}=80 mm \).

\( N_{1}=325, N_{2}=300 rpm , k=? \).

\( F_{c l}=(w / g) \omega_{1}^{2} r_{1}=(20 / 9.81)(2 \pi \times 325 / 60)^{2} \times 0.08=188.92 N \).

\( F_{c 2} \times a=S_{2} \times b / 2 \).

\( S_{2}=188.92 \times 120 \times 2 / 80=566.76 N \).

\( r_{1}=r_{2}+h \times a / b=80+15 \times 120 / 80=102.5 mm \).

\( F_{c 1}=(w / g) \omega_{1}^{2} r=(20 / 9.81)\left(2 \pi \times N_{1} / 60\right)^{2} \times 0.1025=2.2916 \times 10^{-3} \times N_{1}^{2} \).

\( r_{1}-r_{2}=h a / b=15 \times 120 / 80=22.5 mm \).

\( k=2(a / b)^{2}\left[\left(F_{c 1}-F_{c 2}\right) /\left(r_{1}-r_{2}\right)\right] \).

\( 25=2 \times(120 / 80)^{2}\left[\left(2.2916 \times 10^{-3} \times N^{2}-188.92\right) / 22.5\right] \).

\( N_{1}=290.9 rpm \).
