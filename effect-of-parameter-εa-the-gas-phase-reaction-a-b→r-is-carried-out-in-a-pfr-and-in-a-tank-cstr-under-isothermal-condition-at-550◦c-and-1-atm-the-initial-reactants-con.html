Guest Question.

Effect of parameter \(\varepsilon _{A} \)
The gas-phase reaction A + B→R is carried out in a PFR and in a tank (CSTR) under isothermal condition at 550◦C and 1 atm. The initial reactants’ concentrations are equimolar and the inlet volumetric flow is 10 L/min. It is an irreversible second-order reaction and the specific reaction rate is given by:
\(k=7\times 10^{4}.e^{\frac{-9000}{T}}(L/(mol s)) \)
Calculate the average residence time, space time, and reactor volume for a final conversion of 75% by carrying out the reaction in a PFR and CSTR separately. What is the ratio between their volumes?

Admin
Solution

The reaction rate as a function of conversion to an irreversible second-order reaction, with \(C_{A0} =C_{B0} \) will be:
\(-r_{A}=kC^{2}_{A}=\frac{C^{2}_{A0}(1-X_{A})^{2}}{(1+\varepsilon _{A}X_{A})^{2}} \)

And the average residence time in the PFR is as follows:
\(\bar{t}=C_{A0}\int_{0}^{X_{A}}\frac{dX_{A}}{(1+\varepsilon _{A}X_{A})(-r_{A})} \)

Substituting \((−r_{A}) \) into the above equation and integrating, we obtain:
\(\bar{t}=\frac{1}{kC_{A0}}[\frac{1+\varepsilon _{A}X_{A}}{1-X_{A}}+\ln (1-X_{A})] \)

Calculating \(\varepsilon _{A}: \)

A B R Inert Total
0.4 0.4 0 0.2 1
0 0 0.4 0.2 0.6 \(\varepsilon _{A} \) = -0.4 (contraction)

Calculating \(C_{A0}: \)
\(C_{A0}=\frac{\gamma _{A0}P}{RT}=\frac{0.4\times 1}{0.082\times 823}=5.92\times 10^{-3} \)

We obtain k by Arrhenius equation provided:
\(k=0.712 L/(mol s) \)

Substituting the values into equation:
\(\bar{t} =22.7 min \)

Space time can be determined by equation:
\(\tau =\frac{V}{\nu _{0}}=C_{A0}\int_{0}^{X_{A}}\frac{dX_{A}}{(-r_{A})} \)

Substituting \((−r_{A}) \) and integrating:
\(\tau =\frac{1}{kC_{A0}}[(1+\varepsilon _{A})^{2}\frac{X_{A}}{(1-X_{A})}+\varepsilon _{A}^{2}X_{A}+2\varepsilon _{A}(1+\varepsilon _{A})\ln (1-X_{A})] \)

Substituting \(X_{A} =0.70 \), we obtain:
\(\tau =7.38 min \)
Therefore, the PFR volume will be:
\(V_{PFR} =73.8L \)
In the CSTR, we obtain analogously:
\(\tau _{CSTR} =\frac{X_{A}(1 + \varepsilon _{A}X_{A})^{2}}{kC_{A0}(1 − X_{A})^{2}} \)

\(\tau _{CSTR} =15.9 min \)

That leads to a reactor volume of:
\(V_{CSTR} =159 L \)
Thus, the ratio between the different reactors is:
\(\frac{V_{CSTR}}{V_{PFR}} =2.2 \)
