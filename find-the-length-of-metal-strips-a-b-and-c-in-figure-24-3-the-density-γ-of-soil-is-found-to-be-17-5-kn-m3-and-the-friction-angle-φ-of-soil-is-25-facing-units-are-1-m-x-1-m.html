Guest Question.

Find the length of metal strips A, B and C in Figure 24.3. The density \((\gamma)\) of soil is found to be \(17.5 kN/m^{3}\) and the friction angle \((\varphi)\) of soil is \(25^{\circ }.\) Facing units are \(1 m \times 1 m\) and the metal strips are 0.5 m wide. Find the length of the metal strips if required factor of safety is 2.5.

Admin
Solution

Step 1: Find the vertical effective stress at the center of the facing unit
Facing unit “A”
Find the vertical effective stress at the center of the facing unit \((\sigma ^{\prime }_{v});\)

\((\sigma ^{\prime }_{v})=\gamma \times h=17.5\times 6kN/m^{2}.\)

 

\( =105kN/m^{2}\)

Find the lateral earth pressure coefficient \((K_{a});\)

\(K_{a}=\tan ^{2}(45-\varphi /2)\)

 

\( =\tan ^{2}(45-25/2)\)

 

\( =0.406\)

Find the horizontal stress at the center of the facing unit \((\sigma ^{\prime }_{h})\)

\((\sigma ^{\prime }_{h})=K_{a}\times \sigma ^{\prime }_{v}\)

 

\( =0.406\times 105k N/m^{2}.\)

 

\( =42.6kN/m^{2}.\)

Area of the facing unit \("A" =\) Width \(\times\) length \(= 1 \times 1 m^{2} = 1 m^{2}.\)

Total horizontal force on the facing unit = horizontal stress area of the facing unit

\( = 42.6 kN\)

Step 2: Find the metal – soil friction in facing unit “A”;

Metal – soil friction \(( P)=2\times (\sigma ^{\prime }_{v}\times \tan \delta )\times area of the strip \)

 

\( =2\times 105\times \tan 20\times 0.5 L\)

 

\( =38.2L\)

Figure 24.3 Reinforcing strips.

Required factor of safety (FOS) = 2.5

FOS Metal soil friction/horizontal force on the facing unit
\(2.5 = 38.2 /42.6\)
\( L=2.79 m\)

The length of the metal strip within the active soil zone does not contribute to the strength. Hence, the metal strip should extend 2.79 m beyond the active failure zone (Figures 24.4–24.6).

Figure 24.4 Active and passive failure planes.
Figure 24.5 Metal strips and active failure plane.

Figure 24.6 Facing unit \("A"\) and the active failure plane.

Figure 24.6 shows the facing unit \("A"\) and the active failure plane.
Distance \("n"\) can be computed since the distance to the center of the facing unit is 0.5 m.

\(n=0.5\times \tan (45-\varphi /2)\)

 

\(n=0.5\times \tan (45-25/2)\)

 

\(n=0.32 m\)

Total required length of the strip \(= 0.32 + 2.79 = 3.11 m.\)
