Guest Question.

An isosceles triangle with constant thickness t made of aluminium (density \(\rho _{al}\)) is attached to a semicircular disc with the same thickness made of copper (density \(\rho _{co}\)). Determine the maximum height h of the triangle to ensure that the system returns to its initial position after a deflection of \(\left|\varphi \right|\leq 90^{\circ }.\)

Admin
Solution

Introducing a distance a between the overall center of gravity C and the center of the semicircle, the potential energy of the system for an arbitrary position \( (-90^{\circ}\leq \varphi \leq 90^{\circ })\) yields

\(V=-(W_{co}+W_{al}) a (1-\cos \varphi ),\)

where the weight of the semicircular plate can be calculated as

\(W_{co}=g m_{co} , m_{co}=\rho _{co} t \frac{r^{2}\pi }{2}.\)

The weight of the triangular disc is

\(W_{al}=g m_{al} , m_{al}=\rho _{al}g t r h .\)

The equilibrium positions result from

\(\frac{dV}{d\varphi }=0 \rightsquigarrow – (W_{co}+W_{al}) a \sin \varphi =0.\)

Following this, only \(\varphi ^{*}=0\) provides an equilibrium position. This equilibrium state is stable for

\(\frac{d^{2}V}{d\varphi ^{2}}\mid _{\varphi ^{*}}\gt0 \rightsquigarrow -(W_{co}+W_{al}) a \gt 0.\)

This condition is fulfilled, when \(a \lt 0, i.e.\) the overall center of gravity must be below the center of the semicircle:

\(z_{C}=\frac{m_{co} z_{co}+m_{al} z_{al}} {m_{co}+m_{al}} \gt 0 \rightsquigarrow m_{co} z_{co}\gt -m_{al} z_{al} .\)

Using the centers of gravity \(z_{co}=\frac{4}{3\pi }\) and \(z_{al}=-\frac{h}{3},\) one obtains

\(\rho _{co} t\frac{r^{2}\pi }{2}\frac{4}{3\pi }r\gt \rho _{al} t r h\frac{h}{3} \rightsquigarrow \underline{\underline{h \lt r\sqrt{2\frac{\rho _{co}}{\rho _{al}} } } }.\)
