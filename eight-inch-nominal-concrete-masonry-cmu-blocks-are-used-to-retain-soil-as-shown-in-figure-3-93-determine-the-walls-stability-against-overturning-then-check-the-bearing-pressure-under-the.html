Guest Question.

Eight-inch nominal concrete masonry \((CMU)\) blocks are used to retain soil as shown in Figure 3.93. Determine the wall’s stability against overturning, then check the bearing pressure under the footing base at the toe \((A)\). Assume that the allowable bearing pressure for the soil is limited to 2,500 psf.

Assume:\( \gamma_{CMU}=125\# -ft.^{3}\);

\(\gamma_{conc}=150 \frac{\# }{ft^{3} } \); equivalent design fluid pressure

\( \omega^{\prime } = 40 \frac{\# }{ft^{3} } \)
Admin
Solution

Assume a 1-foot-wide width of wall as representative of the entire wall (Figure 3.94).

\(W_{1} =W_{CMU}=(1 \space ft.)(3.33 \space ft.)\left(\frac{8}{12} \space ft.\right) \left(125 \frac{\# }{ft^{3} }\right) =279\#\)

 

\(W_{2} =W_{ftg}=(1 \space ft.)\left(\frac{10}{12} \space ft.\right) (2.33 \space ft.)\left(150 \frac{\# }{ft^{3} }\right) =291\# \)

 

\( W_{total}=W_{1}+W_{2}=279\# +291\# =570\# \)

The horizontal force on the wall due to the fluid pressure is equal to

\( p_{max}=\left(40 \frac{\# }{ft^{3} }\right)(4.17 \space ft.)=167 \space psf\)

 

\( P=\frac{1}{2}(p_{max})(h)(1 \space ft.)=\frac{1}{2}(167\# -ft.^{2} )(4.17 \space ft.)(1 \space ft.)\)

 

\(=348\# \)

The location of the resultant weight \( W_{total}\) is determined by summing moments about the toe at A.

\( M_{A} = W_{1} \times ( 2 \space ft.)+W_{2} \times (1.17\space ft.)\)

 

\( M_{A} = (279\# ) ( 2 \space ft.)+(291\# ) (1.17\space ft.)=899\# -ft.\)

 

\( M_{A} = W_{total}\times (b) =(570\# )\times (b)=899\# -ft.\)

 

\( \therefore b=1.58 \space ft.\cong 1.6\space ft.\)

The resultant force on the retaining wall is

\(R=\sqrt{(570^{2}+348^{2}) }=668\# \)

The overturning moment caused by the horizontal force about the toe at \(A\) is

\( M_{OTM}=(348\# )\times\left( \frac{4.17 \space ft.}{3}\right) =484\# -ft.\)

The righting or stabilizing moment due to the total weight of the retaining wall is calculated as

\(M_{RM} =W_{total}\times b=(570\# )\times (1.6 \space ft.) =912\# -ft. \)

Checking the safety factor against overturning:

\( SF=\frac{M_{RM}}{M_{OTM}}=\frac{912\# -ft.}{484\# -ft.} =1.9\gt 1.5 \space \space \space \space \therefore OK\)

Resultant force \(R\) intersects the footing base at

\( x=\frac{M_{RM}-M_{OTM}}{W_{total}}=\frac{(912-484 )\#-ft.}{570\# } =0.75 \space ft. \)

 

\( \frac{a}{3} =0.78\space ft. \gt x=0.75 \space ft.\)

 

\( \therefore \)The resultant falls outside the middle third section of the footing base.

\( p_{max}=\frac{2W}{3x} =\frac{3(570)}{3(0.75)} =507 \space psf \lt 2,500 \space psf\)

 

\( \therefore OK\)
