Determining the Effects of Gear Ratio on the Effective Motor Torque and Inertia

The parameters of the gearbox shown in Figure 14.7 are \(B_1 = 0.025 Nm/rad/s, ω_1 = 210 rad/s,\) \(B_m = 0.045 kg–m^2, J_m = 0.32 kg–m^2, T_2 = 20 Nm\), and \(ω_2 = 21 rad/s\). Determine (a) the gear ratio \(GR = N_2/N_1\), (b) the effective motor torque \(T_1\), (c) the effective inertia J, and (d) the effective
friction coefficient B.

Admin
Solution

\(B_1 = 0.025 Nm/rad/s, ω_1 = 210 rad/s, B_m = 0.045 kg–m^2, J_m = 0.32 kg–m^2, T_2 = 20 Nm\), and \(ω_2 = 21 rad/s\).

a. Using Eq. (14.14),

\(\frac{\omega _1}{\omega _2} =\frac{N_2}{N_1}\) (14.14)

\(GR=\frac{N_1}{N_2}=\frac{\omega _1}{\omega _2}=\frac{210}{21}=10 \)

b. Using Eq. (14.15),

\(T_2=\left(\frac{N_2}{N_1} \right)^2T_1\) (14.15)

\(T_1=\frac{T_2}{GR^2}=\frac{20}{10^2}=0.2 Nm\)

c. Using Eq. (14.16),

\(J=J_m+\left(\frac{N_1}{N_2} \right)^2J_1\) (14.16)

\(J=J_m+\frac{J_1}{GR^2}=0.32+\frac{0.25}{10^2}=0.323 Kg –m^2\)

d. Using Eq. (14.17),

\(B=B_m+\left(\frac{N_1}{N_2} \right)^2B_1\) (14.17)

\(B=B_m+\frac{B_1}{GR^2} = 0.045+\frac{0.025}{10^2}=0.045 Nm/rad/s\)
