Guest Question.

A space heating unit in Alaska uses propane combustion is the heat supply. Liquid propane comes from an outside tank at -44°C and the air supply is also taken in from the outside at -44°C. The airflow regulator is misadjusted, such that only 90% of the theoretical air enters the combustion chamber resulting in incomplete combustion. The products exit at 1000 K as a chemical equilibrium gas mixture including only \(CO _{2}, CO , H _{2} O , H _{2}, \text { and } N _{2}\). Find the composition of the products.
Hint: use the water gas reaction in .

Admin
Solution

Propane: Liquid, \(T _{1}=-44^{ o } C =229.2 K\)

Air: \(T _{2}=-44^{ o } C =229.2 K\) , 90% Theoretical Air

Products: \(T _{3}=1000 K , CO _{2}, CO , H _{2} O , H _{2}, N _{2}\)

Theoretical Air: \(C _{3} H _{8}+5 O _{2}+18.8 N _{2} \Rightarrow 3 CO _{2}+4 H _{2} O +18.8 N _{2}\)

90% Theoretical Air:

\(C _{3} H _{8}+4.5 O _{2}+16.92 N _{2} \Rightarrow a CO _{2}+ b CO + c H _{2} O + d H _{2}+16.92 N _{2}\)

Carbon: a + b = 3 ; Hydrogen: c + d = 4

Oxygen: 2a + b + c = 9 Where: 2 ≤ a ≤ 3

\(\begin{array}{cllll}\text { Reaction: } & CO &+& H _{2} O & \leftrightarrow& CO _{2} & +& H _{2} \\\text { Initial: } & b && c && a && d \\\text { Change: } & – x && – x && x & &x \\\text { Equil: } & b – x & &c – x && a + x && d + x\end{array}\)

Chose an Initial guess such as: a = 2, b = 1, c = 4, d = 0

Note: A different initial choice of constants will produce a different value for x, but will result in the same number of moles for each product.

\(n _{ CO 2}=2+ x , \quad n _{ CO }=1- x , \quad n _{ H 2 O }=4- x , n _{ H 2}= x , \quad n _{ N 2}=16.92\)

The reaction can be broken down into two known reactions to find K

(1) \(2 CO _{2} \leftrightarrow 2 CO + O _{2} \quad @ 1000 K \quad \ln \left( K _{1}\right)=-47.052\)

(2) \(2 H _{2} O \leftrightarrow 2 H _{2}+ O _{2} \quad @ 1000 K \quad \ln \left( K _{2}\right)=-46.321\)

For the overall reaction: \(\ln K =\left(\ln \left( K _{2}\right)-\ln \left( K _{1}\right)\right) / 2=0.3655 ; K =1.4412\)

\(K =\frac{ y _{ CO 2} y _{ H 2}}{ y _{ CO } y _{ H 2 O }}\left(\frac{ P }{ P _{ o }}\right)^{1+1-1-1}=\frac{ y _{ CO 2} y _{ H 2}}{ y _{ CO } y _{ H 2 O }}=1.4412=\frac{(2+ x ) x }{(1-4)(4- x )}\)

=> x = 0.6462

\(\begin{array}{ll}n _{ CO 2}= 2 . 6 4 6 2 & n _{ CO }=0.3538 \quad n _{ N 2}=16.92 \\n _{ H2O }=3.3538 & n _{ H 2}=0.6462 &\end{array}\)

 

\(y _{ CO 2}=0.111, \quad y _{ CO }=0.015, y _{ N 2}=0.707, y _{ H 2O}=0.140, y _{ H 2}=0.027\)
