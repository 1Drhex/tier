Guest Question.

An Otto cycle engine develops 45 kW at 180 rpm with 90 explosions per minute. The change of speed from the commencement to the end of power stroke must not exceed 0.5% of mean on either side. Find the mean diameter of the flywheel and rim cross-section having width four times the thickness so that the hoop stress does not exceed 3.5 MPa. Assume that the flywheel stores 6% more energy than the energy stored by the rim and the work done during power stroke is 1.4 times the work done during the cycle. Take density of rim material to be 7300 kg/m³.

Admin
Solution

\( \omega=\frac{2 \pi \times 180}{60}=18.85 rad / s \).

Power developed, \( P=T_{m} \omega \).

or \( T_{m}=\frac{45000}{18.85}=2387.3 Nm \).

Since the number of explosions are half of the rpm, therefore, it is a four-stroke engine. The turning moment diagram is shown in Fig.10.34

\( \text { Work done per cycle }=T_{m} \theta=2387.3 \times 4 \pi=30000 N m\).

\( \text { Work done during the power stroke }=1.4 \times 30000=42000 Nm \).

Triangle ABC shows the work done during the power stroke.
Work done during the working stroke=area ABC=0.5 · AC · BF

\( 42000=0.5 T_{\max } \times \pi \).

or \( T_{\max }=26738 Nm \).

Excess torque, \( T_{\text {excess }}=B G=B F-G F=T_{\max }-T_{m} \).

= 26738 – 2387.3=24350.7 N m.

From similar triangles ABC and BDE, we have

\( \frac{D E}{A C}=\frac{B G}{B F} \).

or \( D E=\left(\frac{B G}{B F}\right) \cdot A C=\frac{T_{\text {excess }}}{T_{\max }} \times \pi \).

\( =\left(\frac{24350.7}{26738}\right) \pi=0.9107 \pi \).

Maximum fluctuation of energy,

\( E_{f}=\operatorname{area} B D E=0.5 D E \cdot B G \).

\( =0.5 \times 0.9107 \pi \times 24350.7 \).

=34834 N m.

Hoop stress in flywheel rim \( =\rho v^{2} \).

\( 3.5 \times 10^{6}=7300 v^{2} \).

or \( v=21.896 m / s \).

Fluctuation of speed=1%

\( \text { Coefficient of fluctuation of speed, } K_{s}=0.01 \).

Now \( E_{f}=2 E K_{s} \).

\( 34834=2 \times 0.01 \times E \).

\( E=1741.7 \times 10^{3} N m \).

Energy stored by the flywheel \( =1.06 E=1846.2 \times 10^{3} N m \).

\( =0.5\left(\frac{W}{g}\right) v^{2} \).

\( =0.5 \times W \times \frac{(21.896)^{2}}{9.81} \).

or \( W=75552 N =\pi d b t \rho g \).

\( =\pi \times 2.32 \times 4 t^{2} \times 7300 \times 9.81 \).

or t=190 mm
b=760 mm.
