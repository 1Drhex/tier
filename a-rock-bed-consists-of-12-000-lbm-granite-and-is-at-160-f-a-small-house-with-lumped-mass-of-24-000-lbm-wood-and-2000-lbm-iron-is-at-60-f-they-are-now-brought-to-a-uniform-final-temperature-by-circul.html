Guest Question.

A rock bed consists of 12 000 lbm granite and is at 160 F. A small house with lumped mass of 24 000 lbm wood and 2000 lbm iron is at 60 F. They are now brought to a uniform final temperature by circulating water between the rock bed and the house. Find the final temperature and the irreversibility in the process assuming an ambient at 60 F.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Total Rockbed and house. No work, no Q irreversible process.

Energy eq.: \(m _{\text {rock }}\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ m _{\text {wood }}\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)+ m _{ Fe }\left( u _{2}- u _{1}\right)=0\)

Entropy Eq.: \(m _{\text {rock }}\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)+ m _{\text {wood }}\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)+ m _{ Fe }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)= S _{ gen }\)

\(( mC )_{ rock } \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}}+( mC )_{\text {wood }} \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}}+( mC )_{ Fe } \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}}= S _{ gen }\)

 

Energy eq.: \(( mC )_{\text {rock }}\left( T _{2}-160\right)+\left( m C _{\text {wood }}+ mC _{ Fe }\right)\left( T _{2}-60\right)=0\)

\(\begin{gathered}12000 \times 0.212\left( T _{2}-160\right)+(24000 \times 0.33+2000 \times 0.11)\left( T _{2}-60\right)=0 \\2544\left( T _{2}-160\right)+(7920+220)\left( T _{2}-60\right)=0 \\T _{2}= 8 3 . 8 F = 5 4 3 . 5 R\end{gathered}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}S _{ gen }=& \sum m _{ i }\left( s _{2}- s _{1}\right)_{ i }=( mC )_{\text {rock }} \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}}+( mC )_{\text {wood }} \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}}+( mC )_{ Fe } \ln \frac{ T _{2}}{ T _{1}} \\&=2544 \ln \frac{543.5}{619.67}+(7920+220) \ln \frac{543.5}{519.67}=35.26 Btu / R\end{aligned}\)

 

\({ }_{1} I _{2}= T _{0} S _{ gen }=519.67 \times 35.26= 1 8 3 2 4 \text { Btu }\)
