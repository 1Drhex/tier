Guest Question.

Two streams of moist air, one having flow rate of 3 kg/s at 30ºC and 30% relative humidity, other having flow rate of 2 kg/s at 35ºC and 85% relative humidity get mixed adiabatically. Determine specific humidity and partial pressure of water vapour after mixing. Take C\(_{\text{p, stream}}\) = 1.86 kJ/kg · K

Admin
Solution

Let temperature after mixing be TºC. For getting final enthalpy after adiabatic mixing the enthalpy of two streams are required.
For moist air stream at 30ºC and 30% relative humidity.\(\\\begin{aligned}\phi_{1}&= \frac{p_{v1}}{p_{\text{vsat at 30ºC}}}\\p_{\text{v sat at 30ºC}}&= 0.04246 bar, so, p_{v1} = 0.3 × 0.04246 = 0.01274 bar\\\text{Corresponding to vapour pressure}&\text{ of 0.01274 bar the dew point temperature shall be 10.5ºC.}\\\text{Specific humidity, }ω_{1}&= \frac{0.622 × p_{v1}}{(p – p_{v1})} = \frac{0.622 × 0.01274}{(1.013 – 0.01274)}\\ω_{1}&= \text{0.007922 kg/kg of air}\\\text{At dew point temperature of 10.5º}&\text{C, enthalpy, }h_{\text{g at 10.5ºC}} = 2520.7 kJ/kg.\\h_{1}&= C_{p, air} × T_{1} + ω_{1}\left\{h_{\text{g at 10.5ºC}} – C_{\text{p, stream}} × (T_{1} – T_{dp1})\right\} \\&= (1.005 × 30) + 0.007922 \left\{2520.7 – 1.86 × (30 – 10.5)\right\} \\h_{1}&= \text{49.83 kJ/kg of dry air}\\\text{For second moist air stream at 35º}&\text{C and 85}\%\text{ relative humidity.}\\\phi_{2}&= 0.85 = \frac{p_{v2}}{p_{\text{vsat at 35ºC}}} = \frac{p_{v2}}{0.005628}\\\text{or, }\qquad\qquad\qquad \qquad\qquad\qquad p_{v2}&= 0.004784 bar\\\text{Specific humidity }ω_{2}&= \frac{0.622 × p_{v2}}{(p – p_{v2})} = \frac{0.622 × 0.004784}{(1.013 – 0.004784)}\\ω_{2}&= \text{0.002951 kg/kg of air}\\\text{Corresponding to vapour pressure}&\text{ of 0.004784 bar the dew point temperature is 32ºC.}\\\text{So, enthalpy of second stream, }\quad&\\h_{2}&= C_{p, air} · T_{2} + ω_{2} \left\{h_{\text{g at 32ºC}} + C_{\text{p, stream}} × (T_{2} – T_{dp2})\right\}\\&= 1.005 × 35 + 0.002951 \left\{2559.9 + 1.86 × (35 – 32)\right\} \\h_{2}&= \text{42.75 kJ/kg of dry air}\\\text{Enthalpy of mixture after adiabat}&\text{ic mixing}\\&= \frac{1}{(m_{1} + m_{2})} × \left\{\frac{h_{1} × m_{1}}{(1 + ω_{1})} + \frac{h_{2} × m_{2}}{(1 + ω_{2})} \right\} \\&= \frac{1}{5} × \left\{\left\lgroup\frac{49.83 × 3}{1.007922} \right\rgroup + \left\lgroup\frac{42.75 × 2}{1.002951} \right\rgroup\right\} \\&= \text{46.71 kJ/kg of moist air}\\\text{Mass of vapour per kg of moist air}&= \frac{1}{5} × \left\{\frac{ω_{1} × m_{1}}{(1 + ω_{1})} + \frac{ω_{2} × m_{2}}{(1 + ω_{2})} \right\} \\&= \frac{1}{5} × \left\{\left\lgroup\frac{0.007922 × 3}{1.007922} \right\rgroup + \left\lgroup\frac{0.002951 × 2}{1.002951} \right\rgroup\right\}\\&= \text{0.005893 kg per kg of moist air}\\\text{Specific humidity of mixture}&= \frac{0.005893}{1 − 0.005893}\\&= \text{0.00593 kg/kg of dry air}\\ω&= \frac{0.622 × p_{v}}{p – p_{v}}\\0.00593&= \frac{0.622 × p_{v}}{1.013 – p_{v}}\\\text{Partial pressure of water vapour}&= 0.00957 bar\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Specific humidity of mixture}&=\textbf{0.00593 kg/kg}\text{ dry air}\\\text{Partial pressure of water vapour in mixture}&= \textbf{0.00957 bar}\end{aligned}\)
