Guest Question.

Determine the voltage transfer characteristic for the NMOS gate of Figure 8.6.

Admin
Solution

The voltage transfer characteristic may be determined point by point by equating the drain currents of the transistors and solving for the output voltage. This requires knowledge of the operating modes for the transistors at each point, as can be determined with the aid of the rules summarized in Table 8.2. For \(V_{IN} ≤ 0.5\) V,\( M_{NO}\) is cut off and MNL is linear, so

Cutoff \(V_{GS} ≤ V_{T}\)
Saturation \(V_T ≤ V_{GS} ≤ (V_{DS} + V_{T})\)
Linear \([V_{GS} ≥ V_T]\) and \([V_{GS} ≥ (V_{DS} + V_T)]\)

\(V_{OUT}=5V (V_{IN}\leq 0.5V)\)

If (\(V_{OUT} + 0.5 V) ≤ V_{IN} ≤ 0.5\) V and \(V_{OUT} ≥ 4.7 V, M_{NO}\) is saturated and \(M_{NL}\) is linear, so

\(I_{DD}=\frac{K_O}{2}(V_{IN}-V_{TO})^2\)

and

\(V_{OUT}=V_{DD}-V_{DSL}=V_{DD}-\left[-V_{TL}-\sqrt{(-V_{TL})^2-\frac{K_O(V_{IN}-V_{TO})^2}{K_{L}}} \right] =4.7V +\sqrt{(0.3V)^2-(1.25)(V_{IN}-0.5V)^2}\)

 

\(\left[\left(V_{OUT}+0.5\right)\leq V_{IN}\leq 0.5 V \right]\) and \(\left[V_{OUT}\geq 4.7 V\right]\)

Then, if \(M_{NO}\) is linear and \(M_{NL}\) is saturated so that

\(I_{DD}=\frac{K_{L}}{2}(-V_{TL})^{2}\)

and

\(V_{OUT}=V_{DSO}=(V_{IN}-V_{TO})-\sqrt{(V_{IN}-V_{TO})^2-\frac{K_{L}(-V_{TL})^2}{K_O}} =V_{{IN}}-0.5V-\sqrt{(V_{IN}-0.5V)^2-(-0.3)^2/1.25};\)

\(\left[\left(V_{OUT}+0.5\right)\geq V_{IN}\right]\) and \(\left[V_{OUT}\leq 4.7 V\right]\)
