\(A = 1.414 \left(0.375\right) \left(2\right) = 1.061 \ in^2\)

 

For uniform shear stress on the throat \(k_b= 1\).

 

From Eq. (6–26), p. 290,

 

\(k_c=\begin{cases} 1 & bending \\ 0.850 &axial \\ 0.59&torsion\end{cases} \)

 

\(k_c= 0.59\). From Eqs. (6–8), p. 282

 

 

\(k_c=\begin{cases} 0.5S_{ut} & S_{ut} \leq 200 \ kpsi \left(1400 MPa \right) \\ 100 \ kpsi & S_{ut} \gt 200 \ kpsi \\ 700 \ MPa &S_{ut} \gt 1400 \ MPa \end{cases}\)

and (6–18), p. 287,

 

\(S_e= k_a k_b k_c k_d k_e k_f S`_e\)

 

where \( k_a\)= surface condition modification factor

\( k_b\) = size modification factor

\(k_c\)= load modification factor

\( k_ d\)= temperature modification factor

\( k_e\)= reliability \(factor^{13}\)

\( k_f=\) miscellaneous-effects modification factor

\(S`_e=\) rotary-beam test specimen endurance limit

\(S_e\)= endurance limit at the critical location of a machine part in the geometr and condition of use

 

, and (6–18), p. 287,

 

\(S_{se}=0.702\left(1\right) 0.59\left(1\right) \left(1\right) \left(1\right) 0.5\left(58\right) = 12.0 \ kpsi \)

\(\tau'_a=\tau'_m=\frac{K_{fs} F_a}{A}=\frac{2 \left(1000\right)}{1.061}= 1885 \ psi \)

\(S_{su}=0.67S_{ut}\).
