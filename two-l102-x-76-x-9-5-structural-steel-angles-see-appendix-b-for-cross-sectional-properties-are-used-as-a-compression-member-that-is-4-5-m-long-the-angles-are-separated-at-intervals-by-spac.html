Guest Question.

Two L102 × 76 × 9.5 structural steel angles (see Appendix B for cross-sectional properties) are used as a compression member that is 4.5-m long. The angles are separated at intervals by spacer blocks and connected by bolts (as shown in Figure P16.9), which ensure that the double-angle shape acts as a unified structural member. Assume pinned connections at each end of the column and use E = 200 GPa for the steel. Determine the Euler buckling load for the double-angle column if the spacer block thickness is (a) 5 mm or (b) 20 mm.

Admin
Solution

The following section properties for a standard steel L102 × 76 × 9.5 shape are given in Appendix B:

\(A=1,600 mm ^{2}, I_{x}=1.64 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}, I_{y}=0.787 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}, x=19.7 mm\)

(a) 5-mm spacer block thickness:
Consider buckling about horizontal cross-sectional axis:

\(\begin{aligned}&I=2\left(1.64 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\right)=3.28 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4} \\&P_{c r}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I}{L^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(3.28 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\right)}{(4,500 mm )^{2}}=319,726 N (a)\end{aligned}\)

Consider buckling about vertical cross-sectional axis:

\(\begin{aligned}&I=2\left[0.787 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}+(5 mm / 2+19.7 mm )^{2}\left(1,600 mm ^{2}\right)\right]=3.151 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4} \\&P_{c r}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I}{L^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(3.151 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\right)}{(4,500 mm )^{2}}=307,160 N\end{aligned}\)

Euler buckling load for 5-mm spacer blocks:

\(P_{c r}=307 kN\)

 

(b) 20-mm spacer block thickness:
Consider buckling about horizontal cross-sectional axis:

\(P_{c r}=319,726 N\) calculated previously in Eq. (a)

Consider buckling about vertical cross-sectional axis:

\(\begin{aligned}&I=2\left[0.787 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}+(20 mm / 2+19.7 mm )^{2}\left(1,600 mm ^{2}\right)\right]=4.3967 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4} \\&P_{c r}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I}{L^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(4.3967 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\right)}{(4,500 mm )^{2}}=428,578 N\end{aligned}\)

Euler buckling load for 20-mm spacer blocks:

\(P_{c r}=320 kN\)
