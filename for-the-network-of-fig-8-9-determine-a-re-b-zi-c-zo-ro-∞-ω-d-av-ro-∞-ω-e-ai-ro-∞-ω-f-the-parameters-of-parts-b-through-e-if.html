Guest Question.

For the network of Fig. 8.9, determine:
(a) \(r_{e}\).
(b) \(Z_{i}\)
(c) \(Z_{o} (r_{o} = ∞ Ω)\).
(d) \(A_{v} (r_{o} = ∞ Ω)\).
(e) \(A_{i} (r_{o} = ∞ Ω)\).
(f) The parameters of parts (b) through (e) if \(r_{o} = 1/h_{oe} = 50 k \Omega\) and compare results.

Admin
Solution

(a) DC: Testing \(\beta R_{E} \gt 10 R_{2}\)

 

\((90)(1.5 k \Omega) \gt 10 (8.2 k \Omega)\)

 

\(135 k \Omega \gt 82 k \Omega\) (satisfied)

Using the approximate approach,

\(V_{B} = \frac{R_{2}}{R_{1} + R_{2}}V_{CC} = \frac{(8.2 k \Omega)(22 V)}{56 k \Omega + 8.2 k \Omega} = 2.81 V\)

 

\(V_{E} = V_{B} – V_{BE} = 2.81 V – 0.7 V = 2.11 V\)

 

\(I_{E} = \frac{V_{E}}{R_{E}} = \frac{2.11 V}{1.5 k \Omega} = 1.41 m A\)

 

\(r_{e} = \frac{26 m V}{I_{E}} = \frac{26 m V}{1.41 m A} = 18 .44 \Omega\)

(b) \(R^{′} = R_{1}\parallel R_{2} = (56 k \Omega)\parallel (8.2 k \Omega) = 7.15 k \Omega \)

 

\(Z_{i} = R^{′} \parallel \beta r_{e} = 7.15 k \Omega \parallel (90)(18.44 \Omega) = 7.15 k \Omega \parallel 1.66 k \Omega = 1.35 k \Omega\)

(c) \(Z_{o} = R_{C} = 6.8 k \Omega\)

(d) \(A_{v} = – \frac{R_{C}}{r_{e}} = – \frac{6.8 k \Omega}{18.44 \Omega} = – 368.76\)

(e) The condition \(R^{′} \geq \beta r_{e} (7.15 k \Omega \geq 10(1.66 k \Omega) = 16.6 k \Omega\) is not satisfied. Therefore,

\(A_{i} \cong \frac{\beta R^{′}}{R^{′} + \beta r_{e}} = \frac{(90)(7.15 k \Omega)}{7.15 k \Omega + 1.66 k \Omega} = 73 .04\)

(f) \(Z_{i} = 1.35 k \Omega\)

 

\(Z_{o} = R_{C} \parallel r_{o} = 6.8 k \Omega \parallel 50 k \Omega = 5.98 k \Omega vs. 6.8 k \Omega\)

 

\(A_{v} = – \frac{R_{C} \parallel r_{o}}{r_{e}} = – \frac{5.98 k \Omega}{18.44 \Omega} = – 324.3 vs. – 368.76\)

The condition

\(r_{o} \geq 10 R_{C} (50 k \Omega \geq 10(6.8 k \Omega) = 68 k \Omega)\)

is not satisfied. Therefore,

\(A_{i} = \frac{\beta R^{′}r_{o}}{(r_{o} + R_{C})(R^{′} + \beta r_{e})} = \frac{(90)(7.15 k \Omega)(50 k \Omega)}{(50 k \Omega + 6.8 k \Omega)(7.15 k \Omega + 1.66 k \Omega)} = 64.4 vs. 73.04\)

There was a measurable difference in the results for \(Z_{o}\), \(A_{v}\), and \(A_{i}\) because the condition \(r_{o} \geq 10 R_{C}\) was not satisfied.
