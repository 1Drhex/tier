Guest Question.

A short rectangular post supports compressive loads of P = 34 kN and Q = 18 kN as shown in Figure P15.36a. A top view of the post showing the locations where loads P and Q are applied to the top of the post is shown in Figure P15.36b. Determine the normal stresses at corners A, B, C, and D of the post.

Admin
Solution

Section properties:

\(\begin{aligned}&A=(140 mm )(180 mm )=25,200 mm ^{2} \\&I_{y}=\frac{(180 mm )(140 mm )^{3}}{12}=41.16 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4} \\&I_{z}=\frac{(140 mm )(180 mm )^{3}}{12}=68.04 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\end{aligned}\)

Equivalent forces at base of post:

\(\begin{aligned}&F=34 kN +18 kN =52 kN =52,000 N \\&M_{y}=(34 kN )(45 mm )=1,530 kN – mm =1.53 \times 10^{6} N – mm \\&M_{z}=(34 kN )(70 mm )-(18 kN )(55 mm )=1,390 kN – mm =1.39 \times 10^{6} N – mm\end{aligned}\)

Axial stress due to F:

\(\sigma_{\text {axial }}=\frac{52,000 N }{25,200 mm ^{2}}=2.063 MPa ( C )\)

Bending stress due to \(M _{ y }\):

\(\sigma_{\text {bend }}=\frac{M_{y} z}{I_{y}}=\frac{\left(1.53 \times 10^{6} N – mm \right)(\pm 70 mm )}{41.16 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}}=\pm 2.602 MPa\)

Bending stress due to \(M _{ z }\):

\(\sigma_{\text {bend } z}=\frac{M_{z} y}{I_{z}}=\frac{\left(1.39 \times 10^{6} N – mm \right)(\pm 90 mm )}{68.04 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}}=\pm 1.839 MPa\)

 

Normal stresses at corners A, B, C, and D: The normal stresses acting at each of the four corners of the post can be determined by superimposing the results obtained above. In all instances, the normal stresses act in the vertical direction; that is, the y direction. The sense of the stress, either tension or compression, can be determined by inspection.

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Corner A:

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{A} &=2.063 MPa ( C )+2.602 MPa ( T )+1.839 MPa ( T ) \\&=-2.063 MPa +2.602 MPa +1.839 MPa \\&=2.377 MPa =2.38 MPa ( T )\end{aligned}\)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Corner B:

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{B} &=2.063 MPa ( C )+2.602 MPa ( C )+1.839 MPa ( T ) \\&=-2.063 MPa -2.602 MPa +1.839 MPa \\&=-2.827 MPa =2.83 MPa ( C )\end{aligned}\)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Corner C:

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{C} &=2.063 MPa ( C )+2.602 MPa ( C )+1.839 MPa ( C ) \\&=-2.063 MPa -2.602 MPa -1.839 MPa \\&=-6.504 MPa =6.50 MPa ( C )\end{aligned}\)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Corner D:

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{D} &=2.063 MPa ( C )+2.602 MPa ( T )+1.839 MPa ( C ) \\&=-2.063 MPa +2.602 MPa -1.839 MPa \\&=-1.300 MPa =1.300 MPa ( C )\end{aligned}\)
