Guest Question.

(a) Sandwich each of the six commutation relations in Equations 6.52–6.54 between \( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\right| \text { and }|n \ell m\rangle \) to obtain relations between matrix elements of \( \hat{ V } \) . As an example, Equation 6.52 with the upper signs gives

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{\pm}\right]=0 \) (6.52).

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{\mp}\right]=\pm 2 \hbar \hat{V}_{z} \) (6.54).

\( B_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left(n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime}\left(m^{\prime}-1\right)\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=A_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell(m+1)\right\rangle \).

(b) Using the results in , show that the six expressions you wrote down in part (a) are satisfied by Equations 6.59–6.61.

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-\sqrt{2} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.59).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left(n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right) \). (6.61).

Solution

(a) i. From Equation 6.52 (upper signs),

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{\pm}\right]=0 \) (6.52).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|L_{+} V_{+}-V_{+} L_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=0 \).

\( B_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}-1\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=A_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m+1\right\rangle \).

ii. From Equation 6.52 (lower signs),

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{\pm}\right]=0 \) (6.52).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|L_{-} V_{-}-V_{-} L_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=0 \).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}+1\left|V_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=B_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m-1\right\rangle \).

iii. From Equation 6.53 (upper signs),

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{z}\right]=\mp \hbar \hat{V}_{\pm} \) (6.53).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|L_{+} V_{z}-V_{z} L_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-\hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

\( B_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}-1\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=A_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m+1\right\rangle-\hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

iv. From Equation 6.53 (lower signs),

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{z}\right]=\mp \hbar \hat{V}_{\pm} \) (6.53).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|L_{-} V_{z}-V_{z} L_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=\hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}+1\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=B_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m-1\right\rangle+\hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

v. From Equation 6.54 (upper signs),

\( \left[\hat{L}_{\pm}, \hat{V}_{\mp}\right]=\pm 2 \hbar \hat{V}_{z} \) (6.54).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|L_{-} V_{+}-V_{+} L_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-2 \hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}+1\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=B_{\ell}^{m}\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{+}\right| n \ell m-1\right\rangle-2 \hbar\left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|V_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle \).

(b) i. Plugging in Equation 6.59 to the result from a.i,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-\sqrt{2} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.59).

\( B_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}-1}^{\ell} \stackrel{?}{=} A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+A_{1}^{1} C_{m 2 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \stackrel{1 \ell^{\prime}}{=} A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

\( \text { Since } A_{1}^{1}=0 \), the equation is satisfied.

ii. Plugging in Equation 6.60 to the result from a.ii,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=\sqrt{2} C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.60).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}} C_{m-1 m^{\prime}+1}^{\ell} \stackrel{?}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-1-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-1-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+B_{1}^{-} 1 C_{m-2 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \stackrel{\ell^{\prime}}{=}^{\ell}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-1-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \)

\( \text { Since } B_{1}^{-1}=0 \), the equation is satisfied.

iii. Plugging in Equation 6.59 and 6.61 to the result from a.iii,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-\sqrt{2} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.59).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.61).

\( B_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}} C_{m 0 m^{\prime}-1}^{\ell} \stackrel{?}{=} A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+A_{1}^{0} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}} \stackrel{?}{=} A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

\( \text { Since } A_{1}^{0}=\sqrt{2} \hbar \), the equation is satisfied.

iv. Plugging in Equation 6.60 and 6.61 to the result from a.iv,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=\sqrt{2} C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.60).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right) \) (6.61).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}} C_{m 0 m^{\prime}+1}^{\ell} \stackrel{?}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} 1 \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}+B_{1}^{0} C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}} \stackrel{1 \ell^{\prime}}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-10 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

\( \text { Since } B_{1}^{0}=\sqrt{2} \hbar \text {, } \) the equation is satisfied.

v. Plugging in Equation 6.60 and 6.61 to the result from a.v,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{-}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=\sqrt{2} C_{m-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.60).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right) \) (6.61).

\( B^{m^\prime }_{\ell ^\prime } C^{\ell 1 \ell ^\prime }_{m-1 m\prime -1} \overset{?}{=} A^{m}_{\ell } C^{\ell }_{m+1-1m^\prime } +\sqrt{2} \hbar C^{\ell 1\ell ^\prime }_{m0m^\prime } \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+1-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+A_{1}^{-1} C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell^{\prime} \ell^{\prime}} \stackrel{?}{=} A_{\ell}^{m} C_{m+1-1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell} \).

\( \text { Since } A_{1}^{-1}=\sqrt{2} \hbar \), the equation is satisfied.

vi. Finally, plugging in Equations 6.59 and 6.61 to the result from a.vi,

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{+}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=-\sqrt{2} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.59).

\( \left\langle n^{\prime} \ell^{\prime} m^{\prime}\left|\hat{V}_{z}\right| n \ell m\right\rangle=C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}}\left\langle n \ell^{\prime}\|V\| n \ell\right\rangle \) (6.61).

\( A_{\ell^{\prime}}^{m^{\prime}} C_{m 1 m^{\prime}+1}^{\ell} \stackrel{?}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}} \).

and applying Equation 6.66,

\( A_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M+1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=B_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m_{1}-1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+B_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}-1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \),

\( B_{J}^{M} C_{m_{1} m_{2} M-1}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}=A_{j_{1}}^{m_{1}} C_{m+1 m_{2} M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J}+A_{j_{2}}^{m_{2}} C_{m_{1} m_{2}+1 M}^{j_{1} j_{2} J} \). (6.66).

\( B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+B_{1}^{1} C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}} \stackrel{?}{=} B_{\ell}^{m} C_{m-11 m^{\prime}}^{\ell}+\sqrt{2} \hbar C_{m 0 m^{\prime}}^{\ell 1 \ell^{\prime}} \).

\( \text { Since } B_{1}^{1}=\sqrt{2} \hbar \), the equation is satisfied.
