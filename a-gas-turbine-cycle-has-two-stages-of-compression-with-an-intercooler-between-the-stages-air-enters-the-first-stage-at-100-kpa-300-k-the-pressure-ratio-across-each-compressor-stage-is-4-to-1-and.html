Guest Question.

A gas turbine cycle has two stages of compression, with an intercooler between the stages. Air enters the first stage at 100 kPa, 300 K. The pressure ratio across each compressor stage is 4 to 1, and each stage has an isentropic efficiency of 82%. Air exits the intercooler at 330 K. Calculate the temperature at the exit of each compressor stage and the total specific work required.

Admin
Solution

State 1: \(P _{1}=100 kPa , T _{1}=300 K\)

State 3:

\(\begin{aligned}& T _{3}=330 K \\& P _{2}=4 P _{1}=400 kPa ; \quad P _{4}=4 P _{3}=1600 kPa\end{aligned}\)

Energy Eq.: \(w _{ c 1}+ h _{1}= h _{2} \Rightarrow w _{ c1 }= h _{2}- h _{1} \approx C _{ P }\left( T _{2}- T _{1}\right)\)

Ideal C1 constant s, Eq.6.23: \(T _{2 s }= T _{1}\left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)^{( k -1) / k }=445.8 K\)

\(w _{ c 1 s }= h _{2 s }- h _{1} \approx C _{ P }\left( T _{2 s }- T _{1}\right)=146.4 kJ / kg\),

Actual Eq.7.28: \(w _{ c 1}= w _{ c 1 s } / \eta =146.4 / 0.82=178.5 kJ / kg\)

\(T _{2}= T _{1}+ w _{ c1 } / C _{ P }= 4 7 7 . 8 K\)

Ideal C2 constant s, Eq.6.23: \(T _{4 s }= T _{3}\left( P _{4} / P _{3}\right)^{( k -1) / k }=490.4 K\)

\(w _{ c 2 s }= h _{4 s }- h _{3} \approx C _{ P }\left( T _{4 s }- T _{3}\right)=161 kJ / kg\) ;

Actual Eq.7.28: \(w _{ c 2}= w _{ c 2 s } / \eta=196.4 kJ / kg\)

\(T _{4}= T _{3}+ w _{ c 2} / C _{ P }= 5 2 5 . 6 K\)

Total work in:

\(w = w _{ c 1}+ w _{ c 2}=178.5+196.4= 3 7 4 . 9 kJ / kg\)
