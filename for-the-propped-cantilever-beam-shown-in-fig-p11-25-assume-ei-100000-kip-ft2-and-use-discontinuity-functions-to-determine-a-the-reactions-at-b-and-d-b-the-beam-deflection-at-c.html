Guest Question.

For the propped cantilever beam shown in Fig. P11.25, assume EI = 100,000 \(\text { kip-ft }^{2}\) and use discontinuity functions to determine:
(a) the reactions at B and D.
(b) the beam deflection at C.

Admin
Solution

Moment equation:
(a) Support reactions:

\(\begin{gathered}\Sigma F_{y}=B_{y}+D_{y}-\frac{1}{2}(5 kips / ft )(10 ft ) \\-(5 kips / ft )(12 ft )=0 \\\therefore B_{y}+D_{y}=85 kips (a) \\\Sigma M_{D}=\frac{1}{2}(5 kips / ft )(10 ft )\left(20 ft +\frac{10 ft }{3}\right) \\+(5 kips / ft )(12 ft )(14 ft ) \\-B_{y}(20 ft )+M_{D}=0 \\\therefore B_{y}(20 ft )-M_{D}=1,423.3333 kip – ft (b)\end{gathered}\)

 

Discontinuity expressions:

\(\begin{aligned}w(x)=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{10 ft }\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{1}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{10 ft }\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{1}+5 kips / ft \langle x-10 ft \rangle^{0} \\&+B_{y}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{-1}-5 kips / ft \langle x-10 ft \rangle^{0}+5 kips / ft \langle x-22 ft \rangle^{0} \\&-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{-2}+D_{y}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{-1} \\=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{10 ft }\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{1}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{10 ft }\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{1}+5 kips / ft \langle x-22 ft \rangle^{0} \\&-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{-2}+D_{y}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{-1}\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}V(x)=\int w(x) d x=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{2(10 ft )}\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{2}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{2(10 ft )}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{2}+B_{y}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{0} \\&+5 kips / ft \langle x-22 ft \rangle^{1}-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{-1}+D_{y}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{0} \\M(x)=\int V(x) d x=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{6(10 ft )}\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{3}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{6(10 ft )}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{3}+B_{y}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{1} \\&+\frac{5 kips / ft }{2}\langle x-22 ft \rangle^{2}-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{0}+D_{y}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{1} \\E I \frac{d^{2} v}{d x^{2}}=M(x)=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{6(10 ft )}\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{3}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{6(10 ft )}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{3}+B_{y}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{1} \\&+\frac{5 kips / ft }{2}\langle x-22 ft \rangle^{2}-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{0}+D_{y}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{1}\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}E I \frac{d v}{d x}=&-\frac{5 kips / ft }{24(10 ft )}\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{4}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{24(10 ft )}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{4}+\frac{B_{y}}{2}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{2}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{6}\langle x-22 ft \rangle^{3} \\&-M_{D}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{1}+\frac{D_{y}}{2}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{2}+C_{1} (c) \\E I v=-& \frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}\langle x-0 ft \rangle^{5}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{5}+\frac{B_{y}}{6}\langle x-10 ft \rangle^{3}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{24}\langle x-22 ft \rangle^{4} \\-& \frac{M_{D}}{2}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{2}+\frac{D_{y}}{6}\langle x-30 ft \rangle^{3}+C_{1} x+C_{2} (d)\end{aligned}\)

 

Boundary conditions and evaluate constants:

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { at } x=10 ft , v=0 \\&\quad \therefore-\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}(10 ft )^{5}+C_{1}(10 ft )+C_{2}=0 (e)\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { at } x=30 ft , v=0 \\&\qquad \therefore-\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}(30 ft )^{5}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}(20 ft )^{5}+\frac{B_{y}}{6}(20 ft )^{3} \\&\quad+\frac{5 kips / ft }{24}(8 ft )^{4}+C_{1}(30 ft )+C_{2}=0 (f)\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { at } x=30 ft , \frac{d v}{d x}=0 \\&\quad \therefore-\frac{5 kips / ft }{24(10 ft )}(30 ft )^{4}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{24(10 ft )}(20 ft )^{4}+\frac{B_{y}}{2}(20 ft )^{2}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{6}(8 ft )^{3}+C_{1}=0 (g)\end{aligned}\)

 

(a) Solve for \(B _{y}, D _{y}\), and \(M _{ D }\):
Solve equations (a), (b), (e), (f), and (g) simultaneously to obtain:

\(\begin{aligned}C_{1} &=-59.0000 kip – ft ^{2} \\C_{2} &=1,006.6667 kip – ft ^{3} \\B_{y} &=65.8700 kips =65.9 kips \uparrow \\D_{y} &=19.1300 kips =19.13 kips \uparrow \\M_{D} &=-105.9333 kip – ft =105.9 kip – ft ( cw )\end{aligned}\)

(b) Beam deflection at C: From Eq. (d), the beam deflection at C (x = 22 ft) is computed as follows:

\(\begin{aligned}&E I v_{C}=-\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}(22 ft )^{5}+\frac{5 kips / ft }{120(10 ft )}(12 ft )^{5}+\frac{65.8700 kips }{6}(12 ft )^{3} \\&\quad-\left(59.0000 kip – ft ^{2}\right)(22 ft )+1,006.6667 kip – ft ^{3} \\&=-1,757.4400 kip – ft ^{3} \\&\therefore v_{C}=-\frac{1,757.4400 kip – ft ^{3}}{100,000 kip – ft ^{2}}=-0.017574 ft =-0.2109 in .=0.211 in. \downarrow\end{aligned}\)
