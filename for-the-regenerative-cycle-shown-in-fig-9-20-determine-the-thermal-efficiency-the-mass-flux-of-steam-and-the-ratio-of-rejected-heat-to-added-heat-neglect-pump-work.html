Guest Question.

For the regenerative cycle, shown in Fig. 9.20, determine the thermal efficiency, the mass flux of steam, and the ratio of rejected heat to added heat. Neglect pump work.

Admin
Solution
\( \text { Referring to Fig. } 9.7 b \text { to identify the states and using the steam tables, we find } \)

\( h_{2} \cong h_{1}=191.8 kJ / kg \quad h_{6} \cong h_{7}=762.8 kJ / kg \quad h_{3}=3625.3 kJ / kg \).

The enthalpies of states 4 and 5 are determined by assuming an isentropic process as follows:

\( \left.\begin{array}{l} s_{5}=s_{3}=6.904 kJ / kg \cdot K \\ P_{5}=1 MPa \end{array}\right\} \quad \therefore h_{5}=2932 kJ / kg \).

\( s_{4}=s_{3}=6.904=0.6491+7.5019 x_{4} \quad \therefore x_{4}=0.8338 \).

\( \therefore h_{4}=191.8+(0.8338)(2392.8)=2187 kJ / kg \).

An energy balance on the heater, which is assumed insulated, is \( \dot{m}_{5}\left(h_{5}-h_{6}\right)=\dot{m}_{2}\left(h_{7}-h_{2}\right) \). A mass balance provides (see Fig. 9.10) \( \dot{m}_{7}=\dot{m}_{5}+\dot{m}_{2} . \text { Assuming } \dot{m}_{7}=1 kg / s \), the above two equations are combined to give

\( \dot{m}_{2}=\frac{h_{5}-h_{6}}{h_{7}-h_{2}+h_{5}-h_{6}}=\frac{2932-763}{763-192+2932-763}=0.792 kg / s \).

\( \text { We then have } \dot{m}_{5}=1-\dot{m}_{2}=1-0.792=0.208 kg / s \text {. The turbine power (with } \dot{m}_{7}=1 kg / s \text { ) can now be calculated to be } \)

\( \dot{W}_{T}=\dot{m}_{7}\left(h_{3}-h_{5}\right)+\dot{m}_{2}\left(h_{5}-h_{4}\right)=(1.0)(3625-2932)+(0.792)(2932-2187)=1283 kW \).

The boiler heat rate is

\( \dot{Q}_{ B }=\dot{m}_{7}\left(h_{3}-h_{7}\right)=(1.0)(3625-763)=2862 kW \).

The cycle efficiency is calculated to be

\( \eta=\frac{\dot{W}_{T}}{\dot{Q}_{B}}=\frac{1283}{2862}=0.448 \quad \text { or } \quad 44.8 \% \).

The mass flux of steam is found as

\( \dot{m}_{7}=\frac{\dot{W}_{T}}{\left(\dot{W}_{T}\right)_{\text {with } \dot{m}_{7}=1}}=\frac{20}{1.283}=15.59 kg / s \).

The ratio of rejected heat to added heat is

\( \frac{\dot{Q}_{C}}{\dot{Q}_{B}}=\frac{\dot{Q}_{B}-\dot{W}_{T}}{\dot{Q}_{B}}=1-\frac{\dot{W}_{T}}{\dot{Q}_{B}}=1-\frac{1283}{2862}=0.552 \)
