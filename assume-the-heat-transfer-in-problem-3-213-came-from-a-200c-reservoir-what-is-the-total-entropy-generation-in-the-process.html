Energy Eq.: \(U _{2}- U _{1}={ }_{1} Q _{2}-{ }_{1} W _{2}\)

Entropy Eq.: \(m _{2} s _{2}- m _{ A } s _{ A 1}- m _{ B } s _{ B 1}={ }_{1} Q _{2} / T _{ res }+{ }_{1} S _{2 gen} \)

\(u _{ A 1}=535.08+0.1 \times 2002.14=735.22 kJ / kg ;\)

\(V _{ B 1}=( mv )_{ B 1}=0.77465 m ^{3}\)
