Guest Question.

Propyl alcohol flows at a rate of 0.01 \(m^3/s\) through a 2-nominal schedule 40 wrought iron pipe. The pipe is laid out horizontally and is 250 m long. It contains four regular, threaded 90° elbows and a globe valve. Calculate the pressure drop of the propyl alcohol using loss coefficients, and again using the equivalent length

Admin
Solution

From the property tables, we read propyl alcohol \(ρ = 0.802(1 000) kg/m^3\) \(µ = 1.92 \times 10^{-3} N·s/m^2\)
[App. Table B.1]
2-nom sch 40 ID = D = 5.252 cm \(A = 21.66 cm^2\) [App. Table D.1]
wrought iron ε = 0.004 6 cm [Table 3.1]
The continuity equation for incompressible steady flow through the pipe is \(A_1V_1 = A_2V_2\)
Because \(A_1 = A_2\), then \(V_1 = V_2\). The Bernoulli equation applies:
\(\frac{p_1g_c}{ρg} +\frac{V^2_1}{2g} + z_1 = \frac{p_2g_c}{ρg} +\frac{ V^2_2}{2g} + z_2 + \frac{f L}{D_h}\frac{V^2}{2g} + Σ K \frac{V^2}{2g}\)
where points 1 and 2 are L = 250 m apart, \(z_1 = z_2\) for a horizontal pipe, and \(p_1 – p_2\) is sought. The above equation reduces to \(p_1 – p_2 = \frac{f L}{D_h}\frac{ρV^2}{2g_c} + Σ K\frac{ V^2}{2g}\)
The flow velocity is
\(V =\frac{ Q}{A} = \frac{0.01}{21.66 \times 10^{-4}} = 4.62\) m/s
The Reynolds number is calculated to be
\(Re = \frac{ρVD}{µg_c}=\frac{ 0.802(1 000)(4.62)(0.052 52)}{0.53 \times 10^{-3}} = 1.01 \times 10^5\)
The flow is therefore turbulent. Thus,
\(\left. \begin{matrix}Re = 1.01 \times 10 ^ 5\\Also\quad \frac{ε}{D} = \frac{0.004 6}{5.252} = 0.000 88 \end{matrix} \right\}\) f = 0.022 (Figure 3.3)
For 4 elbows and 1 globe valve, we read values from Table 4.3:
K = 4(1.4) + 10 = 15.6
and
\(\frac{ L_{eq}}{D} = 4(30) + 340 = 460\)
Using the K values, the pressure loss is
\(p_1 – p_2 = \left\lgroup\frac{ f L}{D_h} + K \right\rgroup\frac{ ρV_2}{2g_c}=\left\lgroup\frac{ 0.022(250)}{0.052 52} + 15.6\right\rgroup \frac{0.802(1 000)(4.62)^2}{2}\)
\(p_1 – p_2 = 1 022 912 N/m^2 = 1 022\) kPa (Using K )
With the concept of equivalent length, we write
\(p_1 – p_2 = \left\lgroup\frac{f L}{D_h} + \frac{fL_{eq}}{D_h} \right\rgroup\frac{ρV^2}{2g_c}\)
\(p_1 – p_2 = \left\lgroup \frac{ 0.022(250)}{0.052 52} + 0.022(460) \right\rgroup \frac{0.802(1 000)(4.62)^2}{2}\)
\(p_1 – p_2 = 975 594 N/m^2 = 976\) kPa (Using\( L_{eq}/D)\)
