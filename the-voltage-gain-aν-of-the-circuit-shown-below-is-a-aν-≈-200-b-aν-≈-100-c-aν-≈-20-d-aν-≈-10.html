Guest Question.

The voltage gain \(A_{\nu }\) of the circuit shown below is

(a) \(\left|A_{\nu }\right| \approx\) 200 (b) \(\left|A_{\nu }\right| \approx\) 100

(c) \(\left|A_{\nu }\right| \approx\) 20 (d) \(\left|A_{\nu }\right| \approx\) 10

Admin
Solution

KVL in input loop,

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { 13.7 }-\left(I_{C}+I_{B}\right) 12 k-100 k\left(I_{b}\right)-0.7=0 \\&\Rightarrow I_{B}=9.9 mA \\&I_{C}=\beta / B=0.99 mA \\&1_{\varepsilon}=1 mA \\&\therefore r_{e}=\frac{26 mA }{I_{\varepsilon}}=26 \Omega \\&Z_{i}=\beta r_{e}=2.5 k \Omega \\&\therefore A_{V}=\left(\frac{100 k \| 12 k}{26}\right)=412 \\&Z_{i}^{1}=Z_{i} \|\left(\frac{100 k}{1+412}\right) \\&=221 \Omega\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}A_{V S} &=A_{V} \frac{Z \delta^{1}}{Z_{i}^{1}+R_{S}} \\&=412\left(\frac{221}{221+10 k}\right) \\\left|A_{V S}\right| & \approx 10\end{aligned}\)

Hence, the correct option is (d).
