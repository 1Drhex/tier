Solution

a)\( 0.9850×1000 = ρ_2V_2. 80 000 − p_2 = 0.985×1000(V_2 −1000)\).

\(\frac{V_2^2-1000^2}{2}+\frac{1.4}{0.4}\left\lgroup\frac{p_2}{\rho _2}-287\times 283 \right\rgroup =0. \left\lgroup \rho _1 =\frac{80}{0.287\times 283}=0.9850 Kg/m^3 . \right\rgroup \)

\(\frac{V_2^2}{2}-\frac{1000^2}{2} +\frac{1.4}{0.4}\times \frac{V_2}{985}(-985V_2+1065000)-284300=0 \)

∴\(3V_2^2 − 3784V_2 + 784 300 = 0. ∴V_2 = 261 m/s. ρ_2 = \underline{3.774 kg/m^3} \) .

Substitute in and find \( p_2 = \underline{808 kPa abs} \).

\(M_1 =\frac{1000}{\sqrt{1.4\times 287 \times 283} }=\underline{2.966} . T_2 =\frac{808}{0.287\times 3.774}=746 K or \underline{473 °C}\) .

\(M_2 =\frac{261}{\sqrt{1.4\times 287 \times 746} }=\underline{0.477} \).
