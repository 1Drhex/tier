Guest Question.

For the beam and loading shown in Fig. P10.17, use the double-integration method to determine (a) the equation of the elastic curve for the cantilever beam, (b) the deflection at B, (c) the deflection at the free end, and (d) the slope at the free end. Assume that EI is constant for the beam.

Admin
Solution

Beam FBD:

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{A} &=-M_{A}-\frac{w L}{2}\left(\frac{L}{2}+\frac{L}{4}\right)=0 \\& \therefore M_{A}=-\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8} \\\Sigma F_{y} &=A_{y}-\frac{w L}{2}=0 \\& \therefore A_{y}=\frac{w L}{2}\end{aligned}\)

 

Consider beam segment AB (0 ≤ x ≤ L/2)
Moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{a-a} &=M(x)-M_{A}-A_{y} x=M(x)+\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8}-\frac{w L}{2} x=0 \\& \therefore M(x)=-\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8}+\frac{w L x}{2}\end{aligned}\)

 

Integration of moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}&E I \frac{d^{2} v}{d x^{2}}=M(x)=-\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8}+\frac{w L x}{2} \\&E I \frac{d v}{d x}=-\frac{3 w L^{2} x}{8}+\frac{w L x^{2}}{4}+C_{1} (a) \\&E I v=-\frac{3 w L^{2} x^{2}}{16}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{12}+C_{1} x+C_{2} (b)\end{aligned}\)

 

Boundary conditions:

\(\begin{array}{lll}v=0 & \text { at } & x=0 \\\frac{d v}{d x}=0 & \text { at } & x=0\end{array}\)

Evaluate constants:
Substitute x = 0 and v = 0 into Eq. (b) to determine \(C_{2}=0\) . Next, substitute x = 0 and dv/dx = 0 into Eq.(b) to determine \(C_{1}=0\).

Elastic curve equation for beam segment AB:

\(\begin{aligned}E I v &=-\frac{3 w L^{2} x^{2}}{16}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{12} \\& \therefore v=-\frac{w L x^{2}}{48 E I}[9 L-4 x] \quad(0 \leq x \leq L / 2)\end{aligned}\)

Slope at B: Let x = L/2

\(\left.\frac{d v}{d x}\right|_{B}=\theta_{B}=-\frac{3 w L^{2}(L / 2)}{8 E I}+\frac{w L(L / 2)^{2}}{4 E I}=-\frac{w L^{3}}{8 E I}\)

Deflection at B: Let x = L/2

\(v_{B}=-\frac{w L(L / 2)^{2}}{48 E I}\left[9 L-4\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)\right]=-\frac{7 w L^{4}}{192 E I}\)

Consider beam segment BC (L/2 ≤ x ≤ L)
Moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{b-b} &=M(x)-M_{A}-A_{y} x+\frac{w}{2}\left(x-\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2} \\&=M(x)+\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8}-\frac{w L}{2} x+\frac{w}{2}\left(x-\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}=0 \\\therefore M(x) &=-\frac{w}{2}\left(x-\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}+\frac{w L}{2} x-\frac{3 w L^{2}}{8} \\&=-\frac{w x^{2}}{2}+w L x-\frac{w L^{2}}{2}\end{aligned}\)

 

Integration of moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}&E I \frac{d^{2} v}{d x^{2}}=M(x)=-\frac{w x^{2}}{2}+w L x-\frac{w L^{2}}{2} \\&E I \frac{d v}{d x}=-\frac{w x^{3}}{6}+\frac{w L x^{2}}{2}-\frac{w L^{2} x}{2}+C_{3} (c)\\&E I v=-\frac{w x^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{6}-\frac{w L^{2} x^{2}}{4}+C_{3} x+C_{4} (d)\end{aligned}\)

Continuity conditions:

\(\begin{array}{ll}v=-\frac{7 w L^{4}}{192 E I} \quad \text { at } & x=\frac{L}{2} \\\frac{d v}{d x}=-\frac{w L^{3}}{8 E I} \quad \text { at } & x=\frac{L}{2}\end{array}\)

Evaluate constants:
Substitute the slope continuity condition into Eq. (c) for x = L/2 and solve for \(C_{3}\):

\(\begin{aligned}E I \frac{d v}{d x} &=-\frac{w(L / 2)^{3}}{6}+\frac{w L(L / 2)^{2}}{2}-\frac{w L^{2}(L / 2)}{2}+C_{3}=-\frac{w L^{3}}{8} \\& \therefore C_{3}=\frac{w L^{3}}{48}\end{aligned}\)

Next, substitute the deflection continuity condition into Eq. (d) for x = L/2 and solve for \(C_{4}\)

\(\begin{aligned}E I v &=-\frac{w(L / 2)^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L(L / 2)^{3}}{6}-\frac{w L^{2}(L / 2)^{2}}{4}+\frac{w L^{3}}{48}(L / 2)+C_{4}=-\frac{7 w L^{4}}{192} \\& \therefore C_{4}=-\frac{w L^{4}}{384}\end{aligned}\)

Elastic curve equation for beam segment BC:

\(\begin{aligned}E I v &=-\frac{w x^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{6}-\frac{w L^{2} x^{2}}{4}-\frac{w L^{3} x}{48}-\frac{w L^{4}}{384} \\& \therefore v=-\frac{w}{384 E I}\left[16 x^{4}-64 L x^{3}+96 L^{2} x^{2}-8 L^{3} x+L^{4}\right] \quad(L / 2 \leq x \leq L)\end{aligned}\)

(a) Elastic curve equations for entire beam:

\(\begin{aligned}&v=-\frac{w L x^{2}}{48 E I}[9 L-4 x] \quad(0 \leq x \leq L / 2) \\&v=-\frac{w}{384 E I}\left[16 x^{4}-64 L x^{3}+96 L^{2} x^{2}-8 L^{3} x+L^{4}\right] \quad(L / 2 \leq x \leq L)\end{aligned}\)

(b) Deflection at B:

\(v_{B}=-\frac{7 w L^{4}}{192 E I}\)

(c) Deflection at free end of cantilever:

\(v_{C}=-\frac{w}{384 E I}\left[16(L)^{4}-64 L(L)^{3}+96 L^{2}(L)^{2}-8 L^{3}(L)+L^{4}\right]=-\frac{41 w L^{4}}{384 E I}\)

(d) Slope at free end of cantilever:

\(\begin{aligned}E I \frac{d v}{d x}=&-\frac{8 w(L)^{3}}{48}+\frac{24 w L(L)^{2}}{48}-\frac{24 w L^{2}(L)}{48}+\frac{w L^{3}}{48}=-\frac{7 w L^{3}}{48} \\&\left.\therefore \frac{d v}{d x}\right|_{C}=\theta_{C}=-\frac{7 w L^{3}}{48 E I}\end{aligned}\)
