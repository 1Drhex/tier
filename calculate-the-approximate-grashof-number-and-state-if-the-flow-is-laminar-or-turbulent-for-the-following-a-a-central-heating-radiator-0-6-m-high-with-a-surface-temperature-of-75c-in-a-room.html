Guest Question.

Calculate the approximate Grashof number and state if the flow is laminar or turbulent for the following : (a) A central heating radiator, 0.6 m high with a surface temperature of 75°C in a room at 18°C, \((ρ = 1.2 kg/m^3, Pr = 0.72, and µ = 1.8 × 10^{–5} kg/ms).\) (b) A horizontal oil sump with a surface temperature of 40°C, 0.4 m long and 0.2 m wide containing oil at 75°C. Take \(ρ = 854 kg/m^3, Pr = 546, β = 0.7 × 10^{–3} K^{–1}\) and \(µ = 3.56 × 10^{–2} kg/ms.\)(c) Air at 20°C \((ρ = 1.2 kg/m^3, Pr = 0.72 and µ = 1.8 × 10^{–5} kg/ms)\) adjacent to a 60 mm dia. horizontal light bulb, with a surface temperature of 90°C.

Admin
Solution

The properties are given, thus the Grashof number for any flow situation can be calculated as
\(Gr = \frac{ g β∆t L_{c}^{3}}{v^2} = \frac{ g ρ^2 β∆t L_{c}^{3}}{µ^2}\)
where \(L_c\) = significant length of the body.
\((a) L_c = 0.6 m, ∆T = 75 – 18 = 57°C\)
\( ρ = 1.2 kg/m^3, Pr = 0.72,\)
\(µ = 1.8 × 10^{–5} kg/ms\)
Mean film temperature,
\(T_f = \frac{75 +18}{2} 46.5°C\)
\(β = \frac{1}{T_f +273}=\frac{1}{ 46.5+ 273} \)
\(= 3.13 × 10^{–3} K^{–1}\)
The Grashof number
\(Gr_L = \frac{9.81× (1.2)^2 × 3.13 × 10^{-3} × 57 × (0.6)^3}{(1.8 × 10^{ -5})^2}\)

\(= 1.68 × 10^9.\)
The \(Gr_L > 10^9, \)the flow is turbulent.
(b) A horizontal oil sump
\(T_s = 40°C T_∞ = 75°C,\)
L = 0.4 m, w = 0.2 m,
\(ρ = 854 kg/m^3, Pr = 546,\)
\(β = 0.7 × 10^{–3} K^{–1}\)
\(µ = 3.56 × 10^{–2} kg/ms.\)
For horizontal plate, the characteristic length
Then \(L_c = \frac{A_s}{P}=\frac{L × w}{2(L +w)}\)

\(= \frac{0.4 × 0.2}{2 ×(0.4 + 0.2)}= 0.067 m\)
(i) \(Gr =\frac{9.81 × 854 × 0.7 × 10^{-3} ×(75 – 40 )×(0.067)^3}{(3.56 × 10^{-2})^2}\)

\(= 4.1 × 10^4. \)
(ii) \(Gr < 10^9,\) thus the flow is laminar

(c) Air
\(T_∞ = 20°C, ρ = 1.2 kg/m^3,\)
Pr = 0.72
\(µ = 1.8 × 10^{–5} kg/ms, L_c = D = 60 mm,\)
\(T_s = 90°C\)
\(T_f = \frac{90 +20}{2}= 55°C,\)
\(β = \frac{1}{55 +273} = 3.049 × 10^{–3} K^{–1}\)
\(Gr =\frac{9.81 × (1.2)^2 × 3.049 × 10^{-3} ×(90 – 20 )×(60 × 10^{-3})^3}{(1.8× 10^{-5})^2}\)

\(= 2.0 × 10^6,\) Laminar.
