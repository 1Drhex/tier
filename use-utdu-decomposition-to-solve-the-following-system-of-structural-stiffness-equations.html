Guest Question.

Use \(U^{T}DU\) decomposition to solve the following system of structural stiffness equations.

\(\left[\begin{matrix} 5 & 2 & -1&0 \\ & 6 & -3&2 \\ (symmetric) & & 4&1\\ & & &7 \end{matrix} \right] \left[\begin{matrix} d_{1} \\ d_{2} \\ d_{3}\\d_{4} \end{matrix} \right] =\left[\begin{matrix} -19 \\ -22 \\ 22 \\ -6 \end{matrix} \right]\)
Admin
Solution

Decomposition: By applying Eqs. (9.78)

\(D_{i i}= \begin{cases}S_{i i} & \text { for } i=1 \\ S_{i i}-\sum_{k=1}^{i-1} D_{k k} U_{k i}^{2} & \text { for } i=2,3, \ldots, \text { NDOF }\end{cases}\) (9.78a)

\(U_{i j}= \begin{cases}\frac{S_{i j}}{D_{i i}} & \text { for } i=1 ; j=i+1, i+2, \ldots, N D O F \\ \frac{1}{D_{i i}}\left(S_{i j}-\sum_{k=1}^{i-1} D_{k k} U_{k i} U_{k j}\right) & \text { for } i=2,3, \ldots, N D O F-1 ; \\ j & =i+1, i+2, \ldots, N D O F\end{cases}\) (9.78b)

\(U_{i i}=1 \quad \text { for } i=1,2, \ldots, \text { NDOF } \) (9.78c)

 

\(D_{11} =S_{11}=5\)

\(U_{12}=\frac{S_{12}}{D_{11}}=\frac{2}{5}=0.4\)

\(U_{13}=\frac{S_{13}}{D_{11}}=\frac{-}{5}=-0.2\)

\(U_{14}=0\)

\(U_{11}=U_{22}=U_{33}=U_{44}=1\)

\(D_{22}=S_{22}-D_{11}U_{12}^{2} = 6-5(0.4)^{2}=5.2\)

\(U_{23}=\frac{1}{D_{22}}(S_{23}-D_{11}U_{12}U_{13})=\frac{1}{5.2}[-3-5(0.4)(-0.2)]=-0.5\)

\(U_{24}=\frac{1}{D_{22}}(S_{24}-D_{11}U_{12}U_{14})=\frac{1}{5.2}[2-5(0.4)(0)]=0.38462\)

\(D_{33}=S_{33}-D_{11}U_{13}^{2} -D_{22}U_{23}^{2}= 4-5(-0.2)^{2}-5.2(-0.5)^{2}=2.5\)

\(U_{34}=\frac{1}{D_{33}}(S_{34}-D_{11}U_{13}U_{14}-D_{22}U_{23}U_{24})\)

\(=\frac{1}{2.5}[1-5(-0.2)(0)-5.2(-0.5)(0.38462)]=0.8\)

\(D_{44}=S_{44}-D_{11}U^{2}_{14}-D_{22}U^{2}_{24}-D_{33}U^{2}_{34}\)

\(=7-5(0)^{2}-5.2(0.38462)^{2}-2.5(0.8)^{2}=4.6308\)

Thus,

\(D=\left[\begin{matrix}5&0&0&0\\0&5.2&0&0\\0&0&2.5&0\\0&0&0&4.6308 \end{matrix} \right] U=\begin{bmatrix} 1&0.4&-0.2&0\\0&1&-0.5&0.38462\\0&0&1&0.8\\0&0&0&1 \end{bmatrix}\)

Forward Substitution: Using Eq. (9.83) \(\widehat{d}_{i} =\begin{cases} \frac{\overline{P}_{i} }{D_{ii}} & for i=1 \\ \frac{1}{D_{ii}} \left(\overline{P}_{i}-\sum\limits_{k=1}^{i-1}{D_{kk}U_{ki}\widehat{d}_{k} } \right) & for i=2,3,…,NDOF \end{cases}\) ,

\(\widehat{d}_{1}= \frac{\overline{P}_{1} }{D_{11}}=\frac{-19}{5}=-3.8\)

\(\widehat{d}_{2}= \frac{1 }{D_{22}}(\overline{P}_{2}-D_{11}U_{12}\widehat{d}_{1} )=\frac{1}{5.2}[-22-5(0.4)(-3.8)]=-2.7692\)

\(\widehat{d}_{3}= \frac{1 }{D_{33}}(\overline{P}_{3}-D_{11}U_{13}\widehat{d}_{1}-D_{22}U_{23}\widehat{d}_{2} )\)

\(=\frac{1}{2.5}[22 − 5(−0.2)(−3.8) − 5.2(−0.5)(−2.7692)] = 4.4\)

\(\widehat{d}_{4}= \frac{1 }{D_{44}}(\overline{P}_{4}-D_{11}U_{14}\widehat{d}_{1}-D_{22}U_{24}\widehat{d}_{2} -D_{33}U_{34}\widehat{d}_{3} )\)

\(\frac{1}{4.6308} [−6 − 5(0)(−3.8) − 5.2(0.38462)(−2.7692) − 2.5(0.8)(4.4)] = −2\)

Thus,

\(\widehat{d}=\begin{bmatrix} -3.8\\-2.7692\\4.4\\-2 \end{bmatrix}\)

Back Substitution: Applying Eq. (9.85) \({d}_{i} =\begin{cases}\widehat{d}_{i} & for i=NDOF \\ \widehat{d}_{i}-\sum\limits_{k=i+1}^{NDOF}{U_{ki}d_{k} } & for i=NDOF-1,NDOF-2,…..,1 \end{cases}\) ,

\(d_{4}=\widehat{d}_{4}=-2\)

\(d_{3}=\widehat{d}_{3}-U_{34}d_{4}=4.4-0.8(-2)=6\)

\(d_{2}=\widehat{d}_{2}-U_{23}d_{3}-U_{24}d_{4}= −2.7692 − (−0.5)6 − 0.38462(−2) = 1\)

\(d_{1}=\widehat{d}_{1}-U_{12}d_{2}-U_{13}d_{3}-U_{14}d_{4}= −3.8 − 0.4(1) − (−0.2)6 − 0(−2) = −3\)

Thus, the solution of the given system of equations is

\(d=\begin{bmatrix}-3\\1\\6\\-2 \end{bmatrix}\)
