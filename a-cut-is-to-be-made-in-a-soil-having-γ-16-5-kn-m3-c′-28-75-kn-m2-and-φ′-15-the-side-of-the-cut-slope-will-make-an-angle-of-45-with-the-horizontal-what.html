Guest Question.

A cut is to be made in a soil having γ= 16.5 kN/\(m^{3}\), \(c ^{\prime }\) = 28.75 kN/\(m^2\), and \(\phi ^{\prime }\)= 15°. The side of the cut slope will make an angle of 45° with the horizontal. What should be the depth of the cut slope that will have a factor of safety \((F_{s})\) of 3?

Admin
Solution

Given: \(\phi ^{\prime }=15°; c^{\prime}=28.75 kN/m^{2}. IfF_{s}= 3,then F_{c^{\prime} } and F_{\phi ^{\prime} } \) should both be equal to 3.

\( F_{c^{\prime} }=\frac{c^{\prime} }{c^{\prime}_{d} } \)

or

\(c^{\prime}_{d}=\frac{c^{\prime}}{F_{c^{\prime} }} =\frac{c^{\prime}}{F_{s}} =\frac{28.75}{3} =9.58 kN/m^{2}\)

Similarly,

\( F_{\phi ^{\prime} } =\frac{\tan^{\prime} }{\tan \phi ^{\prime}_{d} }\)

 

\(tan \phi ^{\prime}_{d} =\frac{\tan \phi ^{\prime}_{d}}{F_{\phi ^{\prime} }} =\frac{\tan \phi ^{\prime}}{F_{s}} =\frac{\tan15}{3} \)

or

\(\phi ^{\prime}_{d}=\tan^{-1} [\frac{\tan15}{3} ]=5.1°\)

Substituting the preceding values of \(c^{\prime}_{d} and \phi ^{\prime}_{d}\) in Eq. (15.40),

\( c^{\prime}_{d} =\frac{\gamma H}{4} [\frac{1-\cos(\beta -\phi ^{\prime}_{d} )}{\sin\beta \cos \phi ^{\prime}_{d} } \)

 

\( H=\frac{4 c^{\prime}_{d} }{\gamma } [\frac{\sin\beta .\cos\phi ^{\prime}_{d}}{1-\cos(\beta -\phi ^{\prime}_{d})} ]\)

 

\(=\frac{4\times 9.58}{16.5} [\frac{\sin45.\cos5.1}{1-\cos(45-5.1)} ]=7.03m\)
