Guest Question.

Use the IMC design method to design two controllers for the FOPTD model in Equation
\( \widetilde{G}(s) = \frac{Ke^{-\theta s}}{\tau s+1} \) (1)
Assume that f is specified by Equation
\( f = \frac{1}{(\tau _{c}s+1)^{r}} \) (2)
with r=1, and consider two approximations for the time-delay term:
(a) 1/1 Padé approximation:
\( e^{-\theta s}\cong \frac{1-\frac{\theta }{2}s }{1+\frac{\theta }{2}s} \) (3.1)
(b) First-order Taylor series approximation:
\( e^{-\theta s}\cong 1-\theta s \) (3.2)

Admin
Solution

(a) Substituting Equation (3.1) into Equation (1) gives:
\( \widetilde{G}(s)= \frac{K(1-\frac{\theta }{2}s )}{(1+\frac{\theta }{2}s )(\tau s+1)} \) (4)
Factor this model as \( \widetilde{G} = \widetilde{G}_{+}\widetilde{G}_{-} \) where:
\( \widetilde{G}_{+} = 1-\frac{\theta }{2}s \) (5)
and
\( \widetilde{G}_{-} = \frac{K}{(1+\frac{\theta }{2}s )(\tau s+1)} \) (6)
Note that \( \widetilde{G}_{+} \) has a steady-state gain of one, as required in the IMC design procedure.
Substituting Equations (6) and (2) into Equation
\( G^{∗}_{c} = \frac{1}{\widetilde{G}_{-}}f \)
and setting r=1 gives
\( G^{∗}_{c} = \frac{(1+\frac{\theta }{2}s )(\tau s+1)}{K(\tau _{c}s+1)} \)
The equivalent controller \(G_{c} \) can be obtained from Equation
\( G_{c} = \frac{G^{*}_{c}}{1-G^{*}_{c}\widetilde{G} } \)
\( G_{c} = \frac{(1+\frac{\theta }{2}s )(\tau s+1)}{K(\tau _{c}+\frac{\theta }{2} )s} \)
and rearranged into the PID controller of Equation
\( G_{c} = K_{c}(1+\frac{1}{\tau _{1}s}+\tau _{D}s ) \)
with
\( K_{c}= \frac{1}{K}\frac{2(\frac{\tau }{\theta } )+1}{2(\frac{\tau _{c}}{\theta } )+1},\quad \tau _{1} = \frac{\theta }{2} + \tau , \quad \tau _{D} = \frac{\tau }{2(\frac{\tau }{\theta } )+1} \) (7)

(b) Repeating this derivation for the Taylor series approximation gives a standard PI controller for \( G_{c}: \)
\( K_{c} = \frac{1}{K}\frac{\tau }{\tau _{c}+\theta },\quad \tau _{I} = \tau \) (8)
A comparison of Equations (7) and (8) indicates that the type of controller that is designed depends on the time-delay approximation. Furthermore, the IMC controller in Equation (8) is identical to the DS controller in Equation
\( K_{c} = \frac{1}{K}\frac{\tau }{\tau _{c}+\theta },\quad \tau _{I} = \tau \)
for an FOPTD model.
