Guest Question.

The power plant shown in Fig. 10.24 combines a gas-turbine cycle and a steamturbine cycle. The following data are known for the gas-turbine cycle. Air enters the compressor at 100 kPa, 25°C, the compressor pressure ratio is 14, the heater input rate is 60 MW; the turbine inlet temperature is 1250°C, the exhaust pressure is 100 kPa; the cycle exhaust temperature from the heat exchanger is 200°C. The following data are known for the steam-turbine cycle. The pump inlet state is saturated liquid at 10 kPa, the pump exit pressure is 12.5 MPa; turbine inlet temperature is 500°C. Determine
a. The mass flow rate of air in the gas-turbine cycle.
b. The mass flow rate of water in the steam cycle.
c. The overall thermal efficiency of the combined cycle.

Admin
Solution

a) From Air Tables, A.7 : \(P _{ r1}=1.0913, \quad h _{1}=298.66, \quad h _{5}=475.84 kJ / kg\)

\(\begin{aligned}& s _{2}= s _{1} \Rightarrow P _{ r 2 S }= P _{ r 1}\left( P _{2} / P _{1}\right)=1.0913 \times 14=15.2782 \\& T _{2}=629 K , h _{2}=634.48 kJ / kg \\& w _{ C }= h _{1}- h _{2}=298.66-634.48=-335.82 kJ / kg \\& At T _{3}=1523.2 K : P _{ r 3}=515.493, h _{3}=1663.91 kJ / kg \\&\dot{ m }_{ AIR }=\dot{ Q }_{ H } /\left( h _{3}- h _{2}\right)=\frac{60000}{1663.91-634.48}= 5 8 . 2 8 kg / s\end{aligned}\)

 

b)

\(\begin{aligned}&\begin{aligned}P _{ r 4 S } &= P _{ r 3}\left( P _{4} / P _{3}\right)=515.493(1 / 14)=36.8209 \\&=> T _{4}=791 K , \quad h _{4}=812.68 kJ / kg \\w _{ T gas } &= h _{3}- h _{4}=1663.91-812.68=851.23 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\end{aligned}\)

Steam cycle: \(– w _{ P } \approx 0.00101 m ^{3} / kg (12500-10) kPa =12.615 kJ / kg\)

\(h _{6}= h _{9}- w _{ P }=191.83+12.615=204.45 kJ / kg\)

At 12.5 MPa, 500 °C: \(h _{7}=3341.1 kJ / kg , \quad s _{7}=6.4704 kJ / kg K\)

\(\dot{ m }_{ H _{2} O }=\dot{ m }_{ AIR } \frac{ h _{4}- h _{5}}{ h _{7}- h _{6}}=58.28 \frac{812.68-475.84}{3341.1-204.45}= 6 . 2 5 9 kg / s\)

 

c)

\(\begin{aligned}& s _{8}= s _{7}=6.4704=0.6492+ x _{8} \times 7.501, \quad x _{8}=0.7761 \\& h _{8}=191.81+0.7761 \times 2392.8=2048.9 kJ / kg \\& w _{ T \text { steam }}= h _{7}- h _{8}=3341.1-2048.9=1292.2 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ W }_{ NET } &=\left[\dot{ m }\left( w _{ T }+ w _{ C }\right)\right]_{ AIR }+\left[\dot{ m }\left( w _{ T }+ w _{ P }\right)\right]_{ H _{2} O } \\&=58.28(851.23-335.82)+6.259(1292.2-12.615) \\&=30038+8009=38047 kW =38.05 MW\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\eta_{ TH }=\dot{ W }_{ NET } / \dot{ Q }_{ H }=38.047 / 60= 0 . 6 3 4\)
