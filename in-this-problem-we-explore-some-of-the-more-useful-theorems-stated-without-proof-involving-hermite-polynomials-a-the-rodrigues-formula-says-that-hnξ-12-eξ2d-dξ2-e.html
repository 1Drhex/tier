Guest Question.

In this problem we explore some of the more useful theorems (stated without proof) involving Hermite polynomials.

(a) The Rodrigues formula says that

\( H_{n}(\xi)=(-1)^{n} e^{\xi^{2}}\left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{n} e^{-\xi^{2}} \). (2.87)

Use it to derive \( H_3 \) and \( H_4 \).

(b) The following recursion relation gives you \( H_{n+1} \) in terms of the two preceding Hermite polynomials:

\( H_{n+1}(\xi)=2 \xi H_{n}(\xi)-2 n H_{n-1}(\xi) \) (2.88).

Use it, together with your answer in (a), to obtain \( H_5 \) and \( H_6 \).

(c) If you differentiate an nth-order polynomial, you get a polynomial of order (n-1) . For the Hermite polynomials, in fact,

\( \frac{d H_{n}}{d \xi}=2 n H_{n-1}(\xi) \) (2.89).

Check this, by differentiating \( H_5 \) and \( H_6 \).

(d) H_n(ξ) is the nth z-derivative, at z=0 of the generating function \( \exp \left(-z^{2}+2 z \xi\right) \); or, to put it another way, it is the coefficient of \( z^{n} / n ! \) in the Taylor series expansion for this function:

\( e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{z^{n}}{n !} H_{n}(\xi) \) (2.90).

Use this to obtain \( H_1 , H_2 \) and \( H_3 \).

Admin
Solution

(a)

\( \frac{d}{d \xi}\left(e^{-\xi^{2}}\right)=-2 \xi e^{-\xi^{2}} ;\left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{2} e^{-\xi^{2}}=\frac{d}{d \xi}\left(-2 \xi e^{-\xi^{2}}\right)=\left(-2+4 \xi^{2}\right) e^{-\xi^{2}} \).

\( \left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{3} e^{-\xi^{2}}=\frac{d}{d \xi}\left[\left(-2+4 \xi^{2}\right) e^{-\xi^{2}}\right]=\left[8 \xi+\left(-2+4 \xi^{2}\right)(-2 \xi)\right] e^{-\xi^{2}}=\left(12 \xi-8 \xi^{3}\right) e^{-\xi^{2}} \).

\( \left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{4} e^{-\xi^{2}}=\frac{d}{d \xi}\left[\left(12 \xi-8 \xi^{3}\right) e^{-\xi^{2}}\right]=\left[12-24 \xi^{2}+\left(12 \xi-8 \xi^{3}\right)(-2 \xi)\right] e^{-\xi^{2}}=\left(12-48 \xi^{2}+16 \xi^{4}\right) e^{-\xi^{2}} \).

\( H_{3}(\xi)=-e^{\xi^{2}}\left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{3} e^{-\xi^{2}} = -12 \xi+8 \xi^{3} \); \( H_{4}(\xi)=e^{\xi^{2}}\left(\frac{d}{d \xi}\right)^{4} e^{-\xi^{2}}f = 12-48 \xi^{2}+16 \xi^{4} \).

(b)

\( H_{5}=2 \xi H_{4}-8 H_{3}=2 \xi\left(12-48 \xi^{2}+16 \xi^{4}\right)-8\left(-12 \xi+8 \xi^{3}\right) = 120 \xi-160 \xi^{3}+32 \xi^{5} \).

\( H_{6}=2 \xi H_{5}-10 H_{4}=2 \xi\left(120 \xi-160 \xi^{3}+32 \xi^{5}\right)-10\left(12-48 \xi^{2}+16 \xi^{4}\right) = -120+720 \xi^{2}-480 \xi^{4}+64 \xi^{6} \).

(c)

\( \frac{d H_{5}}{d \xi}=120-480 \xi^{2}+160 \xi^{4}=10\left(12-48 \xi^{2}+16 \xi^{4}\right)=(2)(5) H_{4} \).

\( \frac{d H_{6}}{d \xi}=1440 \xi-1920 \xi^{3}+384 \xi^{5}=12\left(120 \xi-160 \xi^{3}+32 \xi^{5}\right)=(2)(6) H_{5} \).

(d)

\( \frac{d}{d z}\left(e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}\right)=(-2 z+2 \xi) e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi} \), setting z = 0; \( H_{1}(\xi)=2 \xi \).

\( \left(\frac{d}{d z}\right)^{2}\left(e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}\right)=\frac{d}{d z}\left[(-2 z+2 \xi) e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}\right] =\left[-2+(-2 z+2 \xi)^{2}\right] e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi} \); setting z = 0, \( H_{2}(\xi)=-2+4 \xi^{2} \).

\( \left(\frac{d}{d z}\right)^{3}\left(e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}\right)=\frac{d}{d z}\left\{\left[-2+(-2 z+2 \xi)^{2}\right] e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi}\right\} =\left\{2(-2 z+2 \xi)(-2)+\left[-2+(-2 z+2 \xi)^{2}\right](-2 z+2 \xi)\right\} e^{-z^{2}+2 z \xi} \).

setting z = 0; \( H_{3}(\xi)=-8 \xi+\left(-2+4 \xi^{2}\right)(2 \xi) = -12 \xi+8 \xi^{3} \).
