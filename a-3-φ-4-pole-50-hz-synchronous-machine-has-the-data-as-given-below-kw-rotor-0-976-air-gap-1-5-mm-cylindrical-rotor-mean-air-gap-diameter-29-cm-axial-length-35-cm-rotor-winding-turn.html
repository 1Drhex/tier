Guest Question.

A 3\( \phi\), 4-pole, 50 Hz synchronous machine has the data as given below.

\( K_{w}\) (rotor) = 0.976

Air-gap = 1.5 mm (cylindrical rotor)

Mean air-gap diameter = 29 cm

Axial length = 35 cm

Rotor winding turns = 746

Stator winding SPP = 4; conductor/slot = 20

Field current = 20 A

(a) The machine is being used in motoring mode with \(B_{r} \)= 1.6 T. Determine

(i) \( F_{2}\), peak rotor AT

(ii) Maximum torque

(iii) Electrical input at maximum torque

(b) If the machine is used as generator with same field current, determine its open-circuit (no-load) voltage.

Admin
Solution

(i) \( F_{2}=\frac{4}{\pi } K_{{w}}(rotor)\left(\frac{N_{pole}(series)}{P} \right) I_{f}\)

 

\( F_{2}=\frac{4}{\pi } \times 0.976\frac{746}{4} \times 20\)

 

\( = 4635 AT\)

 

(ii) \( B_{r}= 1.6 T\) (given)

Then \( T_{max}=\left(\frac{P}{2} \right) \left(\frac{\pi DL}{2} \right) F_{2}B_{r}\)

 

\( =\frac{4}{2} \times \frac{\pi \times 0.29\times 0.35}{2} \times 4635\times 1.6\)

 

\( = 2364\)

 

(iii) Synchronous speed (motor speed)

\( \omega _{m}=\frac{2}{P} =\frac{4\pi f}{P} =\frac{4\pi \times 50}{4}\)

 

\( = 50 rad (mech)/sec\)

 

Electrical power input, \( P_{in} =T_{max} \times \omega _{m}\)

 

\( = 2632\times 50\pi W\)

 

\( = 371 kW\)

 

The machine is assumed loss-less, so

\( P_{in} = P{in, max} = 371 kW\)

 

(b) Generating Mode

Open Circuit Voltage The armature is not carrying any current, i.e. F1 = 0, F2 = 4630 AT as calculated above.

\( SPP=\frac{S}{3P} =4\)

 

\( \gamma =\frac{180^{\circ }P}{S} =\frac{180}{12} =15^{\circ }\)

 

\( K_{w}=K_{b}\frac{\sin30^{\circ }}{3 \sin15^{\circ }/2} = 0.958\)

 

\( \Phi _{r}=\left(\frac{2Dl}{P} \times \frac{\mu _{0}}{g} \right) F^{2}\)

 

\( =\frac{2\times 0.29\times 0.35\times 4\pi \times 10^{-7}}{4\times 1.5\times 10^{-3}} \times 4635\)

 

\( = 0.197 Wb\)

 

\( N_{ph}=\frac{20\times 4\times 4}{2} =160\)

 

\( E_{ph}=4.44 K_{b} f N_{ph} \Phi\)

 

\( = 4.44 \times 0.958 \times 50\times 160\times 0.197\)

 

\( = 6704 V\)

 

\( E_{line}\)=11610 V or 11.61 kV
