Guest Question.

A Pelton wheel is driven by two similar jets transmits 3750 kW to the shaft running at \( 375 {rev}/{min}\). The total head available is 200 m and losses is \(0.1\% \) of the total head. The diameter of the wheel is 1.45 m, the relative velocity coefficient of the bucket is 0.9, the deflection of the jet is \(165^{\circ}\). Find the hydraulic efficiency, overall efficiency and the diameter of each jet, if the mechanical efficiency is \(90\%\).

Admin
Solution

Peripherial velocity \(u = \frac{\pi DN}{60} = \frac{\pi \times 1.45\times 375}{60} = 28.4 {m}/{s}\)

Total head \(=200 m, h _{f} = 200\times 0.1 = 20 m\)

Effective head H = total head – losses

\( =200 – 20 = 180 m\)

Velocity of the jet \(V_{1} = \sqrt{2gH} = \sqrt{2\times 9.8\times 180} = 59.4 {m}/{s}\)

Speed ratio \(=\frac{u}{V_{1}} = \frac{28.4}{59.4} = 0.478\)

Hydraulic efficiency \(\eta _{h} = 2\frac{u}{V_{1}}\left\lgroup1 – \frac{u}{V_{1}} \right\rgroup\left(1 – k\cos \theta \right)\)

\( = 2\times 0.478\left(1 – 0.478\right)\left(1 – 0.9\times \cos 165^{\circ}\right) = 0.932\)

 

\( \eta_{h}=93.2\%\)

Euler’s head \(E = \frac{u}{g}\left(V_{1} – u\right)\left(1 – k\cos \theta \right)\)

\( E=\frac{28.4}{9.8}\left(59.4 – 28.4\right)\left(1 – 0.9\cos165^{\circ} \right)=167.93 m\)

Relation between \(\eta _{0}, \eta _{h} , \eta _{m}\) is

\(\eta _{0} = \eta _{m} \times \eta _{h} \therefore \eta_{0} = 0.9\times0.932 = 0.838\)

hydraulic power \(= \frac{P}{\eta_{0}} = \frac{3750}{0.838} = 4474 kw\)

also \( \frac{\gamma QH}{1000}\times 2 =4474\)

\( Q =\frac{1000\times 4474}{2\times 9800\times 180} = 1.268 {m^{3}}/{s}\)

Flow rate \( Q = area \times velocity of jet\)

\( Q=\frac{\pi}{4} d^{2} \times V_{1}\)

 

\( d^{2}=\frac{4Q}{\pi\times V_{1}} =\frac{4\times 1.268}{\pi \times 59.4} = 0.0272\)

 

\( d=164 mm\)
