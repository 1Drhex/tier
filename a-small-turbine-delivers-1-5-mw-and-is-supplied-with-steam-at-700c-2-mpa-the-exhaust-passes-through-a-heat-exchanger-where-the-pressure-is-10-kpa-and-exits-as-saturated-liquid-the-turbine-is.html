Solution

Continuity Eq.4.11: Steady

\(\dot{ m }_{1}=\dot{ m }_{2}=\dot{ m }_{3}=\dot{ m }\)

Turbine: Energy Eq.4.13: \(w _{ T }= h _{1}- h _{2}\)

Entropy Eq.7.9: \(s _{2}= s _{1}+ s _{ T \text { gen }} \)

Inlet state: Table B.1.3 \(h _{1}=3917.45 kJ / kg , \quad s _{1}=7.9487 kJ / kg K\)

Ideal turbine \(s _{ T gen }=0, \quad s _{2}= s _{1}=7.9487= s _{ f 2}+ x s _{ fg 2}\)

State 3: P = 10 kPa, \(s _{2}< s _{ g }=>\) saturated 2-phase in Table B.1.2

\(\Rightarrow x _{2, s }=\left( s _{1}- s _{ f 2}\right) / s _{ fg 2}=(7.9487-0.6492) / 7.501=0.9731\)

 

\(\Rightarrow h _{2, s }= h _{ f 2}+ x h _{ fg 2}=191.8+0.9731 \times 2392.8=2520.35 kJ / kg\)

 

\(w _{ T , s }= h _{1}- h _{2, s }= 1 3 9 7 . 0 5 k J / k g\)

 

\(\dot{ m }=\dot{ W } / w _{ T , s }=1500 / 1397=1.074 kg / s\)

 

Heat exchanger:

Energy Eq.4.13: \(q = h _{3}- h _{2}\),

Entropy Eq.7.9: \(s _{3}= s _{2}+\int dq / T + s _{ He gen }\)

\(q = h _{3}- h _{2, s }=191.83-2520.35=- 2 3 2 8 . 5 k J / k g\)

 

\(\dot{ Q }=\dot{ m } q =1.074 kg / s \times(-2328.5) kJ / kg =- 2 5 0 0 kW\)
