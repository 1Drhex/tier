The area counterparts to Eqs. 5/7 are now applied and yield

\(\overline{x} =\frac{\sum{m}\overline{x} }{\sum{m} } \overline{y} =\frac{\sum{m}\overline{y} }{\sum{m} } \overline{z} =\frac{\sum{m}\overline{z} }{\sum{m} }\)

 

\([\overline{X}=\frac{\sum{A}\overline{x} }{\sum{A} } ] \overline{X}= \frac{959}{127.9}=7.50 \) in.
