Guest Question.

Using the data and results given in Examples and together with the additional information that the static pressure at rotor exit is 100 kPa and the nozzle enthalpy loss coefficient, \(\zeta _{N}=0.06\), determine
a. the diameter of the rotor and its speed of rotation; and
b. the vane width to diameter ratio, \(b _{2}/D_{2} \) at rotor inlet.

Admin
Solution

a. The rate of mass flow is given by

 

\(\dot{m}=\rho _{3} c_{m3}A_{3}=\left(\frac{p_{3}}{RT_{3}} \right) \left(\frac{c_{m3}}{U_{2}}\right) U_{2}\pi \left(\frac{r_{3s} }{r_{2} } \right)^{2}\left(1-\nu ^{2} \right) r^{2}_{2} \).

From Eq. (8.25),

\(S = ΔW/h_{01} =1-T_{03}/T_{01}\) (8.25)

\(T_{03}= T_{01}\left(1-S\right) =1050\times 0.8=840 K\),

 

\(T_{3}= T_{03}-c^{2}_{m3}/\left(2C_{p}\right) =T_{03}- \left(\frac{c_{m3}}{U_{2}}\right)^{2} \frac{U^{2}_{2} }{2C_{p}} =840-0.25^{2}\times 5.38.1^{2}/\left(2\times 1150.2\right) \)

 

Hence, \(T_{3}=832.1 K \)

Substituting values into this mass flow equation,

\(1=\left[10^{5}/\left(287\times 832.1\right) \right]\times 0.25\times 538.1\times 0.7^{2}\times \pi \times \left(1-0.4^{2}\right) r^{2}_{2}\)

therefore,

\( r^{2}_{2}=0.01373\) and \( r_{2}=0.1172 m\),

\(D_{2}=0.2343 m\)

\(\Omega =U_{2}/r_{2}=4591.3 rad/s \left(43,843 rev/min\right). \)

b. The rate of mass flow equation is now written as

\(\dot{m}=\rho _{2} c_{m2}A_{2}\), where \(A_{2}=2\pi r_{2} b_{2}=4\pi r^{2}_{2}\left(b _{2}/D_{2}\right) \)

Solving for the absolute velocity at rotor inlet and its components,

\(c_{\theta 2} =SC_{p}T_{01}/U_{2}=0.2\times 1150.2\times 1050/538.1=448.9 m/s\)

\(c_{m 2}=c_{\theta 2}/\tan \alpha _{2} =448.9/3.3163=135.4 m/s \)

\(c_{2}=c_{\theta 2}/\sin \alpha _{2} =448.9/0.9574=468.8 m/s \)

To obtain a value for the static density, \(\rho _{2} \) , we need to determine \(T_{2} \) and \(p_{2} \) :

\(T_{2}= T_{02}-c^{2}_{2}/\left(2C_{p}\right)=1050-468.8^{2}/\left(2\times 1150.2\right) =954.5 K \)

 

\(h_{02}-h_{2}=\frac{1}{2}c^{2}_{2}\) and as \(\zeta _{N}=\frac{h_{2}-h_{2s}}{\left(1/2\right)c^{2}_{2} } , h_{01}-h_{2s}=\frac{1}{2}c^{2}_{2}\left(1+\zeta _{N}\right) \)

so

\(\frac{T_{02}- T_{2s}}{T_{02}} =\frac{c^{2}_{2}\left(1+\zeta _{N}\right)}{2C_{p}T_{02}} =\frac{468.8^{2}\times 1.06}{2\times 1150.2\times 1050} =0.096447 \)

 

\(\frac{T_{2s}}{T_{01}}=\left(\frac{p_{2}}{T_{01}} \right)^{\left(\gamma -1\right)/\gamma }=1-0.09645=0.90355 \)

Therefore,

\(\frac{p_{2}}{p_{01}}= \left(\frac{T_{2s}}{T_{01}} \right)^{\gamma /\left(\gamma -1\right) }=0.90355^{4}=0.66652 \)

 

\(p_{2}=3.109 \times 10^{5} \times 0.66652=2.0722 \times 10^{5} Pa\)

 

\(\frac{b_{2}}{D_{2}}=\frac{1}{4\pi } \left(\frac{RT_{2}}{p_{2}} \right)\left(\frac{\dot{m}}{c_{m2}r^{2}_{2}} \right)=\frac{1}{4\times \pi } \left(\frac{287\times 954.5}{2.0722\times 10^{5}} \right)\frac{1}{135.4\times 0.01373} =0.0566 \)
