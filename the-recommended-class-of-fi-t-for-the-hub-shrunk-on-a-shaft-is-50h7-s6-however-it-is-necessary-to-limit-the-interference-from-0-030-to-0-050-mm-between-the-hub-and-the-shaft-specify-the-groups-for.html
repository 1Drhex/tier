Hub:

\( 50^{\overset{+0.025}{_{+0.000}}} \) \( \text { or } \frac{50.025}{50.000} mm \).

Shaft:

\( 50^{\overset{+0.059}{_{+0.043}}} \) \( \text { or } \frac{50.059}{50.043} mm \).

Step II Maximum and minimum interferences
Maximum interference = 50.059 – 50.000 = 0.059 mm
Minimum interference = 50.043 – 50.025 = 0.018 mm
Step III Selection of groups
Suppose the components are sorted into three groups
(A, B and C) with the following dimensions:

C B A Group
\(\frac{50.025}{50.016}\) \(\frac{50.016}{50.008}\) \(\frac{50.008}{50.000}\) Hub
\(\frac{50.059}{50.055}\) \(\frac{50.055}{50.048}\) \(\frac{50.048}{50.043}\) Shaft

Step IV Maximum and minimum interferences for A, B and C

\( \text { The interferences } I_{A}, I_{B} \text { and } I_{C} \text { are as follows: } \):

\( I_{A} \max =50.048-50.000=0.048 mm \).

\( I_{B} \max =50.055-50.008=0.047 mm \).

\( I_{C} \max =50.059-50.016=0.043 mm \).

The maximum interference is within the limit of 0.050 mm.

\( I_{A} min =50.043-50.008=0.035 mm \).

\( I_{B} \min =50.048-50.016=0.032 mm \).

\( I_{C} \min =50.055-50.025=0.030 mm \).

The minimum interference is above the limit of 0.03 mm.
