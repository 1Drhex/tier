Guest Question.

An Ericsson cycle operates on air with a compression ratio of 10. For a low pressure of 200 kPa, a low temperature of 100°C, and a high temperature of 600°C, calculate the work output and the heat input.

Admin
Solution

For the Ericsson cycle the work output is

\( w_{\text {out }}=w_{1-2}+w_{2-3}+w_{3-4}+w_{4-1}=R T_{1} \ln \frac{v_{2}}{v_{1}}+P_{2}\left(v_{3}-v_{2}\right)+R T_{3} \ln \frac{v_{4}}{v_{3}}+P_{1}\left(v_{1}-v_{4}\right) \).

\( \text { We must calculate } P_{2}, v_{1}, v_{2}, v_{3} \text {, and } v_{4} \text {. We know } \)

\( v_{1}=\frac{R T_{1}}{P_{1}}=\frac{(0.287)(373)}{200}=0.5353 m ^{3} / kg \).

For the constant-pressure process 4 → 1,

\( \frac{T_{4}}{v_{4}}=\frac{T_{1}}{v_{1}} \quad \frac{873}{v_{4}}=\frac{373}{0.5353} \quad v_{4}=1.253 m ^{3} / kg \).

\( \text { From the definition of the compression ratio, } v_{4} / v_{2}=10, \text { giving } v_{2}=0.1253 m ^{3} / kg . \text { Using the ideal-gas law, we have } \)

\( P_{3}=P_{2}=\frac{R T_{2}}{v_{2}}=\frac{(0.287)(373)}{(0.1253)}=854.4 kPa \).

\( \text { The final necessary property is } v_{3}=R T_{3} / P_{3}=(0.287)(873) / 854.4=0.2932 m ^{3} / kg \text {. The expression for work output gives } \)

\( w_{\text {out }}=(0.287)(373) \ln \frac{0.1253}{0.5353}+(854.4)(0.2932-0.1253)+0.287 \times 873 \ln \frac{1.253}{0.2932}+(200)(0.5353-1.253)=208 kJ / kg \).

Finally,

\( \eta=1-\frac{T_{L}}{T_{H}}=1-\frac{378}{873}=0.573 \quad q_{\text {in }}=\frac{w_{\text {out }}}{\eta}=\frac{208}{0.573}=364 kJ / kg \).
