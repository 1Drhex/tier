Guest Question.

The 500-kV, 60-Hz, three-phase line in Problems and has a 180-km length and delivers 1600 MW at 475 kV and at 0.95 power factor leading to the receiving end at full load. Using the nominal π circuit, calculate the (a) ABCD parameters, (b) sending-end voltage and current, (c) sending-end power and power factor, (d) full-load line losses and efficiency, and (e) percent voltage regulation. Assume a 50°C conductor temperature to determine the resistance of this line.

Admin
Solution

From , \(R=\frac{1}{3}(0.0969) \frac{\Omega}{ mi }\left(\frac{1 mi }{1.609 km }\right)=0.0201 \Omega / km\)

From Problems and , \(\bar{z}=0.0201+j 0.335=0.336 \angle 86.6^{\circ} \Omega / km\)

\(\bar{y}=4.807 \times 10^{-6} \angle 90^{\circ} S / km\)

(a)

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{A}=\bar{D}=1+\frac{\bar{Y} \bar{Z}}{2}=& 1+\frac{1}{2}\left(0.336 \times 180 \angle 86.6^{\circ}\right)\left(4.807 \times 10^{-6} \times 180 \angle 90^{\circ}\right) \\&=0.9739 \angle 0.0912^{\circ} pu\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\bar{B}=\bar{Z}=\bar{z} l=0.336(180) \angle 86.6^{\circ}=60.48 \angle 86.6^{\circ} \Omega\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{C}=\bar{Y}\left(1+\frac{\bar{Y} \bar{Z}}{4}\right) &=\left(4.807 \times 10^{-6} \times 180 \angle 90^{\circ}\right)\left(1+0.0131 \angle 176.6^{\circ}\right) \\&=8.54 \times 10^{-4} \angle 90.05^{\circ} S\end{aligned}\)

 

(b)

\(\begin{aligned}&\bar{V}_{R}=\frac{475}{\sqrt{3}} \angle 0^{\circ}=274.24 \angle 0^{\circ} kV _{ LN } \\&\bar{I}_{R}=\frac{P_{R} \angle \cos ^{-1}(p f)}{\sqrt{3} V_{R L L}(p f)}=\frac{1600 \angle \cos ^{-1} 0.95}{\sqrt{3} 475(0.95)}=2.047 \angle 18.19^{\circ} kA\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{gathered}\bar{V}_{S}=\bar{A} \bar{V}_{R}+\bar{B} \bar{I}_{R}=(0.9739 \angle 0.0912)(274.24)+\left(60.48 \angle 86.6^{\circ}\right)\left(2.047 \angle 18.19^{\circ}\right) \\=264.4 \angle 27.02^{\circ} kV _{ LN } ; V_{S}=264.4 \sqrt{3}=457.9 kV _{ LL }\end{gathered}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{I}_{S}=\bar{C} \bar{V}_{R}+\bar{D} \bar{I}_{R} &=\left(8.54 \times 10^{-4} \angle 90.05^{\circ}\right)(274.24)+\left(0.9739 \angle 0.0912^{\circ}\right)\left(2.047 \angle 18.19^{\circ}\right) \\&=2.079 \angle 24.42^{\circ} kA\end{aligned}\)

(d) Full- load line losses \(=P_{S}-P_{R}=1647-1600=47 MW\)

Efficiency \(=\left(P_{R} / P_{S}\right) 100=(1600 / 1647) 100=97.1 \%\)

\(\begin{aligned}&V_{R N L}=V_{S} / A=457.9 / 0.9739=470.2 kV _{ LL } \\&\% V R=\frac{V_{R N L}-V_{R F L}}{V_{R F L}} \times 100=\frac{470.2-475}{475} \times 100=-1 \%\end{aligned}\)
