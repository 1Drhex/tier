Guest Question.

In Figure P17.19/20, collar D, released from rest, slides without friction downward a distance of h = 2.5 in. where it strikes a head fixed to the end of compound rod ABC, which is made of aluminum [E = 10,000 ksi]. Rod segments (1) has a length of \(L_{1}\) = 7 in. and a diameter of \(d_{1}\) = 0.75 in., and rod segment (2) has a length of \(L_{2}\) = 13 in. and a diameter of \(d_{2}\) = 0.50 in. Collar D weighs 20 lbs. Determine:
(a) the equivalent static load for this impact case.
(b) the maximum normal stress in rod segment (1).
(c) the maximum normal stress in rod segment (2).

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}&A_{1}=\frac{\pi}{4}(0.75 in .)^{2}=0.4418 in. ^{2} \\&A_{2}=\frac{\pi}{4}(0.5 in .)^{2}=0.1963 in .^{2}\end{aligned}\)

The total static deformation is

\(\delta_{ st }=\frac{F_{1} L_{1}}{A_{1} E_{1}}+\frac{F_{2} L_{2}}{A_{2} E_{2}}=\frac{F}{E}\left[\frac{L_{1}}{A_{1}}+\frac{L_{2}}{A_{2}}\right]=\frac{20 lbs }{10 \times 10^{6} psi }\left[\frac{7 in .}{0.4418 in .^{2}}+\frac{13 in .}{0.1963 in .^{2}}\right]=1.6411 \times 10^{-4} \text { in. }\)

Compute the impact factor n:

\(\begin{aligned}n &=1+\sqrt{1+\frac{2 h}{\delta_{ st }}} \\&=1+\sqrt{1+\frac{2(2.5 in .)}{1.6411 \times 10^{-4} in .}} \\&=175.5538\end{aligned}\)

(a) Equivalent static load

\(P_{\max }=n P_{ st }=(175.5538)(20 lbs )=3,511.0761 lbs =3,510 lbs\)

(b) Maximum normal stress in segment (1)

\(\sigma_{\max , 1}=\frac{F_{\max }}{A_{1}}=\frac{3,511.0761 lbs }{0.4418 in. ^{2}}=7,947.4509 psi =7,950 psi\)

(c) Maximum normal stress in segment (2)

\(\sigma_{\max , 2}=\frac{F_{\max }}{A_{2}}=\frac{3,511.0761 lbs }{0.1963 in. ^{2}}=17,881.7645 psi =17,880 psi\)
