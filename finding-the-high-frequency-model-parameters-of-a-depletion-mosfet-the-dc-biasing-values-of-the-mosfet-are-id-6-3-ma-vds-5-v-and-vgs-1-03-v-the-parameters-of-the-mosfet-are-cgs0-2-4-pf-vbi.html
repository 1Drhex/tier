Guest Question.

Finding the high-frequency model parameters of a depletion MOSFET The DC biasing values of the MOSFET are \( I_{ D }=6.3 mA , V_{ DS }=5 V , \text { and } V_{ GS }=-1.03 V \). The parameters of the MOSFET are \( C_{ gs 0}=2.4 pF \), \( V_{ bi }=0.8 V \text { for } C_{ g s 0}, C_{ gb 0}=1 pF , C_{ gd 0}=1.6 pF , V_{ bi }=0.8 V \text { for } C_{ gd 0}, g_{ m }=4.98 mA / V , \text { and } r_{ o }=26.77 k \Omega \).

(a) Calculate the capacitances of the MOS model in Fig. 7.50(d).

(b) Find the unity-gain bandwidth \( f_{ T } \).

Admin
Solution

(a) From Eq. (7.107), \( C_{ gs }=2.4 pF /[1+1.03 / 0.8]^{1 / 3}=1.8 pF .\) The gate-drain voltage \( V_{ GD } \) is

 

\( V_{ GD }=V_{ GS }+V_{ SD }=V_{ GS }-V_{ DS }=-1.03-5=-6.03 V \)

 

\( C_{ gs }=\frac{C_{ g 0}}{\left[1+\left|V_{ GS }\right| / V_{ bi }\right]^{1 / 3}}\) (7.107)

 

From Eq. (7.108), \( C_{ gd }=1.6 pF /[1+6.03 / 0.8]^{1 / 3}=0.78 pF .\)

 

\( C_{ gd }=\frac{C_{ gd 0}}{\left[1+\left|V_{ GD }\right| / V_{ bi }\right]^{1 / 3}} \) (7.108)

 

(b) From Eq. (7.116), the unity-gain bandwidth \( f_{ T } \) is

 

\( f_{ T }=\frac{g_{ m }}{2 \pi\left(C_{ gs }+C_{ gd }+C_{ gb }\right)} \quad(\text { in } Hz ) \) (7.116)

 

\( f_{ T }=\frac{g_{ m }}{2 \pi\left(C_{ gs }+C_{ gd }\right)}=\frac{4.98 mA / V }{2 \pi \times(1.8 pF +0.78 pF )}=307.2 MHz \)
