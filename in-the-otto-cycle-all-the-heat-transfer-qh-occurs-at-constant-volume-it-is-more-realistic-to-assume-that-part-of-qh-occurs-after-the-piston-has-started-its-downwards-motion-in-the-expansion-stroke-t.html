Guest Question.

In the Otto cycle all the heat transfer \(q_{ H }\) occurs at constant volume. It is more realistic to assume that part of \(q_{ H }\) occurs after the piston has started its downwards motion in the expansion stroke. Therefore consider a cycle identical to the Otto cycle, except that the first two-thirds of the total \(q_{ H }\) occurs at constant volume and the last one-third occurs at constant pressure. Assume the total \(q_{ H }\) is 700 Btu/lbm, that the state at the beginning of the compression process is 13 \(lbf / in .^{2}\), 68 F, and that the compression ratio is 9. Calculate the maximum pressure and temperature and the thermal efficiency of this cycle. Compare the results with those of a conventional Otto cycle having the same given variables.

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}& P _{1}=13 psia , T _{1}=527.67 R \\& r _{ V }= v _{1} / v _{2}=7 \\& q _{23}=\frac{2}{3} \times 700=466.7 \frac{ Btu }{ lbm } \\& q _{34}=\frac{1}{3} \times 700=233.3 \frac{ Btu }{ lbm }\end{aligned}\)

Constant s compression, Eqs.6.24-25

\(\begin{aligned}&P_{2}=P_{1}\left(v_{1} / v_{2}\right)^{k}=13(9)^{1.4}=281.8 lbf / in ^{2} \\& T _{2}= T _{1}\left( v _{1} / v _{2}\right)^{ k -1}=527.67(9)^{0.4}=1270.7 R\end{aligned}\)

Constant v combustion

\(\begin{aligned}& T _{3}= T _{2}+ q _{23} / C _{ V 0}=1270.7+466.7 / 0.171=4000 R \\& P _{3}= P _{2}\left( T _{3} / T _{2}\right)=281.8 \times 4000 / 1270.7= 8 8 7 . 1 lbf / in ^{2}= P _{4}\end{aligned}\)

Constant P combustion

\(T _{4}= T _{3}+ q _{34} / C _{ P 0}=4000+233.3 / 0.24= 4 9 7 2 R\)

Remaining expansion: \(\frac{ v _{5}}{ v _{4}}=\frac{ v _{1}}{ v _{4}}=\left( P _{4} / P _{1}\right) \times\left( T _{1} / T _{4}\right)=\frac{88.1}{13} \times \frac{527.67}{4972}=7.242\)

\(\begin{aligned}& T _{5}= T _{4}\left( v _{4} / v _{5}\right)^{ k -1}=4972(1 / 7.242)^{0.4}=2252 R \\& q _{ L }= C _{ V 0}\left( T _{5}- T _{1}\right)=0.171(2252-527.67)=294.9 Btu / lbm \\&\eta_{ TH }=1- q _{ L } / q _{ H }=1-294.9 / 700= 0 . 5 7 9\end{aligned}\)

Standard Otto cycle: \(\eta_{ TH }=1-(9)^{-0.4}= 0 . 5 8 5\)
