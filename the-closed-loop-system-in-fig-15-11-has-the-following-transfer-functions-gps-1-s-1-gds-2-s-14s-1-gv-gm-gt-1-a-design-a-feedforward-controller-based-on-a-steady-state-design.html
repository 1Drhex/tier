Guest Question.

The closed-loop system in Fig. \(15.11\) has the following transfer functions:

\(\begin{aligned}G_{p}(s) &=\frac{1}{s+1} \quad G_{d}(s)=\frac{2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)} \\G_{v} &=G_{m}=G_{t}=1\end{aligned}\)

(a) Design a feedforward controller based on a steady-state design.

(b) Design a feedforward controller based on a dynamic analysis.

(c) Design a feedback controller based on the IMC approach of Chapter 12 and \(\tau_{c}=3\).

(d) Simulate the closed-loop response to a unit step change in the disturbance variable using feedforward control only and the controllers of parts (a) and (b).

(e) Repeat part (d) for the feedforward-feedback control scheme of Fig. \(15.11\) and the controllers of parts (a) and (c) as well as (b) and (c).

Admin
Solution

a) Using steady-state gains

\(G_{p}=1, \quad G_{d}=2, \quad G_{v}=G_{m}=G_{t}=1\)

From Eq.15-21

\(G_{f}=-\frac{G_{d}}{G_{t} G_{v} G_{p}}\) (15-21)

\(G_{f}=\frac{-G_{d}}{G_{v} G_{t} G_{p}}=\frac{-2}{(1)(1)(1)}=-2\)

b) Using Eq. 15-21

\(G_{f}=\frac{-G_{d}}{G_{v} G_{t} G_{p}}=\frac{\frac{-2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}}{(1)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)}=\frac{-2}{4 s+1}\)

c) Using Eq. 12-19

\(\widetilde{G}=\widetilde{G}_{+} \widetilde{G}_{-}\) (12-19)

\(\begin{aligned}&\qquad \tilde{G}=G_{v} G_{p} G_{m}=\frac{1}{s+1}=\tilde{G}_{+} \widetilde{G}_{-} \\&\text {where } \quad \tilde{G}_{+}=1, \quad \tilde{G}_{-}=\frac{1}{s+1}\end{aligned}\)

For \(\tau_{c}=3\), and \(r=1\), Eq. \(12-21\) gives,

\(f=\frac{1}{\left(\tau_{c} s+1\right)^{r}}\) (12-21)

\(f=\frac{1}{3 s+1}\)

From Eq. 12-20,

\(G_{c}^{*}=\frac{1}{\widetilde{G}_{-}} f\) (12-20)

\(G_{c}^{*}=\tilde{G}_{-}^{-1} f=(s+1)\left(\frac{1}{3 s+1}\right)=\frac{s+1}{3 s+1}\)

From Eq. 12-16,

\(G_{c}=\frac{G_{c}^{*}}{1-G_{c}^{*} \widetilde{G}}\) (12-16)

\(G_{c}=\frac{G_{c}^{*}}{1-G_{c}^{*} \tilde{G}}=\frac{\frac{s+1}{3 s+1}}{1-\frac{1}{3 s+1}}=\frac{s+1}{3 s}\)

d) For feedforward control only, \(G_{c}=0\) for a unit step change in disturbance, \(D(s)=1 / s\)

Substituting into Eq. 15-20 gives

\(\frac{Y(s)}{D(s)}=\frac{G_{d}+G_{t} G_{f} G_{v} G_{p}}{1+G_{c} G_{v} G_{p} G_{m}}\) (15-20)

\(Y(s)=\left(G_{d}+G_{t} G_{f} G_{\nu} G_{p}\right) \frac{1}{s}\)

For the controller of part (a)

\(\begin{aligned}&Y(s)=\left[\frac{2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}+(1)(-2)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)\right] \frac{1}{s} \\&Y(s)=\left[\frac{-8}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}\right]=\frac{8 / 3}{s+1}-\frac{32 / 3}{4 s+1}=\frac{8 / 3}{s+1}-\frac{8 / 3}{s+1 / 4}\end{aligned}\)

Taking inverse Laplace transforms,

\(y(t)=\frac{8}{3}\left(e^{-t}-e^{-t / 4}\right)\)

For the controller of part (b)

\(Y(s)=\left[\frac{2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}+(1)\left(\frac{-2}{4 s+1}\right)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)\right] \frac{1}{s}=0\)

or \(\quad y(t)=0\)

The step responses are shown in Fig. S15.5 (left panel).

e) Using Eq. 15-20

For the controller of parts (a) and (c),

\(\begin{aligned}Y(s)&=\left[\frac{\frac{2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}+(1)(-2)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)}{1+\left(\frac{s+1}{3 s}\right)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)(1)}\right] \frac{1}{s} \\\text { or } \quad Y(s)&= \frac{-24 s}{(s+1)(4 s+1)(3 s+1)}=\frac{-36}{3 s+1}+\frac{32}{4 s+1}+\frac{4}{s+1} \\&= \frac{-12}{s+1 / 3}+\frac{8}{s+1 / 4}+\frac{4}{s+1}\end{aligned}\)

Thus,

\(y(t)=-12 e^{-t / 3}+8 e^{-t / 4}+4 e^{-t}\)

and for controllers of parts (b) and (c)

\(Y( s )=\left[\frac{\frac{2}{(s+1)(4 s+1)}+(1)\left(\frac{-2}{4 s+1}\right)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)}{1+\left(\frac{s+1}{3 s}\right)(1)\left(\frac{1}{s+1}\right)(1)}\right] \frac{1}{s}=0\)

Thus, \(y(t)=0\)

The closed-loop responses are shown in Fig. S15.5 (right panel).
