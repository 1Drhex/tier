Solution

The magnitude of each stress component is indicated in Figure 5.1. On the basis of the sign conventions reviewed in Section 2.5, the algebraic sign of each stress component is

\(\sigma _{11}=200 MPa \sigma _{22}=-30 MPa \tau _{12}= – 50 MPa\)

Strains can be calculated using either Equation 5.2a or Equation 5.12a.Since ΔT = ΔM = 0, we have

\(\left \{ \begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}=\left [ \begin{matrix} S_{11} & S_{12} & 0 \\ S_{12} & S_{22} & 0 \\ 0 &0 & S_{66} \end{matrix} \right ]\left \{ \begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}+\Delta T\left \{ \begin{matrix}\alpha _{11} \\ \alpha _{22} \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right \}+\Delta M\left \{ \begin{matrix} \beta _{11} \\ \beta _{22} \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right \}\)

Each term within the reduced compliance matrix is calculated using Equations 4.17:

\(\begin{matrix} S_{11}=\frac{1}{E_{11}} S_{22}=\frac{1}{E_{22}} S_{33}=\frac{1}{E_{33}}\\S_{44}=\frac{1}{G_{23}} S_{55}=\frac{1}{G_{13}} S_{66}=\frac{1}{G_{12}}\\ S_{21}=S_{12}=\frac{-\nu _{12}}{E_{11}}=\frac{-\nu _{21}}{E_{22}}\\ S_{31}=S_{13}=\frac{-\nu _{13}}{E_{11}}=\frac{-\nu _{31}}{E_{22}} \\ S_{32}=S_{23}=\frac{-\nu _{23}}{E_{22}}=\frac{-\nu _{32}}{E_{33}} \end{matrix}\) (4.17)

 

\(\left \{ \begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}=\left [ \begin{matrix} \frac{1}{E_{11}} & \frac{-\nu _{12}}{E_{11}} & 0 \\ \frac{-\nu _{12}}{E_{11}} & \frac{1}{E_{22}} &0 \\ 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{G_{12}} \end{matrix} \right ]\left \{ \begin{matrix}\sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}\)

From Table 3.1:

\(E_{11} = 170 GPa E_{22} =10 GPa\)

 

\(ν_{12} = 0.30 G_{12} =13 GPa\)

Using these values:

\(\left \{ \begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}=\left [ \begin{matrix} 5.88 \times 10^{-12} & -1.76\times 10^{-12} & 0 \\ -1.76\times 10^{-12} & 100\times 10^{-12} &0 \\ 0 & 0 &76.9\times 10^{-12} \end{matrix} \right ]\left\lgroup\frac{1}{Pa} \right\rgroup \left \{ \begin{matrix}200\times 10^6 \\ -30\times 10^6 \\ -50\times 10^6\end{matrix} \right \}(Pa)\)

Completing the matrix multiplication indicated, we obtain:

\(\left \{ \begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right \}= \left \{ \begin{matrix} 1230μm/m \\ -3350μm/m \\-3850μrad \end{matrix} \right \}\)
