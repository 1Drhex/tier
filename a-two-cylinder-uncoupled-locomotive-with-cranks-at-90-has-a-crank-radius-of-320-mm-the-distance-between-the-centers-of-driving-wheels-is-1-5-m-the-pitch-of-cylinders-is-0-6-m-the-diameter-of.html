Guest Question.

A two-cylinder uncoupled locomotive with cranks at 90° has a crank radius of 320 mm. The distance between the centers of driving wheels is 1.5 m. The pitch of cylinders is 0.6 m. The diameter of treads of driving wheels is 1.8 m. The radius of centers of gravity of balance masses is 0.7 m. The pressure due to dead load on each of the wheels is 40 kN. The masses of reciprocating and rotating parts per cylinder are 300 and 350 kg, respectively. The speed of the locomotive is 60 km/h. Find (a) the balancing masses in magnitude and position in the planes of driving wheels to balance whole of the revolving and 2/3rd of the reciprocating parts, (b) the swaying couple, (c) the variation in tractive effort, (d) the maximum and minimum pressure on the rails and (e) the maximum speed at which it is possible to run the locomotive, in order that the wheels are not lifted from the rails.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Give: } M=350 kg , R=300 kg , r=0.32 m , l=0.6 m , d_{ w }=1.8 m , b=0.7 m \).

\( P=40 kN , v=60 km / hr , \text { Equivalent mass }=M+\frac{2 R}{3}=350+\frac{2 \times 300}{3}=550 kg \).

From Table 12.22, we have

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=176 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=176 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=79.2 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=184.8 \).

\( \left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=M_{D} r_{D} l_{D} \).

\( \left[(79.2)^{2}+(184.8)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=1.05 M_{D} \).

\( M_{D}=\frac{201.05}{1.05}=191.5 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{D}=\frac{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}}{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}}=\frac{-(184.8)}{-(79.2)}=2.3333 \).

\( \theta_{D}=66.8^{\circ} \text { i.e. } 246.8^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both negative, therefore } \theta_{D} \text { lies in the third quadrant, } \)

\( M_{A} r_{A}=\left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{D} r_{D} \cos \theta_{D}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{D} r_{D} \sin \theta_{D}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( 0.7 M_{A}=\left[\left(0.7 \times 191.5 \cos 246.8^{\circ}+176\right)^{2}+\left(0.7 \times 191.5 \sin 246.8^{\circ}+176\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( =\left[(124.192)^{2}+(52.79)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=134.02 \).

\( M_{A}=191.5 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{A}=\frac{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{D} r_{D} \sin \theta_{D}\right)}{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{D} r_{D} \cos \theta_{D}\right)} \).

\( =\frac{-52.79}{-124.192}=0.42852 \).

\( \theta_{A}=24.2^{\circ} \text { i.e. } 204.2^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both negative, therefore } \theta_{A} \text { lies in the third quadrant, } \)

(b) \( v=\frac{60 \times 10^{3}}{3600}=16.67 m / s \).

\( \omega=\frac{2 v}{d_{w}}=\frac{2 \times 16.67}{1.8}=18.52 rad / s \).

Maximum swaying couple \( =\frac{(1-c) R \omega^{2} r l}{\sqrt{2}} \).

\( =\frac{\left(1-\frac{2}{3}\right) \times 300 \times(18.52)^{2} \times 0.32 \times 0.6}{\sqrt{2}} \).

\( =4656.6 Nm \).

\( \text { (c) Maximum tractive effort variation }=\pm \sqrt{2}(1-c) R \omega^{2} r \).

\( =\pm \sqrt{2}\left(1-\frac{2}{3}\right) \times 300 \times(18.52)^{2} \times 0.32 \).

\( =\pm 15522 N \).

\( \text { (d) } M_{A}=M_{L}=191.5 kg \).

\( \text { Balance mass for reciprocating parts, } B=\frac{191.5 \times 2 \times 300}{3 \times 350}=69.636 kg \).

\( \text { Net pressure on rails }=P \pm B \omega^{2} b \).

\( =40 \pm 69.636 \times(18.52)^{2} \times 0.7 \).

\( =40 \pm 16.719 \).

\( =56.719 kN , 24.281 kN \).

\( 40 \times 10^{3}=69.636 \times \omega^{2} \times 0.7 \).

\( \omega^{2}=820.59, \omega=28.646 rad / s \).

\( v=\frac{28.646 \times 0.9 \times 3600}{1000}=92.81 km / h \).
