Guest Question.

In we found that the trial wave function with shielding (Equation 8.28), which worked well for helium, is inadequate to confirm the existence of a bound state for the negative hydrogen ion.

\( \psi_{1}\left( r _{1}, r _{2}\right) \equiv \frac{Z^{3}}{\pi a^{3}} e^{-Z\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) / a} \) (8.28).
Chandrasekhar used a trial wave function of the form

\( \psi\left( r _{1}, r _{2}\right) \equiv A\left[\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right] \) (8.81).

where

\( \psi_{1}(r) \equiv \sqrt{\frac{Z_{1}^{3}}{\pi a^{3}}} e^{-Z_{1} r / a}, \quad \text { and } \quad \psi_{2}(r) \equiv \sqrt{\frac{Z_{2}^{3}}{\pi a^{3}}} e^{-Z_{2} r / a} \) (8.82).

In effect, he allowed two different shielding factors, suggesting that one electron is relatively close to the nucleus, and the other is farther out. (Because electrons are identical particles, the spatial wave function must be symmetrized with respect to interchange. The spin state—which is irrelevant to the
calculation—is evidently antisymmetric.) Show that by astute choice of the adjustable parameters \( Z_{1} \text { and } Z_{2} \text { you can get }\langle H\rangle \text { less than }-13.6 \text { el } \).

Answer:

\( \langle H\rangle=\frac{E_{1}}{x^{6}+y^{6}}\left(-x^{8}+2 x^{7}+\frac{1}{2} x^{6} y^{2}-\frac{1}{2} x^{5} y^{2}-\frac{1}{8} x^{3} y^{4}+\frac{11}{8} x y^{6}-\frac{1}{2} y^{8}\right) \).

\( \text { where } x \equiv Z_{1}+Z_{2} \text { and } y \equiv 2 \sqrt{Z_{1} Z_{2}} . \text { Chandrasekhar used } Z_{1}=1.039 \)

(since this is larger than 1, the motivating interpretation as an effective nuclear charge cannot be sustained, but never mind—it’s still an acceptable trial wave function) and \( Z_{2}=0.283 \).

Solution

\( 1=\int|\psi|^{2} d^{3} r _{1} d^{3} r _{2}=|A|^{2}\left[\int \psi_{1}^{2} d^{3} r _{1} \int \psi_{2}^{2} d^{3} r _{2}+2 \int \psi_{1} \psi_{2} d^{3} r _{1} \int \psi_{1} \psi_{2} d^{3} r _{2}+\int \psi_{2}^{2} d^{3} r _{1} \int \psi_{1}^{2} d^{3} r _{2}\right] \).

\( =|A|^{2}\left(1+2 S^{2}+1\right) \).

where

\( S \equiv \int \psi_{1}(r) \psi_{2}(r) d^{3} r =\frac{\sqrt{\left(Z_{1} Z_{2}\right)^{3}}}{\pi a^{3}} \int e^{-\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right) r / a} 4 \pi r^{2} d r=\frac{4}{a^{3}}\left(\frac{y}{2}\right)^{3}\left[\frac{2 a^{3}}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{3}}\right]=\left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^{3} \).

\( A^{2}=\frac{1}{2\left[1+(y / x)^{6}\right]} \).

\( H=-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m}\left(\nabla_{1}^{2}+\nabla_{2}^{2}\right)-\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left(\frac{1}{r_{1}}+\frac{1}{r_{2}}\right)+\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} \),

\( H \psi=A\left\{\left[-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m}\left(\nabla_{1}^{2}+\nabla_{2}^{2}\right)-\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left(\frac{Z_{1}}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{2}}{r_{2}}\right)\right] \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right. \) \( \left.+\left[-\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2 m}\left(\nabla_{1}^{2}+\nabla_{2}^{2}\right)-\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left(\frac{Z_{1}}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{2}}{r_{2}}\right)\right] \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\} \)

\( +A \frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left\{\left[\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{2}}\right] \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\left[\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{2}}\right] \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\}+V_{e e} \psi \).

\( \text { where } V_{e e} \equiv \frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} \).

The term in first curly brackets is \( \left(Z_{1}^{2}+Z_{2}^{2}\right) E_{1} \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\left(Z_{2}^{2}+Z_{1}^{2}\right) E_{1} \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right) \), so

\( H \psi=\left(Z_{1}^{2}+Z_{2}^{2}\right) E_{1} \psi \)

\( +A \frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left\{\left[\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{2}}\right] \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\left[\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{2}}\right] \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\}+V_{e e} \psi \).

\( \langle H\rangle=\left(Z_{1}^{2}+Z_{2}^{2}\right) E_{1}+\left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle+A^{2}\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) \)

\( \times\left\{\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\left(\left[\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{2}}\right]\left|\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle+\left[\frac{Z_{2}-1}{r_{1}}+\frac{Z_{1}-1}{r_{2}}\right]\left|\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right)\right\}\right.\right. \).

\( \{\}=\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{1}}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle+\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{2}}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \) \( +\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{1}}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right) \mid \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \) \( +\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \mid \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{2}}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle+\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{1}}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right) \mid \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \) \( +\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \mid \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{2}}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle+\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{1}}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right)\right\rangle \)

\( +\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{r_{2}}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \).

\( =2\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\frac{1}{r}\right\rangle_{1}+2\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\frac{1}{r}\right\rangle_{2}+2\left(Z_{1}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1} \mid \psi_{2}\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{1}\left|\frac{1}{r}\right| \psi_{2}\right\rangle+2\left(Z_{2}-1\right)\left\langle\psi_{1} \mid \psi_{2}\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{1}\left|\frac{1}{r}\right| \psi_{2}\right\rangle \).

\( \text { But }\left\langle\frac{1}{r}\right\rangle_{1}=\left\langle\psi_{1}(r)\left|\frac{1}{r}\right| \psi_{1}(r)\right\rangle=\frac{Z_{1}}{a} ; \quad\left\langle\frac{1}{r}\right\rangle_{2}=\frac{Z_{2}}{a}, \text { so }\langle H\rangle=\left(Z_{1}^{2}+Z_{2}^{2}\right) E_{1} \)

\( +A^{2}\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) 2\left[\frac{1}{a}\left(Z_{1}-1\right) Z_{1}+\frac{1}{a}\left(Z_{2}-1\right) Z_{2}+\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}-2\right)\left\langle\psi_{1} \mid \psi_{2}\right\rangle\left\langle\psi_{1}\left|\frac{1}{r}\right| \psi_{2}\right\rangle\right]+\left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle \).

And \( \left\langle\psi_{1} \mid \psi_{2}\right\rangle=S=(y / x)^{3} \), so

\( \left\langle\psi_{1}\left|\frac{1}{r}\right| \psi_{2}\right\rangle=\frac{\sqrt{\left(Z_{1} Z_{2}\right)^{3}}}{\pi a^{3}} 4 \pi \int e^{-\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right) r / a} r d r=\frac{y^{3}}{2 a^{3}}\left[\frac{a}{Z_{1}+Z_{2}}\right]^{2}=\frac{y^{3}}{2 a x^{2}} \).

\( \langle H\rangle=\left(x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}\right) E_{1}+A^{2}\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) \frac{2}{a}\left\{\left[Z_{1}^{2}+Z_{2}^{2}-\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)\right]+(x-2)\left(\frac{y}{x}\right)^{3} \frac{y^{3}}{2 x^{2}}\right\}+\left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle \).

\( =\left(x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}\right) E_{1}-4 E_{1} A^{2}\left[x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}-x+\frac{1}{2}(x-2) \frac{y^{6}}{x^{5}}\right]+\left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle \).

\( \left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle=\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\left\langle\psi\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi\right\rangle \).

\( =\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) A^{2}\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)+\psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \).

\( =\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) A^{2}\left[2\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle+2\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle\right] \).

\( =2\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) A^{2}(B+C), \text { where } \)

\( B \equiv\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle ; \quad C \equiv\left\langle\psi_{1}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{2}\left(r_{2}\right)\left|\frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|}\right| \psi_{2}\left(r_{1}\right) \psi_{1}\left(r_{2}\right)\right\rangle \).

\( B=\frac{Z_{1}^{3} Z_{2}^{3}}{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)^{2}} \int e^{-2 Z_{1} r_{1} / a} e^{-2 Z_{2} r_{2} / a} \frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} d^{3} r _{1} d^{3} r _{2} \). As on page 334, the \( r _{2} \) integral is

\( \int e^{-2 Z_{2} r_{2} / a} \frac{1}{\sqrt{r_{1}^{2}+r_{2}^{2}-2 r_{1} r_{2} \cos \theta_{2}}} d^{3} r _{2} \).

\( =\frac{\pi a^{3}}{Z_{2}^{3} r_{1}}\left[1-\left(1+\frac{Z_{2} r_{1}}{a}\right) e^{-2 Z_{2} r_{1} / a}\right] \quad\left(\text { Eq. } 8.25, \text { but with } a \rightarrow \frac{2}{Z_{2}} a\right) \).

\( I_{2}=4 \pi\left(\frac{1}{r_{1}} \int_{0}^{r_{1}} e^{-4 r_{2} / a} r_{2}^{2} d r_{2}+\int_{r_{1}}^{\infty} e^{-4 r_{2} / a} r_{2} d r_{2}\right) \)

\( =\frac{\pi a^{3}}{8 r_{1}}\left[1-\left(1+\frac{2 r_{1}}{a}\right) e^{-4 r_{1} / a}\right] \). (8.25).

\( B=\frac{Z_{1}^{3} Z_{2}^{3}}{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)^{2}} \frac{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)}{Z_{2}^{3}} 4 \pi \int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-2 Z_{1} r_{1} / a} \frac{1}{r_{1}}\left[1-\left(1+\frac{Z_{2} r_{1}}{a}\right) e^{-2 Z_{2} r_{1} / a}\right] r_{1}^{2} d r_{1} \).

\( =\frac{4 Z_{1}^{3}}{a^{3}} \int_{0}^{\infty}\left[r_{1} e^{-2 Z_{1} r_{1} / a}-r_{1} e^{-2\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right) r_{1} / a}-\frac{Z_{2}}{a} r_{1}^{2} e^{-2\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right) r_{1} / a}\right] d r_{1} \).

\( =\frac{4 Z_{1}^{3}}{a^{3}}\left[\left(\frac{a}{2 Z_{1}}\right)^{2}-\left(\frac{a}{2\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)}\right)^{2}-\frac{Z_{2}}{a} 2\left(\frac{a}{2\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)}\right)^{3}\right]=\frac{Z_{1}^{3}}{a}\left(\frac{1}{Z_{1}^{2}}-\frac{1}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{2}}-\frac{Z_{2}}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{3}}\right) \).

\( =\frac{Z_{1} Z_{2}}{a\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)}\left[1+\frac{Z_{1} Z_{2}}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{2}}\right]=\frac{y^{2}}{4 a x}\left(1+\frac{y^{2}}{4 x^{2}}\right) \).

\( C=\frac{Z_{1}^{3} Z_{2}^{3}}{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)^{2}} \int e^{-Z_{1} r_{1} / a} e^{-Z_{2} r_{2} / a} e^{-Z_{2} r_{1} / a} e^{-Z_{1} r_{2} / a} \frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} d^{3} r _{1} d^{3} r _{2} \).

\( =\frac{\left(Z_{1} Z_{2}\right)^{3}}{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)^{2}} \int e^{-\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) / a} \frac{1}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} d^{3} r _{1} d^{3} r _{2} \).

The integral is the same as in Eq. 8.21, only with \( a \rightarrow \frac{4}{Z_{1}+Z_{2}} a \). Comparing Eqs. 8.21 and 8.26, we see that the integral itself was

\( \left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle=\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right)\left(\frac{8}{\pi a^{3}}\right)^{2} \int \frac{e^{-4\left(r_{1}+r_{2}\right) / a}}{\left| r _{1}- r _{2}\right|} d^{3} r _{1} d^{3} r _{2} \) (8.21).

\( \left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle=\frac{5}{4 a}\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right)=-\frac{5}{2} E_{1}=34 eV \) (8.26).

\( \frac{5}{4 a}\left(\frac{\pi a^{3}}{8}\right)^{2}=\frac{5}{256} \pi^{2} a^{5} . \quad \text { So } \quad C=\frac{\left(Z_{1} Z_{2}\right)^{3}}{\left(\pi a^{3}\right)^{2}} \frac{5 \pi^{2}}{256} \frac{4^{5} a^{5}}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{5}}=\frac{20}{a} \frac{\left(Z_{1} Z_{2}\right)^{3}}{\left(Z_{1}+Z_{2}\right)^{5}}=\frac{5}{16 a} \frac{y^{6}}{x^{5}} \).

\( \left\langle V_{e e}\right\rangle=2\left(\frac{e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}}\right) A^{2}\left[\frac{y^{2}}{4 a x}\left(1+\frac{y^{2}}{4 x^{2}}\right)+\frac{5}{16 a} \frac{y^{6}}{x^{5}}\right]=2 A^{2}\left(-2 E_{1}\right) \frac{y^{2}}{4 x}\left(1+\frac{y^{2}}{4 x^{2}}+\frac{5 y^{4}}{4 x^{4}}\right) \).

\( \langle H\rangle=E_{1}\left\{x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}-\frac{2}{\left[1+(y / x)^{6}\right]}\left[x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}-x+\frac{1}{2}(x-2) \frac{y^{6}}{x^{5}}\right]-\frac{2}{\left[1+(y / x)^{6}\right]} \frac{y^{2}}{4 x}\left(1+\frac{y^{2}}{4 x^{2}}+\frac{5 y^{4}}{4 x^{4}}\right)\right\} \).

\( =\frac{E_{1}}{\left(x^{6}+y^{6}\right)}\left\{\left(x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}\right)\left(x^{6}+y^{6}\right)-2 x^{6}\left[x^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{2}-x+\frac{1}{2} \frac{y^{6}}{x^{4}}-\frac{y^{6}}{x^{5}}+\frac{y^{2}}{4 x}+\frac{y^{4}}{16 x^{3}}+\frac{5 y^{6}}{16 x^{5}}\right]\right\} \).

\( =\frac{E_{1}}{\left(x^{6}+y^{6}\right)}\left(x^{8}+x^{2} y^{6}-\frac{1}{2} x^{6} y^{2}-\frac{1}{2} y^{8}-2 x^{8}+x^{6} y^{2}+2 x^{7}-x^{2} y^{6}+2 x y^{6}-\frac{1}{2} x^{5} y^{2}-\frac{1}{8} x^{3} y^{4}-\frac{5}{8} x y^{6}\right) \).

\( =\frac{E_{1}}{\left(x^{6}+y^{6}\right)}\left(-x^{8}+2 x^{7}+\frac{1}{2} x^{6} y^{2}-\frac{1}{2} x^{5} y^{2}-\frac{1}{8} x^{3} y^{4}+\frac{11}{8} x y^{6}-\frac{1}{2} y^{8}\right) \).

Mathematica finds the minimum of \( \langle H\rangle \text { at } x=1.32245, y=1.08505, \text { corresponding to } Z_{1}=1.0392, Z_{2}=0.2832\). At this point, \( \langle H\rangle_{\min }=1.0266 E_{1}=-13.962 eV \), which is less than -13.6 eV-but not by much!.
