Guest Question.

Consider the storage tank with sightglass in Fig. E14.22. The parameter values are \(R_{1}=0.5 min / ft ^{2}, R_{2}=2 min / ft ^{2}\), \(A_{1}=10 ft ^{2}, \quad K_{v}=2.5 cfm / mA , \quad A_{2}=0.8 ft ^{2}, \quad K_{m}=1.5 mA / ft\), and \(\tau_{m}=0.5 min\).

(a) Suppose that \(R_{2}\) is decreased to \(0.5 min / ft ^{2}\). Compare the old and new values of the ultimate gain and the critical frequency. Would you expect the control system performance to become better or worse? Justify your answer.

(b) If PI controller settings are calculated using the ZieglerNichols rules, what are the gain and phase margins? Assume \(R_{2}=2 min / ft .\)

Admin
Solution

From modifying the solution to the two tanks in Section 6.4, which have a slightly different configurations,

\(G_{p}( s )=\frac{R_{1}}{\left(A_{1} R_{1} A_{2} R_{2}\right) s^{2}+\left(A_{1} R_{1}+A_{2} R_{1}+A_{2} R_{2}\right) s+1}\)

For \(R_{1}=0.5, R_{2}=2, A_{1}=10, A_{2}=0.8\)

\(G_{p}( s )=\frac{0.5}{8 s^{2}+7 s+1}\) (1)

For \(R_{2}=0.5, \quad G_{p}( s )=\frac{0.5}{2 s^{2}+5.8 s+1}\) (2)

a) \(\quad\) For \(R_{2}=2\)

\(\angle G_{p}=\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-7 \omega_{c}}{1-8 \omega_{c}^{2}}\right] \quad, \quad\left|G_{p}\right|=\left(\frac{0.5}{\sqrt{\left(1-8 \omega_{c}^{2}\right)^{2}+\left(7 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}}}\right)\)

\(K_{c u}\) and \(\omega_{c}\) are obtained using Eqs. 14-7 and 14-8:

\(A R=\frac{\hat{A}}{A}=|G|=\sqrt{R^{2}+I^{2}}\) (14-7)

\(\phi=\angle G=\tan ^{-1}(I / R)\) (14-8)

\(-180^{\circ}=0+0+\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-7 \omega_{c}}{1-8 \omega_{c}^{2}}\right]-\tan ^{-1}\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)\)

Solving, \(\omega_{c}=1.369 rad / min\)

\(1=\left(K_{c u}\right)(2.5)\left(\frac{0.5}{\sqrt{\left(1-8 \omega_{c}^{2}\right)^{2}+\left(7 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}}}\right)\left(\frac{1.5}{\sqrt{\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}+1}}\right)\)

Substituting \(\omega_{c}=1.369 rad / min , K_{c u}=10.96, \omega_{c} K_{c u}=15.0\)

For \(R_{2}=0.5\)

\(\angle G_{p}=\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-5.8 \omega_{c}}{1-2 \omega_{c}^{2}}\right] \quad, \quad\left|G_{p}\right|=\left(\frac{0.5}{\sqrt{\left(1-2 \omega_{c}^{2}\right)^{2}+\left(5.8 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}}}\right)\)

For \(G_{v}=K_{v}=2.5, \quad \varphi_{v}=0, \quad\left|G_{v}\right|=2.5\)

For \(G_{m}=\frac{1.5}{0.5 s+1}, \quad \varphi_{m}=-\tan ^{-1}(0.5 \omega), \quad\left|G_{m}\right|=\frac{1.5}{\sqrt{\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}+1}}\)

\(-180^{\circ}=0+0+\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-5.8 \omega_{c}}{1-2 \omega_{c}^{2}}\right]-\tan ^{-1}\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)\)

Solving, \(\omega_{c}=2.51 rad / min\)

Substituting \(\omega_{c}=2.51 rad / min , K_{c u}=15.93, \omega_{c} K_{c u}=40.0\)

b) From part a), for \(R_{2}=2\),

\(\begin{aligned}&\omega_{c}=1.369 rad / min , \quad K_{c u}=10.96 \\&P_{u}=\frac{2 \pi}{\omega_{c}}=4.59 min\end{aligned}\)

Using Table \(12.4\), the Ziegler-Nichols PI settings are

\(K_{c}=0.45 K_{c u}=4.932 \quad, \quad \tau_{I}=P_{u} / 1.2=3.825 min\)

Using Eqs. \(13.63\) and \(13-62\),

\(\begin{aligned}&\varphi_{c}=-\tan ^{-1}(-1 / 3.825 \omega) \\&\left|G_{c}\right|=4.932 \sqrt{\left(\frac{1}{3.825 \omega}\right)^{2}+1}\end{aligned}\)

Then, from Eq. 14-56

\(A R_{O L}\left(\omega_{c}\right)=\left|G_{O L}\left(j \omega_{c}\right)\right|=1\) (14-56)

\(-180^{\circ}=\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-1}{3.825 \omega_{c}}\right]+0+\tan ^{-1}\left[\frac{-7 \omega_{c}}{1-8 \omega_{c}^{2}}\right]-\tan ^{-1}\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)\)

Solving, \(\omega_{c}=1.086 rad / min\)

Using Eq. 14-57

\(\phi_{O L}\left(\omega_{c}\right)=\angle G_{O L}\left(j \omega_{c}\right)=-180^{\circ}\) (14-57)

\(\begin{aligned}&A_{c}=\left.\operatorname{AR}_{O L }\right|_{\omega=\omega c}= \\&=\left(4.932 \sqrt{\left(\frac{1}{3.825 \omega_{c}}\right)^{2}+1}\right)(2.5)\left(\frac{0.5}{\sqrt{\left(1-8 \omega_{c}{ }^{2}\right)^{2}+\left(7 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}}}\right)\left(\frac{1.5}{\sqrt{\left(0.5 \omega_{c}\right)^{2}+1}}\right) \\&=0.7362\end{aligned}\)

Therefore, gain margin \(G M=1 / A_{c}=1.358\)

Solving Eq.(14-16) for \(\omega_{g}\)

\(G(j \omega)=\frac{G_{a}(j \omega) G_{b}(j \omega) G_{c}(j \omega) \cdots}{G_{1}(j \omega) G_{2}(j \omega) G_{3}(j \omega) \cdots}\) (14-16)

\(\left. AR _{O L}\right|_{\omega=\omega c}=1 \quad\) at \(\quad \omega_{g}=0.925\)

Substituting into Eq. 14-57 gives

\(\varphi_{g}=\left.\varphi\right|_{\omega=\omega g}=-172.7^{\circ}\)

Therefore, phase margin \(P M=180+\varphi_{g}=7.3^{\circ}\)
