Solution

Energy Eq.4.13: \(h _{1}+\frac{1}{2} V _{1}^{2}+ gZ _{1}= h _{2}+\frac{1}{2} V _{2}^{2}+ g Z _{2}\)

Process: \(Z _{1}= Z _{2} \quad \text { and } \quad V _{2}= V _{1}\)

State 1, Table B.5.1: \(h _{1}=234.59 kJ / kg , v _{1}= v _{ f }=0.000829 m ^{3} / kg\)

Use energy eq.: \(\Rightarrow h _{2}= h _{1}=234.59 kJ / kg\)

State 2: \(P _{2} \& h _{2} \quad \Rightarrow \quad 2-\text { phase } \quad \text { and } \quad T _{2}= T _{ sat }(165 kPa )=- 1 5 ^{\circ} C\)

 

\(h _{2}= h _{ f }+ x _{2} h _{ fg }=234.59 kJ / kg\)

 

\(x _{2}=\left( h _{2}- h _{ f }\right) / h _{ fg }=(234.59-180.19) / 209=0.2603\)

 

\(v _{2}= v _{ f }+ x _{2} \times v _{ fg }=0.000746+0.2603 \times 0.11932=0.0318 m ^{3} / kg\)

 

Now the continuity equation with \(V _{2}= V _{1}\) gives, from Eq.4.3,

 

\(\dot{ m }=\rho A V = A V / v = A _{1} V _{1} / v _{1}=\left( A _{2} V _{1}\right) / v _{2}\)

 

\(\left( A _{2} / A _{1}\right)= v _{2} / v _{1}=\left( D _{2} / D _{1}\right)^{2}\)

 

\(\left( D _{2} / D _{1}\right)=\left( v _{2} / v _{1}\right)^{0.5}=(0.0318 / 0.000829)^{0.5}= 6 . 1 9\)
