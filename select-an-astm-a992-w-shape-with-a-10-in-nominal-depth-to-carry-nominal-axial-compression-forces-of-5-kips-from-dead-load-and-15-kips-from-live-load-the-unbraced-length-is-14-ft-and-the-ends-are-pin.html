Guest Question.

Select an ASTM A992 W-shape with a 10 in. nominal depth to carry nominal axial compression forces of 5 kips from dead load and 15 kips from live load. The unbraced length is 14 ft and the ends are pinned. The member also has the following nominal required moment strengths, not including second-order effects:
\(\begin{array}{ll}M_{x D}=15 \text { kip-ft } & M_{x L}=45 \text { kip-ft } \\M_{y D}=2 \text { kip-ft } & M_{y L}=6 \text { kip-ft }\end{array}\)
The member is not subject to sidesway.

Admin
Solution

Material Properties:

ASTM A992 \(F_{y}=50 ksi \quad F_{u}=65 ksi\)

Calculate the required strength, not considering second-order effects

LRFD ASD
\(\begin{aligned}P_{u} &=1.2(5.00 kips )+1.6(15.0 kips ) \\&=30.0 kips \\M_{u x} &=1.2(15.0 kip – ft )+1.6(45.0 kip – ft ) \\&=90.0 kip – ft \\M_{u y} &=1.2(2.00 kip – ft )+1.6(6.00 kip – ft ) \\&=12.0 \text { kip }- ft\end{aligned}\) \(\begin{aligned}P_{a} &=5.00 kips +15.0 kips \\&=20.0 kips \\M_{a x} &=15.0 kip – ft +45.0 \text { kip-ft } \\&=60.0 kip – ft \\M_{a y} &=2.00 kip – ft +6.00 kip – ft \\&=8.00 kip – ft\end{aligned}\)

Try a W10×33

Geometric Properties:

W10×33 \(A=9.71 in ^{2} \quad S_{x}=35.0 in \cdot^{3} \quad Z_{x}=38.8 in \cdot^{3} \quad I_{x}=171 in ^{4}\)

 

\(S_{y}=9.20 \text { in. }^{3} \quad Z_{y}=14.0 \text { in. }^{3} \quad I_{y}=36.6 \text { in. }^{4}\)

 

\(L_{p}=6.85 ft \quad L_{r}=12.8 ft\)

Calculate the required flexural strengths including second order amplification

Use “Amplified First-Order Elastic Analysis” procedure from Section C2.1b. Since the member is not subject to sidesway, only P-\(\delta\) amplifiers need to be added.

\(B_{1}=\frac{C_{m}}{1-\alpha P_{r} / P_{e 1}}\)

Eqn. C2-2

\(C_{m}=1.0\)

X-X axis flexural magnifier

\(P_{e 1}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I_{x}}{\left(K_{1} L_{x}\right)^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}(29,000 ksi )\left(171 in ^{4}\right)}{((1.0)(14.0 ft )(12 in . / ft ))^{2}}=1730 kips\)

Eqn. C2-5

LRFD ASD
\(\alpha=1.0\)

\(B_{1}=\frac{1.0}{1-1.0(30.0 kips / 1730 kips )}=1.02\)

 

\(M_{u x}=1.02(90.0 kip – ft )=91.8 \text { kip-ft }\)

 \(\alpha=1.6\)

\(B_{1}=\frac{1.0}{1-1.6(20.0 kips / 1730 kips )}=1.02\)

 

\(M_{a x}=1.02(60.0 kip – ft )=61.2 kip – ft\)

Eqn. C2-2

Y-Y axis flexural magnifier

\(P_{e 1}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I_{y}}{\left(K_{1} L_{y}\right)^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}(29,000 ksi )\left(36.6 in .^{4}\right)}{((1.0)(14.0 ft )(12 in / ft ))^{2}}=371 kips\)

Eqn. C2-5

LRFD ASD
\(\alpha=1.0\)

\(B_{1}=\frac{1.0}{1-1.0(30.0 kips / 371 kips )}=1.09\)

 

\(M_{u x}=1.09(12.0 kip – ft )=13.1\text { kip-ft }\)

 \(\alpha=1.6\)

\(B_{1}=\frac{1.0}{1-1.6(20.0 kips / 371 kips )}=1.09\)

Eqn. C2-2

\(M_{a x}=1.09(8.0 kip – ft )=8.76 kip – ft\)

Use \(M_{n y}\) = 700 kip-in. or 58.3 kip-ft

LRFD ASD
\(\begin{aligned}\phi_{b} &=0.90 \\M_{c x} &=\phi_{b} M_{n x}=0.90(152 kip – ft )=137 kip – ft \\M_{c y} &=\phi_{b} M_{n y}=0.90(58.3 kip – ft )=52.5 kip – ft\end{aligned}\) \(\begin{array}{l}\Omega_{b}=1.67 \\M_{c x}=M_{n x} / \Omega_{b}=152 kip – ft / 1.67=91.0 kip – ft \\M_{c y}=M_{n y} / \Omega_{b}=58.3 kip – ft / 1.67=34.9 kip – ft\end{array}\)

Check limit for Equation H1-1a

\(\frac{P_{r}}{2 P_{c}}+\left(\frac{M_{r x}}{M_{c x}}+\frac{M_{r y}}{M_{cy}}\right) \leq 1.0\)

 

\(\begin{array}{l}\frac{30.0 \text { kips }}{2(253 kips )}+\left(\frac{91.8 kip – ft }{137kip – ft }+\frac{13.1 kip – ft }{52.5 kip – ft }\right) \\0.0593+0.920=0.979 \leq 1.0 \quad \text { o.k. }\end{array}\)

 \(\begin{aligned}&\frac{P_{r}}{P_{c}}=\frac{P_{a}}{P_{n} / \Omega_{c}}=\frac{20.0 \text { kips }}{168 kips }=0.119, \text { therefore }\\&\text { use Specification Equation } H 1.1 b\end{aligned}\)
