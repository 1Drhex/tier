Span AB

\( a_{A}=a_{B}=\frac{5}{25}=0.2 \quad r_{A}=r_{B}=\frac{4-2}{2}=1.0 \)

Entering Table 13-2 with these ratios, we find

\( \begin{aligned}C_{A B} &=C_{B A}=0.619 \\k_{A B} &=k_{B A}=6.41\end{aligned}\)

Using Eqs. 13-2,

\(\begin{array}{c} K_{A}=\frac{k_{A B} E I_{C}}{L} \quad K_{B}=\frac{k_{B A} E I_{C}}{L} \\ \end{array}\) (13-2)

\(\begin{array}{c} K_{A B}=K_{B A}=\frac{k E I_{C}}{L}=\frac{6.41 E\left(\frac{1}{12}\right)(1)(2)^{3}}{25}=0.171 E \end{array}\)

Since the far end of span \(B A\) is pinned, we will modify the stiffness factor of \(B A\) using Eq. 13-3. We have

\(\begin{array}{l} K_{A}^{\prime}=K_{A}\left(1-C_{A B} C_{B A}\right) \\ \end{array}\) (13-3)

\(\begin{array}{l}K_{B A}^{\prime}=K_{B A}\left(1-C_{A B} C_{B A}\right)=0.171 E[1-0.619(0.619)]=0.105 E \end{array} \)

Uniform load, Table 13-2,

\(\begin{array}{l} (\mathrm{FEM})_{A B}=-(0.0956)(2)(25)^{2}=-119.50 \mathrm{k} \cdot \mathrm{ft} \\ (\mathrm{FEM})_{B A}=119.50 \mathrm{k} \cdot \mathrm{ft} \end{array} \)

Span BC

\( \begin{aligned} a_{B}=a_{C}=\frac{5}{10} &=0.5 \quad r_{B}=\frac{4-2}{2}=1.0 \\ r_{C} &=\frac{5-2}{2}=1.5 \end{aligned}\)

From Table 13-2 we find

\(\begin{array}{ll} C_{B C}=0.781 & C_{C B}=0.664 \\ k_{B C}=13.12 & k_{C B}=15.47 \end{array} \)

Thus, from Eqs. 13-2,

\(\begin{array}{c} K_{A}=\frac{k_{A B} E I_{C}}{L} \quad K_{B}=\frac{k_{B A} E I_{C}}{L} \\ \end{array}\) (13-2)

\( \begin{array}{l} K_{B C}=\frac{k E I_{C}}{L}=\frac{13.12 E\left(\frac{1}{12}\right)(1)(2)^{3}}{10}=0.875 E \\K_{C B}=\frac{k E I_{C}}{L}=\frac{15.47 E\left(\frac{1}{12}\right)(1)(2)^{3}}{10}=1.031 E \end{array} \)

Concentrated load,

\( \begin{aligned} b &=\frac{3}{10}=0.3 \\ (\mathrm{FEM})_{B C} &=-0.1891(30)(10)=-56.73 \mathrm{k} \cdot \mathrm{ft} \\ (\mathrm{FEM})_{C B} &=0.0759(30)(10)=22.77 \mathrm{k} \cdot \mathrm{ft} \end{aligned} \)

Using the foregoing values for the stiffness factors, the distribution factors are computed and entered in the table, Fig. 13-13b. The moment distribution follows the same procedure outlined in Chapter 12. The results in \(\mathrm{k} \cdot \mathrm{ft}\) are shown on the last line of the table.
