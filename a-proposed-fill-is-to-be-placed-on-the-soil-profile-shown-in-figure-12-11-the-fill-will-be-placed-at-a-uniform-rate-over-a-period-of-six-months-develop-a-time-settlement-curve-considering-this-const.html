Guest Question.

A proposed fill is to be placed on the soil profile shown in Figure 12.11. The fill will be placed at a uniform rate over a period of six months. Develop a time-settlement curve considering this construction period.

Admin
Solution

\(H_{fill}\) increases linearly from 0 at t =0 to 12 ft at t = 180 days.

SM/ML stratum:

Assume the consolidation settlement occurs as quickly as the fill is placed {i.e., no significant excess pore water pressures).

Divide into two layers: Layer 1 is the upper 4 ft and layer 2 is the lower 6 ft.

For Layer 1:

\(\sigma ^{\prime }_{z0}=\sum{\gamma H – u} =\left(118 lb/ft^{3}\right) \left(2 ft\right) – 0=236 lb/ft^{2}\)

 

\(\left(\delta _{c}\right) _{ult}=\sum{\frac{C_{c}}{1+e_{0}} H\log \left\lgroup\frac{\sigma ^{\prime }_{zf}}{\sigma ^{\prime }_{z0}} \right\rgroup } =\left(0.02\right) \left(4\right) \log \left\lgroup\frac{236 + \gamma _{fill}H_{fill}}{236} \right\rgroup \)

For Layer 2:

\(\sigma ^{\prime }_{z0}=\sum{\gamma H – u} =\left(118 lb/ft^{3}\right) \left(4 ft\right) +\left(119 lb/ft^{3}\right) \left(3 ft\right) – \left(62.4 lb/ft^{3}\right) \left(3 ft\right) =642 lb/ft^{2}\)

 

\(\left(\delta _{c}\right) _{ult}=\sum{\frac{C_{c}}{1+e_{0}} H\log \left\lgroup\frac{\sigma ^{\prime }_{zf}}{\sigma ^{\prime }_{z0}} \right\rgroup } =\left(0.02\right) \left(4\right) \log \left\lgroup\frac{642 + \gamma _{fill}H_{fill}}{642} \right\rgroup \)

Compute \(Î´_{c}\) for each time using the corresponding \(H_{fill}\).The results of this computation are shown in the table on the next page.

CL Stratum:

The consolidation in this stratum will continue well beyond the construction period, and needs to be computed using program SETTRATE. For t =0 to 180 days, use Equation 12.26 \(t_{adj}=\frac{t}{2} \) to find \(t_{adj}\), and use \(H_{fill}\) linearly increasing with time. For t > 180 days, use Equation 12.27 \(t_{adj}=t – \frac{t_{c}}{2} \) and \(H_{fill}\) = 12 ft.
