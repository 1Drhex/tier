Guest Question.

At the lever ①, rotating with constant angular velocity \(\omega \) about the fixed axis \(\overline{AA} \) , a cylinder ② is mounted. The cylinder rotates about the axis \(\overline{BB} \) parallel to \(\overline{AA} \) with constant angular velocity \(\omega ^{*} \) with respect to the lever. Fixed at the cylinder is a tube where a point \(P\) moves with speed \(v(t)\) with respect to the cylinder.

Determine the absolute velocity and absolute acceleration of \(P\) by using
a) the space fixed system \( x , y , z \) ,
b) the lever-fixed system \(\xi , \eta , \zeta\) ,
c) the cylinder-fixed system \(\xi^{*} , \eta^{*} , \zeta^{*} \) .

Admin
Solution

a) With the angular velocity \(\omega _{2}=\omega +\omega ^{*} \) of the cylinder with respect to the space-fixed system and with

\(\varphi =\omega t\) , \(ϑ=\omega ^{*}t \) , \(\varphi +ϑ=\omega _{2}t \) ,

the position vector of \(P\) in the space-fixed system \(x , y , z\) is given by

\(r_{P}=\left(a\cos \varphi +r\cos \left(\varphi +ϑ\right) \right)e_{x} + \left(a\sin \varphi +r\sin \left(\varphi +ϑ\right) \right)e_{y}+\left(b+s\right)e_{z} \)

 

\(=\left(a\cos \omega t +r\cos \omega_{2}t \right)e_{x} + \left(a\sin \omega t +r\sin \omega_{2}t \right)e_{y}+\left(b+s\right)e_{z} \) .

From its derivatives, by considering \(\dot{s} = v\) , follow

\(\underline{\underline{v_{P}=-\left(a\omega \sin \omega t +r\omega _{2} \sin \omega_{2}t \right)e_{x} + \left(a\omega \cos \omega t +r\omega _{2}\cos \omega_{2}t\right)e_{y}+ve_{z} } } \) ,

\(\underline{\underline{a_{P}=-\left(a\omega^{2} \cos \omega t +r\omega^{2} _{2} \cos \omega_{2}t \right)e_{x} – \left(a\omega^{2} \sin \omega t +r\omega^{2} _{2}\sin \omega_{2}t\right)e_{y}+\dot{v}e_{z} } } \) .

b) The general relations for \(v_{P}\) and \(a_{P}\) are given by

\(v_{P}=\dot{r}_{0}+\omega \times r_{0P}+v_{r} \) ,

\(a_{P}=\ddot{r}_{0}+\dot{\omega } \times r_{0P}+\omega \times \left(\omega \times r_{0P} \right)+2\omega \times v_{r}+a_{r} \) .

For the \(\xi , \eta , \zeta\) system we have

\(\omega =\omega e_{\zeta } \) , \(\dot{\omega } =0 \) , \(r_{0}=\dot{r}_{0}= \ddot{r}_{0}=0 \) , \( ϑ=\omega ^{*} t\)

 

\(r_{0P}=\left(a+r\cos \omega ^{*}t \right)e_{\xi }+r\sin \omega ^{*}te_{\eta }+\left(b+s\right)e_{\zeta } \) ,

\(\omega \times r_{0P}=\omega \left(a+r\cos \omega ^{*}t \right)e_{\eta }-r\omega \sin \omega ^{*}te_{\xi } \) ,

\(v_{r}=-r\omega ^{*}\sin \omega ^{*}te_{\xi }+r\omega ^{*}\cos \omega ^{*}te_{\eta } +ve_{\zeta } \) ,

\(\omega \times \left(\omega \times r_{0P}\right) =-\omega^{2} \left(a+r\cos \omega ^{*}t \right)e_{\xi}-\omega^{2} r\sin \omega ^{*}te_{\eta} \) ,

\(2\omega \times v_{r}=-2r\omega \omega^{*}\sin \omega^{*}t e_{\eta }-2r\omega \omega^{*}\cos \omega^{*}te_{\xi } \) ,

\(a_{r}=-r\omega ^{*2}\cos \omega^{*}te_{\xi } -r\omega ^{*2}\sin \omega^{*}te_{\eta }+\dot{v}e_{\zeta } \) ,

and by considering \(\omega +\omega ^{*} =\omega _{2} \) we obtain

\(\underline{\underline{v_{P}=-r\omega _{2}\sin \omega ^{*}te_{\xi }+\left(a\omega +r\omega_{2}\cos \omega^{*}t \right)e_{ \eta }+ve_{\zeta } } } \) ,

\(\underline{\underline{a_{P}=-\left(a\omega^{2}+r\omega^{2}_{2} \cos \omega ^{*} t \right)e_{\xi }-r\omega ^{2}_{2}\sin \omega ^{*} te_{\eta } +\dot{v}e_{\zeta } } } \) .

c) For the \(\xi^{*} \eta^{*} \zeta^{*} \) system we have

\(\omega =\omega _{2} e^{*}_{\zeta } \) , \(\dot{\omega }=0 \) ,

\(r_{0}=a\cos \omega ^{*}te^{*}_{\xi }-a\sin \omega ^{*} te_{\eta } +be^{*}_{\zeta } \) ,

\(\dot{r}_{0} =-a\omega ^{*} \sin \omega ^{*}te^{*}_{\xi }-a\omega ^{*} \cos \omega ^{*} te_{\eta }\) ,

\(\ddot{r}_{0} =-a\omega ^{*2} \cos \omega ^{*}te^{*}_{\xi }+a\omega ^{*2} \cos \omega ^{*} te_{\eta }\) ,

\(r_{0P}=re^{*}_{\xi }+se^{*}_{\zeta }\) ,

\(v_{r}=ve^{*}_{\zeta } \) ,

\(\omega \times r_{0P}=r\omega _{2} e^{*}_{\eta }\)

 

\(\omega \times \left(\omega \times r_{0P}\right) =-r\omega^{2}_{2} e^{*}_{\xi } \) ,

\(2\omega \times v_{r} =0 \) .

Thus, it follows

\(\underline{\underline{v_{P}=-a\omega ^{*}\sin \omega ^{*}te^{*}_{\xi }-\left(a\omega ^{*}\cos \omega ^{*}t+r\omega _{2} \right)e^{*}_{\eta } +ve^{*}_{\zeta } } } \) ,

\(\underline{\underline{a_{P}=-\left(a\omega ^{*2}\cos \omega ^{*}t+r\omega ^{2}_{2}\right)e^{*}_{\xi }+a\omega ^{*2}\sin \omega ^{*} te^{*}_{\eta } +\dot{v}e^{*}_{\zeta } } } \) .
