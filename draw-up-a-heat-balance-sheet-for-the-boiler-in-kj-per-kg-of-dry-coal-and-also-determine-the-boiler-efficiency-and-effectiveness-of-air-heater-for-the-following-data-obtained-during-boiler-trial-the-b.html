Guest Question.

Draw up a heat balance sheet for the boiler in kJ per kg of dry coal and also determine the boiler efficiency and effectiveness of air heater for the following data obtained during boiler trial. The boiler has economiser and air preheater as two accessories in it.
Atmospheric air temperature: 15°C
Steam generation: 40 bar, 400°C
Steam generated per kg of coal = 8 kg
Feed water temperature at inlet to economiser = 27°C
Feed water temperature at exit of economiser = 137°C
Moisture in coal burnt = 1.5%
Flue gas temperature entering air heater =300°C
Flue gas temperature leaving air heater and entering chimney = 150°C
Temperature of air entering boiler furnace = 120°C
Dry coal composition by mass = 84% C, 4% \(\textbf{H}_{2}\), 7% \(\textbf{O}_{2}\) and remainder ash
Dry flue gas composition by volume = 12.5% \(\textbf{CO}_{2}\), 7.5% \(\textbf{O}_{2}\), 80% \(\textbf{N}_{2}\)
Datum temperature = 15°C
Calorific value of coal = 32600 kJ/kg
For air and dry flue gas, \(\textbf{c}_{p}\) =1.0032 kJ/kg . K
Partial pressure of vapour in flue gas = 0.075 bar
Specific pressure of vapour = 2.0064 kJ/kg . K
Determine the boiler efficiency and the efficiency of heat exchange in air heater.
Also prepare heat balance sheet in kJ per kg of dry coal.

The dry coal is given to have C as 0.84 kg per kg of coal.
\(\begin{aligned}\text{Therefore, the mass of dry flue gas per kg of coal}&= \frac{\text{carbon present per kg of coal}}{\text{carbon present per kg of dry flue gas}}\\&= \frac{0.84}{0.0495} = \text{16.97 kg dry flue gas per kg of coal}\end{aligned}\\\text{For the given coal composition the H}_{2}\text{O produced during combustion = 0.04 × 9 = 0.36}\\\text{Given coal has 0.05 kg ash per kg of coal.}\\\text{Amount of air supplied for combustion of one kg of dry coal}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad \qquad &= 16.97 – (1 – \text{ash content} – H_{2}O \text{ formed})\\&= 16.97 – (1 – 0.05 – 0.36)\\&= 16.38 kg\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Moisture per kg of dry coal}&= \frac{\text{Moisture in coal burnt per kg of coal}}{\text{Mass of dry coal per kg of coal fired}}\\&= \frac{0.015}{(1 – 0.015)}\\&= 0.0152 kg\end{aligned}\\\text{Hence, the total moisture per kg of dry coal = 0.36 + 0.0152 = 0.3752 kg}\\\text{Steam generated per kg of dry coal} = \frac{8}{(1 – 0.015)} = \text{8.12 kg steam}\\\text{From steam tables}\\\text{Enthalpy of steam generated = }h_{\text{at 40 bar, 400°C}} = 3213.6 kJ/kg\\\text{At partial pressure of vapour i.e. 0.075 bar, saturation temperature is 40.29°C and }h_{\text{g at 0.075 bar}} = 1168.79 kJ/kg\\\text{For the given datum temperature of 15°C the heat available with steam, dry flue gas, moisture in flue gas, }\\\text{feed water etc. can be estimated as under.}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Heat available with steam}&= 8.12 (3213.6 – (4.18 × 15)) = 25585.31 kJ\\\text{Heat carried by dry flue gases}&= (16.97 × 1.0032 × (150 – 15)\\&= 2298.28 kJ\\\text{Heat carried by moisture in flue gas}&\\&= 0.3752 × \left\{(168.79 – (4.18 × 15)) + 2.0064 × (150 – 40.29) \right\} \\&= 122.39 kJ\\\text{Heat available with feed water}&= 8.12 × (27 – 15) × 4.18 = 407.29 kJ\\\text{Heat available with one kg of coal}&= 32600 kJ/kg\end{aligned}\\\text{The heat balance sheet may be prepared for datum state of 15°C as under per kg of dry coal.}\)

\(\text{Boiler efficiency can be obtained as under,}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Heat utilized by steam per kg of coal}&= 8.12 (3213.6 – (4.18 × 27))\\&= 25178.01 kJ\\\text{Heat supplied by fuel}&= \text{32600 kJ/kg coal}\\\text{Boiler efficiency}&= \frac{25178.01}{32600} = 77.23\%\end{aligned}\\\text{For getting the efficiency of heat exchange in air heater the accounting may be made for total heat available and heat utilized.}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Heat utilized by air}&= 16.38 × 1.0032 × (120 – 15)\\&= \text{1725.4 kJ/kg coal}\end{aligned}\\\text{Heat available in air heater = Heat available with dry flue gas + Heat supplied by the moisture.}\\= \quad \left\{16.97 × 1.0032 × (300 – 137)\right\} + \left\{0.3752 × 2.0064 × (300 – 137)\right\} \\= \quad \text{2897.67 kJ/kg coal}\\\text{Efficiency of heat exchange in air heater} = \frac{1725.4}{2897.67} = 59.54\%\\\begin{aligned}\text{Boiler efficiency}&= 86.75\%\\\text{Efficiency of heat exchange in air heater}&= 59.54\%\end{aligned}\)
