Guest Question.

The inlet of a centrifugal pump of the type shown in Figure 7.4 is to be designed for optimum conditions when the flow rate of water is \(25 dm^{3}/s\) and the impeller rotational speed is 1450 rev/min. The maximum suction specific speed \(\Omega _{ss}=3.0 \) (rad) and the inlet eye radius ratio is to be 0.3. Determine
a. the blade cavitation coefficient;
b. the shroud diameter at the eye;
c. the eye axial velocity;
d. the NPSH.

Admin
Solution

a. From Eq. (7.9c), squaring both sides,

\(\Omega^{2}_{ss}=\frac{2\pi k\left(2/3\right)^{1.5}}{\sigma _{b} \left(1+\sigma _{b}\right) ^{0.5} } =\frac{3.420k}{\sigma _{b} \left(1+\sigma _{b}\right) ^{0.5} } \) (7.9c)

 

\(\sigma ^{2}_{b}\left(1+\sigma _{b}\right)=\left(3.42k\right) ^{2}/ \Omega^{4}_{ss}=0.1196\)

with \(k=1-\left(r_{h1}/r_{s1}\right) ^{2}=1-0.3^{2} =0.91\). Solving iteratively (e.g., using the Newton–Raphson approximation), we get \(\sigma _{b}=0.3030.\)

b. As \(Q=\pi kr^{2}_{s1}c_{x1} \) and \(c_{x1}=\phi r_{s1}\Omega , r^{3}_{s1}=Q/\left(\pi k\Omega \phi \right) \) and, hence, \(\Omega=1450\pi /30=151.84 rad/s.\)

From Eq. (7.9a),

\(\phi =\left\{\frac{\sigma _{b}}{2\left(1+\sigma _{b}\right)} \right\} ^{1/2} \) (7.9a)

 

\(\phi=\left[0.303/\left(2\times 1.303\right) \right] ^{0.5} =0.3410\)

\(r^{3}_{s1}=0.025/\left(\pi \times 0.91\times 151.84\times 0.341\right) =1.689\times 10^{-4} \)

\(r_{s1}=0.05528 m\)

The required diameter of the eye is 110.6 mm.

c. \(c_{x1}=\phi \phi \Omega r_{s1}=0.341\times 151.84\times 0.05528=2.862 m/s\)

d. From Eq. (7.9b), the NPSH is

 

\(gH_{s}=\frac{3}{2} \sigma _{b}\left(\frac{1}{2} U^{2}_{s1} \right)\) (7.9b)

 

\(H_{s}=\frac{0.75\sigma _{b}c^{2}_{x1}}{g\phi ^{2}} =\frac{0.75\times 0.303\times 2.862^{2}}{9.81\times 0.341^{2}}=1.632 m\)
