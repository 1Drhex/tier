Guest Question.

Develop a computer program that will yield values of \(p / p ^{*}, T / T ^{*}, T _{ o } / T _{ o }^{*}\) , and \(p _{ o } / p _{ o }^{*}\) for Rayleigh line flow with the working fluid consisting of a perfect gas with constant γ = 1.36. Use Mach number increments of 0.10 over the range M = 0 to M = 2.5.

Admin
Solution

The governing relations are

 

\(\frac{ p }{ p ^*}=\frac{1+\gamma}{1+\gamma M ^{2}}\) (10.11)

 

\(\frac{ T }{ T ^*}=\frac{(1+\gamma)^{2} M ^{2}}{\left(1+\gamma M ^{2}\right)^{2}}\) (10.12)

 

\(\frac{ T _{ o }}{ T _{ o }^{*}}=\frac{(1+\gamma) M ^{2}\left[2+(\gamma-1) M ^{2}\right]}{\left(1+\gamma M ^{2}\right)^{2}}\) (10.14)

 

\(\frac{ p _{ o }}{ p _{ o }^{*}}=\left(\frac{1+\gamma M ^{2}}{1+\gamma}\right)\left[\frac{2+(\gamma-1) M ^{2}}{\gamma+1}\right]^{\gamma /(\gamma-1)}\) (10.15)
