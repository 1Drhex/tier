Guest Question.

Suppose you deposit $1,000 in a bank savings account that pays interest at a rate of 8% per year. Assume that you do not withdraw the interest earned at the end of each period (year) but instead let it accumulate. (1) How much would you have at the end of year 3 with simple interest? (2) How much would you have at the end of year 3 with compound interest?

Given: P = $1,000, N = 3 years, and i = 8% per year.
Find: F .

With a beginning principal amount of $1,166.40 plus the $93.31 interest, the total balance would be $1,259.71 at the end of year 3.
