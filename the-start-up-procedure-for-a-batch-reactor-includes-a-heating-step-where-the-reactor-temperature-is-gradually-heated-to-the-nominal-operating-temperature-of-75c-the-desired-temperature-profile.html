The desired temperature profile T(t) is shown in Fig. E3.5. What is T(s)? Solution
\(T(t)=20 S(t)+\frac{55}{30} t S(t)-\frac{55}{30}(t-30) S(t-30)\)

 

\(T(s)=\frac{20}{s}+\frac{55}{30} \frac{1}{s^{2}}-\frac{55}{30} \frac{1}{s^{2}} e^{-30 s}=\frac{20}{s}+\frac{55}{30} \frac{1}{s^{2}}\left(1-e^{-30 s}\right)\)
