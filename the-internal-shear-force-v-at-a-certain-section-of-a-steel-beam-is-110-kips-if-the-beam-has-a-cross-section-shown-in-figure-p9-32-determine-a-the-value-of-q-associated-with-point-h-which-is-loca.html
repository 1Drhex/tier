Guest Question.

The internal shear force V at a certain section of a steel beam is 110 kips. If the beam has a cross section shown in Figure P9.32, determine:
(a) the value of Q associated with point H, which is located 2 in. below the top surface of the flanged shape.
(b) the maximum horizontal shear stress in the flanged shape.

Admin
Solution

Centroid location in y direction:

\(\begin{array}{lccc}{\text { Shape }} & \begin{array}{c}\text { Area } A_{i} \\\left( in .^{2}\right)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}y_{i} \text { (from bottom) } \\( in .)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}y_{i} A_{i} \\\left( in .{ }^{3}\right)\end{array} \\\hline \text { top flange } & 5.0 & 11.5 & 57.5 \\\text { web } & 10.0 & 6.0 & 60.0 \\\text { bottom flange } & 8.0 & 0.5 & 4.0 \\\hline & 23.0 in ^{2} & & 121.5 in .{ }^{3}\end{array}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{y}=\frac{\Sigma y_{i} A_{i}}{\Sigma A_{i}}=\frac{121.5 in .^{3}}{23.0 in .^{2}} &=5.2826 in . \text{(from bottom of shape to centroid) } \\&=6.7174 in. \text{(from top of shape to centroid) }\end{aligned}\)

 

Moment of inertia about the z axis:

\(\begin{aligned}&\begin{array}{lcccc}{\text { Shape }} & \begin{array}{c}I_{C} \\\left( in .{ }^{4}\right)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}d=y_{i}-\bar{y} \\\text { (in.) }\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}d^{2} A \\\left( in .{ }^{4}\right)\end{array} & \begin{array}{c}I_{C}+d^{2} A \\\left( in .{ }^{4}\right)\end{array} \\\hline \text { top flange } & 0.4167 & 6.2174 & 193.2798 & 193.6964 \\\text { web } & 83.3333 & 0.7174 & 5.1465 & 88.4798 \\\text { bottom flange } & 0.6667 & -4.7826 & 182.9868 & 183.6534 \\\hline\end{array}\\&\text { Moment of inertia about the } z \text { axis }\left(\text { in. }^{4}\right)=465.8297\end{aligned}\)

 

(a) Q at point H:

\(Q=(5 in .)(1 in. )(6.2174 in .)+(1 in .)(1 in .)(5.2174 in .)=36.3044 in .^{3}\)

(b) Maximum horizontal shear stress:
At neutral axis:

\(\begin{aligned}&Q_{\max }=(5 in. )(1 in. )(6.2174 in .)+(1 in. )(5.7174 in. )(2.8587 in. )=47.4313 in .{ }^{3} \\&\tau_{\max }=\frac{(110 kips )\left(47.4313 in .{ }^{3}\right)}{\left(465.8297 in. ^{4}\right)(1 in. )}=11.20 ksi\end{aligned}\)
