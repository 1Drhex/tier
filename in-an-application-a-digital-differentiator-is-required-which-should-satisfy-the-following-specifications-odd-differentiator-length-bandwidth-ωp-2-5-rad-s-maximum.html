Guest Question.

In an application, a digital differentiator is required which should satisfy the following specifications:

• Odd differentiator length

• Bandwidth \(\omega_{p}: 2.5\) rad/s

• Maximum passband ripple \(\delta_{p}: 1.0 \times 10^{-6}\)

• Sampling frequency \(\omega_{s}: 2 \pi\) rad/s

Design the lowest-order differentiator that will satisfy the specifications.

Admin
