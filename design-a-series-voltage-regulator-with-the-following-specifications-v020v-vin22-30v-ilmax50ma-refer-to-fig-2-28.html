Guest Question.

Design a series voltage regulator with the following specifications:

\(V_{0}=20V;V_{in}=(22-30)V;I_{L(max)}=50mA \)

Refer to Fig. 2.28.

Admin
Solution

Selection of Zener diode

\(R_{Lmin}=\frac{V_{0} }{I_{L(max)}}=\frac{20}{50\times 10^{-3} }=400\Omega \)

 

\(V_{z} = \frac{V_{0} }{2} =\frac{20}{2}=10V \)

 

Hence, the zener diode 0.5Z10 is chosen.

 

Since \(I_{R1} \gt I_{B2} ,I_{R1} \gt \frac{I_{C2} }{\beta },I_{R1} \gt \frac{10\times 10^{-3} }{150} \)

 

\(I_{R1} \gt 66.7\mu A\)

 

Let \(I_{R1} = I_{R2} = I_{R3} =10mA\) (neglecting \(I_{B2}\))

 

Let \(I_{C2} = I_{E2}=10mA\)

 

So, the current flowing through the Zener,

\(I_{z} =I_{E2}+I_{R1}=20mA\)

 

\(P_{z} =V_{z} I_{z} =10\times 20\times 10^{-3}\)

 

\(=0.2W\lt 0.5W\)

 

Hence selection of 0.5Z10 Zener diode is confirmed.

 

Selection of transistor \(Q_{1}\)

 

\(I_{E1} =I_{R1} +I_{R2} +I_{L} \)

 

\(=(10+10+50)mA=70mA\)

 

\(V_{i(max)} -V_{0}=30-20=10V \)

 

For transistor SL100, the ratings are

\(I_{C(max)} =500mA\)

 

\(V_{CE(max)}=50V\)

 

\(h_{FE}=50-280\)

 

Hence, SL100 can be chosen for \(Q_{1}\).

Selection of transistor \(Q_{2}\)

From the figure, \(V_{CE2(max)}+V_{z}=\left(V_{0}+V_{BE1} \right)\)

 

Therefore, \(V_{CE2(max)}=\left(V_{0}+V_{BE1} \right)-V_{z} =20.6-10=10.6V\)

 

For transistor BC107, the ratings are

\(V_{CEO(max)}=45V\)

 

\(I_{C(max)} =200mA\)

 

\(h_{FE}=125-300 \)

 

Hence, transistor BC107 is selected for \(Q_{2}\).

Selection of resistors \(R_{1}\),\(R_{2}\) and \(R_{3}\)

\(V_{R1}=V_{0}-V_{z}=20-10=10V \)

 

\(R_{1} =\frac{V_{R1}}{I_{R1}} =\frac{10}{10\times 10^{-3} } =1k\Omega \)

 

\(V_{R2}=V_{0}-V_{R3}=20-10.6=9.4V\)

 

\(R_{2} =\frac{V_{R2}}{I_{R2}} =\frac{9.4}{10\times 10^{-3} } =940\Omega \)

 

\(V_{R3}=V_{z}-V_{BE2(sat)}\)

 

\(=10+0.6=10.6V \)

 

\(R_{3} =\frac{V_{R3}}{I_{R3}} =\frac{10.6}{10\times 10^{-3} } =1060\Omega \)

 

Selection of resistor \(R_{4}\)

\(V_{B1}=V_{C2}=V_{0}+ V_{BE1}=20+0.6=20.6V \)

 

\(I_{B1}=\frac{I_{C1} }{\beta } =\frac{70\times 10^{-3} }{50}=1.4mA \)

 

\(I_{R4}=I_{B1}+I_{C2} =11.4mA\)

 

\(R_{4(max)} =\frac{V_{R4(max)}}{I_{R4}} = \frac{V_{i(max)}-V_{B1} }{I_{R4}}\)

 

\(=\frac{30-20.6 }{11.4\times 10^{-3} }=825\Omega \)

 

\(R_{4(min)} =\frac{V_{R4(min)}}{I_{R4}} = \frac{V_{i(min)}-V_{B1} }{I_{R4}}\)

 

\(=\frac{22-20.6 }{11.4\times 10^{-3} }=123\Omega \)

 

\(R_{4} =\frac{R_{4(max)}+R_{4(min)}}{2} =474\Omega \)
