Guest Question.

A for stroke gasoline engine has a compression ratio of 10:1 with 4 cylinders of total displacement 75 \(in ^{3}\). the inlet state is 500 R, 10 psia and the engine is running at 2100 RPM with the fuel adding 600 Btu/lbm in the combustion process. What is the net work in the cycle and how much power is produced?

Admin
Solution

Overall cycle efficiency is from Eq.10.12, \(r_{v}=v_{1} / v_{2}\)

\(\begin{array}{r}\eta_{ TH }=1- r _{ v }^{1- k }=1-10^{-0.4}= 0 . 6 0 2 \\w _{\text {net }}=\eta_{ TH } \times q _{ H }=0.602 \times 600=361.2 Btu / lbm\end{array}\)

We also need specific volume to evaluate Eqs.10.9 to 10.11

\(\begin{aligned}& v _{1}= RT _{1} / P _{1}=53.34 \times 500 /(10 \times 144)=18.52 ft ^{3} / lbm \\& P _{ meff }=\frac{ w _{ net }}{ v _{1}- v _{2}}=\frac{ w _{ net }}{ v _{1}\left(1-\frac{1}{ r _{ v }}\right)}=\frac{361.2}{18.52 \times 0.9} \frac{778}{144}=117.1 psia\end{aligned}\)

Now we can find the power from Eq.10.11

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ W } &= P _{ meff } V _{ displ } \frac{ RPM }{60} \frac{1}{2}=117.1 \times \frac{75}{12} \times \frac{2100}{60} \times \frac{1}{2}= 1 2 8 0 8 lbf-ft / s \\&= 2 3 h p\end{aligned}\)

Recall 1 hp = 550 lbf-ft/s.

Eq.10.9 : \(w_{ net }=\oint P d v \equiv P_{ meff }\left(v_{\max }-v_{\min }\right)\)

Eq. 10.11 : \(\dot{W}=N_{ cyl } m w_{ net } \frac{ RPM }{60}=P_{ meff } V_{ displ } \frac{ RPM }{60}\)

Eq.10.12 : \(\eta_{\text {th }}=1-\frac{T_{1}}{T_{2}}=1-\left(r_{v}\right)^{1-k}=1-\frac{1}{r_{v}^{k-1}}\)
