Guest Question.

Compute \(p_{2}(\mathbf{x})\) for \(\mathbf{x}_{0}=\mathbf{0}\) and \(f(\mathbf{x})=e^{x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}}\).

Admin
Solution

\(f\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)=e^{0}=1\). The second term in (19) is

\(\begin{aligned}\mathbf{f}(\mathbf{x})=& f\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)+\sum_{i=1}^{n} f_{i}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)\left(x_{i}-x_{i}^{(0)}\right)+\frac{1}{2 !} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_{i j}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)\left(x_{i}-x_{i}^{(0)}\right)\left(x_{j}-x_{j}^{(0)}\right) \\&+\frac{1}{3 !} \sum_{i, j, k=1}^{n} f_{i j k}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)\left(x_{i}-x_{i}^{(0)}\right)\left(x_{j}-x_{j}^{(0)}\right)\left(x_{k}-x_{k}^{(0)}\right)+\cdots \\&+\frac{1}{m !} \sum_{i_{1}, i_{2}, \ldots, i_{m}=1}^{n} f_{i_{1} i_{2} \cdots i_{m}}\left(\mathbf{x}_{0}\right)\left(x_{i_{1}}-x_{i_{1}}^{(0)}\right)\left(x_{i_{2}}-x_{i_{2}}^{(0)}\right) \cdots\left(x_{i_{m}}-x_{i_{m}}^{(0)}\right)+R_{m}(\mathbf{x})\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\sum_{i=1}^{n} f_{i}(0) x_{i}\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad (28)\)

Now \(f_{i}(x)=e^{x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}}\), so that \(f_{i}(\mathbf{0})=1\) and (28) becomes

\(\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i}=x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\).

The third term in (19) is

\(\frac{1}{2 !} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_{i j}(\mathbf{0}) x_{i} x_{j}\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad (29)\)

Again \(f_{i j}(0)=1\) and (29) becomes

\(\frac{1}{2 !} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sum_{j=1}^{n} x_{i} x_{j}=\frac{1}{2 !} \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i} \sum_{j=1}^{n} x_{j}=\frac{1}{2 !}\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i}\right)^{2}=\frac{1}{2 !}\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right)^{2}\).

Thus

\(p_{2}(\mathbf{x})=1+\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right)+\frac{1}{2 !}\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right)^{2}\)

We can obtain this answer immediately from formula (4),

\(e^{x} \approx 1+x+\frac{x^{2}}{2 !}+\frac{x^{3}}{3 !}+\cdots+\frac{x^{n}}{n !}\)

 

for if \(x=x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+ x_{n}\), then

\(\begin{aligned}e^{x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n} \approx} & 1+\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right) \\&+\frac{\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right)^{2}}{2 !}+\cdots+\frac{\left(x_{1}+x_{2}+\cdots+x_{n}\right)^{k}}{k !} .\end{aligned}\)
