Guest Question.

A four wheel trolley car of total mass 2000 kg running on rails of 1 m gauge, rounds a curve of 30 m radius at 45 km/h. The track is banked at 10째. The wheels have an external diameter of 0.6 m and each pair of an axle has a mass of 250 kg. The radius of gyration of each pair is 250 mm. The height of C.G. of the car above the wheel base is 1 m. Allowing for centrifugal force and gyroscopic couple action, determine the pressure on each rail.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } M=2000 kg , a=1 m , R=30 m , v=45 km / h \text { or } 12.5 m / s , \theta=10^{\circ} \),

\( d_{w}=0.6 m , M _{w}=250 kg , K_{w}=250 mm , h=1 m \).

\( R_{A}+R_{B}= M g \cos \theta+\frac{M v^{2} \sin \theta}{R} \).

\( =2000 \times 9.81 \times \cos 10^{\circ}+\frac{2000 \times(12.5)^{2} \times \sin 10^{\circ}}{30} \).

= 21131 N.

Refer to fig. 13.14.
Taking moments about B, we have

\( R_{A} \times a=\left(M g \cos \theta+\frac{M v^{2} \sin \theta}{R}\right) \times \frac{a}{2}+\left(M g \sin \theta-\frac{M v^{2} \cos \theta}{R}\right) \times h \).

\( R_{A}=3714 N, \quad R_{B}=17417 N \).

\( \omega_{w}=\frac{v}{r_{w}}=\frac{12.5}{0.3}=41.67 rad / s \).

\( \omega_{p}=\frac{v}{R}=\frac{12.5}{30}=0.4167 rad / s \).

\( C=I \omega_{w} \cos \theta \omega_{p} \).

\( =M_{w} K_{w}^{2} w_{w} \cos \theta \omega_{p} \).

\( =272.3 N m \).

Force at each pair of wheels on inner wheels on rail due to gyroscopic couple,

\( P=\frac{C}{a}=\frac{272.3}{1}=272.3 N \).

\( \text { Total pressure on inner wheels, } P_{i}=R_{A}-P=3441.7 N \).

\( \text { Total pressure on outer wheels, } P_{o}=R_{B}+P=17689.3 N \).
