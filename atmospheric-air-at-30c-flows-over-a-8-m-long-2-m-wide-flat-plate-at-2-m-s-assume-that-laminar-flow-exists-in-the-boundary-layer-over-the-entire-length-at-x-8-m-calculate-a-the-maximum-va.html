Guest Question.

Atmospheric air at 30째C flows over a 8-m-long, 2-m-wide flat plate at 2 m/s. Assume that laminar flow exists in the boundary layer over the entire length. At x = 8 m, calculate (a) the maximum value of v, (b) the wall shear, and (c) the flow rate through the layer. (d) Also, calculate the drag force on the plate.

Admin
Solution

(a) The \(y\) -component of velocity has been assumed to be small in boundary-layer theory. Its maximum value at \(x=8 \mathrm{~m}\) is found, using \(8.6 .51\), to be

\(\begin{aligned}u &=\frac{\partial \psi}{\partial y}=U_{\infty} F^{\prime}(\eta) \\v &=-\frac{\partial \psi}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\frac{\nu U_{\infty}}{x}}\left(\eta F^{\prime}-F\right)\end{aligned} (8.6.51)\)

\(\begin{aligned}v &=\sqrt{\frac{\nu U_{\infty}}{x}} \times \frac{1}{2}\left(\eta F^{\prime}-F\right) \\&=\sqrt{\frac{1.6 \times 10^{-5} \times 2}{8}} \times 0.86=0.00172 \mathrm{~m} / \mathrm{s}\end{aligned}\)

where \(0.86\) comes from Table 8.5. Compare \(v\) with \(U_{\infty}=2 \mathrm{~m} / \mathrm{s}\).

(b) The wall shear at \(x=8 \mathrm{~m}\) is found using Eq. \(8.6 .56\) to be

\(\tau_{0}=\left.\mu \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\right|_{y=0}=0.332 \rho U_{\infty}^{2} \sqrt{\frac{\nu}{x U_{\infty}}} (8.6.56)\)

\(\begin{aligned}\tau_{0} &=0.332 \rho U_{\infty}^{2} \sqrt{\frac{\nu}{U_{\infty} x}} \\&=0.332 \times 1.16 \mathrm{~kg} / \mathrm{m}^{3} \times 2^{2} \mathrm{~m}^{2} / \mathrm{s}^{2} \sqrt{\frac{1.6 \times 10^{-5} \mathrm{~m}^{2} / \mathrm{s}}{2 \mathrm{~m}^{2} / \mathrm{s} \times 8 \mathrm{~m}}} \\&=0.00154 \mathrm{~Pa}\end{aligned}\)

(c) The flow rate through the boundary layer at \(x=8 \mathrm{~m}\) is given by

\(Q=\int_{0}^{\delta} u \times w d y=w \sqrt{\frac{\nu x}{U_{\infty}}} \int_{0}^{5} U_{\infty} F^{\prime} d \eta\)

where we have substituted for \(u\) and \(y\) from Eqs. 8.6.51 and 8.6.48. Recognizing that \(\int F^{\prime} d \eta=F\), the flow rate is

\(\xi=x \quad \eta=y \sqrt{\frac{U_{\infty}}{\nu x}} (8.6.48)\)

\(\begin{aligned}Q &=w U_{\infty} \sqrt{\frac{\nu x}{U_{\infty}}}\left[F(5)-F(0)]\right.\\&=2 \mathrm{~m} \times 2 \mathrm{~m} / \mathrm{s} \sqrt{\frac{1.6 \times 10^{-5} \mathrm{~m}^{2} / \mathrm{s} \times 8 \mathrm{~m}}{2 \mathrm{~m} / \mathrm{s}}} \times 3.28=0.105 \mathrm{~m}^{3} . \mathrm{s}\end{aligned}\)

(d) The drag force is determined to be

\(\begin{aligned}F_{D} &=\frac{1}{2} \rho U_{\infty}^{2} L w C_{f} \\&=\frac{1}{2} \times 1.16 \mathrm{~kg} / \mathrm{m}^{3} \times 2^{2} \mathrm{~m}^{2} / \mathrm{s}^{2} \times 8 \mathrm{~m} \times 2 \mathrm{~m} \times \frac{1.33}{\sqrt{2 \times 8 / 1.6 \times 10^{-5}}} \\&=0.049 \mathrm{~N}\end{aligned}\)
