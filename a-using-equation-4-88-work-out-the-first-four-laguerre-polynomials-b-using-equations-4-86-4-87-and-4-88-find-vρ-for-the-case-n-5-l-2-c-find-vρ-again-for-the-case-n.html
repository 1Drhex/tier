Guest Question.

(a) Using Equation 4.88, work out the first four Laguerre polynomials.

(b) Using Equations 4.86, 4.87, and 4.88, find v(ρ), for the case n = 5 , l = 2.

(c) Find v(ρ) again (for the case n = 5 , l = 2) , but this time get it from the recursion formula (Equation 4.76).

\( L_{q}(x) \equiv \frac{e^{x}}{q !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{q}\left(e^{-x} x^{q}\right) \) (4.88).

\( v(\rho)=L_{n-\ell-1}^{2 \ell+1}(2 \rho) \) (4.86).

\( L_{q}^{p}(x) \equiv(-1)^{p}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{p} L_{p+q}(x) \) (4.87).

\( c_{j+1}=\frac{2(j+\ell+1-n)}{(j+1)(j+2 \ell+2)} c_{j} \) (4.76).

Admin
Solution

(a)

\( L_{0}=e^{x} e^{-x}=1 . \quad L_{1}=e^{x} \frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{-x} x\right)=e^{x}\left[e^{-x}-e^{-x} x\right]=1-x \).

\( L_{2}=\frac{e^{x}}{2}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{2}\left(e^{-x} x^{2}\right)=\frac{e^{x}}{2} \frac{d}{d x}\left(2 x e^{-x}-e^{-x} x^{2}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{2}\left(2 e^{-x}-2 x e^{-x}+e^{-x} x^{2}-2 x e^{-x}\right)=1-2 x+\frac{1}{2} x^{2} \).

\( L_{3}=\frac{e^{x}}{6}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{3}\left(e^{-x} x^{3}\right)=\frac{e^{x}}{6}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{2}\left(-e^{-x} x^{3}+3 x^{2} e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{6} \frac{d}{d x}\left(e^{-x} x^{3}-3 x^{2} e^{-x}-3 x^{2} e^{-x}+6 x e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{6}\left(-e^{-x} x^{3}+3 x^{2} e^{-x}+6 x^{2} e^{-x}-12 x e^{-x}-6 x e^{-x}+6 e^{-x}\right) \).

\( = 1-3 x+\frac{3}{2} x^{2}-\frac{1}{6} x^{3} \).

(b)

\( v(\rho)=L_{2}^{5}(2 \rho) ; \quad L_{2}^{5}(x)=(-1)^{5}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{5} L_{7}(x) \).

\( L_{7}(x)=\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{7}\left(x^{7} e^{-x}\right)=\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{6}\left(7 x^{6} e^{-x}-x^{7} e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{5}\left(42 x^{5} e^{-x}-7 x^{6} e^{-x}-7 x^{6} e^{-x}+x^{7} e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{4}\left(210 x^{4} e^{-x}-42 x^{5} e^{-x}-84 x^{5} e^{-x}+14 x^{6} e^{-x}+7 x^{6} e^{-x}-x^{7} e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{3}\left[840 x^{3} e^{-x}-(210+630) x^{4} e^{-x}\right. \)

\( \left.+(126+126) x^{5} e^{-x}-(21+7) x^{6} e^{-x}+x^{7} e^{-x}\right] \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{2}\left(2520 x^{2} e^{-x}-(840+3360) x^{3} e^{-x}\right. \)

\( \left.+(840+1260) x^{4} e^{-x}-(252+168) x^{5} e^{-x}+(28+7) x^{6} e^{-x}-x^{7} e^{-x}\right) \).

\( =\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)\left[5040 x e^{-x}-(2520+12600) x^{2} e^{-x}+(4200+8400) x^{3} e^{-x}\right. \)

\( \left.-(2100+2100) x^{4} e^{-x}+(420+210) x^{5} e^{-x}-(35+7) x^{6} e^{-x}+x^{7} e^{-x}\right] \).

\( L_{7}(x)=\frac{e^{x}}{7 !}\left[5040 e^{-x}-(5040+30240) x e^{-x}+(15120+37800) x^{2} e^{-x}\right. \) \( -(12600+8400+8400) x^{3} e^{-x}+(2100+2100+3150) x^{4} e^{-x} \)

\( \left.-(630+252) x^{5} e^{-x}+(42+7) x^{6} e^{-x}-x^{7} e^{-x}\right] \).

\( =\frac{1}{7 !}\left(5040-35280 x+52920 x^{2}-29400 x^{3}+7350 x^{4}-882 x^{5}+49 x^{6}-x^{7}\right) \).

\( L_{2}^{5}=-\frac{1}{7 !}\left(\frac{d}{d x}\right)^{5}\left(-882 x^{5}+49 x^{6}-x^{7}\right) \).

\( =-\frac{1}{7 !}\left[-882(5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2)+49(6 \cdot 5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2) x-7 \cdot 6 \cdot 5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 x^{2}\right] \).

\( =\frac{60}{5040}\left[(882 \times 2)-(49 \times 12) x+42 x^{2}\right]=21-7 x+\frac{1}{2} x^{2} \).

\( v(\rho)=21-14 \rho+2 \rho^{2} \).

(c)

Eq. 4.62 \( \Rightarrow v(\rho)=\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_{j} \rho^{j} \).

\( v(\rho)=\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_{j} \rho^{j} \) (4.62).

Eq. 4.76 \( \Rightarrow c_{1}=\frac{2(3-5)}{(1)(6)} c_{0}=-\frac{2}{3} c_{0} \).

\( c_{j+1}=\frac{2(j+\ell+1-n)}{(j+1)(j+2 \ell+2)} c_{j} \) (4.76).

\( c_{2}=\frac{2(4-5)}{(2)(7)} c_{1}=-\frac{1}{7} c_{1}=\frac{2}{21} c_{0} ; \quad c_{3}=\frac{2(5-5)}{(3)(8)} c_{2}=0 \).

\( v(\rho)=c_{0}-\frac{2}{3} c_{0} \rho+\frac{2}{21} c_{0} \rho^{2}=\frac{c_{0}}{21}\left(21-14 \rho+2 \rho^{2}\right) \).
