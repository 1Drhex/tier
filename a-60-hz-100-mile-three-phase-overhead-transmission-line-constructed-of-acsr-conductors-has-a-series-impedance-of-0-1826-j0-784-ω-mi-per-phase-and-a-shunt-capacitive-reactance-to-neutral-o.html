Guest Question.

A 60-Hz, 100-mile, three-phase overhead transmission line, constructed of ACSR conductors, has a series impedance of (0.1826 + j0.784) Ω/mi per phase and a shunt capacitive reactance-to-neutral of 185.5 × \(10^{3}\) ∠-90° Ω-mi per phase. Using the nominal π circuit for a medium-length transmission line, (a) determine the total series impedance and shunt admittance of the line; (b) compute the voltage, the current, and the real and reactive power at the sending end if the load at the receiving end draws 200 MVA at unity power factor and at a line-to-line voltage of 230 kV; and (c) find the percent voltage regulation of the line.

Admin
Solution

(a) The nominal π circuit is shown below:

The total line impedance

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{Z}=(0.1826+j 0.784) 100=& 18.26+j 78.4 \\&=80.5 \angle 76.89^{\circ} \Omega / ph .\end{aligned}\)

The line admittance for 100 mi is

\(\bar{Y}=\frac{1}{X_{C}} \angle 90^{\circ}=\frac{1}{\frac{185.5 \times 10^{3}}{100}} \angle 90^{\circ}=0.5391 \times 10^{-3} \angle 90^{\circ} S / ph .\)

 

(b) \(\bar{V}_{R}=\frac{230}{\sqrt{3}} \angle 0^{\circ}=132.8 \angle 0^{\circ} kV\)

\(\bar{I}_{R}=\frac{200 \times 10^{3}}{\sqrt{3}(230)} \angle 0^{\circ}=502 \angle 0^{\circ} A (\because \text { Unity Power Factor })\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{I}_{Z}=\bar{I}_{R}+\bar{V}_{R}\left(\frac{\bar{Y}}{2}\right) &=502 \angle 0^{\circ}+\left(132,800 \angle 0^{\circ}\right)\left(0.27 \times 10^{-3} \angle 90^{\circ}\right) \\&=502+j 35.86=503.3 \angle 4.09^{\circ} A\end{aligned}\)

 

The sending end voltage

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{S} &=132.8 \angle 0^{\circ}+\left(0.5033 \angle 4.09^{\circ}\right)\left(80.5 \angle 76.89^{\circ}\right) \\&=139.152+j 40.01=144.79 \angle 16.04^{\circ} kV\end{aligned}\)

The line-to-line voltage magnitude at the sending end is \(\sqrt{3}(144.79)=250.784 kV\)

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{I}_{S}=\bar{I}_{Z}+\bar{V}_{S}\left(\frac{\bar{Y}}{2}\right) &=502+j 35.86+\left(144.79 \angle 16.04^{\circ}\right)\left(0.27 \angle 90^{\circ}\right) \\&=491.2+j 73.46=496.7 \angle 8.5^{\circ} A\end{aligned}\)

Sending end power

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{S}_{S(3 \phi)} &=3(144.79)(0.4967) \angle 16.04^{\circ}-8.5^{\circ} \\&=213.88+j 28.31 MVA\end{aligned}\)

So \(P_{S(3 \phi)}=213.88 MW ; Q_{S(3 \phi)}=28.31 MVAR\)

 

(c) Regulation \(=\frac{V_{S}-V_{R}}{V_{R}}=\frac{144.79-132.8}{132.8}=0.09\)
