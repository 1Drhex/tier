\(\left(L_{e}\right)_{i}=\frac{D_{i}}{f_{i}} \Sigma K (11.3.6)\)

\(\bar{R}_{i}=\frac{8 f_{i}\left[L_{i}+\left(L_{e}\right)_{i}\right]}{g \pi^{2} D_{i}^{5}} (11.3.8)\)

Apply Eq. 11.3.11, and the first estimate of W is

\(W=\left(\frac{Q}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} \frac{1}{\sqrt{\bar{R}}_{i}}}\right)^{2} (11.3.11)\)

\(\begin{aligned}W &=\left[\frac{0.020}{\left(7.40 \times 10^{5}\right)^{-1 / 2}+\left(1.38 \times 10^{5}\right)^{-1 / 2}+\left(8.14 \times 10^{4}\right)^{-1 / 2}}\right]^{2} \\&=7.39 \mathrm{~m}\end{aligned}\)

Then with Eq. 11.3.9, estimates of \(Q_{i}\) are made:

\(Q_{i}=\sqrt{\frac{W}{\bar{R}_{i}}} (11.3.9)\)

\(Q_{1}=\left(\frac{7.39}{7.40 \times 10^{5}}\right)^{1 / 2}=0.00316 \mathrm{~m}^{3} / \mathrm{s}\)

\(\begin{array}{l} \\ Q_{2}=\left(\frac{7.39}{1.38 \times 10^{5}}\right)^{1 / 2}=0.00732 \mathrm{~m}^{3} / \mathrm{s} \\Q_{3}=\left(\frac{7.39}{8.14 \times 10^{4}}\right)^{1 / 2}=0.00953 \mathrm{~m}^{3} / \mathrm{s}\end{array}\)

A continuity check is made using Eq. 11.3.4:

\(Q=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{N} Q_{i} (11.3.4)\)

\(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{3} Q_{i}=0.00316+0.00732+0.00953=0.0200 \mathrm{~m}^{3} / \mathrm{s}\)

Even though the sum satisfies the solution, another iteration will be carried out to study the convergence of the solution technique. First, the \(\bar{R}\) -values are updated using Eq. \(11.2 .3\) to evaluate the friction factors.
