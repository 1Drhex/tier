Guest Question.

Find the force and its nature in member AD and BC for given cantilever truss loaded by 40 kN as shown (AB = 4 m and AD = 5 m) in Fig. 7.1(a).

Admin
Solution

(i) For the given cantilever truss, we can start by considering the FBD of joint D. But before this we should workout the geometrical angles.

In Δ ABC By sine rule, we have

\( \frac{AB}{\sin 130°} = \frac{AC}{\sin 30°} \) .

AC = 2.61 m.

In Δ ACD By cosine rule, we have

\( CD = \sqrt{(AC)^2 + (AD)^2 – 2(AC)(AD) \cos 25°} \) .

\( CD = \sqrt{(2.61)^2 + (5)^2 – 2(2.61)(5) \cos 25°} \) .

CD = 2.86 m.

In Δ ACD, by sine rule, we have

\( \frac{CD}{\sin 25°} = \frac{AC}{\sin α} \) .

\( \frac{2.86}{\sin 25°} = \frac{2.61}{\sin α} \) ∴ α = 22.69°.

By geometry α + β = 45° ∴ β = 22.31°.

(ii) Consider the FBD of Joint D

By Lami’s theorem

\( \frac{40}{\sin 22.69°} = \frac{F_{AD}}{\sin (90 + 22.31)°} = \frac{F_{CD}}{\sin (180 + 45)°}\) .

∴ \( F_{AD} = 95.93 \) kN (T).

\( F_{CD} = -73.32 \) kN (Wrong assumed direction).

∴ \( F_{CD} = 73.32 \) kN (C).

\( ∑ F_C = 0 \).

(iii) Consider the FBD of Joint C

By Lami’s theorem

\( \frac{F_{CD}}{\sin 130°} = \frac{F_{BC}}{\sin (20+90+22.31)°} \) .

∴ \( F_{BC} = 70.78 \) kN (C).
