Guest Question.

Figure P6.24 shows a 50-mm-diameter solid shaft made of aluminum [G = 70 GPa] that is subjected to a uniformly distributed torsional loading of t = 4.2 kN-m/m and two concentrated external torques: \(T_{C}\) = 5.0 kN-m and \(T_{D}\) = 2.3 kN-m. Using a = 1.3 m, b = 0.4 m, and c = 0.9 m, calculate:
(a) the angle of twist in shaft segment AB.
(b) the rotation angle \(\phi_{D}\) at the free end of the shaft.

Admin
Solution

Section properties: The polar moment of inertia for the shaft is:

\( J=\frac{\pi}{32}(50 mm )^{4}=613,592.32 mm ^{4} \)

 

Equilibrium

Segment AB

\( \begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{x}=-T &+t(a-x)-T_{C}+T_{D}=0 \\ \\ \therefore T &=t(a-x)-T_{C}+T_{D}=(4.2 kN – m / m )(1.3 m -x)-5.0 kN – m +2.3 kN – m \\ \\ &=-(4.2 kN – m / m ) x+2.76 kN – m\end{aligned} \)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Segment BC

\( \begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{x}=&-T-T_{C}+T_{D}=0 \\ \\ & \therefore T=-T_{C}+T_{D}=-5.0 kN – m +2.3 kN – m =-2.70 kN – m\end{aligned} \)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

Segment CD

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{x}=&-T+T_{D}=0 \\& \therefore T=T_{D}=2.3 kN – m\end{aligned}\)

__________________________________________________________________________________________________

 

(a) Angle of twist in shaft segment AB. The angle of twist can be calculated from:

\( \begin{aligned}\phi &=\int_{0}^{a} \frac{T}{J G} d x \\ \\ &=\frac{1}{J G} \int_{0}^{a}[-(4.2 kN – m / m ) x+2.76 kN – m ] d x \\ \\ &=\frac{1}{J G}\left[-\frac{(4.2 kN – m / m )}{2} x^{2}+(2.76 kN – m ) x\right]_{0}^{a}=-\frac{4.2 kN – m / m }{2 J G} a^{2}+\frac{2.76 kN – m }{J G} a \\ \\ &=-\frac{(4,200 N – m / m )(1,000 mm / m )^{2}}{2\left(613,592.315 mm ^{4}\right)\left(70,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)}(1.3 m )^{2}+\frac{(2,760 N – m )(1,000 mm / m )^{2}}{\left(613,592.315 mm ^{4}\right)\left(70,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)}(1.3 m ) \\ \\ &=-0.082628 rad +0.083536 rad \\ \\ &=908 \times 10^{-6} rad\end{aligned} \)

 

(b) Rotation angle \( \phi_{D}\)
The angle of twist in shaft segment BC is

\( \begin{aligned}&\phi_{B C}=\frac{(-2,700 N – m )(0.4 m )(1,000 mm / m )^{2}}{\left(613,592.315 mm ^{4}\right)\left(70,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)}=-0.025145 rad \\ \\ &\phi_{C D}=\frac{(2,300 N – m )(0.9 m )(1,000 mm / m )^{2}}{\left(613,592.315 mm ^{4}\right)\left(70,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)}=0.048194 rad\end{aligned} \)
