Guest Question.

The building of is fitted with a collector storage wall. All the problem data of apply here with the following additional information about the collector storage wall:

Density = 2200 kg/m3.

Heat capacity = 910 J/kg-°C.

Wall thickness, w = 0.40 m.

Loss coefficient from the wall to the ambient, \(\overline{U}_{o} \) = 4.5 W/m2-°C.

Overall heat transfer coefficient of the wall including glazing, \({U}_{w} \)=2.6 W/m2-°C.

Thermal conductivity of the wall, k = 1.85 W/m-°C.

Estimate the solar fraction for December and the auxiliary energy required for the month.

Admin
Solution

Initially, we need to calculate the loads Lm and Lw. From Eq. (6.45),

\(L_{m}=(UA)_{h}(DD)_{h}=197.3\times 928\times 24\times 3600=15.82\)GJ

As the room temperature is the same as the base temperature, (DD)h = (DD)R.

From Eq. (6.46),

\(L_{w}=U_{w}A_{w}(DD)_{R}=2.6\times 10\times 928\times 24\times 3600=2.08\)GJ

From Eq. (6.50),

\(U_{k}=\frac{h_{i}k}{wh_{i}+k} =\frac{8.33\times 1.85}{0.4\times 8.33+1.85} =2.97 \)W/m2-°C

(note from Chapter 6, Section 6.2.1, hi = 8.33 W/m2-°C). From , \(\overline{R} \)= 1.637. From Eq. (2.107),

\(\overline{R}=\frac{\overline{H}_{t} }{\overline{H}} =\left\lgroup1-\frac{\overline{H}_{D}}{\overline{H}} \right\rgroup \overline{R}_{B}+\left\lgroup\frac{\overline{H}_{D}}{\overline{H}} \right\rgroup \left[\frac{1+\cos (\beta )}{2} \right] +\rho _{G}\left[\frac{1-\cos (\beta )}{2} \right] \)

\(\overline{H}_{t}=\overline{R} \overline{H} =1.637\times 9.1=14.9\) MJ/m2

From Eq. (6.51),

\(\overline{T}_{w}=\frac{\overline{H}_{t}(\overline{\tau \alpha } )+(U_{k}\overline{T}_{R}+\overline{U}_{o}\overline{T}_{a})\Delta t }{(U_{k}+\overline{U}_{o} )\Delta t} \)

\(=\frac{14.9\times 10^6\times 0.76+(2.97\times 18.3+4.5\times 11.1)\times 24\times 3600}{(2.97+4.5)\times 24\times 3600} \)

=31.5°C

Therefore, the total heat transferred into the room through the storage wall is given by Eq. (6.52):

\(Q_{g}=U_{k}A_{w}(\overline{T}_{w}-\overline{T}_{a} )\Delta tN\)

\(=2.97\times 10(31.5-18.3)\times 24\times 3600\times 31\)

=1.05 GJ

From Eq. (11.80),

\(I_{tc}=\frac{1}{(\overline{\tau \alpha } )A_{w}} \left[(UA)\left\lgroup\frac{U_{o}}{U_{k}}+1 \right\rgroup \frac{T_{b-}\overline{T}_{a} }{T_{R}-\overline{T}_{a} }+U_{o}A_{w} \right] (T_{R}-\overline{T}_{a} )\)

\(=\frac{1}{0.76\times 10} \left[145\left\lgroup\frac{4.5}{2.97}+1 \right\rgroup \frac{18.3-11.1}{18.3-11.1}+4.5\times 10 \right] (18.3-11.1)\)

=388.1 W/m2

From Eq. (11.33),

\(\overline{X}_{c} =\frac{I_{tc}}{r_{n}R_{n}\overline{H} } =\frac{388.1\times 3600}{0.171\times 1.208\times 9.1\times 10^6} =0.743\)

From , A = –0.888, B =–0.615, and C = 0.521. From Eq. (11.34),

\(\overline{\Phi } =exp\left\{\left[A+B\left\lgroup\frac{R_{n}}{\overline{R} } \right\rgroup \right]\left[\overline{X}_{c}+C\overline{X^2}_{c} \right] \right\}\)

\(=exp\left\{\left[-0.888-0.615\left\lgroup\frac{1.208}{1.637} \right\rgroup \right]\left[0.743+0.521(0.743)^2\right] \right\}\)

=0.251

From Eq. (11.81),

\(Q_{D}=\frac{U_{k}A_{w}N\overline{S}\overline{\Phi } }{U_{o}+U_{k}} \)

\(=\frac{U_{k}A_{w}N\overline{H}\overline{R}(\overline{\tau \alpha } ) \overline{\Phi } }{U_{o}+U_{k}} \)

\(=\frac{2.97\times 10\times 31\times 9.1\times 10^6\times 1.637\times 0.76\times 0.251}{4.5+2.97} \)

=0.350 GJ

From Eq. (11.82),

\(f_{i}=\frac{L_{w}+Q_{g}}{L_{m}+L_{w}} =\frac{2.08+1.05}{15.82+2.08} =0.175\)

From Eq. (11.85),

\(S_{b}=C_{b}(\Delta T_{b})N=60\times 10^6\times 7\times 31=13.02\) GJ

From Eq. (11.88),

\(S_{w}=\frac{\rho c_{p}\delta ^2Q_{g}}{2k\Delta t} =\frac{2200\times 910\times 0.4^2\times 1.05\times 10^9}{2\times 1.85\times 24\times 3600} =1.052\)GJ

From Eq. (11.89),

\(Y=\frac{S_{b}+0.047S_{w}}{Q_{D}} =\frac{13.02+0.047\times 1.052}{0.350} =37.34\)

From Eq. (11.91b),

\(P=[1-exp(-0.144Y)]^{0.53}=[1-exp(-0.144\times 37.34)]^{0.53}=0.998\)

From Eq. (11.91a),

\(f=min\left\{Pf_{i}+0.88(1-P)[1-exp(-1.26f_{i})],1\right\} \)

\(=min\left\{0.998\times 0.175+0.88(1-0.998)[1-exp(-1.26\times 0.175)],1\right\} \)

\(=min\left\{0.175,1\right\} \)

=0.175

Finally, from Eq. (11.92),

\(Q_{aux}=(1-f)(L_{m}+L_{w})=(1-0.175)\times (15.82+2.08)=14.77\)GJ
