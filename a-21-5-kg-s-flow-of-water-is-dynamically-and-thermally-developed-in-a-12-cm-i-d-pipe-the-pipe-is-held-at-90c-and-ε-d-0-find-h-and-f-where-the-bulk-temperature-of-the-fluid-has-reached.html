Guest Question.

A 21.5 kg/s flow of water is dynamically and thermally developed in a 12 cm I.D. pipe. The pipe is held at 90°C and ε/D = 0. Find h and f where the bulk temperature of the fluid has reached 50°C.

Admin
Solution
\(u_{av}=\frac{\dot{m} }{\rho A_{c}}=\frac{21.5}{977\pi (0.06)^2}=1.946 m/s\)

 

\(Re_{D}=\frac{u_{av}}{v}=\frac{1.946(0.12)}{4.07\times 10^{-7}}=573.700\)

and

\(Pr=2,47 , \frac{\mu _{b}}{\mu _{w}}=\frac{5.38\times 10^{-4}}{3.10\times 10^-4}=1.74\)

From eqn. (7.42)

\(f=\frac{1}{(1.82log_{10}Re_{D}-1.64)}\)

 

\(f = 0.0128 at T_{b}, and since T_{w}> T_{b}, n = 0.11\) in eqn. (7.44).

 

\(Nu_{D}=Nu_{D}\mid_{T _{b}} \left(\frac{\mu _{b}}{\mu _{w}}\right)^n where n =\begin{cases}0.11 for T_{w}\gt T_{b} \\0.25 for T_{w}\lt T_{b}\end{cases}\)

 

Thus, with eqn. (7.41)

 

\(Nu_{D}=\frac{(f/8)Re_{D}Pr}{1.07+12.7\sqrt{f/8}(Pr^{2/3}-1) }\)

we have

\(Nu_{D}=\frac{(0.0128/8)(5.74\times 10^5)(2.47)}{1.07+12.7\sqrt{0.0128/8}(2.47^{2/3}-1) }(1.74)^{0.11}=1617\)

or

\(h=Nu_{D}\frac{k}{D}=1617\frac{0.661}{0.12}=8,907 W/m^2 K\)

 

The corrected friction factor, with eqn. (7.46)

 

\(f=f\mid _{T_{b}}\times K where K=\begin{cases}(7-\mu _{b}/\mu _{w})/6 for T_{w}\gt T_{b} \\(\mu _{b}/\mu _{w})^{-0.24} for T_{w}\lt T_{b}\end{cases}\)

, is

\(f=(0.0128)(7-1.74)/6=0.0122\)
