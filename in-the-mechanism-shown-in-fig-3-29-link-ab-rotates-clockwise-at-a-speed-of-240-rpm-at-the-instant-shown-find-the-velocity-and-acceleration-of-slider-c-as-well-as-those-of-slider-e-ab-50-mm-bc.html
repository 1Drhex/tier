Guest Question.

In the mechanism shown in Fig.3.29, link AB rotates clockwise at a speed of 240 rpm. At the instant shown, find the velocity and acceleration of slider C as well as those of slider E. AB = 50 mm, BC = 120 mm, BD =DC =60 mm, DE=80 mm.

Admin
Solution

Draw configuration diagram shown in Fig.3.29(a).

\( \omega=2 \pi \times 240 / 60=25.13 rad / s \).

\( v_{ d }=\omega \times A B=25.13 \times 50=1256.6 mm / s \).

Draw the velocity diagram as shown in Fig.3.29(b).

\( v_{b}=a b \perp A B \).

\( b c \perp B C \).

\( a c || A C \).

\( a c=v_{c}=5.7 cm =1140 mm / s \).

As CD=DB, hence cd=db=2.3 cm.

\( d e \perp D E \).

\( a e \| Y E \).

\( v_{e}=a e=0.5 cm =100 mm / s \).

\( v_{c b}=b c=4.6 cm =920 mm / s \).

\( v_{e d}=d e=5.3 cm =1060 mm / s \).

The accelerations of various links are;

\( f_{b a}^{n}=v_{b a}^{2} / A B=(1256.6)^{2} / 50=31581 mm / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{c b}^{n}=v_{c b}^{2} / B C=(920)^{2} / 120=7052.3 mm / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{e d}^{n}=v_{e d}^{2} / D E=(1060)^{2} / 80=14045 mm / s ^{2} \).

Draw acceleration diagram as shown in Fig.3.29(c).

\( f_{b a}^{n}=a b \| A B \).

\( f_{c b}^{n}=b c_{1} \| B C \).

\( f_{c b}^{t}=c_{1 c} \perp B C \).

\( a c \| A C \).

Join bc.

\( f_{ c }=a c=4.4 cm =22,000 mm / s ^{2} \).

cd=bd=2.2 cm.

\( f_{e d}^{n}=d e_{1} \| D E \).

\( e e_{1} \perp d e_{1} \).

\( a e \| E Y \).

Join de.

\( f_{e}=a e=0.9 cm =4500 mm / s ^{2} \).
