Guest Question.

A two cylinder locomotive with cranks at 90° has a crank radius of 325 mm. The distance between centres of driving wheels is 1.5 m. The pitch of cylinders is 600 mm. The diameter of treads of driving wheels is 1.8 m. The radius of centres of gravity of balance weights is 650 mm. The pressure due to dead load on each wheel is 40 kN. The weights of reciprocating and rotating parts per cylinder are 4.3 kN and 3 kN respectively. The speed of the locomotive is 60 km/h. Find
(a) the balancing weights both in magnitude and position required to be placed in the planes of driving wheels to balance whole of the revolving and two-third of the reciprocating masses;
(b) the swaying couple;
(c) the variation of tractive effort;
(d) the maximum and minimum pressure on rails; and
(e) what is the maximum speed at which it is possible to run the locomotive, in order that the wheels are not lifted from the rails?

Admin

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=172.25 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=172.25 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=77.5125 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=180.8625 \).

\( \left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=M_{4} r_{4} l_{4} \).

\( \left[(77.5125)^{2}+(180.8625)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=0.975 M_{4} \).

\( M_{4}=\frac{196.772}{0.975}=201.81 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{4}=\frac{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}}{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}} \).

\( =\frac{-(180.8625)}{-(77.5125)}=2.3333 \).

\( \theta_{4}=66.8^{\circ} \text { i.e. } 246.8^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both negative, therefore, } \theta_{4} \text { lies in the third quadrant. } \).

\( M_{1} r_{1}=\left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{4} r_{4} \cos \theta_{4}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{4} r_{4} \sin \theta_{4}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( =\left[(120.574)^{2}+(51.681)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=131.183 \).

\( M_{1}=201.81 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{1}=\frac{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{3} r_{3} \sin \theta_{3}\right)}{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{3} r_{3} \cos \theta_{3}\right)}=\frac{-51.681}{-120.574}=0.4286 \).

\( \omega=\frac{60 \times 1000 \times 2}{3600 \times 1.8}=18.52 rad / s \).

(b) Swaying couple \( =\frac{(1-c) R r \omega^{2}}{\sqrt{2}} \).

\( =\frac{(1-2 / 3) \times 4.3 \times 10^{3} \times 0.325 \times(18.52)^{2} \times 0.6}{\sqrt{2} \times 9.81} \).

\( =5303 N \).

\( \text { (c) Variation of tractive effort }=\sqrt{2}(1-c) R \omega^{2} r \).

\( =\frac{\pm \sqrt{2}(1-2 / 3) \times 4.3 \times 10^{3} \times(18.52)^{2} \times 0.325}{9.81} \).

\( =\pm 17676.8 N \).

\( \text { (d) Balance mass for reciprocating parts, } B=M_{1} \times\left(\frac{2}{3}\right) \times\left(\frac{R}{M+R}\right) \).

\( =201.81 \times\left(\frac{2}{3}\right) \times \frac{4.3}{5.2}=111.25 kg \).

\( \text { Hammer blow }=B b \omega^{2}=111.25 \times 0.65 \times(18.52)^{2}=24.8 kN \).

\( \text { Net pressure on rails }=P \pm B \omega^{2} b \).

\( =40 \pm 24.8 \).

\( =64.8 kN , 15.2 kN \).

\( \text { (e) } 40 \times 10^{3}=111.25 \times \omega^{2} \times 0.65 \).

\( \omega=73.96 rad / s \).

\( v=\frac{73.96 \times 0.9 \times 3600}{1000}=70.164 km / h \).
