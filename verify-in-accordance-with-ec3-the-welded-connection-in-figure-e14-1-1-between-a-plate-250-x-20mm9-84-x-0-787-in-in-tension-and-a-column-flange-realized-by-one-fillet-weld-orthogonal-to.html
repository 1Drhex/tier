Guest Question.

Verify in accordance with EC3, the welded connection in Figure E14.1.1 between a plate 250 × 20mm(9.84 × 0.787 in.) in tension and a column flange, realized by one fillet weld orthogonal to the force axes.
Applied tension force N= 900 kN (202.3 kips)
Steel: S275 \(f_{u}\)= 430 \(N/mm^{2}\) (62.4 ksi) \(\beta_{w}\)=0.85
Fillet length : l = 250 mm (9.84 in.)
Fillet side : d =20 mm (0.787 in.)

Admin
Solution

(1) EC3 directional method
Compute fillet throat dimension, a:
\(a=d/\sqrt{2}=20/\sqrt{2}\)=14 mm(0.551 in.)
\(\sigma _{\perp}=\tau _{\perp}=\frac{N}{l.a}\cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}=\frac{900.10^{3}}{250\times 14}\cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}=181.8N/mm^{2}\)
The verification formulas are:
\(\sqrt{\sigma _{\perp }^{2}+3\left ( \tau _{\perp}^{2}+\tau _{\parallel }^{2} \right )}=\sqrt{181.8^{2}+3\times (181.8^{2}+0^{2})}=363.6 N/mm^{2}\leq \)
\(\leq \frac{f_{u}}{\beta_{w}\cdot \gamma _{M2}}=\frac{430}{0.85\times 1.25}=404.7N/mm^{2}\)OK (52.7 ksi ≤ 58.7 ksi)
\(\sigma _{\perp }=181.8 N/mm^{2}\leq \frac{0.9f_{u}}{\gamma _{M2}}=\frac{0.9\times 430}{1.25}=309.6 N/mm^{2}\)OK (26.4 ksi ≤ 44.9 ksi)
(2) Simplified EC3 method
The verification formula is:
\(F_{w,Ed}\leq F_{w,Rd}=\frac{f_{u}.a}{\sqrt{3}\cdot \beta _{w}\cdot \gamma _{M2}}\)
\(F_{w,Ed}\)=N/l = (900.\(10^{3}\))/250 = 3600N/mm (20.56 kips/in.)
\(F_{w,Ed}=\frac{f_{u}\cdot a}{\sqrt{3}.\beta _{w}}=\frac{430\times 14}{\sqrt{3}\times 0.85\times 1.25}\)= 3271N mm < 3600 N/mm
NOT VERIFIED (18.68 kips/in < 20.56 kips/in.)
As can be noted, in this case the EC3 simplified method is more conservative than the directional method and the weld results are verified with the second method but not with the first one.
