Guest Question.

A pair of parallel helical gears consists of a 20 teeth pinion meshing with a 100 teeth gear. The pinion rotates at 720 rpm. The normal pressure angle is 20°, while the helix angle is 25°. The face width is 40 mm and the normal module is 4 mm. The pinion as well as the gear is made of steel 4OC8 \( \left(S_{u t}=600 N / mm ^{2}\right) \) and heat treated to a surface hardness of 300 BHN. The service factor and the factor of safety are 1.5 and 2 respectively. Assume that the velocity factor accounts for the dynamic load and calculate the power transmitting capacity of gears.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } \quad n_{p}=720 rpm \quad z_{p}=20 \quad z_{g}=100 \).

\( m_{n}=4 mm \quad b=40 mm \quad \psi=25^{\circ} \quad \alpha_{n}=20^{\circ} \).

\( S_{u t}=600 N / mm ^{2} \quad BHN =300 \quad C_{s}=1.5 \quad(f s)=2 \).

Since both gears are made of the same material, the pinion is weaker than the gear.
Step I Beam strength

\( z_{p}^{\prime}=\frac{z_{p}}{\cos ^{3} \psi}=\frac{20}{\cos ^{3}(25)}=26.87 \).

From Table 17.3,

\( Y=0.344+\frac{(0.348-0.344)(26.87-26)}{(27-26)}=0.3475 \).

\( \sigma_{b}=\frac{S_{u t}}{3}=\frac{600}{3}=200 N / mm ^{2} \).

\( S_{b}=m_{n} b \sigma_{b} Y=4(40)(200)(0.3475)=11120 N \).

Step II Wear strength

\( Q=\frac{2 z_{g}}{z_{g}+z_{p}}=\frac{2(100)}{100+20}=1.667 \).

\( d_{p}=\frac{z_{p} m_{n}}{\cos \psi}=\frac{20(4)}{\cos (25)}=88.27 mm \).

\( K=0.16\left(\frac{ BHN }{100}\right)^{2} \).

\( =0.16\left(\frac{300}{100}\right)^{2}=1.44 N / mm ^{2} \).

\( S_{w}=\frac{b Q d_{p} K}{\cos ^{2} \psi}=\frac{40(1.667)(88.27)(1.44)}{\cos ^{2}(25)} \).

= 10 318.58 N
Since wear strength is lower than beam strength, pitting is the criterion of failure.
Step III Tangential force due to rated torque

\( v=\frac{\pi d_{p} n_{p}}{60 \times 10^{3}}=\frac{\pi(88.27)(720)}{60 \times 10^{3}}=3.328 m / s \).

\( C_{v}=\frac{5.6}{5.6+\sqrt{v}}=\frac{5.6}{5.6+\sqrt{3.328}}=0.7543 \).

From Eqs (18.29) and (18.19),

\( K=0.16\left(\frac{ BHN }{100}\right)^{2} \) (18.28).

\( S_{w}=P_{ eff }(f s) \) (18.29).

\( S_{w}=\frac{C_{s} P_{t}(f s)}{C_{v}} \).

or \( 10318.58=\frac{1.5 P_{t}(2)}{0.7543} \).

∴ \( P_{t}=2594.43 N \).

Step IV Power transmitting capacity of gears

\( M_{t}=\frac{P_{t} d_{p}}{2}=\frac{2594.43(88.27)}{2} \).

= 114 505.39 N-mm.

\( k W=\frac{2 \pi n_{p} M_{t}}{60 \times 10^{6}}=\frac{2 \pi(720)(114505.39)}{60 \times 10^{6}}=8.63 \).
