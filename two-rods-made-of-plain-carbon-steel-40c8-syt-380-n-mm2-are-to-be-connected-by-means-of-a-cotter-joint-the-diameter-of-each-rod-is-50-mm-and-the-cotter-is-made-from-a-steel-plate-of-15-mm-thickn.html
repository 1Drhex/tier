Guest Question.

Two rods, made of plain carbon steel 40C8 \( \left(S_{y t}=380 N / mm ^{2}\right) \), are to be connected by means of a cotter joint. The diameter of each rod is 50 mm and the cotter is made from a steel plate of 15 mm thickness. Calculate the dimensions of the socket end making the following assumptions:
(i) the yield strength in compression is twice of the tensile yield strength; and
(ii) the yield strength in shear is 50% of the tensile yield strength.
The factor of safety is 6.

Admin
Solution

Given

\( S_{y t}=380 N / mm ^{2} \quad(f s)=6 \quad t=15 mm \)

d = 50 mm
Step I Permissible stresses

\( \sigma_{c}=\frac{S_{y c}}{(f s)}=\frac{2 S_{y t}}{(f s)}=\frac{2(380)}{6}=126.67 N / mm ^{2} \).

\( \tau=\frac{S_{s y}}{(f s)}=\frac{0.5 S_{y t}}{(f s)}=\frac{0.5(380)}{6}=31.67 N / mm ^{2} \).

\( \sigma_{t}=\frac{S_{y t}}{(f s)}=\frac{380}{6}=63.33 N / mm ^{2} \).

Step II Load acting on rods

\( P=\frac{\pi}{4} d^{2} \sigma_{t} \quad \text { or } \quad P=\frac{\pi}{4}(50)^{2} \).

= 124 348.16 N.

\( \text { Step III Inside diameter of socket }\left(d_{2}\right) \)

From Eq. (4.25b),

\( \sigma_{t}=\frac{P}{\left[\frac{\pi}{4} d_{2}^{2}-d_{2} t\right]} \) or \( P=\left[\frac{\pi}{4} d_{2}^{2}-d_{2} t\right] \sigma_{t} \) (4.25b).

\( P=\left[\frac{\pi}{4} d_{2}^{2}-d_{2} t\right] \sigma_{t} \).

\( 124348.16=\left[\frac{\pi}{4} d_{2}^{2}-d_{2}(15)\right] \).

\( \text { or } \quad d_{2}^{2}-19.1 d_{2}-2500=0 \).

Solving the above quadratic equation,

\( d_{2}=\frac{19.1 \pm \sqrt{19.1^{2}-4(-2500)}}{2} \).

\( \therefore \quad d_{2}=60.45 \text { or } 65 mm \) (i).

\( \text { Step IV Outside diameter of socket }\left(d_{1}\right) \)

From Eq. (4.25d),

\( \sigma_{t}=\frac{P}{\text { area }} \) or \( P=\left[\frac{\pi}{4}\left(d_{1}^{2}-d_{2}^{2}\right)-\left(d_{1}-d_{2}\right) t\right] \sigma_{t} \) (4.25d).

\( P=\left[\frac{\pi}{4}\left(d_{1}^{2}-d_{2}^{2}\right)-\left(d_{1}-d_{2}\right) t\right] \sigma_{t} \).

124 348.16 = \( \left[\frac{\pi}{4}\left(d_{1}^{2}-65^{2}\right)-\left(d_{1}-65\right)(15)\right] \).

\( \text { or } \quad d_{1}^{2}-19.1 d_{1}-5483.59=0 \).

Solving the above quadratic equation,

\( d_{1}=\frac{19.1 \pm \sqrt{19.1^{2}-4(-5483.59)}}{2} \).

\( \therefore \quad d_{1}=84.21 \text { or } 85 mm \) (ii).

\( \text { Step } V \quad \text { Diameter of socket collar }\left(d_{4}\right) \)

From Eq. (4.25i),

\( \sigma_{c}=\frac{P}{\left(d_{4}-d_{2}\right) t} \) (4.25i).

\( \sigma_{c}=\frac{P}{\left(d_{4}-d_{2}\right) t} \).

or \( 126.67=\frac{124348.16}{\left(d_{4}-65\right)(15)} \)

\( \therefore \quad d_{4}=130.44 \quad \text { or } \quad 135 mm \) (iii).

Step VI Dimensions a and c
From Eq. (4.25f),

\( \tau=\frac{P}{2\left(a d_{2}\right)} \) or \( P=2 a d_{2} \tau \) (4.25f).

\( a=\frac{P}{2 d_{2} \tau}=\frac{124348.16}{2(65)(31.67)}=30.20 \text { or } 35 mm \) (iv).

From Eq. (4.25g),

\( \tau=\frac{P}{2\left(d_{4}-d_{2}\right) c} \) or \( P=2\left(d_{4}-d_{2}\right) c \tau \) (4.25g).

\( c=\frac{P}{2\left(d_{4}-d_{2}\right) \tau}=\frac{124348.16}{2(135-65)(31.67)} \).

= 28.04 or 30 mm (v).
