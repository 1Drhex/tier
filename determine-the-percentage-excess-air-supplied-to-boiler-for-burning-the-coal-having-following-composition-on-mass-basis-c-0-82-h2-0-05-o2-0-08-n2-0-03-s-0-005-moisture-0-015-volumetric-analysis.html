Guest Question.

Determine the percentage excess air supplied to boiler for burning the coal having following composition on mass basis,
\(\begin{aligned}C&: 0.82\\H_{2}&: 0.05\\O_{2}&: 0.08\\N_{2}&: 0.03\\S&: 0.005\\moisture&: 0.015\end{aligned}\)
Volumetric analysis of dry flue gases shows the following composition,
\(\begin{aligned}\qquad CO_{2}& = 10\%\\CO& = 1\%\\N_{2}& = 82\%\\O_{2}& = 7\%\end{aligned}\)

Admin
Solution

For constituent components combustion reactions are as under. Out of given constituents only C, \(H_{2}\), and S shall require oxygen for burning reaction.\(\\\begin{aligned}\qquad \qquad C + O_{2}& → CO_{2}\\12 kg + 32 kg& → 44 kg\\1 kg +\frac{8}{3} kg& → \frac{11}{3} kg\\2H_{2} + O_{2} &→ 2H_{2}O\\4 kg + 32 kg &→ 36 kg\\1 kg + 8 kg &→ 9 kg \\S + O_{2} &→ SO_{2}\\32 kg + 32 kg &→ 64 kg\\1 kg + 1 kg &→ 2 kg\end{aligned}\\\text{Net mass of oxygen required per kg of coal}\\\begin{aligned}&= \left\{(0.82 × \frac{8}{3}) + (0.05 × 8) + (0.005 × 1) – (0.08)\right\} \\&= 2.512 kg\\\text{Mass of air }&\text{required per kg of coal, considering oxygen to be 23}\%\text{ in the air by mass,}\\&= \frac{100}{23} × 2.512\\&= \text{10.92 kg air per kg of coal}\end{aligned}\)
