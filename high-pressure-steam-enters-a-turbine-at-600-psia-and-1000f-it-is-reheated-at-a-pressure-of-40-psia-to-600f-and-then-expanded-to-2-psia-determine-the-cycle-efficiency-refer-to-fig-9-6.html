At 40 psia and 600°F we have

\( h_{5}=1333 Btu / lbm \quad \text { and } \quad s_{5}=1.862 Btu / lbm -{ }^{\circ} R \).

\( \text { In the quality region, use } s_{6}=s_{5} \text { and find } \)

\( x_{6}=\frac{1.862-0.175}{1.745}=0.9668 \quad \text { or } \quad 97 \% \).

This quality of 97% is quite acceptable. Any turbine efficiency would move state 6 into the superheated (dry) region.
Thus, \( h_{6}=94+(0.9668)(1022)=1082 Btu / lbm \text {. The energy input and output are } \)

\( q_{B}=\left(h_{5}-h_{4}\right)+\left(h_{3}-h_{2}\right)=1333-1200+1518-94=1557 Btu / lbm \).

\( w_{T}=\left(h_{5}-h_{6}\right)+\left(h_{3}-h_{4}\right)=1333-1082+1518-1200=569 Btu / lbm \).

The thermal efficiency is then calculated to be

\( \eta=\frac{w_{T}}{q_{B}}=\frac{569}{1557}=0.365 \quad \text { or } \quad 36.5 \% \).
