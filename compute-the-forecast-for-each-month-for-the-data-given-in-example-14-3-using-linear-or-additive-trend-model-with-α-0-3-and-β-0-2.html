Guest Question.

Compute the forecast for each month for the data given in using linear or additive trend model with α = 0.3 and β = 0.2.

Admin
Solution

In order to forecast the demand for Jan i.e. \(F_{Jan}\), we need to know the values of the base, \(S_{t}\), and trend \(T_{t}\), for one period before (Initial) i.e. \(S_{Initial}\), and \(T_{Initial}\). Let us take \(S_{Initial}\) = 220 and \(T_{Initial} = 0\).
The computation of forecast for other months is shown below:

\(S_{Jan} = αD_{Jan} + (1 – α)(S_{Initial} + T_{Initial}) \\ = 0.3 × 200 + 0.7 × (220 + 0) \\ = 214\)
\(T_{Jan} = β(S_{Jan} – S_{Initial}) + (1 – β)T_{Initial} \\ = 0.2 × (214 – 220) + 0.8 × 0 \\ = -1.2\)
\(F_{Feb} = S_{Jan} + T_{Jan} \\ = 214 – 1.2 \\ = 212.8\)
\(S_{Feb} = αD_{Feb} + (1 – α)(S_{Jan} + T_{Jan}) \\ = 0.3 × 260 + 0.7 × (214 – 1.2) \\ = 226.96\)
\(T_{Feb} = β(S_{Feb} – S_{Jan}) + (1 – β)T_{Jan} \\ = 0.2 × (226.96 – 214) + 0.8 × (-1.2) \\ = 1.63\)
\(F_{Mar} = S_{Feb} + T_{Feb} \\ = 226.96 + 1.63 \\ = 228.59\)
\(S_{Mar} = αD_{Mar} + (1 – α)(S_{Feb} + T_{Feb}) \\ = 0.3 × 210 + 0.7 × (226.96 + 1.63) \\ = 223.01\)
\(T_{Mar} = β(S_{Mar} – S_{Feb}) + (1 – β)T_{Feb} \\ = 0.2 × (223.01 – 226.96) + 0.8 × 1.63 \\ = 0.51\)
\(F_{Apr} = S_{Mar} + T_{Mar} \\ = 223.01 + 0.51 \\ = 223.52\)
\(S_{Apr} = αD_{Apr} + (1 – α)(S_{Mar} + T_{Mar}) \\ = 0.3 × 220 + 0.7 × (223.01 + 0.51) \\ = 222.46\)
\(T_{Apr} = β(S_{Apr} – S_{Mar}) + (1 – β)T_{Mar} \\ = 0.2 × (222.46 – 223.01) + 0.8 × 0.51 \\ = 0.30\)
\(F_{May} = S_{Apr} + T_{Apr} \\ = 222.46 + 0.30 \\ = 222.76\)
\(S_{May} = αD_{May} + (1 – α)(S_{Apr} + T_{Apr}) \\ = 0.3 × 230 + 0.7 × (222.46 + 0.30) \\ = 224.93\)
\(T_{May} = β(S_{May} – S_{Apr}) + (1 – β)T_{Apr} \\ = 0.2 × (224.93 – 222.46) + 0.8 × 0.30 \\ = 0.73\)
\(F_{Jun} = S_{May} + T_{May} \\ = 224.93 + 0.73 \\ = 225.76\)
\(S_{Jun} = αD_{Jun} + (1 – α)(S_{May} + T_{May}) \\ = 0.3 × 270 + 0.7 × (224.93 + 0.730) \\ = 238.96\)
\(T_{Jun} = β(S_{Jun} – S_{May}) + (1 – β)T_{May} \\ = 0.2 × (238.96 – 224.93) + 0.8 × 0.73 \\ = 3.39\)
\(F_{Jul} = S_{Jun} + T_{Jun} \\ = 238.96 + 3.39 \\ = 242.35\)
\(S_{Jul} = αD_{Jul} + (1 – α)(S_{Jun} + T_{Jun}) \\ = 0.3 × 290 + 0.7 × (238.96 + 3.39) \\ = 256.65\)
\(T_{Jul} = β(S_{Jul} – S_{Jun}) + (1 – β)T_{Jun} \\ = 0.2 × (256.65 – 238.96) + 0.8 × 3.39 \\ = 6.25\)
\(F_{Aug} = S_{Jul} + T_{Jul} \\ = 256.65 + 6.25 \\ =262.90\)
