Guest Question.

Three steady flows are mixed in an adiabatic chamber at 150 kPa. Flow one is 2 kg/s of \(O _{2}\) at 340 K, flow two is 4 kg/s of \(N _{2}\) at 280 K and flow three is 3 kg/s of \(CO _{2}\) at 310 K. All flows are at 150 kPa the same as the total exit pressure. Find the exit temperature and the rate of entropy generation in the process.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Mixing chamber, no heat transfer, no work.

Continuity Eq.4.9: \(\dot{ m }_{1}+\dot{ m }_{2}+\dot{ m }_{3}=\dot{ m }_{4}\)

Energy Eq.4.10: \(\dot{ m }_{1} h _{1}+\dot{ m }_{2} h _{2}+\dot{ m }_{3} h _{3}=\dot{ m }_{4} h _{4}\)

Entropy Eq.7.7: \(\dot{ m }_{1} s _{1}+\dot{ m }_{2} s _{2}+\dot{ m }_{3} s _{3}+\dot{ S }_{ gen }=\dot{ m }_{4} s _{4}\)

Assume ideal gases and since T is close to 300 K use heat capacity from A.5 in the energy equation as

\(\begin{aligned}&\dot{ m }_{1} C _{ P O 2}\left( T _{1}- T _{4}\right)+\dot{ m }_{2} C _{ P N 2}\left( T _{2}- T _{4}\right)+\dot{ m }_{3} C _{ P CO 2}\left( T _{3}- T _{4}\right)=0 \\&(2 \times 0.922 \times 340+4 \times 1.042 \times 280+3 \times 0.842 \times 310) kW \\&=(2 \times 0.922+4 \times 1.042+3 \times 0.842) kW / K \times T _{4}\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\Rightarrow \quad 2577.06 K =8.538 T _{4} \quad \Rightarrow \quad T _{4}= 3 0 1 . 8 3 K\)

 

State 4 is a mixture so the component exit pressure is the partial pressure. For each component \(s _{ e }- s _{ i }= C _{ P } \ln \left( T _{ e } / T _{ i }\right)- R \ln \left( P _{ e } / P _{ i }\right)\) and the pressure ratio is \(P _{ e } / P _{ i }= y P _{4} / P _{ i }= y\) for each.

\(n =\sum \frac{ m }{ M }=\frac{2}{32}+\frac{4}{28.013}+\frac{3}{44.01}=0.0625+0.1428+0.06817=0.2735\)

 

\(y _{ O 2}=\frac{0.0625}{0.2735}=0.2285, y _{ N 2}=\frac{0.1428}{0.2735}=0.5222, y _{ CO 2}=\frac{0.06817}{0.2735}=0.2493\)

 

The entropy generation becomes

\(\begin{aligned}\dot{ S }_{ gen }=& \dot{ m }_{1}\left( s _{4}- s _{1}\right)+\dot{ m }_{2}\left( s _{4}- s _{2}\right)+\dot{ m }_{3}\left( s _{4}- s _{3}\right) \\=& 2[0.922 \ln (301.83 / 340)-0.2598 \ln (0.2285)] \\&+4[1.042 \ln (301.83 / 280)-0.2968 \ln (0.5222)] \\&+3[0.842 \ln (301.83 / 310)-0.1889 \ln (0.2493)] \\=& 0.5475+1.084+0.2399= 1 . 8 7 1 k W / K\end{aligned}\)

Eq.7.7 : \(\sum \dot{m}_{e} s_{e}-\sum \dot{m}_{i} s_{i}=\sum_{ c.s. } \frac{\dot{Q}_{ c.v. }}{T}+\dot{S}_{ gen }\)
