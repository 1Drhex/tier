Guest Question.

Design a digital differentiator using the Hamming window.

Admin
Solution

Step 1: The desired frequency response of the analog differentiator, H(r), is defined:\(H(s)=s\Rightarrow H(r)=j\omega |_{\begin{matrix} \omega =2\pi f \\ f=rf_{s} \end{matrix} }=j2\pi f_{s}r\)

Step 2: \(h_{i}[nT]\) is computed.

\(h_{i}[nT]=\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{H(r)e^{j2\pi nr}dr} =\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{(j2\pi f_{s}r)e^{j2\pi nr}dr}\)
\(\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{H(r)e^{j2\pi nr}dr}=\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{(j2\pi f_{s}r)e^{j2\pi nr}dr}=\frac{f_{s}}{n}\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{j2\pi nre^{j2\pi nr}dr} \)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{n}\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{rde^{j2\pi nr}r}=\frac{f_{s}}{n}[re^{j2\pi nr}|^{r=1/2}_{r=-1/2}-\int\limits_{-1/2}^{1/2}{e^{j2\pi nr}d(r)}]\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{n}[re^{j2\pi nr}-\frac{1}{j2\pi n}e^{j2\pi nr} ]_{r=-1/2}^{r=1/2}\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{n}[\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{j2\pi n} ]e^{j\pi n}-\frac{f_{s}}{n}[-\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{j2\pi n} ]e^{-j\pi n}\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{2n}[e^{j\pi n}+e^{-j\pi n}]-\frac{f_{s}}{j2\pi n^{2}} [e^{j\pi n}-e^{-j\pi n}]\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{2n}2\cos \pi n-\frac{f_{s}}{j2\pi n^{2}}[2j\sin \pi n]\)

Thus,

\(h_{i}[nT]= \begin{cases} \frac{f_{s}}{n}(-1)^{n}-\frac{f_{s}2j}{j2\pi n^{2}}\sin \pi n=\frac{f_{s}}{n}(-1)^{n} & n\neq 0 \\ \frac{f_{s}}{n}-\frac{f_{s}}{\pi n^{2}}\sin \pi n=\frac{f_{s}[\pi n-\sin \pi n]}{\pi n^{2}} \end{cases} \)
\(\Rightarrow f_{s}\underset{n\rightarrow 0}{\lim }\frac{\frac{d}{dn}[\pi n-\sin \pi n] }{\frac{d\pi n^{2}}{dn} }\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{2} \underset{n\rightarrow 0}{\lim }\frac{\pi -\pi \cos \pi n}{\pi n} \)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{2} \underset{n\rightarrow 0}{\lim }\frac{d(\pi -\pi \cos \pi n)/dn}{d(\pi n)/dn}\)
\(=\frac{f_{s}}{2} \underset{n\rightarrow 0}{\lim }\frac{\pi ^{2}\sin \pi n}{\pi } =0\) n=0
\(h_{i}[nT]=\begin{cases} \frac{f_{s}}{n}(-1)^{n} & n\neq 1\\ 0 & n=0 \end{cases}\)

Step 3: Design of the filter using the Hamming window (M=7).

Hamming window: \(w_{h}[n]=\begin{cases} 0.54+0.46\cos \frac{n\pi }{M} & |n|\leq M\\ 0 & |n|>M \end{cases}\)
\(H(z)=\sum\limits_{n=-7}^{n=-1}{\frac{f_{s}}{n}(-1)^{n}w_{h}[n]z^{-n}}+\sum\limits_{n=1}^{n=7}{\frac{f_{s}}{n}(-1)^{n}w_{h}[n]z^{-n}} \)
\(H_{c}(z)=z^{-7}H(z)\)
