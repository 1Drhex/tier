Guest Question.

 

The arm of an automatic assembly machine starts moving from rest with constant acceleration \(a_{0} \) along a straight track. At the same time, the arm begins to rotate with constant angular velocity \(\omega _{0}\) and the slider \(P\) begins to move towards point \(A\) with constant relative acceleration \(a_{r}\) . The initial positions of the arm and the slider are given by \(\varphi _{0}=0 \) and \(s_{0}\) .

Determine the absolute velocity and acceleration of the slider \(P\) in dependence of time \(t\) .

Admin
Solution

We use the fixed coordinate system \( x, y, z \) , where the \(x\)-axis coincides with the track. In addition, we introduce the moving coordinate system \( \xi ,\eta , \zeta \) , where the \(\xi \)-axis rotates with the arm. Then, the general equations for the absolute velocity and acceleration are

\(v=v_{A}+\omega \times r_{AP}+v_{r} \) ,

\(a=a_{A}+\dot{\omega }\times r_{AP}+\omega \times \left(\omega \times r_{AP} \right) +2\omega \times v_{r }+ a_{r } \) .

We start counting time t from the beginning of the motion. then, by using the given accelerations, angular velocity and initial conditions, we obtain

\(a_{A}=a_{0}e_{x}\rightsquigarrow v_{0}=a_{0}te_{x}\) ,

\(\omega =\omega _{0}e_{\zeta }\) , \(\dot{\omega } =0\) ,

\(a_{r}=-a_{r}e_{\xi }\rightsquigarrow v_{r}=-a_{r}te_{\xi } \rightsquigarrow r_{AP}=\left(-\frac{1}{2}a_{r}t^{2}+s_{0} \right)e_{\xi } \) ,

and

\(\omega \times r_{AP}=\omega \left(-\frac{1}{2}a_{r}t^{2}+s_{0 } \right)e_{\eta }\) , \(2\omega \times v_{r}=-2\omega _{0}a_{r}te_{\eta }\) ,

\(\omega \times \left(\omega \times r_{AP} \right)=-\omega ^{2}_{0}\left(-\frac{1}{2}a_{r}t^{2}+s_{0} \right) e_{\xi } \) .

Taking into account the relations \(e_{x}=e_{\xi } \cos \varphi -e_{\eta }\sin \varphi \) , where \(\varphi =\omega _{0}t \) , we finally obtain

\(\underline{\underline{v=\left[a_{0}t\cos\omega _{0} t-a_{r}t \right]e_{\xi }+\left[-a_{0}t\sin \omega _{0}t+\omega _{0}\left(-\frac{1}{2}a_{r}t^{2}+s_{0} \right) \right]e_{\eta } } } \) ,

\(\underline{\underline{a=\left[a_{0}\cos\omega _{0} t-\omega ^{2}_{0}\left(-\frac{1}{2}a_{r}t^{2}+s_{0} \right) – a_{r} \right]e_{\xi }-\left[-a_{0}\sin \omega _{0}t+2\omega _{0}a_{r}t \right]e_{\eta } } } \) .
