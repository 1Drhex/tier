Guest Question.

A motor car negotiates a curve of 40 m radius at a speed of 60 km/h. Determine the magnitudes of the centrifugal and gyroscopic couples acting on the motor car and state the effect of each of these on the road reactions on the wheels. Assume the following:
(a) Each road wheel has a moment of inertia of 4 kg.mÂ² and an effective wheel radius of 0.5 m.
(b) The rotating parts of the engine and transmission are equivalent to a flywheel of mass 80 kg with a radius of gyration of 0.1 m. The engine turns in a clockwise direction when viewed from the front.
(c) The back axle ratio is 4:1 and the drive through the gear box is direct.
(d) The car weighs 10 kN and has its centre of gravity at 0.6 m above the road level. The car takes a right hand turn.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } R=40 m , v=60 km / h , I_{w}=4 kg – m ^{2}, r_{w}=0.5 m , M =80 kg , K =0.1 m , i=4, W=10 kN, h = 0.6 m \).

\( v=60 \times \frac{1000}{3600}=16.67 m / s \).

Angular velocity of wheel,

\( \omega_{w}=\frac{v}{r}=\frac{16.67}{0.5}=314.33 rad / s \).

Angular velocity of precession of wheels,

\( \omega_{p w}=\frac{v}{R}=\frac{16.67}{40}=0.417 rad / s \).

(a) Gyroscopic couple due to wheels,

\( C_{w}=4 I_{w} \omega_{w} \omega_{p w} \).

\( =4 \times 4 \times 314.33 \times 0.417=223 Nm \).

The reaction gyro-couple due to wheels will tend to lift the inner wheels and depress the outer wheels.
(b) Gyroscopic couple due to engine rotating parts,

\( C_{e}=I_{e} \omega_{e} \omega_{p e} \).

\( =M_{e} K_{e}^{2} \times i \omega_{w} \omega_{p e} \) \( \left[\because \omega_{p e}=\omega_{p w}\right] \)

\( =80 \times 0.1^{2} \times 4 \times 314.33 \times 0.417 \).

= 44.48 N m.

The reaction gyro-couple due to engine rotating parts will tend to lift the front wheels and depress the rear wheels.
(c) Centrifugal force,

\( F_{c}=\frac{W v^{2}}{g R} \).

\( =10 \times 1000 \times \frac{(16.67)^{2}}{9.81 \times 40} \).

\( =7078.95 N \).

Centrifugal couple, \( C_{c}=F_{c} h=7078.95 \times 0.6=4247.4 Nm \).

The reaction centrifugal couple will tend to lift the inner wheels and depress the outer wheels.
