Guest Question.

A hot surface at 100°C is to be cooled by attaching 3 cm long, 0.25 cm diameter aluminium fins (k = 237 W/m.K) to it, with a centre to centre distance of 0.6 cm. The temperature of surrounding air is 30°C and heat transfer coefficient on surface is \(35 W/m^2.K.\) Calculate the rate of heat transfer from the surface for a 1 m × 1 m section of the plate. Also determine the overall effectiveness of the fins.

Admin
Solution

Given : A hot surface attached with pin fins.
\(T_0 = 100°C, L = 3 cm = 0.03 m,\)
\(d = 0.25 cm = 0.25 × 10^{–2} m,\)
k = 237 W/m.K, S = 0.6 cm,
\(T_∞ = 30°C, h = 35 W/m^2.K.\)
To find :
(i) Heat transfer rate from \(1 m^2 \)finned surface,
(ii) Overall fin effectiveness.
Assumptions :
(i) Steady state conditions.
(ii) Finite long fin, but L >> d, hence assuming insulated tip.
(iii) Uniform heat transfer coefficient on fins as well as on base surface.
(iv) Constant properties.
Analysis : (i) Heat transfer rate from finned surface :
No. of fins in a row =\( \frac{w}{S} = \frac{100 cm}{0.6 cm}= 166.67\)
≈ 167
Similarly the no. of fins in the column
\(= \frac{100} {0.6 }= 167\)
These fins are in matrix of n × n, thus Total no. of fins in
\(N_{fin}\) = 167 × 167 = 27,889 fins/\(m^2\)
For a fin
\(A_c = \frac{π}{4} d^2 = \frac{π}{4} × (0.25 × 10–2 m)^2\)
\(= 4.9087 × 10^{–6} m^2\)
\(P = πd = π × 0.25 × 10^{–2}\)
\(= 7.8539 × 10^{–3} m\)
\(m = \sqrt{\frac{hP}{k A_{c}}}\sqrt{\frac{35 × 7.8539 × 10^{–3} }{237 × 10^{–3} 4.9087 × 10^{–6}}} \)

= 15.37
The heat transfer rate from all fins
\(Q_{fin} =N_{fin}\sqrt{ hPk A_c} (T_0 – T_∞)\) tanh mL
\(= 27,889 × \sqrt{35 × 7.8539 × 10^{–3} × 237 × 4.9087 × 10^{–6}} × (100 – 30) × tanh (15.37 × 0.03)\)
\(= 15.046 × 10^3 W\)
The heat transfer from unfinned portion
\(A_{unfin} = 1 m^2 – N_{fin} × A_c\)
\(= 1 m^2 – 27,889 × 4.9087 × 10^{–6}\)
\(= 0.863 m^2\)
\(Q_{unfin} = h A_{unfin} × (T_0 – T_∞)\)
= 35 × 0.863 × (100 – 30)
= 2114.6 W
Total heat transfer from finner surface/\(m^2\)
\(Q_{total fin} =Q_{fin} + Q_{unfin}\)
\(= 15.046 × 10^3 + 2114.6\)
\(= 17.16 × 10^3 W.\)
(ii) Overall effectiveness
\(ε_{overall} = \frac{Q_{total fin}}{Q_{no fin}} = \frac{Q_{total fin}}{h(1 m^2)(T_0 – T_∞)}\)
\(\frac{17.16 × 10^3 }{35 × 1 ×(100 – 35)} = 7.0\)
