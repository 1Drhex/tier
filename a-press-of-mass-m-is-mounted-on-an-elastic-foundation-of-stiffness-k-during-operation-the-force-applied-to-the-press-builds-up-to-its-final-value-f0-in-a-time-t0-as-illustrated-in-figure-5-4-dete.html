Guest Question.

A press of mass m is mounted on an elastic foundation of stiffness k. During operation, the force applied to the press builds up to its final value \(F_{0}\) in a time \(t_{0}\) , as illustrated in Figure 5.4. Determine the response of the press for (a) \(t \lt t_{0}\), and (b) \(t \gt t_{0}\).

Admin
Solution

The force applied to the press can be expressed as

\( f(t)= \begin{cases} F_{0}\frac{t}{t_{0}} & t\lt t_{0}\\ F_{0} & t\geq t_{0} \end{cases} \) (a)

For an undamped system, the convolution integral of Equation (5.25) becomes

\( x(t)=\frac{1}{m\omega _{n}}\int_{0}^{t}{F(\tau )} \sin \omega _{n}(t-\tau)d\tau\) (b)

(a) For \( t\lt t_{0}\), the convolution integral yields

\(x(t)=\frac{1}{m\omega _{n}}\int_{0}^{t}{F_{0}\frac{\tau }{t_{0}}\sin\omega _{n}(t-\tau )d\tau } \)

 

\( =\frac{F_{0}}{m\omega _{n}t_{0}}\left[\frac{\tau }{\omega _{n}}\cos\omega _{n}(t-\tau )+\frac{1}{\omega ^{2}_{n}} \sin\omega _{n}(t-\tau ) \right]^{\tau =t}_{\tau =0}\)

\( =\frac{F_{0}}{m\omega ^{2}_[n]t_{0}}\left(t-\frac{1}{\omega _{n}}\sin \omega _{n}t\right)\) (c)

\( x(t)=\frac{1}{m_{eq}\omega _{d}}\int_{0}^{t}{F(\tau )e^{-\zeta \omega _{n}(t-\tau )}} \sin\omega _{d}(t-\tau )d\tau\) (5.25)

(b) For \( t\gt t_{0}\), application of the convolution integral leads to

\( x(t)=\frac{1}{m\omega _{n}} \left[\int_{0}^{t_{0}}{F_{0}\frac{\tau }{t_{0}} \sin\omega _{n}(t-\tau )d\tau}+\int_{t_{0}}^{t}{F_{0}\sin\omega _{n}(t-\tau )d\tau } \right]\)

 

\(\begin{matrix} =\frac{F_{0}}{m\omega _{n}}\left\{\left[\frac{\tau }{\omega _{n} }\cos\omega _{n}(t-\tau )+\frac{1}{\omega ^{2}_{n}}\sin\omega _{n}(t-\tau ) \right]^{\tau =t_{0}}_{\tau =0} \right. \\ \left. +\left[\frac{1}{\omega _{n}}\cos\omega _{n} (t-\tau )\right]^{\tau =t}_{\tau =t_{0}} \right\} \end{matrix} \)

\(\begin{matrix} =\frac{F_{0}}{m\omega^{2}_{n}t_{0} }\left[t_{0}\cos\omega _{n}(t-t_{0})+\frac{1}{\omega _{n} }\sin\omega _{n}(t-t_{0})-\frac{1}{\omega _{n}}\sin\omega _{n}t+ \frac{1}{\omega _{n}}\right.\\ \left.-\frac{1}{\omega _{n}}\cos\omega _{n}(t-t_{0}) \right] \end{matrix} \) (d)
