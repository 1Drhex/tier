MULTIPLE-LIQUID MANOMETER
Water flows through pipes \(A\) and \(B\). Lubricating oil is in the upper portion of the inverted U. Mercury is in the bottom of the manometer bends. Determine the pressure difference, \(p_A - p_B\), in units of lbf/in\({}^2\).

Admin
Solution

Given: Multiple-liquid manometer as shown.
Find: Pressure difference, \(p_A – p_B\), in lbf/in\({}^2\).

Solution:
Governing equations: \(\quad \Delta p=g \sum_{i} \rho_{i} h_{i} \quad S G=\frac{\rho}{\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}}}\)
Assumptions:
(1) Static fluid.
(2) Incompressible fluid.
Applying the governing equation, working from point \(B\) to \(A\)
\(p_{A}-p_{B}=\Delta p=g\left(\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{5}+\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} d_{4}-\rho_{\text {oil }} d_{3}+\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} d_{2}-\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{1}\right)\) (1)

This equation can also be derived by repeatedly using Eq. 3.7 in the following form:
\(p_{2}-p_{1}=\rho g\left(h_{2}-h_{1}\right)\)

 

Beginning at point \(A\) and applying the equation between successive points along the manometer gives
\(\begin{aligned}&p_{C}-p_{A}=+\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} g d_{1} \\&p_{D}-p_{C}=-\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} g d_{2} \\&p_{E}-p_{D}=+\rho_{\text {oil }} g d_{3} \\&p_{F}-p_{E}=-\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} g d_{4} \\&p_{B}-p_{F}=-\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} g d_{5}\end{aligned}\)
Multiplying each equation by minus one and adding, we obtain Eq. (1)
\(\begin{aligned}p_{A}-p_{B} &=\left(p_{A}-p_{C}\right)+\left(p_{C}-p_{D}\right)+\left(p_{D}-p_{E}\right)+\left(p_{E}-p_{F}\right)+\left(p_{F}-p_{B}\right) \\&=-\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} g d_{1}+\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} g d_{2}-\rho_{\text {oil }} g d_{3}+\rho_{\mathrm{Hg}} g d_{4}+\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} g d_{5}\end{aligned}\)
Substituting \(\rho=S G \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}}\) with \(S G_{\mathrm{Hg}}=13.6\) and \(S G_{\text {oil }}=0.88\) (Table A.2), yields

\(\begin{aligned}p_{A}-p_{B} &=g\left(-\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{1}+13.6 \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{2}-0.88 \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{3}+13.6 \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{4}+\rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} d_{5}\right) \\&=g \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}}\left(-d_{1}+13.6 d_{2}-0.88 d_{3}+13.6 d_{4}+d_{5}\right) \\p_{A}-p_{B} &=g \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}}(-10+40.8-3.52+68+8) \text { in. } \\p_{A}-p_{B} &=g \rho_{\mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}} \times 103.3 \text { in. } \\&=32.2 \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{\mathrm{s}^{2}} \times 1.94 \frac{\text { This } \mathrm{E}}{\mathrm{ft}^{3}} \times 103.3 \text { in. } \times \frac{\mathrm{ft}}{12 \text { in. }} \times \frac{\mathrm{ft}^{2}}{144 \text { in. }^{2}} \times \frac{\mathrm{lbf} \cdot \mathrm{s}^{2}}{\text { slug } \cdot \mathrm{ft}} \end{aligned}\)

 

\(p_{A}-p_{B} =3.73 \mathrm{lbf} / \text { in. }^{2}\) \( \xleftarrow[]{p_A – p_B} \)
