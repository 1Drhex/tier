Guest Question.

Determine the resultant force and the resultant couple of the force system shown in Fig. 4.18,
where \( F_1 = 100 \) N, \( F_2 = 20\sqrt{2} \) N, \( F_3 = 40 \) N, \( F_4 = 40 \) N, \( C_1 = 250 \) N-m and \( C_2 = 100 \) N-m.

Admin
Solution

Given : \( F_1 = 100 \) N \( (A \rightarrow E) \);

\( F_2 = 20 \sqrt{2} \) N \( (A \rightarrow C) \);

\( F_3 = 40 \) N \( (E \rightarrow D) \);

\( F_4 = 40 \) N \( (B \rightarrow A) \);

\( C_1 = 250 \) N-m \( (D \rightarrow B) \);

\( C_2= 100 \) N-m \( (A \rightarrow O) \);

[By right-hand-thumb rule]

(i) Coordinates : O(0, 0, 0); A(3, 0, 0); B(3, 3, 0); C(0, 3, 0); D(0, 3, 4); E(0, 0, 4).

(ii) Force vector

\( \overline{F}_1 = (F_1)(\overline{e}_{AE}) = (100) \biggl[\frac{-3 i + 4 k}{\sqrt{9+16} }\biggr] \).

\( \therefore \overline{F}_1 = -60 i + 80 k \).

\( \overline{F}_2 = (F_2)(\overline{e}_{AC}) = ( 20 \sqrt{2}) \biggl[\frac{-3 i + 3 j}{\sqrt{9+9} }\biggr] \).

\( \therefore \overline{F}_2 =-20 i + 20 j \).

\( \overline{F}_3 = (F_3)(\overline{e}_{ED}) = 40 j \).

\( \overline{F}_4 = (F_4)(\overline{e}_{BA}) = -40 j \).

\( \overline{C}_1 = (C_1)(\overline{e}_{DB}) = (250) \biggl[\frac{3 i – 4 k}{\sqrt{9+16} }\biggr] \).

\( \therefore \overline{C}_1 = 150 i – 200 k \).

\( \overline{C}_2 = (C_2)(\overline{e}_{AO}) = (100) \biggl[\frac{-3 i }{\sqrt{9} }\biggr] \).

\( \therefore \overline{C}_2 = -100 i \).

(iii) Position vectors

\( \overline{r}_{1} = 3 i \); \( \overline{r}_{2} = 3 i \); \( \overline{r}_{3} = 4 k \); \( \overline{r}_{4} = 3 i \).

(iv) Moment vectors

\( \overline{M}_{1} = \overline{r}_{1} ×\overline{F}_{1} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 3 & 0 & 0\\ -60 & 0 & 80 \end{vmatrix} = i (0) – j (240) + k (0) \) \( \therefore \overline{M}_{1} = -240 j \) .

\( \overline{M}_{2} = \overline{r}_{2} ×\overline{F}_{2} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 3 & 0 & 0\\ 20 & 20 & 0\end{vmatrix} = i (0) – j (0) + k (60) \) \( \therefore \overline{M}_{2} = 60 k \) .

\( \overline{M}_{3} = \overline{r}_{3} ×\overline{F}_{3} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 0 & 0 & 4\\ 0 & 40 &0 \end{vmatrix} = i (-160) – j (0) + k (0) \) \( \therefore \overline{M}_{3} = -160 i \) .

\( \overline{M}_{4} = \overline{r}_{4} ×\overline{F}_{4} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 3 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & -40 &0 \end{vmatrix} = i (0) – j (0) + k (-120) \) \( \therefore \overline{M}_{3} =-120 k\) .

From point (ii), we have

\( \overline{C}_1 = 150 i – 200 k \) and \( \overline{C}_2 = -100 i \) .

[Direction of couple is taken by right-hand-thumb rule]

(v) Resultant force vector

\( \overline{R} = \overline{F}_{1} + \overline{F}_{2} + \overline{F}_{3} + \overline{F}_{4} \).

\( \overline{R} = (-60 i + 80 k) + (-20 i + 20 j) + 40 j – 40 j \).

\( \overline{R} = -80 i + 20 j + 80 k \) Ans.

(vi) Resultant moment (couple) vector

\( ∑ \overline{M} = \overline{M}_1 + \overline{M}_2 + \overline{M}_3 + \overline{M}_4 + \overline{C}_1 + \overline{C}_2 \).

\( ∑ \overline{M} = -240 j + 60 k – 160 i – 120 k + (150 i – 200 k ) – 100 i \).

\( ∑ \overline{M} = -110 i – 240 j – 260 k \) Ans.
