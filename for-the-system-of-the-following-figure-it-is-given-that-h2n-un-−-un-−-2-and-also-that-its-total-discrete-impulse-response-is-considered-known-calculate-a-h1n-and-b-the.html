Guest Question.

For the system of the following figure, it is given that \(h_{2}[n] = u[n] − u[n − 2]\) and also that its total discrete impulse response is considered known. Calculate: (a) \(h_{1}[n]\) and (b) the response of the system for the input x[n] = δ[n] − δ[n − 1].

Admin
Solution

Two different approaches for the solution of both parts of the problem are given next.

a. It is \(h[n] = h_{1} [n]* h_{2} [n]* h_{2} [n]\)(1)

\(h_{2}[n]*h_{2}[n]=h_{3}[n]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{2}[k]h_{2}[n − k]}=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[n] + h_{2}[1]h_{2}[n-1]+h_{2}[2]h_{2}[n-2]+…\)(2)

and \(h_{2}[n] = u[n] − u[n − 2].\) Thus,

(2)\(\Rightarrow \left. \begin{matrix} h_{3}[0]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[0]=1 \\ h_{3}[1]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[1]+h_{2}[1]h_{2}[0]=2 \\h_{3}[2]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[2]+h_{2}[1]h_{2}[1]+h_{2}[2]h_{2}[0]=1 \\h_{3}[2]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[3]+h_{2}[1]h_{2}[2]+h_{2}[2]h_{2}[1]+h_{2}[3]h_{2}[0]=0 \end{matrix} \right\} \to h_{3}[n]=\left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},2,1,0 \right\}\)
(1)\(\Rightarrow h[n]=h[n]*h[n]=h[n]*([n]+2\delta [n-1]+\delta [n-2])\Rightarrow h[n]=h_{1}[n]+2h_{1}[n-1]+h_{1}[n-2]\)(3)
(3)\(\Rightarrow h[0]=h_{1}[0]+2h_{1}[-1]+h_{1}[-2]\Rightarrow h[0]=1\)
\(h[1]=h_{1}[1]+2h[0]+h_{1}[-1]\Rightarrow h[1]=h[1]-2h[0]=3\)
\(h[2]= 10=h_{1}[2]+2h_{1}[1]+h_{1}[0]\Rightarrow h_{1}[2]=3\)
\(h[3]= 11=h_{1}[3]+2h_{1}[2]+h_{1}[1]\Rightarrow h_{1}[3]=2\)
\(h[4]= 8=h_{1}[4]+2h_{1}[3]+h_{1}[2]\Rightarrow h_{1}[4]=1\)

\(h_{1}[n] = 0\) ∀n ≥ 5

Second proposed solution:

It is \(h[n] = h_{1} [n]* h_{2} [n]* h_{2} [n]\)(1)

and \( h_{2}[n] = u[n] − u[n − 2] = \left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},-1 \right\}\) (2)

\(h_{2}[n]*h_{2}[n]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{2}[k]} h_{2}[n-k]=h_{2}^{'}[n]\)
(2)\(\Rightarrow h_{2}^{'}[0]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{2}[k]} h_{2}[-k]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[0]=1.1=1\)
\(h_{2}^{'}[1]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{2}[k]} h_{2}[1-k]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[1]+h_{2}[1]h_{2}[0]=1.1+1.1=2\)
\(h_{2}^{'}[2]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{2}[k]} \)\(h_{2}[2-k]=h_{2}[0]h_{2}[2]+h_{2}[1]h_{2}[1]+h_{2}[2]h_{2}[0]=1\Rightarrow\)
\(h_{2}^{'}[n]=\left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},2,1 \right\}\)(3)
(1)\(\Rightarrow h[n]=h_{1}[n]*h_{2}^{'}[n]\)

\(h_{1}[n]\) will be computed using deconvolution.
\(h[n]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{1}[k]h_{2}^{'}[n-k]}\)(4)
(4)\(\Rightarrow h[0]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{1}[k]h_{2}^{'}[-k]}=h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow \)\(h_{1}[0]=\frac{h[0]}{h_{2}^{'}[0]} =\frac{1}{1}=1\)
\(h[1]=h_{1}[0]+h_{2}^{'}[1]+h_{1}[1]+h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow h_{1}[1]=\frac{h[1]-h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[1]}{h_{2}^{'}[0]}=\frac{5-1.2}{1}=3\)
\(h[2]=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{\infty }{h_{1}[k]h_{2}^{'}[2-k]}=h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[2]+h_{1}[1]h_{2}^{'}[1]+h_{1}[2]+h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow\)
\(10=1.1+3.2+h_{1}[2].1\Rightarrow h_{1}[2]=10-7=3\)

\(h[3]=h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[3]+h_{1}[1]h_{2}^{'}[2]+h_{1}[2]h_{2}^{'}[1]+h_{1}[3]h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow\)
\(11=1.0+3.1+3.2+h_{1}[3].1\Rightarrow h_{1}[3]=11-9=2\)
\(h[4]=h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[4]+h_{1}[1]h_{2}^{'}[3]+h_{1}[2]h_{2}^{'}[2]+h_{1}[3]h_{2}^{'}[1]+h_{1}[4]h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow 8=3.1+2.2+h_{1}[4].1\Rightarrow h_{1}[4]=8-7=1\)
\(h[5]=h_{1}[0]h_{2}^{'}[5]+h_{1}[1]h_{2}^{'}[4]+h_{1}[2]h_{2}^{'}[3]+h_{1}[3]h_{2}^{'}[2]+h_{1}[4]h_{2}^{'}[1]+h_{1}[5]h_{2}^{'}[0]\Rightarrow \)\(4=2.1+1.2+h_{1}[5].1\Rightarrow \)\(h_{1}[5]=4-4=0\)

Thus, \(h_{1}[n] = \left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},3,3,2,1 \right\}\)

Second proposed solution

It is true that y[n] = x[n]* h[n]

\(x[n]=\delta [n]-\delta [n-1]=\left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},-1 \right\} and h[n]= \left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},5,10,11,8,4,1 \right\}\)

Hence,

\(y[0]=x[0]h[0]=1\times 1=1\)
\(y[1]=x[0]h[1]+x[1]h[0]=5-1=4\)
\(y[2]=x[0]h[2]+x[1]h[1]+x[2]h[0]=1\times 10-1\times 5=5\)
\(y[3]=x[0]h[3]+h[2]=11-10=1\)
\(y[4]=x[0]h[4]+x[1]h[3]=8-11=-3\)
\(y[5]=x[0]h[5]+x[1]h[4]=4-8=-4\)
\(y[6]=x[0]h[6]+x[1]h[5]=1-4=-3\)
\(y[7]=x[0]h[7]+x[1]h[6]=0-1=-1\)

So, the response of the system will be:

\(y[n]=\left\{\underset{\uparrow }{1},4,5,1,-3,-4,-3,-1 \right\}\)
