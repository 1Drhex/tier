Guest Question.

Synthesize a four-bar mechanism to generate a function \( y=\sin x \text { for } 0 \leq x \leq 90^{\circ} \). The range of the output crank may be chosen as 60° while that of input crank be 120°. Assume three precision point obtained form Chebyshev spacing. Assume fixed link to be 50 mm long and \( \theta_{1}=105^{\circ} \text { and } \phi_{1}=60^{\circ} \).

Where \( \theta_{d} \text { and } \phi_{d} \) are the angles made by input link at j th position.

Admin
Solution

Refer to Fig.16.32.

\( y=\sin x \text { for } 0 \leq x \leq 90^{\circ} \).

\( x_{i}=0^{\circ}, x_{f}=90^{\circ} \).

Cheybyshev’s precision points are given by:

\( x_{m}=a+b \cos \left[\frac{(2 m-1) \pi}{2 n}\right] \).

\( a=\frac{1}{2}\left(x_{i}+x_{f}\right)=\frac{1}{2}(0+90)=45^{\circ} \).

\( \frac{\phi_{3}-60}{0.105-0}=60, \phi_{3}=6.63^{\circ} \).

The Freudenstein’s equation is

\( k_{1} \cos \phi+k_{2} \cos \theta+k_{3}=\cos (\theta-\phi) \).

where \( k_{1}=\frac{d}{a}, k_{2}=-\frac{d}{c} \text { and } k_{3}=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}+c^{2}+d^{2}}{2 a c} \).

\( \text { Given } d_{1}=50 mm \).

Substituting the values, we have

\( \left[\begin{array}{rrr} -0.494 & -0.8 & 1 \\ -0.215 & -0.966 & 1 \\ 0.993 & -0.391 & 1 \end{array}\right]\left\{\begin{array}{l} k_{1} \\ k_{2} \\ k_{3} \end{array}\right\}=\left\{\begin{array}{r} -0.955 \\ 0.460 \\ -0.282 \end{array}\right\} \).

\( [A]\{k\}=\{B\} \).

\( |A|=0.36095,\left|A_{1}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{rrr} -0.955 & -0.8 & 1 \\ 0.460 & -0.966 & 1 \\ -0.282 & -0.391 & 1 \end{array}\right|=0.69045 \).

\( \left|A_{2}\right|=\left[\begin{array}{rrr} -0.494 & -0.955 & 1 \\ -0.215 & 0.460 & 1 \\ 0.993 & -0.282 & 1 \end{array}\right]=-1.91634 \).

\( \left|A_{3}\right|=\left[\begin{array}{ccc} -0.494 & -0.8 & -0.955 \\ -0.215 & -0.966 & 0.460 \\ 0.993 & -0.391 & -0.282 \end{array}\right]=-1.5367 \).

\( k_{1}=\left|\frac{A_{1}}{A}\right|=1.913, k_{2}=\left|\frac{A_{2}}{A}\right|=-5.309, k_{3}=\left|\frac{A_{3}}{A}\right| \).

\( k_{1}=\frac{d}{a}, a=\frac{d}{k_{1}}=\frac{50}{1.913}=26.137 mm \).

\( k_{2}=-\frac{d}{c}, c=-\frac{d}{k_{2}}=\frac{-50}{-5.309}=9.418 mm \).

\( k_{3}=\frac{a^{2}-b^{2}+c^{2}+d^{2}}{2 a c} \).

\( -4.2574 \times 2 \times 26.137 \times 9.418=(26.137)^{2}-b^{2}+(9.418)^{2}+50^{2} \).

\( b^{2}=5367.82, b=73.265 mm \).

\( \text { Hence } a=26.137 mm , b=73.265 mm , c=9.418 mm , \text { and } d=50 mm \).
