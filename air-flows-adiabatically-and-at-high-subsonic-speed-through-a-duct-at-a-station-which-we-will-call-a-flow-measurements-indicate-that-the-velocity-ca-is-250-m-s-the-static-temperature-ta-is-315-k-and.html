Guest Question.

Air flows adiabatically and at high subsonic speed through a duct. At a station which we will call A, flow measurements indicate that the velocity \( c_{A} \) is 250 m/s, the static temperature \( T_{A} \) is 315 K and the static pressure \( p_{A} \) is 180 kPa. Determine the values of the stagnation temperature \( T_{0A} \) , the Mach number \( M_{A} \) the stagnation pressure \( p_{0A} \) and the stagnation density \( \rho_{A} \) . If the duct cross-sectional area is 0.1 \( m^{2} \) , calculate the air mass flow rate. For air take R = 287 J/kg K and \(\gamma =1.4 \).

Admin
Solution

From Eq. (1.34a)

\(C_{p} T_{0} =C_{p} T+\frac{c^{2} }{2} = C_{p} T + \frac{M^{2} \gamma RT }{2} \) (1.34a)

 

\( T_{0A} = T_{A} +\frac{c^{2}_{A}}{2 C_{p}} = 346 K \)

 

From Eq. (1.33)

\(M=\frac{c}{a} =\frac{c}{\sqrt{\gamma RT} } \) (1.33)

 

\( M_{A}=\frac{c_{A}}{\sqrt{\gamma RT_{A}} } =0.703 \)

 

From Eq. (1.36)

\(\frac{p_{0} }{p} = \left(\frac{T_{0} }{T}\right) ^{\gamma/\left(\gamma -1\right) } =\left(1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2} M^{2} \right) ^{\gamma/\left(\gamma -1\right)} \) (1.36)

 

\( p_{0A} = p_{A} \left(1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2} M^{2}_{A} \right) ^{\frac{\gamma }{\gamma -1} }=250 kPa \)

 

From Eq. (1.38)

\(\frac{\rho_{0} }{\rho} = \left(1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2} M^{2} \right) ^{1/\left(\gamma -1\right) } \) (1.38)

 

\( \rho _{0A} = \rho _{A} \left(1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2} M^{2}_{A} \right) ^{\frac{1 }{\gamma -1} } \) where \(\rho _{A} =\frac{p_{A} }{RT_{A} } =1.991 kg/m^{3} \)

 

\( \therefore \rho _{0A} = 2.52 kg/m^{3} \)

 

Here, it will be obvious that the stagnation density can be evaluated more directly using the gas law:

\(\rho _{0A} =\frac{p_{0A} }{RT_{0A} } =2.52 kg/m^{3} \)

 

There are also two ways to evaluate the air mass flow rate. Using Eq. (1.8)

\( \dot{m} =\rho _{1} c_{1} A_{n1} =\rho _{2} c_{2} A_{n2}=\rho c A_{n} \) (1.8)

 

\( \dot{m} =\rho _{A} A_{A} c_{A} = 1.99\times 0.1\times 250 =49.8 kg/s \)

 

Alternatively, from Eq. (1.39) or Table C.1,

\( \frac{\dot{m} \sqrt{C_{p} T_{0} } }{A_{n} p_{0} } =\frac{\gamma }{\sqrt{\gamma -1} } M \left(1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2} M^{2} \right) ^{-\frac{1 }{2}\left(\frac{\gamma +1}{\gamma -1} \right) } \) (1.39)

 

\( \frac{\dot{m} \sqrt{C_{p} T_{0A} } }{p_{0A} A_{A} } = f \left(0.703\right)=1.1728 \)

\( \therefore \dot{m}=1.1728\times \frac{p_{0A} A_{A}}{ \sqrt{C_{p} T_{0A} } } =49.7 kg/s \)
