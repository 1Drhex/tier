Guest Question.

A force of 5 kN is acting along AB, where position vector of point A is \( \overline{r}_{A} \) = (2 i + 3 j – 5 k) m and \( \overline {r}_{B} \) = (5 j – 3 k) m. Another force acting at point B 6 kN and it makes angles 30° and 75° with x and y axes respectively. Find (i) resultant of the two forces, (ii) component of resultant along line BD where coordinates of D are (3, 2, -1) m and (iii) moment of the resultant about line CD where coordinate of C are (-2, 5, 0) m.

Admin
Solution

(i) Resultant of the two forces

(a) Coordinates

A(2, 3, -5) m, B(0, 5, -3) m.

(b) Force vector (\( \overline{F}_{1} \))

Magnitude \( F_1 = 5 \) kN.

\( \overline{F}_{1} = (5) (\overline{e}_{AB})\).

\( \overline{F}_{1} = (5) \biggl[\frac{-2 i + 2 j + 2 k}{\sqrt{2^2+2^2+2^2} }\biggr] \).

\( \overline{F}_{1} = -2.89 i + 2.89 j + 2.89 \) k.

(c) Force vector (\( \overline{F}_{2} \) ).

Magnitude \( F_2 = 6 kN ; θ_x = 30° , θ_y = 75° \).

By direction cosine rule, we have

\( \cos θ_z = + \sqrt{1-\cos^2 θ_x – \cos^ θ_y} \) .

\( \therefore θ_z = 64.67° \) .

\( \overline{F}_{2} = (6) \biggl[ (\cos 30° ) i + (\cos 75° ) j + (\cos 64.67° )\biggr] k \) .

\( \overline{F}_{2} = 5.196 i + 1.553 j + 2.567 \) k.

(d) Resultant force vector

\( \overline{R} = \overline{F}_{1}+\overline{F}_{2} \) .

\( \overline{R} = 2.306 i + 4.443 j + 5.457 \) k (kN) Ans.

(ii) Component of resultant along line BD (\( R_{BD} \) )

(a) Coordinates

B(0, 5, -3) m , D(3, 2, 1) m.

(b) Unit vector (\( \overline{e}_{BD} \) ).

\( \overline{e}_{BD} = \frac{\overline{BD} }{\left|\overline{BD}\right| } = \frac{3 i – 3 j + 4 k}{\sqrt{3^2+3^2+4^2} } \) .

\( \overline{e}_{BD} = 0.514 i – 0.514 j + 0.686 \) k.

(c) Scalar component (\( R_{BD} \) )

\( R_{BD} = \overline{R} . \overline{e}_{BD} \) (dot product)

\( R_{BD} = (2.306 i + 4.443 j + 5.457 k ) . (0.514 i – 0.514 j + 0.686 k) \).

\( R_{BD} = 2.645 \) kN Ans.

(iii) Moment of resultant about line CD (\( M_{CD} \) ).

(a) Coordinates

B(0, 5, -3) m ,C(-2, 5, 0) m , D(3, 2, 1) m.

(b) Moment vector (\( \overline{M}_{C} \) )

Resultant force vector

\( \overline{R} = 2.306 i + 4.443 j + 5.457 \) k.

Position vector

\( \overline{r}_{CB} = \overline{CB} = 2 i + 0 j – 3 \) k.

\( \overline{M}_{C} = \overline{r}_{CB} × \overline{R} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 2 & 0 & -3\\ 2.306 & 4.44 & 5.457 \end{vmatrix} \) .

\( \overline{M}_{C} = 13.32 i – 17.818 j + 8.88 \) k.

(c) Unit vector (\( \overline{e}_{CD} \) ).

\( \overline{e}_{CD} = \frac{\overline{CD} }{\left|\overline{CD}\right| } = \frac{5 i – 3 j + k}{\sqrt{5^2+3^2+1^2} } \) .

\( \overline{e}_{BD} = 0.845 i – 0.507 j + 0.169 \) k.

(d) Moment of resultant about line CD (\( M_{CD} \) ).

\( M_{CD} = \overline{M}_{C} . \overline{e}_{CD} \) (dot product)

\( M_{CD} = (13.32 i – 17.818 j + 8.88 k) . (0.845 i – 0.507 j + 0.169 k) \) .

\( M_{CD} = 21.79 \) kN-m Ans.
