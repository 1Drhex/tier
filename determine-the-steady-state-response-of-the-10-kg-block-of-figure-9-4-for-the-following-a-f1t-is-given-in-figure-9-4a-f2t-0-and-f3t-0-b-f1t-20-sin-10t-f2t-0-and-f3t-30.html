Guest Question.

Determine the steady-state response of the 10 kg block of Figure 9.4 for the following.
(a) \(F_1(t)\) is given in Figure 9.4(a), \(F_2(t) = 0\), and \(F_3(t) = 0\)
(b) \(F_1(t) =20 \sin 10t, F_2(t) =0\), and \(F_3(t) = 30 \sin 20t\)

Admin
Solution

The differential equations governing the motion of the three degree-of-freedom system of Figure 9.4 are

\(\begin{bmatrix}10 & 0 & 0 & \\0 & 12 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 14\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}\ddot{x}_{1}\\ \ddot{x}_{2}\\ \ddot{x}_{3} \end{bmatrix} +\begin{bmatrix} 50 & 0 & 0\\0 & 100 & -100\\0 & -100 & 100 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \dot{x}_{1}\\x_{2}\\x_{3} \end{bmatrix}\)

\(+\begin{bmatrix} 1500 & -100 & 0\\-1000 & 1700 & -700\\0 & -700 & 700 \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} x_{1}\\x_{2}\\x_{3} \end{bmatrix} =\begin{bmatrix}F_{1}(t)\\F_{2}(t)\\F_{3}(t)\end{bmatrix} \) (a)

Taking the Laplace transform of Equation (a) and using all initial conditions as zero leads to

\(\begin{bmatrix} 10s^{2}+50s+1500 & -1000 & 0 \\-1000 & 12s^{2}+100s +1700 & -100s-700\\0 & -100s-700 & 14s^{2}-100s+100 \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} X_{1}(s)\\X_{2}(s)\\X_{3}(s) \end{bmatrix}\)

\(=\begin{bmatrix} F_{1}(s) \\ F_{2}(s)\\F_{3}(s)\end{bmatrix} \) (b)

The matrix in Equation (b) is Z(s).
(a) Taking the Laplace transform of the excitation leads to

\(\begin{bmatrix} 10s^{2}+50s+1500 & -1000 & 0 \\-1000 & 12s^{2}+100s +1700 & -100s-700\\0 & -100s-700 & 14s^{2}-100s+100 \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix} X_{1}(s)\\X_{2}(s)\\X_{3}(s) \end{bmatrix}\)

\(\begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{s}(1-e^{-0.5t}) \\ 0\\F_{0/s}\end{bmatrix}\) (c)

The inverse of Z(s) is obtained as

\(\pmb{Z}^{-1}(s)\)

\(=\frac{1}{D(s)}\left[\begin{matrix} 2.1s^{4}+32.5s^{3}+402.5s^{2}+1250s+8750 \\ 175s^{2}+1250s+8750 \\ 1250s+8750 \end{matrix} \right. \\ \left.\begin{matrix}&&&&&175s^{4}+1250s+8750\\&&&&&7s^{4}+85s^{3}+1650s^{2}+9250s+52,500 \\&&&&&25s^{3} +300s^{3}+4625s+26250 \end{matrix} \right. \\ \left.\begin{matrix}&&&&& 1250s+8750 \\&&&&& 25s^{3}+300s^{3}+4625s+26,250 \\&& &&&3s^{4}+40s^{3}+1000s^{2}+58,755+38,750 \end{matrix} \right] \) (d)

where

\(D(s)=21s^{6}+430s^{5}+8800s^{4}+81,375s^{3}+578750s^{2}\\ +1,062,500s+4,375,000\) (e)

The roots of D(s) are obtained as

\( s=-3.278\pm 13.95i, -6.550\pm 7.67i, -0.4097\pm 3.13i\) (f)

Multiplying F(s) by \(Z^{-1}(s)\) and solving for \(X_1(s)\) leads to

\(X_{1}(s)=\frac{(21s^{4}+32.5s^{3}+402.5s^{2}+1250s+8750)(1-e^{-0.5s})}{s(21s^{6}+430s^{2}+8800s^{4}+81,375s^{3}+578,750s^{2}+1,062,500s+4,365,000)}\) (g)

A partial fraction decomposition of Equation (g) leads to

\(\begin{aligned}X_{1}(s)=& 10^{-4}\left(\frac{20}{s}+\frac{-14 s+16}{s^{2}+0.820 s+9.96}+\frac{-2.93 s+19}{s^{2}+13.71 s+101.73}\right.\\&\left.+\frac{-3.53 s+13}{s^{2}+6.54 s+205.2}\right)\left(1-e^{-0.5 s}\right)\end{aligned}\) (h)

Inversion of the transform yields

\(x_{1}(t)=10^{-4} \begin{cases}20+e^{0.409t}(-14\cos3.16t+4.081\sin3.16t)\end{cases} \)

\(+e^{-6.56t}(-2.93\cos10.08t+2.46\sin10.08t) \\ +e^{-3.28}(-3.53\cos14.32t+1.40 \sin 14.32t)\\-u(t-0.5) \begin{cases} 20+e^{-0.409(t-0.5)} \end{cases} \left. \right\}[-14\cos3.16(t-0.5)\\ + 4.801\sin3.16(t-0.5] e^{-6.56(t-0.5)} [-2.93\cos10.08(t-0.5) \\ +2.36\sin10.08(t-0.5) ] +e^{-3.28(t-0.5)}[-3.53\cos14.32(t-0.5) \\ +1.4\sin14.32(t-0.5)]\left. \right\}\left.\right\}\)

(i)

(b) From Equation (9.16) for the given forces

\(x_{1}(t)=20\left|G_{1,1}(10j)\right|\sin(10t+\phi _{1,1})+30\left|G_{1,3}(20j)\right| \sin(20t+\phi _{1,3}) \) (j)

\(x_{k}(t)=\sum\limits_{j-1}^{n}{\left|G_{k,j}(i\omega _{j})\right| F_{j(\omega _{j}t+\phi_{k,j} )}}\) (9.16)

where

\(G_{1,1}(10i)\)

\(=\frac{2.1(10i)^{4}+32.5(10i)^{3}+402.5(10i)^{2}+1250(10i)+8750}{\begin{matrix}21(10i)^{6}+430(10i)^{5}+8800(10i)^{4}+81,375(10i)^{3} \\ +578,750(10i)^{2}+1,062,500(10i)+4,375,000 \end{matrix} }\)

\(=\frac{-1.05\times10^{4}-2.00\times10^{4}i}{1.26\times10^{7}-2.775\times10^{7i}} = 4.55\times 10^{-4}-5.85\times 10^{-4}i\) (k)

and

\(G_{1,3}(20i)\)

\(=\frac{1250(20i)+8750}{\begin{matrix}21(20i)^{6}+430(20i)^{5}+8800(20i)^{4}+81,375(20i)^{3} \\ +578,750(20i){2}+1,062,500(20i)+4,375,000 \end{matrix} }\)

\(=\frac{8.75\times 10^{3}+2.5\times10^{4}i}{-1.671\times10^{8}+7.463\times 10^{8}i}=2.94\times10^{-5}-1.83\times10^{-5}i\) (l)

The steady-state solution is

\(x_{1}(t)=20(7.414\times10^{-4})\sin(10t-0.910)++30(3.463\times10^{-5})\sin(20t-0.557)\)

\(-0.0148 \sin (10t-0.910)+0.00106\sin(20t-0.557)\) (m)
