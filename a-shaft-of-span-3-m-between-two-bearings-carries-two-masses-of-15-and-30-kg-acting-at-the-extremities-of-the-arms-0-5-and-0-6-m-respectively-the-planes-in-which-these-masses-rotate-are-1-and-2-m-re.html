Guest Question.

A shaft of span 3 m between two bearings carries two masses of 15 and 30 kg acting at the extremities of the arms 0.5 and 0.6 m, respectively. The planes in which these masses rotate are 1 and 2 m, respectively, from the left end bearing. The angle between the arms is 60°. The speed of rotation of the shaft is 240 rpm. If the masses are balanced by two counter masses rotating with the shaft acting at radii of 0.25 m and placed at 0.3 m from each bearing centre, determine the magnitude of the two balance masses and their orientation with respect to the 15 kg mass.

From Table 12.21, we have

\( \sum M_{i} r_{i}^{*} \cos \theta_{i}=16.5 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=15.5884 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=20.55 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=26.5 \).

\( \left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=M_{M} r_{M} l_{M} \).

\( \left[(26.5)^{2}+(20.55)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=0.675 M_{M} \).

\( M_{M}=\frac{34.534}{0.675}=49.68 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{M}=\frac{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}}{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}}=\frac{-(26.5)}{-(20.55)}=1.28954 \).

\( \theta_{M}=52.2^{\circ} \text { i.e. } 232.2^{\circ} \).

Since the numerator and denominator are both negative therefore \( \theta_{M} \) lies the in the third quadrant.

\( M_{L} r_{L}=\left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \cos \theta_{M}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \sin \theta_{M}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( 0.25 M_{L}=\left[\left(0.25 \times 49.68 \cos 232.2^{\circ}+16.5\right)^{2}+\left(0.25 \times 49.68 \sin 232.2^{\circ}+15.5884\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( =\left[(8.888)^{2}+(5.7746)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=10.599 \).

\( M_{L}=42.4 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{L}=\frac{-\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \sin \theta_{M}\right)}{-\left(\sum M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \cos \theta_{M}\right)}=\frac{-5.7746}{-8.888}=0.6497 \).

\( \theta_{L}=33^{\circ} \text { i.e. } 213^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both negative, therefore } \theta L \text { lies in the third quadrant. } \)
