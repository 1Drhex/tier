Guest Question.

For the loaded emitter-follower configuration of Fig. 10.27 with a source resistance
and the no-load two-port parameters of \(Z_{i} = 157.54 k \Omega , Z_{o} = 21.6 \Omega\) , and \(A_{v_{NL}} = 0.993\) with \(r_{e} = 21.74 \Omega\) and \(\beta = 65\), determine:
(a) The new values of \(Z_{i}\) and \(Z_{o}\) as determined by the load and \( R_{s}\), respectively.
(b) \(A_{v}\) using the systems approach.
(c) \(A_{v_{s}}\) using the systems approach.
(d) \(A_{i} = I_{o}/I_{i}\).

Admin
Solution

Eq. (10.37): \(Z_{i} = R_{B}\parallel \beta(r_{e} + R_{E}\parallel R_{L}) = 560 k \Omega\parallel 65(21.74 \Omega + \underbrace{3.3 k \Omega\parallel 2.2 k \Omega}_{1.32 k \Omega} = 560 k \Omega\parallel 87.21 k \Omega = 75.46 k \Omega\)

versus \(157.54 k \Omega \) (no-load).

\(Z_{o} = R_{E}\parallel \left(\frac{R_{s}}{\beta} + r_{e}\right) = 3.3 k \Omega\parallel \left(\frac{0.56}{65} + 21.74 \Omega\right) = 3.3 k \Omega \parallel 30.36 \Omega = 30.08 \Omega\)

versus \(21.6 \Omega\) (no \(R_{s}\)):

(b) Substituting the two-port equivalent network will result in the small-signal ac
equivalent network of Fig. 10.28.

\(V_{o} = \frac{R_{L} A_{v_{NL}} V_{i}}{R_{L} + R_{o}} = \frac{(2.2 k \Omega)(0.993)V_{i}}{2.2 k \Omega + 30.08 \Omega}\cong 0.98 V_{i}\)

with \(A_{v} = \frac{V_{o}}{V_{i}} \cong 0.98\)

(c) \(V_{i} = \frac{Z_{i} V_{s}}{Z_{i} + R_{s}} = \frac{(75.46 k \Omega) V_{s}}{75.46 k \Omega + 0.56 k \Omega} = 0.993 V_{s}\)

so that \(A_{v_{s}} = \frac{V_{o}}{V_{s}} = \frac{V_{o}}{V_{i}}\frac{V_{i}}{V_{s}} = (0.98)(0.993) = 0.973\)

(d) \(A_{i} = \frac{I_{o}}{I_{i}} = – A_{v} \frac{Z_{i}}{R_{L}} = – (0.98)\left(\frac{75.46 k \Omega}{2.2 k \Omega}\right) = – 33.61\)
