Guest Question.

The length of the crank of a reciprocating engine is 120 mm and its connecting rod is 600 mm long. It rotates at 360 rpm and at a particular instant makes an angle of 50째 with the inner dead centre. Find the (a) velocity and acceleration of the piston, (b) velocity and acceleration of the midpoint of the connecting rod, and (c) angular velocity and angular acceleration of the connecting rod.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } r=120 mm , l=600 mm , N=360 rpm , \theta=50^{\circ} \).

\( n=\frac{l}{r}=\frac{600}{120}=5 \).

\( \omega=\frac{2 \pi \times 360}{60}=37.7 rad / s \).

The slider-crank mechanism is shown in Fig.10.2(a).

(a) \( v_{p}=\omega r\left[\sin \theta+\frac{\sin 2 \theta}{2 n}\right]=37.7 \times 0.12\left[\sin 50^{\circ}+\frac{\sin 100^{\circ}}{10}\right] \).

\( =3.91 m / s \).

\( f_{p}=\omega^{2} r\left[\cos \theta+\frac{\cos 2 \theta}{n}\right] \).

\( =(37.7)^{2} \times 0.12\left[\cos 50^{\circ}+\frac{\cos 100^{\circ}}{5}\right] \).

\( =103.7 m / s ^{2} \).

(b) \( v_{c}=\omega r=37.7 \times 0.12=4.524 m / s \).

Draw velocity polygon as shown in Fig 10.2(b) to a scale of 1 cm=1 m/s.

\( v_{c}=o c \perp O C, c _{p} \perp C P, o p \| O P \).

Velocity of midpoint of connecting rod,

\( v_{b}=o b=4 cm =4 m / s \).

Velocity of \( C P=c p=3 cm =3 m / s \).

\( v_{p}=o p=4 cm =4 m / s \).

\( f_{c}^{n}=\frac{v_{c}^{2}}{r}=\frac{(4.524)^{2}}{0.12}=170.55 m / s ^{2} \).

\( f_{c p}^{n}=\frac{v_{c p}^{2}}{C P}=\frac{9}{0.6}=15 m / s ^{2} \).

\( \text { Draw acceleration diagram as shown in Fig.10.2 (c) to scale of } 1 cm =20 m / s ^{2} \).

\( o c=f_{c}^{n}\left\|O C, f_{c p}^{n}=c p^{\prime}\right\| C P^{\prime}, f_{c p}^{t}=p^{\prime} p \perp C P, o p=f_{p} \| O P \).

\( f_{b}=o b=6.2 cm =124 m / s ^{2} \).

(c) \( \omega_{r}=\frac{\omega \cos \theta}{n^{2}-\sin ^{2} \theta} \).

\( =\frac{37.7 \times \cos 50^{\circ}}{25-\sin ^{2} 50^{\circ}} \).

\( =4.9 rad / s \).

\( \alpha_{r}=\frac{-\omega^{2} \sin \theta}{n}=\frac{-(37.7)^{2} \sin 50^{\circ}}{5}=-217.7 rad / s ^{2} \).
