Guest Question.

Design an exhaust valve for a horizontal diesel engine using the following data:
Cylinder bore = 150 mm
Length of stroke = 275 mm
Engine speed = 500 rpm
Maximum gas pressure = 3.5 MPa
Seat angle = 45°
Calculate:
(i) diameter of the valve port;
(ii) diameter of the valve head;
(iii) thickness of the valve head;
(iv) diameter of the valve stem; and
(v) maximum lift of the valve.

Admin
Solution

Given D = 150 mm l = 275 mm N = 500 rpm

\( p_{\max .}=3.5 MPa =3.5 N / mm ^{2} \quad \alpha=45^{\circ} \).

Step I Diameter of the valve port

\( a=\text { area of piston }=\left(\frac{\pi(150)^{2}}{4}\right) mm ^{2} \).

v = mean velocity of piston

\( =2 l\left(\frac{N}{60}\right)=2\left(\frac{275}{1000}\right)\left(\frac{500}{60}\right) m / s \).

Assume [from Table 25.2],

Table 25.2 Allowable mean velocities of the gas \( \left(v_{p}\right) \)

Mean velocity of gas (m/s) Type of engine
Exhaust valve Inlet valve
40–50 33–40 Low-speed
engine
50–60 35–45 Medium speed
engine
90–100 80–90 High-speed
engine
\( v_{p}=50 m / s \)

\( a_{p}=\left(\frac{\pi d_{p}^{2}}{4}\right) mm ^{2} \).

Therefore,

\( a \times v=a_{p} \times v_{p} \).

\( \left(\frac{\pi(150)^{2}}{4}\right) \times 2\left(\frac{275}{1000}\right)\left(\frac{500}{60}\right)=\left(\frac{\pi d_{p}^{2}}{4}\right) \times(50) \).

\( d_{p}^{2}=2062.5 mm ^{2} \).

\( d_{p}=45.41 mm \text { or } 46 mm \). (i)

Step II Diameter of valve head
For a seat angle of 45°, the projected width of the valve seat is given by the following empirical relationship:

\( w=(0.05 \text { to } 0.07) d_{p} \)

or \( w=0.06 d_{p}=0.06(46)=2.76 \text { or } 3 mm \).

\( \text { The diameter of the valve head }\left(d_{v}\right) \text { is given by, } \)

\( d_{v}=\left(d_{p}+2 w\right)=46+2 \times 3=52 mm \). (ii).

Step III Thickness of the valve head For a steel valve,

\( k=0.42 \quad \sigma_{b}=50 N / mm ^{2} \).

\( t=k d_{p} \sqrt{\frac{p_{\max } .}{\sigma_{b}}}=(0.42)(46) \sqrt{\frac{3.5}{50}}=5.11 \).

= 5.5 mm.

Step IV Diameter of valve stem

\( d_{s}=\left[\frac{d_{p}}{8}+6.35\right] \text { to }\left[\frac{d_{p}}{8}+11\right] \).

\( =\left[\frac{46}{8}+6.35\right] \text { to }\left[\frac{46}{8}+11\right] \).

= 12.1 to 16.75 mm

\( d_{s}=15 mm \) (iv).

Step V Maximum lift of valve

\( h_{\max .}=\frac{d_{p}}{4 \cos \alpha}=\frac{46}{4 \cos (45)}=16.26 mm \) (v).
