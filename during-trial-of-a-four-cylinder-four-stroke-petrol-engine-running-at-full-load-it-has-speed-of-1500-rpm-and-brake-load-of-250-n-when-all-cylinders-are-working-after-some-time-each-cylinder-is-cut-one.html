Guest Question.

During trial of a four cylinder four stroke petrol engine running at full load it has speed of 1500 rpm and brake load of 250 N when all cylinders are working. After some time each cylinder is cut one by one and then again brought back to same speed of engine. The brake readings are measured as 175 N, 180 N, 182 N and 170 N. The brake drum radius is 50 cm. The fuel consumption rate is 0.189 kg/min with the fuel whose calorific value is 43 MJ/kg and A/F ratio of 12. Exhaust gas temperature is found to be 600ºC. The cooling water flows at 18 kg/min and enters at 27ºC and leaves at 50ºC. The atmospheric air temperature is 27ºC. Take specific heat of exhaust gas as 1.02 kJ/kg K. Determine the brake power output of engine, its indicated power and mechanical efficiency. Also draw a heat balance on per minute basis.

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}\text{Brake power output of engine}&= \frac{2π NT}{60} = \frac{2π × 1500 × 250 × 0.50 × 10^{–3}}{60} = 19.63 kW\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Brake power when cylinder 1 is cut}&= \frac{2 × π × 1500 × 175 × 0.5 × 10^{–3}}{60} = 13.74 kW\end{aligned}\\\text{Indicated power of first cylinder = 19.63 – 13.74 = 5.89 kW}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Brake power when cylinder 2 is cut}&= \frac{2 × π × 1500 × 180 × 0.5 × 10^{–3}}{60} = 14.14 kW\\\text{Indicated power of second cylinder}&= \text{19.63 – 14.14 = 5.49 kW}\\\text{Brake power when cylinder 3 is cut}&= \frac{2 × π × 1500 × 182 × 0.5 × 10^{–3}}{60} = 14.29 kW\end{aligned}\\\text{Indicated power of third cylinder = 19.63 – 14.29 = 5.34 kW}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Brake power when cylinder 4 is cut}&= \frac{2 × π × 1500 × 170 × 0.5 × 10^{–3}}{60} = 13.35 kW\end{aligned}\\\text{Indicated power of fourth cylinder = 19.63 – 13.35 = 6.28 kW}\\\text{Total indicated power output = 5.89 + 5.49 + 5.34 + 6.28 = 23 kW}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Engine’s mechanical efficiency}&= \frac{\text{Brake power}}{\text{Indicated power}} = \frac{19.63}{23} = \text{0.8535 or 85.35}\%\end{aligned}\\\text{B.P. = 19.63 kW, I.P. = 23 kW, }η_{mech} = \textbf{83.35}\pmb{\%}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Heat liberated by fuel, kJ/min}&= 0.189 × 43 × 10^{3} = 8127 kJ/min\\\text{Heat carried by exhaust gases}&= (12 + 1) × 0.189 × 1.02 × (600 – 27) = 1436.02 kJ/min\\\text{Heat carried by cooling water}&= 18 × 4.18 × (50 – 27) = 1730.52 kJ/min\\\text{Energy to brake power}&= 19.63 × 60 = 1177.8 kJ/min\\\text{Unaccounted losses}&= 8127 – (1436.02 + 1730.52 + 1177.8) = 3782.66 kJ/min\end{aligned}\\\qquad\qquad\qquad \text{Heat balance sheet on per minute basis}\)
