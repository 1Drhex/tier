Solution

Using Eq. 1.5.3 \(\rho_{H_2O}=1000-\frac{(T-4)^2}{180} \), \(\gamma_{H_2O}=9800-\frac{(T-4)^2}{18} \) we have

\(\rho =1000-{(T-4)^2}/{180}=1000 − (70 − 4)^2/180 = \underline{976 kg/m^3} \) ,

\(\gamma =9800-{(T-4)^2}/{18}= 9800 − (70 − 4)^2/180 = \underline{9560 N/m^3} \)

Using Table B.1 the density and specific weight at 70°C are

\(\rho =977.8 kg/m^3\)

 

\(\gamma =977.8\times 9.81 9592.2 N/m^3 \)
