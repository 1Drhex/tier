Guest Question.

Consider the induction machine in and simulate its dynamic response during free acceleration when it is subjected to the rated volt-age and frequency and accelerates from standstill. Assume there is no active load torque on the shaft and that the damping coefficient (B) is negligible.

Admin
Solution

Let us use the machine parameters in their original units. The Equations 4.19,

\(\lambda _{qs}=(L_{ls}+L_{M})i_{qs}+L_{M}i_{qr}\)

\(\lambda _{ds}=(L_{ls}+L_{M})i_{ds}+L_{M}i_{dr}\)
\(\lambda _{0s}=L_{ls}i_{0s}\)
\(\lambda _{qr}=(L_{lr}+L_{M})i_{qr}+L_{M}i_{qs}\)
\(\lambda _{dr}=(L_{lr}+L_{M})i_{dr}+L_{M}i_{ds}\)
\(\lambda _{0r}=L_{ls}i_{0r}\)

4.21,

\(\upsilon _{qs}=r_{s}i_{qs}+\omega \lambda _{ds}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{qs}\)

 

\(\upsilon _{ds}=r_{s}i_{ds}-\omega \lambda _{qs}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{ds}\)

 

\(\upsilon _{0s}=r_{s}i_{0s}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{0s}\)

 

\((0=)\upsilon _{qs}=r_{r}i_{qr}-(\omega -\omega _{r})\lambda _{dr}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{qr}\)

 

\((0=)\upsilon _{dr}=r_{r}i_{dr}-(\omega -\omega _{r})\lambda _{qr}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{dr}\)

 

\(\upsilon_{0r}=r_{r}i_{0r}+\frac{d}{dt}\lambda _{0r}\)

4.25,

\(T_{e}=\frac{3}{2}\frac{P}{2}L_{M}(i_{qs}i_{dr}-i_{ds}i_{qr})\)

and 4.26

Step 1: Solve the electrical state equations of the machine

\(\lambda _{qs}(t+\Delta t)=\lambda _{qs}(t)+(\upsilon _{qs}(t)-r_{s}i_{qs}(t)-\omega _{e}\lambda_{ds}(t))\Delta t\)

\(T_{e}(t+\Delta t)=\frac{3}{2}\frac{P}{2}L_{M}(i_{qs}(t+\Delta t)i_{dr}(t+\Delta t)-i_{ds}(t+\Delta t)i_{qr}(t+\Delta t))\)

\(\omega _{m}(t+\Delta t)=\omega _{m}(t)+\frac{1}{J}(T_{e}(t+\Delta t )-T_{L}-B\omega _{m}(t))\Delta t \)

Step 5: Obtain the rotor speed in electrical rad/s

\(\omega _{r}(t+\Delta t)=\frac{P}{2}\omega _{m}(t+\Delta t) \)

Step 6: Proceed to step 1
