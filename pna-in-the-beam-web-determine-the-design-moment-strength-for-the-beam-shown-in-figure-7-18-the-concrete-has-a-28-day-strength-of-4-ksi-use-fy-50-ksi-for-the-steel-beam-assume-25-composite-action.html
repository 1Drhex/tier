Guest Question.

PNA in the Beam Web

Determine the design moment strength for the beam shown in Figure 7-18. The concrete has a 28-day strength of 4 ksi; use \(F_y = 50\) ksi for the steel beam. Assume 25% composite action.

Admin
Solution

From AISCM, Table 1-1,

Table 1-1 Structural steel material properties

\(F_{u} \)
(ksi)		 \(F_{y} \)
(ksi)		 ASTM Designation or Grade of Structural Steel Steel Type

60 35 A53 Grade B

\(\underline{\underline{W18 \times 35} }\)

\(A=10.3in^{2}\) \(t_{f}=0.425\) in.

\( d = 17.7\) in. \(b_{f}=600\) in.

\(I = 510 in^{4}\) \(t_{w}=0.0300\) in.

\(C_{c}\)= Smaller of \(0.85f^{′}_{c}A_{c} or F_{y}A_{s}\)(V′ from eqs. (7-4) and (7-5))

\(V^{′}=0.85f^{′}_{c}A_{c} \) (7-4)

\(0.85f^{′}{c}A_{c} = (0.85)(4)(50)(4)=680 \) kips

\(V^{′}=F_{y}A_{s}\) (7-5)

\(F_{y}A_{s}=(50)(10.3) =515 kips ←\) Controls

Assuming 25% composite action, \(C_{c}= (0.25)(515) = 129\) kips.

\(C_{c}=0.85f^{′}_{c}ab\rightarrow 129\) kips= (0.85)(4)(a)(50 in.) → a = 0.757 in.

\(Y_{2} =h_{r}+t_{c}-\frac{a}{2}+4-\frac{0.757}{2} = 5.62\) in.

Use equations (7-22) and (7-23), to determine\( Y_{1}\)as follows, (if \( Y_{1}\) is greater than \(t_{f}\), then PNA is, in fact, in the beam web):

\(Y_{1} =\frac{A_{s}F_{y}-0.85f^{′}_{c}A_{c}-2b_{f}t_{f}F_{y}}{2t_{w}F_{y} } +t_{f}\) (7-22)

\( =\frac{(10.3)(50) – (0.85)(4)(50)(0.757) – (2)(6.0)(0.425)(50)}{(2)(0.300)(50)} +0.425=4.80\) in.

Since \(Y_{1}\) is greater than the beam flange thickness (\(Y_{1}= 4.80\) in. > \(t_{f}=0.425\) in.) the PNA must be in the beam web. The design bending strength is then found from equation (7-23) as follows:

\(\phi M_{n}=\phi \left[0.85f^{′}_{c}A_{c}(Y_{1}+Y_{2})+2b_{f}t_{f}F_{y}\left(Y_{1}-\frac{t_{f} }{2} \right)+2t_{w}F_{y}(Y_{1}-t_{f} )\left(\frac{Y_{1}-t_{f} }{2} \right)+A_{s}F_{y}\left(\frac{d}{2}-Y_{1} \right) \right]\) (7-23)

\(\phi M_{n}=0.9\left[(0.85)(4)(50)(0.757)(4.80 + 5.62) + (2)(6.0)(0.425)(50)\left(4.80-\frac{0.425}{2} \right)…. \right]\)

\(\left[….+(2)(0.300)(50)(4.80 – 0.425)\left(\frac{4.80 – 0.425}{2} \right)+(10.3)(50)\left(\frac{17.7}{2}-4.80 \right) \right] =4396\) in.-kips

\(\phi M_{n}=367\) ft.-kips
