Guest Question.

Determine the reactions and draw the shear and bending moment diagrams for the beam shown in \(Fig. 14.3(a)\) by using the three-moment equation.

Admin
Solution

Redundant The beam has one degree of indeterminacy. The bending
moment \(M_B\), at the interior support \(B\), is the redundant.

Three-Moment Equation at Joint \(B\) Considering the supports, \(A, B\), and \(C\) as \(l, c\), and \(r\), respectively, and substituting \(L_l = 24 ft, L_r = 20 ft, I_l = 2I , I_r = I , P_{l1} = 30 k, k_{l1} = 1/3, P_{l2} = 20 k, k_{l2} = 2/3, w_r = 2.5 k/ft\), and \(P_r = w_l = \Delta_l = \Delta_c = \Delta_r = 0\), into Eq. (\(\frac{M_lL_l}{I_l}+ 2M_c(\frac{L_l}{I_l}+\frac{L_r}{I_r})\)

 

\(-\sum{\frac{P_l L^2_lk_l}{I_l}(1 – k^2_l)}-\sum{\frac{P_r L^2_rk_r}{I_r}(1 – k^2_r)} -\frac{w_lL^3_l}{4I_l}-\frac{w_rL^3_r}{4I_r}\)

 

\(-6E(\frac{\Delta_l – \Delta_c}{L_l}+\frac{\Delta_r – \Delta_c}{L_r})\)), we obtain

\(\frac{M_A(24)}{2I} + 2M_B(\frac{24}{2I}+\frac{20}{I}) + \frac{M_C(20)}{I}= -\frac{30(24)^2(1/3)}{2I}[1 – (1/3)^2]\)

 

\(-frac{20(24)^2(2/3)}{2I}[1 – (2/3)^2] -\frac{2.5(20)^3}{4I}\)

Since \(A\) and \(C\) are simple end supports, we have by inspection

\(M_A = M_C = 0\)

Thus, the three-moment equation becomes

\(64M_B = -9,693.33\)

from which we obtain the redundant bending moment to be

\(M_B = -151.5 k-ft\) Ans.

Span End Shears and Reactions The shears at the ends of the spans \(AB\) and \(BC\) of the continuous beam can now be determined by applying the equations of equilibrium to the free bodies of the spans shown in Fig. 14.3(b). Note that the negative bending moment \(M_B\) is applied at the ends \(B\) of spans \(AB\) and \(BC\) so that it causes tension in the upper fibers and compression in the lower fibers of the beam. By considering the equilibrium of span \(AB\), we obtain

\(+\circlearrowleft \sum{M_B}=0\) \(-A_y(24) + 30(16) + 20(8) – 151.5 = 0\)

 

\(A_y = 20.4 k \uparrow\) Ans.

\(+\uparrow \sum{F_y}=0\) \(20.4 – 30 – 20 + B^{AB}_y=0\)

 

\(B^{AB}_y=29.6 k \uparrow\)

Similarly, for span \(BC\),

\(+\circlearrowleft \sum{M_C}=0\) \(-B^{BC}_y(20) + 151.5 + 2.5(20)(10) = 0\)

 

\(B^{BC}_y = 32.6 k \uparrow\)

 

\(+\uparrow \sum{F_y}=0\) \(32.6 – 2.5(20) + C_y = 0\)

 

\(C_y = 17.4 k \uparrow\) Ans.

By considering the equilibrium of joint \(B\) in the vertical direction, we obtain

\(B_y =B^{AB}_y + B^{BC}_y = 29.6 + 32.6 = 62.2 k \uparrow\) Ans.

The reactions are shown in Fig. 14.3(c).

Shear and Bending Moment Diagrams See Fig. 14.3(d). Ans.
