Guest Question.

In the circuit of Fig. 9.47, find \(i_{o}\) when:

(a) ω = 1 rad/s

(b) ω = 5 rad/s

(c) ω = 10 rad/s

Admin
Solution

(a) For \(\omega=1\),

\(1 \mathrm{H} \longrightarrow \quad \mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{L}=\mathrm{j}(1)(1)=\mathrm{j}\)

 

\(0.05 \mathrm{F} \longrightarrow \frac{1}{\mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{C}}=\frac{1}{\mathrm{j}(1)(0.05)}=-\mathrm{j} 20\)

 

\({Z}=\mathrm{j}+2 \|(-\mathrm{j} 20)=\mathrm{j}+\frac{-\mathrm{j} 40}{2-\mathrm{j} 20}=1.98+\mathrm{j} 0.802\)

 

\({I}_{0}=\frac{{V}}{{Z}}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{1.98+\mathrm{j} 0.802}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{2.136 \angle 22.05^{\circ}}=1.872 \angle-22.05^{\circ}\)

 

Hence, \(\mathrm{i}_{\mathrm{o}}(\mathrm{t})={1.872} \cos \left(\mathrm{t}-22.05^{\circ}\right) \mathrm{A}\)

(b) For \(\omega=5\),

\(1 \mathrm{H} \longrightarrow \quad \mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{L}=\mathrm{j}(5)(1)=\mathrm{j} 5\)

 

\(0.05 \mathrm{F} \longrightarrow \frac{1}{\mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{C}}=\frac{1}{\mathrm{j}(5)(0.05)}=-\mathrm{j} 4\)

 

\({Z}=\mathrm{j} 5+2 \|(-\mathrm{j} 4)=\mathrm{j} 5+\frac{-\mathrm{j} 4}{1-\mathrm{j} 2}=1.6+\mathrm{j} 4.2\)

 

\({I}_{0}=\frac{{V}}{{Z}}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{1.6+\mathrm{j} 4}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{4.494 \angle 69.14^{\circ}}=0.89 \angle-69.14^{\circ}\)

 

Hence, \(\mathrm{i}_{\mathrm{o}}(\mathrm{t})={{0} .89} \cos \left(5 \mathrm{t}-69.14^{\circ}\right) \mathrm{A}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&(c) \text { For } \omega=10 \text { , }\\\\&1 \mathrm{H} \longrightarrow \quad \mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{L}=\mathrm{j}(10)(1)=\mathrm{j} 10\\\\&0.05 \mathrm{F} \longrightarrow \frac{1}{\mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{C}}=\frac{1}{\mathrm{j}(10)(0.05)}=-\mathrm{j} 2\\\\&{Z}=\mathrm{j} 10+2 \|(-\mathrm{j} 2)=\mathrm{j} 10+\frac{-\mathrm{j} 4}{2-\mathrm{j} 2}=1+\mathrm{j} 9\\\\&{I}_{\mathrm{o}}=\frac{{V}}{{Z}}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{1+\mathrm{j} 9}=\frac{4 \angle 0^{\circ}}{9.055 \angle 83.66^{\circ}}=0.4417 \angle-83.66^{\circ}\\\\&\text { Hence, } \mathrm{i}_{\mathrm{o}}(\mathrm{t})={{0} .4417 \cos \left(10 \mathrm{t}-83.66^{\circ}\right) \mathrm{A}}\end{aligned}\)
