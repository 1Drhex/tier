Gilotti’s Pizzeria Sales (Variance and Standard Deviation)
Calculate the standard deviation of daily sales for Gilotti Pizzeria, Location 1. From Table 2.3 the daily sales for Location 1 are:
6 8 10 12 14 9 11 7 13 11

Table 2.3 Gilotti’s Pizzeria Sales

VARIABLE MEAN MIN. Q1 MEDIAN Q3 MAX. IQR RANGE

Location 2 10.2 1.0 2.75 10.5 17.25 19.0 14.5 18.0
Location 3 18.4 2.0 15.00 21.0 25.00 26.0 10.0 24.0
Location 4 12.5 8.0 9.75 10.5 14.75 22.0 5.0 14.0

Solution

To calculate sample variance and standard deviation follow these three steps:
Step 1: Calculate the sample mean, x, using Equation 2.2. It is equal to 10.1.

\(\overline{x} =\frac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}{x_{i}}}{n}\)

Step 2: Find the difference between each of the daily sales and the mean of 10.1.
Step 3: Square each difference. The result is Table 2.4.

\(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{10}{x_{i}} = 101\) \(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{10}{(x_{i}-\overline{x})} =0\) \( \sum\limits_{i=1}^{10}{(x_{i}-\overline{x})^{2} } = 60.9\)
\(\overline{x}=\frac{\sum{x^{2}_{i} } }{n} =10.1\) \(s^{2}=\frac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}{(x_{i}-\overline{x})^{2} } }{n-1} =\frac{60.9}{9}=6.7\overline{6}\)
\( s=\sqrt{s^{2} } =\sqrt{6.7\overline{6}} \approx 2.6\)
