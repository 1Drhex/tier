Single-Plate Shear Connection

Determine the capacity of the connection shown in Figure 9-36 using the AISCM, Table 10-9. The bolts are 3⁄4-in.-diameter A490N in short-slotted holes transverse to the direction of the load (SSLT). The plate is ASTM A36 and the beam is ASTM A992, grade 50.

Admin
Solution
