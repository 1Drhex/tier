Guest Question.

Locate the centroid of the area bounded by the curve as shown in Fig. 5.10(a).

Admin
Solution

Step I : To locate \( \overline{x} \)

(i) Differential Area Element

Consider a small elemental strip of thickness dx at a distance x from y-axis and parallel to y-axis as shown in Fig. 5.10(b).

The area of the elementary strip is

dA = y dx.

(ii) Moment About the y-axis

Taking moment of area of the elemental strip about y-axis, we have

\( \overline{x} = \frac{\int{x} dA}{\int{dA} } = \frac{\int_{0}^{a}{xy}dx }{\int_{0}^{a}{y}dx } \) .

\(\therefore \overline{x} = \frac{\int_{0}^{a}{x\frac{ax^2}{10}dx } }{\int_{0}^{a}{\frac{ax^2}{10} }dx } \) .

\(\therefore \overline{x} = \frac{\int_{0}^{a}{\frac{a}{10} x^3}dx }{\int_{0}^{a}{\frac{a}{10} x^2} dx} \) .

\(\therefore \overline{x} = \frac{\frac{a}{10}\bigl[\frac{x^4}{4}\bigr]^a_0 }{\frac{a}{10}\bigl[\frac{x^3}{3}\bigr]^a_0} \) .

\(\therefore \overline{x} = \frac{a^4}{4} Ã— \frac{3}{a^3} \) .

\(\therefore \overline{x} = \frac{3}{4}a \) Ans.

Step II : To locate \( \overline{y} \)

(i) Differential Area Element

Consider a small elemental strip of thickness dy, which is at a distance y from x-axis and parallel to x-axis, as shown in Fig. 5.10(c).

Width of the element = (a – x).

The area of the elementary strip is

dA = (a – x) dy.

(ii) Moment About the x-axis

Taking moment of area of the elemental strip about the x-axis, we have

\( \overline{y} = \frac{\int{y} dA}{\int{dA} } = \frac{\int_{0}^{a/4}{y}(a-x)dy }{\int_{0}^{a/4}{(a-x)}dy } \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{\int_{0}^{a/4}{y\Bigl(a-\frac{\sqrt{10}\sqrt{y} }{\sqrt{a} }\Bigr)dy } }{\int_{0}^{a/4}{\Bigl(a-\frac{\sqrt{10}\sqrt{y} }{\sqrt{a} }\Bigr)dy }} = \frac{\int_{0}^{a/4}{ay}dy – \int_{0}^{a/4}{\frac{\sqrt{10} }{\sqrt{a} }y^{3/2} }dy }{\int_{0}^{a/4}{a dy} – \int_{0}^{a/4}{\frac{\sqrt{10} }{\sqrt{a} }y^{1/2} }dy } \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{\Bigl[a\frac{y^2}{2}\Bigr]^{a/4}_0 – \Bigl[\frac{\sqrt{10} }{\sqrt{a} }y^{5/2} \times \frac{2}{5} \Bigr]^{a/4}_0 }{\bigl[a y\bigr]^{a/4}_0 – \biggl[\frac{\sqrt{10} }{\sqrt{a} }y^{3/2} \times \frac{2}{3} \biggr]^{a/4}_0 } = \frac{\Bigl[\frac{a}{2} \times \frac{a^2}{16} \Bigr] -\Bigl[\frac{2\sqrt{10} }{3\sqrt{a} } \times \frac{a^{5/2}}{30} \Bigr]}{\Bigl[a \times \frac{a}{4} \Bigr] – \Bigl[\frac{2\sqrt{10} }{3\sqrt{a} } \times \frac{a^{3/2}}{8} \Bigr]} \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{\Bigl[\frac{a^3}{32}\Bigr] -\Bigl[\frac{\sqrt{10} }{80} \times a^2\Bigr] }{\Bigl[\frac{a^2}{4}\Bigr] -\Bigl[\frac{\sqrt{10} }{12} \times a\Bigr]} \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{\Bigl(\frac{5 a^3 -2 \sqrt{10} a^2}{160}\Bigr) }{\Bigl(\frac{3 a^2 – \sqrt{10} a}{12}\Bigr)} = \frac{\frac{a^2}{160}(5a-2\sqrt{10} ) }{\frac{a}{12}(3a-\sqrt{10} ) } \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{a^2}{160} (5a -2 \sqrt{10} ) \times \frac{12}{a} \times \frac{1}{(3a-\sqrt{10} )} \) .

\( \therefore \overline{y} = \frac{3a}{40} \frac{(5a-2\sqrt{10} )}{(3a-\sqrt{10} )} \) Ans.
