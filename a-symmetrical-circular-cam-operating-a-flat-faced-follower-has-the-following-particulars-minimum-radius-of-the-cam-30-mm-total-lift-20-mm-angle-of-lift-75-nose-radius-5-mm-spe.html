Guest Question.

A symmetrical circular cam operating a flat-faced follower has the following particulars :
Minimum radius of the cam = 30 mm ; Total lift = 20 mm ; Angle of lift = \(75^{\circ}\) ; Nose radius = 5 mm ; Speed = 600 r.p.m. Find : 1. the principal dimensions of the cam, and 2. the acceleration of the follower at the beginning of the lift, at the end of contact with the circular flank , at the beginning of contact with nose and at the apex of the nose.

Admin
Solution

Given : \(r_{1}=O E=30 mm ; x=J K=20 mm ; \alpha=75^{\circ} ; r_{2}=O F=Q K=5 mm\) ; \(N=600 \text { r.p.m. or } \omega=2 \pi \times 600 / 60=62.84 rad / s\)

1. Principal dimensions of the cam

A symmetrical circular cam operating a flat faced follower is shown in Fig. 20.46.

Let OQ = Distance between cam centre and nose centre,

R = PE = Radius of circular flank, and

\(\phi\) = Angle of contact on the circular flank.

We know that lift of the follower (x),

\(20=O Q+r_{2}-r_{1}=O Q+5-30=O Q-25\)

 

\(\therefore \quad O Q=20+25=45 mm\)

 

We know that \(P Q=P F-F Q=P E-F Q=O P+O E-F Q\)

\(=O P+30-5=(O P+25) mm\)

Now from a triangle OPQ,

\((P Q)^{2}=(O P)^{2}+(O Q)^{2}-2 \times O P \times O Q \cos \beta\)

 

\((O P+25)^{2}=(O P)^{2}+45^{2}-2 \times O P \times 45 \cos \left(180^{\circ}-75^{\circ}\right)\)

 

\((O P)^{2}+50 O P+625=(O P)^{2}+2025+23.3 O P\)

 

\(50 O P-23.3 O P=2025-625\)

 

or \(26.7 O P=1400\)

 

and \(O P=1400 / 26.7=52.4 mm\)

 

\(\therefore \) Radius of circular flanks,

\(R=P E=O P+O E=52.4+30\)

 

= 82.4 mm

 

and \(P Q=O P+25=52.4+25\)

 

= 77.4 mm

In order to find angle \(\phi\) , consider a triangle OPQ. We know that

\(\frac{O Q}{\sin \phi}=\frac{P Q}{\sin \beta}\)

 

or \(\sin \phi=\frac{O Q \times \sin \beta}{P O}=\frac{45 \times \sin \left(180^{\circ}-75^{\circ}\right)}{77.4}=0.5616\)

 

\(\therefore \quad \phi=34.2^{\circ}\)

 

2. Acceleration of the follower

We know that acceleration of the follower at the beginning of the lift,

\(a=\omega^{2}\left(R-r_{1}\right) \cos \theta=\omega^{2}\left(R-r_{1}\right)\) \(\ldots\left(\because \text { At the beginning of lift, } \theta=0^{\circ}\right)\)

 

\(=(62.84)^{2}(82.4-30)=206930 mm / s =206.93 m / s ^{2}\)

Acceleration of the follower at the end of contact with the circular flank,

\(a=\omega^{2}\left(R-r_{1}\right) \cos \theta=\omega^{2}\left(R-r_{1}\right) \cos \phi\) \(\ldots(\because \text { At the end of contact with the circular flank, } \theta=\phi)\)

 

\(=-(62.84)^{2}(82.4-30) \cos 34.2^{\circ}=171130 mm / s ^{2}=171.13 m / s ^{2}\)

Acceleration of the follower at the beginning of contact with nose,

\(a=-\omega^{2} \times O Q \cos (\alpha-\theta)=-\omega^{2} \times O Q \cos (\alpha-\phi)\) \(\ldots(\because \text { At the beginning of contact with nose, } \theta=\phi)\)

 

\(=-(62.84)^{2} 45 \cos \left(75^{\circ}-34.2^{\circ}\right)=-134520 mm / s ^{2}=-134.52 m / s ^{2}\)

 

\(=134.52 m / s ^{2} \text { (Retardation) }\)

and acceleration of the follower at the apex of nose,

\(a=-\omega^{2} \times O Q \cos (\alpha-\theta)=-\omega^{2} \times O Q\) \((\because \text { At the apex of nose, } \alpha-\theta=0)\)

 

\(=-(62.84)^{2} 45=-177700 mm / s ^{2}=-177.7 m / s ^{2}\)

 

\(=177.7 m / s ^{2} \text { (Retardation) }\)
