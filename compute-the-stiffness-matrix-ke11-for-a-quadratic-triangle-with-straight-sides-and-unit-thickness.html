Guest Question.

Compute the stiffness matrix \(K _{11}^{(e)}\) for a quadratic triangle with straight sides and unit thickness.

Admin
Solution

The first step is to obtain the cartesian derivatives for the shape function \(N_{1}\) expressed in terms of the area coordinates as

 

\(\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{1}} \frac{\partial L_{1}}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{2}} \frac{\partial L_{2}}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{3}} \frac{\partial L_{3}}{\partial x}\)

 

\(\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{1}} \frac{\partial L_{1}}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{2}} \frac{\partial L_{2}}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{3}} \frac{\partial L_{3}}{\partial y}\)

 

From Eq.(5.32) we deduce

 

\(N_{1}=l_{2}^{1}\left(L_{1}\right)=\frac{\left(L_{1}-1 / 2\right) L_{1}}{(1-1 / 2) 1}=\left(2 L_{1}-1\right) L_{1}\) (5.32)

 

\(\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{1}}=4 L_{1}-1 \quad ; \quad \frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{2}}=\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial L_{3}}=0\)

 

and from Eq.(5.45)

 

\(\frac{\partial L_{i}}{\partial x}=\frac{b_{i}}{2 A^{(e)}} \quad \text { and } \quad \frac{\partial L_{i}}{\partial y}=\frac{c_{i}}{2 A^{(e)}}\) (5.45)

 

\(\frac{\partial L_{i}}{\partial x}=\frac{b_{i}}{2 A(e)} \quad ; \quad \frac{\partial L_{i}}{\partial y}=\frac{c_{i}}{2 A^{(e)}}\)

 

Therefore

 

\(\frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial x}=\frac{b_{1}}{2 A^{(e)}}\left(4 L_{1}-1\right) \quad ; \quad \frac{\partial N_{1}}{\partial y}=\frac{c_{1}}{2 A^{(e)}}\left(4 L_{1}-1\right)\)

 

and

 

\(B _{1}=\frac{\left(4 L_{1}-1\right)}{2 A^{(e)}}\left[\begin{array}{ll}b_{1} & 0 \\0 & c_{1} \\c_{1} & b_{1}\end{array}\right]\)

 

Matrix \(K _{11}^{(e)}\) is thus obtained by

 

\(K _{11}^{(e)}=\iint_{A^{(e)}} B _{1}^{T} D B t d A=\frac{t}{\left(2 A^{(e)}\right)^{2}}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}b_{1} & 0 & b_{1} \\0 & c_{1} & c_{1}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}d_{11} & d_{12} & 0 \\d_{12} & d_{22} & 0 \\0 & 0 & d_{33}\end{array}\right] \times\)

 

\(\times\left[\begin{array}{cc}b_{1} & 0 \\0 & c_{1} \\c_{1} & b_{1}\end{array}\right] \iint_{A^{(e)}}\left(4 L_{1}-1\right)^{2} d A\)

 

We deduce from Eq.(5.47a)

 

\(\iint_{A^{(e)}} L_{1}^{k} L_{2}^{l} L_{3}^{m} d A=2 A^{(e)} \frac{k ! l ! m !}{(2+k+l+m) !}\) (5.47a)

 

\(\iint_{A^{(e)}}\left(4 L_{1}-1\right)^{2} d A=2 A^{(e)}\left[\frac{16 \cdot 2 !}{4 !}-\frac{8 \cdot 1 !}{3 !}+\frac{1}{2}\right]=A^{(e)}\)

 

which leads to

 

\(K _{11}^{(e)}=\frac{1}{4 A^{(e)}}\left[\begin{array}{ll}b_{1}^{2} d_{11}+c_{1}^{2} d_{33} & b_{1} c_{1}\left(d_{33}+d_{12}\right) \\b_{1} c_{1}\left(d_{33}+d_{12}\right) & b_{1}^{2} d_{33}+c_{1}^{2} d_{22}\end{array}\right]\)

 

The rest of the \(K _{i j}^{(e)}\) matrices are obtained following an identical procedure. The complete expression of the stiffness matrix for the quadratic triangle can be found in [CMPW] and [WJ].
