Guest Question.

Given: A combined footing supporting two square columns. Column \(C_{1}\) is 16 in. \(\times\) 16 in. and carries a service load of 220 kip. Column \(C_{2}\) is 18 in. \(\times 18\) in. and carries a service load of 440 kip (Figs. E7.4a and E7.4b).

Determine: The soil pressure distribution caused by the service loads \(P_{1}\) and \(P_{2}\).

Admin
Solution

Locate the centroid of the area

 

\(A=(9)(9)+(13)(7)=172\, ft^{2}\)

 

\(L_{1}=\frac{81(17.5)+91(6.5)}{172}=11.68\) ft

 

\(d_{1}=11.68-1.5=10.18\) ft

 

Locate the resultant force

 

\(R=P_{1}+P_{2}=220+440=660\) kip

 

\(x_{1}=\frac{P_{2}d}{R}=\frac{440(16)}{660}=10.67\) ft

 

\(e=-0.49\) ft

 

The peak pressures are

 

\(q_{1}=\left \{ \frac{R}{A}+\frac{(R_{e})L_{1}}{I_{y}} \right \}=\frac{660}{172}+\frac{660(-0.49)11.68}{7014}=3.3\: kip/ft^{2}\)

 

\(q_{2}=\left \{ \frac{R}{A}+\frac{(R_{e})L_{2}}{I_{y}} \right \}=\frac{660}{172}+\frac{660(-0.49)10.32}{7014}=4.3\: kip/ft^{2}\)
