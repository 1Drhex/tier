Guest Question.

Design a slider-crank mechanism so that displacement of the slider is proportional to the crank rotation in the interval 30° ≤ θ ≤ 100°. Assume initial distance of the slider equal to 15 cm and final distance to be 10 cm.

Admin
Solution

Given: \( s-s_{i} \propto\left(\theta-\theta_{i}\right) \).

where c=constant of proportionality.

Now \( s=s_{f}=10 cm \text { when } \theta=\theta_{f}=100^{\circ} \).

and \( s=s_{i}=15 cm \text { when } \theta=\theta_{i}=30^{\circ} \).

∴ \( s_{f}-s_{i}=c\left(\theta_{f}-\theta_{i}\right) \).

\( c=\frac{10-15}{100-30}=\frac{-5}{70}=-\frac{1}{14} \).

∴ \( s-s_{i}=\left(-\frac{1}{14}\right)\left(\theta-\theta_{i}\right) \).

The Chebyshev’s precision spacing is given by:

\( \theta_{m}=a+b \cos \left[\frac{(2 m-1) \pi}{2 n}\right], m=1,2,3 \).

where \( a=\frac{\theta_{i}+\theta_{f}}{2}=\frac{30+100}{2}=65^{\circ} \).

\( b=\frac{\theta_{f}-\theta_{i}}{2}=\frac{1}{2}(100-30)=35^{\circ} \).

\( \theta_{1}=65+35 \cos \frac{\pi}{6}=95.3^{\circ} \).

\( \theta_{2}=65+35 \cos 90^{\circ}=65^{\circ} \).

\( \theta_{3}=65+35 \cos \frac{5 \pi}{6}=34.69^{\circ} \).

Now \( s_{m}-s_{i}=\left(-\frac{1}{14}\right)\left(\theta_{m}-\theta_{i}\right) \).

∴ \( s_{1}-15=\left(-\frac{1}{14}\right)(98.3-30), s_{1}=10.336 cm \).

\( s_{2}-15=\left(-\frac{1}{14}\right)(65-30), s_{2}=12.50 cm \).

\( s_{3}-15=\left(-\frac{1}{14}\right)(34.69-30), s_{3}=14.665 cm \).

Now

\( k_{1} s \cos \theta+k_{2} \sin \theta+k_{3}=s^{2} \).

\( k_{1} \times 10.336 \times \cos 95.3^{\circ}+k_{2} \sin 95.3^{\circ}+k_{3}=(10.336)^{2} \).

\( -0.95474 k_{1}+0.99572 k_{2}+k_{3}=106.833 \) (1).

\( k_{1} \times 12.50 \times \cos 65^{\circ}+k_{2} \sin 65^{\circ}+k_{3}=(12.50)^{2} \).

\( 5.28273 k_{1}+0.90631 k_{2}+k_{3}=156.25 \) (2).

\( k_{1} \times 14.665 \times \cos 34.69^{\circ}+k_{2} \sin 34.69^{\circ}+k_{3}=(14.665)^{2} \).

\( 12.0582 k_{1}+0.56914 k_{2}+k_{3}=215.062 \) (3).

Subtracting Eqs. (1) and (2) from Eq. (3), we get

\( 13.013 k_{1}-0.4266 k_{2}=108.229 \) (4).

\( 6.775 k_{1}-0.3372 k_{2}=58.812 \) (5).

\( 30.504 k_{1}-k_{2}=253.70 \) (6).

\( 20.092 k_{1}-k_{2}=174.41 \) (7).

Subtracting Eq. (7) from Eq. (6), we get

\( 10.412 k_{1}=79.29 \).

∴ \( k_{1}=7.615 \) .

Then \( k_{2}=-21.4 \).

\( k_{3}=135.418 \).

Now \( k_{1}=2 l_{2}, l_{2}=\frac{7.615}{2}=3.808 cm \).

\( k_{2}=2 l_{2} e, e=\frac{-21.4}{7.615}=-2.81 cm \).

\( k_{3}=l_{3}^{2}-l_{3}^{2}-e^{2} \).

\( 135.418=l_{3}^{2}-(3.808)^{2}-(2.81)^{2} \).

\( l_{3}^{2}-157.815 \).

\( l_{3}=12.562 cm \).

Thus \( l_{2}=3.808 cm , l_{3}=12.562 cm , e=-2.81 cm \).
