Guest Question.

For the beam and loading shown in Fig. P10.9, use the double-integration method to determine (a) the equation of the elastic curve for segment AB of the beam, (b) the deflection midway between A and B, and (c) the slope at B. Assume that EI is constant for the beam.

Admin
Solution

Beam FBD:

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{A} &=-\frac{w L^{2}}{2}-P\left(\frac{5 L}{4}\right)+B_{y} L=0 \\& \therefore B_{y}=\frac{w L}{2}+\frac{5 P}{4} \\\Sigma F_{y} &=A_{y}+B_{y}-w L-P=0 \\& \therefore A_{y}=\frac{w L}{2}-\frac{P}{4}\end{aligned}\)

 

Moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}\Sigma M_{a-a} &=M(x)+\frac{w x^{2}}{2}-A_{y} x=M(x)+\frac{w x^{2}}{2}-\frac{w L x}{2}+\frac{P x}{4}=0 \\& \therefore M(x)=-\frac{w x^{2}}{2}+\frac{w L x}{2}-\frac{P x}{4}\end{aligned}\)

 

Integration of moment equation:

\(\begin{aligned}&E I \frac{d^{2} v}{d x^{2}}=M(x)=-\frac{w x^{2}}{2}+\frac{w L x}{2}-\frac{P x}{4} \\&E I \frac{d v}{d x}=-\frac{w x^{3}}{6}+\frac{w L x^{2}}{4}-\frac{P x^{2}}{8}+C_{1} (a)\\&E I v=-\frac{w x^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{12}-\frac{P x^{3}}{24}+C_{1} x+C_{2} (b)\end{aligned}\)

 

Boundary conditions:

\(\begin{array}{lll}v=0 & \text { at } & x=0 \\v=0 & \text { at } & x=L\end{array}\)

 

Evaluate constants:
Substitute x = 0 and v = 0 into Eq. (b) to determine \(C_{2}:\)

\(E I(0)=-\frac{w(0)^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L(0)^{3}}{12}-\frac{P(0)^{3}}{24}+C_{1}(0)+C_{2} \quad \therefore C_{2}=0\)

Substitute x = L and v = 0 into Eq. (b) to determine \(C_{1}:\)

\(E I(0)=-\frac{w(L)^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L(L)^{3}}{12}-\frac{P(L)^{3}}{24}+C_{1}(L) \quad \therefore C_{1}=-\frac{w L^{3}}{24}+\frac{P L^{2}}{24}\)

(a) Elastic curve equation for segment AB of the beam:

\(\begin{aligned}E I v &=-\frac{w x^{4}}{24}+\frac{w L x^{3}}{12}-\frac{P x^{3}}{24}-\frac{w L^{3} x}{24}+\frac{P L^{2} x}{24} \\& \therefore v=\left[-\frac{w x}{24 E I}\left[x^{3}-2 L x^{2}+L^{3}\right]-\frac{P x}{24 E I}\left[x^{2}-L^{2}\right]\right.\end{aligned}\)

(b) Deflection at midspan:

\(\begin{aligned}v_{x=L / 2} &=-\frac{w(L / 2)}{24 E I}\left[\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^{3}-2 L\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}+L^{3}\right]-\frac{P(L / 2)}{24 E I}\left[\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}-L^{2}\right] \\&=-\frac{5 w L^{4}}{384 E I}+\frac{P L^{3}}{64 E I}\end{aligned}\)

(c) Slope at B:

\(\left.\frac{d v}{d x}\right|_{B}=\theta_{B}=-\frac{w(L)^{3}}{6 E I}+\frac{w L(L)^{2}}{4 E I}-\frac{P(L)^{2}}{8 E I}-\frac{w L^{3}}{24 E I}+\frac{P L^{2}}{24 E I}=\frac{w L^{3}}{24 E I}-\frac{P L^{2}}{12 E I}\)
