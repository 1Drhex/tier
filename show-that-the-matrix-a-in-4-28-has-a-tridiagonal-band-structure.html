Guest Question.

Show that the matrix A in (4.28) has a tridiagonal band structure.

\(A\left[\ddot\Pi_{2} \left(t_{2}\right) \quad \cdot \cdot \cdot \quad \ddot\Pi_{N +1} \left(t_{N} + 1\right)\right]^{T}\) = \(b \) (4.28)

Admin
Solution

By using (4.13), (4.14), (4.23), (4.16), (4.25), the time derivative of Πk(t)in (4.27) can be written as

\(\Pi _{k – 1} \left(t_{k}\right) \) = \(\Pi _{k} \left(t_{k}\right)\) (4.23)

\(\ddot\Pi_{k – 1} \left(t_{k}\right) \) = \(\ddot\Pi_{k } \left(t_{k}\right)\) (4.25)

\(\ddot\Pi_{k – 1} \left(t_{k}\right) \) = \(\ddot\Pi_{k } \left(t_{k}\right)\) (4.16)

\(\Pi _{k – 1} \left(t_{k}\right)\) = \(\Pi _{k} \left(t_{k}\right) \) (4.14)

\(\Pi _{k – 1} \left(t_{k}\right) \) = \(q_{k }\) (4.13)

\(\Pi _{k}(t)=\frac{\ddot{\Pi }_k(t_k) }{6\Delta t_k} \left(t_{k+1}-t\right)^3+ \frac{\ddot{\Pi }_{k}(t_{k+1}) }{6\Delta t_k} \left(t-t_k\right)^3 + \left(\frac{\Pi _k(t_{k+1}) }{\Delta t_k} – \frac{\Delta t_k\ddot{\Pi }_{k}(t_{k+1}) }{6} \right) \left(t-t_k\right)+\left(\frac{\Pi _k(t_{k}) }{\Delta t_k} – \frac{\Delta t_k\ddot{\Pi }_{k}(t_{k}) }{6} \right) \left(t_{k+1}-t\right) \qquad k=1,\cdot \cdot \cdot ,N+1,\) (4.27)

\(\dot{\Pi }_{k} \left(t\right) \) = \(-\frac{\ddot{\Pi }_{k}\left(t_{k}\right) }{2 \Delta t_{k}} \left(t_{k+1} – t\right)^{2} + \frac{\ddot{\Pi }_{k+1} \left(t_{k + 1}\right) }{2\Delta t_{k} } \left(t-t_{k }\right)^{2} + \frac{q_{k + 1} – q_{k}}{\Delta t_{k}} + \frac{\Delta t_{k} }{6} \left(\ddot{\Pi }_{k}\left(t_{k}\right) – \ddot{\Pi }_{k}\left(t_{k + 1}\right) \right)\) k = 1 , . . . , N + 1 (S4.1)

where \(q_{N}\) and \(q_{N + 1}\) are unknown. Then, using (4.15), (4.24) leads to

\(\dot{\Pi }_{K – 1} \left( t_{k}\right) \) = \(\dot{\Pi_{k}} \left( t_{k}\right)\) (4.15)

\(\dot{\Pi }_{K – 1} \left( t_{k}\right) \) = \(\dot{\Pi_{k}} \left( t_{k}\right)\) (4.24)

\(\frac{\Delta t_{k} }{6} \ddot{\Pi }_{k} \left(t_{k}\right) + \frac{\Delta t_{k} + \Delta t_{k + 1} }{3} \ddot{\Pi }_{k + 1} \left(t_{k + 1}\right) + \frac{\Delta t_{k + 1} }{6} \ddot{\Pi }_{k + 2}\left(t_{k + 2}\right) \) = \(\frac{q_{k}}{ \Delta t_{k} } – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{k + 1} } + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{k }} \right) q_{k + 1} + \frac{q_{k + 2}}{\Delta t_{k + 1} } \) k = 1, . . . , N

where \( \ddot{\Pi }_{1} \left(t_{1}\right) \) = \(\ddot{q_{i}}\) as in (4.19) and \(\ddot{\Pi }_{N + 2} \left(t_{N + 2}\right) \)= \(\ddot{q_{f}}\) as in (4.22).

\( \ddot {\Pi _{1}}\left( t_{1}\right)\) = \( \ddot {q_{i}} \) (4.19)

\( \ddot {\Pi }_{N + 1 }\left( t_{N + 2}\right)\) = \( \ddot {q}_{f} \) (4.22)

Evaluating (S4.1) for k = 1 at t = \( t_{1} \)and applying (4.17), (4.18) yields

\( \Pi _{1}\left( t_{1}\right) \) = \( q_{i} \) (4.17)

\( \dot {\Pi _{1}}\left( t_{1}\right)\) = \( \dot {q_{i}} \) (4.18)

\(q_{2}\) = \(q_{i} + \dot{q_{i}}\Delta t_{1} + \ddot{q_{i}}\frac{\Delta t_{1}^{2} }{6} \) (S4.3)

Likewise, evaluating (S4.1) for k = N+1 at t = \( t_{N + 2}\) and applying (4.20), (4.21) yields

\( \Pi _{N + 1}\left( t_{N + 2}\right) \) = \( q_{f} \) (4.20)

\( \dot {\Pi }_{N + 1 }\left( t_{N + 2}\right)\) = \( \dot {q}_{f} \) (4.21)

\(q_{N + 1}\) = \( q_{f} – \dot{q_{f}}\Delta t_{N + 1} + \ddot{q_{f}}\frac{\Delta t^{2} _{N + 1}}{3} + \ddot{\Pi_{N + 1}} \left(t_{N + 1}\right) \frac{\Delta t^{2} _{N + 1} }{6} \) (S4.4)

In the case N>3, substituting (S4.3) into (S4.2) for k = 1,2 and (S4.4) into (S4.2) for k = N−1, N leads to writing the linear system of N equations into N unknowns given in (4.28).

The coefficient matrix takes on the tridiagonal band structure

 

A = \(\left [ \begin{matrix} a_{11} & a_{12} & ….. & 0 & 0 \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & ……. & 0 & 0 \\ . & . & . & . & .\\ . & . &.& . & . \\ . & . &.& . & . \\ 0 & 0& ….. & a_{N – 1,N – 1} & a_{N – 1, N} \\ 0 & 0 & …… & a_{N ,N – 1} & a_{NN} \end{matrix} \right ] \)

where

\(\begin{cases} a_{11} = \frac{\Delta t_{1} }{2} + \frac{\Delta t_{2} }{3} + \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{1} }{6 \Delta t_{2} } \\ a_{kk} = \frac{\Delta t_{k} + \Delta t_{k + 1} }{3}\qquad k = 2,…,N−1 \\ a_{NN} = \frac{\Delta t_{N} }{3} + \frac{\Delta t_{N + 1} }{2} + \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{N + 1} }{6 \Delta t_{N} } \\ a_{21}= \frac{\Delta t_{2} }{6} – \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{1} }{6\Delta t_{2} } \\ a_{k ,k – 1}=\frac{\Delta t_{k} }{6} \qquad k = 3 , . .., N \\ a_{k ,k+1}\frac{\Delta t_{k + 1} }{6} \qquad k = 1,…, N−2 \\ a_{N – 1 , N } = \frac{\Delta t_{N} }{6} – \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{N + 1} }{6 \Delta t_{N} }\end{cases} \)

which depend only on the given time intervals \(\Delta t_{k} \), k = 1, . . . , N + 1.
Further, the components of the vector b of known terms in (4.28) are:

\(\begin{cases} b_{1}= \frac{q_{3} – q_{i} }{\Delta t_{2}} – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{2}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{1} } \right) \left(\dot{q_{i}}\Delta t_{1} + \ddot{q_{i}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{1}}{3} \right) – \ddot{q_{i}}\frac{\Delta t_{1}}{ 6} \\ b_{2}= \frac{1}{\Delta t_{2}} \left(q_{i} + \dot{q_{i}}\Delta t_{1} + \ddot{q_{i}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{1}}{3} \right) – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{3}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{2} } \right) q_{3} + \frac{q_{4}}{ \Delta t_{3}} \\ b_{k}= \frac{q_{k}}{ \Delta t_{k}} – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{K + 1}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{K}} \right) q_{k + 1} + \frac{q_{k + 2}}{\Delta t_{k + 1} } \qquad k = 3, . . . , N − 2 \\b_{N – 1}=\frac{q_{N – 1}}{ \Delta t_{N – 1}} – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{N}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{N – 1}} \right) q_{N} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{N }} \left(q_{f} – \dot{q_{f} }\Delta t_{N + 1} + \ddot{q_{f}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{N + 1}}{3} \right) \\ b_{N} =\frac{q_{N } – q_{f} }{ \Delta t_{N }} – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{N + 1}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{N }} \right) \left( – \dot{q_{f} } \Delta t_{N + 1} + \ddot{q_{f}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{N + 1}}{3} \right) – \ddot{q_{f}} \frac{\Delta t_{N + 1}}{6}\end{cases} \)

where the values \(q_{K}\) are given for k = 1, 3,. .., N, N+2.

On the other hand, in the case N = 3, the same expressions for the a’s can be used; for the b’s, instead, \(b_{1} \) and \(b_{3} \) can be computed as above and \( b_{2} \) takes on the expression

\(b_{2} \) = \(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{2 }} \left(q_{i} + \dot{q_{i} }\Delta t_{1} + \ddot{q_{i}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{ 1}}{3} \right) – \left(\frac{1}{\Delta t_{3}} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{2}} \right) q_{3} + \frac{1}{\Delta t_{3 }} \left(q_{f} – \dot{q_{f} }\Delta t_{4} + \ddot{q_{f}} \frac{\Delta t^{2}_{ 4}}{3} \right) \)
