Guest Question.

A WT205 × 30 structural steel section (see Appendix B for cross-sectional properties) is used for a 6.5-m column. Assume pinned connections at each end of the column. Determine:
(a) the slenderness ratio.
(b) the Euler buckling load. Use E = 200 GPa for the steel.
(c) the axial stress in the column when the Euler load is applied.

Admin
Solution

The following section properties for a standard steel WT205 × 30 shape are given in Appendix B:

\(A=3,800 mm ^{2}, I_{x}=13.8 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}, r_{x}=87.7 mm , I_{y}=5.99 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}, r_{y}=39.6 mm\)

(a) Slenderness ratio:

\(\frac{L}{r}=\frac{6,500 mm }{39.6 mm }=164.1\)

(b) Euler buckling load:

\(P_{c r}=\frac{\pi^{2} E I}{L^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}\left(200,000 N / mm ^{2}\right)\left(5.99 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\right)}{(6,500 mm )^{2}}=279,853 N =280 kN\)

(c) Axial stress at \(P _{c r}\):

\(\sigma=\frac{279,853 N }{3,800 mm ^{2}}=73.6 MPa\)
