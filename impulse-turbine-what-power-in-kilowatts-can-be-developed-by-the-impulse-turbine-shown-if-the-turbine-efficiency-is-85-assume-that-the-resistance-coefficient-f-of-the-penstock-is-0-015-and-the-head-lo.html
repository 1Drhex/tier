Guest Question.

IMPULSE TURBINE

What power in kilowatts can be developed by the impulse turbine shown if the turbine efficiency is 85%? Assume that the resistance coefficient f of the penstock is 0.015 and the head loss in the nozzle itself is negligible. What will be the angular speed of the wheel, assuming ideal conditions \(\left(V_{j}=2 V_{\text {bucket }}\right)\), and what torque will be exerted on the turbine shaft?

Problem Definition

Situation: Impulse turbine with 85% efficiency.

Find:

1. Power (in kW) developed by turbine.

2. Angular (in rpm) speed of wheel for maximum efficiency.

3. Torque (in \(kN \cdot m\)) on turbine shaft.

Assumptions:

1. There is no entrance loss.

2. Head loss in nozzle is negligible.

3. Water density is 1000 \(kg / m ^{3}\).

Sketch:

Plan

1. Apply energy equation, Eq. (7.29), to find nozzle velocity.

\(\left(\frac{p_{1}}{\gamma}+\alpha_{1} \frac{\bar{V}_{1}^{2}}{2 g}+z_{1}\right)+h_{p}=\left(\frac{p_{2}}{\gamma}+\alpha_{2} \frac{\bar{V}_{2}^{2}}{2 g}+z_{2}\right)+h_{t}+h_{L}\) (7.29)

2. Use Eq. (14.22) for power.

\(P=\rho Q \frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2}\) (14.22)

3. For maximum efficiency, \(\omega r=\left(V_{j} / 2\right)\).

4. Calculate torque from \(P=T \omega\).

Admin
Solution

1. Energy equation

 

\(\frac{p_{1}}{\gamma}+\frac{V_{1}^{2}}{2 g}+z_{1}=\frac{p_{j}}{\gamma}+\frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2 g}+z_{j}+h_{L}\)

 

• Values in energy equation

 

\(p_{1}=0, z_{1}=1670 m , V_{1}=0, p_{j}=0, z_{j}=1000 m\)

 

• Penstock-supply pipe velocity ratio

 

\(V_{\text {penstock }}=\frac{V_{j} A_{j}}{A_{\text {penstock }}}=V_{j}\left(\frac{0.18 m }{1 m }\right)^{2}=0.0324 V_{j}\)

 

• Head loss

 

\(h_{L}=f \frac{L}{D} \frac{1}{2 g} V_{\text {penstock }}^{2}\)

 

\(=\frac{0.015 \times 6000}{1}(0.0324)^{2} \frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2 g}=0.094 \frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2 g}\)

 

• Jet velocity

 

\(z_{1}-z_{2}=1.094 \frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2 g}\)

 

\(V_{j}=\left(\frac{2 \times 9.81 m / s ^{2} \times 670 m }{1.094}\right)^{1 / 2}=109.6\) m/s

 

2. Gross power

 

\(P=Q \gamma \frac{V_{j}^{2}}{2 g}=\frac{\gamma A_{j} V_{j}^{3}}{2 g}\)

 

\(=\frac{9810(\pi / 4)(0.18)^{2}(109.6)^{3}}{2 \times 9.81}=16,750\) kW

 

Power delivered

 

\(P=16,750 \times \text { efficiency }=14,240\) kW

 

3. Angular speed of wheel

 

\(V_{\text {bucket }}=\frac{1}{2}(109.6 m / s )=54.8\) m/s

 

\(r \omega=54.8\) m/s

 

\(\omega=\frac{54.8 m / s }{1.5 m }=36.5\) rad/s

 

Wheel speed

 

\(N=(36.5 rad / s ) \frac{1 rev }{2 \pi rad }(60 s / min )=349\) rpm

 

4. Torque

 

\(T=\frac{\text { power }}{\omega}=\frac{14,240 kW }{36.5 rad / s }=390 kN \cdot m\)
