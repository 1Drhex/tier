Guest Question.

You have just inherited $100,000 as a lump-sum amount from your distant aunt. After depositing the money in a practically risk-free certificate of deposit (CD) earning 5% per year, you plan to withdraw $10,000 per year for your living expenses. How many years will your $100,000 last in view of these withdrawals? (Hint: It is longer than 10 years!) (4.7)
