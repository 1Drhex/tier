Guest Question.

3 kg of air at 1.5 bar pressure and 77°C temperature at state 1 is compressed polytropically to state 2 at pressure 7.5 bar, index of compression being 1.2. It is then cooled at constant temperature to its original state 1. Find the net work done and heat transferred. [U.P.S.C. 1992]

Admin
Solution

Different states as described in the problem are denoted as 1, 2 and 3 and shown on p-V diagram. Process 1-2 is polytropic process with index 1.2

\(\begin{aligned}\text{So,} \quad \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n}} \\\text{or,} \quad T_{2}&= T_{1}\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n}} \\&= 350 . \left\lgroup\frac{7.5}{1.5} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.2 – 1}{1.2}}\\T_{2}&= 457.68 K\\\text{At state 1,} \quad P_{1}V_{1}&= mRT_{1}\\\frac{1.5 × 10^{5}}{10^{3}} . V_{1}&= 3 × 0.287 × 350\\\text{or,} \quad V_{1}&= 2.009 ≈ 2.01 m^{3}\\\text{For process 1-2,} \quad V_{2}^{1.2}&= \frac{P_{1}V_{1}^{1.2}}{P_{2}}, V_{2} = \left\{\frac{1.5 \times (2.01)^{1.2} \times 10^{5}}{7.5 \times 10^{5}} \right\}^{\frac{1}{1.2}} \\\text{or,} \quad V_{2}&= 0.526 m^{3}\\\text{Process 2-3 is constant pressure process, so } &\frac{P_{2}V_{2}}{T_{2}} = \frac{P_{3}V_{3}}{T_{3}}\text{ gets modified as,}\\\Longrightarrow V_{3}&= \frac{V_{2} . T_{3}}{T_{2}}\\\text{Here process 3-1 is isothermal process, so } \quad& T_{1} = T_{3}\\\text{or,} \quad V_{3}&= \frac{0.526 × 350}{457.68}\\\text{or,} \quad V_{3}&= 0.402 m^{3}\\\text{During process 1-2 the compression work;} \quad &\\W_{1-2}&= \frac{m.R.(T_{1} – T_{2})}{1 – n}\\&= \frac{3 × 0.287(457.68 – 350)}{(1 – 1.2)}\\W_{1-2}&= – 463.56 kJ\\\text{Work during process 2-3,} \qquad &\\W_{2-3}&= P_{2}(V_{3} – V_{2})\\&= 7.5 × 10^{5} (0.402 – 0.526)\\&= – 93 kJ\\\text{Work during process 3-1,} \qquad &\\W_{3-1}&= P_{3}V_{3}\ln \left\lgroup\frac{V_{1}}{V_{3}} \right\rgroup = 7.5 × 10^{5} × 0.402 × \ln \left\lgroup\frac{2.01}{0.402} \right\rgroup \\W_{3-1}&= 485.25 kJ\\\text{Net work,} \qquad W_{net}&= W_{1–2} + W_{2–3} + W_{3–1}\\&= – 463.56 – 93 + 485.25\\\text{Network}&= \textbf{– 71.31 kJ}\end{aligned}\\\text{–ve work shows work done upon the system. Since it is the cycle, so}\\\begin{aligned} W_{net}&= Q_{net}\\\phi dW&= \phi dQ = – 71.31 kJ\\\text{Heat transferred from system}&= \textbf{71.31 kJ}\end{aligned}\)
