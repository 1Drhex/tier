Solution

Overall ratio = 12 000/2650 = 4.528.

First stage ratio= \( \sqrt{4.528} \) =2.128.

This could be achieved using a gear with 38 teeth and pinion with 18 teeth (ratio = 38/18 =2.11).

The gear materials listed in Tables 6.7 to 6.10 are 817M40 and 655M13 steels. From Table 6.11,
