Guest Question.

An orthotropic composite is subjected to a state of stress that causes the following state of strain:

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \varepsilon _{33} \\ \gamma _{23} \\ \gamma _{13} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left \{ \begin{matrix} -1500 μm/m \\2000 μm/m \\ 1000 μm/m \\ -2500 μrad \\ 500 μrad \\ -2000 μrad \end{matrix} \right \}\)

Determine the stresses that caused these strains (use material properties listed in ).

Admin
Solution

Since the composite is orthotropic, stresses are calculated using Equation 4.18

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left [ \begin{matrix} C_{11} & C_{12}& C_{13} & 0 &0 & 0 \\ C_{12} & C_{22} &C_{23} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ C_{13} & C_{23}&C_{33}&0 &0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & C_{44} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &C_{55} & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &C_{66} \end{matrix} \right ] \left\{\begin{matrix}\varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\\varepsilon _{33} \\ \gamma _{23} \\ \gamma _{13} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}\) (4.18)

The stiffness matrix can be obtained by (a) inverting the compliance matrix determined as a part of (b) through the use of Equation 4.19,

\(\begin{matrix} C_{11}= \frac{S_{22}S_{33}-S_{23}^2}{S} \quad C_{12}= \frac{S_{13}S_{23}-S_{12}S_{33}}{S} \quad C_{13}= \frac{S_{12}S_{23}-S_{13}S_{22}}{S} \\[0.5cm] C_{22}= \frac{S_{11}S_{33}-S_{13}^2}{S} \quad C_{23}= \frac{S_{12}S_{13}-S_{11}S_{23}}{S} C_{33}= \frac{S_{11}S_{22}-S_{12}^2}{S} \\[0.5cm] C_{44}=\frac{1}{S_{44}} \quad C_{55}=\frac{1}{S_{55}} \quad C_{66}=\frac{1}{S_{66}} \end{matrix} \) (4.19)

or (c) through the use of Equation 4.20.

\(\begin{matrix} C_{11}= \frac{(E_{22}-\nu _{23}^2E_{33})E_{11}^2}{\Omega }\quad C_{12}=\frac{(\nu _{12}E_{22}+\nu _{13}\nu _{23}E_{33})E_{11}E_{22}}{\Omega } \\[0.5cm] C_{13}= \frac{(\nu _{12}\nu _{23}+\nu _{13})E_{11}E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega }\quad C_{22}=\frac{(E_{11}-\nu ^2_{13}E_{33})E_{22}^2}{\Omega } \\[0.5cm]C_{23}= \frac{(\nu _{23}E_{11}+\nu _{12}\nu _{13}E_{22})E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega }\quad C_{33}=\frac{(E_{11}-\nu ^2_{12}E_{22})E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega } \\[0.5cm] C_{44}=G_{23}\quad C_{55}=G_{13}\quad C_{66}=G_{12}\end{matrix} \) (4.20)

All three methods are entirely equivalent, and which procedure is selected for use is simply a matter of convenience. Equation 4.20 will be used in this example:

\(\Omega = E_{11}E_{22}-\nu ^2_{12}E^2_{22}-\nu ^2_{13}E_{22}E_{33}- \nu ^2_{23}E_{11}E_{33}-2\nu _{12}\nu _{13}\nu _{23}E_{22}E_{33}\)

 

\(\Omega = {(170)(10) – (0.30)^2 (10)^2 – (0.35)^2 (10)(8) – (0.40)^2 (170)(8) – 2(0.30)(0.35)(0.40)(10)(8)}(GPa)^2\)

 

\(\Omega = 1457(GPa)^2\)

 

\(C_{11}=\frac{(E_{22} – \nu ^2_{23}E_{33})E^2_{11}}{\Omega }= \frac{\left\{(10 GPa)- (0.40)^2(8GPa)\right\}(170 GPa)^2 }{1457(GPa)^2}= 172.98 GPa\)

 

\(C_{12}=\frac{(\nu _{12}E_{22}+\nu _{13}\nu _{23}E_{33})E_{11}E_{22}}{\Omega }\)

 

\(=\frac{\left\{(0.30)(10 GPa)+(0.35)(0.40)(8 GPa)\right\}(170 GPa)(10 GPa) }{1457(GPa)^2}= 4.808 GPa\)

 

\(C_{13}=\frac{(\nu _{12}\nu _{23}+\nu _{13})E_{11}E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega }\)

 

\(=\frac{\left\{(0.30)(0.40)+(0.35)\right\}(170 GPa)(10 GPa)(8 GPa) }{1457(GPa)^2}= 4.387 GPa\)

 

\(C_{22}=\frac{(E_{11}-\nu _{13}^2 E_{33})E_{22}^2}{\Omega } =\frac{\left\{(170 GPa)-(0.35)^2(8 GPa)\right\} (10 GPa)^2}{(1457 GPa)^2} =11.602 GPa\)

 

\(C_{23}=\frac{(\nu _{23}E _{11}+\nu _{12}\nu _{13}E_{22})E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega }\)

 

\(=\frac{\left\{(0.40)(170 GPa)+(0.30)(0.35)(10 GPa)\right\}(10 GPa)(8 GPa) }{1457(GPa)^2}= 3.792 GPa\)

 

\(C_{33}=\frac{(E _{11}-\nu^2 _{12}E_{22})E_{22}E_{33}}{\Omega }\)

 

\(=\frac{\left\{(170 GPa)-(0.30)^2(10 GPa)\right\}(10 GPa)(8 GPa) }{1457(GPa)^2}= 9.286 GPa\)

 

\(C_{44} = C_{23} = 8 GPa C_{55} = G_{13}= 10 GPa C_{66} = G_{12} = 13 GPa\)

Applying Equation 4.18, the stresses are

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left [ \begin{matrix} 172.98 & 4.808& 4.387 & 0 &0 & 0 \\4.808 & 11.602 &3.792 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\4.387 & 3.792&9.286&0 &0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 8.0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &10.0 & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &13.0 \end{matrix} \right ](GPa) \left\{\begin{matrix}-1500μm/m \\ 2000 μm/m \\1000 μm/m \\ – 2500 μrad\\ 500μrad \\ – 2000μrad \end{matrix} \right\} \)

 

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left \{ \begin{matrix} -245.5 MPa \\19.78 MPa \\ 10.29 MPa \\ -20.0 MPa \\ 5.00 MPa \\ -26.0 MPa \end{matrix} \right \}\)
