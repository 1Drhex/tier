Guest Question.

A refrigerator working on simple vapour compression cycle operates between the temperature of 25ºC and – 15ºC with NH\(_{3}\), refrigerant. Ammonia is found to be dry after compression and no under cooling of liquid refrigerant occurs in cycle. Using following data obtain COP of system.
\(\textbf{Properties of NH}_{\textbf{3}}\)

Temperature Enthalpy, kJ/kg Entropy, kJ/kg · K
\(h_{f}\) \(h_{g}\) \(s_{f}\) \(s_{g}\)
– 15ºC – 54.5 1303.74 – 0.2132 5.0536
25ºC 99.94 1317.95 0.3386 4.4809
Admin
Solution
\(\begin{aligned}T_{1}&= –15ºC or 258 K, T_{2} = 25ºC or 298 K\\h_{2}&= h_{\text{g at 25ºC}} = 1317.95 kJ/kg\\s_{2}&= s_{\text{g at 25ºC}} = 4.4809 kJ/kg·K\\h_{3}&= h_{\text{f at 25ºC}} = 99.94 kJ/kg\\s_{3}&=s_{\text{f at 25ºC}} = 0.3386 kJ/kg·K\\\text{Here work done, W}&= \text{Area 1– 2 – 3 – 9 – 1}\\\text{Refrigeration effect}&= \text{Area 1– 5 – 6 – 4 – 1}\\\text{Area 3 – 8 – 9}&= \text{(Area 3 – 11 – 7) – (Area 9 – 11 – 10) – (Area 9 – 8 – 7 – 10)}\\&= (h_{3}) – (h_{9}) – T_{1} × (s_{3} – s_{9})\\\text{here }h_{9}&= h_{\text{f at – 15ºC}} = – 54.51 kJ/kg,\\s_{9}&= h_{\text{f at – 15ºC}} = – 0.2132 kJ/kg · K\\\text{Substituting }\qquad\qquad\qquad&\\\text{Area 3 – 8 – 9}&= (99.94 – ( – 54.51) – 258 (0.3386 + 0.2132)\\&= 12.09 kJ/kg\\\text{During throttling process be}&\text{tween 3 and 4.}\\h_{3}&= h_{4}\\\text{(Area 3 – 11 – 7 – 3)}&= \text{(Area 4 – 9 – 11 – 6 – 4)}\\\text{(Area 3 – 8 – 9) + (Area}&\text{ 8 – 9 – 11 – 7 – 8)}\\&= \text{(Area 4 – 6 – 7 – 8 – 4) + (Area 8 – 9 – 11 – 7 – 8)}\\\text{or }\qquad\qquad\text{(Area 3 – 8 – 9)}&= \text{(Area 4 – 6 – 7 – 8 – 4)}\\\text{(Area 4 – 6 – 7 – 8 – 4)}&= 12.09 kJ/k\\\text{Also, (Area 4 – 6 – 7 – 8 – 4)}&= T_{1} × (s_{4} – s_{8})\\12.09&= 258 × (s_{4} – s_{8})\\s_{4} – s_{8}&= 0.0469 kJ/kg · K\\\text{Also, }\qquad\qquad\qquad s_{3} = s_{8}&= 0.3386 kJ/kg · K\\\text{therefore, }\qquad\qquad\qquad s_{4}&= 0.3855 kJ/kg · K\\\text{Also, }\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad s_{1}&= s_{2} = 4.4809 kJ/kg\\\text{Refrigeration effect}&= \text{Area (1 – 5 – 6 – 4 – 1)} = T_{1} × (s_{1} – s_{4})\\&= 258 × (4.4809 – 0.3855)\\&= 1056.61 kJ/kg\\\text{Work done}&= \text{Area 1 – 2 – 3 – 9 – 1}\\&= \text{(Area 3 – 8 – 9) + }((T_{2} – T_{1}) × (s_{1} – s_{8}))\\&= 12.09 + ((298 – 258) × (4.4809 – 0.3386))\\&= 177.782 kJ/kg\\COP&= \frac{\text{Refrigeration effect}}{\text{Work done}} = \frac{1056.61}{177.782} = 5.94\\COP&= \textbf{5.94}\end{aligned}\)
