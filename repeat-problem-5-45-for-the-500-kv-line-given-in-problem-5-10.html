\(\begin{aligned}P_{R} &=\frac{500(0.95 \times 500)}{60.48} \cos \left(86.6^{\circ}-35^{\circ}\right)-\frac{0.9739(0.95 \times 500)^{2}}{60.48} \cos \left(86.6^{\circ}-0.0912^{\circ}\right) \\&=2439.2-221.2=2218 MW (3 \phi)\end{aligned}\)
