Guest Question.

Verify, according to EC3, the connection in Figure E13.1.1 (all dimensions are in millimetres). It is a single lap bearing type joint with one shear plane. Ultimate design load, \(N_{Sd}\), is 140 kN (31.5 kips). Bolts have a 16mm (0.63 in.) diameter, class 8.8, not preloaded and the threaded portion of the shank is located in the bearing length. Holes have 18mm(0.709 in.) diameter. Plates to be connected by bolts are 150mm(5.91 in.) wide and 5mm (0.197 in.) thick. The steel of the plates is S235.
Procedure
The verification of this bearing type bolted connection goes through the following steps:
• check the positioning of the holes (spacing and end and edge distances);
• evaluation of shear design force for each shear plane of each bolt (\(V_{Ed}\));
• evaluation of the design shear resistance for each shear plane (\(F_{v.Rd}\));
• evaluation of the design bearing resistance (\(F_{b.Rd}\));
• evaluation of design ultimate tensile resistance of the connected plate net cross-section at holes (\(N_{u,Rd}\)).

Admin
Solution

• Check of positioning of the holes: with reference to EC3 prescriptions about spacing and edge distances of
holes (Table 13.4) we get:

\(p_{1}\) ≥ 2.2.\(d_{0}\) 70 mm ≥ (2.2.18) =39.6 mm
\(p_{1}\) ≤ min (14\(t_{min}\);200 mm) 70 mm ≤ min (14.5;200 mm) =70.0 mm
\(p_{2}\) ≥ 2.4 \(d_{0}\) 60 mm≥ (2.4.18) =43.2 mm
\(p_{2}\) ≤ min (14\(t_{min}\);200 mm) 60 mm≤ min (14.5;200 mm)=70.0 mm
\(e_{1} ≥ 1.2 d_{0}\) 50 mm ≥ (1.2.18) =21.6 mm
\(e_{1} ≤ 40 mm + 4t_{min}\) 50 mm ≤ (40 + 4.5) =60.0 mm
\(e_{2} ≥ 1.2 d_{0}\) 45 mm ≥ (1.2.18) =21.6 mm
\(e_{2} ≤ 40 mm+ 4t_{min}\) 45 mm≤ (40 + 4.5) =60.0 mm

Design shear load for each bolt:\(F_{v,Sd}=\frac{N_{Sd}}{4}=\frac{140}{4}\)=35 kN(7.87 kips)
• Design shear resistance per bolt (Eq. (13.16a)):
\(F_{v,Rd}=\frac{0.6\cdot f_{ub}\cdot A_{s}}{\gamma _{M2}}=\frac{0.6\times 800\times 157}{1.25}\cdot 10^{-3}\)=60.3 kN(13.56 kips)
Check:
\(F_{V,Sd}\) =35kN ≤ \(F_{V,Rd}\) =60.3 kN OK
• Design bearing resistance (Eqs. (13.18), (13.19a,b) and (13.20a,b)) edge row bolt:
\(a_{b}=min\left \{ \frac{e_{1}}{3.d_{0}};\frac{f_{ub}}{f_{u}};1.0 \right \}\)=min (0.926;1.123;2.222;1) = 0,929 (13.19a)
\(k_{1}=min\left \{ 2.8\cdot \frac{e_{2}}{d_{0}}-1.7;2.5 \right \}\)=min {5.3;2.5 }=2.5 (13.20a)
\(F_{b,Rd,ext}=\frac{k_{1}\cdot a_{b}\cdot f_{u}\cdot d\cdot t}{\gamma _{M2}}=\frac{2.5\times 0.926\times 360\times 16\times 5}{1.25}\cdot 10^{-3}\)=53.3 kN(11.98 kips) (13.18)
internal row bolt:
\(a_{b}=min\left \{ \frac{p_{1}}{3.d_{0}}-\frac{1}{4};\frac{f_{ub}}{f_{u}};1,0 \right \}\)=min (1.0462;2.222;1) = 1.000 (13.19b)
\(k_{1}=min\left \{ 1.4\cdot \frac{p_{2}}{d_{0}}-1.7;2.5 \right \}\)=min {2.769;2.5 }=2.5 (13.20b)
\(F_{b,Rd,int}=\frac{k_{1}\cdot a_{b}\cdot f_{u}\cdot d\cdot t}{\gamma _{M2}}=\frac{2.5\times 1.000\times 360\times 16\times 5}{1.25}\cdot 10^{-3}\)=57.6 kN(12.95 kips) (13.18)
Check:
\(F_{v,Sd} =35kN ≤ min (F_{b,Rd}, int ;F_{b,Rd,ext})=min (53.3;57.6)=53.3kN= F_{b,Rd,ext} OK\)
• Plate design tensile resistance (Eqs. (5.3a)\(N_{pl,Rd}=\frac{A.f_{y}}{\gamma _{M0}}\) and (5.3b))\(N_{u,Rd}=0.9.\frac{A_{net}.f_{u}}{\gamma _{M2}}\)
Compute gross area, A
A= 5 × 150 = 750 \(mm^{2}\) (1.163 in\(.^{2}\))
Compute net area, \(A_{net}\)
\(A_{net}\)= (5 × 150) −2× (5×18) = 570 \(mm^{2}\) (0.884 in\(.^{2}\))
\(N_{pl,Rd}=\frac{A.f_{y}}{\gamma _{M0}}=\frac{750\times 235}{1.00}\cdot 10^{-3}\)=176.3 kN(39.6 kips)
\(N_{u,Rd}=0.9\frac{A_{net}.f_{u}}{\gamma _{M2}}=\frac{0.9\times 570\times 360}{1.25}\cdot 10^{-3}\)=147.7 kN(33.2 kips)
Check:
\(N_{Ed}\)= 140 kN ≤ min \({N_{pl,Rd};N_{u,Rd}}\) =min {176.3;147.7} = 147.7 kN OK
