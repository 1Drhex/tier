Guest Question.

The laminated sheet steel section of Figure 12–27 has a stacking factor of 0.9. Compute the current required to establish a flux of \(\Phi =1.4\times 10^{-4}\) Wb. Neglect fringing. All dimensions are shown in inches.

Admin
Solution

Convert all dimensions to metric.
Cast Iron

\(\ell _{iron}=\ell _{adef}-\ell _{g}=2.5+2+2.5-0.2=6.8 in=0.173 m\)

 

\(A _{iron}=(0.5 in)(0.8 in)=0.4 in^{2}=0.258\times 10^{-3}m^{2}\)

 

\(B_{iron}=\Phi /A_{iron}=(1.4\times 10^{-4})/(0.258\times 10^{-3})=0.54 T\)

 

\(H_{iron}=1850 At/m\) (from Figure 12–19)

 

Sheet Steel

\(\ell _{steel}=\ell _{fg}+\ell _{gh}+\ell _{ha}=0.25+2+0.25=2.5 in=6.35\times 10^{-2} m\)

 

\(A _{steel}=(0.9)(0.258\times 10^{-3})=0.232\times 10^{-3} m^{2}\)

 

\(B_{steel}=\Phi /A_{steel}=(1.4\times 10^{-4})/(0.232\times 10^{-3})=0.60 T\)

 

\(H_{steel}=125 At/m\) (from Figure 12–19)

 

Air Gap

\(\ell _{g}=0.2 in=5.08\times 10^{-3}m\)

 

\(B_{g}=B_{iron}=0.54 T\)

 

\(H_{g}=(7.96\times 10^{5})(0.54)=4.3\times 10^{5}At/m\)

 

Ampere’s Law

\(NI=H_{iron}\ell _{iron}+H_{steel}\ell _{steel}+H_{g}\ell _{g}\)

 

\(=(1850)(0.173)+(125)(6.35\times10^{-2})+(4.3\times10^{5})(5.08\times10^{-3})\)

 

\(= 320 + 7.9 + 2184 = 2512 At\)

 

\(I=2512/N=2512/150=16.7 amps\)
