Annual Worth for a Park with Geometrically Increasing Use

MetroCity is growing rapidly and expanding its freeway network. It expects to use a 10-acre land parcel for a highway connection in 5 years. Meanwhile, the land could be developed as a set of playing fields with parking. The spring soccer leagues and the fall football leagues need additional room.
The development cost is $40,000, and the annual operating cost is $4000. The soccer fields would serve 3000 people in the first year, with annual increases of 20%. The football fields would serve 5000 people in the first year, with annual increases of 3%. Assume each user receives a benefit of $3. Compare the number of soccer and football users, and find the PW if MetroCity uses an i of 6%.
