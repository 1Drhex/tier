Guest Question.

An air compressor has eight stages of equal pressure 1.35. The flow rate through the compressor and its’ overall efficiency are 50 kg/s and 82% respectively. If the air enters the compressor at a pressure of 1 bar and temperature of 313 K, determine;
(i) State of air at the exit of compressor
(ii) Polytropic or small stage efficiency
(iii) Efficiency of each stage
(iv) Power required to drive the compressor assuming overall efficiency as 90%.
[U.P.S.C. 1992]

Admin
Solution

(i) Theoretically state of air at exit can be determined by the given stage pressure ratio of 1.35. Let pressure at inlet to first stage be \(P_{1}\) and subsequent intermediate pressure be \(P_{2}, P_{3}, P_{4}, P_{5}, P_{6}, P_{7}, P_{8}, \) and exit pressure being \(P_{9}\\\text{Therefore,}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \qquad \frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}}&= \frac{P_{3}}{P_{2}} = \frac{P_{4}}{P_{3}} = \frac{P_{5}}{P_{4}} = \frac{P_{6}}{P_{5}} = \frac{P_{7}}{P_{6}} = \frac{P_{8}}{P_{7}} = \frac{P_{9}}{P_{8}} = 1.35\\\text{or} \qquad\qquad\qquad\frac{P_{9}}{P_{1}}&= (1.35)^{8} = 11.03\end{aligned}\\\)Theoretically, the temperature at exit of compressor can be predicted considering isentropic compression of air (\(\gamma\) = 1.4)\(\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \qquad \frac{T_{9}}{T_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{9}}{P_{1}}\right\rgroup ^{\frac{\gamma – 1}{\gamma} } = (11.03)^{\frac{0.4}{1.4}}\\T_{9}&= 621.9 K\end{aligned}\\\)Considering overall efficiency of compression 82% the actual temperature at compressor exit can be obtained\(\\\begin{aligned}\quad\qquad \frac{T_{9} – T_{1}}{T_{9, actual} – T_{1}}&= 0.82\\T_{9, actual}&= 689.71 K\end{aligned}\\\text{Let the actual index of compression be ‘n’, then}\\\begin{aligned}\quad\qquad \left\lgroup\frac{T_{9, actual}}{T_{1}} \right\rgroup&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{9}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } \\\left\lgroup\frac{689.71}{313} \right\rgroup &= (11.03)^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } \\n& = 1.49\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{State of air at exit of compressor, Pressure}&= \textbf{11.03 bar}\\\text{Temperature}&= \textbf{689.71 K}\end{aligned}\\\text{(ii) Let polytropic efficiency be } η_{\text{polytropic}}\text{ for compressor, then;}\\\begin{aligned}\frac{n – 1}{n}&= \left\lgroup\frac{γ – 1}{γ}\right\rgroup × \frac{1}{η_{polytropic}}\\\frac{(1.49 – 1)}{1.49}&= \left\lgroup\frac{1.4 – 1}{1.4}\right\rgroup × \frac{1}{η_{polytropic}}\\η_{polytropic}&= 0.8688 or 86.88\%\\\text{Polytropic efficiency}&= \textbf{86.88}\pmb{\%}\end{aligned}\\\text{(iii) Stage efficiency can be estimated for any stage. Say first stage.}\\\text{Ideal state at exit of compressor stage }⇒ \frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}} = \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ} }\\\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad T_{2} = 341.05 K\\\text{Actual temperature at exit of first stage can be estimated using polytropic index 1.49.}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \frac{T_{2, actual}}{T_{1}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{n – 1}{n} }\\T_{2, actual}&= 345.47 K\end{aligned}\\\text{Stage efficiency for first stage, }η_{s, 1} = \frac{(T_{2} – T_{1})}{(T_{2, actual} – T_{1})}\\\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad = \frac{(341.05 – 313)}{(345.47 – 313)} = 0.8638 or 86.38\%\\\text{Actual temperature at exit of second stage,}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \frac{T_{3, actual}}{T_{2, actual}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{3}}{P_{2}} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{n – 1}{n} }\\T_{3, actual}&= 381.30 K\end{aligned}\\\text{Ideal temperature at exit of second stage}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \frac{T_{3}}{T_{2, actual}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{3}}{P_{2}} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ} }\\T_{3}&= 376.43 K\end{aligned}\\\text{Stage efficiency for second stage, }η_{s, 2} = \frac{T_{3} – T_{2, actual}}{T_{3, actual} – T_{2, actual}} = \frac{376.43 – 345.47}{381.3 – 345.47}\\\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad η_{s,2}= 0.8641 or 86.41\%\\\text{Actual temperature at exit of third stage,}\\\begin{aligned}\qquad\qquad\quad \frac{T_{4, actual}}{T_{3, actual}}&= \left\lgroup\frac{P_{4}}{P_{3}} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{n – 1}{n} }\\T_{4, actual}&= 420.85 K\end{aligned}\\\text{Ideal temperature at exit of third stage,}\\\qquad\qquad\quad \frac{T_{4}}{T_{3, actual}} = \left\lgroup\frac{P_{4}}{P_{3}} \right\rgroup ^{\frac{γ – 1}{γ} } , T_{4} = 415.47 K\\\text{Stage efficiency for third stage, }η_{s, 3} = \frac{T_{4} – T_{3, actual}}{T_{4, actual} – T_{3, actual}} = \frac{(415.47 – 381.3)}{(420.85 – 381.3)}\\\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad = 0.86396 or 86.40\%\\\qquad\text{Stage efficiency} = \textbf{86.4}\pmb{\%}\\\text{(iv) From steady flow energy equation,}\\\begin{aligned}W_{C}&= \int\limits_{1}^{9}{dw} = \int\limits_{1}^{9}{dh,}\text{ and dh = du + pdv + vdp}\\dh&= dq + vdp\\\text{for dq = 0 in adiaba}&\text{tic process} \\dh&= vdp\\W_{C}&= \int\limits_{1}^{9}{vdp}\\\text{Here for polytropic}&\text{ compression}\\Pv^{1.49}&= \text{constant i.e. n = 1.49}\\W_{C} &= \left\lgroup\frac{n}{n – 1} \right\rgroup mRT_{1}\left[\left\lgroup\frac{P_{9}}{T_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{n – 1}{n} } – 1\right] \\&= \left\lgroup\frac{1.49}{0.49} \right\rgroup × 50 × 0.287 × 313 \left[\left\lgroup\frac{11.03}{1.0} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.49 – 1}{1.49} } – 1\right] \\W_{C}&= 16419.87 kJ/s\end{aligned}\\\text{Due to overall efficiency being 90 }\% \text{ the actual compressor work = }\frac{W_{c}}{0.90}\\\qquad\qquad \quad W_{C, actual} = 18244.30 kJ/s\\\text{Power required to drive compressor = }\textbf{18244.30 kJ/s}\)
