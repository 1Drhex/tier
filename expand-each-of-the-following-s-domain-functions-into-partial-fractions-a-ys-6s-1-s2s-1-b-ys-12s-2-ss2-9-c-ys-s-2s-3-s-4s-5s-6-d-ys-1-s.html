Guest Question.

Expand each of the following s-domain functions into partial fractions:

 

(a) \(Y(s)=\frac{6(s+1)}{s^{2}(s+1)}\)

 

(b) \(Y(s)=\frac{12(s+2)}{s\left(s^{2}+9\right)}\)

 

(c) \(Y(s)=\frac{(s+2)(s+3)}{(s+4)(s+5)(s+6)}\)

 

(d) \(Y(s)=\frac{1}{\left[(s+1)^{2}+1\right]^{2}(s+2)}\)

Admin
Solution

a) \(Y(s)=\frac{6(s+1)}{s^{2}(s+1)}=\frac{6}{s^{2}}=\frac{\alpha_{1}}{s}+\frac{\alpha_{2}}{s^{2}}\)

 

\(\alpha_{2}=\left.s^{2} \frac{6}{s^{2}}\right|_{s=0}=6 \quad \alpha_{1}=0\)

 

\(Y(s)=\frac{6}{s^{2}}\)

 

b) \(Y(s)=\frac{12(s+2)}{s\left(s^{2}+9\right)}=\frac{\alpha_{1}}{s}+\frac{\alpha_{2} s+\alpha_{3}}{s^{2}+9}\)

 

Multiplying both sides by \(s\left(s^{2}+9\right)\)

 

\(12(s+2)=\alpha_{1}\left(s^{2}+9\right)+\left(\alpha_{2} s+\alpha_{3}\right)(s)\) or

 

\(12 s+24=\left(\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}\right) s^{2}+\alpha_{3} s+9 \alpha_{1}\)

 

Equating coefficients of like powers of s,

 

\(s^{2}: \quad \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}=0\)

 

\(s^{1}: \alpha_{3}=12\)

 

\(s^{0}: 9 \alpha_{1}=24\)

 

Solving simultaneously,

 

\(\alpha_{1}=\frac{8}{3} \quad, \quad \alpha_{2}=\frac{-8}{3} \quad, \quad \alpha_{3}=12\)

 

\(Y(s)=\frac{8}{3} \frac{1}{s}+\frac{\left(-\frac{8}{3} s+12\right)}{s^{2}+9}\)

 

c) \(Y(s)=\frac{(s+2)(s+3)}{(s+4)(s+5)(s+6)}=\frac{\alpha_{1}}{s+4}+\frac{\alpha_{2}}{s+5}+\frac{\alpha_{3}}{s+6}\)

 

\(\alpha_{1}=\left.\frac{(s+2)(s+3)}{(s+5)(s+6)}\right|_{s=-4}=1\)

 

\(\alpha_{2}=\left.\frac{(s+2)(s+3)}{(s+4)(s+6)}\right|_{s=-5}=-6\)

 

\(\alpha_{3}=\left.\frac{(s+2)(s+3)}{(s+4)(s+5)}\right|_{s=-6}=6\)

 

\(Y(s)=\frac{1}{s+4}-\frac{6}{s+5}+\frac{6}{s+6}\)

 

d) \(Y(s)=\frac{1}{\left[(s+1)^{2}+1\right]^{2}(s+2)}=\frac{1}{\left(s^{2}+2 s+2\right)^{2}(s+2)}\)

 

\(=\frac{\alpha_{1} s+\alpha_{2}}{s^{2}+2 s+2}+\frac{\alpha_{3} s+\alpha_{4}}{\left(s^{2}+2 s+2\right)^{2}}+\frac{\alpha_{5}}{s+2}\)

 

Multiplying both sides by \(\left(s^{2}+2 s+2\right)^{2}(s+2)\) gives

 

\(1=\alpha_{1} s^{4}+4 \alpha_{1} s^{3}+6 \alpha_{1} s^{2}+4 \alpha_{1} s+\alpha_{2} s^{3}+4 \alpha_{2} s^{2}+6 \alpha_{2} s+4 \alpha_{2}+\alpha_{3} s^{2}+2 \alpha_{3} s+ \alpha_{4} s+2 \alpha_{4}+\alpha_{5} s^{4}+4 \alpha_{5} s^{3}+8 \alpha_{5 S}^{2}+8 \alpha_{5} s+4 \alpha_{5}\)

 

Equating coefficients of like power of s,

 

\(s^{4}: \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{5}=0\)

 

\(s^{3}: 4 \alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}+4 \alpha_{5}=0\)

 

\(s^{2}: 6 \alpha_{1}+4 \alpha_{2}+\alpha_{3}+8 \alpha_{5}=0\)

 

\(s^{1}: 4 \alpha_{1}+6 \alpha_{2}+2 \alpha_{3}+\alpha_{4}+8 \alpha_{5}=0\)

 

\(s^{0}: 4 \alpha_{2}+2 \alpha_{4}+4 \alpha_{5}=1\)

 

Solving simultaneously:

 

\(\alpha_{1}=-1 / 4 \quad \alpha_{2}=0 \quad \alpha_{3}=-1 / 2 \quad \alpha_{4}=0 \quad \alpha_{5}=1 / 4\)

 

\(Y(s)=\frac{-1 / 4 s}{s^{2}+2 s+2}+\frac{-1 / 2 s}{\left(s^{2}+2 s+2\right)^{2}}+\frac{1 / 4}{s+2}\)
