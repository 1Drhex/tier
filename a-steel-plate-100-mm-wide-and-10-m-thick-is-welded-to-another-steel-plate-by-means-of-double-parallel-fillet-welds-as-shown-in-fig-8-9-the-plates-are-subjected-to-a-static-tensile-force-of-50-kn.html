Guest Question.

A steel plate, 100 mm wide and 10 m thick, is welded to another steel plate by means of double parallel fillet welds as shown in Fig. 8.9. The plates are subjected to a static tensile force of 50 kN. Determine the required length of the welds if the permissible shear stress in the weld is 94 N/mm².

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } P=50 kN \quad \tau=94 N / mm ^{2} \quad h=10 mm \).

Step I Length of weld
From Eq. (8.7),

\( P=1.414 hl \tau \) (8.7).

P = 1.414 h/t
or 50 × 10³ = 1.414 (10) / (94)
∴ l = 37.62 mm.

Adding 15 mm of length for starting and stopping of the weld run, the length of the weld is given by,
l = 37.62 + 15 = 52.62 or 55 mm.
