Guest Question.

Two rigid, insulated tanks are connected with a pipe and valve. One tank has 0.5 kg air at 200 kPa, 300 K and the other has 0.75 kg air at 100 kPa, 400 K. The valve is opened and the air comes to a single uniform state without any heat transfer. Find the final temperature and the change in entropy of the air.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Total tank. Control mass of constant volume.
Mass and volume: \(m _{2}= m _{ A }+ m _{ B } ; \quad V = V _{ A }+ V _{ B }\)

Energy Eq.: \(U _{2}- U _{1}= m _{2} u _{2}- m _{ A } u _{ A 1}- m _{ B } u _{ B 1}={ }_{1} Q _{2}-{ }_{1} W _{2}=0\)

Process Eq.: V = constant \(\Rightarrow{ }_{1} W _{2}=0 \text { ; }\) Insulated \(\Rightarrow \quad{ }_{1} Q _{2}=0\)

Ideal gas at A1: \(V _{ A }= m _{ A } RT _{ A1 } / P _{ A1 }=0.5 \times 0.287 \times 300 / 200=0.2153 m ^{3}\)

Ideal gas at B1: \(V _{ B }= m _{ B } RT _{ B 1} / P _{ B 1}=0.75 \times 0.287 \times 400 / 100=0.861 m ^{3}\)

State 2: \(m _{2}= m _{ A }+ m _{ B }=1.25 kg ; V _{2}= V _{ A }+ V _{ B }=1.0763 m ^{3}\)

Energy Eq.: \(u _{2}=\frac{ m _{ A } u _{ A 1}+ m _{ B } u _{ B 1}}{ m _{2}}\) and use constant specific heat

\(T _{2}=\frac{ m _{ A }}{ m _{2}} T _{ A1 }+\frac{ m _{ B }}{ m _{2}} T _{ B 1}=\frac{0.5}{1.25} 300+\frac{0.75}{1.25} 400= 3 6 0 K\)

 

\(P _{2}= m _{2} RT _{2} / V =1.25 kg \times 0.287 kJ / kgK \times 360 K / 1.0763 m ^{3}=120 kPa\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}S _{2}- S _{1} &= m _{ A }\left[ C _{ P } \ln T _{2} / T _{ A 1}- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{ A 1}\right)\right]+ m _{ B }\left[ C _{ P } \ln T _{2} / T _{ B 1}- R \ln \left( P _{2} / P _{ B 1}\right)\right] \\&=0.5\left[1.004 \ln \frac{360}{300}-0.287 \ln \frac{120}{200}\right]+0.75\left[1.004 \ln \frac{360}{400}-0.287 \ln \frac{120}{100}\right] \\&=0.5 \times 1.3514+0.75 \times(-0.1581)= 0 . 5 5 7 1 ~ k J / K\end{aligned}\)
