Guest Question.

In a triple stage reciprocating compressor of single acting type the air enters at 1 bar, 27°C. The compressor has low pressure cylinder with bore of 30 cm and stroke of 20 cm. Clearance volume of LP cylinder is 4% of the swept volume. The final discharge from compressor takes place at 20 bar. The expansion and compression index may be taken uniformly as 1.25 for all the stages. The intercooling between the stages may be considered to be at optimum intercooling pressure and perfect intercooling. Determine, the interstage pressures, effective swept volume of low pressure cylinder, temperature and volume of air delivered in each stroke and the work done per kg of air.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Here }P_{1} = 1 bar, T_{1} = 300 K, C = 0.04, P_{10} = 20 bar, n = 1.25,\text{See Fig. 16.35 }\\\text{For optimum and perfect intercooling,}\\\begin{aligned}\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}}= \frac{P_{6}}{P_{2}} = \frac{P_{10}}{P_{6}} = \left\lgroup\frac{20}{1} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{3}} &= 2.714\\P_{2}&= 2.714 bar, T_{5} = T_{1} = 300 K\\P_{6}&= 7.366 bar T_{9} = T_{1} = 300 K\\\text{Volumetric efficiency of LP}&\text{ stage,}\\\eta_{vol, LP}&= 1 + C – C \left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1}{1.25}}\\&= 1 + 0.04 – 0.04 (2.714)^{1/1.25}\\&= \text{0.9511 or 95.11}\%\end{aligned}\\\text{LP swept volume, }V_{1} – V_{3} = \frac{\pi}{4} D^{2} L = \frac{\pi}{4} × (0.30)^{2} × 0.20 = 0.01414 m^{3}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Effective swept volume of LP cylinder, }V_{1} – V_{4}& = \eta_{vol, LP} × (V_{1} – V_{3})\\V_{1} – V_{4}& = 0.9511 × 0.01414 = 0.01345 m^{3}\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Temperature of air delivered, }T_{10} = T_{9} × \left\lgroup\frac{P_{10}}{P_{6}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.25 – 1}{1.25}}&= 300 × (2.714)^{\frac{(1.25 – 1)}{1.25}}\\&= 366.31 K\end{aligned}\\\text{For the compression process of air as perfect gas;}\\\begin{aligned}\frac{P_{1} × (V_{1} – V_{4})}{T_{1}} &= \frac{P_{10} × (V_{10} – V_{11})}{T_{10}} \\V_{10} – V_{11}&= \frac{(V_{1} – V_{4}) × T_{10} × P_{1}}{T_{1} × P_{10}} \\&= \frac{0.01345 × 366.31 × 1 × 10^{2}}{300 × 20 × 10^{2}}\\\text{Volume of air delivered}&= V_{10} – V_{11} = 8.2115 × 10^{-4} m^{3}\\\text{Total Work done per kg air,}&\\W&= 3 × \left\{\frac{n}{n – 1} RT_{1}\left\lgroup\left\lgroup\frac{P_{2}}{P_{1}} \right\rgroup^{\frac{1.25 – 1}{1.25} } – 1\right\rgroup \right\} \\&= 3 × \left\{\left\lgroup\frac{1.25}{1.25 – 1} \right\rgroup × 0.287 × 300 × \left\lgroup(2.714)^{\frac{(1.25 – 1)}{1.25} } – 1\right\rgroup \right\} \\&= \text{285.44 kJ/kg of air}\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Intermediate pressure}&= \text{2.714 bar, 7.366 bar}\\\text{Effective swept volume of LP cylinder}&= 0.01345 m^{3}\\\text{Temperature of air delivered}&= 366.31 K\\\text{Volume of air delivered}&= 8.2115 × 10^{-4} m^{3}\\\text{Work done}&= \text{285.44 kJ/kg of air}\end{aligned}\)
