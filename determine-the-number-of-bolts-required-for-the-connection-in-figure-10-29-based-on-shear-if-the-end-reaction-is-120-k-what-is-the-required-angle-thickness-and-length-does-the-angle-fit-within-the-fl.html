Guest Question.

Determine the number of bolts required for the connection in Figure 10.29 based on shear if the end reaction is 120 k. What is the required angle thickness and length? Does the angle fit within the flanges?

Admin
Solution

From Table 10.3, this connection requires five \(\frac{7}{8}^{\prime \prime} \phi\) A325-N bolts through the beam web and ten \(\frac{7}{8}^{\prime \prime} \phi\) bolts through the girder web.

Shear capacity =126 k > 120 k.

Angles are \(\frac{3}{8}^{\prime \prime} \) thick and \(14 \frac{1}{2}^{\prime \prime}\) long.

Because T = 15.5″ is greater than L = 14.5″, the connection angles should fit adequately between the flanges.
