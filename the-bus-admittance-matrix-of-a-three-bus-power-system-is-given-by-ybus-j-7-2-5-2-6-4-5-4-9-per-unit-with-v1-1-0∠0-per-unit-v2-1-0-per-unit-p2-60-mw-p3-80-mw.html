Guest Question.

The bus admittance matrix of a three-bus power system is given by

\(Y _{\text {bus }}=-j\left[\begin{array}{rrr}7 & -2 & -5 \\-2 & 6 & -4 \\-5 & -4 & 9\end{array}\right] \text { per unit }\)

with \( V _{1}=1.0 \angle 0^{\circ} \text { per unit; } V _{2}=1.0 \text { per unit } ; P _{2}=60 MW ; P _{3}=-80 MW ; Q _{3}=-60 Mvar \) (lagging) as a part of the power flow solution of the system. Find \(V _{2} \text { and } V _{3}\) within a tolerance of 0.01 per unit by using the GaussSeidel iteration method. Start with \(\delta_{2}=0, V _{3}=1.0 \text { per unit, and } \delta_{3}=0\).

 

Admin
Solution

Gauss-Seidel iterative scheme:

With \(\delta_{2}=0 ; V_{3}=1 \text { p.u.; } \delta_{3}=0 \text { (starting values) }\)

Given \(\bar{Y}_{\text {Bus }}=-j\left[\begin{array}{ccc}7 & -2 & -5 \\-2 & 6 & -4 \\-5 & -4 & 9\end{array}\right]\) ;

Also given

\(\begin{aligned}&\bar{V}_{1}=1.0 \angle 0^{\circ} p . u . \\&\bar{V}_{2}=1.0 p \cdot u \cdot ; P_{2}=60 MW \\&P_{3}=-80 MW ; Q_{3}=-60 M var (\text { lag })\end{aligned}\)

Iteration 1:

\(\begin{aligned}Q_{2} &=-\operatorname{Im}\left(\bar{Y}_{22} \bar{V}_{2}+\bar{Y}_{21} \bar{V}_{1}+\bar{Y}_{23} \bar{V}_{3}\right) \bar{V}_{2}^{*} \\&=-\operatorname{Im}\{[-j 6(1 \angle 0)+j 2(1 \angle 0)+j 4(1 \angle 0)] 1 \angle 0\}=0\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{2} &=\frac{1}{\bar{Y}_{22}}\left(\frac{P_{2}-j Q_{2}}{\bar{V}_{2}^{*}}-\bar{Y}_{21} \bar{V}_{1}-\bar{Y}_{23} \bar{V}_{3}\right) \\&=\frac{1}{-j 6}\left[\frac{0.6+j 0}{1 \angle 0}-j 2(1 \angle 0)-j 4(1 \angle 0)\right] \\&=1+j 0.1 \simeq 1 \angle 5.71^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)

 

Repeat:

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{2} &=1+\frac{0.1 \angle 90^{\circ}}{1 \angle-5.71^{\circ}}=0.99005+j 0.0995 \\&=0.995 \angle 5.74^{\circ} \\\bar{V}_{2} &=1 \angle 5.74^{\circ}=0.995+j 0.1\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{3} &=\frac{1}{\bar{Y}_{33}}\left[\frac{P_{3}-j Q_{3}}{\bar{V}_{3}^{*}}-\bar{Y}_{31} \bar{V}_{1}-\bar{Y}_{32} \bar{V}_{2}\right] \\&=\frac{1}{-j 9}\left[\frac{-0.8+j 0.6}{1 \angle 0}-j 5(1 \angle 0)-j 4(0.995+j 0.1)\right] \\&=0.9978+j 0.0444-\frac{0.1111 \angle 53.13^{\circ}}{1 \angle 0} \\&=0.9311-j 0.0444=0.9322 \angle-2.74^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)

 

Repeat:

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{3} &=0.9978+j 0.0444-\frac{0.1111 \angle 53.13^{\circ}}{0.9322 \angle 2.74^{\circ}} \\&=0.9218-j 0.0474=0.923 \angle-2.94^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)

 

Check:

\(\begin{aligned}\Delta \bar{V}_{2} &=(0.995+j 0.100)-(1-j 0)=-0.005+j 0.1 \\\Delta \bar{V}_{3} &=(0.9218+j 0.0474)-(1-j 0)=-0.0782-j 0.0474 \\\Delta x_{\max } &=0.1\end{aligned}\)

 

Iteration 2:

\(\begin{aligned}Q_{2}=&-\operatorname{Im}\{[-j 6(0.995+j 0.1)+j 2(1 \angle 0)\\&+j 4(0.9218-j 0.0474)](0.995-j 0.1)\} \\=& 0.36\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{2} &=\frac{1}{-j 6}\left[\frac{0.6-j 0.36}{1 \angle-5.74^{\circ}}-j 2(1 \angle 0)-j 4(0.9218-j 0.0474)\right] \\&=0.9479+j 0.316+\frac{0.1166 \angle 59.04}{1 \angle-5.74}=1 \angle 4.24=0.9973+j 0.0739\end{aligned}\)

 

Repeat:

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{2} &=0.9479-j 0.0316+\frac{0.1166 \angle 59.04}{1 \angle-4.24}=1.0003+j 0.0725 \\& \simeq 1 \angle 4.15=0.9974+j 0.0723\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{3} &=\frac{1}{j 9}\left[\frac{-0.8+j 0.6}{0.9230 \angle 2.94}-j 5(1 \angle 0)-j 4(0.9974+j 0.0723)\right] \\&=0.9988+j 0.0321-\frac{0.1111 \angle 53.13^{\circ}}{0.923 \angle 2.94}=0.9217-j 0.0604 \\&=0.9237 \angle-3.75\end{aligned}\)

 

Repeat:

\(\begin{aligned}\bar{V}_{3} &=0.9988+j 0.0321-\frac{0.1111 \angle 53.13^{\circ}}{0.9237 \angle 3.75^{\circ}}=0.9205-j 0.0592 \\&=0.9224 \angle-3.68\end{aligned}\)

 

Check:

\(\begin{aligned}\Delta \bar{V}_{2} &=(0.9974+j 0.0732)-(0.995+j 0.1)=0.0024-j 0.0277 \\\Delta \bar{V}_{3} &=(0.9205-j 0.0592)-(0.9218-j 0.0474)=-0.0013-j 0.0118 \\\Delta x_{\max } &=0.028\end{aligned}\)

 

Third iteration yields the following results within the desired tolerance:

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}\bar{V}_{2}=0.9981+j 0.0610=1 \angle 3.5^{\circ} \\\bar{V}_{3}=0.9208-j 0.0644=0.923 \angle-4^{\circ}\end{array}\right\} \leftarrow\)
