Guest Question.

In the internal expanding shoe brake shown in Fig.7.24, the distance \( O O_{1}=80 mm \). The internal radius of the brake drum is 105 mm. Angle \( B O O_{1}=45^{\circ} \text { and } A O O_{1}=135^{\circ} \). The brake is applied by means of a force at C perpendicular to the line \(O_{1} C \text {, the distance } O_{1} C \) being 150 mm. The coefficient of friction may be taken as 0.4 and the braking torque required is 25 Nm. Calculate the force required at C to operate the brake when (a) the drum rotates clockwise, and (b) the drum rotates anticlockwise.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given: } O O_{1}=80 mm , r=105 mm , \theta_{1}=45^{\circ}=\frac{\pi}{4}, \theta_{2}=135^{\circ}=\frac{3 \pi}{4}, l=150 mm , \mu=0.4, T_{b}=25 Nm \).

Let F=force applied at C.

(a) When drum rotates clockwise.

\( T_{ b }=\mu p_{1} b r^{2}\left(\cos \theta_{1}-\cos \theta_{2}\right) \).

\( 25 \times 10^{3}=0.4 \times p_{1} b \times 105^{2}\left(\cos \frac{\pi}{4}-\cos \frac{3 \pi}{4}\right) \).

\( =6236.68 \times p_{1} b \).

\( p_{1} P b=4 \).

Total moment of normal forces about the fulcrum \( O_1\) ,

\( M_{r}=\frac{1}{2}\left(p_{1} b\right) r \times O O_{1}\left[\left(\theta_{2}-\theta_{1}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\sin 2 \theta_{1}-\sin 2 \theta_{2}\right)\right] \).

\( =\frac{1}{2} \times 4 \times 105 \times 80\left[\left(\frac{3 \pi}{4}-\frac{\pi}{4}\right)+\frac{1}{2}\left(\sin 90^{\circ}-\sin 270^{\circ}\right)\right] \).

\( =43189 Nmm \).

Total moment of friction force about the fulcrum \( O_1\) ,

\( M_{ f }=\mu p_{1} b r\left[r\left(\cos \theta_{1}-\cos \theta_{2}\right)+\frac{O O_{1}}{4}\left(\cos 2 \theta_{2}-\cos 2 \theta_{1}\right)\right] \).

\( =0.4 \times 4 \times 105\left[105\left(\cos 45^{\circ}-\cos 135^{\circ}\right)+\frac{80}{4}\left(\cos 270^{\circ}-\cos 90^{\circ}\right)\right] \).

\( =24947 Nmm \).

Taking moments about \( O_1\) ,

\( F l=M_{n}+M_{f} \).

\( F \times 150=43189+24947=68136 \).

F=454.24 N.

(b) When drum rotates anticlockwise.

\( F l=M_{n}-M_{f} \).

\( F \times 150=43189-24947=18242 \).

F=121.6 N.
