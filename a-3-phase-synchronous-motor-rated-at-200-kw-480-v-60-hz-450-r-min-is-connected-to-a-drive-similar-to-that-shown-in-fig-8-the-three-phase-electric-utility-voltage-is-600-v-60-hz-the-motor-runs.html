Guest Question.

A 3-phase synchronous motor rated at 200 kW, 480 V, 60 Hz, 450 r/min, is connected to a drive similar to that shown in Fig. 8. The three-phase electric utility voltage is 600 V, 60 Hz.
The motor runs at a speed of 535 r/min. The effective terminal voltage is 511 V and the motor draws an effective line current \(I_{ s }\) of 239 A at a power factor of 95%. The motor has an efficiency of 93%. Neglecting the losses in the converters, calculate

a. The frequency applied to the stator

b. The fundamental component of the stator current \(I_{ s }\)

c. The current I flowing in the dc link

d. The firing angle \(\alpha_{2}\) of converter 2

e. The voltage \(E_{ 2 }\) of the dc link

f. The firing angle \(\alpha_{1}\) of converter 1

g. The reactive power supplied to converter 1

h. The mechanical power developed by the motor

Admin
Solution

a. The frequency applied to the motor is proportional to its speed. Because the rated speed is 450 r/min at a frequency of 60 Hz, the frequency at 535 r/min is

 

\(f=\frac{535 r / min }{450 r / min } \times 60 Hz =71.3 Hz\)

 

b. Fundamental component of the stator current

 

\(\begin{aligned}I_{ F } &=0.955 I_{ s } \\&=0.955 \times 239=228 A\end{aligned}\)

 

c. Current in the dc link

 

\(\begin{aligned}I_{ d } &=\frac{I_{ F }}{0.78} \\&=\frac{228}{0.78}=293 A\end{aligned}\)

 

d. Firing angle \(\alpha_{2}\)

 

Converter 2 acts as an inverter, consequently,

 

\(\begin{aligned}\alpha_{2} &=-\arccos F P=-\arccos 0.95 \\&=180^{\circ}-18.2^{\circ}=161.8^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)

 

e. Voltage \(E_{2}\) of the dc link

 

\(\begin{aligned}E_{2} &=1.35 E_{ s } \cos \alpha_{2} \\&=1.35 \times 511 \times \cos 161.8^{\circ}=-655 V\end{aligned}\)

 

The dc voltage drop across inductor L is negligible, consequently, \(E_{1}=E_{2}=655 V\)

 

f. Firing angle \(\alpha_{1}\)

 

Converter 1 acts as a rectifier, hence

 

\(\begin{aligned}E_{1} &=1.35 E_{ L } \cos \alpha_{1} \\655 &=1.35 \times 600 \times \cos \alpha_{1} \\\alpha_{1} &=\arccos 0.808=36.0^{\circ}\end{aligned}\)

 

g. Active power supplied to converter 1

 

\(P=E_{1} I=655 \times 293=191915 W =192 kW\)

 

Displacement power factor of converter 1

 

\(P F=\cos \alpha_{1}=\cos 36.0^{\circ}=0.809=80.9 \%\)

 

Apparent power absorbed by converter 1

 

\(S=192 kW / 0.809=237 kVA\)

 

Reactive power absorbed by converter 1

 

\(\begin{aligned}Q &=\sqrt{S^{2}-P^{2}} \\&=\sqrt{237^{2}-192^{2}}=139 kvar\end{aligned}\)

 

h. Mechanical power developed by the motor

 

\(P_{ m }=192 kW \times 0.93=179 kW \approx 240 hp\)
