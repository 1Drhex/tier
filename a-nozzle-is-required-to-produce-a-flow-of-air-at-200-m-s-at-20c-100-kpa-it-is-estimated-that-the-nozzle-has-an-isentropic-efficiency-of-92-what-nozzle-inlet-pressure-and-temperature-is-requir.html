For the real process: \(h _{ i }= h _{ e }+ V _{ e }^{2} / 2\) or

\(T _{ i }= T _{ e }+ V _{ e }^{2} / 2 C _{ P 0}=293.2+200^{2} / 2 \times 1000 \times 1.004= 3 1 3 . 1 K\)

For the ideal process, from Eq.7.30:

\(V _{ es }^{2} / 2= V _{ e }^{2} / 2 \eta_{ s }=200^{2} / 2 \times 1000 \times 0.92=21.74 kJ / kg\)

and \(h _{ i }= h _{ es }+\left( V _{ es }^{2} / 2\right)\)

\(T _{ es }= T _{ i }- V _{ es }^{2} /\left(2 C _{ P 0}\right)=313.1-21.74 / 1.004=291.4 K\)

\(\Rightarrow \quad P_{i}=P_{e}\left(T_{i} / T_{e s}\right)^{\frac{k}{k-1}}=100\left(\frac{313.1}{291.4}\right)^{3.50}=128.6 kPa\)
