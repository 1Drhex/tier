Guest Question.

A gage pressure of 52.3 kPa is read on a gage. Find the absolute pressure if the elevation is:
(a) At sea level (b) 1000 m
(c) 5000 m (d) 10 000 m
(e) 30 000 m

Admin
Solution

Use the values from Table B.3 in the Appendix:
b) At an elevation of 1000 m the atmospheric pressure is 89.85 kPa. Hence, the absolute pressure is 52.3 + 89.85 = \(\underline{142.2 kPa} \)

d) At an elevation of 10,000 m the atmospheric pressure is 26.49 kPa, and the absolute pressure is 52.3 + 26.49 = \(\underline{78.8 kPa} \)
