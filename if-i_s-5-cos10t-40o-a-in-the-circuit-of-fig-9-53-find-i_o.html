Guest Question.

If \(i_{s} = 5 cos(10t + 40^{o} )\) A in the circuit of Fig. 9.53, find \(i_{o}\) .

Admin
Solution
\(\begin{array}{l}\mathrm{i}_{\mathrm{s}}=5 \cos \left(10 \mathrm{t}+40^{\circ}\right) \longrightarrow \mathrm{I}_{\mathrm{s}}=5 \angle 40^{\circ} \\\\0.1 \mathrm{F} \longrightarrow \frac{1}{\mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{C}}=\frac{1}{\mathrm{j}(10)(0.1)}=-\mathrm{j} \\\\0.2 \mathrm{H} \longrightarrow \quad \mathrm{j} \omega \mathrm{L}=\mathrm{j}(10)(0.2)=\mathrm{j} 2\end{array}\)

 

Let \(\quad {Z}_{1}=4 \| \mathrm{j} 2=\frac{\mathrm{j} 8}{4+\mathrm{j} 2}=0.8+\mathrm{j} 1.6, \quad \mathrm{Z}_{2}=3-\mathrm{j}\)

\({I}_{0}=\frac{{Z}_{1}}{{Z}_{1}+{Z}_{2}} {I}_{\mathrm{s}}=\frac{0.8+\mathrm{j} 1.6}{3.8+\mathrm{j} 0.6}\left(5 \angle 40^{\circ}\right)\) \({I}_{\mathrm{o}}=\frac{\left(1.789 \angle 63.43^{\circ}\right)\left(5 \angle 40^{\circ}\right)}{3.847 \angle 8.97^{\circ}}=2.325 \angle 94.46^{\circ}\)

 

Thus, \(\mathrm{i}_{0}(\mathrm{t})={2.325 \cos \left(10 \mathrm{t}+94.46^{\circ}\right) \mathrm{A}}\)
