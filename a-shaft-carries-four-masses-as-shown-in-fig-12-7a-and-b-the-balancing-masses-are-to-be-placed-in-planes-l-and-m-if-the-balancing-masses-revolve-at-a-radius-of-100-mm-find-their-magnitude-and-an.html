\( \sum M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=0.961 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=8.832 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}=-7.589 \).

\( \Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}=-1.028 \).

\( \left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=M_{M} r_{M} l_{M} \).

\( \left[(-7.589)^{2}+(-1.028)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=0.04 M_{M} \).

\( M_{M}=\frac{7.658}{0.04}=191.45 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{M}=\frac{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \sin \theta_{i}}{-\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} l_{i} \cos \theta_{i}} \).

\( =\frac{-(-1.028)}{-(-7.589)}=0.13546 \).

\( \theta_{M}=7.71^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both positive, therefore } \theta_{M} \text { lies in the first quadrant. } \).

\( M_{L} r_{L}=\left[\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i} M_{M} r_{M} \cos \theta_{M}\right)^{2}+\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+M_{M} r_{M} \sin \theta_{M}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( 0.1 M L=\left[\left(0.961+0.1 \times 191.45 \times \cos 7.71^{\circ}\right)^{2}+\left(8.832+0.1 \times 191.45 \times \sin 7.71^{\circ}\right)^{2}\right]^{0.5} \).

\( =\left[(19.933)^{2}+(11.400)^{2}\right]^{0.5}=22.963 \).

\( M_{L}=229.63 kg \).

\( \tan \theta_{L}=\frac{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \sin \theta_{i}+m_{m} r_{m} \sin \theta_{M}\right)}{-\left(\Sigma M_{i} r_{i} \cos \theta_{i}+m_{m} r_{m} \cos \theta_{M}\right)} \).

\( =\frac{-11.400}{-19.933}=0.5719 ; \quad \theta_{L}=29.76^{\circ}+180^{\circ}=209.76^{\circ} \).

\( \text { Since the numerator and denominator are both negative, therefore } \theta_{L} \text { lies in the third quadrant. } \).
