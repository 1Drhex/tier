Guest Question.

Water is siphoned from the open tank. Determine the volumetric discharge from the 20-mm-diameter hose. Draw the energy and hydraulic grade lines for the hose using a datum at B.

Admin
Solution

Bernoulli Equation. Since the tank is a large source,\( V_A = 0.\) Here, A and B are exposed to the atmosphere,\( p_A = p_B = 0. \)If the datum is set to coincide with the horizontal line through B,\( z_A = 0.3 m + 0.4 m = 0.7 m and z_B = 0. \)

\(\frac{P_A}{\gamma } + \frac{V_A^2}{2g} + z_A= \frac{P_B}{\gamma } + \frac{V_B^{2}}{2g} + z_B \)

 

\(0 + 0 + 0.7 m = 0 + \frac{V_B^2}{2(9.81 m/s^2)} + 0 \) \(V_B = 3.706 m/s \)

Discharge.

\(Q = V_BA_B = (3.706 m/s) [\pi (0.01 m)^2] \)

 

\(= 0.00116 m^3/s \)
