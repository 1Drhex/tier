Guest Question.

A geothermal source provides 10 kg/s of hot water at 500 kPa, 145째C flowing into a flash evaporator that separates vapor and liquid at 200 kPa. Find the three fluxes of exergy (inlet and two outlets) and the irreversibility rate.

Admin
Solution

C.V. Flash evaporator chamber. Steady flow with no work or heat transfer.

Cont. Eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{1}=\dot{ m }_{2}+\dot{ m }_{3}\);

Energy Eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{1} h _{1}=\dot{ m }_{2} h _{2}+\dot{ m }_{3} h _{3}\)

Entropy Eq.: \(\dot{ m }_{1} s _{1}+\dot{ S }_{ gen }=\dot{ m }_{2} s _{2}+\dot{ m }_{3} s _{3}\)

B.1.1: \(h _{ o }=104.87, s _{ o }=0.3673\) , B.1.4: \(h _{1}=610.7, s _{1}=1.7902\)

B.1.2: \(h _{2}=2706.63, \quad s _{2}=7.1271, \quad h _{3}=504.68, \quad s _{3}=1.530\)

 

\(h _{1}= xh _{2}+(1- x ) h _{3} \Rightarrow x =\dot{ m }_{2} / \dot{ m }_{1}=\frac{ h _{1}- h _{3}}{ h _{2}- h _{3}}=0.04815\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{2}= x \dot{ m }_{1}=0.4815 kg / s \quad \dot{ m }_{3}=(1- x ) \dot{ m }_{1}=9.5185 kg / s\)

 

\(\dot{ S }_{ gen }=0.4815 \times 7.1271+9.5185 \times 1.53-10 \times 1.7902=0.093 kW / K\)

 

Flow exergy Eq.8.22: \(\psi=\left( h – T _{0} s \right)-\left( h _{ o }- T _{ o ^{ S } o }\right)= h – h _{ o }- T _{ o }\left( s – s _{ o }\right)\)

 

\(\begin{array}{l}\psi_{1}=610.7-104.87-298.15(1.7902-0.3673)=81.59 kJ / kg \\\psi_{2}=2706.63-104.87-298.15(7.1271-0.3673)=586.33 kJ / kg \\\psi_{3}=504.68-104.87-298.15(1.53-0.3673)=53.15 kJ / kg\end{array}\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{1} \psi_{1}= 8 1 5 . 9 2 k W \quad \dot{ m }_{2} \psi_{2}= 2 8 2 . 3 k W \quad \dot{ m }_{3} \psi_{3}= 5 0 5 . 9 k W\)

 

Balance of flow terms in Eq.8.38

\(\dot{ I }=\dot{ m }_{1} \psi_{1}-\dot{ m }_{2} \psi_{2}-\dot{ m }_{3} \psi_{3}=27.7 kW \quad\left(= T _{ o } \dot{ S }_{ gen }\right)\)

\(\begin{aligned}\psi &=\left(h_{ tot }-T_{0} s\right)-\left(h_{ tot 0}-T_{0} s_{0}\right) \\&=\left(h-T_{0} s+\frac{1}{2} V ^{2}+g Z\right)-\left(h_{0}-T_{0} s_{0}+g Z_{0}\right)\end{aligned}\)

Eq.8.38 :
\(\frac{d \Phi}{d t}=\sum\left(1-\frac{T_{0}}{T}\right) \dot{Q}_{ c.v . }\) Transfer by heat at T
\(-\dot{W}_{ c.v. }+P_{0} \frac{d V}{d t}\) Transfer by shaft/boundary work
\(+\sum \dot{m}_{i} \psi_{i}-\sum \dot{m}_{e} \psi_{e}\) Transfer by flow
\(-T_{0} \dot{S}_{ gen }\) Exergy destruction
