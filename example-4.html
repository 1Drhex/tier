Solution

From the given figure, we have

(i) Moment of couple \(M = 40 \times 1.5 = 60\) N-m \((\circlearrowleft )\)

(ii) Moment of forces about point A

\(M_{A} = 40 \times 1.5 = 60\) N-m\((\circlearrowleft )\)

(iii) Moment of forces about point B

\(M_{B} = 40 \times 1.5 = 60\) N-m\((\circlearrowleft )\)

(iv) Moment of forces about point C

\(M_{C} = 40 \times 3.5 – 40 \times 2 = 40(3.5 – 2)= 40 \times 1.5 = 60\) N-m\((\circlearrowleft )\)

(v) Moment of force about point D

\(M_{D} = 40 \times 6 – 40 \times 4.5 = 40(6 – 4.5)= 40 \times 1.5 = 60\) N-m\((\circlearrowleft )\)
