Guest Question.

The turning moment diagram for one revolution of a multicylinder engine is shown in Fig.10.30. The vertical and horizontal scales are:

1 mm=600 N m and 2.5°, respectively. The fluctuation of speed is limited to ±1.5 % of mean speed, which is 250 rpm. The hoop stress in rim material is limited to 5.6 N/mm². Neglecting effect of boss and arms, determine the suitable diameter and cross-section of flywheel rim. Take density of rim material as 7200 kg/m³, and width to be four times the thickness.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Hoop stress, } \sigma_{\theta}=\rho v^{2} \).

\( v=\pi d N_{m} K_{s} / 60 \).

\( =13.286 d m / s \).

\( 5.6 \times 10^{6}=7200 \times(13.286 d )^{2} \).

d=2.1 m.

Let E=energy at A

Energy (mm²) Point
E -30 B
E – 30+410 =E+380 C
E+380 – 275=E+105 D
E+105+340=E+445 E
E+445 – 320=E+125 F
E+125+245=E+370 G
E+370 – 385=E – 15 H
E – 15+276=E+261 J
E+261 – 261=E K

\( \text { Fluctuation of energy, } E_{f}=E_{\max }-E_{\min } \).

\( =(E+445)-(E-30) \).

\( =475 mm ^{2} \).

\( =475 \times 10^{-6} \times\left(2.5 \times \pi \times 10^{3} / 180\right) \times 600 \times 10^{3} \).

= 12435.47 N m.

\( W=\left(900 \times g \times E_{f}\right) /\left(\pi^{2} \times K^{2} \times N_{m}^{2} \times C_{s}\right) \).

\( =(900 \times 9.81 \times 12435.47) /\left(\pi^{2} \times 1.05^{2} \times 250^{2} \times 0.03\right) \).

= 5381.4 N.

Now \( W =\pi d b t \rho g \).

\( 5381.4=\pi \times 2.1 \times 4 t \times t \times 7200 \times 9.81 \).

\( t=53.73 mm \quad \text { and } \quad b =214.92 mm \).
