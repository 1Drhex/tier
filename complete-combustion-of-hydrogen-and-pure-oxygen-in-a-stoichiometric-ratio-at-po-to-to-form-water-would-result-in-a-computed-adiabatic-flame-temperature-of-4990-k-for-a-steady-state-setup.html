Guest Question.

Complete combustion of hydrogen and pure oxygen in a stoichiometric ratio at \(P_{o}, T_{o}\) to form water would result in a computed adiabatic flame temperature of 4990 K for a steady state setup. How should the adiabatic flame temperature be found if the equilibrium reaction \(2 H _{2}+ O _{2} \Leftrightarrow 2 H _{2} O\) is considered? Disregard all other possible reactions (dissociations) and show the final equation(s) to be solved.

Admin
Solution
\(2 H _{2}+ O _{2} \Leftrightarrow 2 H _{2} O\)

 

\(\begin{array}{cccc}\text { Species } & H _{2} & O _{2} & H _{2} O \\\text { Initial } & 2 & 1 & 0 \\\text { Shift } & -2 x & – x & 2 x \\\hline \text { Final } & 2-2 x & 1- x & 2 x\end{array}\)

 

\(Keq =\frac{ y _{ H _{2} O }^{2}}{ y _{ H _{2}}^{2} y _{ O _{2}}}\left(\frac{ P }{ P ^{0}}\right)^{-1}, \quad n _{ tot }=2-2 x +1- x +2 x =3- x\)

 

Energy Eq.: \(H _{ p }= H _{ R }= H _{ p }^{ o }+\Delta H _{ p }= H _{ R }^{ o }=0\)

\(H _{ P }=(1- x )\left(2 \Delta \overline{ h }_{ H _{2}}+\Delta \overline{ h }_{ O _{2}}\right)+2 x \left(\overline{ h }_{ fH 2 O }^{ o }+\Delta \overline{ h }_{ H 2 O }\right)=0\) (1)

Equilibrium constant:

\(K _{ eq }=\frac{4 x ^{2}}{(3- x )^{2}} \frac{(3- x )^{2}}{(2-2 x )^{2}} \frac{3- x }{1- x }=\frac{ x ^{2}(3- x )}{(1- x )^{3}}= K _{ T }\) (2)

 

A.10: \(\overline{ h }_{ fH 2 O }^{ o }=-241826 kJ / kmol\); A.11: \(\ln \left( K _{ T }\right)\)

A.9: \(\Delta \overline{ h }_{ H _{2}}( T ), \Delta \overline{ h }_{ O _{2}}( T ), \Delta \overline{ h }_{ H _{2} O }( T )\),

Trial and error (solve for x, T) using Eqs. (1) and (2).

Guess \(T =3600 K : K =\exp (1.996)=7.35956, \quad x =0.53986\)

\(\begin{aligned}H _{ P }=&(1-0.53986)(2 \times 111367+122245) \\&+2 \times 0.53986 \times(-241826+160484)=70912 \text { high }\end{aligned}\)

 

Guess \(T =3400 K : K =\exp (3.128)=22.8283, x =0.648723\)

\(\begin{aligned}H _{ P }=&(1-0.648723)(2 \times 103736+114101) \\&+2 \times 0.648723 \times(-241826+149073)=-7381 \text { low }\end{aligned}\)

Now do a linear interpolation to get \(H _{ P }=0\):

\(\begin{aligned}& T =3400+200 \times 7381 /(70912+7381)= 3 4 1 9 ~ K , K =20.50 \\& x =0.63905, y _{ O _{2}}=0.153 ; y _{ H _{2}}=0.306 ; y _{ H _{2} O }=0.541\end{aligned}\)
