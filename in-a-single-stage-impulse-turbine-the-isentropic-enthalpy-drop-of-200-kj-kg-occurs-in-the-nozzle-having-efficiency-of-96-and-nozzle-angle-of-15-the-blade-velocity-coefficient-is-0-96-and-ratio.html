Guest Question.

In a single stage impulse turbine the isentropic enthalpy drop of 200 kJ/kg occurs in the nozzle having efficiency of 96% and nozzle angle of 15°. The blade velocity coefficient is 0.96 and ratio of blade speed to steam velocity is 0.5. The steam mass flow rate is 20 kg/s and velocity of steam entering is 50 m/s. Determine
(a) the blade angles at inlet and outlet if the steam enters blades smoothly and leaves axially.
(b) the blade efficiency
(c) the power developed in kW
(d) the axial thrust.
Solve using velocity diagram.

Admin
Solution
\(\text{Let the state at inlet to nozzle, leaving nozzle and leaving blade be denoted using subscripts 0, 1 and 2.}\\\text{Using nozzle efficiency the actual enthalpy drop may be calculated as.}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Actual enthalpy drop in nozzle, }Δh_{N}&= 200 × 0.96 = 192 kJ/kg\\\text{Velocity of steam leaving nozzle, }C_{1}&= \sqrt{2 · Δh_{N} + (C_{0})^{2}}\\C_{1}&= \sqrt{2 × 192 × 10^{3} + (50)^{2}}\\C_{1}&= 621.69 m/s\\\text{Given:}\qquad\qquad\qquad \qquad\qquad \qquad \frac{U}{C_{1}}&= 0.5, \frac{V_{2}}{V_{1}} = 0.96, m = 20 kg/s, α_{1} = 15°\\\text{or,}\qquad\qquad\qquad \qquad\qquad \qquad \qquad U&= 0.5 × 621.69 = 310.845 m/s\end{aligned}\\\text{Let us draw the velocity diagram across blade i.e. states 1 and 2.}\\\text{Let us use scale as 1 cm = 100 m/s}\\\text{From ΔABD, AD }= C_{1} = 621.69 m/s, AB = U = 310.845 m/s\\\text{Length BD gives V}_{1} ,\text{ from measurement it is seen to be 3.3 cm,}\\\begin{aligned}\text{hence, }\qquad V_{1}&= 330 m/s, so V_{2} = 0.96 × 330\\V_{2}&= 316.8\\\text{From velocity di}&\text{agram, }β_{1} = 28.5°, β_{2} = 12°, ΔC_{w} = 595 m/s,\\V_{a1}&= 170 m/s\\V_{a2}&= 75 m/s\\\text{Blade}&\text{ angle at inlet = 28.5°}\\\text{Blade}&\text{ angle at exit= 12°}\\\text{Blade efficiency}&= \frac{m U · ΔC_{w}}{m\left\lgroup\frac{C_{1}^{2}}{2} \right\rgroup } = \frac{2 × 310.845 × 535}{(621.69)^{2}} = 0.9571\\\text{or}\qquad \qquad \qquad &= 95.71\%\\\text{Blade}&\text{ efficiency = 95.71}\%\end{aligned}\\\begin{aligned}\text{Power developed}&= U · ΔC_{w} × m = 20 × 310.845 × 595\\&= 369905.55 J/s\\\text{or }\qquad\qquad \qquad &= 369.91 kJ/s\\\text{Power}&\text{ developed = 369.91 kW}\\\text{Axial thrust, }F_{a}&= m · (V_{a1} – V_{a2})\\&= 20 × (170 – 75)\\&= 1900 N\\\text{Axial}&\text{ thrust = 1900 N}\end{aligned}\)
