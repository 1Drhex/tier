Guest Question.

Given: The symmetrical beam shown in Fig. E10.2a. Assume EI is constant. Take L = 6 m, \(I=180(10)^{6}mm^{4}\), and E = 200 \(KN/mm^{2}\).

Case (i), the middle support settles an amount \(v_{B}=40\) mm.

Case (ii), the left support settles an amount \(v_{A}=40\) mm.

Determine: The end actions, the shear and bending moment diagrams.

Admin
Solution

Case (i): Support settlement at B (Fig. E10.2b)

 

Noting (10.17), the chord rotations due to settlement at B are:

 

\(\rho _{AB}=\frac{v_{B}-v_{A}}{L}=-\frac{v_{B}}{L}\)

 

\(\rho _{BC}=\frac{v_{C}-v_{B}}{L}=+\frac{v_{B}}{L}\)

 

Substituting for \(\rho _{AB}\) and \(\rho _{BC}\), the corresponding slope-deflection equation (10.12a) take the form

\(M_{AB}=\frac{2EI}{L}\left\{2\theta _{A}+\theta _{B}-3\left(\frac{\nu _{B}-\nu _{A}}L{} \right) \right\} +M^{F}_{AB} \) (10.12a)

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}\left\{\theta _{A}+2\theta _{B}-3\left(\frac{\nu _{B}-\nu _{A}}L{} \right) \right\} +M^{F}_{BA} \) (10

12a)

 

\(M_{AB}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{A}+\theta _{B})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{B}+\theta _{A})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(M_{BC}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{B}+\theta _{C})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{BC}\)

 

\(M_{CB}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{C}+\theta _{B})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{BC}\)

 

We enforce moment equilibrium at nodes A, B, and C.

 

The corresponding equations are:

 

\(M_{AB}=0\Rightarrow 2\theta _{A}+\theta _{B}=-3\frac{v_{B}}{L}\)

 

\(M_{BA}+M_{BC}=0\Rightarrow \theta _{A}+4\theta _{B}+\theta _{C}=0\)

 

\(M_{CB}=0\Rightarrow 2\theta _{C}+\theta _{B}=3\frac{v_{B}}{L}\)

 

Solving for the \(\theta s\) leads to

 

\(\theta _{B}=0\)

 

\(\theta _{A}=-\frac{3v_{B}}{2L}\)

 

\(\theta _{C}=+\frac{3v_{B}}{2L}\)

 

The corresponding end moments are:

 

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}\left ( -\frac{3v_{B}}{2L} \right )-\frac{6EI}{L}\left ( -\frac{v_{B}}{L} \right )=+\frac{3EI}{L^{2}}v_{B}=\frac{3(200)(180)10^{6}}{(6000)^{2}}(40)\)

 

= 120,000 kN mm = 120 kN m

 

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}\left ( \frac{3v_{B}}{2L} \right )-\frac{6EI}{L}\left ( \frac{v_{B}}{L} \right )=-\frac{3EI}{L^{2}}v_{B}=-120\) KN m

 

Next, we determine the end shear forces using the static equilibrium equations for the members.

 

\(V_{AB}=V_{CB}=+\frac{3EI}{L^{3}}v_{B}=\frac{3(200)(180)10^{6}}{(6000)^{3}}(40)=20\, KN\uparrow\)

 

\(V_{BA}=V_{BC}=-\frac{3EI}{L^{3}}v_{B}=20\, KN\downarrow\)

 

The corresponding reactions are:

 

\(R_{A}=V_{AB}=20\, KN\uparrow\)

 

\(R_{B}=V_{BA}+V_{BC}=40\, KN\downarrow\)

 

\(R_{C}=V_{CB}=20\, KN\uparrow\)

 

One should expect that \(\theta _{B}=0\) because of symmetry. The shear and moment diagrams are plotted below.

 

Case (ii): Support settlement at A (Fig. E10.2c)

 

Settlement at A produces chord rotation in member AB only. The chord rotation for member AB due to settlement of node A is \(\rho _{AB}=v_{A}/L\). Substituting for \(\rho _{AB}\), the corresponding slope-deflection equation (10.12a) take the form

 

\(M_{AB}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{A}+\theta _{B})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{B}+\theta _{A})-\frac{6EI}{L}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(M_{BC}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{B}+\theta _{C})\)

 

\(M_{CB}=\frac{2EI}{L}(2\theta _{C}+\theta _{B})\)

 

Setting \(M_{AB}=M_{CB}=0\) and \(M_{BA}=M_{BC}=0\) leads to

 

\(2\theta _{A}+\theta _{B}=3\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(2\theta _{C}+\theta _{B}=0\)

 

\(4\theta _{B}+\theta _{A}+\theta _{C}=3\rho _{AB}\)

 

Solving for the \(\theta s\) leads to

 

\(\theta _{A}=\frac{5}{4}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(\theta _{B}=\frac{1}{2}\rho _{AB}\)

 

\(\theta _{C}=-\frac{1}{4}\rho _{AB}\)

 

Finally, the bending moment at B due to support settlement at A is:

 

\(M_{BA}=\frac{2EI}{L}\left ( \frac{v_{A}}{L}+\frac{5v_{A}}{4L} \right )-\frac{6EIv_{A}}{L^{2}}=-\frac{1.5EI}{L^{2}}v_{A}=\frac{1.5(200)(180)10^{6}}{(6000)^{2}}(40)\)

 

= -60,000 kN mm = -60 kN m

 

\(M_{BC}=-M_{BA}=60\) KN m counterclockwise

 

Next, we determine the end shear forces using the static equilibrium equations for the members.

 

Then,

 

\(R_{A}=R_{C}=-\frac{1.5EI}{L^{3}}v_{A}=-\frac{1.5(200)(180)10^{6}}{(6000)^{3}}(40)=-10\) KN

 

\(R_{B}=\frac{1.5EI}{L^{3}}v_{A}+\frac{1.5EI}{L^{3}}v_{A}=+20\) KN

 

The shear and moment diagrams are plotted below.
