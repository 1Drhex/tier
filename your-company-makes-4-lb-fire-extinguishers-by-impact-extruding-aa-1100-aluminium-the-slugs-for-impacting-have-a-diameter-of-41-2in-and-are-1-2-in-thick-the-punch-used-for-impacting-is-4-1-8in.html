Guest Question.

Your company makes 4 lb fire extinguishers by impact extruding AA-1100 aluminium. The slugs for impacting have a diameter of \( 4 \frac{1}{2} \) in and are \( \frac{1}{2} \) in thick. The punch used for impacting is \( 4 \frac{1}{8} \)in diameter, as is the fire extinguisher inside diameter. It is required that 100 fire extinguishers be extruded per minute. Calculate the punch force and average power required.

Admin
Solution
\( A_{o}=\pi \frac{d^{2}_{o}}{4} =\pi \frac{4.5^{2}}{4} = 15.9in ^{2}\)

 

\( A_{f}=\pi \frac{(d^{2}_{o}- d^{2}_{f})}{4}= \pi \frac{(4.5^{2}- 4.125^{2})}{4}=2.54 in ^{2}\)

 

\( A_{puneh}=\pi \frac{d^{2}_{punch}}{4} =\pi \frac{4.125^{2}}{4}= 13.4in ^{2}\)

 

\( R_{e}= \frac{A_{o}}{A_{f}} =6.25; \epsilon =\ln R_{e}= 1.83 \)

For AA-1100 from Table 3.1, K= 140 MPa or 20 304 psi and n = 0.25. The average mean true stress for the process is calculated as follows

\( \sigma _{tm}=K\left[\frac{\epsilon ^{n}}{n+1} \right] =20394\left[\frac{1.83^{0.25}}{1.25} \right] =18 976 psi\)

 

\( Q_{e}= 0.8+1.2\ln R_{e}=2.99\)

 

\(p_{e}= 18976\times 2.99= 56 738 psi\)

 

\(F_{P}=p_{e}\times A_{p}= 56 738 \times 13.4= 760 293 lb_{f}\approx 380t\)

Work for extrusion

\( W=K \frac{\epsilon ^{n+1}}{n+1} \times V\)

 

\( V(volume)= \pi \frac{4.5^{2}}{4}\times 0.5=8 in^{3}\)

 

\(W=20304\frac{(1.83^{1.25})}{1.25}\times 8=23 000ftlb_{f}\)

The average time for extrusion of one fire extinguisher is 60/100 =0.6s per unit. The average power (time rate of doing Work) is

\(P_{av}=23 000 ft lb_{f} per 0.6 s = 38 300 ft lb_{f} per s or \approx 70 horsepower\)

Note: During impact extrusion no account is made for frictional effects, because the metal is extruded up around the punch (Fig. 4.12(c)) and it therefore is not in contact with the chamber during deformation
