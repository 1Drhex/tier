Guest Question.

A smaller power plant produces 25 kg/s steam at 3 MPa, 600°C in the boiler. It cools the condenser with ocean water coming in at 15°C and returned at 18°C so the condenser exit is at 50°C. Find the net power output and the required mass flow rate of ocean water.

Admin
Solution

The states properties from Tables B.1.1 and B.1.3

1: \(45^{\circ} C , x =0: h _{1}=188.42 kJ / kg , v _{1}=0.00101 m ^{3} / kg , P _{ sat }=9.59 kPa\)

3: \(3.0 MPa , 600^{\circ} C : \quad h _{3}=3682.34 kJ / kg , \quad s _{3}=7.5084 kJ / kg K\)

C.V. Pump Reversible and adiabatic.

Energy: \(w _{ p }= h _{2}- h _{1}\); Entropy: \(s _{2}= s _{1}\)

since incompressible it is easier to find work (positive in) as

\(w _{ p }=\int v dP = v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right)=0.00101 m ^{3} / kg (3000-9.6) kPa =3.02 kJ / kg\)

C.V. Turbine : \(w _{ T }= h _{3}- h _{4} ; s _{4}= s _{3}\)

\(\begin{aligned}s _{4}= s _{3} &=7.5084=0.6386+ x _{4}(7.5261) \quad \Rightarrow \quad x _{4}=0.9128 \\& \Rightarrow h _{4}=188.42+0.9128(2394.77)=2374.4 kJ / kg\end{aligned}\)

 

\(w _{ T }=3682.34-2374.4=1307.94 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\dot{ W }_{ NET }=\dot{ m }\left( w _{ T }- w _{ p }\right)=25(1307.94-3.02)= 3 2 . 6 ~ M W\)

 

C.V. Condenser : \(q _{ L }= h _{4}- h _{1}=2374.4-188.42=2186 kJ / kg\)

\(\dot{ Q }_{ L }=\dot{ m } q _{ L }=25 kg / s \times 2186 kJ / kg =54.65 MW =\dot{ m }_{\text {ocean }} C _{ p } \Delta T\)

 

\(\dot{ m }_{\text {ocean }}=\dot{ Q }_{ L } / C _{ p } \Delta T =\frac{54650}{4.18 \times(18-15)}= 4 3 5 8 kg / s\)
