Guest Question.

A closed rigid container is charged with propene, \(C _{3} H _{6}\) , and 150% theoretical air at 100 kPa, 298 K. The mixture is ignited and burns with complete combustion. Heat is transferred to a reservoir at 500 K so the final temperature of the products is 700 K. Find the final pressure, the heat transfer per kmole fuel and the total entropy generated per kmol fuel in the process.

Admin
Solution
\(C _{3} H _{6}+ v _{ O _{2}}\left( O _{2}+3.76 N _{2}\right) \rightarrow 3 CO _{2}+3 H _{2} O + x N _{2}\)

Oxygen, \(O _{2}\) , balance: \(2 v_{ O _{2}}=6+3=9 \Rightarrow \quad v_{ O _{2}}=4.5\)

Actual Combustion: \(\phi=1.5 \Rightarrow v_{ O _{2} ac }=1.5 \times 4.5=6.75\)

\(C _{3} H _{6}+6.75 O _{2}+25.38 N _{2} \rightarrow 3 CO _{2}+3 H _{2} O +25.38 N _{2}+2.25 O _{2}\)

 

\(P _{2}= P _{1} \frac{ n _{ p } T _{2}}{ n _{ R } T _{1}}=100 \times \frac{33.63 \times 700}{33.13 \times 298.15}= 2 3 8 . 3 ~ k P a\)

 

Enthalpies from Table A.9

\(\begin{aligned}\Delta H _{ P 700} &=3 \times 17754+3 \times 14190+25.38 \times 11937+2.25 \times 12499 \\&=426916 \frac{ kJ }{ kmol \text { fuel }}\end{aligned}\)

Enthalpy of combustion from table 13.3 converted to mole basis

\(H _{ RP }^{\circ}=-45780 \times 42.081=-1926468 kJ / kmol \text { fuel }\)

 

\(U _{2}- U _{1}={ }_{1} Q _{2}-0= H _{2}- H _{1}- n _{2} R \overline{ T }_{2}+ n _{1} R \overline{ T }_{1}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}{ }_{1} Q _{2}=& H _{ RP }^{\circ}+\Delta H _{ P 700}- n _{ P } R \overline{ T }_{2}+ n _{1} R \overline{ T }_{1} \\=&-1926468+426916-33.63 \times 8.3145 \times 700 \\&+33.13 \times 8.3145 \times 298.15=- 1 . 6 1 3 \times 1 0 ^{6} \frac{ kJ }{\text { kmol fuel }}\end{aligned}\)

 

Entropies from Table A.9 and pressure correction

\(\begin{array}{cccccc}\text { Reactants: } & n _{ i } & y _{ i } & \overline{ s }_{ i }^{\circ} & -\overline{ R } \ln \left( y _{ i } P / P _{0}\right) & S _{ i } \\C _{3} H _{8} & 1.0 & 0.0302 & 267.066 & 29.104 & 296.17 \\O _{2} & 6.75 & 0.2037 & 205.143 & 13.228 & 218.376 \\N _{2} & 25.38 & 0.7661 & 191.609 & 2.216 & 189.393\end{array}\)

 

\(S _{1}=296.17+6.75 \times 218.376+25.38 \times 189.393=6577 \frac{ kJ }{ kmol \text { fuel } K }\)

 

\(\begin{array}{cccccc}\text { Products: } & n _{ i } & y _{ i } & \overline{ s }_{ i }^{\circ} & -\overline{ R } \ln \left( y _{ i } P / P _{0}\right) & S _{ i } \\CO _{2} & 3 & 0.0892 & 250.752 & +12.875 & 263.627 \\H _{2} O & 3 & 0.0892 & 218.739 & +12.875 & 231.614 \\O _{2} & 2.25 & 0.0669 & 231.465 & +15.266 & 246.731 \\N _{2} & 25.38 & 0.7547 & 216.865 & -4.88 & 211.985\end{array}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}S _{2} &=3(263.627+231.614)+2.25 \times 246.731+25.38 \times 211.985 \\&=7421 kJ / kmol \text { fuel } K\end{aligned}\)

 

\({ }_{1} S _{2 gen }= S _{2}- S _{1}-{ }_{1} Q _{2} / T _{\text {res }}=7421-6577+\frac{1.613 \times 10^{6}}{500}= 4 0 7 0 \frac{ kJ }{ kmol \text { fuel } K }\)
