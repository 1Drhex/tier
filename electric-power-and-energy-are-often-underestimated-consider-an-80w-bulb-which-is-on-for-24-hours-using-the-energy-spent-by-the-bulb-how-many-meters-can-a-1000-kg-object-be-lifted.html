Guest Question.

Electric power and energy are often underestimated. Consider an 80W
bulb, which is on for 24 hours. Using the energy spent by the bulb, how many meters
can a 1000 kg object be lifted?

Admin
Solution

The energy spent by the bulb is

\(w_{b} =24\times 60\times 80=6.912\times 10^{6} J\) .

Then, assuming \(g=10m/s^{2}\) and using \(w_{p}=mgh\) for the potential energy,

we have \(1000\times 10\times h=60912\times 10^{6} \longrightarrow h=691.2m\)
